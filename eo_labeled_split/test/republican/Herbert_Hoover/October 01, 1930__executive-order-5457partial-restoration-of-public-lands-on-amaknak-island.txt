Alaska

It is hereby directed that Executive Order No. 5243 of December 19, 1929, restoring a portion of Amaknak Island, of the Aleutian group, Alaska, to the public domain, be, and the same is hereby, amended so as to revoke Executive order of April 2, 1901, withdrawing certain lands on the island for public purposes, and to exclude from the restoration the lands on the island set apart for lighthouse purposes by Executive order of January 13, 1899, amended by Executive order of June 10, 1902, and the lands reserved for the use of the Navy Department as a wireless station by Executive Order No. 1456 of January 6, 1912.
