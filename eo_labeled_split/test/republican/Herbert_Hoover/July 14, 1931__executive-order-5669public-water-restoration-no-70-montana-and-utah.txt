So much of Executive orders of April 28, 1917, creating Public Water Reserve No. 49, Montana No. 4, and March 29, 1912, creating Public Water Reserve No. 1, Utah No. 1, as affects the land hereinafter listed is hereby revoked, and all such land not otherwise reserved or withdrawn is hereby restored to the public domain and shall become subject to disposition under the laws applicable thereto upon such date and after such notice as may be hereafter determined and announced.
