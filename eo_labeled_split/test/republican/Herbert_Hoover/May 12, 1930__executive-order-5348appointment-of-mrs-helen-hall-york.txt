Mrs. Helen Hall York may be appointed a clerk in the classified service of the Treasury Department without compliance with the requirements of the civil service rules.

Mrs. York is the widow of former Prohibition Agent Lamar W. York, who met his death while in the performance of his official duties at Washington, D.C., on April 12, 1930, leaving Mrs. York with three small children and without adequate means of support.

This order is recommended by the Secretary of the Treasury.
