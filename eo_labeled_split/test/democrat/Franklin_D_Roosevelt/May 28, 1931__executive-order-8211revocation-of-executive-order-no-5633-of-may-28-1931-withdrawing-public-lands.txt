CALIFORNIA

By virtue of the authority vested in me by the act of June 25, 1910, ch. 421, 36 Stat. 847, Executive Order No. 5633 of May 28, 1931, withdrawing public lands in California pending a resurvey, is hereby revoked.

This order shall become effective upon the date of the official filing of the plat of the resurvey of the lands involved.
