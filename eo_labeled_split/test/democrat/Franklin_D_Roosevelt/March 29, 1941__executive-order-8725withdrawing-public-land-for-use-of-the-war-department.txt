FLORIDA

By virtue of the authority vested in me by section 1 of the act of July 9, 1918, c. 143, 40 Stat. 845, 848 (U.S.C., title 10, sec. 1341), it is ordered that, subject to valid existing rights, all the public lands within the following-described areas be, and they are hereby, withdrawn from all forms of appropriation under the publicland laws, including the mining laws, and reserved for the use of the War Department in connection with aerial gunnery ranges:

TALLAHASSEE MERIDIAN

T. 6 S., R. 12 W., sec. 7, all, sec. 16, that part lying west of longitude 85°27'30" west, sec. 17, all sec. 18, all;

T. 6 S., R. 13 W., sec. 1, lot 11, sec. 2, lot 2, sec. 11, lots 1, 2, 6, 7, 8, and SW ¼ NE ¼, sec. 12, lots 1 to 7, inclusive, sec. 13, all;

aggregating 1,744.86 acres.
