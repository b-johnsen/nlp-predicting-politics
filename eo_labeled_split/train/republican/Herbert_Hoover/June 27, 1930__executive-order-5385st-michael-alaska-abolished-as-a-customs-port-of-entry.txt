By virtue of the authority vested in me by the act of Congress approved August 1, 1914 (38 Stat. 623), making appropriations for the sundry civil expenses of the Government for the fiscal year ended June 30, 1915, St. Michael, Alaska, is hereby abolished as a port of entry in Customs Collection District No. 31 (Alaska), with headquarters at Juneau, Alaska, effective July 1, 1930.
