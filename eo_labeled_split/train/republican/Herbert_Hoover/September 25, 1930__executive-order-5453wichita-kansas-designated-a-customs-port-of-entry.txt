By virtue of the authority vested in me by the act of Congress approved August 1, 1914 (38 Stat. 623), making appropriations for the sundry civil expenses of the Government for the fiscal year ending June 30, 1915, Wichita, Kans., is hereby designated a port of entry in Customs Collection District No. 45 (St. Louis), with headquarters at St. Louis, Mo., effective 30 days from the date of this order.
