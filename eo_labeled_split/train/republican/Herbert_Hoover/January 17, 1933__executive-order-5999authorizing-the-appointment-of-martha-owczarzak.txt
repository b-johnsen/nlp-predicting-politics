Mrs. Martha Owczarzak of Bay City, Michigan, may be appointed a charwoman in the Custodian Service, Post Office, Bay City, Michigan, without regard to her standing on the Civil Service register for classified laborer, (female).

Mrs. Owczarzak is the wife of Frank Owczarzak who has had both of his legs amputated. They have four children dependent upon them for support.

This Order is recommended by the Secretary of the Treasury.
