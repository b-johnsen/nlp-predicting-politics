Under authority contained in the act of June 25, 1910 (36 Stat. 847), as amended by the act of August 24, 1912 (37 Stat. 497), and subject to the conditions, provisions, and limitations of said acts, it is hereby ordered that the public lands in Tps. 41, 42, 43, and 44 N., Rs. 83 and 84 W. of the sixth principal meridian, Wyoming, be, and the same are hereby, withdrawn from settlement, location, sale, entry, and all forms of appropriation, pending a resurvey of said townships under the act of May 29, 1908 (35 Stat. 465).

This order shall continue in full force and effect unless and until revoked by the President, or by an act of Congress.
