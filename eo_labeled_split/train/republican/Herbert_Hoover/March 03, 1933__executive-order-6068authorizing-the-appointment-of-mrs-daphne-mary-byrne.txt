Mrs. Daphne Mary Byrne may be appointed as clerk-typist at $1,260 per annum in the classified service under the Veterans' Administration. This order is issued upon the recommendation of the Administrator of Veterans' Affairs. He states that Mrs. Byrne is the widow of Joseph V. Byrne, an employee of the Veterans' Administration who died December 30, 1932. He also states that Mr. Byrne enlisted August 3, 1917, and was discharged September 30, 1919, that he served overseas and suffered from a service-connected disability, resulting in his death.
