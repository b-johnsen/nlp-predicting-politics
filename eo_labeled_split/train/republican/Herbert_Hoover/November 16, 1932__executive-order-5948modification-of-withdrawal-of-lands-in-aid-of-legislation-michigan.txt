Pursuant to the authority conferred upon me by the act of Congress approved June 25, 1910 (36 Stat. 847-848), as amended by the act of Congress approved August 24, 1912 (37 Stat. 497), Executive Order No. 4693, of July 18, 1927, withdrawing SW. ¼ SW. ¼ sec. 20, T. 49 N., R. 34 W., Michigan meridian, Michigan, from all forms of appropriation, is hereby modified, for the purpose of permitting the State of Michigan to select the land under the provisions of the act of July 31, 1912 (37 Stat. 241).
