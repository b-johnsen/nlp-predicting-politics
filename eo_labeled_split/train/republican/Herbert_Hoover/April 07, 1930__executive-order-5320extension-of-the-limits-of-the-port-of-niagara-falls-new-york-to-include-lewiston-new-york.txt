By virtue of the authority vested in me by the act of Congress approved August 1, 1914, making appropriations for the sundry civil expenses of the Government for the fiscal year ended June 30, 1915, the limits of the port of Niagara Falls, N.Y., are hereby extended to include, in addition to Niagara Falls, Lewiston, N.Y., in Customs Collection District No. 9 (Buffalo) with headquarters at Buffalo, N.Y., effective thirty days from the date of this order.
