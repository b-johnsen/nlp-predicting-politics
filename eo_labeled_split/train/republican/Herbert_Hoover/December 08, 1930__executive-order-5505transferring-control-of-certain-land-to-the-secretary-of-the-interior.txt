Mississippi

It is hereby ordered that the land included in Dog Island (unsurveyed) in the Mississippi Sound, south of Biloxi, Miss., approximately in T. 9 S., R. 8 W., St. Stephens meridian, Mississippi, which was reserved for military purposes by Executive order of August 30, 1847, be, and it is hereby, placed under the control of the Secretary of the Interior for disposition as provided by section 1 of the act of July 5, 1884 (23 Stat. 103), or as may be otherwise provided by law.
