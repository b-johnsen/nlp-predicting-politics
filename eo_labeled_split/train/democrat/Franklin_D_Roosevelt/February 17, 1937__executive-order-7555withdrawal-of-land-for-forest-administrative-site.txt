Section 1. Executive Order No. 6910 of November 26, 1934, as amended, temporarily withdrawing all public lands in certain States for classification and other purposes, is hereby revoked as to the following-described tract of public land in California:

MOUNT DIABLO MERIDIAN

T. 29 N., R. 2 E., sec. 6, lot 18, 36.20 acres.

Section 2. Subject to valid existing rights, the tract of land described in section 1 of this order is hereby temporarily withdrawn from settlement, location, sale or entry, and reserved for use by the Forest Service of the Department of Agriculture as an administrative site in connection with the administration of the Lassen National Forest.

Section 3. The withdrawal made by section 2 of this order shall remain in force until revoked by the President or by act of Congress.
