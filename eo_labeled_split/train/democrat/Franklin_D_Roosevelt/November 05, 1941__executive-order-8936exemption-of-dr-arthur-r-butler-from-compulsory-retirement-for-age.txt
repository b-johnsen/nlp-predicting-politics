Whereas

, in my judgment, the public interest requires that Dr. Arthur R. Butler, senior medical officer, United States Civil Service Commission, who, during the month of March, 1942, will reach the retirement age prescribed for automatic separation from the service, applicable to him, be exempted from compulsory retirement for age for a period of one year:

Now, Therefore

, by virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by section 204 of the act of June 30, 1932, 47 Stat. 382, 404 (U.S.C., title 5, sec. 715a), I hereby exempt the said Dr. Arthur R. Butler from compulsory retirement for age for a period of one year ending March 31, 1943.
