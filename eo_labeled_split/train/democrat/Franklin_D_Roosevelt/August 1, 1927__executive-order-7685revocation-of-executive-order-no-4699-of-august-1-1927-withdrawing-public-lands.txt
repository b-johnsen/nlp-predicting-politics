CALIFORNIA

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of June 25, 1910, ch. 421, 36 Stat. 847, as amended by the act of August 24, 1912, ch. 369, 37 Stat. 497, Executive Order No. 4699 of August 1, 1927, withdrawing public lands in California pending resurvey, is hereby revoked.

This order shall become effective upon the date of the official filing of the plat of the resurvey of the said lands.
