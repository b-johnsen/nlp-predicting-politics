By virtue of the authority vested in me by the Act of June 12, 1934, entitled "An Act to amend the Tariff Act of 1930" (48 Stat. 943), as amended, the Committee for Reciprocity Information, created by Executive Order No. 6750 of June 27, 1934, is hereby placed under the jurisdiction and control of the Department of State, its functions to be exercised under the direction and supervision of the Secretary of State, who shall designate from the membership of the Committee the Chairman thereof.

The Executive Committee on Commercial Policy, created by Executive Letter of November 11, 1933, and continued by Executive Orders No. 6656 of March 27, 1934, and No. 7260 of December 31, 1935, shall continue to exercise its function of selecting certain members of' the said Committee for Reciprocity Information.

This order shall become effective on July 1, 1939.
