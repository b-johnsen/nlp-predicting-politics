MONTANA

By virtue of the authority vested in me by the act of June 25, 1910, c. 421, 36 Stat. 847, Executive Order No. 4411 of April 1, 1926, temporarily withdrawing certain public lands in Montana for classification and in aid of legislation, is hereby revoked so far as it affects the following-described land:

Principal Meridian

T. 15 N., R. 6 W., sec. 7, NW¼SE¼, 40 acres.
