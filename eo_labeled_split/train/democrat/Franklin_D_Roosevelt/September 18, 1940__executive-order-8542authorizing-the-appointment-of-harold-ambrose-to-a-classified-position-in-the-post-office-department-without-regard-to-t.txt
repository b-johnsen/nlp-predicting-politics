By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the provisions of paragraph Eighth of subdivision SECOND of section 2 of the Civil Service Act (22 Stat. 403, 404), it is hereby ordered that Harold Ambrose, who, since June 12, 1933, has been serving in a position in the Post Office Department excepted from civil service requirements, may be appointed to a classified position in the Post Office Department without compliance with the requirements of the Civil Service Rules.
