WYOMING

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of June 25, 1910, ch. 421, 36 Stat. 847, as amended by the act of August 24, 1912, ch. 369, 37 Stat. 497, Executive Order No. 6795 of July 26, 1934, withdrawing, together with other lands, the public lands in the following-described township in Wyoming, pending resurvey, is hereby revoked as to said township:

SIXTH PRINCIPAL MERIDIAN

T. 16 N., R. 98 W.

This order shall become effective upon the date of the official filing of the plat of resurvey of said township.
