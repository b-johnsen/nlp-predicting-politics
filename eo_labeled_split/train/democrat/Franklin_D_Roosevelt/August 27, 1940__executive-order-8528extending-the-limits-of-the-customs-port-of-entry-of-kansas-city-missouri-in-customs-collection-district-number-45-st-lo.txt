By virtue of the authority vested in me by section 1 of the act of August 1, 1914. 38 Stat. 609, 623 (U.S.C., title 19, sec. 2), it is ordered that the limits of the customs port of entry of Kansas City, Missouri, in Customs Collection District Number 45 (St. Louis), be, and they are hereby, extended to include the territory embracing the municipalities of Kansas City, Kansas, and North Kansas City, Missouri.

This order shall become effective thirty days from the date hereof.
