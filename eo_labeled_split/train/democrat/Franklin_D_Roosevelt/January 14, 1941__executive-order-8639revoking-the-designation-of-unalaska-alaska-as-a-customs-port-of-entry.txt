By virtue of the authority vested in me by section 1 of the Act of August 1, 1914, 38 Stat. 609, 623 (U.S.C. title 19, sec. 2), it is ordered that the designation of Unalaska, Alaska, as a customs port of entry in Customs Collection District No. 31 (Alaska), be, and it is hereby, revoked.

This order shall become effective at the close of business January 15, 1941.
