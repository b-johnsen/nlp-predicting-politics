 . 2. The commission is authorized and directed to investigate and inquire into the issues raised by the aforementioned proposals as set forth in the notices incorporated in the joint recommendation for settlement signed and accepted on  , by the parties involved in the aforementioned controversy, with the objective of making a report to the President, including its findings and recommendations with respect to the controversy, and assisting in achieving an amicable settlement and agreement with respect to issues in dispute between the parties.
