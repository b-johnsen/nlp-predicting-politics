Executive Order
 
Grover Cleveland

The executive offices and   at the seat of Government, including the public printing establishment, will be closed at noon on  ,   instant, to enable persons employed therein to attend the exercises at the unveiling of the statue of the late president  .

And employees in such offices and   who desire to accompany any organization to which they belong in the parade or other exercises preceding on   the unveiling ceremonies may, by permission of the heads of their respective offices or  , also be granted such leave of absence as may be necessary for that purpose.

Members of   desiring to attend any meeting of such society on  , the 11th instant, may, by special permission of the respective heads of   and offices, be excused from duty during the hours on   as said meetings may be held.

GROVER CLEVELAND
