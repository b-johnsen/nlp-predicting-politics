Executive Order  —Suspending a Provision of Section 5751(b) of Title 10, United States Code, Which Relates to Officers of   in   Colonel
 
 

By virtue of the authority vested in me by section 5785 (b) of title 10 of the United States Code, I hereby suspend the provision of section 5751 (b) of title 10 of the United States Code which relates to the service-in-grade requirement for officers of   in the grade of lieutenant colonel for eligibility for consideration by a selection board for promotion to the next higher grade.

 

 

 

NOTE: Executive Order   was not made public in the form of a   press release.
