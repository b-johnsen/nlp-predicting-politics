Executive Order 7872—Amendment of Subdivision I, Schedule B, Civil Service Rules
 
 

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the provisions of paragraph  , Subdivision SECOND, section 2 of the Civil Service Act (22 Stat. 403, 404), it is ordered that     be, and it is hereby, amended by adding thereto the following paragraph:

"8. Such administrative or custodial positions in the field service of   relating to the management or maintenance of Federal low-rent housing projects, which in the opinion of   cannot be filled satisfactorily through open competitive examination:

Provided

, that no positions shall be filled under this paragraph unless it is clearly demonstrated that the best interests of the service will be served thereby."
