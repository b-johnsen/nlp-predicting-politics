By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by   of 1935 (Public Res. 11, 74th  .; 49 Stat. 115), the first paragraph of part I of regulation no. 1, prescribed in Executive Order No.  , of  , is hereby amended by adding thereto the following:
