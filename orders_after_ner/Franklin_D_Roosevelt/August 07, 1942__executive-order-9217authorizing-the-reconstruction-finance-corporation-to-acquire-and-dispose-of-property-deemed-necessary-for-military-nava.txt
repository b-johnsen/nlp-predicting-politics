Executive Order 9217—Authorizing   To Acquire and Dispose of Property Deemed Necessary for Military,  , or Other War Purposes
 
 

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by Title II of the Second War Powers Act,  , approved   (Public Law 507, 77th  ),   is hereby authorized to exercise the authority contained in the said Title II of the Second War Powers Act,  , to acquire, use, and dispose of any real property, temporary use thereof, or other interest therein, together with any personal property located thereon, or used therewith, that the   shall deem necessary for military, naval or other war purposes.
