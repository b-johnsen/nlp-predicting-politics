Executive Order  —Partial Revocation of Executive Order No. 6119 of  ,  
 
 

 

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of  , ch. 421, 36 Stat. 847, as amended by the act of  , ch. 369, 37 Stat. 497, Executive Order No. 6119 of  , withdrawing, together with other lands, public lands in the therein-described subdivisions in T. 23 N.,   9 E., T. 22 N.,   10 E., and T. 21 N.,   11 E. of the San Bernardino meridian,  , pending a resurvey, is hereby revoked as to said townships.

This order shall become effective upon the date of the official filing of the plats of resurvey of said townships.
