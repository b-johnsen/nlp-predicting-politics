Executive Order 9456—Suspending Certain Statutory Provisions Relating to Employment in the Canal Zone
 
 

By virtue of the authority vested in me by section 2 of the War Department Civil Appropriation Act,   (Public Law. 352, 78th  ), section 108 of the Naval Appropriation Act,   (Public Law 347, 78th  ), and section 7 of the Military Appropriaton Act,   (Public Law 374, 78th  ), relating to certain kinds of employment in the Canal Zone, and deeming such course to be in the public interest, I hereby suspend, from and including the effective date of the said Acts, compliance with the provisions of the said sections during the continuance of any of the wars in which   is now engaged.
