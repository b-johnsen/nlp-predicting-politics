Executive Order 7165—Preparation and Publicity of Written Decisions in Respect of Overassessments of Income, Profits, Estate, and   in Excess of $20,000
 
 

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by section 257(a) of title II of the Revenue Act of   (ch. 27, 44 Stat. 9,  ); section 55 of title I of the Revenue Act of   (ch. 852, 45 Stat. 791, 809); section 55 of title I of the Revenue Act of   (ch. 209,  . 169, 189), as amended by section 218(h) of   (ch. 90, 48 Stat. 195, 209); and section 55(a) of title I of the Revenue Act of   (ch. 277, 48 Stat. 680, 698), it is hereby ordered that written decisions in respect of overassessments of income, profits, estate, and gift taxes allowed in excess of $20,000 shall be prepared and shall be open to public inspection in accordance with the regulations prescribed by the Secretary of the   amending paragraph 16 of   Decision  , as amended, and approved by me this date.
