Executive Order  —Revocation in Part of Executive Order No.  , of  ,  
 
 

WYOMING

By virtue of the authority vested in me by the act of   (ch. 421, 36 Stat. 847), as amended by the act of   (ch. 369, 37 Stat. 497), it is ordered that Executive Order No.  , dated  , withdrawing, with other lands, public lands in  . 51 N., Rs. 60 and 61 W. of the sixth principal meridian,  , pending resurvey, be, and it is hereby, revoked as to said townships.

It is further ordered, pursuant to Public Resolution 85, approved   (ch. 471, 46 Stat. 580), that, subject to valid rights and the provisions of existing withdrawals, the public lands in said townships shall be opened only to entry under the homestead or desert-land laws by qualified ex-service men for whose service recognition is granted by said resolution, under the terms and conditions of said resolution and the regulations issued thereunder, for a period of   beginning with the date of the filing of the plats of resurvey of said townships, and thereafter to appropriation by the general public under any public-land law applicable thereto.

Subsequent to the date hereof and prior to the date of restoration to general disposition as herein provided, no right may be acquired to the released land by settlement in advance of entry or otherwise except strictly in accordance with the provisions of this order.
