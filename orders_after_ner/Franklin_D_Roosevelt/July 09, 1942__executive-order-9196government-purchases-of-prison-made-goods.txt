Executive  —Government Purchases of Prison-Made Goods
 
 

Under and by virtue of the authority vested in me as President of  , and in order to remove any doubts which might otherwise exist and to insure the effective utilization of all existing productive facilities, it is hereby ordered that Executive Order No. 325A of  , be, and the same is hereby, suspended for the period of the war and for   thereafter to the extent necessary to permit officers and agencies of   charged with the purchase or procurement of articles necessary in the conduct of the war to procure, directly or indirectly, through any contractor or subcontractor or otherwise, articles of any kind produced in any   or territorial prison, provided such articles are not produced pursuant to any contract or other arrangement under which prison labor is hired out to, or employed or used by, any private person, firm or corporation.
