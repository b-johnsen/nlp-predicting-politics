Executive Order 6900—Amending Code of Fair Competition for the Wine Industry
 
 

Whereas

  has submitted for my approval certain amendments to the Code of Fair Competition for the Wine Industry and has rendered its report and recommendations and findings thereon; and

Whereas

due notice of public hearings on said proposed amendments has been given and public hearings thereon have been held pursuant to such notice:

Now, Therefore

, by virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by title I of   of   (ch. 90, 48 Stat. 195), and in order to effectuate the purposes of said title, I hereby approve said amendments and modify my previous approval of the Code of Fair Competition for the Wine Industry to include an approval of said code in its entirety as hereby amended.
