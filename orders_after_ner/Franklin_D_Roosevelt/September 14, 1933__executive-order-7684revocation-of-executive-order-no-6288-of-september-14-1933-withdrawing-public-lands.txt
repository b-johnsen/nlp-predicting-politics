Executive Order  —Revocation of Executive Order No. 6288 of September 14,  ,  
 
 

WYOMING

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of  , ch. 421, 36 Stat. 847, as amended by the act of  , ch. 369, 37 Stat. 497, Executive Order No. 6288 of September 14,  , withdrawing public lands in   pending resurvey, and heretofore partially revoked, is hereby revoked as to the remainder of the lands involved.

This order shall become effective upon the date of the official filing of the plat of the resurvey of the said remaining lands.
