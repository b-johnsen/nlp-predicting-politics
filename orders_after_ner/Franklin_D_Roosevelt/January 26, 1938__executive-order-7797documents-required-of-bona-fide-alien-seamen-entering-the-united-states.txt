Masters of maritime vessels (except government vessels and such other vessels as the Secretary of  , in his discretion, may indicate) of all nationalities sailing for a port of   must submit for visa a list of all the alien members of the vessel's crew to the   consular officer at the port from which the vessel commences its voyage.
