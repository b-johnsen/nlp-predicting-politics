Executive Order  —  with the Director of  
 

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me under title II of the act of   (ch. 18,   Stat. 20), it is hereby ordered as follows:

(1) Each executive department, independent establishment, and emergency agency shall file with the Director of   a functional organization chart, indicating its various existing bureaus, divisions, sections, etc., and containing a description of the functions respectively performed, and shall file such additional charts, from time to time, as may be necessary to show all changes made therein.

(2) Every executive department, independent establishment, and emergency agency hereafter created shall within   after the appointment of the head thereof file a preliminary functional organization chart with the Director of  .

(3) The Director of   is hereby authorized to prescribe, subject to the approval of the President, such rules and regulations as will indicate the information desired and the form of chart to be furnished.
