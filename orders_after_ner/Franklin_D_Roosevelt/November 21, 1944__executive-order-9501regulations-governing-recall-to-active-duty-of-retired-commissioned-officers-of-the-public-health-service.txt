By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by section 211 (d) of  , approved   (58 Stat. 682), I hereby prescribe the following regulations governing the recall to active duty of retired commissioned officers of  :

3. For the purposes of this order, the term \"retired officers\" shall include officers placed on permanent \"waiting orders.\"
