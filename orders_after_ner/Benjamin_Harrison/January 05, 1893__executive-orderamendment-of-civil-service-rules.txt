Executive Order—Amendment of Civil Service Rules
 
 

Section 2 of Postal Rule I is hereby amended so as to read as follows:

The classification of the postal service made by   under section 6 of the act of  , is hereby extended to all free-delivery post-offices; and hereafter whenever any post-office becomes a free-delivery office the said classification or any then existing classification made by   under said section and act shall apply thereto; and   shall provide examinations to test the fitness of persons to fill vacancies in all free-delivery post-offices, and these rules shall be in force therein; but this shall not include any post-office made an experimental free-delivery office under the authority contained in the appropriation act of  . Every revision of the classification of any post-office under section 6 of the act of  , and every inclusion of a post-office within the classified postal service shall be reported to the President.
