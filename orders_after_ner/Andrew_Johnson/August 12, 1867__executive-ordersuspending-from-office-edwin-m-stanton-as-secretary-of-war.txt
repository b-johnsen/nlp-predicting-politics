Executive Order—Suspending from Office   as Secretary of War
 
 .  ,

Secretary of War

.

Sir: By virtue of the power and authority vested in me as President by the Constitution and laws of  , you are hereby suspended from office as Secretary of War, and will cease to exercise any and all functions pertaining to the same.

You will at once transfer to General  , who has   been authorized and empowered to act as Secretary of War ad interim, all records, books, and other property now in your custody and charge.
