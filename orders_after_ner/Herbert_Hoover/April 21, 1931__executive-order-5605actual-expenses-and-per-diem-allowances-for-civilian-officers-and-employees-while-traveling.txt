Executive Order 5605—Actual Expenses and Per Diem Allowances for Civilian Officers and Employees While Traveling
 
 

Executive Order No.  , issued  , prescribing regulations for the allowance and payment of actual expenses and the fixing and payment of per diem allowances for civilian officers and employees while traveling on official business and away from their designated posts of duty, is hereby revoked, effective  .
