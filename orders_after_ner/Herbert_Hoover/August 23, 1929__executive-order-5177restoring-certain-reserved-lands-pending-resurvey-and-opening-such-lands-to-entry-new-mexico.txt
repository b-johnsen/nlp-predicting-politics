Executive Order 5177—Restoring Certain Reserved Lands Pending Resurvey and Opening Such Lands to Entry,  
 
 

It is hereby ordered that Executive Order No.   dated  , withdrawing the public lands in T. 17 N.,   10 E.,  ,  , pending a resurvey, be and the same is hereby revoked.

And it is hereby ordered, pursuant to Public Resolution No. 29 of   (41 Stat. 434), as amended   and   (42 Stat.  ), that the above described lands, if otherwise subject to disposition, subject to valid rights, shall be opened to entry only by qualified ex-service men of the World War under the homestead or desert land laws under the terms and conditions of such resolutions and regulations issued thereunder, for a period of   beginning with the official filing of the plat of resurvey, and thereafter to appropriation by the general public under any public land law applicable thereto.

Subsequent to the date hereof, and prior to the date of restoration to general disposition as herein provided, no right may be acquired to the released lands by settlement in advance of entry, or otherwise, except strictly in accordance herewith.
