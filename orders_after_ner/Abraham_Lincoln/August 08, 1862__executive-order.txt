Executive Order
 
 

WAR DEPARTMENT,

By direction of the President of  , it is hereby ordered that until further order no citizen liable to be drafted into the militia shall be allowed to go to a foreign country. And all marshals, deputy marshals, and military officers of   are directed, and all police authorities, especially at the ports of   on the seaboard and on the frontier, are requested, to see that this order is faithfully carried into effect. And they are hereby authorized and directed to arrest and detain any person or persons about to depart from   in violation of this order, and report to Major  , judge-advocate at  , for further instructions respecting the person or persons so arrested or detained.

II. Any person liable to draft who shall absent himself from his county or   before such draft is made will be arrested by any provost-marshal or other   or   officer, wherever he may be found within the jurisdiction of  , and be conveyed to the nearest military post or depot and placed on military duty for the term of the draft; and the expenses of his own arrest and conveyance to such post or depot, and also the sum of $5, as a reward to the officer who shall make such arrest, shall be deducted from his pay.

III. The writ of

habeas corpus

is hereby suspended in respect to all persons so arrested and detained, and in respect to all persons arrested for disloyal practices.

Secretary of War.
