RATES OF PAY AND ALLOWANCES

By the authority vested in me as President by the Constitution and laws of  , and in accord with Section 101 (c) of Public Law 96-86 approved  , it is hereby ordered as follows:

1-101. In order to provide notice of the effect of appropriations limitations on the payable rates of salary and basic pay, additional footnotes are added to each of the pay schedules attached to Executive Order No.   of October 9, 1979.

1-102. In order to preserve existing comparability in new   positions for purposes of applying the recent appropriations limitations, comparable pay levels are designated in an additional footnote to   attached to Executive Order No.  .
