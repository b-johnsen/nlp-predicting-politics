Executive Order 9702—Creating an Emergency Board to Investigate a Dispute Between   and Other Carriers, and Certain of Their Employees
 
 

WHEREAS a dispute exists between   and other carriers, as set forth in the list attached hereto and made a part hereof, and certain of their employees represented by   and  , labor organizations; and

WHEREAS the dispute has not heretofore been adjusted under the provisions of the Railway Labor Act, as amended; and

WHEREAS the dispute, in the judgment of  , threatens substantially to interrupt interstate commerce to a degree such as to deprive the country of essential transportation service:

NOW THEREFORE, by virtue of the authority vested in me by section 10 of the Railway Labor Act, as amended (45 U.S.C. 160), I hereby create a board of three members, to be appointed by me, to investigate said dispute. No member of the said board shall be pecuniarily or otherwise interested in any organization of railway employees or any carrier.

The board shall report its findings to the President with respect to the said dispute within   from the date of this order.

As provided by section 10 of the Railway Labor Act, as amended, from this date and for   after the board has made its report to the President, no change, except by agreement, shall be made by   and the other carriers, or their employees in the condition out of which the said dispute arose.
