Executive Order 9609—Authorizing   and  
 
 

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by Title II of the Second War Powers Act,   (56 Stat. 177) as amended,   is hereby authorized to exercise the authority contained in the said Title II of the Second War Powers Act,  , to acquire, use, and dispose of any real property, temporary use thereof, or other interest therein, together with any personal property located thereon, or used therewith, which   shall deem necessary for military, naval, or other war purposes.

HARRY S. TRUMAN
