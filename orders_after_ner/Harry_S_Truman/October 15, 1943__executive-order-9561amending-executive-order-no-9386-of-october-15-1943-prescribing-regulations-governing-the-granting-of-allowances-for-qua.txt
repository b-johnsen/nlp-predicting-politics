Executive Order  —Amending Executive Order No.   of  , Prescribing Regulations Governing the Granting of Allowances for Quarters and   to Enlisted Men
 
 

By virtue of the authority vested in me by section 10 of the act of  , as amended, 56 Stat. 363 (37 U.S.C.  . 110), it is ordered as follows:

1. Paragraph "A. General" and Exception No. 1 of paragraph "B. Special" of Section I of Executive Order No.  , dated  , prescribing allowances for quarters and subsistence to enlisted men not furnished quarters or rations in kind, are hereby amended to provide that the   subsistence allowance therein authorized where no Government messing facilities are furnished shall be $2.25 instead of $1.80.

2. This order shall be effective from  , until  , unless sooner modified or revoked.

HARRY S. TRUMAN
