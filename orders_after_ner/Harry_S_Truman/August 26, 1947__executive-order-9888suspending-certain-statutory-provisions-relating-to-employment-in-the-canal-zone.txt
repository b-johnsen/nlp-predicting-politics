Executive Order 9888—Suspending Certain Statutory Provisions Relating to Employment in the Canal Zone
 
 

By virtue of the authority vested in me by section 2 of the War Department Civil Appropriation Act,   (Public Law 296, 80th  ), section 104 of  ,   (Public Law 202, 80th  ), and section 5 of the Military Appropriation Act,   (Public Law 267, 80th  ), relating to certain kinds of employment in the Canal Zone, and deeming such course to be in the public interest, I hereby suspend, from and including the effective dates of the said Acts, compliance with the provisions of the said sections during the continuance of any of the wars in which   is now engaged or of the present national emergency.

Findings such action necessary because of a shortage of housing, I hereby suspend, for  , the application of those portions of the cited sections of the respective Acts which require the employment of citizens of   or of   in skilled, technical, clerical, administrative executive, or supervisory positions.
