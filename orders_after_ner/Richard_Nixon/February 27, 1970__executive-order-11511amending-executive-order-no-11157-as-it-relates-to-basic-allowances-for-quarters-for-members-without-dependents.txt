Executive Order  —Amending Executive Order No.   as it Relates to Basic Allowances for Quarters for Members Without Dependents
 
 

By virtue of the authority vested in me by section 403 (g) of title  , United States Code, and as President of   and Commander in Chief of the armed forces of  , Executive Order No.  , as amended, is further amended by deleting the words "at his permanent station" in clause (b) of section 403 thereof.

RICHARD NIXON

 

 

NOTE: Executive Order   was not issued in the form of a   press release.
