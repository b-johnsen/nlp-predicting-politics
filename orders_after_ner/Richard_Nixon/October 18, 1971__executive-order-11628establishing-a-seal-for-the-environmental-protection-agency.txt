Executive Order  —Establishing a Seal for  
 
 

The Administrator of   has caused to be made, and has recommended that I approve, a seal for  , the design of which accompanies and is hereby made a part of this order, and which is described as follows:

A flower with a bloom which is symbolic of all the elements of the environment. The bloom is a sphere, the component parts of which represent the blue sky, green earth, blue-green water. A white circle within the sphere denotes either the sun or the moon. All are symbolic of a clean environment and are superimposed on a disc with a white background, circled by the title "  PROTECTION  " in blue letters.

It appears that such seal is of suitable design and appropriate for adoption as the official seal of  :

Now, THEREFORE, by virtue of the authority vested in me as President of  , I hereby approve such seal as the official seal of  .

RICHARD NIXON
