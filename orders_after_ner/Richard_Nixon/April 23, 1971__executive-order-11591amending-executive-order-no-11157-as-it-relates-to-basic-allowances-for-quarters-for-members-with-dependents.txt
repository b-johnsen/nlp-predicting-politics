Executive Order  —Amending Executive Order No.   as it Relates to Basic Allowances for Quarters for Members with Dependents
 
 

By virtue of the authority vested in me by section 403 (g) of title  , United States Code, and as President of   and Commander in Chief of  , section 403 (ii) of Executive Order No.  , is amended by inserting the words "duty or" before the word "leave".

RICHARD NIXON

 

 

NOTE: The Executive order was not issued in the form of a   press release.
