Executive Order 11549—Revoking Executive Order No.  , Establishing the Whittier Defensive Sea Area,  
 
 

By virtue of the authority vested in me by Section 2152 of Title 18, United States Code, and as President of  , Executive Order No.  , establishing the Whittier Defensive Sea Area,  , is hereby revoked.

RICHARD NIXON

 

 

NOTE: Executive Order   was not issued in the form of a   press release.
