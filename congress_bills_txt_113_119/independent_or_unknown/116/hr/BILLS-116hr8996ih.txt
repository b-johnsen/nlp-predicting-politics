1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Jones Act Repeal Act
.
2.
Repeal of provisions popularly known as the Jones Act
Title 46, United States Code, is amended by repealing the following provisions:
(1)
Chapter 551, and the item relating to that chapter in the analysis at the beginning of part D of subtitle V of that title.
(2)
Section 12102, and the item relating to that section in the analysis at the beginning of chapter 121 of that title.
