119th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 165
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
April 9, 2025
Mr. Sanders
submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the
Committee on Veterans' Affairs
RESOLUTION
Expressing the sense of the Senate that the plan of President Trump and Elon Musk to fire 83,000 employees of the Department of Veterans Affairs is unacceptable and must be rescinded.
Whereas, at the direction of President Trump and Elon Musk, the Department of Veterans Affairs is preparing a plan to fire up to 83,000 employees who provide care and benefits to our veterans: Now, therefore, be it
That it is the sense of the Senate that the Department of Veterans Affairs must immediately reject and rescind its Agency Reduction in Force and Reorganization Plan.
