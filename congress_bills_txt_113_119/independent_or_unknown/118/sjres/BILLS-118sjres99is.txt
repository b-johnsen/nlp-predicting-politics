JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Council on Environmental Quality relating to
National Environmental Policy Act Implementing Regulations Revisions Phase 2
.

National Environmental Policy Act Implementing Regulations Revisions Phase 2
(89 Fed. Reg. 35442 (May 1, 2024)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
