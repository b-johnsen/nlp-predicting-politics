CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
Directing the President, pursuant to section 5(c) of the War Powers Resolution, to remove United
States Armed Forces deployed to Iraq or Syria on or after August 7, 2014,
other than Armed Forces required to protect United States diplomatic
facilities and personnel, from Iraq and Syria.

Pursuant to section 5(c) of the War Powers Resolution (
50 U.S.C. 1544(c)
), Congress directs the President to remove United States Armed Forces deployed to Iraq or Syria on or after August 7, 2014, other than Armed Forces required to protect United States diplomatic facilities and personnel, from Iraq and Syriaâ

by no later than the end of the period of 30 days beginning on the day on which this concurrent resolution is adopted; or
