1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Federal Employee Gender Diversity Investment Act
.
2.
Findings
Congress finds the following:
(1)
A proven track record of taking advantage of the talents, ideas, and contributions of women is an excellent indicator of companies that are primed for long-term growth and sustainability.
(2)
A report reviewing 2,360 global companies found that those with women directors outperformed those without in return on equity.
(3)
Companies with greater numbers of women in senior management outperformed those without women directors in return on equity and scored higher when being evaluated on organization, leadership, accountability and innovation.
(4)
Investing in women and girls is one of the most important strategies for alleviating poverty and promoting sustainable development around the globe.
