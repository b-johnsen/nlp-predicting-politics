1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Safe Bridges Investment Act of 2015
.
2.
Eligible projects
Section 119(d)(2) of title 23, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
(Q)
Construction, reconstruction, resurfacing, restoration, rehabilitation, and preservation of, or operational improvements for, a bridge not on the National Highway System if such bridge is on the Federal-aid Highway System
.
