1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Rail Crossings Safety Improvement Act
.
2.
Amendment
Section 20154(i) of title 49, United States Code, is amended by striking
$350,000,000 for each of the fiscal years 2006 through 2009
and inserting
$100,000,000 for each of the fiscal years 2016 through 2019
.
