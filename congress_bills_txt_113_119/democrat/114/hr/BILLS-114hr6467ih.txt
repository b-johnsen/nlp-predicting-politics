1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Use It or Choose It Act of 2016
.
2.
Election to retain work benefits
(a)
In general
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, an individual, when moving from one position within the workforce to another, may elect to retain any benefits earned when occupying such position.
(b)
Application
Contractual providers of such benefits shall extend the provisions of such benefits or the same terms and conditions as those provided to employees in the former position, or those similarly situated. Employers accruing funds or reserves corresponding to leave or other benefits shall transfer such funds or reserves to new employers upon request.
