1.
Major Gregory E. Barney Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 6691 Church Street in Riverdale, Georgia, shall be known and designated as the
Major Gregory E. Barney Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Major Gregory E. Barney Post Office Building
.
