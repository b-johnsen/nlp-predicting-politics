1.
EXTENSION OF MORTGAGE INSURANCE PREMIUMS TREATED AS QUALIFIED RESIDENCE INTEREST
(a)
In General
Subclause (I) of
section 163(h)(3)(E)(iv)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking
December 31, 2014
and inserting
December 31, 2015
.
(b)
Effective Date
The amendments made by this section shall apply to amounts paid or accrued after December 31, 2014.
