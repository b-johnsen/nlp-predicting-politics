Section 487(a) of the Higher Education Act of 1965 (
20 U.S.C. 1094(a)
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(30)
In the case of an institution that maintains student housing facilities (as defined in section 485(i)(8)), the institution willâ
(A)
include in all materials (including electronic materials) relating to such facilities, information that describes whether such facilities are equipped with a student housing facility fire safety system, which includes an automatic fire sprinkler system in each bedroom;
(B)
notify each student selecting such a facility during a student housing facility selection process, in writing and during such selection process, whether the facility is equipped with a student housing facility fire safety system, which includes an automatic fire sprinkler system in each bedroom; and
(C)
notify each student who plans to live in such a facility, in writing and on the date that the student moves into the facility, whether the facility is equipped with a student housing facility fire safety system, which includes an automatic fire sprinkler system in each bedroom, and how such system is described in subparagraph (B) of section 485(i)(1) for purposes of the fire safety report required under such section.
