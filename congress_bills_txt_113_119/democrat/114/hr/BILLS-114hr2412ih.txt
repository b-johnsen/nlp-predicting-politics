1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
New Energy for America Act
.
2.
Extension of residential energy efficient property
(a)
In general
Subsection (g) of
section 25D
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking
December 31, 2016
and inserting
December 31, 2021
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by this section shall take effect on the date of the enactment of this Act.
