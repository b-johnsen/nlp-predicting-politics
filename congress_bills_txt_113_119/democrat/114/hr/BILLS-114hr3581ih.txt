2.
Findings
The Congress finds as follows:
(1)
Children are our Nationâs future and greatest treasure.
(2)
A high-quality education is the surest way for every child to reach his or her full potential.
(3)
Title I of the Elementary and Secondary Schools Act helps address inequity in education in school districts across the country to provide a high-quality education to every student.
(4)
The Individuals with Disabilities Education Act guarantees all children with disabilities a first-rate education.
(5)
The Individuals with Disabilities Education Improvement Act committed Congress to providing 40 percent of the national current average per-pupil expenditure for students with disabilities.
(6)
A promise made must be a promise kept.
