2.
Purchase paper United States savings bonds with tax refund
Section 3106 of title 31, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
(d)
(1)
During the period ending on December 31, 2020, the Secretary shall provide an option on individual returns of tax under subtitle A of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to purchase United States savings bonds in paper form with a portion or all of a refund of overpayment of such tax for the purchaser or anyone.
