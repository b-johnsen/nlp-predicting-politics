1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Fair COLA for Seniors Act of 2016
.
2.
One-time cost-of-living (COLA) increase for calendar year 2016
(a)
3.9% COLA provided To match recent growth in CEO pay
Effective with the month of June 2016, the Commissioner of Social Security shall increase each of the amounts described in subclauses (I) through (III) of section 215(i)(2)(A)(ii) of the Social Security Act by 3.9 percent.
(b)
No effect on annual cost-of-Living increases
For purposes of applying subparagraphs (A) and (B) of section 215(i)(1) of the Social Security Act in calendar years 2016 and 2017, the increase under subsection (a) shall not be considered a general benefit increase under title II of the Social Security Act.
