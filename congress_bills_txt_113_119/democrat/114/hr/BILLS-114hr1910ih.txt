1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Put a Woman on the Twenty Act of 2015
.
2.
Selection of a portrait for the $20 Federal Reserve note
The Secretary of the Treasury shall make such arrangements as are necessary to convene a panel of United States citizens to solicit recommendations from the public for a portrait of a woman to be used in a redesign of the $20 Federal Reserve note. The panel shall submit a recommendation to the Secretary of the Treasury on a date determined by the Secretary.
