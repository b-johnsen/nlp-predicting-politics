1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Carbon Capture Research and Development Act
.
2.
Fossil energy
Section 961(a) of the Energy Policy Act of 2005 (
42 U.S.C. 16291(a)
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(8)
Improving the conversion, use, and storage of carbon dioxide produced from fossil fuels.
.
