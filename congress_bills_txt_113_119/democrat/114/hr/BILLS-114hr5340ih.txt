1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Funding for Aviation Screeners and Threat Elimination Restoration Act
or the
FASTER Act
.
2.
Repeal of requirement regarding crediting and deposit of a portion of aviation security fees
(a)
Repeal
Subsection (i) of section 44940 of title 49, United States Code, is repealed.
(b)
Conforming amendment
Subsection (d)(4) of such section is amended by striking
, other than subsection (i),
.
