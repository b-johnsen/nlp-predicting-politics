1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Nuclear Worker Residual Contaminations Compensation Act
.
2.
Study on residual contaminations of workers at certain contractor and subsidiary facilities
(a)
Study
The Secretary of Labor shall oversee a study on residual contamination of workers at certain facilities that are members of the Special Exposure Cohort (described in section 3621(14) of the Energy Employees Occupational Illness Compensation Program Act of 2000) owned or operated by contractors of the Department of Energy and subsidiaries of such contractors. Such study shall be conducted by independent advisors and medical experts of each atomic energy employer designated by the Secretary of Energy under section 3621(4)(B) of such Act and Beryllium vendor (as defined in paragraph (6) of such section).
(b)
Report
The Secretary shall transmit to Congress a report on the results of such study not later than 6 months after the date of enactment of this Act.
