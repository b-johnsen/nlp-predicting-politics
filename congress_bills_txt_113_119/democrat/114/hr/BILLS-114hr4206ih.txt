1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
21st Century Power Grid Act
.
2.
Technology demonstration on the distribution system
(a)
In general
The Secretary of Energy shall establish a financial assistance program to carry out eligible projects related to the modernization of the electric grid, including the application of technologies to improve observability, advanced controls, and prediction of system performance on the distribution system and related transmission system inter-dependencies.
