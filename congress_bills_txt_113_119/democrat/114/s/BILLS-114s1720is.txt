1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Harriet Tubman Currency Tribute Act of 2015
.
2.
Likeness of Harriet Tubman required to be included on the face of $10 Federal
reserve notes
The eighth undesignated paragraph of section 16 of the Federal Reserve Act (
12 U.S.C. 418
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
The Secretary of the Treasury shall ensure that the face of all $10 Federal reserve notes printed after December 31, 2020, shall bear the likeness of Harriet Tubman.
.
