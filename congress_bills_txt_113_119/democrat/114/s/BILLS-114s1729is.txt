1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Disaggregating Student Outcomes by Disability Category Act
.
2.
Disaggregating student outcomes by disability category
Section 1111(h)(1)(C)(i) of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965 (
20 U.S.C. 6311(h)(1)(C)(i)
) is amended by inserting
disability category as described in subparagraphs (A)(i) and (if applicable for the State) (B)(i) of section 602(3) of the Individuals with Disabilities Education Act,
after
disability status,
.
