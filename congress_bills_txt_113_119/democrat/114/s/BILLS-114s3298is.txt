2.
Labeling requirement to alert all consumers of opiates that addiction is possible
(a)
Misbranding
Section 502 of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (
21 U.S.C. 352
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(dd)
If it is a drug containing an opiate (as defined in section 102 of the Controlled Substances Act) unless the label prominently states that the drug contains an opiate and addiction is possible.
.
(b)
Delayed applicability
The amendment made by subsection (a) applies beginning on the date that is 180 days after the date of enactment of this Act.
