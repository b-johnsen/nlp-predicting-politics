1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Energy Productivity Innovation Challenge Act of 2015
or the
EPIC Act of 2015
.
2.
Purpose
The purpose of this Act is to assist energy policy innovation in the States to promote the goal of doubling electric and thermal energy productivity by January 1, 2030.
3.
Definitions
In this Act:
(1)
Energy productivity
The term
energy productivity
means, in the case of a State or Indian tribe, the gross State or tribal product per British thermal unit of energy consumed in the State or tribal land of the Indian tribe, respectively.
(2)
Indian
tribe
The term
Indian tribe
has the meaning given the term in section 4 of the Indian Self-Determination and Education Assistance Act (
25 U.S.C. 450b
).
(3)
State
The term
State
has the meaning given the term in section 3 of the Energy Policy and Conservation Act (
42 U.S.C. 6202
).
4.
