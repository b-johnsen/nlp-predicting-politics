2.
Phase-in of new requirements
(a)
In general
Except as provided in subsection (b), the amendments made by section 203 of the
Visa Waiver Program Improvement and Terrorist Travel Prevention Act of 2015
shall take effect on the date that is 180 days after the date of the enactment of such Act.
(b)
Extension
The Secretary of Homeland Security or the Secretary of State may waive the application of section 217(a)(12) of the Immigration and Nationality Act, as added by section 203 of the
Visa Waiver Program Improvement and Terrorist Travel Prevention Act of 2015
, for a period of up to 90 days, if either Secretary determines that such waiver is in the national security interests of the United States.
3.
Sunset
Section 217(a)(12) of the Immigration and Nationality Act (
8 U.S.C. 1187(a)(12)
) shall be repealed on December 18, 2020.
