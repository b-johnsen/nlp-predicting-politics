RESOLUTION
Expressing support for the designation of April 10, 2015, as
Venture Smith芒聙聶s Freedom Day
.
Whereas 250 years ago, in the year 1765, Venture Smith, at the age of 36, purchased his freedom;
Whereas Venture Smith went on to free his family from the bondage of slavery;
Whereas Venture Smith went on to become a successful businessman in the State of Connecticut; and
Whereas, April 10, 2015, would be an appropriate date to designate as
Venture Smith's Freedom Day
: Now, therefore, be it
That the House of Representatives supports the designation of
Venture Smith's Freedom Day
, which would recognize the 250th anniversary of Venture Smith purchasing his freedom and going on to become a successful businessman in Connecticut.
