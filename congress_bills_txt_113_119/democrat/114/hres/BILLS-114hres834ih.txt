RESOLUTION
Recognizing the important role pollinators play in supporting ecosystems and economies.
Whereas bees, birds, butterflies, and other pollinators are vital to sustaining a healthy ecosystem;
Whereas pollinators are responsible for an estimated one out of every three bites of food that we eat and nearly $20,000,000,000 annually to the economy;
Whereas diversity of pollinators is necessary for diversity of plant life and the stability of our food supply;
Whereas several serious stressing factors have led to an alarming decline of pollinators worldwide, especially vital commercial honey bees;
Whereas since 2006, over 10,000,000 hives with a value of roughly $2,000,000,000 have been lost; and
Whereas a continued decline in pollinators would put our food security, economy, and healthy ecosystems at risk: Now, therefore, be it
