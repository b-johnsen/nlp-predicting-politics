section 707

Covered employees: right to representation during examinations

In general
The Secretary shall give to each covered employee an opportunity to be represented by a representative of the choice of, on duty time if applicable, the covered employee at any examination of such covered employee ifâ

(2)

Definitions
In this section:
(1)
The term

means an individual occupying a position at the Department, but does not includeâ
(A)
