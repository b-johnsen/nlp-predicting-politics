1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Stop Schemes, Cyberfraud, Abuse, Manipulation, and Swindles Act
or the
Stop SCAMS Act
.

(c)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)
Agency
The term
agency
has the meaning given that term in section 551 of title 5, United States Code.
(2)
Bureau
The term
Bureau
means the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection.
(3)
Commission
The term
Commission
means the Federal Trade Commission.
(4)
Director
The term
Director
means the Director of the Federal Bureau of Investigation.
