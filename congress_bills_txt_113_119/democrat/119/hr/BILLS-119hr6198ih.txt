2.
Sense of Congress regarding naming of naval vessel for Rear Admiral Alene Duerk
(a)
Sense of Congress
It is the sense of Congress that the Secretary of the Navy should name a vessel of the United States Navy the
U.S.S. Rear Admiral Alene Duerk
in honor of Rear Admiral Alene Duerk, for the acts of valor described in subsection (b).
