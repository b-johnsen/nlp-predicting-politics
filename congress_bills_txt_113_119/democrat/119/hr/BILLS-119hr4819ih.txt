1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Click to Cancel Act of 2025
.
2.
Codification of click-to-cancel rule
(a)
Codification
The rule issued by the Federal Trade Commission on November 15, 2024, relating to the
Negative Option Rule
(89 Fed. Reg. 90476), shall have the force and effect of law.
(b)
Enforcement
(1)
Unfair or deceptive acts or practices
A violation of this section shall be treated as a violation of a regulation under section 18(a)(1)(B) of the Federal Trade Commission Act (
15 U.S.C. 57a(a)(1)(B)
) regarding unfair or deceptive acts or practices.
(2)
Powers of Federal Trade Commission
The Federal Trade Commission shall enforce this section in the same manner, by the same means, and with the same jurisdiction, powers, and duties as though all applicable terms and provisions of the Federal Trade Commission Act (
15 U.S.C. 41 et seq.
) were incorporated into and made a part of this section, and any person who violates this section shall be subject to the penalties and entitled to the privileges and immunities provided in the Federal Trade Commission Act.
