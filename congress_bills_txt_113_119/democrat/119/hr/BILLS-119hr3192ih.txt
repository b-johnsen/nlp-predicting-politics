1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the Reinstating Employee Salaries To Original Rates and Entitlements Act
or the
RESTORE Act
.
2.
Back pay for reinstated employees of the Department of Veterans Affairs
(a)
Back pay
Any individual who was an employee of the Department of Veterans Affairs (including employees who were serving under a probationary or trial period due to a promotion) and who was involuntarily removed and subsequently reinstated or appointed to a position at the Department during the period beginning on January 20, 2025, and ending on the date of the enactment of this Act shall be entitled to back pay in accordance with section 5596 of title 5, United States Code.
(b)
Limitation
Subsection (a) shall not apply to any individual removed from a political position.
