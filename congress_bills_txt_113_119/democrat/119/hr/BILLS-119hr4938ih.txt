Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Facts First Act
.
2.
Limitation on removal of Commissioner of Labor Statistics
Section 2 of the Act of June 13, 1888, entitled
An act to establish a department of Labor
(25 Stat. 182;
29 U.S.C. 3
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
The Commissioner may be removed only with proof of inefficiency, neglect of duty, or malfeasance in office.
.
