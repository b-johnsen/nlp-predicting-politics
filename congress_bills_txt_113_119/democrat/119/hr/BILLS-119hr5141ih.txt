1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Stop the Rate Hikes Act
.
2.
Consideration of measures to cap retail utility rate increases to once a year
Section 111(d) of the Public Utility Regulatory Policies Act of 1978 (
16 U.S.C. 2621(d)
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(22)
Cap on retail utility rate increases to once a year
Each electric utility shall request no more than one rate increase once per year.
.
