2.
Prohibition of oil and gas exploration, development, and production on the outer Continental Shelf off the coast of California, Oregon, and Washington
Section 8 of the Outer Continental Shelf Lands Act (
43 U.S.C. 1337
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(q)
Prohibition of Oil and Gas Exploration, Development, and Production in Certain Areas of the Outer Continental Shelf
(1)
In general
Notwithstanding any other provision of this section or any other law, the Secretary shall not issue a lease or any other authorization for the exploration, development, or production of oil or natural gas in the planning areas described in paragraph (2).
