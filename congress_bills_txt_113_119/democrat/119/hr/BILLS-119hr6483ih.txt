Section 607 of the Neighborhood Reinvestment Corporation Act (

(b)
(1)
The accounts of the corporation shall be audited annually by an independent external auditor.
(2)
Notwithstanding any other audit work performed by the Office of Inspector General of the corporation, the audits required under paragraph (1) shall be conducted in accordance with generally accepted auditing standards by independent certified public accountants who are certified by a regulatory authority of the jurisdiction in which the audit is undertaken.
