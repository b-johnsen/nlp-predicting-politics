1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Gynecologic Pain Management Study Act
.
2.
Study on barriers to pain management methods during gynecologic procedures
(a)
In general
The Secretary of Health and Human Services shall conduct a comprehensive study to assess barriers to health care providers offering, and patients accessing, pain management methods during gynecologic procedures (such as whether lack of access is related to insurance coverage, training, or resources issues). Such study shall include a review of current literature and, if necessary, new research.

(d)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)
Pain management methods
The term
pain management methods
means methods or strategies to prevent, reduce, or manage physical pain associated with a medical procedure.
(2)
Secretary
The term
Secretary
means the Secretary of Health and Human Services.
