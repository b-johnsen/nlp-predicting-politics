3.
Definitions
In this Act:
(1)
Oak Flat
The term
Oak Flat
means the approximately 2,422 acres of Forest Service land in the Tonto National Forest in southeastern Arizona commonly known as
Oak Flat
and generally depicted as
Oak Flat Withdrawal Area
on the map titled
Save Oak Flat from Foreign Mining
and dated November 2025.
(2)
Resolution copper
The term
Resolution Copper
means Resolution Copper Mining, LLC, formed by foreign mining entities, Rio Tinto (55 percent) and BHP (45 percent), to acquire Oak Flat and extract and monetize United States copper located beneath it.
