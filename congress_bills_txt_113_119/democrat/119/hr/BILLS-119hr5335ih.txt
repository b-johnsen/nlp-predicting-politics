1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Promoting Economic Resilience and Unity Act of 2025
or the
PERU Act of 2025
.
2.
Nonimmigrant traders and investors
For purposes of clauses (i) and (ii) of section 101(a)(15)(E) of the Immigration and Nationality Act (
8 U.S.C. 1101(a)(15)(E)
), Peru shall be considered to be a foreign state described in such section if the Government of Peru provides similar nonimmigrant status to nationals of the United States.
