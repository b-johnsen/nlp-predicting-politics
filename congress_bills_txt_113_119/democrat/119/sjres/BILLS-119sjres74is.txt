IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES

introduced the following joint resolution; which was read twice and referred to the

JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Pipeline and Hazardous Materials Safety Administration relating to
Pipeline Safety: Editorial Change To Reflect the Name Change of the Gulf of Mexico to the Gulf of America
.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Pipeline and Hazardous Materials Safety Administration relating to
Pipeline Safety: Editorial Change To Reflect the Name Change of the Gulf of Mexico to the Gulf of America
(90 Fed. Reg. 21434 (May 20, 2025)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
