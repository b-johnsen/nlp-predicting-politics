JOINT RESOLUTION
Terminating the national emergency declared to impose duties on articles imported from Brazil.
That, pursuant to section 202 of the National Emergencies Act (
50 U.S.C. 1622
), the national emergency declared on July 30, 2025, by the President in Executive Order 14323 (90 Fed. Reg. 37739) is terminated.
