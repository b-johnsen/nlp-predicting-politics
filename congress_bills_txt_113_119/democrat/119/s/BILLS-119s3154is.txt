1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Service Academies District of Columbia Equality Act of 2025
.

appointed to military service academies
(a)

Five
and inserting
Fifteen
.
(b)
United States Naval Academy
Section 8454(a)(5) of title 10, United States Code, is amended by striking
Five
and inserting
Fifteen
.
(c)
United States Air Force Academy
Section 9442(a)(5) of title 10, United States Code, is amended by striking
Five
and inserting
Fifteen
.
