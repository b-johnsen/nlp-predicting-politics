1.
Revocation of charter of incorporation of the Lower Sioux Indian Community
The request of the Lower Sioux Indian Community in the State of Minnesota to surrender the charter of incorporation issued to that community and ratified on July 17, 1937, pursuant to section 17 of the Act of June 18, 1934 (commonly known as the
Indian Reorganization Act
) (48 Stat. 988, chapter 576;
25 U.S.C. 5124
), is hereby accepted and that charter of incorporation is hereby revoked.
