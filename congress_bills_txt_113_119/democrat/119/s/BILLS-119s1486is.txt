Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Clean Ocean And Safe Tourism Anti-Drilling Act of 2025
or the
COAST Anti-Drilling Act of 2025
.
2.
Prohibition of oil and gas leasing in certain areas of outer Continental Shelf
Section 8 of the Outer Continental Shelf Lands Act (
43 U.S.C. 1337
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(q)
Prohibition of oil and gas leasing in certain areas of outer Continental Shelf
(1)
In general
Notwithstanding any other provision of this section or any other law, the Secretary shall not issue a lease or any other authorization for the exploration, development, or production of oil, natural gas, or any other mineral in the planning areas described in paragraph (2).
