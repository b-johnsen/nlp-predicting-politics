1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Plug Offshore Wells Act
.
2.
Annual report on decommissioning offshore oil and gas wells, platforms, and pipelines
(a)
Definitions
In this Act:
(1)
Decommissioning
The term
decommissioning
has the meaning given the term in section 250.1700 of title 30, Code of Federal Regulations (or a successor regulation).
(2)
Secretary
The term
Secretary
means the Secretary of the Interior.
