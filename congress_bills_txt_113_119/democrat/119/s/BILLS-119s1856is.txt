1.
Exclusion of military bonuses
(a)
In general
Subsection (a) of
section 134
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking
any qualified military benefit
and inserting
any bonus paid to a member of the uniformed services under
chapter 5
of title 37, United States Code, or any qualified military benefit
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by this section shall apply to taxable years beginning after December 31, 2024.
