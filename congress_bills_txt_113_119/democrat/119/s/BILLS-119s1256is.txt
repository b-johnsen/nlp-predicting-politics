4.
Fire Island AIDS Memorial
(a)
Authorization To establish Memorial
Subject to subsection (c) and any terms and conditions the Secretary considers appropriate, the Foundation may establish and maintain a memorial within the boundaries of Fire Island National Seashore to honor the residents of Fire Island who died of AIDS, to be known as the
Fire Island AIDS Memorial
.
(b)
Use of federal funds prohibited
Federal funds may not be used to design, procure, prepare, install, or maintain the Memorial.
(c)
Design and location of memorial
The design and location of the Memorial shall be subject to the approval of the Secretary.
