1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Telecom Cybersecurity Transparency Act
.
2.
Full release of unclassified report
Not later than 30 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Secretary of Homeland Security shall publicly release, in full, the unclassified report titled
U.S. Telecommunications Insecurity 2022
that was prepared for the Cybersecurity and Infrastructure Security Agency under a contract through the Science and Technology Directorate of the Department of Homeland Security.
