4.
Effective date
The amendments made by this Act shall apply to any individual who is under a disability (as defined
in section 223(d) of the Social Security Act) that began before, on, or
after the date of enactment of this Act and continues, but only with
respect to child's insurance benefits or disability insurance benefits
under title II of the Social Security Act for months beginning after such
date of enactment.
