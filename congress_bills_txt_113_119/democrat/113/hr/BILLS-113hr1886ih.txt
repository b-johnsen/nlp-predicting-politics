1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Promoting Border Commerce and Travel
Act
.
2.
Prohibition on
land border crossing fees
The
Secretary of Homeland Security may not establish, collect, or otherwise impose
a border crossing fee for pedestrians or passenger vehicles at land ports of
entry along the southern border or the northern border of the United States,
and may not conduct any study relating to the imposition of such a fee.
