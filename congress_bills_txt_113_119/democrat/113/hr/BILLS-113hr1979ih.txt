(2)
Administration
by Secretary and similar terms
The Federal Direct Stafford Loans
funded under this subsection for the award year beginning July 1, 2013, shall
be administered by the Secretary and shall be made in accordance with the
requirements, and be subject to all terms and conditions, of this part, except
that appropriations provided under the first sentence of subsection (a) shall
not be used for any Federal Direct Stafford Loans made during such
period.
