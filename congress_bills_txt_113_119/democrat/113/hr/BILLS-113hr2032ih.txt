Amendment

152.
Radioactive metal
(a)
Prohibition
The Department of Energy shall not plan or
undertake sales, trades, barters, or transfers of any metal that has been used
or stored in a radiological area other than for the purposes of storage
pursuant to subsection (b).

) for the storage or disposal of radioactive waste; or
(3)
at a site on

Secretary for the storage or disposal of radioactive
waste.
