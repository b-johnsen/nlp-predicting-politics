(a)
In General
No person in the custody or under the effective control of the United States shall be subject to
any treatment or technique of interrogation not authorized by and listed
in the United States Army Field Manual on Human Intelligence Collector
Operations.
(b)
Applicability
Subsection (a) shall not apply with respect to any person in the custody or under the effective
control of the United States pursuant to a criminal law or immigration law
of the United States.
(c)
Construction
Nothing in this section shall be construed to affect the rights under the United States
Constitution of any person in the custody or under the physical
jurisdiction of the United States.
