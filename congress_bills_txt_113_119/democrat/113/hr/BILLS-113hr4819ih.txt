1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Jobs Investment Act of 2014
.
2.
Class life recommendations
(a)
In general
The Secretary of the Treasury shall, based on the monitoring and analysis of actual experience with
respect to depreciable property under section 168(i)(1) of the Internal
Revenue Code of 1986, develop recommendations for prescribing to any
depreciable property a class life, classification, or recovery period that
more equitably provides for capital cost recovery across all industries
without any significant effect on total tax revenue.
(b)
Proposal
Not later than January 31, 2019, and every 5 years thereafter, the Secretary shall submit to the
Committee on Ways and Means of the House of Representatives and the
Committee on Finance of the Senate the recommendations developed under
subsection (a).
