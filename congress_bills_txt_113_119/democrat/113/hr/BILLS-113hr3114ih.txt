1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Helping Families and Seniors Save on Health Care Act of 2013
.
2.
Repeal of increase in income threshold for determining medical care deduction
Section 9013 of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act (and the amendments made by such section) are hereby repealed, and the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 shall be applied and administered as if such section (and amendments) had not been enacted.
