1.
Loss of eligibility for hazing crime conviction
Section 484 of the Higher Education Act of 1965 (
20 U.S.C. 1091
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(u)
Loss of eligibility for hazing crime conviction
No student who is convicted of a criminal hazing offense under State law shall be eligible to receive any grant, loan, or work assistance under this title.
