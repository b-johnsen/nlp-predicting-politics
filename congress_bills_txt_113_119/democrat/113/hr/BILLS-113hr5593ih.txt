3.
Limitation
None of the amendments made by this Act shall be construed as requiring increased consideration of
information relating to minor financial or mental health issues of an
individual in evaluating the access to classified information of such
individual.
4.
Effective date
The amendments made by this Act shall take effect 1 year after the date of enactment of this Act.
