1.
Dain Taylor Venne Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 3260 Broad Street in Port Henry, New York, shall be known and designated as the
Dain Taylor Venne Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Dain Taylor Venne Post Office Building
.
