7.
Encryption and
privacy technology of electronic devices and software
Notwithstanding any other provision of law,
the Federal Government shall not mandate that the manufacturer of an electronic
device or software for an electronic device build into such device or software
a mechanism that allows the Federal Government to bypass the encryption or
privacy technology of such device or software.
