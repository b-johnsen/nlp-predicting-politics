Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Accelerate Our Manufacturers Act
.
2.
100 Percent Bonus for Manufacturing Property
(a)
In general
Section 168(k) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by adding at the end the following
new paragraph:
(6)
Special rule for manufacturing property
(A)
In general
In the case of qualified manufacturing property, paragraph (1)(A) shall be applied by substituting
100 percent
for
50 percent
.
