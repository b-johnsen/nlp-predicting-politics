1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
All-American Flag
Act
.
2.
Requirement for
purchase of domestically made United States flags for use by Federal
Government
Only such flags of
the United States of America, regardless of size, that are 100 percent
manufactured in the United States, from articles, materials, or supplies 100
percent of which are grown, produced, or manufactured in the United States, may
be acquired for use by the Federal Government.
3.
Effective
date
Section 2 shall apply to
purchases of flags made on or after 180 days after the date of the enactment of
this Act.
