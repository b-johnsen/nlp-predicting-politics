1.
Reauthorization of
Neotropical Migratory Bird Conservation Act
Section 10 of the Neotropical Migratory Bird
Conservation Act (
16 U.S.C. 6109
) is amended to read as follows:
10.
Authorization of
appropriations
(a)
In
general
There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this
Act such sums as are necessary for each of fiscal years 2014 through
2019.
(b)
Use of
funds
Of the amounts made available under subsection (a) for each
fiscal year, not less than 75 percent shall be expended for projects carried
out at a location outside of the United
States.
