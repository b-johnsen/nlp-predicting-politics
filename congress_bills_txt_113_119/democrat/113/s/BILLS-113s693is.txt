City of Hermiston, Oregon, water recycling

(a)
Authorization
The Secretary, in cooperation with the City
of Hermiston, Oregon, is authorized to participate in the design, planning, and
construction of permanent facilities to reclaim and reuse water in the City of
Hermiston, Oregon.
(b)
Cost share
The Federal share of the costs of the
project described in subsection (a) shall not exceed 25 percent of the total
cost.
(c)
Limitation
The Secretary shall not provide funds for
the operation and maintenance of the project described in subsection
(a).
