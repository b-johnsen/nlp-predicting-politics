1.
National anthem
(a)
Findings
Congress finds the following:
(1)
America the Beautiful is an anthem that far better represents the scope and majesty of the
geography of the United States of America as well as the principles of
freedom, liberty, fraternity, and progress that are the unifying beliefs
of our democracy.
(2)
America the Beautiful enshrines the sacrifice of those who have given their lives to create and
preserve our democracy.
(3)
America the Beautiful should replace the Star-Spangled Banner as the national anthem.
(b)
Amendment
Section 301(a)
of title 36, United States Code, is amended to read as follows:
(a)
Designation
The composition consisting of the words and music known as America the Beautiful is the national
anthem.
