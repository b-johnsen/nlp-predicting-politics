2.

(a)
Treatment
(1)
In
general
Notwithstanding any
other provision of law, effective for discharges occurring on or after October
1, 2014, for purposes of making payments under section 1886(d) of the Social
Security Act (
42 U.S.C. 1395ww
) to a hospital described in paragraph (2), the
wage index that applies to such hospital under such section shall be the
highest wage index assigned to hospitals in the core-based statistical area in
which the hospital is located (regardless of which State the hospital is
located in) as a result of the application of section 4410(a) of the Balanced
Budget Act of 1997 (
42 U.S.C. 1395ww
note).
