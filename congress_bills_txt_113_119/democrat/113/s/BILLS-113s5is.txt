build upon the

coalitions created by VAWA and expanding the reach of VAWA to meet the
remaining unmet needs of victims;
(4)
continue to
provide the training, tools, and resources necessary for law enforcement
officers and victim service providers to hold the perpetrators of domestic and
sexual violence accountable and to keep victims safe; and
(5)
ensure that all
victims of domestic and sexual violence, including Native American women, gay
and lesbian victims, and battered immigrant women, receive the support and
protections provided by VAWA.
