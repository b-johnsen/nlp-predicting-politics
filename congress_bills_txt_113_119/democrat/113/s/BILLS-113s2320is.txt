1.
Lance Corporal Daniel Nathan Deyarmin, Jr., Post Office Building
(a)
Redesignation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 162 Northeast Avenue in Tallmadge,
Ohio, shall be known and designated as the
Lance Corporal Daniel Nathan Deyarmin, Jr., Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to
the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a
reference to the
Lance Corporal Daniel Nathan Deyarmin, Jr., Post Office Building
.
