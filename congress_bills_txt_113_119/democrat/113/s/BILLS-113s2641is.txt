1.
Loans held on portfolio treated as qualified mortgages
Section 129C(b)(2) of the Truth in Lending Act (
15 U.S.C. 1639c(b)(2)
) is amended by adding at the
end the following:
(F)
Loans held on portfolio
The term
qualified mortgage
includes a residential mortgage loan made by a creditor having less than $10,000,000,000 in total
assets so long as such loan appears on the
balance sheet of such creditor.
