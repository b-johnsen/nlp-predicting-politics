the report of the Military Compensation and Retirement Modernization

Department of Defense for the defense commissary system during fiscal year
2015 may not be reduced below the average monthly amount of funds made
available for that system during fiscal year 2014 until the date of the
report of the Military Compensation and Retirement Modernization
Commission under section 674(f) of the National Defense Authorization Act
