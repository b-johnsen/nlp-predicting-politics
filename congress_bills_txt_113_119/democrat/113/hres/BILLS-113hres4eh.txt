IV
113th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 4
In the House of Representatives, U. S.,
January 3, 2013
RESOLUTION
That the Clerk be instructed to inform the President of the United States that the House of Representatives has elected John A. Boehner, a Representative from the State of Ohio as Speaker, and Karen L. Haas, a citizen of the State of Maryland as Clerk of the House of Representatives of the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress.
Karen L. Haas,
Clerk.
