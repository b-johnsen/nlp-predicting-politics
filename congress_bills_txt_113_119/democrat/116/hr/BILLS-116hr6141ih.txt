2.
Support for maternity care coordination
(a)
Authorization of appropriations
There is authorized to be appropriated to the Secretary of Veterans Affairs $15,000,000 for fiscal year 2022 to improve maternity care coordination for women veterans throughout pregnancy and the one-year postpartum period beginning on the last day of the pregnancy. Such amounts are authorized in addition to any other amounts authorized for such purpose.

Report on maternal mortality and severe maternal morbidity among women veterans
(a)
GAO report
