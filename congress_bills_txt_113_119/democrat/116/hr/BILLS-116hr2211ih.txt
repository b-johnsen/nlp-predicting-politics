1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Stop Tip-overs of Unstable, Risky Dressers on Youth Act
or the
STURDY Act
.
2.
Consumer product safety standard to protect against tip-over of free-standing clothing storage units
(a)
Free-Standing clothing storage unit defined
(1)
In general
In this section, the term
free-standing clothing storage unit
means any piece of furniture manufactured in the United States or imported for use in the United States that is intended for the storage of clothing, including furniture items thatâ

(2)

The Consumer Product Safety Commission may adjust the definition under paragraph (1) if the Commission determines that inclusion of additional furniture items is reasonably necessary to protect public health and safety.
