(c)
Applicability
(1)
In general
Except as provided in paragraph (2), subsection (f) of section 232 of the Trade Expansion Act of 1962 (
19 U.S.C. 1862
), as amended by subsection (b), shall apply to adjustments of imports under that section on or after July 1, 2018.
(2)
Exception
Subsection (f) of section 232 of the Trade Expansion Act of 1962 (
19 U.S.C. 1862
), as amended by subsection (b), shall not apply to the presidential actions taken under that section on March 8, 2018, relating to the adjustment of imports of steel and aluminum, or any subsequent actions (including proclamations, Executive orders, or other Executive acts) relating to those presidential actions.
