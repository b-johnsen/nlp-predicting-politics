1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Ensuring Protection In Childcare Act
or the
EPIC Act
.
2.
Authority to purchase personal protective equipment to protect the health of children who receive certain child care services
A child care provider who receives payment for services provided under the Child Care and Development Block Grant Act of 1990 (
42 U.S.C. 9857
et seq.) may use a portion of such payment to purchase personal protective equipment, and other equipment, necessary to protect the health of children who receive such services and child care workers who provide such services.
