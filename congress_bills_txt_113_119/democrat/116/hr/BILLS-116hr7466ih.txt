1.
Short Title
This Act may be cited as the
Emancipation Statue Removal Act
.
2.
Removal of Emancipation Monument
(a)
Removal
The Secretary of the Interior shall ensure that the Emancipation Monument is removed from Lincoln Park in the District of Columbia.
(b)
Donation
The Secretary shall donate the Emancipation Monument to a museum or other similar entity. The recipient of the Emancipation Monument may not store, display, or exhibit the monument outside. If the monument is stored, displayed, or exhibited outside, ownership of the monument will revert back to the Federal Government.
