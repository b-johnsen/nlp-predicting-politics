1.
Exemption of reimbursement for certain travel to return to the United States
Section 4 of the State Department Basic Authorities Act of 1956 (
22 U.S.C. 2671
) is amended by adding at the end the following new subsection:
(e)
In the case of a communicable disease for which the Federal Government has issued a travel alert or travel warning, the reimbursement requirements under subsections (b)(2)(A)(ii) and (d) shall not apply.
