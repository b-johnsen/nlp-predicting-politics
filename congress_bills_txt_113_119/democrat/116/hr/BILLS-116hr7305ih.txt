1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Federal Emergency Judicial Tolling Act
.
2.
Tolling statutes of limitations during the COVID-19 emergency period
(a)
In general
The COVID-19 emergency period shall not be considered for purposes of a statute of limitations for any action described under subsection (b).

(2)

(c)
COVID-19 emergency period defined
The term
COVID-19 emergency period
means the period beginning on January 31, 2020, and ending on December 31, 2020.
