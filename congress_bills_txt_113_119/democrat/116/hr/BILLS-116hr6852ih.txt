1.
Prohibition on waivers
Section 4003(c)(3)(A)(iii) of the CARES Act (
15 U.S.C. 9042(c)(3)(A)(iii)
) is amended to read as follows:
(iii)
Prohibition on waivers
The Secretary and the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System may not waive any requirement under this section or of a rule issued to carry out this section.
