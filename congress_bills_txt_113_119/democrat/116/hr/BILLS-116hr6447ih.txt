1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Northern Border Regional Commission Financial Flexibility Act
.
2.
Economic and infrastructure development grants
Section 15501(d) of title 40, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
(4)
Special contribution rule
The maximum contribution percentage for a project or activity carried out in a county described in paragraph (2) using a grant awarded between January 1, 2019, and December 31, 2021, shall be treated as 100 percent.
.
