1.
Clarification for purposes of employment by a House Member
(a)
In general
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, for purposes of being hired and employed by an office of a Member of the House of Representatives, and being paid compensation by the House of Representatives, an alien who has a valid employment authorization document issued to the alien under the Deferred Action for Childhood Arrivals Program of the Secretary of Homeland Security, established pursuant to the memorandum from the Secretary of Homeland Security entitled
Exercising Prosecutorial Discretion with Respect to Individuals Who Came to the United States as Children
, dated June 15, 2012, shall be deemed to be a person who owes allegiance to the United States.
(b)
Effective date
This section shall apply with respect to fiscal year 2019 and each succeeding fiscal year.
