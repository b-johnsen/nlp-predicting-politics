(c)
Timing for authority To collect recordings
(1)
Initiation
As soon as practicable after the enactment of this Act, the Librarian shall begin collecting video and audio recordings under subsection (a)(1).

Sunset
The authority of the Librarian to collect video and audio recordings under subsection (a)(1) shall terminate upon the expiration of the 5-year period which begins on the date of the enactment of this Act.
