1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Carbon Capture and Sequestration Extension Act of 2019
.
2.
Extension of credit for carbon oxide sequestration
(a)
In general
Section 45Q(d)(1)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking
January 1, 2024
and inserting
January 1, 2025
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by this section applies to facilities the construction of which begins after December 31, 2023.
