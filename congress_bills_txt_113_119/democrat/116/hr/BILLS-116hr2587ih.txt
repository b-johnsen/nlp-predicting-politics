1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Reef Safe Act of 2019
.
2.
Labeling criteria for
reef safe
sunscreen
(a)
In general
As soon as practicable, but not later than 2 years after the date of enactment of this Act, the Secretary, acting through the Commissioner, shall develop labeling criteria for a
Reef Safe
designation for nonprescription sunscreen, in consultation with the Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency and the Administrator of the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration.
(b)
Reef safe label
(1)
In general
Not later than 2 years after the date of enactment of this Act, the Secretary, acting through the Commissioner, shall develop standards for use of the term
Reef Safe
on the labeling of nonprescription sunscreen, which shall conform with the requirements of section 502 of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (
21 U.S.C. 352
).
(2)
