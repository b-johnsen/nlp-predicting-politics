1.
Mother Frances Xavier Cabrini Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 8320 13th Avenue in Brooklyn, New York, shall be known and designated as the
Mother Frances Xavier Cabrini Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Mother Frances Xavier Cabrini Post Office Building
.
