(C)
If the community care provider is not described in subparagraph (A) or (B) of this paragraph, the Secretary shall administer the claim pursuant to section 1725 or 1728 of such title, as the case may be.

(d)
Application
This section shall apply to medical care or services furnished during the period of a covered public health emergency, regardless of whether such care or services were furnished before the date of the enactment of this Act.
