of title 10, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following new section:
256.
Termination of authority
Authority exercised under section 251, 252, or 253 of this title shall terminate on the date that is 14 days after the first day on which such authority is exercised unless such authority is specifically extended pursuant to the enactment of a law or joint resolution.
