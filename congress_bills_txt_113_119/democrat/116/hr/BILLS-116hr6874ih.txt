1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Paycheck Protection Program Transparency Act of 2020
.
2.
Full disclosure of entities receiving paycheck protection program loans
(a)

In this section:
(1)

(2)
Lender
The term

means any lender or intermediary (as defined under section 7(m) of the Small Business Act (

).
(3)
Paycheck protection program loan
The term
Paycheck Protection Program Loan
means a covered loan under section 7(a)(36) of the Small Business Act (
15 U.S.C. 636(a)(36)
).
(b)
Website
(1)
Recipients

(A)

(B)

(F)

(G)

(K)

(2)
