1.
Extension of participation in
8(a)
program
The Administrator of the Small Business Administration shall ensure that a small business concern participating in the program established under section 8(a) of the Small Business Act (
15 U.S.C. 637
) on or before March 13, 2020, may elect to extend such participation by a period of up to 2 years, regardless of whether such concern previously elected to suspend participation in such program pursuant to guidance of the Administrator.
