1.
Training in use of medical cannabis for Department of Veterans Affairs primary care providers
(a)
Training
Not later than one year after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Secretary of Veterans Affairs shall provide for all primary care providers of the Department of Veterans Affairs an initial training in the use of medical cannabis. The Secretary shall provide supplemental training as necessary.
(b)
Partnerships with medical schools
In developing and providing the training to be provided under subsection (a), the Secretary shall enter into partnerships with medical schools that have incorporated education on medical cannabis into their curricula.
