COVIDГўВҖВ“19 data collection requirements
(a)
Federal detention facilities
The Director of U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement, the Commissioner of U.S. Customs and Border Protection, the Director of the Office of Refugee Resettlement, and any senior official acting in, or performing the duties of, any such position shall, during the COVIDГўВҖВ“19 emergency data collection periodГўВҖВ”

(A)

(B)

(c)

(1)

The Department of Health and Human Services such use amounts otherwise appropriated for the Office of Refugee Resettlement to carry out its responsibilities under this section.
(2)
