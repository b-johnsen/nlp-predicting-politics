Study and report by National Academy of Medicine
(a)
In general
Not later than 90 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Administrator of the Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (referred to in this section as the
Administrator
) shall enter into an agreement with the National Academies of Sciences, Engineering, and Medicine (referred to in this section as the
National Academies
) to conduct a study to review the methodology the Administrator has implemented for the private payor rate-based clinical laboratory fee schedule under the Medicare program under title XVIII of the Social Security Act (
42 U.S.C. 1395
et seq.).

Scope of study
In carrying out the study described in subsection (a), the National Academies shall consider the following:
