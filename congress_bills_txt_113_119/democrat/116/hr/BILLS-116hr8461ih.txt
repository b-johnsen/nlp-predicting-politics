1.
Requirements relating to export licenses for firearm silencers
Section 38 of the Arms Export Control Act (
22 U.S.C. 2778
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(l)
Requirements relating to export licenses for firearm silencers
(1)
In general
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the President may approve or otherwise issue a license for the export of firearm silencers only if the recipient end-user of the firearm silencers is the government of a foreign country or any person acting on behalf of such government.
(2)
Firearm silencer defined
In this subsection, the term
firearm silencer
has the meaning given that term in section 921(a)(24) of title 18, United States Code.
.
