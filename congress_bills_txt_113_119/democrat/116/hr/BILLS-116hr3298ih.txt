1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Child Care Quality and Access Act of 2019
.
2.
Increase in entitlement funding for child care
Section 418(a)(3) of the Social Security Act (
42 U.S.C. 618(a)(3)
) is amended by striking
$2,917,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2017 and 2018
and inserting
$3,917,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2020 and 2021
.
