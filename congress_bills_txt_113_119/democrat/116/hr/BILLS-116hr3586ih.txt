201.
Voluntary nature of building asset rating program
(a)
In general
Any program of the Secretary that may enable the owner of a commercial building or a residential building to obtain a rating, score, or label regarding the actual or anticipated energy usage or performance of a building shall be made available on a voluntary, optional, and market-driven basis.
