Statute of limitations for Commission actions
(a)
In general
Section 21 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 (
15 U.S.C. 78u
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(j)
Statute of limitations
(1)
Civil monetary penalties
(A)
In general
An action or proceeding brought or instituted by the Commission under any provision of the securities laws for a civil monetary penalty may be brought not later than 10 years after the alleged violation.
(B)
Exclusion
The period of limitations in subparagraph (A) does not run during any time when an alleged violator is absent from the United States or has no reasonably ascertainable place of abode or work within the United States.
