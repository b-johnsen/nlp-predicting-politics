1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Ending Bankruptcy Discrimination Act of 2020
.
2.
Protection against discriminatory treatment
Section 525(b) of title 11, United States Code, is amended by inserting
deny employment to,
after
may
.
3.
Effective date; application of amendment
(a)
Effective date
Except as provided in subsection (b), this Act and the amendment made by this Act shall take effect on the date of the enactment of this Act.
(b)
Application of amendment
The amendment made by this Act shall not apply with respect to conduct that occurs before the date of the enactment of this Act.
