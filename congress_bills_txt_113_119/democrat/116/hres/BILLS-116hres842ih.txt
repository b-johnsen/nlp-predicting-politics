RESOLUTION
Requiring an affirmative vote of a majority of the Members present and voting, a quorum being
present, on final passage of House Joint Resolution 79.
That an affirmative vote of a majority of the Members present and voting, a quorum being present, shall be required on final passage of House Joint Resolution 79.
