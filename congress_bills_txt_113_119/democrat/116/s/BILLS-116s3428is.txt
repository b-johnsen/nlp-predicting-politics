(c)
Guidance
(1)
Definitions
In this subsection, the terms
executive agency
and
telework
have the meanings given those terms in section 6501 of title 5, United States Code, as amended by this section.
(2)
Requirement
Not later than 90 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Director of the Office of Personnel Management, in collaboration with the Chief Human Capital Officers Council, shall establish uniform guidance for executive agencies regarding how to collect data on, set goals for, and report cost savings achieved through telework, which shall account for cost savings achieved through telework that relate to travel, energy use, and the use of real estate.
