1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Correctional Facility Disaster Preparedness Act of 2020
.
2.
Definitions
In this Act, the term
major disaster
means a major disaster declared by the President under section 401 of the Robert T. Stafford Disaster Relief and Emergency Assistance Act (
42 U.S.C. 5170
).
