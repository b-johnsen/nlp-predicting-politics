(1)
In general
A borrower experiencing a financial hardship due, directly or indirectly, to a declared disaster may request forbearance from any mortgage payment for not less than 180 days, which may be extended for an additional 180 days at the request of the borrower, provided that, at the borrower's request, either the initial or extended period of forbearance may be shortened.
(2)
