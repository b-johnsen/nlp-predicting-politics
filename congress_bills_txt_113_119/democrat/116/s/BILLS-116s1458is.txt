3.
Grants authorized
(a)
In general
The Secretary shall establish an outdoor recreation legacy partnership program under which the Secretary may award grants to eligible entities for projectsâ
(1)

(2)

(b)
Matching requirement
(1)
In general
As a condition of receiving a grant under subsection (a), an eligible entity shall provide matching funds in the form of cash or an in-kind contribution in an amount equal to not less than 100 percent of the amounts made available under the grant.
(2)
Sources
The matching amounts referred to in paragraph (1) may include amounts made available from State, local, nongovernmental, or private sources.
