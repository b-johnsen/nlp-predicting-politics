(b)
Purpose
The primary purpose of this Act is to establish the Office of Critical Technologies and Securityâ
(1)

to develop a long-term strategy to achieve and maintain United States technological supremacy with respect to critical emerging, foundational, and dual-use technologies and ensure supply chain integrity and security for such technologies.
