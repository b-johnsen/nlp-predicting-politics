Congress finds the following:
(1)
The widespread use of face coverings by the public is essential to help slow the spread of the virus that causes COVID芒聙聯19 and help prevent the transmission of such virus by individuals who do not show symptoms.

Overwhelming scientific evidence has shown that face coverings block a high portion of respiratory droplets that spread the virus that causes COVID芒聙聯19.

The widespread use of face coverings is necessary (but not by itself sufficient) to slow the spread of the virus that causes COVID芒聙聯19 and should be promoted along with regular handwashing, social distancing, testing, and minimizing nonessential activities outside of the household.
