Energy storage
The term

(A)

Island
The term

, with respect to a distributed generator or energy storage equipment, means that the generator or equipment continues to power a location in the absence of electric power from a primary source.

(6)
Rural electric cooperative
The term

means an electric cooperative (as defined in section 3 of the Federal Power Act (
16 U.S.C. 796

(7)
Secretary
The term
