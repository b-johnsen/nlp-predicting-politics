1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Coronavirus CDFI Funding Act
.
2.
Supplemental appropriation
There is appropriated to the Community Development Financial Institutions Fund established under section 104(a)(1) of the Community Development Banking and Financial Institutions Act of 1994 (
12 U.S.C. 4703(a)(1)
), out of amounts in the general fund of the Treasury not otherwise appropriated, $1,000,000,000 for fiscal year 2020, for providing financial assistance and technical assistance under subparagraphs (A) and (B) of section 108(a)(1) of the Community Development Banking and Financial Institutions Act of 1994 (
12 U.S.C. 4707(a)(1)
), except that subsections (d) and (e) of such section 108 shall not apply to the provision of such assistance.
