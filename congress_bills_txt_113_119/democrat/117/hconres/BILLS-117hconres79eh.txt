IV
117th CONGRESS
2d Session
H. CON. RES. 79
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
Directing the Clerk of the House of Representatives to make a correction in the enrollment of H.R. 2471.
That in the enrollment of the bill H.R. 2471, the Clerk of the House of Representatives shall make the following correction:
Amend the title so as to read:
Making consolidated appropriations for the fiscal year ending September 30, 2022, and for providing emergency assistance for the situation in Ukraine, and for other purposes.
.
Passed the House of Representatives March 9, 2022.
Cheryl L. Johnson,
Clerk.
