RESOLUTION
Congratulating the Tampa Bay Buccaneers for winning Super Bowl LV.
Whereas, on February 7, 2021, the Tampa Bay Buccaneers (referred to in this preamble as the
Bucs
) won the 2021 National Football League championship, Super Bowl LV;
