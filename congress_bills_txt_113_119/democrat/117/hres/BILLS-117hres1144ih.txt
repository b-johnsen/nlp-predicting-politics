Whereas every year in the United States, more than 3,500 children and teens are killed by gun violence and 15,000 children and teens are shot and wounded;
Whereas approximately 8,500 people in the United States under the age of 25 die because of gun violence annually, including Hadiya Pendleton, who, in 2013, was killed at 15 years of age in Chicago, Illinois, while standing in a park;
