117th CONGRESS
2d Session
H. RES. 1496
In the House of Representatives, U. S.,
November 29, 2022
RESOLUTION
Expressing the profound sorrow of the House of Representatives on the death of the Honorable A. Donald McEachin.
That the House has heard with profound sorrow of the death of the Honorable A. Donald McEachin, a Representative from the Commonwealth of Virginia.
That the Clerk communicate these resolutions to the Senate and transmit a copy thereof to the family of the deceased.
That when the House adjourns today, it adjourn as a further mark of respect to the memory of the deceased.
Cheryl L. Johnson,
Clerk.
