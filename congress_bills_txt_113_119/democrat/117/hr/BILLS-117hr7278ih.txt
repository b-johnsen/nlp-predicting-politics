1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Disabled Jurors Nondiscrimination Act
.
2.
Exclusion from Federal juries on account of disability
(a)
Discrimination prohibited
Section 1862 of title 28, United States Code, is amended by inserting
disability,
after
origin,
.
(b)
Qualifications for jury service
Section 1865 of title 28, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
(c)
No person may be disqualified from serving on grand and petit juries in the district court under paragraph (2) or (3) of subsection (b) on account of disability.
.
