1.
Samuel Ballton Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 55 Broadway in Greenlawn, New York, shall be known and designated as the
Samuel Ballton Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Samuel Ballton Post Office
.
