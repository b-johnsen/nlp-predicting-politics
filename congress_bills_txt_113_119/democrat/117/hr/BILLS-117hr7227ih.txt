1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Information on Federal Resources for Military Servicemembers Act
or the
INFORMS Act
.

Fact sheets regarding VA and Social Security benefits and compensation
(a)
Establishment
Not later than six months after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Secretary of Veterans Affairs, after consultation with the Commissioner of the Social Security Administration and stakeholders (including veterans service organizations), shall create fact sheets, one for veterans and one for survivors of deceased veterans, that compares, for such individualsâ

(b)
Elements
A fact sheet created under subsection (a) shall include, with respect to such benefits and compensation, monthly insurance benefits, and supplemental security income, the following:
(1)

(2)

(3)

(4)
Sources of additional information or assistance in applications.

The Secretary shall update a fact sheet created under subsection (a), to reflect amendments to laws described in such subsection, not less than once annually.
