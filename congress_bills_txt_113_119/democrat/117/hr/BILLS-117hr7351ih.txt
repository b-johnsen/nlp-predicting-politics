The Bureau shall exercise its authority under paragraph (1) in a manner designed to ensure that such exercise, with respect to covered nondepository lenders, is based on the assessment by the Bureau of the risks posed to minority-owned businesses, women-owned businesses, and small businesses in the relevant product markets and geographic markets, and taking into consideration, as applicable芒聙聰
