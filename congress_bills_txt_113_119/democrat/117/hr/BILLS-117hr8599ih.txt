1.
Short titles
This Act may be cited as the
Generating Resources and Opportunities Within Affordable Housing Act
or the
GROW Affordable Housing Act
.
2.
Affordable housing allocations
Section 1337(a) of the Federal Housing Enterprises Financial Safety and Soundness Act of 1992 (
12 U.S.C. 4567(a)
) is amended by striking
4.2 basis points
each place such term appears and inserting
10 basis points
.
