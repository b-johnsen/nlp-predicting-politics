1.
1st Lieutenant Andres Zermeno Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 1535 East Los Ebanos Boulevard in Brownsville, Texas, shall be known and designated as the
1st Lieutenant Andres Zermeno Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
1st Lieutenant Andres Zermeno Post Office Building
.
