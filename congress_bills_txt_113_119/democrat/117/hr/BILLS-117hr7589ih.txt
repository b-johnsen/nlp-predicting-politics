.
(b)
Clerical amendment
The table of sections at the beginning of chapter 17 of such title is amended by striking the item relating to section 1722B and inserting the following new item:
1722B. Copayments: waiver of and prohibition on collection of copayments for certain visits of veterans.
.
(c)
Applicability
The amendments made by subsection (a) shall apply with respect to mental health outpatient care visits occurring on or after the date of the enactment of this Act.
