1.
Cora Reynolds Anderson Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 404 U.S. Highway 41 North in Baraga, Michigan, shall be known and designated as the
Cora Reynolds Anderson Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Cora Reynolds Anderson Post Office
.
