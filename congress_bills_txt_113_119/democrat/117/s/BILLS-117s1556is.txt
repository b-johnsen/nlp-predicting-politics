1.
Supply chain center of excellence
Not later than 1 year after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Administrator of the National Aeronautics and Space Administration (referred to in this section as
NASA
) shall submit to the Committee on Commerce, Science, and Transportation of the Senate and the Committee on Science, Space, and Technology of the House of Representatives a report on the feasibility and benefits of establishing a supply chain center of excellence in a State in which NASA does not, as of the date of the enactment of this Act, have a research center or test facility.
