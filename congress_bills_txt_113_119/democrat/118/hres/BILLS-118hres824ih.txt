RESOLUTION
Supporting the goals and ideals of
National Veterans Small Business Week
.
Whereas the Armed Forces of the United States trains individuals with the skills, discipline, and leadership necessary to establish and operate a successful business;
Whereas there are over 1,900,000 businesses owned by veterans in the United States, employing over 5,000,000 individuals;
Whereas businesses owned by veterans make up 5.9 percent of all businesses in the United States;
Whereas veterans account for more than $1,300,000,000 in business every year;
Whereas business ownership since 2012 by women veterans has increased significantly to over 165,000 businesses;
Whereas the Small Business Administration serves thousands of veterans, service-disabled veterans, women veterans, and military spouses annually;
Whereas the Small Business Administration will be hosting events honoring
National Veterans Small Business Week
from October 30 through November 3, 2023;
Whereas Congress and the Small Business Administration will be celebrating
National Veterans Small Business Week
during the week of October 30 through November 3, 2023; and
Whereas the week of October 30 through November 3, 2023, would be an appropriate time to celebrate
National Veterans Small Business Week
: Now, therefore, be it
That the House of Representatives joins with the Small Business Administration inâ
(1)
supporting the goals and ideals of
National Veterans Small Business Week
;
(2)
supporting veterans through the Small Business Administration that funds veteran-owned small business, trains veterans for entrepreneurship, and provides veterans with contracting opportunities;
(3)
celebrating the millions of people in the United States, especially the veterans of the United States and the spouses of those veterans, who benefit from the programs of the Small Business Administration; and
(4)
expressing appreciation for the continued service to the United States by the veterans of the United States through small business ownership and entrepreneurship.
