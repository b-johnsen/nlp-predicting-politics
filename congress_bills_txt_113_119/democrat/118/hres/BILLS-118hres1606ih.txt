Whereas the United States Space Force plays a critical role in maintaining and advancing the United States military readiness, superiority, and technological dominance in space;
Whereas space has emerged as a warfighting domain essential to national security, including safeguarding military communications, missile warning systems, global navigation, and intelligence operations;
Whereas the mission of the United States Space Force is to organize, train, and equip space professionals to ensure freedom of operation in, from, and to space, and to deter and defeat threats across the space domain;
Whereas the importance of the United States Space Force is growing in an era of geopolitical competition with adversaries who have developed capabilities to challenge United States and allied access to space;
Whereas the guardians of the United States Space Force are committed to defending national interests, enabling joint force operations, and protecting critical infrastructure in the increasingly contested space domain; and
