Rule XI of the Rules of the House of Representatives is amended by adding at the end the following new clause:
Remote attendance at committee proceedings in certain cases
7.

such remote participation shall not be considered an absence for purposes of clause 5(c) of rule X or clause 2(d) of rule XI; and
(b)
such Member participating remotely pursuant to this clause shall be counted for the purpose of establishing a quorum under the rules of the House and the committee.
