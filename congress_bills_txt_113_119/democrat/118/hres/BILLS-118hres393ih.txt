Honoring all families.
Whereas there are 1,800,000 adopted children in the United States;
Whereas more than 320,000 children in the United States were cared for by foster families in fiscal year 2021;
Whereas 13 percent of adults in the United States are stepparents;
Whereas for many children in the United States, family members and loved ones serving as guardians fulfill the roles and responsibilities of parents every day;
Whereas millions of people and families in the United States face barriers to biological parenthood, including, but not limited to, LGBTQI+ people, people with disabilities, people who cannot conceive, people who experience pregnancy loss, and more; and
Whereas between 2,000,000 and 3,700,000 children under age 18 have an LGBTQI+ parent who may not have a biological relationship to their child: Now, therefore, be it
