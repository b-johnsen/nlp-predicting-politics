RESOLUTION
Celebrating Hindu Americans, and condemning attacks on Hindu places of worship, Hinduphobia, and anti-Hindu bigotry, and for other purposes.

Whereas inclusive religious celebrations such as Diwali and Holi are observed vibrantly in Hindu homes and at thousands of Hindu religious houses of worship (also known as mandirs) which are thriving throughout the country;

Whereas, according to the Federal Bureau of Investigation芒聙聶s Hate Crimes Statistics Report, anti-Hindu hate crimes targeting mandirs and persons are annually on the rise while in parallel Hinduphobia in American society is unfortunately rising: Now, therefore, be it
That the House of Representatives芒聙聰
