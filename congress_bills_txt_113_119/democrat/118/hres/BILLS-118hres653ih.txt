RESOLUTION
Expressing support for the recognition of August 13th through August 19, 2023, as
Warehouse Worker Recognition Week
, the national event celebrating the workers in the logistics industry.
Whereas, warehouse workers are important in supporting and developing the United States economy;
Whereas, according to Bureau of Labor Statistics data, the total number of warehousing and storage workers employed is over 9,000,000;
Whereas, 25.8 percent of all warehouse employees are women, while 67.6 percent are men;
Whereas supply chain disruptions have impacted the logistics industry;
Whereas warehouse workers work around the clock, with little rest;
Whereas warehouse workers in the summer months work in dangerously hot conditions, and warehouses can reach up to 90 degrees Fahrenheit;
