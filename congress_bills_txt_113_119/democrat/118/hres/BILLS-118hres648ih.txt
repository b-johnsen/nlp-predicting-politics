RESOLUTION
Reaffirming the close partnership between the United States and the Republic of Kosovo.
Whereas the United States has enduring national interests in the peace and security of the Western Balkans and the greater integration of the region into the Euro-Atlantic community of democratic states;
Whereas the United States recognized the Republic of Kosovo芒聙聶s independence on February 18, 2008, and pledged

Whereas the United States-Kosovo partnership has fostered meaningful cooperation in various fields, including security education and economic development;
Whereas the Iowa National Guard, through the State Partnership Program, coordinates with the Kosovo Security Force (KSF) in addition to American forces participating with the NATO-backed Kosovo Force (KFOR) to promote regional security and cooperation;

That it is the sense of the House of Representatives that芒聙聰
