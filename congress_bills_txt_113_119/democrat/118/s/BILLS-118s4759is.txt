(c)
Report
Not later than June 30 of the first year beginning after the date of enactment of this Act, and annually thereafter, the Administrator of the Small Business Administration shall submit to the Committee on Small Business and Entrepreneurship of the Senate and the Committee on Small Business of the House of Representatives a report on the results of the exclusion under subparagraph (C) of section 303(b)(2) of the Small Business Investment Act of 1958 (
15 U.S.C. 683(b)(2)
), as amended by subsection (b), including the economic activity generated and jobs directly and indirectly created by the exclusion.
