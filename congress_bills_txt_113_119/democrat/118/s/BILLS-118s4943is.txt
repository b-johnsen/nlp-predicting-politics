Shared liability of institutions involved in an unauthorized or fraudulently induced electronic fund transfer

Crediting of consumer account
A financial institution shall reimburse the consumer for the amount of an electronic fund transfer that was unauthorized or fraudulently induced, subject to any liability of the consumer under subsection (a).

Liability sharing

Rule of Construction
