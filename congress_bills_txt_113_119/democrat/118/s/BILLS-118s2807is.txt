2.
Grants to conserve, restore, and manage kelp forest ecosystems
(a)
Establishment
The Administrator shall establish, not later than 180 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, and carry out a grant program under which the Administrator shall award grants to eligible entities described in subsection (b) to carry out eligible projects described in subsection (c) relating to the conservation, restoration, or management of kelp forest ecosystems.
