1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Stop Judge Shopping Act
.
2.
Jurisdiction over certain actions with nationwide effect
The United States District Court for the District of Columbia shall have original and exclusive jurisdiction over any civil action for declaratory or injunctive relief (including a nationwide injunction, stay, vacatur, or any other relief with similar nationwide force and effect) against the enforcement of any Federal law (including regulations and Executive orders) if the relief extends beyond the parties to the civil action.
