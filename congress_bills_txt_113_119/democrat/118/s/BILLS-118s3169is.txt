(c)
University centers
(1)
In general
The Secretary shall make awards to institutions of higher education for such institutions to serve as university centers.
(2)
Geographic coverage
The Secretary shall ensure that at least one university center is established in each State to provide services in such State.
(3)
Prioritization of historically Black colleges and universities, Tribal colleges and universities, Hispanic-Serving institutions, and minority-Serving institutions
In establishing university centers under this subsection, the Secretary shall prioritize institutions of higher education that are eligible to receive funds under section 371(a) of the Higher Education Act of 1965 (
20 U.S.C. 1067q(a)
).
