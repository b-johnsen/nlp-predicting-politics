2.
Regulations on nonclinical testing methods
(a)
In general
Not later than 180 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Secretary of Health and Human Services, acting through the Commissioner of Food and Drugs, shall publish a final rule to implement section 505(z) of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (
21 U.S.C. 355(z)
).
