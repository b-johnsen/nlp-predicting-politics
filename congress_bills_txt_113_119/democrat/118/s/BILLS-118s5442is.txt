2.
Removing differentiation between mead and low alcohol by volume wine
(a)
In general
Section 5041(h)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended to read as follows:
(h)
Low alcohol by volume wine
(1)
In general
For purposes of subsections (a) and (b)(1), low alcohol by volume wine shall be deemed to be still wines containing not more than 16 percent of alcohol by volume.
