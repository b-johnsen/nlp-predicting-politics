1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Savannah CRTC Dining Hall Construction Act
.
2.
Authorization of amounts for construction of a dining hall and services training facility at Savannah Air National Guard Base, Georgia
There is authorized to be appropriated to the Secretary of Defense for fiscal year 2025 $27,000,000 for the construction of a dining hall and services training facility at Savannah Air National Guard Base, Georgia.
