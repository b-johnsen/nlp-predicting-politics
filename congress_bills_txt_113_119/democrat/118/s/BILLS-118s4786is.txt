1.
Cancellation of oil or gas leases held by persons that manipulate the market price of oil or gas in violation of Federal law
(a)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)
Federal oil and gas lease
The term
Federal oil and gas lease
means an oil and gas lease issued or authorized by the Secretary of the Interior pursuant to applicable Federal law.
(2)
OPEC country
The term
OPEC country
means Algeria, the Democratic Republic of Congo, Equatorial Guinea, Gabon, Iran, Kuwait, Libya, Nigeria, Saudi Arabia, United Arab Emirates, or Venezuela.
(3)
OPEC plus country
The term
OPEC plus country
means Azerbaijan, Bahrain, Brunei, Kazakhstan, Malaysia, Mexico, Oman, Russia, South Sudan, or Sudan.
