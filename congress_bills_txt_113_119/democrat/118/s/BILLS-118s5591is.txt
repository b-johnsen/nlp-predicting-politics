1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Holcombe Rucker Park National Commemorative Site Act
.
2.
Definitions
In this Act:
(1)
City
The term
City
means New York City, New York.
(2)
Commemorative Site
The term
Commemorative Site
means the Holcombe Rucker Park National Commemorative Site designated by section 3(a).
(3)
Secretary
The term
Secretary
means the Secretary of the Interior.
(4)
State
The term
State
means the State of New York.
