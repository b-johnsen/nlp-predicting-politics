1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Enhance Access To SNAP Act of 2023
or the
EATS Act of 2023
.
2.
Student eligibility under supplemental nutrition assistance program
(a)
Definition of household
Section 3(m)(5) of the Food and Nutrition Act of 2008 (
7 U.S.C. 2012(m)(5)
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(F)
Individuals who are bona fide students enrolled at least half time in any recognized school, training program, or institution of higher education.
