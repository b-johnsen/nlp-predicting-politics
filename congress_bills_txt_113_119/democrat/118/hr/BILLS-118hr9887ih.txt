1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as
Native American Seeds Act of 2024
.
2.
Definitions
In this Act:
(1)
Indian Tribe
The term
Indian Tribe
has the meaning given the term in section 4 of the Indian Self-Determination and Education Assistance Act (
25 U.S.C. 5304
).
(2)
Native American seed
The term
Native American seed
means a seed of traditional or cultural significance to an Indian Tribe.
(3)
Secretary
The term
Secretary
means the Secretary of the Interior.
