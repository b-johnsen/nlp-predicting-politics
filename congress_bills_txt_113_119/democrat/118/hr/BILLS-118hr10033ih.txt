1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Expanding Clean Fuel Production Act of 2024
.
2.
Extension of clean fuel production credit
(a)
In general
Section 45Z(g)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking
December 31, 2027
and inserting
December 31, 2037
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by this section shall apply to fuel sold after December 31, 2027.
