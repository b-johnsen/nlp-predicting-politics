1.
Reaffirmation of law
The applicability of the Act of June 18, 1934 (
25 U.S.C. 5101 et seq.
; 48 Stat. 984, chapter 576) (commonly known as the
Indian Reorganization Act
) is reaffirmed for the Samish Indian Nation.
2.
No impact on treaty rights
Nothing in this Act shall be interpreted as affecting treaty rights under the Treaty of Point Elliott.
