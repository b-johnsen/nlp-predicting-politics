1.
Mayor Rob Gordan Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 108 North Main Street in Bucoda, Washington, shall be known and designated as the
Mayor Rob Gordan Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Mayor Rob Gordan Post Office
.
