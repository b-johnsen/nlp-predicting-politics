1.
Larry Lavagnino Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 201 East Battles Road in Santa Maria, California, shall be known and designated as the
Larry Lavagnino Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Larry Lavagnino Post Office Building
.
