1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Removing Access Barriers to Running for Elected Office for People with Disabilities Act
.
2.
Disregarding campaign work performed by individuals with disabilities for purposes of Federally supported benefits
(a)
In general
Any campaign work performed by a qualified individual, and any compensation received by such individual for such work, shall be disregarded for the purpose of determining such individual's eligibility for, and amount of, benefits or assistance under any Federal program or under any State or local program financed in whole or in part with Federal funds.
(b)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)
Campaign work
The term
campaign work
means work performed by an individual as a candidate for an elected Federal, State, or local office.
(2)
Qualified individual
The term
qualified individual
means an individual who is entitled to a benefit under title II or XVI of the Social Security Act (
42 U.S.C. 401 et seq.
, 1381 et seq.) by reason of a disability.
