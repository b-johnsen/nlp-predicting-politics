(b)
Rulemaking
The Secretary of Housing and Urban Development shall, not later than 90 days after the date on which the amendments made by subsection (a) take effect, issue such rules and guidance as the Secretary determines necessary to carry out section 8(o)(13)(B)(ii)(II) of the United States Housing Act of 1937, as added by subsection (a).
(c)
Effective date
The amendments made by subsection (a) shall take effect beginning in the first fiscal year that begins after the date of the enactment of this Act.
