Compensation
(A)
Director
The Director shall be compensated at the rate now or hereafter provided for level II of the Executive Schedule under section 5313 of title 5, United States Code.

Associate Director
The Associate Director shall be compensated at a rate not exceeding the rate now or hereafter provided for level III of the Executive Schedule under section 5314 of title 5, United States Code.

Young American
The term
