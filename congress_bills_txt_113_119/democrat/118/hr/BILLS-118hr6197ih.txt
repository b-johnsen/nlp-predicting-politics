1.
Department of Veterans Affairs program to provide grants to certain surviving spouses of veterans to pay for housing
(a)
In general
Not later than one year after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Secretary of Veterans Affairs shall establish a program to provide grants to eligible surviving spouses to pay for housing.
(b)
Applications
An eligible surviving spouse desiring a grant under this section shall submit to the Secretary an application in such form and containing such information and assurances as the Secretary determines appropriate.
(c)
Priority
In making grants under this section, the Secretary shall give priority to eligible surviving spouses on the basis of need.
(d)
Authorization of appropriations
There are authorized to be appropriated to the Secretary $250,000,000 for fiscal years 2025 through 2029 to carry out this section.
