1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Antisemitism Inclusion in DEI Act of 2023
.
2.
Disclosure of diversity, equity, and inclusion program to combat antisemitism
Section 13 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 (
15 U.S.C. 78m
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(t)
Disclosure of diversity, equity, and inclusion program To combat antisemitism
Each issuer required to file an annual report under subsection (a) shall disclose in that report if the issuer has a diversity, equity, and inclusion program that includes any initiative and training to combat antisemitism.
.
