1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Senator Dianne Feinstein Memorial Trail Act
.
2.
Senator Dianne Feinstein Memorial Trail
(a)
In general
The Elk River Trail in Headwaters Forest Reserve, California, shall hereafter be known and designated as the
Senator Dianne Feinstein Memorial Trail
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the Elk River Trail in Headwaters Forest Reserve shall be deemed to be a reference to the Senator Dianne Feinstein Memorial Trail.
