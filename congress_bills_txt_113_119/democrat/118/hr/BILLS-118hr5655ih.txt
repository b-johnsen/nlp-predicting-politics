(C)
Qualified personal locator beacon
The term
qualified personal locator beacon
means a device designed to be carried by an individual thatâ
(i)
transmits distress signals at a frequency between 406.0 and 406.1 MHz,
(ii)
is manufactured by a company approved to manufacture beacons by the International Cospas-Sarsat Programme,

(iv)
is acquired for use on commercial fishing vessels by the taxpayer who owns or operates an eligible vessel.
(D)
Qualified vessel
The term

means an eligible vessel which is equipped with at least 1 qualified personal locator beacon for each individual on board such vessel.
(2)

The term

(3)
Eligible vessel
The term
