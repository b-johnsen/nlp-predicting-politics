(2)
Maximum stipends for fiscal year 2025 and each succeeding fiscal year
(A)
Fiscal year 2025
In determining maximum stipend amounts with respect to any project assisted under this section for fiscal year 2025, each of the dollar amounts referenced in paragraph (1) for fiscal year 2024 shall be increased by a percentage equal to the estimated percentage change increase in the Consumer Price Index (as determined by the Secretary, using the definition in section 478(f)), and rounding the result to the nearest $10.
(B)
Fiscal year 2026 and each succeeding fiscal year
In determining maximum stipend amounts with respect to any project assisted under this section for fiscal year 2026 and each succeeding fiscal year, each of the dollar amounts determined under this paragraph for the preceding fiscal year shall be increased by a percentage equal to the estimated percentage change increase in the Consumer Price Index (as determined by the Secretary, using the definition in section 478(f)), and rounding the result to the nearest $10.
