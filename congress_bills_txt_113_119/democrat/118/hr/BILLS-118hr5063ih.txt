1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Small Business Contracting Credit Act
.
2.
Extension of double credit for Puerto Rico businesses and covered territory businesses
Section 15(x)(1) of the Small Business Act (
15 U.S.C. 644(x)(1)
) is amended by striking
during the period beginning on the date of enactment of this subsection and ending on the date that is 4 years after such date of enactment
and inserting the following:
during the period beginning on the date that is 90 days after the date of the enactment of the Small Business Contracting Credit Act and ending on the date that is 3 years after the date of the enactment of such Act
.
