(b)
Modification of prior contracts
Under subsection (a) of 3702 of title 10, United States Code, in connection with a prime contract with respect to which such subsection applies and that was entered into before the date of the enactment of this Act, the head of the agency that entered into such contract shall modify the contract as soon as practicable to reflect paragraphs (2) and (3) of such subsection, as amended by subsection (a) of this Act, without requiring consideration.
(c)
Applicability
The amendments made by subsection (a) of this Act to paragraph (1) of section 3702(a) of title 10, United States Code, shall apply only with respect to contracts entered into after the date of the enactment of this Act.
