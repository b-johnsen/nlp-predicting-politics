1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Ban All Neurotoxic Organophosphate Pesticides From Our Food Act
or the
BAN OPs From Our Food Act
.
2.
Prohibition on use of organophosphates on food
Section 402 of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (
21 U.S.C. 342
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(j)
If it bears or contains any organophosphate pesticide, including any residue of an organophosphate pesticide, or any other added substance the presence of which is primarily as a result of the metabolism or other degradation of an organophosphate pesticide, regardless of whether any tolerance or exemption with respect to organophosphate is in effect under section 408.
.
