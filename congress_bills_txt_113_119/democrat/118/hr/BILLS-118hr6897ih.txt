1.
Domestic synthetic biology pharmaceutical manufacturing pilot program
(a)
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Synthetic Biology and Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients Act
.
(b)
In general
Not later than January 1, 2025, the Secretary of Defense shall establish a pilot program to evaluate the use of novel technologies using synthetic biology to expand domestic manufacturing of covered active pharmaceutical ingredients and covered key starting materials to reduce the reliance of the Department of Defense on foreign countries for such covered active pharmaceutical ingredients and covered key starting materials.
(c)
Sunset
The pilot program established under subsection (a) shall terminate on the date that is three years after the date of the enactment of this Act.
