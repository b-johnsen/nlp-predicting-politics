CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
Authorizing the use of Emancipation Hall in the Capitol Visitor Center for the 12th Annual Fallen Firefighters Congressional Flag Presentation Ceremony.
1.
Use of Emancipation Hall for 12th Annual Fallen Firefighters Congressional Flag Presentation Ceremony
(a)
Authorization
Emancipation Hall in the Capitol Visitor Center is authorized to be used on April 10, 2024, for the 12th Annual Fallen Firefighters Congressional Flag Presentation Ceremony, in order to honor firefighters who died in the line of duty and will be included on the 2024 Roll of Honor of the National Fallen Firefighters Memorial.
(b)
Preparations
Physical preparations for the conduct of the ceremony described in subsection (a) shall be carried out in accordance with such conditions as the Architect of the Capitol may prescribe.
