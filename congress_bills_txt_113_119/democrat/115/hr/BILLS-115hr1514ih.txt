1.
Prohibition on salary changes for the Governor, Lieutenant Governor, and Senator of the Guam
Legislature
Section 6 of the Organic Act of Guam (
48 U.S.C. 1422
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
No law varying the compensation for the services of the Governor of Guam, the Lieutenant Governor of Guam, or Senators in the Guam Legislature shall take effect until a General Election of the Guam Legislature shall have intervened.
