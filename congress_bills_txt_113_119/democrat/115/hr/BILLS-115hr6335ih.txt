1.
Veterans Memorial Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 322 Main Street in Oakville, Connecticut, shall be known and designated as the
Veterans Memorial Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Veterans Memorial Post Office
.
