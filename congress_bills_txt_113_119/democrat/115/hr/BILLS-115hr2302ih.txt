1.
Dr. John F. Nash, Jr. Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 259 Nassau Street, Suite 2 in Princeton, New Jersey, shall be known and designated as the
Dr. John F. Nash, Jr. Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Dr. John F. Nash, Jr. Post Office
.
