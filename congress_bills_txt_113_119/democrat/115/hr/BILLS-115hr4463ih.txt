1.
Mabel Lee Memorial Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 6 Doyers Street in New York, New York, shall be known and designated as the
Mabel Lee Memorial Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Mabel Lee Memorial Post Office
.
