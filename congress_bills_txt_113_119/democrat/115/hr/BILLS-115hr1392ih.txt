1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
TechCorps Act
.
2.
OPM database of TechCorps-eligible positions
(a)
In general
Not later than 60 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Director of the Office of Personnel Management shall establish and maintain an electronic database of job vacancies across the Government relating to information technology. The Director shall consult with each Federal agency in establishing and periodically updating the database.
(b)
Publication
The database of vacant positions described under subsection (a) shall be published and available on the USA Jobs Internet Web site, and each position in the database shall be described on such Web site as a
TechCorps-eligible position
.
