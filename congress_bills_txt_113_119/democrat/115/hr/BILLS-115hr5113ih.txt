Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Federal Contractor Anti-Harassment Training Act of 2018
.

In this section, the term

The table of sections at the beginning of such chapter is amended by adding at the end the following new item:
6310. Sexual harassment training for employees of Federal contractors.
.
(b)
Applicability
The amendment made by subsection (a)(1) shall apply with respect to contracts awarded on or after the date that is 90 days after the date of the enactment of this Act.
