1.
Requirement to release assassination records
(a)
Determination
Not later than 2 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the President shall make a determination about whether to release any assassination record (in whole or in part) that was not released on October 26, 2017.
(b)
Records released
Those records determined to be released under subsection (a) shall be released not later than 2 days after such determination.
