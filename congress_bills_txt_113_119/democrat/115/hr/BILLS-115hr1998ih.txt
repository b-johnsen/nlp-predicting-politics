1.
Notification of termination or expiration of a HUD-VASH contract
(a)
Notification
Not later than 90 days before the date on which a HUD-VASH contract terminates or expires, the Secretary of Veterans Affairs shall notify the relevant committees and Members of Congress of such termination or expiration.
(b)
Plan
Not later than 60 days after the date of a notification under subsection (a), the Secretary shall notify the relevant committees and Members of Congress of the plan of the Secretary to provide assistance to veterans served under such contract.
(c)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)

HUD-VASH contract

42 U.S.C. 1437f(o)(19)
).

The term
Member of Congress
