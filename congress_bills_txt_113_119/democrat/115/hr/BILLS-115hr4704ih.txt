3.
Loan fund
(a)
Establishment
The Secretary of Health and Human Services shall, subject to subsection (e), establish a loan program that provides loans to qualifying nursing facilities and skilled nursing facilities for costs relating to coming into compliance with the requirement of paragraph (4) of section 1819(a) of the Social Security Act (
42 U.S.C. 1395i芒聙聯3(a)
) and paragraph (4) of section 1919(a) of such Act (
42 U.S.C. 1396r(a)
), as added by section 2.
(b)
Application
No loan may be provided under this section to a qualifying nursing facility or skilled nursing facility, except pursuant to an application that is submitted and approved in a time, manner, and form specified by the Secretary. A loan under this section shall be on such terms and conditions and meet such requirements as the Secretary determines appropriate.
(c)
Selection
(1)
In general
The Secretary shall establish criteria for selecting among qualifying nursing facilities and skilled nursing facilities that apply for a loan under this section.

Qualifying facilities

(d)
Fees
