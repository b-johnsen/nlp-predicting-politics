Minimum number of refugees to be admitted
Section 207(a) of the Immigration and Nationality Act (
8 U.S.C. 1157(a)
) is amended by inserting after
in any fiscal year after fiscal year 1982 shall be such number as the President determines,
the following:
except that in any fiscal year after fiscal year 2018, such number may not be less than 110,000
