4.
Report on status of Jamal Khashoggi
(a)
In general
Not later than 30 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Secretary of State shall submit to the appropriate congressional committees a report on the status of Jamal Khashoggi, the progress and findings of the United States investigation into his disappearance and its aftermath, and the role of the Government of Saudi Arabia and its agents and instrumentalities in such status, disappearance, and aftermath.
(b)
Form
The report required by
subsection (a)
shall be submitted in unclassified form but may contain a classified annex.
