1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
MadeInAmerica.gov Act
.
2.
Made in America website
(a)
Website creation
Not later than 2 years after the date of enactment of this Act, the Secretary of Commerce shall create and maintain a
MadeInAmerica.gov
website where consumers can find information about products that are made in America.
(b)
Regulations
The Secretary shall issue such regulations as are necessary to implement this Act.
