1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Federal Infrastructure Flood Resiliency Act of 2017
.
2.
Legal effect
Notwithstanding any other provision of law or Executive order, Executive Order 13690 (80 Fed. Reg. 6425), signed on January 30, 2015, and entitled
Establishing a Federal Flood Risk Management Standard and a Process for Further Soliciting and Considering Stakeholder Input
shall have the force and effect of law.
