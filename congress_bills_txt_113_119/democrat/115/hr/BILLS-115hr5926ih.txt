1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Naloxone Guidelines Act of 2018
.
2.
Guidelines for prescribing naloxone
(a)
In general
Not later than 180 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Secretary of Health and Human Services shall issue guidelines for prescribing an opioid overdose reversal drug.
(b)
Contents
In issuing guidelines under subsection (a), the Secretary shall address the following:
(1)
Co-prescribing an opioid overdose reversal drug in conjunction with any prescribed opioid.
(2)
Dosage safety.
(3)
Prescribing an opioid overdose reversal drug to an individual other than a patient.
(4)
Standing orders.
(5)
Other distribution, education, and safety measures as determined necessary.
