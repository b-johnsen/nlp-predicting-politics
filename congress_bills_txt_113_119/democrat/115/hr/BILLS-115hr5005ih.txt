1.
Special resource study of James Weldon Johnson birthplace
(a)
Study
The Secretary of the Interior shall conduct a special resource study to determine the suitability and feasibility of establishing the birthplace of James Weldon Johnson located at Adams & Lee Street in Jacksonville, Florida, as a unit of the National Park System.
(b)
Study requirements
The Secretary shall conduct the study in accordance with section 100507 of title 54, United States Code.

Report
