1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Promoting Cybersecurity for Rural Electric Utilities Act
.
2.
Cybersecurity and grid security improvements
Title III of the Rural Electrification Act of 1936 (
7 U.S.C. 931
et seq.) is amended by adding at the end the following:
319.
Cybersecurity and grid security improvements
The Secretary may make or guarantee loans under this title and title I for cybersecurity and grid security improvements.
.
