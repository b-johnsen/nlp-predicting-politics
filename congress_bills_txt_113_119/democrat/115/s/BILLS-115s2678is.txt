(b)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)
The term
recovery housing
means a family-like, shared living environment free from alcohol and illicit drug use and centered on peer support and connection to services that promote sustained recovery from substance use disorders.
(2)
The term
State
includes any of the several States, the District of Columbia, and any territory or possession of the United States.
