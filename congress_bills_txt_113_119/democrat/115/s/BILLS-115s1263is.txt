1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Atlantic Seismic Airgun Protection Act
.
2.
Moratorium on oil-, gas-, and methane hydrate-related seismic activities in North Atlantic,
Mid-Atlantic,
South Atlantic, and Straits of Florida planning areas of outer
Continental Shelf
Section 11 of the Outer Continental Shelf Lands Act (
43 U.S.C. 1340
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(i)
Moratorium on oil-, gas-, and methane hydrate-Related seismic activities in North Atlantic,
Mid-Atlantic, South
Atlantic, and Straits of Florida planning areas
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, no person or agency of the United States may conduct geological or geophysical activities (as those terms are described in the final programmatic environmental impact statement of the Bureau of Ocean Energy Management entitled
Atlantic OCS Proposed Geological and Geophysical Activities, Mid-Atlantic and South Atlantic Planning Areas
and completed February 2014) in support of oil, gas, or methane hydrate exploration and development in any area located in the North Atlantic, Mid-Atlantic, South Atlantic, or Straits of Florida planning areas of the outer Continental Shelf.
.
