1.
Certain career training expenses treated as qualified higher education expenses
(a)
In general
Paragraph (3) of
section 529(e)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by adding at the end the following new subparagraph:
(C)
Certain career training expenses
(i)
In general
In the case of an individual who is enrolled in or attending a program to obtain a recognized postsecondary credential or occupational license, the term
qualified higher education expenses
includes expenses similar to the expenses described in subparagraph (A) which are required for such program.
(ii)
Program to obtain a recognized postsecondary credential

), and
(II)
when used with respect to obtaining such a credential, the term
program
means only a program which is included, and is offered by a provider which is included, on the list described in section 122(d) of the Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act (
29 U.S.C. 3152(d)
).
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by this section shall apply to expenses paid or incurred in taxable years beginning after the date of the enactment of this Act.
