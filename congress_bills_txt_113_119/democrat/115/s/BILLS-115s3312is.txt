3.
Suspension of proposed rulemaking by Pruitt
(a)
In general
The Administrator of the Agency shall not finalize or engage in any rulemaking with respect to a rule for which the advance notice of proposed rulemaking or the notice of proposed rulemaking was signed by Scott Pruitt until the date on which the report described in subsection (b) becomes publicly available.
(b)
Report
On completion of the audits, reviews, investigations, and probe described in section 2, the Inspector General of the EPA OIG shall make publicly available a report that describes the findings and conclusions of each audit, review, investigation, and probe.
