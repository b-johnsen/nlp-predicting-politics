1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Intelligence Budget Transparency Act of 2018
.
2.
Funding for intelligence activities of each element of the Government
Section 1105(a) of title 31, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
(40)
(A)
the total dollar amount proposed in the budget for intelligence or intelligence-related activities of each element of the Government engaged in such activities in the fiscal year for which the budget is submitted and the estimated amount required to be appropriated for each of the following 4 fiscal years.
(B)
In subparagraph (A), the term
element of the Government
means each element of the intelligence community (as defined in section 3(4) of the National Security Act of 1947 (
50 U.S.C. 3003(4)
)).
.
