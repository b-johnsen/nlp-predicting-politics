RESOLUTION
Amending the Rules of the House of Representatives to observe a 60-second moment of silence in the House for those killed by gun violence in the United States on the next legislative day after any such individual dies.
That rule XIV of the Rules of the House of Representatives is amended by adding at the end the following new clause:
7.
There shall be a 60-second moment of silence in the House for victims of firearm homicide in the United States on the next legislative day after any such individual dies.
