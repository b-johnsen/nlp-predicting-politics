RESOLUTION
Amending rule XXVIII of the Standing Rules of the Senate to provide for timely establishment of
conference committees.
1.
Motions to go to conference
Rule XXVIII of the Standing Rules of the Senate is amended by striking paragraph 2(b) and inserting the following:
(b)
Consideration of a motion described in subparagraph (a), including consideration of any debatable motion or appeal in connection therewith, shall be limited to not more than 2 hours.
