RESOLUTION
Commending the bravery of the United States Capitol Police, the Police Department of Alexandria,
Virginia, and all
first responders who protected Members of Congress, their staff, and
others during the shooting on June 14, 2017, at Eugene Simpson Stadium
Park
in the Del Ray neighborhood of Alexandria, Virginia.

recognizes the Police Department, the Office of the Sheriff, and the Fire Department of Alexandria, Virginia, who reported to the scene without hesitation, and the first responders who treated the wounded;
