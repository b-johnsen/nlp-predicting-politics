RESOLUTION
Designating the week of November 19 through November 25, 2017, during which the holiday of
Thanksgiving is observed, as
National Family Week
.
Whereas the family is the basic strength of any free and orderly society;
Whereas it is appropriate to honor the family unit as essential to the continued well-being of the United States; and
Whereas it is fitting that official recognition be given to the importance of family loyalties and ties: Now, therefore, be it
That the Senateâ
(1)
designates the week of November 19 through November 25, 2017, during which the holiday of Thanksgiving is observed, as
National Family Week
;
(2)
encourages States and local governments to designate the week of November 19 through November 25, 2017, as
National Family Week
; and
(3)
encourages the people of the United States to observe
National Family Week
with appropriate ceremonies and activities.
