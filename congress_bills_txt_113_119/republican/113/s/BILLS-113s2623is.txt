1.
Prohibition on land management modifications relating to Lesser Prairie Chicken
Notwithstanding any other provision of law (including regulations), the Secretary of Agriculture
and the Secretary of the Interior shall not implement or limit any
modification
to a public or private land-related policy or subsurface mineral
right-related policy or practice that is in effect on the date of
enactment of
this Act relating to the listing of the Lesser Prairie Chicken as a
threatened species or endangered species under the Endangered Species Act
of 1973 (
16 U.S.C. 1531 et seq.
).
