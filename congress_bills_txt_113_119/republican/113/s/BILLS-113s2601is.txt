Residual interest requirement
Any rules or regulations requiring a futures commission merchant to maintain a residual interest in
accounts held for the benefit of customers in amounts at least sufficient
to exceed the sum of all uncollected margin deficits of the customers
shall provide that a futures commission merchant shall meet the residual
interest requirement as of the end of each business day calculated as of
the close of business on the previous business day.
