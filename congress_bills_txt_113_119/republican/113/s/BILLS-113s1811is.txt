1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Commercial Flight Courtesy Act
.
2.
Limitation on mobile communications devices
(a)
In general
Subchapter I of
chapter 417
of title 49, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
41725.
Limitation on mobile communications devices
(a)
In general
Except as provided in subsection (b), an individual may not engage in voice communications through a mobile communication device while onboard an aircraft in scheduled passenger interstate or intrastate air transportation.
(b)
Exceptions

individuals traveling on aircraft operated by a foreign carrier unless such travel is between airports located within the United States;
(2)

(3)
the use of in-flight phones that are affixed to the aircraft; or
(4)
chartered or private flights.
.
