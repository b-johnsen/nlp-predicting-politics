1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
UAC State Notification Act of 2014
.
2.
Notification of State Governors
Section 235 of the William Wilberforce Trafficking Victims Protection Reauthorization Act of 2008
(
8 U.S.C. 1232
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(j)
Notification to States
The Secretary of Homeland Security or the Secretary of Health and Human Services shall notify the
Governor of a State not later than 48 hours prior to the transfer of an
unaccompanied alien child in the custody of such Secretary to such State.
.
