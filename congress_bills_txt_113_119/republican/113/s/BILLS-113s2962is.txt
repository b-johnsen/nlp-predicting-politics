1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Medical Expense Deduction Act
.
2.
Repeal of increase in minimum deduction for medical, dental, etc., expenses
(a)
In general
Subsection (a) of
section 213
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking
10 percent
and inserting
7.5 percent
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by this section shall apply to taxable years beginning after December 31, 2014.
