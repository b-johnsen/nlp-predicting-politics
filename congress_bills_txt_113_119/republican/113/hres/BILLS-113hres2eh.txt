IV
113th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 2
In the House of Representatives, U. S.,
January 3, 2013
RESOLUTION
That the Senate be informed that a quorum of the House of Representatives has assembled; that John A. Boehner, a Representative from the State of Ohio, has been elected Speaker; and that Karen L. Haas, a citizen of the State of Maryland, has been elected Clerk of the House of Representatives of the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress.
Karen L. Haas,
Clerk.
