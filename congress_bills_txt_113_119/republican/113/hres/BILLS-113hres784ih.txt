Committee on Oversight and Government Reform
RESOLUTION
Designating December 20 of each year as a national day of remembrance for victims of anti-police violence.
Whereas, on December 20, 2014, NYPD officers Wenjian Liu and Rafael Ramos were shot and killed in an anti-police attack.
That the House of Representatives supports the designation of a day of remembrance for victims of anti-police violence.
