JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for the approval of the Congress of the proposed agreement for cooperation between the
American Institute in Taiwan and the Taipei Economic and Cultural
Representative Office in the United States concerning peaceful uses of
nuclear energy pursuant to the Atomic Energy Act of 1954.
That the Congress does favor the proposed agreement for cooperation transmitted to the Congress by
the President on January 7, 2014.
