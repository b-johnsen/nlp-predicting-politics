IV
113th CONGRESS
2d Session
H. CON. RES. 123
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
Directing the Clerk of the House of Representatives to make a correction in the enrollment of the bill H.R. 3979.
That, in the enrollment of the bill H.R. 3979, the Clerk of the House of Representatives shall make the following correction: In section 1207(e)(2), strike
categories I, II, III, VII, and X
and insert
categories I, II, III, VII, X, XI, and XIII
.
Passed the House of Representatives December 10, 2014.
Karen L. Haas,
Clerk.
