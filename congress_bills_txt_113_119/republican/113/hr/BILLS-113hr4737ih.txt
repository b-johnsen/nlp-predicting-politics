1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
State Fisheries Act of 2014
.
2.
State jurisdiction over fisheries in the Gulf of Mexico
Section 306(b) (
16 U.S.C. 1856(b)
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(3)
Notwithstanding section 3(11) and subsection (a) of this section, for purposes of managing
fisheries in the Gulf of Mexico, the seaward boundary of a coastal State
in the Gulf of Mexico is a line 9 nautical miles seaward from the baseline
from which the territorial sea of the United States is measured.
.
