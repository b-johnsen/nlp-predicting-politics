1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Sandy Reinvestment Extension Act of 2014
.
2.
Extension of replacement period for nonrecognition of gain for property involuntarily converted in
the Hurricane Sandy disaster area
(a)
In general
Section 1033(a)(2)(B)(i)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 shall be applied by substituting
5 years
for
2 years
with respect to property in the Hurricane Sandy disaster area which is compulsorily or involuntary
converted on or after October 29, 2012, by reason of Hurricane Sandy, but
only if substantially all of the use of the replacement property is in
such area.
(b)
Hurricane Sandy disaster area
For purposes of this section, the term
Hurricane Sandy disaster area
means an area with respect to which a major disaster has been declared by the President under
section 401 of the Robert T. Stafford Disaster Relief and Emergency
Assistance Act by reason of Hurricane Sandy and determined by the
President to warrant individual or individual and public assistance from
the Federal Government under such Act with respect to damages attributable
to Hurricane Sandy.
