1.
Transfer of criminal enforcement and investigative authority and functions to the Federal Bureau of Investigation
(a)
Repeal of EPA powers
Section 3063
of title 18, United States Code, is repealed.
(b)
Transfer of authority to FBI
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, any criminal enforcement authority and functions and any criminal investigative authority and functions of the Environmental Protection Agency shall be transferred to the Director of the Federal Bureau of Investigation, except for any criminal enforcement authority and functions and any criminal investigative authority and functions delegated to State or local law enforcement.
