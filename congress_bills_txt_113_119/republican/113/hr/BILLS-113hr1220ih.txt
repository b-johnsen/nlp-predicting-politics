Rule of

Nothing in this
Act shall be construed to limit or otherwise affect the authority of the Food
and Drug Administration under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (21
U.S.C. 301 et seq.) to ensure the safety and effectiveness of CFC epinephrine
inhalers to be distributed, sold, or consumed pursuant to this Act.
