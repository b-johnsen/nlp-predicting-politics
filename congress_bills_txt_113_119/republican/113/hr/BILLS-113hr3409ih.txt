1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
National Wildlife Refuge Expansion Limitation Act of 2013
.
2.
Prohibition on expansion of national wildlife refuges
Section 4(a) of the National Wildlife Refuge System Administration Act of 1966 (
16 U.S.C. 668dd(a)
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(6)
The Secretary may not expand any national wildlife refuge except as expressly authorized by a law enacted after January 3, 2013.
.
