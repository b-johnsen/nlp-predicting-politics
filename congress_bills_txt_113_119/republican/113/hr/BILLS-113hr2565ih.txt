1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Stop Targeting Our Politics IRS
Act
or as the
STOP IRS Act
.
2.
Termination of
employment of Internal Revenue Service employees for taking official actions
for political purposes
Paragraph (10) of section 1203(b) of the
Internal Revenue Service Restructuring and Reform Act of 1998 is amended to
read as follows:
(10)
performing, delaying, or failing to perform
(or threatening to perform, delay, or fail to perform) any official action
(including any audit) with respect to a taxpayer for purpose of extracting
personal gain or benefit or for a political
purpose.
.
