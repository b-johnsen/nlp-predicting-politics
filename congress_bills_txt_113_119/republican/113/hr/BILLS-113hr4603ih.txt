1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Independent Counsel Reauthorization Act of 2014
.
2.
Reauthorization of
chapter 40
of title 28, United States Code
Section 599
of title 28, United States Code, is amended by striking
This chapter shall cease to be effective five years after the date of the enactment of the Independent Counsel Reauthorization Act of 1994
and inserting
This chapter shall have effect during the period beginning on the date of the enactment of the
Independent Counsel Reauthorization Act of 2014
and ending on the day 5 years after such date
.
