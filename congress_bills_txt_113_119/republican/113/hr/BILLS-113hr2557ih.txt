1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Imprisonment for Tax Targeting of
Americans Act of 2013
.
2.
Mandatory fines,
imprisonment, and costs of prosecution for unauthorized disclosure of returns
and return information
(a)
Federal
employees and other persons
The second sentence of section 7213(a)(1)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking
a
fine
and all that follows through
or both
and inserting
a fine in accordance with title 18, United States Code, and imprisonment
of 5 years
.
(b)
State and other
employees
The second sentence
of section 7213(a)(2) of such Code is amended by striking
a fine
and all that follows through
or both
and inserting
a fine
in accordance with title 18, United States Code, and imprisonment of 5
years
.
(c)
Other
persons
The second sentence
of section 7213(a)(3) of such Code is amended by striking
a fine
and all that follows through
or both
and inserting
a fine
in accordance with title 18, United States Code, and imprisonment of 5
years
.
(d)
Solicitation
The second sentence of section 7213(a)(4)
of such Code is amended by striking
a fine
and all that follows
through
or both
and inserting
a fine in accordance with
title 18, United States Code, and imprisonment of 5 years
.
(e)
Effective

The amendments made by this section shall apply to
violations occurring after the date of the enactment of this Act.
