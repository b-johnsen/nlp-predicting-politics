1.
Specialist Keith Erin Grace, Jr. Memorial Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 601 West Baker Road in Baytown, Texas,
shall be known as the
Specialist Keith Erin Grace, Jr. Memorial Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to
the facility referenced to subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a
reference to the
Specialist Keith Erin Grace, Jr. Memorial Post Office
.
