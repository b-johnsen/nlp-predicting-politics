1.
Establishment of chain of
command for Army National Military Cemeteries
(a)
Military chain
of command required
The
Secretary of the Army shall establish a chain of command for the Army National
Military Cemeteries, to include a military commander of the Army National
Military Cemeteries to replace the current civilian director upon the
termination of the tenure of the director.

amendment
