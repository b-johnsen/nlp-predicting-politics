1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Fiduciary Responsibility and Fairness Act of 2013
.
2.
Exemption for expenditures or obligations of funds derived from user fees
(a)
In general
Section 1341(a)
of title 31, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
(3)
This subsection does not apply in the case of any expenditure or obligation of funds derived from user fees and authorized to be appropriated for such purpose.
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by subsection (a) shall apply with respect to expenditures and obligations made or authorized after the date of the enactment of this Act.
