In general
Section 182 b. of the Atomic Energy Act of 1954 (

) is amended by striking

(1)

The Advisory Committee
.
(2)
Limitation on review
Section 182 of the Atomic Energy Act of 1954 (
42 U.S.C. 2232
) is amended by inserting at the end of subsection b., as amended by paragraph (1), the following:
(2)
Limitation on review
With respect to the applications described in paragraph (1), the Advisory Committee on Reactor Safeguards may only review such applications that present a novel issue or an issue of significant safety concern, based on a risk-informed determination by the Committee.
.
