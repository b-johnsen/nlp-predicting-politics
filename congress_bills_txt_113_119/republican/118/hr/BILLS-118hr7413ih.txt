247.
Ongoing studies of State programs for poll worker recruiting and safety training
(a)
Studies; voluntary considerations
On an ongoing basis, the Commission shall conduct studies of State programs for recruiting poll workers and providing poll workers with safety training in order to identify voluntary considerations that may be used by States to carry out such programs.
(b)
Reports
Not later than December 31, 2024, and every two years thereafter, the Commission shall submit to the States and publish a report on the most recent study conducted under this section, and shall include in the report a description of the voluntary considerations for carrying out the programs studied in the report.
(c)
Poll worker defined
For purposes of this section, the term
poll worker
means any individual who, pursuant to the laws of a State, acts in a nonpartisan capacity as an employee or representative of a unit of the State or local government to aid in the administration of an election at a polling place in the State.
.
