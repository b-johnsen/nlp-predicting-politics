3.
Update of independent intra-agency appellate process for reviewing material supervisory determinations
(a)
Update of independent intra-Agency appellate process
Not later than 180 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System, the Comptroller of the Currency, the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation Board, and the National Credit Union Administration Board shall revise the independent intra-agency appellate process required under section 309(a) of the Riegle Community Development and Regulatory Improvement Act of 1994 (
12 U.S.C. 4806(a)
).

(9)

With respect to the appeal of a material supervisory determination under this subsection:
