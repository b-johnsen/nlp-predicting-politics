1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Lower Grocery Prices Act
.
2.
GAO study on grocery costs to U.S. consumers
(a)
Study
The Comptroller General of the United States shall conduct a study on the changes in the Consumer Price Index for food at home for the 20-year period ending on the date of the enactment of this Act, including such other relevant economic metrics as may be necessary to assess the impact of food at home costs on United States consumers.
