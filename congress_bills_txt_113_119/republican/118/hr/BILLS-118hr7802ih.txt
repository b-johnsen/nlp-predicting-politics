1.
Jimmy Buffett Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 400 Whitehead Street in Key West, Florida, shall be known and designated as the
Jimmy Buffett Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Jimmy Buffett Post Office Building
.
