1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
No IDs for Illegals Act
.
2.
No Federal funds for ICE Secure Docket Card Program
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, no Federal funds may be used to establish, implement, or otherwise support the activities of the U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement Secure Docket Card Program, or any other similar program of the Department of Homeland Security.
