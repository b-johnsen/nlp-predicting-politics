(b)
Briefing required
(1)
Briefing
Not later than 120 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Director of National Intelligence shall provide to the appropriate committees of Congress a briefing on the information declassified and described under subsection (a).
(2)
Requirement
The briefing under paragraph (1) shall be conducted jointly with the Secretary of Homeland Security.
