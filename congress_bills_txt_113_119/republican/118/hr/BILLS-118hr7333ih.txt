1.
Name of Department of Veterans Affairs medical center, West Palm Beach, Florida
The Department of Veterans Affairs medical center in West Palm Beach, Florida, shall after the date of the enactment of this Act be known and designated as the
Thomas H. Corey VA Medical Center
. Any reference to such medical center in any law, regulation, map, document, record, or other paper of the United States shall be considered to be a reference to the Thomas H. Corey VA Medical Center.
