1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Farm Equipment Safety Act
.
2.
Exemption of nonroad farm engines and vehicles from Clean Air Act emission standards
Section 213 of the Clean Air Act (
42 U.S.C. 7547
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(e)
Exemption
The standards under this section do not apply to emissions from nonroad engines and nonroad vehicles that are used for agricultural purposes.
.
