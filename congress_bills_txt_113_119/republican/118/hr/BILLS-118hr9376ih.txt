2.
Wireless emergency alerts
(a)
Definition
In this section, the term
Alert Message
has the meaning given the term in section 10.10(a) of title 47, Code of Federal Regulations, or any successor regulation.
(b)
Requirement
Not later than 60 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Federal Communications Commission shall amend part 10 of title 47, Code of Federal Regulations, or any successor regulations, to provide that a shark attack is an event for which an Alert Message may be transmitted under that part (or successor, if applicable).
