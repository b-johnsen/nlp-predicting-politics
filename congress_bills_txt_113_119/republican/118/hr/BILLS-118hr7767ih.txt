1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Safeguarding Tactics and Operational Procedures from Enemies Act of 2024
or the
STOP Enemies Act of 2024
.
2.
Aiding the enemy definition for purposes of the Uniform Code of Military Justice
Section 903b(2) of title 10, United States Code (article 103b(2) of the Uniform Code of Military Justice) is amended by inserting
provides military education, military training, or tactical advice to,
after
gives intelligence to,
.
