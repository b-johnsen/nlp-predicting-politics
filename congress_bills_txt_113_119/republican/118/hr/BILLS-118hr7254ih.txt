1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Hearing Device Coverage Clarification Act
.
2.
Active middle ear implant coverage
(a)
In general
Not later than 60 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Administrator of the Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services shall clarify that fully implanted active middle ear hearing devices are prosthetics that are not subject to the hearing aid coverage exclusion under section 1862(a)(7) of the Social Security Act (
42 U.S.C. 1395y(a)(7)
).
(b)
Prosthetic defined
The term
prosthetic
has the meaning given such term in section 414.202 of title 42, Code of Federal Regulations.
