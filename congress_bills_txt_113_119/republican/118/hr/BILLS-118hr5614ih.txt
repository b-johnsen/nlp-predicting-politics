3.
Modifications to the Harmonized Tariff Schedule of the United States
The President shall proclaim such modifications as may be necessary to Harmonized Tariff Schedule of the United States to restore such special tariff treatment to articles that lost such treatment due to prior modifications made to U.S. notes, tariff headings, subheadings or statistical suffixes in chapters 1 through 97 of the Harmonized Tariff System of the United States before the date of the enactment of this Act and remain eligible for such tariff treatment due to such section 213A as so amended.
