Prohibition on the use of single-sex facilities on federal property other than those corresponding to the biological sex of an individual
(a)
In general
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, except as provided in subsection (c), no person may access or use a single-sex facility on Federal property, other than those corresponding to the biological sex of such person.

(2)
Federal property

(3)
Biological sex
The term
biological sex
means the biological determination as to whether an individual is male or female, as such terms are defined in this section.
(4)

The term
female
means an individual who naturally has, had, will have, or would have, but for a developmental or genetic anomaly or historical accident, the reproductive system that at some point produces, transports, and utilizes eggs for fertilization.
(5)

male
means an individual who naturally has, had, will have, or would have, but for a developmental or genetic anomaly or historical accident, the reproductive system that at some point produces, transports, and utilizes sperm for fertilization.
