1.
SGT Bill Hooser Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 20 West Main Street in Santaquin, Utah, shall be known and designated as the
SGT Bill Hooser Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
SGT Bill Hooser Post Office Building
.
