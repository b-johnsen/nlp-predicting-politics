1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
No Loan Forgiveness for Hamas Supporters Act
.
2.
Prohibition of funds
(a)
In general
Any person convicted of an unlawful activity on or after October 7, 2023, on a campus of an institution of higher education shall be ineligible for the public service loan forgiveness program under section 455(m) of the Higher Education Act of 1965 (
20 U.S.C. 1087e(m)
).
