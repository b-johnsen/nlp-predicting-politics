1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Health Care Facility Janitorial Services Classification and Cap Enhancement Act of 2024
.
2.
Separate NAICS code for health care facility janitorial services
(a)
NAICS code
Not later than 180 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Director of the Office of Management and Budget shall update the North American Industry Classification System (in this section referred to as
NAICS
) to establish a separate, unique code for the health care facility janitorial services industry.
(b)
Size standard
Not later than 90 days after the date on which an NAICS code is established under subsection (a), the Administrator of the Small Business Administration shall establish a size standard under section 3(a) of the Small Business Act (
15 U.S.C. 632(a)
) for an entity assigned such NAICS code. Such size standard may not be more than double the size standard for an entity assigned NAICS code 561720 (relating to janitorial services) on the date of enactment of this Act.
(c)
Health care facility janitorial services industry defined
In this section, the term
health care facility janitorial services industry
means an industry that comprises entities primarily engaged in cleaning interiors of health care facilities, including hospitals, outpatient facilities, clinical laboratories, or other medical facilities.
