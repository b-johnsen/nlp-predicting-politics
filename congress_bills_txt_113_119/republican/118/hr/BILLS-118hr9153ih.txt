Policy Statement
It is the policy of the United States to bring to justice the leadership of HAMAS and Palestine Islamic Jihad as well as affiliated terrorists for their role in the terrorist attack committed against Israel on October 7, 2023, and its aftermath, including the death of United States citizens and the holding of United States citizens hostage.
