1.
Colonel Joseph William Kittinger II Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 109 Live Oaks Boulevard in Casselberry, Florida, shall be known and designated as the
Colonel Joseph William Kittinger II Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Colonel Joseph William Kittinger II Post Office Building
.
