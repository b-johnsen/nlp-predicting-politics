1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Sentinel Program Risk Mitigation Act of 2023
.
2.
Form of contracting authorized to mitigate risk to Sentinel program schedule and cost
Notwithstanding section 3323 of title 10, United States Code, the Secretary of Defense may authorize the use of contracts using cost-plus incentive-fee contracting for military construction projects associated with launch facilities, launch centers, and related infrastructure of the Sentinel intercontinental ballistic missile weapon system program of the Department of Defense for not more than one low-rate initial production lot at each of the following locations:
(1)
F.E. Warren Air Force Base, Wyoming.
(2)
Malmstrom Air Force Base, Montana.
(3)
Minot Air Force Base, North Dakota.
