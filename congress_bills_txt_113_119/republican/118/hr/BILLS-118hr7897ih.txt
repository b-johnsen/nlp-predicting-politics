1.
Finalization of proposed rule to remove Apache trout from Federal List of Endangered and Threatened Wildlife
Not later than 90 days after the date of the enactment of this section, the Secretary of the Interior, acting through the Director of the United States Fish and Wildlife Service, shall finalize the proposed rule titled
Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Removing the Apache Trout From the List of Endangered and Threatened Wildlife
(88 Fed. Reg. 54548; published August 11, 2023).
