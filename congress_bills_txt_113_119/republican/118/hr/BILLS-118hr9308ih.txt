3.
Prohibition on Federal funds for covered projects
(a)
Prohibition
Except as provided in subsection (b), no Federal funds may be provided for use in any covered project.
(b)
Termination of prohibition
Subsection (a) shall cease to apply with respect to a covered project on the date on which the Secretary of Transportation certifies to the appropriate committees of Congress that at least 1 mile of rail or electromagnetic guideway has been completed under the covered project.
