Coverage of certain technologies and medical devices
(i)
In general
For plan year 2025 and subsequent plan years, in addition to any supplemental health care benefits otherwise provided under this paragraph, an MA plan, including a specialized MA plan for special needs individuals (as defined in section 1859(b)(6)), may provide the supplemental benefits described in clause (ii).
(ii)
Supplemental benefits described
(I)
In general
The supplemental benefits described in this clause are as follows:
(aa)
A medical device or technology that has a national coverage determination or local coverage determination under this title for an indication that is different than the indication for such coverage determination.
(bb)
A technology that does not fit into another benefit category under this title.
(II)
Authority to waive uniformity requirements
The Secretary may, only with respect to supplemental benefits provided under this subparagraph, waive the uniformity requirements under this part, as determined appropriate by the Secretary.
