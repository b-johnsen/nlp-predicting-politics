1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Recruiters on Campus Act
or the
ROC Act
.
2.
ESEA military recruiter access
(a)
In general
Subpart 2 of Part F of title VIII of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965 (
20 U.S.C. 7901 et seq.
) is amended by inserting after section 8528 the following:
8528A.
Military recruiter access to secondary school campuses
Each local educational agency receiving assistance under this Act shall provide military recruiters the same access to the campus of each secondary school served by the local educational agency for the purpose of recruiting students who are at least 17 years of age that is provided to any prospective employer, institution of higher education, or other recruiter.
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by this Act shall take effect 1 year after the date of enactment of this Act.
