1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Fair Assistance and Impartial Treatment of Help In Small Business Act
or the
FAITH in Small Business Act
.
2.
Ensuring equal treatment for certain faith-based organizations in certain Small Business Administration programs
The proposed rule of the Small Business Administration titled
Ensuring Equal Treatment for Faith-Based Organizations in SBA's Loan and Disaster Assistance Programs
issued January 19, 2021, shall have the force and effect of law.
