Name of Department of Veterans Affairs medical center, Tomah, Wisconsin
The Department of Veterans Affairs medical center located at 500 East Veterans Street, Tomah, Wisconsin, shall after the date of the enactment of this Act, be known and designated as the
Jason Simcakoski Department of Veterans Affairs Medical Center
. Any reference to such medical center in any law, regulation, map, document, paper, or other record of the United States shall be considered to be a reference to the Jason Simcakoski Department of Veterans Affairs Medical Center.
