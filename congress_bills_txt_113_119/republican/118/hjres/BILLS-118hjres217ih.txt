Providing for congressional disapproval under

of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services relating to
Medicare Program; FY 2025 Hospice Wage Index and Payment Rate Update, Hospice Conditions of Participation Updates, and Hospice Quality Reporting Program Requirements

That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services relating to
Medicare Program; FY 2025 Hospice Wage Index and Payment Rate Update, Hospice Conditions of Participation Updates, and Hospice Quality Reporting Program Requirements
(89 Fed. Reg. 64202 (August 6, 2024)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
