JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services and the Executive Officer for Immigration Review relating to
Circumvention of Lawful Pathways
.
That Congress disapproves the final rule submitted by U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services and the Executive Officer for Immigration Review relating to
Circumvention of Lawful Pathways
(88 Fed. Reg. 31314 (May, 16, 2023)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
