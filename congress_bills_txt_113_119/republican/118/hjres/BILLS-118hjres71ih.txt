JOINT RESOLUTION
Relating to a national emergency declared by the President on May 22, 2003.
That, pursuant to section 202 of the National Emergencies Act (
50 U.S.C. 1622
), the national emergency declared by the finding of the President on May 22, 2003, in Executive Order 13303 is hereby terminated.
