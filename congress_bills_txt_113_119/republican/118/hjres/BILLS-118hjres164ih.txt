JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Department of Commerce relating to
Revision of Firearms License Requirements
.

Revision of Firearms License Requirements
(89 Fed. Reg. 34680), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
