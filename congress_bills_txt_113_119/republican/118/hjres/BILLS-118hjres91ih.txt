Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Department of Energy relating to the
Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedure for Consumer Water Heaters and Residential-Duty Commercial Water Heaters

Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedure for Consumer Water Heaters and Residential-Duty Commercial Water Heaters
(88 Fed. Reg. 40406 (June 21, 2023)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
