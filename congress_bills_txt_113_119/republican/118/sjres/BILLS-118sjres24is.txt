JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the United States Fish and Wildlife Service relating to
Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Endangered Species Status for Northern Long-Eared Bat
.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the United States Fish and Wildlife Service relating to
Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Endangered Species Status for Northern Long-Eared Bat
(87 Fed. Reg. 73488 (November 30, 2022)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
