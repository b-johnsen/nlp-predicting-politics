JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Department of Justice and the Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms and Explosives relating to
Factoring Criteria for Firearms With Attached
Stabilizing Braces
.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Department of Justice and the Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms and Explosives relating to
Factoring Criteria for Firearms With Attached
Stabilizing Braces
(88 Fed. Reg. 6478 (January 31, 2023)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
