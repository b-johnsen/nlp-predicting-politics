Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Securities and Exchange Commission relating to
Cybersecurity Risk Management, Strategy, Governance, and Incident Disclosure

That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Securities and Exchange Commission relating to
Cybersecurity Risk Management, Strategy, Governance, and Incident Disclosure
(88 Fed. Reg. 51896 (August 4, 2023)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
