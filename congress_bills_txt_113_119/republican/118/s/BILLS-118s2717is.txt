1.
Brigadier General John T. Wilder Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 231 North Franklin Street in Greensburg, Indiana, shall be known and designated as the
Brigadier General John T. Wilder Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Brigadier General John T. Wilder Post Office
.
