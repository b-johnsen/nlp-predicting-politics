(b)
Bull Mountains Mining Plan Modification reinstatement
(1)
In general
Not later than 30 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Secretary of the Interior shall, without modification or delay, reinstate the Bull Mountains Mining Plan Modification.
(2)
Duration
On reinstatement under paragraph (1), the Bull Mountains Mining Plan Modification shall remain in effect and operational until mining under the Bull Mountains Mining Plan Modification is complete, as determined by the Montana Department of Environmental Quality.
