(1)
The Secretary shall ensure that the family of a person buried in a national cemetery has direct access to that cemetery.

3.
Prioritization of grants for States without veterans' cemeteries
Section 2408(a) of title 38, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph:
(3)
In making grants under this section, the Secretary shall prioritize grants for establishing veterans' cemeteries in States that do not have such a cemetery.
