2.
Limitations on provision of incentives for critical skills to Senior Executive Service employees of Department of Veterans Affairs
Section 706(d) of title 38, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
(7)
(A)
A critical skill incentive may not be provided under paragraph (1) to an employee of the Department employed in a Senior Executive Service position (or equivalent position) whose position is based out of the central office of the Department, regardless of the actual location where the employee performs the functions of the position.
