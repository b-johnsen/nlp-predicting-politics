1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Partnership for Advancing Regional Training and Narcotics Enforcement Response Strategies Act
or the
PARTNERS Act
.
2.
Statement of policy on military capacity building and security cooperation with the Government of Mexico
It is the policy of the United States Government to counter the threat posed by transnational criminal organizations, including through military capacity building and security cooperation with the Government of Mexico.
