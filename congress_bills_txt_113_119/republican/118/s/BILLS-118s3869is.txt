(c)
State energy program
Section 362 of the Energy Policy and Conservation Act (
42 U.S.C. 6322
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(h)
USMCA-Compliant vehicle requirement
(1)
Definition of USMCA-compliant vehicle
In this subsection, the term
USMCA-compliant vehicle
means a vehicle that qualifies as an originating good under section 202 of the United States-Mexico-Canada Agreement Implementation Act (
19 U.S.C. 4531
).
(2)
Requirement
Subject to paragraph (3), any vehicle purchased as part of a State energy conservation plan shall be required to be a USMCA-compliant vehicle.
(3)
More stringent State law
The requirement under paragraph (2) shall not apply in the case of a State energy conservation plan that requires vehicles purchased under the plan to be produced in the United States.
.
