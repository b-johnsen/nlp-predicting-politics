1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Give Americans Stability at Pumps as Rising Inflation Causes Emergencies Act
or the
GAS PRICE Act
.
2.
Report on policies, regulations, and Executive orders affecting energy prices
Not later than 60 days after the date of enactment of this Act, and every year thereafter, the Energy Information Administration shall submit to Congress and make publicly available a report detailing each Federal agency policy or regulation and each Executive order with an effective date of January 20, 2021, or later that the Energy Information Administration determines has increased or may increase energy prices in the United States.
