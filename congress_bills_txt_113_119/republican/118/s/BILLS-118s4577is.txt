(b)
GAO evaluation
Not later than 180 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Comptroller General of the United States shall evaluate all national resource centers authorized or supported under the Older Americans Act of 1965 (
42 U.S.C. 3001 et seq.
) for functions to streamline, as appropriate, and to improve the efficiencies of taxpayer funds.
