5.

(a)
Requirement for regulations
(1)
In general

19 U.S.C. 1321(a)(2)(C)
) is sought, including obtaining information with respect to the origin of materials used in the production of such articles, in order to ensure that the articles for which the exemption is sought are not articles described in paragraph (2) or articles that are otherwise prohibited from entering the United States.
