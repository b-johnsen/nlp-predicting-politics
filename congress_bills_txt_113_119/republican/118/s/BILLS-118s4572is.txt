1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Removing Our Unsecure Technologies to Ensure Reliability and Security Act
or the
ROUTERS Act
.
2.
Study of national security risks posed by certain routers and modems
(a)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)
Covered country
The term
covered country
has the meaning given the term
covered nation
in section 4872(d) of title 10, United States Code.
(2)
Secretary
The term
Secretary
means the Secretary of Commerce, acting through the Assistant Secretary of Commerce for Communications and Information.
(b)
Study
The Secretary shall conduct a study of the national security risks posed by consumer routers, modems, and devices that combine a modem and router that are designed, developed, manufactured, or supplied by persons owned by, controlled by, or subject to the influence of a covered country.
(c)
Report to Congress
Not later than 1 year after the date of enactment of this Act, the Secretary shall submit to the Committee on Commerce, Science, and Transportation of the Senate and the Committee on Energy and Commerce of the House of Representatives a report on the results of the study conducted under subsection (b).
