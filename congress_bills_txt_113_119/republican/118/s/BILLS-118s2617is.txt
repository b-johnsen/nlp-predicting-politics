2.
Prohibition on restrictions on direct access to medical countermeasures
(a)
In general
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the Secretary of Health and Human Services may not implement any policy that would restrict hospitals or other appropriate health care facilities from ordering and receiving medical countermeasures, including biological products, drugs, and devices, directly from manufacturers and distributors of such countermeasures.
