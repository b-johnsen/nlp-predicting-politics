IV
118th CONGRESS
2d Session
H. CON. RES. 134
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
Directing the Clerk of the House of Representatives to make a correction in the enrollment of the bill H.R. 5009.
That in the enrollment of the bill H.R. 5009, the Clerk of the House of Representatives shall make the following correction: Amend the long title so as to read:
An Act to authorize appropriations for fiscal year 2025 for military activities of the Department of Defense, for military construction, and for defense activities of the Department of Energy, to prescribe military personnel strengths for such fiscal year, and for other purposes.
.
Passed the House of Representatives December 11, 2024.
Kevin F. McCumber,
Clerk.
