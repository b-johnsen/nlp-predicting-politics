CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
Agreeing that the Chinese Communist Party is the greatest threat to freedom and to the free world.
Whereas it is the opinion of Congress that the Chinese Communist Party is the greatest threat to freedom and to the free world: Now, therefore, be it
That Congress agrees that the Chinese Communist Party is the greatest threat to freedom and to the free world.
