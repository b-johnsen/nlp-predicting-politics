RESOLUTION
Providing for the expulsion of Representative Jamaal Bowman from the House of Representatives.
That, pursuant to article I, section 5, clause 2 of the Constitution of the United States, Representative Jamaal Bowman be, and he hereby is expelled, from the House of Representatives.
