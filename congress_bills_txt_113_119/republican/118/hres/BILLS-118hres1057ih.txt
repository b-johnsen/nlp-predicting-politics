RESOLUTION
Denouncing calls for a cease-fire in Gaza.
Whereas Hamas brutally attacked Israel on October 7, 2023, causing the death of over 1,200 Israeli citizens, including women and children;
Whereas the United Nations Relief and Works Agency (UNRWA) employed several Hamas terror operatives;
Whereas the Biden administration has funded UNRWA with over $1,000,000,000 American taxpayer dollars;
Whereas the Biden administration restarted aid to the Palestinian Authority, which President Trump had suspended;
Whereas additional developmental aid to any Palestinian entity is illegal under the Taylor Force Act, after dozens of American citizens were killed under the Biden administrationâs watch by terrorists affiliated with Hamas and the Palestinian Authority, including on October 7;
Whereas the Palestinian Authority continues to pay salaries to terrorists and their families who have murdered American citizens;
Whereas progressive interest groups have demanded President Biden call for a cease-fire that would result in the laying down of arms by Israel and increased offensive measures by Hamas;
Whereas those same groups are demanding the establishment of an independent State of Palestine;
Whereas the Arabs rejected the 1947 United Nations Partition plan to establish an independent State of Palestine; and
Whereas the United States unequivocally stands with Israel and its indisputable right to defend itself: Now, therefore, be it
That the House of Representativesâ
(1)
denounces calls for a cease-fire in Gaza;
(2)
calls for an end of United States funding of Hamas via UNRWA;
(3)
condemns calls for the establishment of an independent State of Palestine; and
(4)
calls for a termination of United States assistance to Palestinian entities, government officials, relief agencies, and NGOs until an independent and comprehensive investigation is completed.
