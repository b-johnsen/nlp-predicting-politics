Whereas George Washington Carver was one of the greatest American agricultural scientists and humanitarians of the 20th century;
Whereas George Washington Carver was born enslaved in Missouri, in circa 1864, and eventually pursued higher education in Iowa;
Whereas George Washington Carver was Iowa State UniversityГўВҖВҷs first Black student and Black faculty member, and later earned international renown as a faculty member at the Tuskegee University in Alabama;
Whereas George Washington Carver gained an international reputation in research, teaching and outreach, and created hundreds of products from peanuts, soybeans, sweet potatoes, and other crops; and
Whereas his humanitarian efforts changed the lives of countless farmers and families in poverty: Now, therefore, be it
That the House of Representatives supports a
George Washington Carver Day
to celebrate and commemorate George Washington CarverГўВҖВҷs enduring legacy and contributions to our Nation.
