IV
118th CONGRESS
2d Session
H. RES. 1150
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
April 16, 2024
Mr. Carter of Georgia
(for himself and
Mr. Clyde
) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the
Committee on Oversight and Accountability
RESOLUTION
Expressing support for the designation of April 16, 2024, as
FairTax Day
.
Whereas the current United States tax code is over 7,000 pages;
Whereas Americans will spend over 6,000,000,000 hours working on filing their taxes this year;
Whereas the FairTax would repeal the current tax code and replace it with a single national consumption tax that is progrowth and allows Americans to keep every cent of their hard-earned money;
Whereas the FairTax allows Americans to keep 100 percent of their hard-earned paycheck and ends the Internal Revenue Service headache;
Whereas a family of four could spend $30,000 annually without paying a penny in taxes;
Whereas, in addition to eliminating all personal and corporate income taxes, the death tax, gift taxes, and the payroll tax, the FairTax would also eliminate the need for the Internal Revenue Service; and
Whereas the FairTax is the only tax system that is simple, efficient, friendly to economic growth, nondiscriminatory, unintrusive, and FAIR: Now, therefore, be it
That the House of Representatives supports the designation of
FairTax Day
.
