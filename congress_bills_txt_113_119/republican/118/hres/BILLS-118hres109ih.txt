Expressing the sense of the House of Representatives that Social Security should be preserved and protected for current beneficiaries, and for future generations to come.

Whereas the policies of the Biden Administration have resulted in record inflation, rising prices, and putting severe strain on Americans, in particular those on Social Security; and
