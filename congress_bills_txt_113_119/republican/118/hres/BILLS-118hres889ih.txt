RESOLUTION
Condemning the Hamas-led terrorist attack on the State of Israel on October 7, 2023, and calling for Hamas to immediately and unconditionally surrender, cease its attacks, and safely release all hostages.
Whereas Hamas is a United States-designated Foreign Terrorist organization whose founding charter states a commitment to destroying the State of Israel;
Whereas, since Hamas was founded in 1987, Hamas terrorists have repeatedly carried out lethal attacks in Israel and, since taking control of the Gaza Strip in 2007, fought numerous wars against Israel;
Whereas, on October 7, 2023, Hamas terrorists led an attack on the Israel, murdering upwards of 1,200 individuals, taking over 240 individuals hostage, and launching thousands of rockets toward Israel;
Whereas, since the attack, Israel has launched operations to defend its people, eradicate Hamas terrorists, and rescue hostages held in the Gaza Strip;
Whereas Israel, like other sovereign states, has a right to defend itself;
Whereas in past Hamas-Israel conflicts, Hamas has repeatedly broken negotiated ceasefire agreements and initiated attacks against Israel;
