RESOLUTION
Providing for the consideration of the bill (H.R. 5863) to provide tax relief with respect to certain Federal disasters.
That immediately upon adoption of this resolution, the House shall proceed to the consideration in the House of the bill (H.R. 5863) to provide tax relief with respect to certain Federal disasters. All points of order against consideration of the bill are waived. The bill shall be considered as read. All points of order against provisions in the bill are waived. No amendment to the bill shall be in order. The previous question shall be considered as ordered on the bill to final passage without intervening motion except: (1) one hour of debate equally divided and controlled by the chair and ranking minority member of the Committee on Ways and Means or their respective designees; and (2) one motion to recommit.
2.
Clause 1(c) of rule XIX shall not apply to the consideration of H.R. 5863.
