and child-care professionals to take measures to prevent sunburns in the
minors they care for, and expressing the sense of the House of
Representatives that State, local, and community entities should continue
to support efforts to curb the incidences of skin cancer beginning with
childhood skin protection.

That the House of Representativesâ
(1)
