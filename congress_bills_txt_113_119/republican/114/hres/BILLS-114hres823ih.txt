RESOLUTION
Condemning in the strongest terms the terrorist attacks in Istanbul, Turkey, on June 28, 2016, that
resulted in the loss of at least 44 lives.
Whereas, on June 28, 2016, suicide bombers attacked the Ataturk International Airport in Istanbul Turkey, opened fire and detonated explosives, resulting in the loss of at least 44 innocent lives and severely wounding over 200, including an American citizen;
Whereas Turkish first responders and law enforcement reacted swiftly and heroically, caring for the wounded and taking immediate actions to prevent subsequent attacks and further loss of innocent life;
