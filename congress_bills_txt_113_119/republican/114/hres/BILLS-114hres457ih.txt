RESOLUTION
Amending the Rules of the House of Representatives to require that the House be in session at least 40 hours each week other than a week that is designated as a district work period.
That rule XIV is amended by adding at the end the following new clause:
7.
(a)
Except as provided by paragraph (b), the House shall be in session at least 40 hours each week other than a week that is designated as a district work period.
(b)
In any week in which a legal holiday occurs, other than a week that is designated as a district work period, the House shall be in session at least 8 hours per day for the remaining days of such week.
