IV
114th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. J. RES. 76
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
JOINT RESOLUTION
Appointing the day for the convening of the second session of the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress.
That the second regular session of the One Hundred
Fourteenth Congress shall begin at noon on Monday, January 4, 2016.
Passed the House of Representatives December 15, 2015.
Karen L. Haas,
Clerk.
