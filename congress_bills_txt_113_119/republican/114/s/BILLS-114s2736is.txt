1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Patient Access to Durable Medical Equipment Act of 2016
.
2.
Extension of the transition to new payment rates for durable medical equipment under the
Medicare program
The Secretary of Health and Human Services shall extend the transition period described in clause (i) of section 414.210(g)(9) of title 42, Code of Federal Regulations, from June 30, 2016, to September 30, 2017 (with the full implementation described in clause (ii) of such section applying to items and services furnished with dates of service on or after October 1, 2017).

medical equipment and of health outcomes
