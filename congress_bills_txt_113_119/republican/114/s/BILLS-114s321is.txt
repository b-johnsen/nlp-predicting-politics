1.
Revocation of charter of incorporation
The request of the Miami Tribe of Oklahoma to surrender the charter of incorporation issued to that tribe and ratified by its members on June 1, 1940, pursuant to the Act of June 26, 1936 (
25 U.S.C. 501 et seq.
; commonly known as the
Oklahoma Welfare Act
), is hereby accepted and that charter of incorporation is hereby revoked.
