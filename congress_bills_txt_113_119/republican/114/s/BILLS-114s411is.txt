(a)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)
Gas gathering line and associated field compression unit
(A)
In general
The term

meansâ
(i)

The term

(B)
located adjacent to or withinâ
(i)

an existing corridor for a right-of-way.

Paragraph (1) shall apply to Indian land, or a portion of Indian land, for which the Indian tribe with jurisdiction over the Indian land submits to the Secretary of the Interior a written request that paragraph (1) apply to that Indian land (or portion of Indian land).
