RESOLUTION
To elect Orrin G. Hatch, a Senator from the State of Utah, to be President pro tempore of the
Senate of the United States.
That Orrin G. Hatch, a Senator from the State of Utah, be, and he is hereby, elected President of
the Senate pro tempore.
