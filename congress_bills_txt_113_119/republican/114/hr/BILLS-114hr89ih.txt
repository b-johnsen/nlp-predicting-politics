3.
Public disclosure of export destinations
Section 3 of the Natural Gas Act (
15 U.S.C. 717b
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(g)
Public disclosure of LNG export destinations
As a condition for approval of any authorization to export LNG, the Secretary of Energy shall
require the applicant to publicly disclose the specific destination or
destinations of any such authorized LNG exports.
