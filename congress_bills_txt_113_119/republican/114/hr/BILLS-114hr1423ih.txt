1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Self-Insurance Protection Act
.
2.
Certain medical stop loss insurance obtained by certain plan sponsors of group health plans not included under the definition of health insurance coverage
(a)
PHSA
Section 2791(b)(1) of the Public Health Service Act (
42 U.S.C. 300ggâ91(b)(1)
) is amended by adding at the end the following new sentence:
Such term shall not include a stop loss policy obtained by a self-insured health plan or a plan sponsor of a group health plan that self-insures the health risks of its plan participants to reimburse the plan or sponsor for losses that the plan or sponsor incurs in providing health or medical benefits to such plan participants in excess of a predetermined level set forth in the stop loss policy obtained by such plan or sponsor.
.
