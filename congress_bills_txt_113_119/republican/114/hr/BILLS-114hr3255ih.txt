Exclusion of certain gain by student farmers from gross income
(a)
In general
Part III of subchapter B of
chapter 1
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by inserting after section 139E the following new section:
139F.
Gain from property produced or raised by a student farmer
(a)
In general
In the case of a student farmer, gross income shall not include so much of the gains from qualified dispositions for the taxable year as does not exceed $5,000.
