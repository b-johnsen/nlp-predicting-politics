1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Refugee Relocation Security Act
.
2.
Suspension of Admission of Syrian and Iraqi Refugees
Beginning on the date of the enactment of this Act, the Secretary of Homeland Security may not admit into the United States an alien from the countries of Syria and Iraq into the United States under section 207 of the Immigration and Nationality Act (
8 U.S.C. 1157
) until such time as Congress passes a joint resolution giving the Secretary the authority to resume admitting such aliens under such sections.
