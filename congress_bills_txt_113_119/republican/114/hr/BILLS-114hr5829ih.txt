1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Agriculture Delivering Vastly Improved Science at the EPA Now Act
or the
ADVISE Now Act
.
2.
Establishment of Committee
(a)
Amendment
Section 8(e)(2)(A)(i) of the Environmental Research, Development, and Demonstration Authorization Act of 1978 (
42 U.S.C. 4365(e)(2)(A)(i)
) is amended by inserting
, not later than 30 days after the date of enactment of the
ADVISE Now Act
,
after
shall establish
.
(b)
Contingent appointment by Secretary of Agriculture
If an agriculture committee is not established pursuant to the amendment made by subsection (a) within the deadline provided, the Secretary of Agriculture shall establish and appoint members to such a committee within 15 days.
(c)
Continuing authority
If the contingent establishment occurs under subsection (b), the Secretary of Agriculture shall have the continuing authority to appoint members to the committee so established.
