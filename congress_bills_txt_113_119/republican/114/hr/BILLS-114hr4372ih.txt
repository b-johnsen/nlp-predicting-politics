1.
Barry G. Miller Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 15 Rochester Street, Bergen, New York, shall be known and designated as the
Barry G. Miller Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Barry G. Miller Post Office
.
