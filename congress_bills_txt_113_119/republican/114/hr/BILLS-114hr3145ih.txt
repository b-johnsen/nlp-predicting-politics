1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Protect Local Independence in Housing Act of 2015
.
2.
Disparate impact
cases under the Fair Housing Act
Section 804(f) of the Fair Housing Act (
42 U.S.C. 3604(f)
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(10)
Nothing in this title shall be construed to prohibit conduct resulting in a disparate impact on a class protected by this title unless the person engaging in that conduct intended that impact.
.
