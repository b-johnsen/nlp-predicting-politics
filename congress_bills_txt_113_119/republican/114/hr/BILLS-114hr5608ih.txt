1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
No Ex-Im Financing for Iran Act
.
2.
Prohibition on assistance benefiting Iran
Section 2(b) of the Export-Import Bank Act of 1945 (
12 U.S.C. 635(b)
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(14)
Prohibition on assistance benefiting Iran

(A)
the Government of Iran, or an entity in which the Government of Iran participates; or
(B)
an entity that is created under Iranian law; or
(C)
an operation in Iran.
