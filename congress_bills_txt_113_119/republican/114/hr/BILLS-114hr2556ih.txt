Repeal of program development and implementation grants authorization
Section 406(b) of the Federal Water Pollution Control Act (
33 U.S.C. 1346(b)
) is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph:
(5)
Repeal of authority
The Administrator may not make a grant to a State or local government under this subsection after the date of enactment of this paragraph.
