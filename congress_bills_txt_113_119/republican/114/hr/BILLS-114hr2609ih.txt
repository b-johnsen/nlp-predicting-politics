1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Right-of-Way for American Drivers Act of 2015
.
2.
Repeal of transportation alternatives program
(a)
In general
The following provisions of title 23, United States Code, are repealed:
(1)
Section 101(a)(29).
(2)
Section 133(b)(11).
(3)
Section 213 (and the item relating to that section in the analysis for chapter 1).
(b)
Applicability
The repeals made by subsection (a) shall apply to fiscal years beginning after the date of enactment of this Act.
