4.
Best practices for training air traffic controllers
Section 44506 of title 49, United States Code, is further amended by adding at the end the following:
(h)
Best practices for training
The Administrator, in consultation with the Association of Collegiate Training Institutions, the National Air Traffic Controllers Association, and the University Aviation Association, shall take into consideration any training initiatives for air traffic controllers that are locally developed at institutions of higher education participating in the Collegiate Training Initiative program for use in establishing best practices nationwide.
