2.
Criminal penalty for wearing or displaying a revoked Presidential Medal of Freedom
(a)
In general
Chapter 33
of title 18, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
717.
Revoked Presidential Medal of Freedom
Whoever, with the intent to defraud any person, wears or publicly displays a Presidential Medal of Freedom that has been declared to be revoked by the President shall be fined under this title or imprisoned for not more than one year, or both.
.
(b)
Clerical amendment
The table of sections at the beginning of
chapter 33
of title 18, United States Code, is amended by inserting after the item relating to
section 716
the following:
717. Revoked Presidential Medal of Freedom.
