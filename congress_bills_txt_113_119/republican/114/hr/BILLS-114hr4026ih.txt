1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Nondiscriminatory Transportation Screening Act
or the
TSA Act
.
2.
Treatment of concealed handgun license as verifying identity document for purposes of aircraft passenger security screening
For purposes of part 1560 of title 49, Code of Federal Regulations, a concealed handgun license shall be treated as a verifying identity document.
3.
Prohibition on Federal recordkeeping relating to concealed handgun licenses
The Federal Government may not collect or store information about an individual relating to a concealed handgun license.
4.
Concealed handgun license defined
In this Act the term
concealed handgun license
means a license, permit, or other document issued by a State or a political subdivision of a State that authorizes an individual to carry a concealed handgun.
