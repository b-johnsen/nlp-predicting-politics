1.
Lieutenant Colonel James
Maggie
Megellas Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 99 West 2nd Street in Fond du Lac, Wisconsin, shall be known and designated as the
Lieutenant Colonel James
Maggie
Megellas Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Lieutenant Colonel James
Maggie
Megellas Post Office
.
