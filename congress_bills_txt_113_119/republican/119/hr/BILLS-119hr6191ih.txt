1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
No Taxpayer Funded Abortion Travel for Illegal Aliens Act
.
2.
Prohibiting Federal funds to be used for certain abortion services
(a)
Prohibition
No Federal funds may be used to assist an illegal alien in accessing covered abortion services.
