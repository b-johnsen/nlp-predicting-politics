1.
Short title

Prohibiting Automatic Rights to Enter National Territory Act of 2025
or as the
PARENT Act of 2025

8 U.S.C. 1101
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(j)
For purposes of section 301(a), the term
subject to the jurisdiction thereof
means, with respect to a person born in the United States, that the person was born to a parent who is, at the time of the personâs birth, a citizen of the United States or an alien who is a lawful permanent resident of the United States.
.
3.
Effective date
This Act, and the amendments made by this Act, shall apply to persons born after the date of enactment of this Act.
