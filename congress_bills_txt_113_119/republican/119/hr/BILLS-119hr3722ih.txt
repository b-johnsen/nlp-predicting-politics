(b)
Definitions
In this Act:
(1)
The term
covered color additive
means the following color additives (as such term is defined in section 201 of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (
21 U.S.C. 321
)):
(A)
Red No. 40.
(B)
Yellow No. 5.
(C)
Yellow No. 6.
(D)
Green No. 3.
(E)
Blue No. 1.
(F)
Blue No. 2.
(G)
Any additive that is substantially similar to the additives specified in subparagraphs (A) through (F).
(2)
The term
qualified color additive
means the following color additives:
(A)
Citrus Red No. 2.
(B)
Orange B.
(C)
Any additive that is substantially similar to the additives specified in subparagraphs (A) and (B).
