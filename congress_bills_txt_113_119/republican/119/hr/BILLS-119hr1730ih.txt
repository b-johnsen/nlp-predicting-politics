1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
No Alipay Act of 2025
.
2.
Prohibition on use of Alipay
No financial transactions between AliPay (China) Internet Technology Company Limited (referred to in this Act as
AliPay
) and a United States person are permitted, including the use of any application or payment processing services operated by Alipay.
