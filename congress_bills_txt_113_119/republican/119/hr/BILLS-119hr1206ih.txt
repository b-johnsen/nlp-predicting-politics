1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Western Economic Security Today Act of 2025
or the
WEST Act of 2025
.
2.
Withdrawal of BLM rule
The final rule based on the proposed rule of the Bureau of Land Management entitled
Conservation and Landscape Health
(88 Fed. Reg. 19583 (April 3, 2023)) shall have no force or effect.
