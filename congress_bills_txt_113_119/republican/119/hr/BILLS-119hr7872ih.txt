1.
Bonus payments for certain coal leases issued under Mineral Leasing Act
Section 2(a) of the Mineral Leasing Act (
30 U.S.C. 201(a)
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(6)
The bonus payments for a lease issued under this subsection under a system of deferred bonus payment shall be payable in 10 equal annual installments, the first of which shall be submitted with the bid for such lease.
