Increase in credit for projects that use water submetering
(a)
In general
Paragraph (5) of
section 42(d)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by adding at the end the following new subparagraph:
(C)
Increase in credit for projects that use water submetering
In the case of any buildingâ
(i)
which contains 4 or more units,
(ii)

the eligible basis of such building shall be 105 percent of such basis determined without regard to this subparagraph.
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by this section shall apply to buildings which receive allocations of housing credit dollar amount or, in the case of projects financed by tax-exempt obligations as described in
section 42(h)(4)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986, which are first taken into account under section 146 of such Code, after the date of the enactment of this Act.
