1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Keep Our Girls Safe Act of 2025
.
2.
Prohibition
It shall be unlawful under title IX of the Education Amendments of 1972 (
20 U.S.C. 1681 et seq.
) for an individual of one sex (as determined solely on reproductive biology and genetics at birth) to use a locker room while such room is in active use by individuals of a different sex in connection with an education program or activity.
3.
Effective date
This Act shall take effect 30 days after the date of the enactment of this Act.
