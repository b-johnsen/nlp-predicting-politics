1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
No Tax Exemptions For Terror Act
.
2.
Council on American-Islamic relations and similar organizations subject to taxation
(a)
In general
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the Council on American-Islamic Relations, or any other organization found to have ties to terrorism or terrorist organizations, shall not be treated as described in
section 501(c)(3)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986.
(b)
Effective date
This section shall apply to taxable years ending after the date of the enactment of this Act.
