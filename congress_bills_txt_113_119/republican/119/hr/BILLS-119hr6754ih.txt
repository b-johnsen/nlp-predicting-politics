(b)
Nonapplicability
Subsection (b) of section 218 of the Further Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2024 shall not apply to the amendment made by subsection (a) and shall not have any force or effect at law relating thereto.
(c)
Exemption
The Administrator of the Transportation Security Administration shall exempt a Member of the House of Representatives or a Senator of the Senate, including the leadership of either of such House, from Federal passenger and baggage screening if such Member or Senator, as the case may be, is traveling between the home airport of such Member or Senator, as the case may be, and any other location.
