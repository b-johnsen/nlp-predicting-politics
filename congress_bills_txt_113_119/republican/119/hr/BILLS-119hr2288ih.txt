1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Common Sense Air Regulations Act
.
2.
Nullification of final rule relating to Reconsideration of the National Ambient Air Quality Standards for Particulate Matter
The final rule of the Environmental Protection Agency titled
Reconsideration of the National Ambient Air Quality Standards for Particulate Matter
(89 Fed. Reg. 16202 (March 6, 2024)) shall have no force or effect.
