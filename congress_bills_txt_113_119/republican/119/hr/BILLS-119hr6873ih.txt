1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Geothermal Tax Parity Act
.
2.
Amortization of geological and geophysical expenditures in connection with exploration for or development of geothermal deposits
(a)
In general
Section 167(h)(1)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking
oil or gas
and inserting
oil, gas, or geothermal deposits
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by this section shall apply to amounts paid or incurred in taxable years beginning after the date of the enactment of this Act.
