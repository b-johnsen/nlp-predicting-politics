.
2.
Removal and dismissal of qualified civil liability actions
Section 3 of the Protection of Lawful Commerce in Arms Act (
15 U.S.C. 7902
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(c)
Removal and dismissal
(1)
Removal
In any civil action in a State court in which a defendant that is a manufacturer, seller, or trade association asserts that the civil action is a qualified civil liability action, that defendant may remove the civil action to the district court of the United States for the district and division embracing the place where the civil action is pending.
(2)
Dismissal

(A)

(B)
dismiss the civil action accordingly.
