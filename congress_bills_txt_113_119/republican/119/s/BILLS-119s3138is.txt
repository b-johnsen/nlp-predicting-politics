1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Veterans Supporting Prosthetics Opportunities and Recreational Therapy Act
or the
Veterans SPORT Act
.
2.
Inclusion of adaptive prostheses and terminal devices for sports and other
recreational activities in medical services furnished to eligible veterans by the
Secretary of Veterans Affairs
Section 1701 of title 38, United States Code, is amended, in paragraph (6)(F)(i), by inserting
(including adaptive prostheses and terminal devices for sports and other recreational activities)
after
artificial limbs
.
