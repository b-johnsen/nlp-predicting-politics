1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Local Data for Better Conservation Act
.
2.
Listing determinations
Section 4 of the Endangered Species Act of 1973 (
16 U.S.C. 1533
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(j)
State data
In making a determination to list, or delist, a species as a threatened species or an endangered species under this section, the Secretary shall accept and integrate State-collected data.
.
