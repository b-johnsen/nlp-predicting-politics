Short title

Stop Funding Religiously Oppressive Regimes Act of 2025

(3)

3.
Prohibition on assistance to foreign governments that violate human rights with respect to religion
The United States Government is prohibited from obligating or expending any Federal funds to provide assistance to the government of any country identified in the report submitted by the President pursuant to section 2.
