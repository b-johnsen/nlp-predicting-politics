1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Chairman James Mountain Inhofe Recognition Act of 2025
.
2.
Redesignation of Africa Center for Strategic Studies as James M. Inhofe Africa Center for Strategic Studies
(a)
Reference to regional centers for strategic studies
Section 342(b)(2)(D) of title 10, United States Code, is amended by striking
Africa Center for Strategic Studies
and inserting
James M. Inhofe Africa Center for Strategic Studies
.
(b)
References
Any reference to the Department of Defense Africa Center for Strategic Studies in any law, regulation, map, document, record, or other paper of the United States shall be deemed to be a reference to the James M. Inhofe Africa Center for Strategic Studies.
