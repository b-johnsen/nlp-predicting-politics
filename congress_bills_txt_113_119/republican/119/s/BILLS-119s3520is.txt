1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Community Law Enforcement Authority Restoration Act of 2025
or the
CLEAR Act of 2025
.
2.
Invaliding Forest Service rule relating to criminal prohibitions
(a)
In general
The rule of the Forest Service entitled
Law Enforcement; Criminal Prohibitions
(89 Fed. Reg. 92808 (November 25, 2024)) shall have no force or effect.
(b)
Implementation
The Secretary of Agriculture shall not administer, implement, or enforce the rule described in subsection (a) or any substantially similar rule.
