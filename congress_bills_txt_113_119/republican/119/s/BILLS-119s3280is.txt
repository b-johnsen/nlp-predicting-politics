1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Measuring Availability of Providers (MAP) for Veterans Act
.
2.
Feasibility study for full-service hospital of Department of Veterans Affairs in certain States
(a)
In general
The Secretary of Veterans Affairs shall conduct a study on the feasibility of establishing a full-service hospital of the Department of Veterans Affairs in Alaska, Hawaii, and New Hampshire.
(b)
Publication
Not later than one year after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Secretary shall publish the results of the study conducted under subsection (a) on a publicly available website of the Department.
3.
Continued access to care under Veterans Community Care Program in States that establish full-service medical facility of Department of Veterans Affairs
Section 1703(d)(1)(B) of title 38, United States Code, is amended by inserting
as of the date of the enactment of the
Measuring Availability of Providers (MAP) for Veterans Act
before the semicolon at the end.
