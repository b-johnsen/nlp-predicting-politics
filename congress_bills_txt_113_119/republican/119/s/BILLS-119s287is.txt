1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Marcella LeBeau Recognition Act
.
2.
Marcella LeBeau Federal Building
(a)
Designation
The Federal building located at 225 South Pierre Street in Pierre, South Dakota, shall be known and designated as the
Marcella LeBeau Federal Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the Federal building referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Marcella LeBeau Federal Building
.
