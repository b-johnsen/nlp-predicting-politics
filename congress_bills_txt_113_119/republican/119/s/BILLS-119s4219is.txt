1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Abraham Accords Defense Cooperation Act of 2026
.
2.
Definitions
In this Act:
(1)
Abraham Accords country
The term

(B)

(2)
Initiative
The term
Initiative
means the United States-Abraham Accords Defense Cooperation Initiative established under section 3(a).
(3)
Secretary
The term
Secretary
means the Secretary of Defense.
3.
United States-Abraham Accords Defense Cooperation Initiative
(a)
Establishment
The Secretary shall establish an initiative to bolster defense cooperation between the United States and the militaries of covered countries, to be known as the
United States-Abraham Accords Defense Cooperation Initiative
.
(b)
Relationship to existing authorities
The Initiative shall be carried out pursuant to the authorities provided in title 10, United States Code.
