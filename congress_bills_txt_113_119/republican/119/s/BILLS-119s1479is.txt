1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Building Out Optimized Space Testing for Engines Act
or the
BOOST for Engines Act
.
2.
Definitions
In this Act:
(1)
Administrator
The term
Administrator
means the Administrator of the National Aeronautics and Space Administration.
(2)
NASA
The term
NASA
means the National Aeronautics and Space Administration.
