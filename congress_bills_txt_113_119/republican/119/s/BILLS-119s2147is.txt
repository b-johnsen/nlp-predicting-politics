1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Collegiate Sports Integrity Act
.
2.
Antitrust exemption for intercollegiate athletic conferences and
associations
(a)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)
Antitrust laws
The term
antitrust laws
has the meaning given the term in the first section of the Clayton Act (
15 U.S.C. 12
), except that such term includes section 5 of the Federal Trade Commission Act (
15 U.S.C. 45
) to the extent such section 5 applies to unfair methods of competition.
(2)
Institution of higher education
The term
institution of higher education
has the meaning given the term in section 101 of the Higher Education Act of 1965 (
20 U.S.C. 1001
).

(4)
Interstate intercollegiate athletic association
The term

â

(ii)

(B)

(b)
Exemption
The antitrust laws shall not apply to intercollegiate athletic conferences or interstate intercollegiate athletic associations.
