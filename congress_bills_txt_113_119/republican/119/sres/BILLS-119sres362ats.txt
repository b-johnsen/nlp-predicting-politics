) submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to
RESOLUTION
To provide for the printing of the Senate Manual for the One Hundred Nineteenth Congress.
That a revised edition of the Senate Manual for the One Hundred Nineteenth Congress be prepared by the Committee on Rules and Administration and printed as a Senate document, and that 1,200 additional copies shall be printed and bound for the use of the Senate, bound and delivered as may be directed by the Committee on Rules and Administration.
