JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Commodity Futures Trading Commission relating to
Commission Guidance Regarding the Listing of Voluntary Carbon Credit Derivative Contracts
.
That Congress disapproves the final guidance submitted by the Commodity Futures Trading Commission relating to
Commission Guidance Regarding the Listing of Voluntary Carbon Credit Derivative Contracts
(89 Fed. Reg. 83378 (October 15, 2024)) and such rule shall have no force or effect.
