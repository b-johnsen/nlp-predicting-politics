Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to
National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants: Rubber Tire Manufacturing

National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants: Rubber Tire Manufacturing
(89 Fed. Reg. 94886 (November 29, 2024)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
