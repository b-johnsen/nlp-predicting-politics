CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
Authorizing the use of the rotunda of the Capitol for a ceremony to award the Congressional Gold
Medal to Bob Dole.
1.
Use of the rotunda of the Capitol for ceremony to award the Congressional Gold Medal to Bob Dole
(a)
Authorization
The rotunda of the Capitol is authorized to be used on January 17, 2018, for a ceremony to award the Congressional Gold Medal to Bob Dole.
(b)
Preparations
Physical preparations for the conduct of the ceremony described in subsection (a) shall be carried out in accordance with such conditions as may be prescribed by the Architect of the Capitol.
