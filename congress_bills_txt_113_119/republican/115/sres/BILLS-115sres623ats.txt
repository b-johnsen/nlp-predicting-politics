115th CONGRESS
2d Session
S. RES. 623
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
September 6, 2018
Mr. McConnell
submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to
RESOLUTION
To constitute the majority party's membership on certain committees for the One Hundred Fifteenth Congress, or until their successors are chosen.
That the following shall constitute the majority party's membership on the following committees for the One Hundred Fifteenth Congress, or until their successors are chosen:
Committee on Armed Services:
Mr. Inhofe (Chairman), Mr. Wicker, Mrs. Fischer, Mr. Cotton, Mr. Rounds, Mrs. Ernst, Mr. Tillis, Mr. Sullivan, Mr. Perdue, Mr. Cruz, Mr. Graham, Mr. Sasse, Mr. Scott, Mr. Kyl.
Committee on Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs:
Mr. Johnson (Chairman), Mr. Portman, Mr. Paul, Mr. Lankford, Mr. Enzi, Mr. Hoeven, Mr. Daines, Mr. Kyl.
Committee on Indian Affairs:
Mr. Hoeven (Chairman), Mr. Barrasso, Mrs. Murkowski, Mr. Lankford, Mr. Daines, Mr. Crapo, Mr. Moran, Mr. Kyl.
