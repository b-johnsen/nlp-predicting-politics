1.
Robert H. Jenkins Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 100 Mathe Avenue in Interlachen, Florida, shall be known and designated as the
Robert H. Jenkins Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Robert H. Jenkins Post Office
.
