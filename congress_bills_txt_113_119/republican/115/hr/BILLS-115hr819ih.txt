2.
Disallowing earned income tax credit for aliens in unlawful immigration status
(a)
In general
Section 32(c)(1)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by adding at the end the following new subparagraph:
(G)
Exception for aliens in unlawful immigration status
The term
eligible individual
does not include any alien individual who, at any point during the taxable year, is in an unlawful immigration status.
.
