1.
Designation
The J. Marvin Jones Federal Building and Courthouse located at 205 SE 5th Ave., Amarillo, Texas, shall be known and designated as the
J. Marvin Jones Federal Building and Mary Lou Robinson United States Courthouse
.
2.
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the Federal building and United States courthouse referred to in section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to the
J. Marvin Jones Federal Building and Mary Lou Robinson United States Courthouse
.
