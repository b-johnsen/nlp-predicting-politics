1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Lake Fannin Conveyance Act
.
2.
Definitions
In this Act:
(1)
County
The term
County
means the County of Fannin, Texas.
(2)
Map
The term
map
means the map entitled
Lake Fannin Conveyance
and dated November 21, 2013.
(3)
Secretary
The term
Secretary
means the Secretary of Agriculture, acting through the Chief of the Forest Service.
3.
Conveyance of land and improvements
(a)
In general
Subject to the provisions of this Act, if the County submits a written request for conveyance not later than 180 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Secretary shall convey to the County all right, title, and interest of the United States in and to approximately 2,025 acres of National Forest System land, as generally depicted on the map.
(b)
Map
(1)
Availability of map
The map shall be kept on file and available for public inspection in the appropriate office of the Forest Service.
(2)
Correction of errors
The Secretary may correct minor errors in the map.
