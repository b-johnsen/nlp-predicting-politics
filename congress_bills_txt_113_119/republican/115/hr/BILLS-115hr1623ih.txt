1.
Advanced Technology Vehicles Manufacturing Incentive Program repeal
(a)
Cessation of authority
Effective on the date of enactment of this Act, the Secretary of Energy may not make any additional awards or loans under section 136 of the Energy Independence and Security Act of 2007 (
42 U.S.C. 17013
).
(b)
Repeal
Upon the repayment, in accordance with the terms of the loan agreements, of all loans made before the date of enactment of this Act under subsection (d) of such section, section 136 of the Energy Independence and Security Act of 2007 (
42 U.S.C. 17013
) is repealed.
