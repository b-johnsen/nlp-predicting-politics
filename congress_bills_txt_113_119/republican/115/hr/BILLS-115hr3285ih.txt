Finding
Congress finds that, although section 1304(b) of the National Flood Insurance Act of 1968 (
42 U.S.C. 4011(b)

Rule of construction

42 U.S.C. 4011(b)
), as amended by paragraph (1), shall be construed to permit the Administrator to choose which types of properties described in paragraphs (1) through (5) of such section 1304(b) should be eligible for ICC insurance rather than offering ICC insurance to all such types of properties as required under such section 1304(b).
