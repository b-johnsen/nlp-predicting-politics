1.
Logan S. Palmer Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 1355 North Meridian Road in Harristown, Illinois, shall be known and designated as the
Logan S. Palmer Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Logan S. Palmer Post Office
.
