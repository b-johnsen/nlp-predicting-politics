1.
Harmon Killebrew Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 915 Center Avenue in Payette, Idaho, shall be known and designated as the
Harmon Killebrew Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Harmon Killebrew Post Office Building
.
