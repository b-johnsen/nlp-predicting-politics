2.
Unborn children permitted to be account beneficiaries of section 529 education savings accounts
(a)
In general
Section 529(e)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph:
(6)
Treatment of unborn children
(A)
In general
Nothing shall prevent an unborn child from being treated as a designated beneficiary or an individual under this section.
(B)
Unborn child

.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by this section shall apply to contributions made after the date of the enactment of this Act.
