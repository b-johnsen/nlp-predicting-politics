(b)
Conforming amendment
Section 125(d)(2) of such Code is amended by adding at the end the following new subparagraph:
(E)
Exception for health flexible spending arrangements
Subparagraph (A) shall not apply to a plan to the extent of amounts in a health flexible spending arrangement which may be carried forward as described in section 106(g).
.
(c)
Effective date
The amendment made by this subsection shall apply to plan years beginning after December 31, 2018.
