1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Stop Taxpayer-Funded Alcohol Marketing Act
.
2.
Value-added agricultural product market development grants
(a)
Exclusion of alcohol products from definition
Section 231(a)(5) of the Agricultural Risk Protection Act of 2000 (
7 U.S.C. 1632a(a)(5)
) is amended by adding below subparagraph (B) the following:
The term
value-added agricultural product
does not include beer, wine, distilled spirits, hard cider, or other alcohol product.
.
(b)
Rescission
Of the funds made available under section 231(b)(7)(A) of the Agricultural Risk Protection Act of 2000 (
7 U.S.C. 1632a(b)(7)(A)
) to the Secretary of Agriculture to make value-added agricultural product market development grants and unobligated as of the date of the enactment of this Act, $8,000,000 is hereby rescinded.
