1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Alex Diekmann Peak Designation Act of 2017
.
2.
Designation of Alex Diekmann Peak, Montana
(a)
In general
The unnamed 9,765-foot peak located 2.2 miles west-northwest of Finger Mountain on the western boundary of the Lee Metcalf Wilderness, Montana (UTM coordinates Zone 12, 457966 E., 4982589 N.), shall be known and designated as
Alex Diekmann Peak
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, record, or other paper of the United States to the peak described in subsection (a) shall be considered to be a reference to
Alex Diekmann Peak
.
