1.
Delay of reimposition of annual fee on health insurance providers until after 2020
(a)
In general
Section 9010(j)(3) of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act is amended by striking
December 31, 2019
and inserting
December 31, 2020
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by this section shall apply to calendar years beginning after December 31, 2019.
