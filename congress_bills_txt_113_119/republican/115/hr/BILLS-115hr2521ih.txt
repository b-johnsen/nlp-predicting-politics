1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
South Carolina Peanut Parity Act of 2017
.
2.
South Carolina inclusion in Virginia/Carolina peanut producing region
Section 1308(c)(2)(B)(iii) of the Farm Security and Rural Investment Act of 2002 (
7 U.S.C. 7958(c)(2)(B)(iii)
) is amended by striking
Virginia and North Carolina
and inserting
Virginia, North Carolina, and South Carolina
.
