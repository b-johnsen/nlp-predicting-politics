1.
J. Elliott Williams Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 201 Tom Hall Street in Fort Mill, South Carolina, shall be known and designated as the
J. Elliott Williams Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
J. Elliott Williams Post Office Building
.
