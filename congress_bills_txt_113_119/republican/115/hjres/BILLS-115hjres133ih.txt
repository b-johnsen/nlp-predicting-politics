JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for the reappointment of Barbara M. Barrett as a citizen regent of the Board of Regents
of the Smithsonian Institution.
That, in accordance with section 5581 of the Revised Statutes of the United States (
20 U.S.C. 43
), the vacancy on the Board of Regents of the Smithsonian Institution, in the class other than Members of Congress, occurring by reason of the expiration of the term of Barbara M. Barrett of Arizona on January 10, 2019, is filled by the reappointment of the incumbent. The appointment is for a term of 6 years, beginning on January 11, 2019, or the date of the enactment of this joint resolution, whichever occurs later.
