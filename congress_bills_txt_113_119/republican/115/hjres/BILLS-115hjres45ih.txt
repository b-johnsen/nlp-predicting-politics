JOINT RESOLUTION
Disapproving the rule submitted by the United States Fish and Wildlife Service of the Department of the Interior relating to management of non-Federal oil and gas rights.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the United States Fish and Wildlife Service of the Department of the Interior relating to
Management of Non-Federal Oil and Gas Rights
(published at 81 Fed. Reg. 79948 (November 14, 2016)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
