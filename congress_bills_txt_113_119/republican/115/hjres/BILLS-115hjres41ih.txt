JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of a rule
submitted by the Securities and Exchange Commission relating to
Disclosure of Payments by Resource Extraction Issuers
.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Securities and Exchange Commission relating to
Disclosure of Payments by Resource Extraction Issuers
(published at 81 Fed. Reg. 49359 (July 27, 2016)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
