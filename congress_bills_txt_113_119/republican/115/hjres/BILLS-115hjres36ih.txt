JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the
final rule of the Bureau of Land Management relating to
Waste Prevention, Production Subject to Royalties, and Resource Conservation
.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Bureau of Land Management relating to
Waste Prevention, Production Subject to Royalties, and Resource Conservation
(published at 81 Fed. Reg. 83008 (November 18, 2016)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
