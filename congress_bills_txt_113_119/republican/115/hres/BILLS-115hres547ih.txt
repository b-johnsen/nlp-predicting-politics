Whereas in 2016, the National Crime Information Center had nearly 88,000 cases of actively missing Americans, of those, more than 76,000 were under the age of 21;
Whereas the establishment of United States Missing Persons Day is due in part to the efforts of Jo Ann Lowitzer, whose daughter, Alexandria Joy
