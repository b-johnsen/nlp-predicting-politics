William Gard and Constance Rhoda Keely Yates to pursue innovative medical

Whereas a baby is not a statistic to be managed but rather a life, to be supported in every way possible;
Whereas an experimental treatment exists in the United States that could prolong the life of Charles Matthew William Gard (
Charlie

That the courts of the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Island and the European Court of Human Rights should allow Charles William Gard and Constance Rhoda Keely Yates to pursue innovative medical care for their son, Charles Matthew William Gard (
Charlie
