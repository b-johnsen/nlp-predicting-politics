JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the
final rule submitted by the Secretary of Health and Human Services
relating to compliance with title X requirements by project recipients in
selecting subrecipients.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Secretary of Health and Human Services relating to compliance with title X requirements by project recipients in selecting subrecipients (81 Fed. Reg. 91852; December 19, 2016), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
