JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule
submitted by the Securities and Exchange Commission relating to the
disclosure of
payments by resource extraction issuers.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Securities and Exchange Commission relating to the disclosure of payments by resource extraction issuers (81 Fed. Reg. 49360 (July 27, 2016)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
