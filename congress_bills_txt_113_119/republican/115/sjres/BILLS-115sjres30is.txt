JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for the reappointment of Steve Case as a citizen regent of the Board of Regents of the
Smithsonian Institution.
That, in accordance with section 5581 of the Revised Statutes (
20 U.S.C. 43
), the vacancy on the Board of Regents of the Smithsonian Institution, in the class other than Members of Congress, occurring by reason of the expiration of the term of Steve Case of Virginia on April 25, 2017, is filled by the reappointment of the incumbent. The reappointment is for a term of 6 years, beginning on the later of April 26, 2017, or the date of the enactment of this joint resolution.
