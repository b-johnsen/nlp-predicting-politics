1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
USPTO Funds for Efficient and Effective Services Act
or the
USPTO FEES Act
.
2.
Extension of sunset on fee-setting authority
Section 10(i)(2) of the Leahy-Smith America Invents Act (
35 U.S.C. 41
note) is amended by striking
the 7-year period beginning on the date of the enactment of this Act
and inserting
the 8-year period beginning on the date of enactment of the USPTO FEES Act
.
