(d)
Enhanced communication
(1)
Communications with Congress
Each fiscal year, as applicable, representatives from the Center for Drug Evaluation and Research and the Center for Biologics Evaluation and Research shall meet with representatives from the Committee on Health, Education, Labor, and Pensions of the Senate and the Committee on Energy and Commerce of the House of Representatives regarding the contents of the corrective action reports described in subsection (c)(2) and the annual performance reports under subsection (a).
