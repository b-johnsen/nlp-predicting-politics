1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Consumer Financial Protection Bureau Examination and Reporting Threshold Act of 2017
.
2.
Increase in the examination threshold
Section 1025(a) of the Consumer Financial Protection Act of 2010 (
12 U.S.C. 5515(a)
) is amended by striking
$10,000,000,000
each place that term appears and inserting
$50,000,000,000
.
3.
Increase in the reporting threshold
Section 1026(a) of the Consumer Financial Protection Act of 2010 (
12 U.S.C. 5516(a)
) is amended by striking
$10,000,000,000
each place that term appears and inserting
$50,000,000,000
.
4.
Effective date
This Act and the amendments made by this Act shall take effect on the date that is 45 days after the date of enactment of this Act.
