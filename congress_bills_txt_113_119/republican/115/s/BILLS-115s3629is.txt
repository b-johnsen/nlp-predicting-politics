1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Income Verification Act
.
2.
Verification of TANF income eligibility
(a)
Requirement
Section 408(a) of the Social Security Act (
42 U.S.C. 608(a)
) is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph:
(13)
Verification of income using Federal tax return information
A State to which a grant is made under section 403 shall use return information disclosed to the State agency responsible for administering the State program funded under this part in accordance with
section 6103(l)(7)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to verify the determination of an individual's or family's income for purposes of determining the individual's or family's eligibility for, and amount of, assistance under the State program funded under this part.
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by this section shall take effect on the date that is 180 days after the date of enactment of this Act.
