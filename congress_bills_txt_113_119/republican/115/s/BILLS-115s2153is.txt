2.
Electric vehicle weight limitation
Section 127 of title 23, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
(u)
Electric vehicles
(1)
In general
Except as provided in paragraph (2), a vehicle propelled exclusively by means of electric battery power may exceed any vehicle weight limit under this section by an amount that is equal to the difference betweenâ
(A)
the weight of the electric batteries and wiring system of the vehicle; and
(B)
the weight of a comparable diesel tank and fueling system.
(2)
Maximum weight
A vehicle propelled exclusively by means of electric battery power may exceed any vehicle weight limit under this section up to a maximum gross vehicle weight of 82,000 pounds.
