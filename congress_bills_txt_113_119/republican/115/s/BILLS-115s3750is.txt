1.
Short Title
This Act may be cited as the
Common Sense Credit Union Capital Relief Act of 2018
.
2.
Delay in effective date
Notwithstanding any effective date set forth in the rule issued by the National Credit Union Administration titled
Risk-Based Capital
(published at 80 Fed. Reg. 66626 (October 29, 2015)), such final rule shall take effect on January 1, 2021.
