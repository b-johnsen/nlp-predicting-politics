(7)
Renewal guarantee
The term
renewal guarantee
, with respect to short-term limited duration insurance, means a provision in the contract that permits a policyholder, when purchasing the initial insurance contract, to pay an additional amount for a guarantee that the policyholder can elect to purchase, for periods of time following expiration of the initial contract, another policy or policies at some future date, at a specific premium that would not reflect any additional underwriting.
