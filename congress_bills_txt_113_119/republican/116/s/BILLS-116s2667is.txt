(1)
Married parents

(2)
Divorced or separated parents
Parental income and assets for a student whose parents are divorced or separated, but not remarried, is determined:
(A)
By including only the income and assets of the parent with whom the student resided for the greater portion of the 12-month period preceding the date of the application.

If both parents have died, the student shall not report any parental income or assets.

Remarried parents
