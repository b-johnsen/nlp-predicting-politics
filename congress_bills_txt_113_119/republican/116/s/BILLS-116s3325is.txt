Definitions of parenting time agreement and noncustodial parent
(1)
Parenting time agreement
For purposes of subsection (f)(2), the term
parenting time agreement
means an agreement governing how much time a child spends with the child's custodial parent and the child's noncustodial parent that is mutually agreed to by the parents and is not contested by either parent in any forum.
(2)
Noncustodial parent
For purposes of paragraph (1), the term
noncustodial parent
means the parent of a child that the child does not live with for the majority of the child's time.
