2.
Addressing the threat from investments that finance Communist Chinese military companies
Not later than one year after the date of enactment of this Act, the Secretary of Labor, in consultation with the Secretary of Defense and, as the Secretary of Labor determines appropriate, heads of other Federal agencies, shall prohibit pension plans (as defined in section 3 of the Employee Retirement Income Security Act of 1974 (
29 U.S.C. 1002
)) from investing in any persons on the list of Communist Chinese military companies under section 1237(b) of the Strom Thurmond National Defense Authorization Act of Fiscal Year 1999 (
