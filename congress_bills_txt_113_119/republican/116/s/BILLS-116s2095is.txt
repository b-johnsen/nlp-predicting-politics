1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Enhancing Grid Security through Public-Private Partnerships Act
.
2.
Definitions
In this Act:
(1)
Electric Reliability Organization
The term
Electric Reliability Organization
has the meaning given the term in section 215(a) of the Federal Power Act (
16 U.S.C. 824o(a)
).
(2)
Electric utility; State regulatory authority
The terms
electric utility
and
State regulatory authority
have the meanings given those terms in section 3 of the Federal Power Act (
16 U.S.C. 796
).
(3)
Secretary
The term
Secretary
means the Secretary of Energy.
