2.
Reimbursement of interest payments related to public assistance
Title IV of the Robert T. Stafford Disaster Relief and Emergency Assistance Act (
42 U.S.C. 5170
et seq.) is amended by adding at the end the following:
431.
Reimbursement of interest payments related to public assistance
(a)
In general
The President may provide financial assistance to a local government as reimbursement for qualifying interest.
(b)
Definitions
In this section, the following definitions apply:
(1)
Prime rate
The term
prime rate
means the average predominant prime rate quoted by commercial banks to large businesses, as determined by the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System.
