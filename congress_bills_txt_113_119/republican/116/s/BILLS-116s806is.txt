1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Own Your Own Data Act
.
2.
Protection of personal information generated on internet
(a)
In general
Each individual owns and has an exclusive property right in the data that an individual generates on the internet under section 5 of the Federal Trade Commission Act (
15 U.S.C. 45
).

(2)
Regulations
The Federal Trade Commission shall promulgate regulations carrying out this subsection, which shall be approved by Congress.
(c)
Enforcement
The Federal Trade Commission shall enforce the provisions of this Act.
