(b)
Repeal of headquarters location requirement
Section 72 of title 4, United States Code, is repealed.
(c)
Prohibition on location of headquarters in Washington metropolitan area
With respect to a headquarters of an Executive agency that is located in the Washington metropolitan area as of the date of enactment of this Act, no new construction or major renovation may be undertaken, or lease agreement entered into or renewed, for the headquarters after the date of enactment of this Act, except as otherwise expressly provided by law.
