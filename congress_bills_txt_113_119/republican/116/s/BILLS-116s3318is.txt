1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Transparent Health Care Pricing Act of 2020
.
2.
Transparency in All Health Care Pricing
(a)
In general
All providers to the public of health care items, products, services, or procedures shall disclose to the public, on a continuous basis, the cash prices for such items, products, services, or procedures in accordance with this section.
