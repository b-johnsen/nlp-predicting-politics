(b)
Small Business Administration programs at historically Black colleges and universities
Not later than 180 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Administrator shall submit to the Committee on Small Business and Entrepreneurship of the Senate and the Committee on Small Business of the House of Representatives a report that examines the number and scope of programs of the Administration at historically Black colleges or universities, as compared with other institutions of higher education.
