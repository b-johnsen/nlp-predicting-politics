Amendments to definitions of the Economic Growth and Regulatory Paperwork Reduction Act

12 U.S.C. 252
note) is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraphs:

Covered person
The term
covered person

(9)
Federal financial regulator
The term
Federal financial regulator
means the Office of the Comptroller of the Currency, the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation, the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System, the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection, and the National Credit Union Administration Board.
