5.
Longitudinal study on the effectiveness of student loan counseling
(a)
In general

(b)
Contents
(1)

(2)

(c)
Interim Reports
Not later than 18 months after the commencement of the study under subsection (a), and annually thereafter, the Secretary of Education shall evaluate the progress of the study and report any short-term findings to the appropriate committees of Congress.
