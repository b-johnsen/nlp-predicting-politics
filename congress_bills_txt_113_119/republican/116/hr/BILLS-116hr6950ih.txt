1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Generating Artificial Intelligence Networking Security Act
or the
GAINS Act
.
2.
Artificial intelligence study
(a)
In general
(1)
Study required
Not later than 2 years after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Secretary of Commerce and the Federal Trade Commission, in coordination with the head of any other appropriate Federal agency, shall conduct a study on the impact of artificial intelligence, including machine learning, on United States businesses conducting interstate commerce.

Not later than 6 months after the completion of the study required pursuant to subsection (a), the Secretary and the Commission, in coordination with any other appropriate Federal agency, shall submit to the Committee on Energy and Commerce of the House of Representatives and the Committee on Commerce, Science, and Transportation of the Senate a report that containsâ
