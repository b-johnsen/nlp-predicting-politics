1.
Melinda Gene Piccotti Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 445 Main Street in Laceyville, Pennsylvania, shall be known and designated as the
Melinda Gene Piccotti Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Melinda Gene Piccotti Post Office
.
