1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Giving Retailers and Our Convenience Employees Relief Act of 2020
or as the
GROCER Act of 2020
.
2.
Exclusion from gross income for certain workers
(a)
In general
For purposes of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986, gross income shall not include specified income of any qualified employee.
(b)
Limitation
The aggregate amount not included in the gross income of any individual by reason of subsection (a) shall not exceed $25,000.
(c)
Qualified employee
For purposes of this section, the term
qualified employee
means any individual who provides services at a business concern that is assigned a North American Industry Classification System code beginning with 4244, 4247, 4451, or 4471, and which is located in a county that has at least one confirmed case of COVID-19.

Applicable period
