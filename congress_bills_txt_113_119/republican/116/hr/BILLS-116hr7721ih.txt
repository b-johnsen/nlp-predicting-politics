2.
Displaced farm worker credit
(a)
In general
Subpart C of part IV of subchapter A of chapter 1 is amended by inserting before section 37 the following new section:
36C.
Displaced farm worker credit
(a)
In general
In the case of a taxpayer engaged in the trade or business of farming, there shall be allowed as a credit against the tax imposed by this subtitle an amount equal to 40 percent of the qualified first-year wages of an eligible replacement worker.
