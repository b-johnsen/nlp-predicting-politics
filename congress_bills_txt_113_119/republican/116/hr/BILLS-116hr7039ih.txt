1.
Officer Paul Dunn Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 210 North Missouri Avenue in Lakeland, Florida, shall be known and designated as the
Officer Paul Dunn Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Officer Paul Dunn Post Office Building
.
