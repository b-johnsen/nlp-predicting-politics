1.
Captain Kimberly Nicole Hampton Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 810 South Pendleton Street in Easley, South Carolina, shall be known and designated as the
Captain Kimberly Nicole Hampton Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Captain Kimberly Nicole Hampton Post Office Building
.
