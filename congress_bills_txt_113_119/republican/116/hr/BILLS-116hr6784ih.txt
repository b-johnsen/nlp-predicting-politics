1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Time to Repeal an Archaic Policy Act of 2020
or the
TRAP Act of 2020
.
2.
Repeal of provision relating to private correspondence with foreign governments
(a)
Repeal
Section 953 of title 18, United States Code, is repealed.
(b)
Conforming amendment
The table of sections at the beginning of
chapter 45
of title 18, United States Code, is amended by striking the item relating to section 953.
(c)
Effective date
The repeal made by subsection (a) shall take effect on the date of the enactment of this Act.
