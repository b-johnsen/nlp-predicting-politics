(d)
Written record of interactions with court
Such section is further amended by adding at the end the following new subsection:
(l)
Written record of interactions
The Attorney General shall maintain all written communications with the court established under subsection (a), including the identity of the employees of the court to or from whom the communications were made, regarding an application or order made under this title in a file associated with the application or order. The Attorney General shall document a summary of any oral communications with the court, including the identity of the employees of the court to or from whom the communications were made, relating to such an application or order and keep such documentation in a file associated with the application or order.
.
