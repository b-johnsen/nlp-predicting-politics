1.
Replacement of certain John H. Chafee coastal barrier resources system maps
(a)
Cape San Blas P30/P30P (1 of 2)
The map entitled
Cape San Blas Unit P30/P30P (1 of 2)
and dated January 11, 2016, as included in the set of maps entitled
Coastal Barrier Resources System
referred to in section 4(a) of the Coastal Barrier Resources Act (
16 U.S.C. 3503(a)
), is hereby replaced by another map relating to that unit entitled
Cape San Blas Unit P30/P30P (1 of 2)
and dated December 19, 2018.
(b)
Cape San Blas P30/P30P (2 of 2)
The map entitled
Cape San Blas Unit P30/P30P (2 of 2)
and dated January 11, 2016, as included in the set of maps entitled
Coastal Barrier Resources System
referred to in section 4(a) of the Coastal Barrier Resources Act (
16 U.S.C. 3503(a)
), is hereby replaced by another map relating to that unit entitled
Cape San Blas Unit P30/P30P (2 of 2)
and dated December 19, 2018.
