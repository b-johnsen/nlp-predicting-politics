1.
Certificate of documentation for vessel
Safari Voyager
(a)
In general
Notwithstanding sections 12112 and 12132 of title 46, United States Code, the Secretary of the department in which the Coast Guard is operating shall issue a certificate of documentation with a coastwise endorsement for the vessel
Safari Voyager
(International Maritime Organization number 8963753).
(b)
Revocation of effectiveness of certificate
A certificate of documentation issued under subsection (a) is revoked on the date of the sale of the vessel by the owner of the vessel.
