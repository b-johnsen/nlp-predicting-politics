1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Protecting Life in Global Health Assistance Act of 2019
.
2.
Prohibition on assistance to foreign nonprofits, foreign nongovernmental organizations, and quasi-autonomous nongovernmental organizations that promote or perform abortions
Chapter 1 of part III of the Foreign Assistance Act of 1961 (
22 U.S.C. 2351
et seq.) is amended by adding at the end the following new section:
620N.
Prohibition on assistance to foreign nonprofits, foreign nongovernmental organizations, and quasi-autonomous nongovernmental organizations that promote or perform abortions
No assistance may be furnished under this Act to any foreign nonprofit, foreign nongovernmental organization, or quasi-autonomous nongovernmental organization that promotes or performs abortion, except in cases of rape or incest or when the life of the mother would be endangered if the fetus were carried to term.
.
