(1)

The Secretary of the Interior (referred to in this section as the

(3)

(C)

(D)
consider alternatives for the management, administration, and protection of the site by the Federal Government, State or local governmental entities, or private and non-profit organizations;
(E)
determine the effect of the designation of the site as a unit of the National Park System on existing commercial and recreational uses and the effect on State and local governments to manage those activities; and
