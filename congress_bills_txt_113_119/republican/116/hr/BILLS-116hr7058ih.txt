2.
Payroll credit for bonuses paid to employees who worked continuously during the COVID-19 pandemic
(a)
In general
In the case of an employer, there shall be allowed as a credit against applicable employment taxes for the calendar quarter which includes the last day of the covered period an amount equal to 100 percent of the qualified bonuses paid to eligible employees of such employer during such calendar quarter.

For purposes of section 1324 of title 31, United States Code, any amounts due to the employer under this paragraph shall be treated in the same manner as a refund due from a credit provision referred to in subsection (b)(2) of such section.
