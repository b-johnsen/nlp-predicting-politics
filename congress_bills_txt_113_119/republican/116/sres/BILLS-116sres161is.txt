Supporting the goals and ideals of World Malaria Day.

Whereas malaria is a leading cause of death and disease in many developing countries, despite being preventable and treatable;

1/2

Whereas children under the age of 5 account for an estimated 61 percent of malaria deaths each year;
Whereas the World Malaria Report 2018 published by the World Health Organization states that, in 2017, approximately 435,000 people died from malaria, which is a decrease of approximately 50 percent, as compared to 2000;
