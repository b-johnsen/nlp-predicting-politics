1.
Prohibition on Russian air carriers
(a)
In general
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the Secretary of Transportation shall ensure that the following aircraft may not operate in the national airspace system:
(1)
A private aircraft listed by the Secretary of State pursuant to subsection (b).
(2)
Any aircraft of a foreign air carrier, as such term is defined in section 40102 of title 49, United States Code, owned or operated by an entity organized under the laws of the Russian Federation, or any jurisdiction within the Russian Federation.
