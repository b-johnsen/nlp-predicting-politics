1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Republic of Georgia Sovereignty Act
.
2.
Prohibition against United States recognition of the claims of sovereignty of South Ossetia or Abkhazia within the Republic of Georgia
(a)
Statement of policy
It is the policy of the United States not to recognize the claims of sovereignty of the following areas within the Republic of Georgia:
(1)
South Ossetia.
(2)
Abkhazia.
(b)
Prohibition
In accordance with subsection (a), no Federal department or agency may take any action or extend any assistance that implies recognition of the claims of sovereignty of South Ossetia or Abkhazia within the Republic of Georgia, or any other closely-related successor entities.
