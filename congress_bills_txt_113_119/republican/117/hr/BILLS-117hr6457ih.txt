1.
Prohibition on Federal funds for certain local educational agencies
(a)
In general
No Federal funds shall be provided to a local educational agency unless in-person instruction is available to all students at each public elementary school and secondary school under the jurisdiction of the local educational agency.
(b)
Definitions
In this Act, the terms
elementary school
,
local educational agency
, and
secondary school
have the meanings given such terms in section 8101 of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965 (
20 U.S.C. 7801
).
