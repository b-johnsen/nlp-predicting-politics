1.
Regulation of natural gas connections
(a)
In general
No State, local government, or State regulatory authority may prohibit a natural gas consumer from connecting or reconnecting to a gas utility for natural gas service.
(b)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)
The terms
State regulatory authority
and
gas utility
have the meanings given such terms in section 302 of the Public Utility Regulatory Policies Act of 1978 (
15 U.S.C. 3202
).
(2)
The term
natural gas consumer
has the meaning given the term
gas consumer
in section 302 of the Public Utility Regulatory Policies Act of 1978 (
15 U.S.C. 3202
).
