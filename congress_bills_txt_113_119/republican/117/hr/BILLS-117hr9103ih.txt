3.
Fire, insects, and diseases
Nothing in this Act or the amendment made by section 2 shall be construed to limit the authority of the Secretary of Agriculture under section 4(d)(1) of the Wilderness Act (
16 U.S.C. 1133(d)(1)
), in accordance with existing laws (including regulations).
4.
References to wilderness area
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the wilderness areas described in the amendment made by section 2, including areas previously designated as the Flatside Wilderness and the Bethune Woods-Flatside Wilderness, shall be deemed to be a reference to the Flatside-Bethune Wilderness.
