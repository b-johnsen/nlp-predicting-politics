2.
Prohibition on Federal funding for National Public Radio
(a)
In general
After the date of the enactment of this Act, no Federal funds may, directly or indirectly, be made available to or used to support the organization described in subsection (b), including through the payment of dues to or the purchase of programming from such organization by a public broadcast station using Federal funds received by such station.
