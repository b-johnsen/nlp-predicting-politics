1.
Prohibition on purchase of public or private real estate in the United States by foreign governments
(a)
In general
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the President shall take such actions as may be necessary to prohibit the purchase of public or private real estate located in the United States by a foreign government, including a foreign government using a shell corporation that is owned or controlled by such government.
