1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Partial Birth Abortion Is Murder Act
.
2.
Increased penalty
Section 1531(a) of title 18, United States Code, is amended by striking
Any physician who, in or affecting interstate or foreign commerce, knowingly performs a partial-birth abortion and thereby kills a human fetus shall be fined under this title or imprisoned not more than 2 years, or both.
and inserting the following:
Any physician who, in or affecting interstate or foreign commerce, knowingly performs a partial-birth abortion and thereby kills a human fetus shall be imprisoned for any term of years or for life (but in no event for not less than 5 years), may in addition be fined under this title, and shall not be eligible for supervised release.
.
