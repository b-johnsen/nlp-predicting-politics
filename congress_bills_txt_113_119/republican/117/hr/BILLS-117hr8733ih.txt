1.
Definition of economically disadvantaged community
(a)
In general
In carrying out section 160 of the Water Resources Development Act of 2020 (
33 U.S.C. 2201
note), the Secretary of the Army shall include in the definition of
economically disadvantaged community
any area that is designated as eligible to receive assistance under the Robert T. Stafford Disaster Relief and Emergency Assistance Act (
42 U.S.C. 5121 et seq.
) as a result of a major disaster declaration under section 401 of such Act (
42 U.S.C. 5170
).
(b)
Applicability
An area described in subsection (a) may only be included in the definition of
economically disadvantaged community
during the 5-year period beginning on the date on which a major disaster with respect to such area is declared.
