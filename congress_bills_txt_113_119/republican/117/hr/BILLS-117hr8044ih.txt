1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Planes Not Panels Act
.
2.
Limitation on presidential action
Section 303(a) of the Defense Production Act of 1950 (
50 U.S.C. 4533(a)
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(8)
Solar panels
(A)
In general
The President may not use any authority under this section to make provision for purchases of or commitments to purchase solar panels.
(B)
Solar panel defined
In this paragraph, the term
solar panel
means a crystalline silicon photovoltaic cell or module.
.
