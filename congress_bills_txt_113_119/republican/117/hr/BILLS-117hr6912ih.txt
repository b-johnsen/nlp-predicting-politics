1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Pooled Employer Plan Fix Act
.
2.
Pooled employer plan fiduciary requirement
Section 3(43)(B)(ii) of the Employee Retirement Income Security Act of 1974 (
29 U.S.C. 1002(43)(B)(ii)
) is amended to read as follows:
(ii)
designate a named fiduciary (other than an employer in the plan) to be responsible for collecting contributions to the plan and require such fiduciary to implement written contribution collection procedures that are reasonable, diligent, and systematic;
.
