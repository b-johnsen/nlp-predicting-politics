1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Military Fitness Incentive Tuition Act
or the
Military FIT Act
.
2.
Allowance for gym membership for members of the Armed Forces who reside more than 10 miles from a military installation
(a)
Establishment
Chapter 7
of title 37, United States Code, is amended by inserting after
section 425
the following new section:
426.
Allowance for gym membership for members of the armed forces who reside more than 10 miles from a military installation
(a)
Allowance authorized
The Secretary concerned shall pay, to a member of the armed forces who resides more than 10 miles from a military installation, a monthly allowance for a gym membership.
(b)
Amount
The monthly allowance to a member under this section shall be in an amount determined by the Secretary of Defense based on the average cost of a gym membership in the military housing area in which the member resides.
.
(b)
Clerical amendment
The table of sections at the beginning of such chapter is amended by inserting after the item relating to section 425 the following:
426. Allowance for gym membership for members of the armed forces who reside more than 10 miles from a military installation.
.
