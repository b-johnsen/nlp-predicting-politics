Limitation of preference
Beginning on the date of enactment of this paragraph and ending on the date that is 5 years after such date, intercity and commuter rail passenger transportation provided by or for Amtrak shall not have preference over freight transportation in using a rail line, junction, or crossing if such rail line, junction, or crossing is located within 50 miles of a port or rail yard.
(3)
Waiver by Secretary
The Secretary may issue such regulations as are necessary to continue the preference waiver described in paragraph (2).
