1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
ESG Rule Prevention Act
.
2.
Prohibition on requiring disclosure of greenhouse gas emissions and implementation of greenhouse gas emission reduction targets by Federal contractors
(a)
In general
Chapter 47
of title 41, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
4715.
Prohibition on requiring disclosure of greenhouse gas emissions and implementation of greenhouse gas emission reduction targets by Federal contractors.
(a)
Prohibition
The head of an agency may not require as a condition of eligibility for a contract with the agency that a personâ
(1)
disclose to the head of the agency the amount of greenhouse gas emissions the person is responsible for; or
(2)
meet an emissions reduction target for greenhouse gas emissions during the term of the contract concerned.
(b)
Agency defined
In this section, the term
agency
has the meaning given such term in section 551 of title 5.
.
(b)
Application
The prohibition established under section 4715 of title 41, United States Code, shall only apply to contracts entered into after the date of the enactment of this section.

Table of sections

of title 41, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
