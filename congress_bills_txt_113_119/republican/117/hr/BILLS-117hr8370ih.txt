1.
Corporal Joseph Rodney Chapman Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 415 High Street in Freeport, Pennsylvania, shall be known and designated as the
Corporal Joseph Rodney Chapman Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Corporal Joseph Rodney Chapman Post Office
.
