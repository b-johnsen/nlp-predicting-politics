RESOLUTION
Celebrating the 100th anniversary of the Mississippi Farm Bureau Federation.
Whereas the American Farm Bureau was established in 1919 to disseminate college research results to farmers and has since grown into one of the strongest agriculture advocacy organizations in the United States, with 6,000,000 members and affiliated services and member benefits;
Whereas the Mississippi Farm Bureau Federation was officially chartered on October 30, 1922, in Jackson, Mississippi, and C.L. Neill of Jones County was elected as the first president of the Mississippi Farm Bureau Federation;
Whereas the Mississippi Farm Bureau Federation is a strong voice in agriculture for farmers, ranchers, and rural Mississippians;
Whereas the mission of the Mississippi Farm Bureau Federation is to create an environment in which Mississippi farmers, ranchers, and Farm Bureau members can have a better life and make a better living; and
Whereas October 30, 2022, marks the 100th anniversary of the Mississippi Farm Bureau Federation: Now, therefore, be it
