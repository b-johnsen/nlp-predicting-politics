Whereas Russia has conscripted involuntary soldiers serving in its military;
Whereas there are reports that over 5,000 Russian troops have been killed or captured as of March 1, 2022;
Whereas there are reports of Russian soldiers abandoning military equipment;
Whereas captured Russian soldiers have expressed confusion as to the purpose of the war with Ukraine;
Whereas Russian military authorities have threatened physical abuse or worse to enforce discipline in some units;
Whereas Russian soldiers could be executed for desertion;
Whereas Russian soldiers and citizens have reported that they initially believed the Russian military was only conducting training exercises;

Whereas Ukraine has offered amnesty and compensation for Russian soldiers who surrender;
