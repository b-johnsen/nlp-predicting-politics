1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
SALT Deduction Fairness Act
.
2.
Increase in limitation on deduction for individuals filing jointly on state and local taxes
(a)
In general
Section 164(b)(6)(B)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking
$10,000 ($5,000 in the case of a married individual filing a separate return)
and inserting
$10,000 (twice such amount in the case of a joint return)
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by this section shall apply to taxable years beginning after December 31, 2020.
