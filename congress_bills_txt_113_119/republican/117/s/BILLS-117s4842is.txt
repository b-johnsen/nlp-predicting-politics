2.
Findings; sense of Congress
(a)
Findings
Congress makes the following findings:
(1)
The United States Uniformed Services are composed of the worldâs most highly trained and professional servicemembers.
(2)

(3)
Attracting additional trained and credentialed mariners, particularly from active duty servicemembers and military veterans, will support United States national security requirements and provide meaningful, well-paying jobs to United States veterans.
(4)
There is a need to ensure that the Federal Government has a robust, state of the art, and efficient merchant mariner credentialing system to support economic and national security.
