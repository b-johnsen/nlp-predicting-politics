1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
No Taxpayer Funds for Illegal Alien Abortions Act
.
2.
Restriction on the transportation of aliens
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, an employee or contractor of U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement or the Department of Health and Human Services, acting within the scope of the individual's employment or contract, may not transport, or arrange for the transportation of, any alien who is unlawfully present in the United States across any State line for the purpose of procuring an abortion for such alien.
