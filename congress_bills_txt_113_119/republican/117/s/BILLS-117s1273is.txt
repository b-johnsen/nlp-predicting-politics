(b)
Eligible employer plan
For purposes of this section, the term
eligible employer plan
means a qualified employer plan (within the meaning of section 4972(d)) in which all eligible military spouse employees of the covered small employerâ

(c)
Covered small employer
For purposes of this section, the term
covered small employer
means an eligible employer (within the meaning of section 408(p)(2)(C)(i)).

In general

Any certification under subparagraph (C) shall include the servicemember's name, rank, and military branch and the employee's uniformed services identification card number.
(2)
Limitation
An individual may not be treated as an eligible military spouse with respect to any covered small employer for more than 3 taxable years.
