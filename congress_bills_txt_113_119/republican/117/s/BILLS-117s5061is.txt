(A)
The term
advanced psychology trainee
means a postdoctoral resident who has obtained a doctoral degree in psychology, is seeking a license to practice psychology, and is engaged in a 1- or 2-year period of additional supervised experiential training to acquire the skills or hours required for licensure through a program accredited by an organization determined appropriate by the Secretary.
(B)
The term
general supervision
has the meaning given that term in section 410.26(a)(3) of title 42, Code of Federal Regulations (or any successor regulation).

(2)
Effective date
The amendments made by this subsection shall apply to services furnished on or after January 1, 2024.
(b)
Establishment of modifier
Not later than January 1, 2024, the Secretary of Health and Human Services shall establish a modifier to indicate services furnished by an advanced psychology trainee pursuant to the amendments made by subsection (a).
