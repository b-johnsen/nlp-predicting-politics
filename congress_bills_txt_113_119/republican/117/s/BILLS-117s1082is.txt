1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Telling Agencies to Stop Tweaking what Employees Eat Act of 2021
or the
TASTEE Act of 2021
.
2.
Prohibition on dining facilities
(a)
Definition of agency
In this section, the term
agency
has the meaning given the term in section 551 of title 5, United States Code.
(b)
Prohibition
An agency, or a contractor of an agency that provides food services to a dining facility of the agency, may not establish a policy that prohibits a dining facility of the agency from serving a particular type of food.
