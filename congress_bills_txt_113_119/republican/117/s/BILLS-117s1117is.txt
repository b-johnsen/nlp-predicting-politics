(d)
Effective date
(1)
In general
Except as provided in paragraph (2), the amendments made by this section shall take effect on the date of enactment of this Act.
(2)
Delayed compliance
A State plan shall not be regarded as failing to comply with the requirements imposed by the amendments made by this section before the end of the 60-day period that begins on the day after the last day of the 1st regular session of the State legislature that begins after the date of enactment of this Act. For purposes of the previous sentence, in the case of a State that has a 2-year legislative session, each year of such session shall be deemed to be a separate regular session of the State legislature.
