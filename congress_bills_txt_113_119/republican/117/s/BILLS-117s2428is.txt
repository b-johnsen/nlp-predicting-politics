2.
False claims procedure
(a)
Proving materiality
Section 3729 of title 31, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
(e)
Proving materiality
(1)
In general
In an action under this section, the Government or relator may establish materiality by a preponderance of the evidence.
(2)
Rebuttal
A defendant may rebut an argument of materiality under paragraph (1) by clear and convincing evidence.
.
