Frozen fruits and vegetables under the special supplemental nutrition program for women, infants, and children
Section 17(f)(11) of the Child Nutrition Act of 1966 (
42 U.S.C. 1786(f)(11)
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(D)
Frozen fruits and vegetables
In the regulations prescribed under subparagraph (A), the Secretary shall require a State agency to make available under the program frozen fruits and frozen vegetables.
