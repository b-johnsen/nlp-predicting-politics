1.
Reduced user fees
(a)
Amendments
Section 7528(b)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph:
(5)
Rules relating to reduced fees
(A)
In general
Any fee as reduced under paragraph (2)(A) shall not exceed 5 percent of the lowest fee charged under this subsection for any non-qualified person.
(B)
Applicability
(i)
In general
The Secretary shall provide for a reduced fee under paragraph (2)(A) only for persons who are not non-qualified persons.
(ii)
Non-qualified person
For purposes of this paragraph, with respect to any taxable year, the term
non-qualified person
means any person whose gross income for the taxable year is $5,000,000 or more.

Adjustment for Inflation

In general

2021

2016
in subparagraph (A)(ii) thereof.
(ii)
Rounding
If any amount as adjusted under clause (i) is not a multiple of $10,000, such amount shall be rounded to the nearest multiple of $10,000.
(D)
Termination
This paragraph shall not apply to any taxable year beginning after December 31, 2025.

(b)
Effective date
The amendments made by this section shall apply to fees charged after the date of the enactment of this Act.
