1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Seniors Saving on Insulin Act
.
2.
Continuation of Part D Senior Savings Model
Section 1115A of the Social Security Act (
42 U.S.C. 1315a
) is amended by adding at the end the following new subsection:
(h)
Part D Senior Savings Model
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the Secretary shall provide for the continued implementation on a permanent basis of the Part D Senior Savings Model under this section, under the same parameters under which such model was implemented for plan year 2021.
.
