2.
Prohibition on use of funds for the Montreal Protocol on Substances that Deplete the Ozone Layer until China is no longer defined a developing country
(a)
In general
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, no Federal funds may be obligated or expended to implement the Montreal Protocol until the President certifies to the appropriate congressional committees that the Parties to the Montreal Protocol have amended their Decision I/12E,
Clarification of terms and definitions: developing countries,
made at the First Meeting of the Parties to remove the People's Republic of China.
