1.
Short titles
This Act may be cited as the
Fighting Oppression until the Reign of Castro Ends Act
or the
FORCE Act
.
2.
Prohibition on removal
(a)
In general
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, neither the President nor the Secretary of State may remove Cuba from the list of state sponsors of terrorism until the President makes the determination described in section 205 of the Cuban Liberty and Democratic Solidarity (LIBERTAD) Act of 1996 (
22 U.S.C. 6065
).
(b)
Defined term
In this section, the term
state sponsor of terrorism

);
(2)
section 620A of the Foreign Assistance Act of 1961 (
22 U.S.C. 2371
);
(3)
section 40 of the Arms Export Control Act (
22 U.S.C. 2780
); or
(4)
any other provision of law.
