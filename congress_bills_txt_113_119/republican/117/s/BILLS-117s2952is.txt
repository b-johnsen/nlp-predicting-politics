Nonclinical test or study defined
For purposes of this section, the term
nonclinical test or study
means a test or study conducted in vitro, in silico, in chemico, or in vivo that occurs before or during the clinical trial phase of the investigation of the safety and effectiveness of a drug, and may include the following:
(1)
Cell-based assays.
(2)
Organ chips and microphysiological systems.
(3)
Computer models.
(4)
Other non-animal or human biology-based test methods.
