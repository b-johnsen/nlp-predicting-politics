Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Council on Environmental Quality relating to
National Environmental Policy Act Implementing Regulations Revisions

National Environmental Policy Act Implementing Regulations Revisions
(87 Fed. Reg. 23453 (April 20, 2022)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
