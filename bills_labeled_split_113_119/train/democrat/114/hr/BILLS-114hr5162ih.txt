Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Vet Connect Act of 2016
.
2.
Authority to disclose certain medical records of veterans who receive non-Department of Veterans Affairs health care
Section 7332(b)(2) of title 38, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following new subparagraph:
(H)
To a non-Department entity (including private entities and other departments or agencies of the Federal Government) that provides hospital care or medical treatment to veterans.
.
