1.
Designation
The Federal building and United States courthouse located at 511 East San Antonio Avenue in El Paso, Texas, shall be known and designated as the
R.E. Thomason Federal Building and United States Courthouse
.
2.
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the Federal building and United States courthouse referred to in section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to the
R.E. Thomason Federal Building and United States Courthouse
.
