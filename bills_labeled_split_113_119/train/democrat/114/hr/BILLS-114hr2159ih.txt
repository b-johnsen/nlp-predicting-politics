1.
EXTENSION OF CREDIT FOR ENERGY-EFFICIENT NEW HOMES
(a)
In General
Subsection (g) of
section 45L
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking
December 31, 2014
and inserting
December 31, 2015
.
(b)
Effective Date
The amendments made by this section shall apply to homes acquired after December 31, 2014.
