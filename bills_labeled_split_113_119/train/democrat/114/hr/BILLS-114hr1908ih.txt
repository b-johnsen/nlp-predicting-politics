Housing Financial Literacy Act of 2015
.
2.
Discount on mortgage insurance premium payments for first-time homebuyers who complete financial literacy housing counseling programs
The second sentence of subparagraph (A) of section 203(c)(2) of the National Housing Act (
12 U.S.C. 1709(c)(2)(A)
) is amended by striking
not exceed 2.75 percent of the amount of the original insured principal obligation of the mortgage
and inserting
be 25 basis points lower than the premium payment amount established by the Secretary under the first sentence of this subparagraph
.
