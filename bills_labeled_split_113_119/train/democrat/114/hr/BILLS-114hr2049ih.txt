1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Keeping Foreign Money Out of Politics Act of 2015
.
2.
Prohibiting contributions and donations by foreign nationals in connection with ballot initiatives and referenda
(a)
Prohibition
Section 319(a)(1)(A) of the Federal Election Campaign Act of 1971 (
52 U.S.C. 30121(a)(1)(A)
) is amended by striking
election;
and inserting the following:
election, including a State or local ballot initiative or referendum;
.
(b)
Effective Date
The amendment made by subsection (a) shall apply with respect to contributions or donations made, solicited, accepted, or received on or after the date of the enactment of this Act.
