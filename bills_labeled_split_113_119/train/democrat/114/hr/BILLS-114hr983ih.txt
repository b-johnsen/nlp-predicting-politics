1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Safe And Fair Environment on Highways Achieved through Underwriting Levels Act of 2015
or the
SAFE HAUL Act of 2015
.
2.
Minimum financial responsibility for transporting property
(a)
In general
Section 31139(b)(2) of title 49, United States Code, is amended by striking
$750,000.
and inserting
$4,532,550 and the Secretary, in consultation with the Bureau of Labor Statistics, shall adjust such amount annually for inflation relating to medical care.
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by this section shall take effect on the date that is 180 days after the date of enactment of this Act.
