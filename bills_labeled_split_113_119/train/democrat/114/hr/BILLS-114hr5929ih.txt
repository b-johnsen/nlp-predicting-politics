1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Menstrual Products for Employees Act
.
2.
Menstrual hygiene products
Section 6 of the Occupational Safety and Health Act of 1970 (
29 U.S.C. 655
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(g)
The Secretary shall by rule promulgate a requirement that each employer with not less than 100 employees provide menstrual hygiene products free of charge for employees of the employer.
.
