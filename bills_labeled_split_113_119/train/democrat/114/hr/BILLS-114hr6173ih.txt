(a)

Subpart C of part IV of subchapter A of

Commuting mass transit costs

In general
In the case of an individual, there shall be allowed as a credit against the tax imposed by this subtitle for such taxable year an amount equal to 15 percent of the amount paid or incurred by the taxpayer during such taxable year for a transit pass (as defined in section 132(f)(5)(A)) for use by the taxpayer or the taxpayerâs spouse or dependents for travel between the residence and place of employment of such taxpayer, spouse, or dependent, as the case may be.

(1)

The credit allowed under subsection (a) with respect to transit passes for use by any individual for any taxable year shall not exceed $460.
