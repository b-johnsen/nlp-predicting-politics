Prohibition on premium subsidy for harvest price policies
Section 508(e) of the Federal Crop Insurance Act (
7 U.S.C. 1508(e)

(9)
Prohibition on premium subsidy for harvest price policies
Notwithstanding any other provision of law and beginning with the 2016 reinsurance year, the Corporation may not pay any amount of premium subsidy in the case of a policy or plan of insurance that is based on the actual market price of an agricultural commodity at the time of harvest.
