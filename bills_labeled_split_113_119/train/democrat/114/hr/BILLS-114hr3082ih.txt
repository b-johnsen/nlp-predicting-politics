1.
Daryle Holloway Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 5919 Chef Menteur Highway in New Orleans, Louisiana, shall be known and designated as the
Daryle Holloway Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Daryle Holloway Post Office Building
.
