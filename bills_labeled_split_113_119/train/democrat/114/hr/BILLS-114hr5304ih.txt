(b)
Effect of employer-Sponsored health plan coverage
Paragraph (7) of such subsection is amended by adding at the end the following new subparagraph:
(C)
During any period during the final 180 days of the one-year transition period specified in paragraph (4) that the member is employed and eligible for an employer-sponsored health plan by reason of such employment, the member may not be eligible for transitional health care under this subsection.
.
(c)
Application
The amendments made by this section shall apply with respect to a member of the Armed Forces who is separated from the Armed Forces before, on, or after the date of the enactment of this Act.
