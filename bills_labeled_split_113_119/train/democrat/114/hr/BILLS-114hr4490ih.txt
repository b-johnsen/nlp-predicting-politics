(b)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)
In general
The terms defined in section 40102 of title 49, United States Code, have, respectively, the definitions given such terms in that section.
(2)
Seat pitch
The term
seat pitch
means the distance from any point on one seat to the same point on the seat in front of or behind it.
