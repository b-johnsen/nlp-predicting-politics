Additional district judgeships for the district of Colorado
(a)
Additional district judgeships
The President shall appoint, by and with the advice and consent of the Senate, 2 additional district judges for the district of Colorado.
(b)
Technical and conforming amendment
The table in section 133(a) of title 28, United States Code, is amended by striking the item relating to Colorado and inserting the following:
Colorado
9
.
(c)
Effective date
This Act, and the amendments made by this Act, shall take effect on January 21, 2017.
