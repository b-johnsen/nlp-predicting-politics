1.
Herman Badillo Post Office
Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 442 East 167th Street in Bronx, New York, shall be known and designated as the
Herman Badillo Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Herman Badillo Post Office Building
.
