1.
Officer Daryl R. Pierson Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 206 West Commercial Street in East Rochester, New York, shall be known and designated as the
Officer Daryl R. Pierson Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Officer Daryl R. Pierson Post Office
.
