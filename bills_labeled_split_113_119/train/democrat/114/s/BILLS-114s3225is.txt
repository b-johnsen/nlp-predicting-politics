Public reporting of procurement quotas for opioid analgesics
(a)
In general
Section 306 of the Controlled Substances Act (
21 U.S.C. 826

(i)
(1)
In this subsection, the term
opioid procurement quota
means a quota established by the Attorney General for the quantity of opioid analgesics that a registered manufacturer may procure for purposes of manufacturing dosage forms or other substances.
