1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Conserving Ecosystems by Ceasing the Importation of Large (CECIL) Animal Trophies Act
.
2.
Amendment to Endangered Species Act of 1973
Section 9(a)(1) of the Endangered Species Act of 1973 (
16 U.S.C. 1538(a)(1)
) is amended by striking subparagraph (A) and inserting the following:
(A)
for any such species listed, or proposed to be listed, under section 4, import the species into, or export the species from, the United States;
.
