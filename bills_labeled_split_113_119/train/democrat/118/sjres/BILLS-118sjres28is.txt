JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for the reappointment of Roger W. Ferguson as a citizen regent of the Board of Regents of the Smithsonian Institution.
That in accordance with section 5581 of the Revised Statutes of the United States (
20 U.S.C. 43
) the vacancy on the Board of Regents of the Smithsonian Institution, in the class other than Members of Congress, occurring by reason of the expiration of the term of Roger W. Ferguson of Florida on April 25, 2023, is filled by the reappointment of the incumbent. The reappointment is for a term of six years, beginning on the later of April 25, 2023, or the date of enactment of this joint resolution.
