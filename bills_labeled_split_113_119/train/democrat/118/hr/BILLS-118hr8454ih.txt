Counseling in the Transition Assistance Program regarding sexual assault, sexual or gender harassment, and intimate partner violence
Section 1142(b) of title 10, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph:
(20)
Information concerning health care (including mental health care) furnished by the Secretary of Veterans Affairs to veterans and members of the Armed Forces who have survived sexual assault, sexual or gender harassment, or intimate partner violence.
