(b)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)
Federal mineral leasing law
The term
Federal mineral leasing law
means any Federal law administered by the Secretary of the Interior authorizing the disposition under lease of oil or gas.
(2)
Federal oil and gas lease
The term
Federal oil and gas lease
means an oil and gas lease issued by the Secretary of the Interior under any of the Federal mineral leasing laws.
(3)
OPEC country
The term
OPEC country
means Algeria, Congo, Equatorial Guinea, Gabon, Iran, Kuwait, Libya, Nigeria, Saudi Arabia, United Arab Emirates, or Venezuela.
(4)
OPEC plus country
The term
OPEC plus country
means Azerbaijan, Bahrain, Brunei, Kazakhstan, Malaysia, Mexico, Oman, Russia, South Sudan, or Sudan.
