6.
Rule of construction
Nothing in this Act, or an amendment made by this Act, shall be construed to alter legal standards regarding, or affect the rights (including remedies and procedures) available to individuals under the Constitution of the United States or other Federal laws that establish protections for freedom of speech or expression.
