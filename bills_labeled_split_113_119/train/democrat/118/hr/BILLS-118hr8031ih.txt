4.
Designation of chuckwalla national monument
(a)
Establishment
Subject to valid existing rights, there is established the Chuckwalla National Monument in the State, consisting of approximately 621,000 acres of Federal land administered by the Bureau of Land Management, as generally depicted on the Map.
(b)

The purpose of the Monument is to conserve, protect, and enhance for the benefit and enjoyment of present and future generations, through cooperative and collaborative management with culturally affiliated Indian Tribes, the ecological, scenic, wildlife, recreational, cultural, historical, natural, educational, and scientific resources of the Monument.
