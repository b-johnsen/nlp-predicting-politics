1.
Report on Department of Defense restrictions on employment of former Department employees by certain countries
(a)
In general
Not later than March 30, 2024, the Secretary of Defense shall submit to the Committees on Armed Services of the Senate and House of Representatives a report that includes each of the following:
(1)
The policy of the Department of Defense in effect as of the date of the enactment of this Act with respect to any restrictions on the employment of former Department employees by countries that exhibit adversarial behavior against the interests of the United States.
(2)
An identification of the positions in the Department to which such restrictions apply.
(3)
An identification of any incident when a former employee of the Department worked for, or sought to work for, any such country in contravention of such a restriction.
(b)
Form of report
The report required by subsection (a) shall be submitted in unclassified form, but may include a classified annex.
