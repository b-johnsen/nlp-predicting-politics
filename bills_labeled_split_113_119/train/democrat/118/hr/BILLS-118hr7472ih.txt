1.
Alberta Odell Jones Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 835 South 7th Street in Louisville, Kentucky, shall be known and designated as the
Alberta Odell Jones Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Alberta Odell Jones Post Office Building
.
