1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Trust and Modernization in Tax Governance Act
or the
TMTG Act
.
2.
Limitation for deferral of gain on sale of property to comply with conflict-of-interest requirements
(a)
In general
Section 1043(b)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph:
(7)
Limitation for executive branch officers
(A)
In general
In the case of an individual who is an officer of the executive branch of the Federal Government (including the President and the Vice President), the aggregate amount of gain with respect to which such individual may elect the application of subsection (a) for all taxable years shall not exceed $100,000,000.
(B)
Recognition of gain after service

shall be recognized in such taxable year.
