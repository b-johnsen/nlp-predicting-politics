Short title

.
2.
Termination of authority for certain educational assistance program
(a)
Survivors' and Dependents' Educational Assistance
Chapter 35
of title 38, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following new subchapter:
VIII
Termination
3571.
Termination of authority
(a)
In general
The authority to provide educational assistance under this chapter shall terminate on August 1, 2028.
(b)
Eligibility for post-9/11 educational assistance
An individual who is no longer eligible for educational assistance under this chapter pursuant to subsection (a) shall be entitled to educational assistance under chapter 33 of this title in the same manner as if the individual had elected to receive educational assistance under section 3327 of this title, notwithstanding the restrictions on crediting service under section 3322(h)(1) of this title, but subject to the limitations under section 3327(d)(2) of this title.
.
