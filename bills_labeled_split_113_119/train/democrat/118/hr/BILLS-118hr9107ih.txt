1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Airborne Hazards and Open Burn Pit Registry (AHOBPR) 2.0 Act
.
2.
Report on Airborne Hazards and Open Burn Pit Registry 2.0
Not later than 90 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Secretary of Veterans Affairs shall submit to Congress a report on the current status and timeline for when the redesigned Airborne Hazards and Open Burn Pit Registry 2.0 will be completed.
