4.
Comptroller General report on efforts of Department of Veterans Affairs in supporting family caregivers transitioning away from caregiving
Not later than two years after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Comptroller General of the United States shall submit to Congress a report assessing the efforts of the Department of Veterans Affairs in supporting individuals serving as family caregivers under section 1720G(a) of title 38, United States Code, in transitioning away from caregiving, either by assisting those individuals with retirement planning or returning to work.
