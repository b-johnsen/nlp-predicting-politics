1.
Gladys P. Todd Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 1285 Emancipation Highway in Fredericksburg, Virginia, shall be known and designated as the
Gladys P. Todd Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Gladys P. Todd Post Office
.
