1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Protect Our Local workers Act
or the
POL Act
.
2.
Use of funds under HAVA for physical security of polling places and poll workers
(a)
Authorizing use of payments
Section 101(b)(1) of the Help America Vote Act of 2002 (
52 U.S.C. 20901(b)(1)
) is amended by adding at the end the following new subparagraph:
(I)
Ensuring the physical security of election offices, including polling places, and election workers, including poll workers.
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by subsection (a) shall apply with respect to funds made available to States under the Help America Vote Act of 2002 at any time before or after the date of the enactment of this Act.
