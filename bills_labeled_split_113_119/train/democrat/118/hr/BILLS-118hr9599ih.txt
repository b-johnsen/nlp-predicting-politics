Workforce program
Title IV of the Violent Crime Control and Law Enforcement Act of 1994 is amended by adding at the end the following:

(b)
Use of funds
Any grant awarded under this section may be used for programs that provide the following:
(1)
Activities to help survivors secure employment, including obtaining employment counseling, occupational training, job retention counseling, and counseling concerning re-entry into the workforce.
(2)
Outreach activities to make survivors aware of services offered under this program.
(3)
Outreach activities to connect survivors with employers that meet their needs and that support a safe workspace for survivors.

(c)
Application
To receive a grant under this section, an eligible entity shall submit an application to the Director at such time, in such manner, and accompanied by such information as the Director may reasonably require.

(e)
Survivors defined
The term

means a survivor of domestic violence, dating violence, sexual assault, gender-based violence, trafficking, sexual harassment, and stalking.
