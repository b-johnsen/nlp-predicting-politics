(a)
In general
Not later than 180 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Secretary of Labor, working through the Employment and Training Administration, shall establish a grant program to provide eligible entities described in subsection (d) with funds to assist individuals who are unemployed or underemployed in order to document in writing and images the current state of the American experience.
