Definition of airport development

(ii)
The requirements described in this clause are the following:
(I)
The airport and the facilities and fixed-based operators on, or connected with, the airport are operated and maintained in a manner the Secretary of Transportation considers suitable for disaster relief. An airport shall not be considered as failing to meet the requirement under the preceding sentence if a runway is unusable because the runway is under scheduled maintenance or is in need of necessary repairs.
(II)
The airport has developed an emergency natural disaster management plan in coordination with State and local officials.
