1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Defending Hunters' Education Act of 2023
.
2.
Exception
Section 8526(7) of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965 (
20 U.S.C. 7906(7)
) is amended by inserting
, except that this paragraph shall not apply to the use of funds under this Act for activities carried out under programs authorized by this Act that are otherwise permissible under such programs and that provide students with educational enrichment activities and instruction, such as archery, hunter safety education, or culinary arts
before the period at the end.
