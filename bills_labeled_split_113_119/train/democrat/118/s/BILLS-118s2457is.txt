1.
Office of Self-Governance
(a)
Establishment
Subtitle A of the Department of Agriculture Reorganization Act of 1994 (
7 U.S.C. 6912 et seq.
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
224B.
Office of Self-Governance
(a)
Authorization
The Secretary shall establish in the Office of the Secretary an Office of Self-Governance.
(b)
Director
The Office of Self-Governance shall be headed by a Director.
(c)
Duties
The Director of the Office of Self-Governance shall oversee self-determination contracts and self-governance compact agreements entered into between the Secretary and Tribal organizations under the Indian Self-Determination and Education Assistance Act (
25 U.S.C. 5301 et seq.
).
.
(b)
Conforming amendments
(1)
Subtitle A of the Department of Agriculture Reorganization Act of 1994 (
7 U.S.C. 6912 et seq.
) is amended by redesignating section 225 (relating to a Food Access Liaison) (
7 U.S.C. 6925
) as section 224A.
(2)
Section 296(b) of the Department of Agriculture Reorganization Act of 1994 (
7 U.S.C. 7014(b)
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(11)
The authority of the Secretary to carry out section 224B.
.
