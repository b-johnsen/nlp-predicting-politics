1.
Senator Dianne Feinstein Federal Building
(a)
Designation
The Federal building located at 50 United Nations Plaza in San Francisco, California, shall be known and designated as the
Senator Dianne Feinstein Federal Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the Federal building referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Senator Dianne Feinstein Federal Building
.
