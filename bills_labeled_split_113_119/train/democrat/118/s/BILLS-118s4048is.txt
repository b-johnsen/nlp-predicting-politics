1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
North American Wetlands Conservation Extension Act
.
2.
Reauthorization
Section 7(c) of the North American Wetlands Conservation Act (
16 U.S.C. 4406(c)
) is amended by striking
of this Act not to exceed $60,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2021 through 2025
and inserting
not more than $65,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2024 through 2028
.
