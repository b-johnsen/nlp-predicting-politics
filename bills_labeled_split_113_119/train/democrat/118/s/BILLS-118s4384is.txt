Short title

Georgia Stormwater Management Act
.
2.
Columbus, Henry, and Clayton Counties, Georgia
Section 219(f) of the Water Resources Development Act of 1992 (106 Stat. 4835; 113 Stat. 334; 136 Stat. 3808) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(406)
Columbus, Henry, and Clayton Counties, Georgia
$10,000,000 for environmental infrastructure, including water and wastewater infrastructure (including stormwater management), Columbus, Henry, and Clayton Counties, Georgia.
