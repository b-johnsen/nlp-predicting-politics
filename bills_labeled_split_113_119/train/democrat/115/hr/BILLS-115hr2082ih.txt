1.
Waiver of grounds for removal of, or denial of admission to, Daniela Vargas
(a)
In general
Notwithstanding sections 212(a) and 237(a) of the Immigration and Nationality Act, Daniela Vargas may not be removed from the United States, or denied admission to the United States, by reason of any act of hers, or on her behalf during the period of infancy, that is a ground for removal or denial of admission and is reflected in the records of the Department of Homeland Security, or the Visa Office of the Department of State, on the date of the enactment of this Act.
(b)
Rescission of outstanding order of removal
The Secretary of Homeland Security shall rescind any outstanding order of removal, or any finding that Daniela Vargas is inadmissible or subject to removal, that has been entered against Daniela Vargas by reason of any act described in subsection (a).
