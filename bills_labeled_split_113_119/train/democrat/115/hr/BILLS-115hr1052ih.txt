1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
High-Risk Military Occupation Veteran Suicide Prevention Act
.
2.
Care of high-risk military occupation veterans in Department of Veterans Affairs mental health care and suicide prevention programs
Subparagraph (E) of section 1709B(a)(2) of title 38, United States Code, is amended to read as follows:
(E)
propose best practices for caring for individuals who suffer from mental health disorders, are at risk of suicide, or previously held high-risk military occupations, including such practices conducted or suggested by other departments or agencies of the Federal Government, including the Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration of the Department of Health and Human Services; and
.
