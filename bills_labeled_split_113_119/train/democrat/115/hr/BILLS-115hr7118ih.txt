1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Excessive Use of Force Prevention Act of 2018
.
2.
Chokeholds as civil rights violations
Section 242 of title 18, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
For the purposes of this section, the application of any pressure to the throat or windpipe which may prevent or hinder breathing or reduce intake of air is a punishment, pain, or penalty.
.
