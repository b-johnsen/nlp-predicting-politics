1.
Sergeant First Class Alwyn Crendall Cashe Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 567 East Franklin Street in Oviedo, Florida, shall be known and designated as the
Sergeant First Class Alwyn Crendall Cashe Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Sergeant First Class Alwyn Crendall Cashe Post Office Building
.
