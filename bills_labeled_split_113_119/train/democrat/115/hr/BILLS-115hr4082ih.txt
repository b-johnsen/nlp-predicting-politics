1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Protect Access to Birth Control Act
.
2.
Repeal
The rules issued by the Department of the Treasury, Department of Labor, and Department of Health and Human Services entitled
Religious Exemptions and Accommodations for Coverage of Certain Preventive Services Under the Affordable Care Act
(82 Fed. Reg. 47792 (October 13, 2017)) and
Moral Exemptions and Accommodations for Coverage of Certain Preventive Services Under the Affordable Care Act
(82 Fed. Reg. 47838 (October 13, 2017)) shall have no force or effect, and shall be treated as though such rules had never taken effect.
