An eligible entity may not submit, either individually or as part of a joint application, more than 1 application for a grant under this section during any 1 fiscal year.
(h)

The amount of a single grant provided under this section for any 24-month period shall not exceed $5,000,000.
(i)

The non-Federal share of the cost of a job training and education program carried out using a grant under this section shall be not less than 25 percent of the total cost.
