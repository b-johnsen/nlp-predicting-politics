3.
Fellowship program
(a)
Establishment
The Secretary of Defense shall establish a fellowship program, which shall be known as the
Ronald V. Dellums Memorial Fellowship for Women of Color in STEAM
, to provide scholarships and internships for eligible students with high potential talent in STEAM.

(B)

(C)

(2)

The Secretary may not award fewer than 50 percent of the fellowships under this section to eligible students who attend historically Black colleges and universities and other minority-serving institutions, including Hispanic-serving institutions, Asian American and Native American Pacific Islander-serving institutions, American Indian Tribally controlled colleges and universities, Alaska Native and Native Hawaiian-serving institutions, Predominantly Black Institutions, and Native American-serving, Nontribal institutions.
