Short title

2.
Sense of Congress regarding disaster loan processing
It is the sense of Congress that the Administrator of the Small Business Administration should use district offices of the Administration whenever possible to expedite the processing of disaster loans under section 7(b) of the Small Business Act (
15 U.S.C. 636(b)
).
