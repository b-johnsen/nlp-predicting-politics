1.
Captain Joshua E. Steele Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 108 West D Street in Alpha, Illinois, shall be known and designated as the
Captain Joshua E. Steele Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Captain Joshua E. Steele Post Office
.
