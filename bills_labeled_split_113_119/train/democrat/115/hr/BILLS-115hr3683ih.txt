1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Student and Families Tax Reduction Act
.
2.
Permanent extension of deduction for qualified tuition and related expenses
(a)
In general
Section 222
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking subsection (e).
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by this section shall apply to taxable years beginning after December 31, 2016.
