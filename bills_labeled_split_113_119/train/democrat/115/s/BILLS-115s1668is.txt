1.
Joseph Sanford Jr. Channel
(a)
In general
The waterway in the State of New York designated as the
Negro Bar Channel
shall be known and redesignated as the
Joseph Sanford Jr. Channel
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the waterway referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Joseph Sanford Jr. Channel
.
