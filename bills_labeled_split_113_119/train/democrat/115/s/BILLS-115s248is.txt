2.
Prohibition against use of funds
No funds, resources, or fees made available to the Secretary of Homeland Security, or to any other official of a Federal agency, by this Act or any other Act for any fiscal year, may be used to implement or enforce the Executive order titled
Protecting the Nation From Foreign Terrorist Entry Into the United States
signed on January 27, 2017.
