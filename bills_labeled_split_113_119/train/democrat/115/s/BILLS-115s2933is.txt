4.
Regulations
Not later than 1 year after the date of enactment of this Act, the Register of Copyrights may promulgate regulations to modify the notice, deposit, and registration requirements under sections 401 through 410 of title 17, United States Code, with respect to a sound recording fixed before February 15, 1972.
5.
Effective date
Except with respect to any regulations that the Register of Copyrights promulgates under section 4, this Act, and the amendments made by this Act, shall take effect on the date that is 1 year after the date of enactment of this Act.
