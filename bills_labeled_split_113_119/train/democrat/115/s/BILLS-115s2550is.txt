1.
Tuskegee Airman Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 99 Macombs Place in New York, New York, shall be known and designated as the
Tuskegee Airman Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Tuskegee Airman Post Office Building
.
