1.
Redesignation of the Hobe Sound National Wildlife Refuge
(a)
Redesignation
The Hobe Sound National Wildlife Refuge, located in the State of Florida, is redesignated as the
Nathaniel P. Reed Hobe Sound National Wildlife Refuge
.
(b)
References
Any reference in any statute, rule, regulation, Executive order, publication, map, paper, or other document of the United States to the Hobe Sound National Wildlife Refuge is deemed to refer to the Nathaniel P. Reed Hobe Sound National Wildlife Refuge.
