1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Stopping Threats on Pedestrians Act of 2017
or the
STOP Act of 2017
.
2.
Traffic barrier installation grant program
(a)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)
Secretary
The term
Secretary
means the Secretary of Transportation.
(2)
Traffic barrier installation project
The term
traffic barrier installation project
means a project to install traffic barriers, which may include bollards, jersey barriers, planters, or other barriers approved by the Secretary, on a sidewalk, bicycle path, or pedestrian plaza or walkway adjacent to a roadway that are designed to slow or stop a motor vehicle.
(b)
Establishment
Not later than 1 year after the date of enactment of this Act, the Secretary shall establish and carry out a competitive grant program to provide assistance to eligible entities for traffic barrier installation projects designed to prevent pedestrian injuries and acts of terrorism in areas used by large numbers of pedestrians.
