United Kingdom includes nearly two and a half centuries of cultural,
political, military, economic, and diplomatic relations;

values of democracy, individual freedom, and the rule of law;
Whereas British statesman Winston Churchill in 1946 labeled the relationship between the United
States and the United Kingdom the Special Relationship because of the
unprecedented depth of cooperation and the extensive historic ties between
the two nations;

fought together in several struggles, including the First and Second World
Wars, in which millions of American and British soldiers sacrificed for
their countries;

Government, a referendum will be held in Scotland on September 18, 2014,
on whether Scotland should be an independent country; and
