1.
Designation
The United States courthouse located at 333
West Broadway in San Diego, California, shall be known and designated as the
James M. Carter and Judith N. Keep United States
Courthouse
.
2.
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation,
document, paper, or other record of the United States to the United States
courthouse referred to in section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to the
James M. Carter and Judith N. Keep United States
Courthouse
.
