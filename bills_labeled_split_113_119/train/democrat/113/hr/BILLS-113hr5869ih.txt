2.
Concealment of homicide
Chapter 51
of title 18, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following new
section:
1123.
Concealment of homicide
Any person who, in violation of section 1111, 1114, 1116, or 1121 of this title, in an interstate
act conceals or attempts to conceal a homicide shall be punished by
imprisonment for any term of years or for life.
