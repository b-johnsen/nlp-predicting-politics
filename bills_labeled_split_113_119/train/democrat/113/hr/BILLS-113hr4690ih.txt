2.
Authorization to establish memorial
(a)
In general
The National Emergency Medical Services Memorial foundation may establish the National Emergency
Medical Services Memorial as a commemorative work on Federal land in the
District of Columbia to honor the commitment and ultimate sacrifice of
emergency medical service providers.
(b)
Compliance With Standards for Commemorative Works
The memorial shall be established in accordance with
chapter 89
of title 40, United States Code.
(c)
Prohibition on the Use of Federal Funds
The United States shall not pay any expense of the establishment of the memorial.
