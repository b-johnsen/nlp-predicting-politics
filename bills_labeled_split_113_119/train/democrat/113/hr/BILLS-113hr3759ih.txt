1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Military Reserve Small Business Jobs Act of 2013
.
2.
Extension of employer wage credit for activated military reservists
(a)
In general
Section 45P(f)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking
December 31, 2013
and inserting
December 31, 2018
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by this section shall apply to payments made after December 31, 2013.
