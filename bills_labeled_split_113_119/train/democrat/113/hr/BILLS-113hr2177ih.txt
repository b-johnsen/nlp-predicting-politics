2.
Eliminating
application of 251A sequestration to certain unemployment benefits
(a)
Fiscal year
2013
Notwithstanding the
presidential order issued on March 1, 2013, under section 251A of the Balanced
Budget and Emergency Deficit Control Act of 1985, the budgetary resources
sequestered under that order for any Federal payments to a State under section
204 of the Federal-State Extended Unemployment Compensation Act of 1970 and for
any Federal payments to a State under section 4003(a) of title IV of the
Supplemental Appropriations Act, 2008, pursuant to a Federal-State agreement
under section 4001 of such title, shall be available for obligation in the same
amount and manner as if such order had not been issued.
