1.
Study on insurance steering and redlining
(a)
Study
The Director of the Federal Insurance Office shall carry out a study to determine whether the
insurance industry is engaging in the illegal practices of insurance
steering or redlining. In carrying out such study, the Director shall
investigate insurance products, pricing, promotions, and placement of
services being offered to consumers in the United States.

Report
