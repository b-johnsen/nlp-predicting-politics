(A)
Waiver
The Attorney General, in consultation with the Director of National Intelligence, may waive the requirement to make a decision, order, or opinion described in subsection (c) publicly available under such subsection if the Attorney General determines the public disclosure of such decision, order, or opinion (including public disclosure in redacted form) would cause harm to the national security of the United States.
(B)
Report
The Attorney General shall quarterly make publicly available an estimate of the number of decisions, orders, or opinions for which the Attorney General waived under subparagraph (A) the requirement under subsection (c) to make such decisions, orders, or opinions publicly available and the reasons for waiving such requirement.
