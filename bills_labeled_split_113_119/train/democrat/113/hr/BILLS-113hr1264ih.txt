1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
District of Columbia Medicaid Reimbursement Act of 2013
.
2.
Increasing the Medicaid Federal medical assistance percentage for the District of Columbia to 75 percent
(a)
In general
Section 1905(b)(3) of the Social Security Act (
42 U.S.C. 1396d(b)(3)
) is amended by striking
70 percent
and inserting
75 percent
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by subsection (a) shall apply to calendar quarters beginning on or after October 1, 2013.
