1.
Reestablishment of
Professional Certification and Licensure Advisory Committee
(a)
Reestablishment
Section 3689(e)(5)
of title 38, United
States Code, is amended by striking
December 31, 2006
and
inserting
December 31, 2019
.
(b)
Appointment of
new members
In reestablishing
the Professional Certification and Licensure Advisory Committee under
subsection (e) of
section 3689
of title 38, United States Code, pursuant to
subsection (a), the Secretary of Veterans Affairs may appoint new members of
the Committee under paragraph (3)(A) of such subsection (e) without regard to
the individuals who served as members before the date of the enactment of this
Act.
