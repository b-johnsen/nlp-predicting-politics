1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Brownfields Redevelopment Tax Incentive Reauthorization Act of 2014
.
2.
Extension of expensing of environmental remediation costs
(a)
In general
Section 198(h)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking
December 31, 2011
and inserting
December 31, 2018
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by subsection (a) shall apply to expenditures paid or incurred after December
31, 2011.
