1.
Redesignation
The lock and dam located in Modoc, Illinois, authorized by the Act entitled
An Act authorizing the construction, repair, and preservation of certain public works on rivers and harbors, and for other purposes
, approved July 3, 1930 (46 Stat. 927), and commonly known as the Kaskaskia Lock and Dam, is redesignated as the
Jerry F. Costello Lock and Dam
.
2.
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the lock and dam referred to in section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Jerry F. Costello Lock and Dam
.
