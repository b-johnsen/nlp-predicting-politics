2.
Safety incentives to prevent operation of motor vehicles by intoxicated persons
(a)
In general
Section 163
of title 23, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
(g)
Marijuana impaired driving
In addition to the other requirements of this section, in the case of a State in which the use of
marijuana is legal under the laws of the State with or without medical
justification, the State shall be eligible for a grant under subsection
(b), and shall be exempt from withholding under subsection (e), for a
fiscal year only if the Stateâ
(1)
has in effect a law that prohibits an individual from driving or being in actual physical control
of a motor vehicle while impaired by marijuana as determined using
measures established by the State; and
(2)
enforces that law using training and methods for determining cognitive or physical marijuana
impairment.
.
(b)
Applicability
The amendment made by subsection (a) shall apply to fiscal years beginning after the date of
enactment of this Act.
