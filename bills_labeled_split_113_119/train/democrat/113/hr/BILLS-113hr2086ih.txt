Subchapter III of

5127.
Interim

(a)
In
general
In the case of a
claim described in subsection (b), prior to adjudicating the claim, the
Secretary shall make interim payments of monetary benefits to the claimant
based on any disability for which the Secretary has made a decision. Upon the
adjudication of the claim, the Secretary shall pay to the claimant any monetary
benefits awarded to the claimant for the period of payment under section 5111
of this title less the amount of such benefits paid to the claimant under this
section.
