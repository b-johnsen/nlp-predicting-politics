1.
Timely submission of certain
claims
Notwithstanding any
other provision of law, a claim submitted to an Indian Health Service
contracting officer pursuant to the Indian Self-Determination and Education
Assistance Act (
25 U.S.C. 450 et seq.
) on or before October 31, 2005, involving
a claim that accrued after October 1, 1995, and on or before September 30,
1999, shall be deemed to be timely submitted.
