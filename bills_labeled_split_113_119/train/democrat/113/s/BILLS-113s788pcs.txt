sequester
Notwithstanding the
sequestration order issued by the President pursuant to section 251A(7)(A) of
the Balanced Budget and Emergency Deficit Control Act of 1985 (2 U.S.C.

sections 3001 and 3004 of the Consolidated and Further Continuing
Appropriations Act, 2013, and any sequestration order issued by the
President.
