1.
Designation
The segment of Interstate Route 35 between milepost 133 at Forest Lake, Minnesota, and milepost 259
at Duluth, Minnesota, shall be known and designated as the
James L. Oberstar Memorial Highway
.
2.
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to
the segment of Interstate Route 35 referred to in section 1 shall be
deemed to be a reference to the
James L. Oberstar Memorial Highway
.
