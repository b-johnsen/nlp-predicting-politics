No person may extend any consumer credit
which is otherwise subject to this title through the use by the consumer of a
check, other negotiable instrument, or such other similar product as the Bureau

creditor sent the check, negotiable instrument, or other similar
product.

A certificate,
voucher, or other nonnegotiable instrument provided to a consumer in connection
with a solicitation for an extension of credit that has the appearance of a
check or other negotiable instrument is not an application or request for an
extension of credit for purposes of this section.
