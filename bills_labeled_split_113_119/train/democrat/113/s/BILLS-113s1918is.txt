1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Clarity for Military Retiree Health Care Act
.
2.
Special change in status rule for employees who become eligible for TRICARE
(a)
In general
Subsection (g) of
section 125
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph:
(5)
Change in status relating to TRICARE eligibility
For purposes of this section, if a cafeteria plan permits an employee to revoke an election during a period of coverage and to make a new election based on a change in status event, an event that causes the employee to become eligible for coverage under the TRICARE program shall be treated as a change in status event.
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by this section shall apply to events occurring after the date of the enactment of this Act.
