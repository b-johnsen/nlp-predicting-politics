2d Session
H. RES. 1128
In the House of Representatives, U. S.,
September 21, 2020
RESOLUTION
Expressing the profound sorrow of the House of Representatives on the death of the Honorable Ruth Bader Ginsburg.
That the House has heard with profound sorrow of the death of the Honorable Ruth Bader Ginsburg, Associate Justice of the Supreme Court of the United States.
That the House tenders its deep sympathy to the members of the family of the late Associate Justice in their bereavement.
That the Clerk communicate these resolutions to the Senate and to the Supreme Court and transmit a copy of the same to the family of the late Associate Justice.
That when the House adjourns today, it adjourn as a further mark of respect to the memory of the late Associate Justice.
Cheryl L. Johnson,
Clerk.
