In the House of Representatives, U. S.,
January 3, 2019
RESOLUTION
To inform the Senate that a quorum of the House has assembled and of the election of the Speaker and the Clerk
That the Senate be informed that a quorum of the House of Representatives has assembled; that Nancy Pelosi, a Representative from the State of California, has been elected Speaker; and that Karen L. Haas, a citizen of the State of Maryland, has been elected Clerk of the House of Representatives of the One Hundred Sixteenth Congress.
Karen L. Haas,
Clerk.
