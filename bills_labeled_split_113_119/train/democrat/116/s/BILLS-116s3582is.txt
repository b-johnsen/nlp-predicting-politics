1.
Findings
Congress finds the following:
(1)
In 2018, the United States aerospace and defense manufacturing industry contributed 2,550,000 jobs, accounted for $151,000,000,000 in exports, and supported 1,670,000 supply chain jobs.
(2)
In 2019, aerospace and defense parts manufacturing and distribution across the United States accounted for $25,400,000,000 in economic activity.
(3)
The United States aerospace and defense manufacturing industry is critical to the national economy and national defense.
