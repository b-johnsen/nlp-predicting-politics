4.
Termination of certain executive actions
(a)
Termination
Presidential Proclamations 9645 and 9822 and Executive Orders 13769, 13780, and 13815 shall be void beginning on the date of the enactment of this Act.
(b)
Effect
All actions made pursuant to any proclamation or executive order terminated under subsection (a) shall cease on the date of the enactment of this Act.
