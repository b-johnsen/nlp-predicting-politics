1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Local Water Protection Act
.
2.
Nonpoint source management programs
Section 319 of the Federal Water Pollution Control Act (
33 U.S.C. 1329
) is amended by striking subsection (j) and inserting the following:
(j)
Authorization of appropriations
(1)
In general
There is authorized to be appropriated to carry out subsections (h) and (i) $200,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2020 through 2024, of which not more than $7,500,000 for each fiscal year may be used to carry out subsection (i).
(2)
Availability
Amounts made available under paragraph (1) shall remain available until expended.
