1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Protecting Military Firefighters from PFAS Act
.
2.
Provision of blood testing for firefighters of Department of Defense to determine exposure to perfluoroalkyl and polyfluoroalkyl substances
The Secretary of Defense shall provide blood testing to determine and document potential exposure to perfluoroalkyl and polyfluoroalkyl substances (commonly known as
PFAS
) for each firefighter of the Department of Defense during the annual physical exam conducted by the Department for each such firefighter.
