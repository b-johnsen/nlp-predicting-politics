1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Remote Areas Border Security Enhancements Act
.
2.
Defined term
In this Act, the term
rural or remote area
means an area within the United States along the international border between the United States and Mexico that is not within an area defined and designated by the Bureau of the Census as an urbanized area.

5.
Agent Mobility Demonstration Program
(a)
In general
The Secretary of Homeland Security shall establish a 5-year pilot program in the El Paso Sector, to be known as the
Agent Mobility Program
, under which agents assigned within the El Paso Sector may laterally transfer to a designated, hard-to-fill station within the El Paso Sector for a period of at least 3 years.

6.
Authorization of appropriations
In addition to amounts otherwise authorized to be appropriated, there is authorized to be appropriated, to U.S. Customs and Border Protection, the amount determined to be necessary under the study required under section 3(a)(1) to carry out the activities described in section 3(a)(2).
