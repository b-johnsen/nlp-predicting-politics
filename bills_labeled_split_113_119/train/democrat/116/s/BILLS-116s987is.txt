8.
Report on the China Coast Guard
(a)
In general
Not later than 180 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Secretary of Defense and the Secretary of Homeland Security shall jointly submit to the appropriate congressional committees a report on the China Coast Guard (referred to in this section as the
CCG
).
(b)
Contents
The report submitted under subsection (a) shall include an assessment of the following:
(1)
How the change in the command structure of the CCG affects the status of the CCG as a law enforcement entity considering that the CCG now reports to the Central Military Commission.
(2)
The implications of the new command structure of the CCG with respect to the use of the CCG as a coercive tool in
gray zone

(3)
How the change in the command structure of the CCG may affect interactions between the United States Navy and the United States Coast Guard and the China Coast Guard.
(4)
Whether the China Coast Guard should be designated as a military force by the Secretary of Defense and the Secretary of Homeland Security.
(c)
Form
The report submitted under subsection (a) shall be submitted in unclassified form, but may include a classified annex.
