1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Battery Storage Innovation Act of 2019
.
2.
Loan guarantees for battery storage technologies
Section 1703(b) of the Energy Policy Act of 2005 (
42 U.S.C. 16513(b)
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(11)
Battery storage technologies for residential, industrial, or transportation applications.
.
