1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Military Spouse Relocation and Contract Reimbursement Act
.
2.
Availability to military spouses of amounts in My Career Advancement Account Program accounts for fines for breaking employment contracts in connection with member permanent changes of station
(a)
In general
Amounts in the My Career Advancement Account (MyCAA) Program account of a military spouse may be used to reimburse the military spouse for payment by the military spouse of a fine imposed on the military spouse by an employer in connection with the breaking of an employment contract if the military spouse breaks the employment contract for purposes of relocation with the member of the Armed Forces concerned due to a permanent change of station.
