2.
Findings; sense of Congress
(a)
Findings
Congress finds the following:
(1)
Women are now the fastest growing cohort of veterans and are expected to double in population by 2045.
(2)
Women comprise approximately 2,000,000 veterans in the United States and 10 percent of the total veteran population.
(3)
Women veterans are nearly two times more likely to commit suicide than civilian women.
(4)
On average, women do not connect with the Veterans Health Administration until 2.7 years after separating from military service.
(5)
Female veterans have a higher rate of homelessness than their male counterparts.
(6)
One in five female members of the Armed Forces report experiencing military sexual trauma.
