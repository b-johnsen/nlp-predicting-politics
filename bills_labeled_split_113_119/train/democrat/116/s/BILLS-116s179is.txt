Department of Veterans Affairs clinical trial of the effects of cannabis on certain health outcomes of adults with chronic pain and post-traumatic stress disorder

Clinical trial required
(1)
In general
The Secretary of Veterans Affairs shall carry out a double-blind randomized controlled clinical trial of the effects of medical-grade cannabis on the health outcomes of covered veterans diagnosed with chronic pain and covered veterans diagnosed with post-traumatic stress disorder.

(b)
Covered veterans
In this section, the term
covered veteran
means a veteran who is enrolled in the patient enrollment system of the Department of Veterans Affairs under section 1705 of title 38, United States Code.
