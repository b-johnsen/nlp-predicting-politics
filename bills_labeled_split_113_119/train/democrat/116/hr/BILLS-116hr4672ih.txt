1.
Petty Officer 2nd Class (SEAL) Matthew G. Axelson Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 21701 Stevens Creek Boulevard in Cupertino, California, shall be known and designated as the
Petty Officer 2nd Class (SEAL) Matthew G. Axelson Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Petty Officer 2nd Class (SEAL) Matthew G. Axelson Post Office Building
.
