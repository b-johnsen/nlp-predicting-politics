A group health plan or a health insurance issuer offering group or individual health insurance coverage may not impose a limitation or exclusion of benefits with respect to coverage of a drug described in subsection (a) other than a limitation or exclusion of benefits with respect to a use of such drug identified as contraindicated by the Food and Drug Administration.
