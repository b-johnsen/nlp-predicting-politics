2.
Report regarding veterans who receive benefits under laws administered by the Secretary of Veterans Affairs
(a)
Report required
Not later than 180 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Secretary of Veterans Affairs shall publish a report regarding veterans who receive benefits under laws administered by the Secretary, including the Transition Assistance Program under sections 1142 and 1144 of title 10, United States Code.
