1.
Findings
Congress makes the following findings:
(1)
The global shortage of medical supplies due to the COVID-19 pandemic has paralyzed healthcare systems around the world and threatens to collapse the United States healthcare system.
(2)
There are approximately 160,000 ventilators in the United States.
(3)
It has been estimated that as many as 742,000 could be needed.
(4)
The Department of Health and Human Services estimated that healthcare providers may need 3,500,000,000 face masks to meet potential long-term need.
