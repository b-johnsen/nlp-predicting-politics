1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Mary Ann Shadd Cary Post Office Dedication Act
.
2.
Mary Ann Shadd Cary Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 500 Delaware Avenue, Suite 1, in Wilmington, Delaware, shall be known and designated as the
Mary Ann Shadd Cary Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Mary Ann Shadd Cary Post Office
.
