1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Investor Choice Against Gun Proliferation Act
.
2.
Disclosure of substantial financial relationship with any manufacturer or dealer of firearms or ammunition
Section 13 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 (
15 U.S.C. 78m
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(s)
Disclosure of substantial financial relationship with manufacturer or dealer of firearms or
ammunition
Each issuer required to file an annual report under subsection (a) shall disclose in such report any substantial financial relationship such issuer has with any manufacturer or dealer of firearms or ammunition.
.
