(f)
Prohibition on security clearances to relatives of the President
(1)
In general
Except as provided for under paragraph (2), an agency, including the Department of Defense, may not grant eligibility for access to classified information to a relative of the President for the purposes of Federal employment unless a favorable determination is submitted to the head of the agency pursuant to paragraph (2)(C).
