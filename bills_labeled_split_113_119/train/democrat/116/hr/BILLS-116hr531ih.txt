1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Fighting for Intelligent, Rational, and Ethical Dismissal Act
or the
FIRED Act
.
2.
Removal of the Director of the Federal Bureau of Investigation
(a)
Removal for cause
Section 1101(b) of title VI of the Omnibus Crime Control and Safe Streets Act of 1968 (
Public Law 90â351
) is amended by adding at the end the following new sentence:
The President may remove the Director for inefficiency, neglect of duty, or malfeasance in office.
.
(b)
Application
The amendment made by subsection (a) shall apply to any Director of the Federal Bureau of Investigation appointed on or after the date of the enactment of this Act.
