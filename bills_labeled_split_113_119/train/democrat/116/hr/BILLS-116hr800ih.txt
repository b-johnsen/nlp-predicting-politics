2.
CRA credit for providing assistance during Government shutdowns
Section 804 of the Community Reinvestment Act of 1977 (
12 U.S.C. 2903
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(e)
Assistance during Government shutdowns
(1)
In general
In assessing and taking into account, under subsection (a), the record of a financial institution, the appropriate Federal financial supervisory agency shall consider, as a factor, efforts of the financial institution to modify terms on existing loans, extend new loans with low- to no-interest rates, or the waiving of fees to help covered employees, if such modification or extension is done in a safe and sound manner.
