1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Banning Smoking on Amtrak Act of 2019
.
2.
Prohibition on smoking on Amtrak trains
(a)
In general
Chapter 243
of title 49, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
24323.
Prohibition on smoking on Amtrak trains
(a)
Prohibition
Beginning on the date of enactment of the
Banning Smoking on Amtrak Act of 2019
, Amtrak shall prohibit smoking on board Amtrak trains.
(b)
Electronic Cigarettes
(1)
Inclusion
The use of an electronic cigarette shall be treated as smoking for purposes of this section.
(2)
Electronic cigarette defined
In this section, the term
electronic cigarette
means a device that delivers nicotine or other substances to a user of the device in the form of a vapor that is inhaled to simulate the experience of smoking.
.
(b)
Conforming amendment
The table of sections for
chapter 243
of title 49, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
24323. Prohibition on smoking on Amtrak trains.
.
