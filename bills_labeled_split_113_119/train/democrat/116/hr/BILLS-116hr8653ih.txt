1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Keep Law Enforcement Local Act of 2020
.
2.
Limitations on deputation authority of U.S. Marshals Service
(a)
Consent required
The Director of the United States Marshals Services shall not deputize local law enforcement officers without the consent of the chief executive of the unit of local government for which the law enforcement officer is employed.
(b)
Withdrawal of consent
The chief executive of the unit of local government that provides consent pursuant to subsection (a), may withdraw such consent at any time.
