Short title

Home Visiting Resiliency in Pandemics Act
.

(b)
Virtual home visit defined
In subsection (a), the term
virtual home visit
means a visit that is conducted solely by electronic means.
(c)
Authority To delay deadlines
(1)
In general
The Secretary of Health and Human Services may extend the deadline by which a requirement of section 511 of the Social Security Act must be met, by such period of time as the Secretary deems appropriate.
(2)
Guidance
The Secretary shall provide to eligible entities funded under section 511 of the Social Security Act information on the parameters used in extending a deadline under paragraph (1) of this subsection.
(d)
Supplemental appropriation
In addition to amounts otherwise appropriated, out of any money in the Treasury of the United States not otherwise appropriated, there are appropriated to the Secretary of Health and Human Services $100,000,000, to enable eligible entities to conduct programs funded under section 511 of the Social Security Act pursuant to this section, which shall remain available for obligation not later than January 31, 2021.
