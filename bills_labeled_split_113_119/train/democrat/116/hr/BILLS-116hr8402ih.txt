1.
Placement of statue of Ruth Bader Ginsburg in National Statuary Hall
(a)
Obtaining statue
Not later than 2 years after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Joint Committee on the Library shall enter into an agreement to obtain a statue or bust of Ruth Bader Ginsburg, under such terms and conditions as the Joint Committee considers appropriate consistent with applicable law.
(b)
Placement
The Joint Committee shall place the statue or bust obtained under subsection (a) in the United States Capitol in a suitable permanent location in National Statuary Hall.
