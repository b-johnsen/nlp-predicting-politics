2.
National Urban Security Technology Laboratory
(a)
In general
Title III of the Homeland Security Act of 2002 is amended by adding at the end the following new section:
321.
National Urban Security Technology Laboratory
(a)
In general
The Secretary, acting through the Under Secretary for Science and Technology, shall designate the laboratory described in subsection (b) as an additional laboratory pursuant to the authority under section 308(c)(2). Such laboratory shall be used to test and evaluate emerging technologies and conduct research and development to assist emergency response providers in preparing for, and protecting against, threats of terrorism.
