1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
21st Century Investment Act of 2019
.
2.
Increase in research credit for contracted research with United States businesses
(a)
In general
Section 41
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by adding at the end the following new subsection:
(i)
Special rule for contracted research with United States manufacturing business
(1)
In general
If the taxpayer elects the application of this subsection, subsection (a)(1) shall be applied by substituting
25 percent
for
20 percent
with respect to qualified United States research expenses.
(2)
Qualified United States research expenses
For purposes of this subsection, the term
qualified United States research expenses
means any amount paid or incurred by the taxpayer to any person (other than an employee of the taxpayer) for qualified research, substantially all of which occurs in the United States.
(3)
Separate application of section
In the case of any election of the application of this subsection, this section shall be applied separately with respect to qualified United States research expenses.
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendments made by this section shall apply to amounts paid or incurred in taxable years beginning after the date of the enactment of this Act.
