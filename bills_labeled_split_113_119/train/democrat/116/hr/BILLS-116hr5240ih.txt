1.
Look-thru rule for related controlled foreign corporations made permanent
(a)
In general
Section 954(c)(6)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking subparagraph (C).
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by this section shall apply to taxable years of foreign corporations beginning after December 31, 2019, and to taxable years of United States shareholders with or within which such taxable years of foreign corporations end.
