1.
Eligibility for unemployment compensation during Government shutdown
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, during fiscal year 2019, with respect to any lapse in appropriations beginning on or after December 22, 2018, any Federal employee who is excepted from furlough and is not being paid due to a lapse in appropriations shall be deemed to be separated from Federal service and eligible for unemployment compensation benefits under subchapter I of
chapter 85
of title 5, United States Code, with no waiting period for such eligibility to accrue.
