1.
Deputy Sandeep Singh Dhaliwal Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 315 Addicks Howell Road in Houston, Texas, shall be known and designated as the
Deputy Sandeep Singh Dhaliwal Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Deputy Sandeep Singh Dhaliwal Post Office Building
.
