1.
Ray Chavez Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 13308 Midland Road in Poway, California, shall be known and designated as the
Ray Chavez Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Ray Chavez Post Office Building
.
