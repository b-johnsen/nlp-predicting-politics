1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
TASER Access, Safety, and Effectiveness Review and Study Act
or the
TASERS Act
.

Study

3.
Definitions
In this Act:
(1)
Firearm
The term
firearm
has the meaning given to the term in section 921(a)(3)(A) of title 18, United States Code.
(2)
Taser
The term
taser
means a hand-held device designed to expel by means of an explosive two electrical contacts (barbs) connected by two wires attached to a high-voltage source.
