(b)
Processing
Section 7(a)(36) of the Small Business Act (
15 U.S.C. 636(a)(36)
) is amended by adding at the end the following new subparagraph:
(T)
Application processing
(i)
In general
The Administrator and the Secretary of the Treasury shall, at least twice weekly, provide separate 8-hour period during which the Administrator and the Secretary shall process the following applications under this paragraph:
(I)
Applications compiled by lenders with consolidated assets of less than $1,000,000,000 that are authorized to make loans under this paragraph.
(II)
Applications compiled by lenders from eligible recipients that have 20 or fewer employees for a principal amount of a covered loan of less than $200,000.
(ii)
Requirements
The Administrator and the Secretary shall, at least 72 hours in advance, provide notice of when an 8-hour period described in clause (i) will begin. Such 8-hour period may not be between 12 a.m. and 8 a.m.
.
