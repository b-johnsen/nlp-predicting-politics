1.
Gold Star Families Parks Pass
(a)
Short title
This Act may be referred to as the
Gold Star Families Parks Pass Act
.
(b)
Gold Star Families parks pass
Section 805(b) of division J of the Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2005 (
16 U.S.C. 6804(b)
; 118 Stat. 3386), is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph:
(3)
Gold Star Families Parks Pass
The Secretary shall make the National Parks and Federal Recreational Lands Pass available, at no cost, to members of Gold Star Families, as defined by section 3.2 of Department of Defense Instruction 1348.36.
.
