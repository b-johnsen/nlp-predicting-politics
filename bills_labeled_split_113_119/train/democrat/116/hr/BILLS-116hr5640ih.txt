1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Data Mapping to Save Moms' Lives Act
.
2.
Mapping broadband connectivity and maternal health outcomes
(a)
Incorporation of maternal health outcome data
The Federal Communications Commission shall incorporate data on maternal health outcomes for not less than 1 year postpartum, as recommended by the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention under subsection (b), into the broadband health mapping tools of the Commission.
(b)
Consultation with CDC
The Federal Communications Commission shall consult with the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention regarding the maternal health outcomes that should be incorporated into the broadband health mapping tools of the Commission under subsection (a).
