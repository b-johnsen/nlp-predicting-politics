2.
Exemption of certain earnings from retirement earnings test
(a)
In general
For purposes of subsection (b) of section 203 of the Social Security Act (
42 U.S.C. 403(b)
), wages paid to an individual who attests to being employed in the healthcare workforce or as a first responder (as determined by the Commissioner of Social Security) for months beginning with January 2020, and ending with December 2020, shall not be included in the determination of the individualâs excess earnings under subsection (f)(3) of such section for any taxable year.
(b)
Guidance
Not later than 30 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Commissioner of Social Security shall issue guidance providing for appropriate procedures for the implementation of subsection (a).
