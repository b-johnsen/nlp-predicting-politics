1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Enhancing Oversight to End Discrimination in Policing Act
.
2.
Rescission of department of justice memorandum relating to civil consent decrees
The memorandum issued by the Attorney General titled
Principles and Procedures for Civil Consent Decrees and Settlement Agreements with State and Local Governmental Entities
on November 8, 2018, or any other substantially similar memorandum, shall have no force or effect.

(2)
State enforcement
(A)
In general
Whenever an attorney general of a State has reasonable cause to believe that a violation of subsection (a) has occurred by a governmental authority, or agent thereof, of the State, including a prosecutor or other agency involved in the administration of justice, the attorney general, or another official or agency designated by a State, may in a civil action brought in any appropriate district court of the United States obtain appropriate equitable and declaratory relief to eliminate the pattern or practice.
(B)
Technical assistance
The Attorney General shall provide technical assistance and training to States and units of local government to assist States and units of local governments in carrying out pattern or practice investigations and cases.
