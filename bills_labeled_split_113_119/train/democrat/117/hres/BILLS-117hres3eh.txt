In the House of Representatives, U. S.,
January 3, 2021
RESOLUTION
That the Senate be informed that a quorum of the House of Representatives has assembled; that Nancy Pelosi, a Representative from the State of California, has been elected Speaker; and that Cheryl L. Johnson, a citizen of the State of Louisiana, has been elected Clerk of the House of Representatives of the One Hundred Seventeenth Congress.
Cheryl L. Johnson,
Clerk.
