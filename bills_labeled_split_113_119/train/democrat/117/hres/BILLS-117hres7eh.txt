Whereas, Alcee L. Hastings, a Representative-elect from the Twentieth District of the State of Florida, has been unable from illness to appear in person to be sworn as a Member of the House, and there being no contest or question as to his election: Now, therefore, be it
That the Speaker, or deputy named by her, is hereby authorized to administer the oath of office to the Honorable Alcee L. Hastings at Fort Lauderdale, Florida, and that such oath be accepted and received by the House as the oath of office of the Honorable Alcee L. Hastings.
Cheryl L. Johnson,
Clerk.
