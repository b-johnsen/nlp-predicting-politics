1.
Esteban E. Torres Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 14011 Clarkdale Avenue in Norwalk, California, shall be known and designated as the
Esteban E. Torres Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Esteban E. Torres Post Office Building
.
