(2)
Updated reports
The Under Secretary of Commerce for Minority Business Development shall update the report required under paragraph (1)(B) every 2 years.
3.
MBDA Conference
(a)
In general
The Under Secretary of Commerce for Minority Business Development shall, every 5 years, convene a conference of institutions of higher education, minority- or women-owned investment advisers, minority- or women-owned asset management firms, and investment consultants to help those institutions, advisers, and firms to share best practices for investment and bond transactions, including increasing opportunities for such advisers and asset management firms, and learn about the benefits of using such advisers and firms for investment and bond transactions.
(b)
Authorization of appropriations
There is authorized to be appropriated to the Under Secretary of Commerce for Minority Business Development $1,000,000 to carry out this section.
