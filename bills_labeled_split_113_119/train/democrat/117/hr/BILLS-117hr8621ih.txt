1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Get Our Kids to School Act of 2022
.
2.
Waivers for school bus drivers
(a)
Extended waiver period
In the case of a covered waiver, the period specified in section 31315(a)(1) of title 49, United States Code, shall be treated as a period not in excess of 1 year.
(b)
Public comments on further extension of waiver
Not later than 120 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Secretary shall solicit public comments on the feasibility and potential benefits of granting a covered waiver of unlimited duration.
(c)
Availability of funding
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, funds made available to a State under section 31313 of title 49, United States Code, may be used by the State to assist with costs associated with a covered waiver.
(d)
Covered waiver defined
In this section, the term
covered waiver
