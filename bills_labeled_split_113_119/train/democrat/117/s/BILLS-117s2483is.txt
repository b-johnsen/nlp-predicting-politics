(d)
Report on incentivizing cybersecurity for small organizations
(1)
In general
Not later than 1 year after the date of enactment of this Act, the Secretary shall submit to Congress a report describing methods to incentivize small organizations to improve their cybersecurity, including through the adoption of policies, controls, products and services that have been demonstrated to reduce cybersecurity risk.
