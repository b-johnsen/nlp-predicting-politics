1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
American Cybersecurity Literacy Act
.
2.
Sense of Congress
It is the sense of the Congress that the United States has a national security and economic interest in promoting cybersecurity literacy amongst the general public.
3.
Establishment of cybersecurity literacy campaign
(a)
In general
The Assistant Secretary for Communications and Information (referred to in this section as the
Assistant Secretary
) shall, in consultation with the Director of the Cybersecurity and Infrastructure Security Agency of the Department of Homeland Security, develop and conduct a cybersecurity literacy campaign to increase the knowledge and awareness of people in the United States of best practices to reduce cybersecurity risks.
