1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Affordable Housing Production Act
.
2.
Use of TARP amounts
(a)
In General
The Emergency Economic Stabilization Act of 2008 (
12 U.S.C. 5201
et seq.) is amended by inserting after section 110 (
12 U.S.C. 5220
) the following:
110A.
Other assistance
Notwithstanding section 115(a), section 120, or any other provision of law, the Secretary may use any amounts made available under this title that are not otherwise obligated for deposit in and use only to provide assistance under the Housing Trust Fund under section 1338 of the Federal Housing Enterprises Financial Safety and Soundness Act of 1992 (
12 U.S.C. 4568
).
.
(b)
Clerical amendment
The table of contents in section 1(b) of the Emergency Economic Stabilization Act of 2008 (
12 U.S.C. 5201
note) is amended by inserting after the item relating to section 110 the following:
110A. Other assistance.
.
