(B)
Studies
At the request of the Chairman or Ranking Member of a committee of the Senate or the House of Representatives, the Director shall, to the extent practicable, conduct a study of a bill, joint resolution, amendment, amendment between the Houses, or conference report containing changes in spending on children that includes, separately for each category of spending on children, information and data, including the amount of the change in spending on children for each category of spending on children.
(C)
