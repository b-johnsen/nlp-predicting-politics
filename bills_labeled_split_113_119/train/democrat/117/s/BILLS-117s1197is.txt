Special rules
(1)

The Secretary shall strive to ensure that, in each year of the program under this section, at least one new graduate level participant is studying in an advanced quantum research field.

The Secretary shall consider emerging technologies, such as quantum computing and information sciences and artificial intelligence, as skills and disciplines that are critical to the national security functions of the Department of Defense.

The Secretary shall encourage increased participation in the program under this section by students studying relevant cross-disciplinary educational tracks that touch on more than one area of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.
