Survey on Native American languages
(a)
In general
Not later than 18 months after the date of enactment of this section, and every 5 years thereafter, the Secretary of Health and Human Services, acting through the Commissioner of the Administration for Native Americans (referred to in this section as the
Secretary
), shall undertake a survey of the use of all Native American languages in the United States.
(b)
Updates
Prior to conducting each subsequent survey after the initial survey under subsection (a), the Secretary shall update the survey in accordance with this section.

(4)

(5)
information on any unmet Native American language resource needs of Indian tribes and Native American language communities; and
