1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Competitive Onshore Mineral Policy via Eliminating Taxpayer-Enabled Speculation Act
or the
COMPETES Act
.
2.
Statement of policy
It is the policy of the United States that the Secretary of the Interior shall not issue onshore oil and gas leases except through a competitive bidding process.
