1.
Officer Leo Pavlak Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 236 Concord Exchange North in South Saint Paul, Minnesota, shall be known and designated as the
Officer Leo Pavlak Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Officer Leo Pavlak Post Office Building
.
