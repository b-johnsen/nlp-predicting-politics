1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Fair Social Security for Domestic Violence Survivors Act
.
2.
Modification of 10-year marriage rule in cases of domestic violence
(a)
In general
Section 216(d) of the Social Security Act (
42 U.S.C. 416(d)
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(9)
(A)
In the case of a divorced woman described in paragraph (1) or (2) or a divorced man described in paragraph (4) or (5) who provides to the Commissioner of Social Security a finding made by a court of law that the divorced woman or divorced man was the victim of domestic violence committed by the spouse during the course of the marriage, such paragraph as may be applicable with respect to such divorced woman or divorced man shall be applied for purposes of this title by substituting
5 years
for
10 years
.
(B)
For purposes of subparagraph (A), the term
domestic violence
has the meaning given such term in section 40002(a) of the Violence Against Women Act of 1994.
.

5 years
for
10 years
.

5 years
for

.
.
(d)
Effective date
The amendments made by this section shall apply with respect to monthly insurance benefits for months beginning at least 18 months after the date of enactment of this Act.
