1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Reaffirming Unified Boundaries for Integrity and Oversight Act
or the
RUBIO Act
.
2.
Prohibition on Secretary of State holding or otherwise carrying out duties of other
Federal positions
The Secretary of State may not hold or otherwise carry out the duties of any other Federal position, and no Federal funds may be obligated or expended for the salaries or expenses of the Secretary if the Secretary so holds or otherwise carries out duties of such other positions.
