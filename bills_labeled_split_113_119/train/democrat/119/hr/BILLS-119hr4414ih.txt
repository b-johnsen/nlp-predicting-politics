1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Satellite-Based Agricultural Data Act
.
2.
Inclusion of commercial weather as AFRI priority area
Subsection (b)(2)(E)(iii) of the Competitive, Special, and Facilities Research Grant Act (
7 U.S.C. 3157(b)(2)(E)(iii)
) is amended by inserting
, including through the use of data and tools available from commercial weather services
after
mitigation measures
.
