In general
Not later than 90 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Secretary of Education shall reissue the solicitation and award a contract described in subsection (b) as if the contract described in such subsection had not previously been awarded.

(c)
Prohibition of cancellation of contract
A contract awarded under this section may not be canceled without the approval of Congress.
