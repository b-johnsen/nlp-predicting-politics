(1)

(4)

(5)
his family, employer, and community would be better served if Miguel Lopez Luvian were to return to his home in Livermore, California.
(b)
Naturalization
Notwithstanding section 337(a) or any other provision of title III of the Immigration and Nationality Act (
8 U.S.C. 1401 et seq.
), Miguel Lopez Luvian shall be considered to be a naturalized citizen of the United States as of the date of the enactment of this Act and shall be furnished by the Attorney General with a certificate of naturalization.
