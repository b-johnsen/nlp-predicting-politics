2.
Sense of Congress
It is the sense of Congress thatâ
(1)
the Alternative Manure Management Program (AMMP) is a successful program in California that supports the livestock industry and is an example of what can be done nationally in the United States through a variety of practices on responsible manure management;

(3)
these practices enhance sustainability and nurture continued environmental stewardship, ultimately leading to enhanced local air quality and recycled manure for use as a natural fertilizer or compost for healthier soil.
