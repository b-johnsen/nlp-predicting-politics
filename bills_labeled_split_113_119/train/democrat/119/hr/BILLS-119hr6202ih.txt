1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Brand USA Restoration Act
.
2.
Appropriation to Travel Promotion Fund
There are appropriated for fiscal year 2026, out of any money in the Treasury not otherwise appropriated, $80,000,000, to be deposited into the Travel Promotion Fund established by subsection (d) of the Travel Promotion Act of 2009 (
22 U.S.C. 2131(d)
).
