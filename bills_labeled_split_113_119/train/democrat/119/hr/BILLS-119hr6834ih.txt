Report
Not later than 2 years after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Secretary of Agriculture shall carry out a study and produce a reportâ
(1)
on the shortages to meet the total emergency food needs for refrigerated and frozen storage space, as well as refrigerated trucks and other delivery vehicles, for use by emergency food organizations to meet the needs of their communities, and
(2)
an estimate of the cost to meet the needs of all emergency food organizations, including food banks and food pantries, nationally for new coolers, freezers, refrigerated and dry trucks, and trailers.
