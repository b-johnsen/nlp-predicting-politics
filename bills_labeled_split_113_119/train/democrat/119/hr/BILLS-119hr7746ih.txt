1.
Chuck Stone Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 8390 North Broadway in St. Louis, Missouri, shall be known and designated as the
Chuck Stone Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Chuck Stone Post Office
.
