1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Respect for Essential Workers Act
.
2.
Temporary Protected Status for essential workers
(a)
In general
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, a covered alien may not be removed from the United States on the basis that the grant of temporary protected status under section 244 of the Immigration and Nationality Act (
8 U.S.C. 1254a
) has been terminated.
(b)
Adjustment of status
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, a covered alien may adjust to the status of an alien lawfully admitted for permanent residence under section 245 of the Immigration and Nationality Act (
8 U.S.C. 1255
) beginning on the date that is 90 days after the date of enactment of this Act.
