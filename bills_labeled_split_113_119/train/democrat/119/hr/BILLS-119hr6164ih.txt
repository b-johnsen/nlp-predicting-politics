1.
Deputy Ryan Clinkunbroomer Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 28201 Franklin Parkway in Santa Clarita, California, shall be known and designated as the
Deputy Ryan Clinkunbroomer Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Deputy Ryan Clinkunbroomer Post Office Building
.
