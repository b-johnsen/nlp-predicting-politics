JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of
title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Environmental Protection
Agency relating to
National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants for
Coke Ovens: Pushing, Quenching, and Battery Stacks, and Coke Oven Batteries;
Residual Risk and Technology Review, and Periodic Technology
Review
.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to
National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants for Coke Ovens: Pushing, Quenching, and Battery Stacks, and Coke Oven Batteries; Residual Risk and Technology Review, and Periodic Technology Review
(90 Fed. Reg. 29997 (July 8, 2025)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
