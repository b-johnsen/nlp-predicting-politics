JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of
title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Federal Highway Administration
relating to
Rescinding Regulations Regarding Management Systems Pertaining to the
Fish and Wildlife Service and the Refuge Roads Program
.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Federal Highway Administration relating to
Rescinding Regulations Regarding Management Systems Pertaining to the Fish and Wildlife Service and the Refuge Roads Program
(90 Fed. Reg. 45136 (September 19, 2025)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
