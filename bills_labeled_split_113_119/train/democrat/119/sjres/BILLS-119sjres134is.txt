Truth in Lending (Regulation Z); Use of Digital User Accounts To Access Buy Now, Pay Later Loans, 89 Fed. Reg. 47068 (May 31, 2024)
(90 Fed. Reg. 20084 (May 12, 2025)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
