JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United
States Code, of the rule submitted by the Office of the Comptroller of the Currency
relating to
Rescission of Principles for Climate-Related Financial Risk
Management for Large Financial Institutions
.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Office of the Comptroller of the Currency relating to
Rescission of Principles for Climate-Related Financial Risk Management for Large Financial Institutions
(90 Fed. Reg. 51756 (November 18, 2025)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
