1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Responsive Energy Demand Unlocks Clean Energy Act
or the
REDUCE Act
.
2.
Aggregator bidding into organized power markets
(a)
In general
Notwithstanding any prohibition established by State law or a State commission (as defined in section 3 of the Federal Power Act (
16 U.S.C. 796
)) with respect to who may bid into an organized wholesale electric market, each Transmission Organization shall, consistent with any applicable market rules that do not establish such a prohibition, allow aggregators of retail customers to submit bids that aggregate demand flexibility of customers of utilities that distributed more than 4,000,000 megawatt-hours in the previous fiscal year.
(b)
Rulemaking
Not later than 1 year after the date of enactment of this Act, the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission shall issue a rule to carry out the requirements of subsection (a).
