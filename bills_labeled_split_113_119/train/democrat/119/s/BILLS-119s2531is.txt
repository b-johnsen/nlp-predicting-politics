(b)
Use of funds
A State receiving a grant under this section may use the grant for the following activities, with respect to increasing early detection of, and intervention for, uterine fibroids:
(1)
Screening procedures, including advanced gynecological imaging, including payment for such procedures.
(2)
Patient navigation services.
(3)
Implementation of evidence-based or evidence-informed strategies formulated under section 2(a)(2).
(4)
Facilitating access to health care settings.
