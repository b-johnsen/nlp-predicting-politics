(a)
In general
Part VI of subchapter B of
chapter 1

177.
Payroll deduction for qualified small businesses
(a)
In general
In the case of a qualified small business, in addition to the deduction allowed by section 162(a)(1), there shall be allowed as a deduction an amount equal to the sum of the applicable amounts determined under subsection (b)(2) with respect to each full-time employee of the employer which has been designated by such employer pursuant to subsection (b)(1).
