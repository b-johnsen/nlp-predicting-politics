(I)
In general
Temporary immigration judges may serve for up to 4 consecutive 6-month terms.

Temporary immigration judges who have reached the 2-year service limit described in subclause (I) shall not be reappointed to this temporary position until at least 3 years after the conclusion of their temporary service.
