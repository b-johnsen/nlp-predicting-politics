2.
Findings
Congress finds the following:
(1)
The Republic of Turkey has been a longstanding ally of the United States, not only bilaterally, but also through the NATO alliance.
(2)
The United States is concerned with the increasing cooperation between Recep Erdogan, the President of Turkey, and Vladimir Putin, the President of the Russian Federation.
(3)
The United States remains concerned with patterns of antidemocratic behavior espoused by the Government of Turkey under the leadership of President Erdogan.
