(3)
Use of funds
Amounts received through a grant under this subsection shall be used to purchase high-throughput diagnostic equipment and such materials as are necessary to administer, store, and process applicable tests, including diagnostic and serological tests.
(4)
Amount of grant
The amount of a grant under paragraph (1) may not exceed $2,000,000, except in the case of eligible entity described in paragraph (2)(A)(iv).
(5)
High-throughput diagnostic equipment defined
In this subsection, the term
high-throughput diagnostic equipment
means legally-marketed equipment and supplies capable of performing multichannel analysis for use in clinical laboratory diagnostic testing.
