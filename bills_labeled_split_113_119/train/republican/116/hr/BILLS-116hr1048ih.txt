1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Yakima River Basin Water Enhancement Project Phase III Act
.
2.
Authorization of phase III
(a)

The term

Public Law 103ГўВЂВ“434
(108 Stat. 4550; 114 Stat. 1425), including conducting applicable feasibility studies, environmental reviews, and other relevant studies required to develop those plans.
