(C)
Obligations of the recipient
(i)
Acknowledgement of receipt of request
Within 3 business days after a person receives a request made in accordance with subparagraph (A) or a subsequent communication from the Commission in relation to the request, the person shall acknowledge to the Commission that the recipient has received the request or communication.
(ii)
Response to request
Within 10 business days after a person receives such a request or communication, the person shall respond to the request or communication in accordance with subparagraph (D).
(iii)
Retention of requested records
A person who receives such a request shall retain all records identified in the request until the request or any subpoena for the records has been resolved.
