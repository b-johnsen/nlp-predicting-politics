1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Brigadier General George
Bud
Day, the Most Decorated Veteran in American History Next to General Douglas MacArthur, Act
.
2.
General George
Bud
Day Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 214 Jackson Street in Sioux City, Iowa, shall be known and designated as the
General George
Bud
Day Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
General George
Bud
Day Post Office Building
.
