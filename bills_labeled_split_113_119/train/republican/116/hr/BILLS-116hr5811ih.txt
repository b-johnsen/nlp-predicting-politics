2.
Nursing facilities for Transportation Security Administration personnel
(a)
In general
Not later than 180 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Administrator of the Transportation Security Administration shall ensure that all Administration personnel have adequate access to facilities designated for use by nursing mothers in the workplace. Such facilities shall beâ
(1)
assessed for cleanliness, safety, and accessibility to personnel duty stations;
(2)
free of charge;
(3)
located in a place, other than a bathroom, that is shielded from view and free from intrusion from coworkers and the public; and
(4)
available for personnel to use with a reasonable break time to express breast milk for a child of such personnel each time such personnel has a need to express milk.
(b)
Duration
The Administrator of the Transportation Security Administration shall ensure that the availability to Administration personnel described in subsection (a)(4) is for a period of time equal to at least one year from the birth of a child of an Administration employee.
(c)
Coordination with stakeholders
In carrying out the requirements in subsection (a), the Administrator of the Transportation Security Administration shall coordinate with the National Institutes for Health and the labor organization representing Administration screening personnel.
