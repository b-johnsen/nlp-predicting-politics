1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Preventing Pandemic Profiting Act
.
2.
Prohibition
(a)
In general
Chapter 11
of title 18, United States Code, is amended by inserting after
section 220
the following:
221.
Price gouging during a state of emergency declaration
(a)
In general
Whoever, in any location that has received a state of emergency declaration, knowingly sells or offers for sale, during the effective period of such declaration, any goods or services identified by the Secretary of Health and Human Services as vital and necessary for the health, safety, and welfare of consumers, including medical treatment, at an unconscionably excessive price, shall be fined not more than $1,000, imprisoned not more than 5 years, or both.

â

(b)

The table of sections for

(c)
Application
This Act and the amendments made by this Act shall apply with respect to conduct occurring on or after the date of enactment of this Act.
