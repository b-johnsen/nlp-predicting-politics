1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Buy a Brick, Build the Wall Act of 2019
.
2.
Border wall trust fund
(a)
Border wall trust fund
At the end of subchapter III of
chapter 33
of title 31, United States Code, insert the following:
3344.
Border wall trust fund.
(a)
Authority To receive gifts
Notwithstanding section 3113, the Secretary of the Treasury may accept for the Government a gift of money on the condition that it be used to plan, design, construct, or maintain a barrier along the international border between the United States and Mexico.
