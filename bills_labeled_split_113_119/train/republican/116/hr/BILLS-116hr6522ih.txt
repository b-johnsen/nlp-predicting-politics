1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Paycheck Protection Program Expansion Act of 2020
or the
PPP Expansion Act of 2020
.
2.
Inclusion of certain business organizations in the paycheck protection program
Section 7(a)(36)(A)(vii) of the Small Business Act is amended by striking
section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986
and inserting
paragraph (3), (5), or (6) of
section 501(c)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986
.
