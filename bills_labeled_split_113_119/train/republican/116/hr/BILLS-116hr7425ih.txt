1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Direct Interstate Retail Exemption for Certain Transactions Act
or the
DIRECT Act
.
2.
Interstate internet sales of certain State-inspected meat allowed
(a)
In general
Section 301(c)(2) of the Federal Meat Inspection Act (
21 U.S.C. 661(c)(2)
) is amended, by inserting after the first sentence, the following:
For the purposes of this subparagraph, any retail store, restaurant, or similar retail-type establishment may sell over the internet and ship by carrier in commerce (other than for export to a foreign country) any State-inspected meat or meat food product, provided the meat or meat food product so inspected is shipped directly to household consumers and in normal retail quantities.
.
