1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Puerto Rico Federal Matching Requirement Equity Act of 2019
.
2.
Removal of statutory FMAP limitation for Puerto Rico
(a)
In general
Section 1905(b)(2) of the Social Security Act (
42 U.S.C. 1396d(b)(2)
) is amended by striking
Puerto Rico,
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by subsection (a) shall apply with respect to medical assistance furnished in calendar quarters beginning on or after October 1, 2019.
