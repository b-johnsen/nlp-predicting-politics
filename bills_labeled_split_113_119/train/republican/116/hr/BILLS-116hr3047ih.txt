Sense of Congress
It is the sense of Congress that United States assistance to Ukraine under chapter 5 of part II of the Foreign Assistance Act of 1961 and the Arms Export Control Act should be increased to provide opportunities to establish and build long-term bilateral defense relationships and to achieve important goals, including the effective use, security, and storage of United States-origin defense articles.
