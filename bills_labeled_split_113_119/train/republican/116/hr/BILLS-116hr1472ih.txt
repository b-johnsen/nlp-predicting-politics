1.
Homestead National Historical Park
(a)
In General
The first section of the Act of March 19, 1936 (
16 U.S.C. 450u
), is amended by striking
designated
and all that follows through the end and inserting
designated the
Homestead National Historical Park
.
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the unit of the National Park System known as
The Homestead National Monument of America
shall be considered to be a reference to the
Homestead National Historical Park
.
