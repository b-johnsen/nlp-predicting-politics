1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Resurveys Entitle Adjacent Landowners to Protection Act
or the
REAL Protection Act
.
2.
Resurvey Transparency; Notice requirements
(a)
Notice
(1)
In general
Not later than 30 days before the commencement of a resurvey of Federal land under the administrative jurisdiction of the Bureau of Land Management, the National Park Service, or the Forest Service, the Secretary concerned shall notify all property owners with land abutting or adjacent to the Federal land being resurveyed of the pending resurvey. If a resurvey extends the boundaries of Federal land, the Secretary concerned shall notify affected landowners of the results of the resurvey not later than 30 days after the completion of the survey.
(2)
Notification
The Secretary concerned shall use certified or registered mail to notify landowners under this subsection.
(3)
Identification of landowners
When identifying affected landowners for the purpose of notification under this subsection, the Secretary concerned shall use the most recently available tax records.
(b)
Public comment
Not later than 30 days after completing a resurvey, the Secretary concerned shall publish a notice in the Federal Register. Affected landowners may comment to the Secretary concerned and by submitting formal comments to the Federal Register notice.
