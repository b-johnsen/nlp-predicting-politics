Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Improve Data on Sexual Violence Act

Not later than 180 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Director of the Office of Management and Budget shall establish an interagency working group toâ

(c)
Composition
The Working Group shall be composed of not less than 1 representative, who shall be selected by the head of the Federal agency of which the representative is a representative, from each of the following Federal agencies:
(1)
The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention.
(2)
The Department of Defense.
(3)
The Department of Education.
(4)
The Department of Health and Human Services.
(5)
The Department of Justice.

(D)
