1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Cell-Cultured Meat and Poultry Regulation Act of 2019
.
2.
Definitions
In this Act:
(1)
Commissioner
The term
Commissioner
means the Commissioner of Food and Drugs.
(2)
Food product
The term
food product
means a cell-cultured food product derived from livestock or poultry.
(3)
Secretary
The term
Secretary
means the Secretary of Agriculture.
