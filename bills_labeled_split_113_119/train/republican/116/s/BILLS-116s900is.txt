1.
Designation of Travis W. Atkins Department of Veterans Affairs Clinic in Bozeman, Montana
(a)
Designation
The community-based outpatient clinic of the Department of Veterans Affairs located at 300 North Willson Avenue, Bozeman, Montana, shall after the date of the enactment of this Act be known and designated as the
Travis W. Atkins Department of Veterans Affairs Clinic
or the
Travis W. Atkins VA Clinic
.
(b)
Reference
Any reference in any law, regulation, map, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the community-based outpatient clinic referred to in subsection (a) shall be considered to be a reference to the Travis W. Atkins Department of Veterans Affairs Clinic.
