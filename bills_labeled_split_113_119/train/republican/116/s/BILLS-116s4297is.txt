Allowance of delay in making 2020 retirement contributions
(a)
In general
An eligible participant in one or more applicable retirement plans may make additional contributions to such plans for any taxable year beginning in 2021 or 2022 in an aggregate amount not exceeding the participant's unused 2020 contribution amount.
