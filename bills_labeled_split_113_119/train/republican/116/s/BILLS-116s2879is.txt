In general

399H.
Programs for fetal alcohol spectrum disorders
(a)
Definition
In this part, the terms
fetal alcohol spectrum disorders
and
FASD
mean the range of effects that can occur in an individual who is prenatally exposed to alcohol. Such effects may include physical, mental, behavioral, and learning disabilities, with possible lifelong implications.

(C)

(F)

(G)
Implementing recommendations from relevant agencies and organizations, including the State advisory group, on the identification and prevention of FASD, and intervention programs or services for individuals with FASD.
(H)

(J)
