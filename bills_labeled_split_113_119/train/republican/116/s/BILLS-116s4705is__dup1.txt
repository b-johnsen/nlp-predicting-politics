(c)
Effective date
(1)
In general
Except as provided in paragraph (2), the amendments made by this section shall take effect on the date of enactment of this Act.
(2)
Delay permitted if State legislation required
In the case of a State plan under title XIX of the Social Security Act (
42 U.S.C. 1396
et seq.) which the Secretary of Health and Human Services determines requires State legislation (other than legislation appropriating funds) in order for the plan to meet the additional requirements imposed by the amendments made by this section, the State plan shall not be regarded as failing to comply with the requirements of such title solely on the basis of the failure of the plan to meet such additional requirements before the first day of the first calendar quarter beginning after the close of the first regular session of the State legislature that begins after the date of enactment of this Act. For purposes of the previous sentence, in the case of a State that has a 2-year legislative session, each year of such session shall be deemed to be a separate regular session of the State legislature.
