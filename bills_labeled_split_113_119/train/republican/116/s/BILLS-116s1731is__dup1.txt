(b)
Rules
(1)
Proposals
Not later than 90 days after the date of enactment of this Act, each national securities exchange shall, in accordance with section 19(b) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 (
15 U.S.C. 78s(b)
) and any rules prescribed by the Commission under that section, file with the Commission any proposed change to the rules of the exchange that is required as a result of the amendments made by this section.
(2)
Adoption
Not later than 1 year after the date of enactment of this Act, each national securities exchange shall have each proposed change described in paragraph (1) approved by the Commission.
