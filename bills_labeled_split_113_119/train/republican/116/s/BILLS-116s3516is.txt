Pro rata amounts
The pro rata amount for each partial emergency family relief period shall be calculated by multiplying the monthly amount for a full emergency family relief period that would be paid to the beneficiary based on household size for such a period by the percentage calculated by dividing the number of days of the partial emergency family relief period by the number of days of the month during which the majority of the emergency family relief period occurs, or, if the emergency family relief period is an even number of days, by the number of days of the shorter month during which it falls.
