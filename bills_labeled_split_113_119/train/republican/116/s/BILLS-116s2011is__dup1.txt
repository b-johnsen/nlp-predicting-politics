1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Veteran STEM Scholarship Improvement Act of 2019
.
2.
Reduction in credit hour requirement for Edith Nourse Rogers STEM scholarship
Section 3320(b)(4)(A)(i) of title 38, United States Code, is amended by striking
more than the standard 128 semester (or 192 quarter) credit hours
and inserting
at least the standard 120 semester (or 180 quarter) credit hours
.
