(d)
Termination
No grant shall be awarded under the Temporary Federal ESOP Program after September 30, 2022.
(e)
Authorized transfer of appropriations
The Secretary of the Treasury may transfer any unobligated amount of the appropriation under section 4027 of the Coronavirus Economic Stabilization Act of 2020 for the purposes of carrying out the Temporary Federal ESOP Program.
(f)
Secretary of the Treasury
For purposes of this section, any reference to the Secretary of the Treasury includes a reference to any delegate of such Secretary.
