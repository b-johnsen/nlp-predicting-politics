1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Stadiums Operating under New Guidance Act
or the
SONG Act
.
2.
Revision of section 91.145 of title 14, Code of Federal Regulations
Not later than one year after the enactment of this Act, the Secretary of Transportation, acting through the Administrator of the Federal Aviation Administration, shall revise section 91.145 of title 14, Code of Federal Regulations (or any successor regulation), such that the term
sporting
does not limit the types of major events described in such section.
