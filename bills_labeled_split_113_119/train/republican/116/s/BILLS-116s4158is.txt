1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
PPE Supply Chain Transparency Act of 2020
.
2.
Report
(a)
Definition
In this section, the terms
personal protective equipment
and
PPE
means protective clothing, helmets, gloves, face shields, goggles, facemasks, and other equipment designed to protect the wearer from injury or the spread of infection or illness, as determined by the Commissioner of Food and Drugs.
(b)
Report
Not later than 90 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Administrator of the Federal Emergency Management Agency shall, in consultation with the Secretary of Health and Human Services, submit to the Committee on Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs of the Senate and the Committee on Homeland Security of the House of Representatives, and make publicly available, a report examining the security and resilience of the supply chain for personal protective equipment during the COVIDâ19 pandemic.
