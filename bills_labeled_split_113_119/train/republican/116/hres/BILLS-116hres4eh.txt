In the House of Representatives, U. S.,
January 3, 2019
RESOLUTION
Authorizing the Clerk to inform the President of the election of the Speaker and the Clerk
That the Clerk be instructed to inform the President of the United States that the House of Representatives has elected Nancy Pelosi, a Representative from the State of California as Speaker, and Karen L. Haas, a citizen of the State of Maryland as Clerk, of the House of Representatives of the One Hundred Sixteenth Congress.
Karen L. Haas,
Clerk.
