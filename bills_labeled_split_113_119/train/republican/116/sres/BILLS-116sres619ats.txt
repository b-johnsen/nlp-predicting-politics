2d Session
S. RES. 619
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
June 15, 2020
Mr. Blunt
submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to
RESOLUTION
To provide for the printing of the Senate Manual for the One Hundred Sixteenth Congress.
That a revised edition of the Senate Manual for the One Hundred Sixteenth Congress be prepared by the Committee on Rules and Administration and printed as a Senate document, and that fifteen hundred additional copies shall be printed and bound for the use of the Senate, bound and delivered as may be directed by the Committee on Rules and Administration.
