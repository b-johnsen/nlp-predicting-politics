JOINT RESOLUTION
Directing the President to terminate the use of the United States Armed Forces with respect to the
military intervention led by Saudi Arabia in the Republic of Yemen.
That Congress directs the President to terminate the use of the United States Armed Forces with respect to the military intervention led by Saudi Arabia in the Republic of Yemen not later than the end of the period of 30 days beginning on the date of the enactment of this joint resolution unless and until a declaration of war or specific authorization for such use of the Armed Forces has been enacted into law.
