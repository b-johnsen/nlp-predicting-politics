(a)
In general
Section 1473(1)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by redesignating subparagraph (C) as subparagraph (D) and by inserting after subparagraph (B) the following new subparagraph:
(C)

(III)

(IV)

(b)
Effective date
The amendments made by this section shall apply to payments made after the date of the enactment of this Act.
