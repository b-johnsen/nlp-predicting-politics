2.
Limited authority of States to reduce frequency with which qualified law enforcement officers must meet firearm training standards in order to be eligible to carry a concealed firearm
Section 926C(c)(4) of title 18, United States Code, is amended by striking
12-month period
and inserting
period of 12 months (or, at the option of the State under the law of which the former agency of the individual operates, such greater number of months, not exceeding 36, as the State may by law provide)
