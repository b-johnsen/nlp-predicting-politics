In general

Refunds To be made by electronic funds transfer

Except as otherwise provided in this subsection, any refund or payment made by the Secretary under this title with respect to an income tax return which is filed electronically shall be made by electronic funds transfer.

(B)
in other circumstances as may be necessary.

3.
Refunds to prepaid debit cards, Direct Express cards, etc
The Secretary of the Treasury, or his designee, shallâ
(1)
