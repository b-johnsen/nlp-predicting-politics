1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Freedom to Use Contracted Entities Act
.
2.
State flexibility to use contractors to make eligibility determinations on behalf of State
Section 1902(a)(5) of the Social Security Act (
42 U.S.C. 1396a(a)(5)
) is amended by inserting before the semicolon at the end the following:
, but such determinations of eligibility may be made, at the option of a State, under a contract with another State or local agency or a contractor so long as the contract does not provide incentives for the agency or contractor to delay eligibility determinations or to deny eligibility for individuals otherwise eligible for medical assistance
.
