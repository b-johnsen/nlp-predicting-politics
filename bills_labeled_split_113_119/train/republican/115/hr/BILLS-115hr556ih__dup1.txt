1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Blue Line Use Exception Act of 2017
or the
BLUE Act of 2017
.
2.
Manual on Uniform Traffic Control Devices exception
Notwithstanding any provision in the Manual on Uniform Traffic Control Devices relating to the space between parallel longitudinal lines on roads or the color of line markings, the Administrator of the Federal Highway Administration shall grant an exception to allow for the use of colored markings in the space between longitudinal parallel lines for ceremonial or celebratory purposes if the State transportation department (as defined in section 101 of title 23, United States Code) of the State where such markings are to be used approves of such use.
