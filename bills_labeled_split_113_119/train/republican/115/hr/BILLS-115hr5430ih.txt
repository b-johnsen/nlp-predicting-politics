1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Puerto Rico Real Estate Exchange Fairness Act of 2018
.
2.
Puerto Rico treated as part of United States for purposes of determination of like-kind exchanges
(a)
In general
Section 1031(h)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph:
(3)
United States
For purposes of this subsection, the term
United States
shall be treated as including Puerto Rico.
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by this section shall apply to exchanges completed after the date of the enactment of this Act.
