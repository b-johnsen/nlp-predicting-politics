1.
Sergeant David Kinterknecht Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 3025 Woodgate Road in Montrose, Colorado, shall be known and designated as the
Sergeant David Kinterknecht Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Sergeant David Kinterknecht Post Office
.
