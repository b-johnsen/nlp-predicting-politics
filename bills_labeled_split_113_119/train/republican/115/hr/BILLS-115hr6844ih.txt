1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
No Early Release for Fentanyl Traffickers Act
.
2.
Credit toward service of sentence for satisfactory behavior
Section 3624(b) of title 18, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
(5)
No credit may be awarded under this subsection to any prisoner who is serving a term of imprisonment for a violation of section 401(b)(1)(A)(vi) of the Controlled Substances Act (
21 U.S.C. 841
).
.
