1.
Planted crop treated as harvested crop
If the Secretary of Agriculture determines that market conditions have discouraged or prevented a producer of a soybean crop from harvesting such crop or entering such crop in to the stream of commerce, the Secretary shall, for purposes of the Market Facilitation Program under part 1409 of title 7, Code of Federal Regulations (established pursuant to the Commodity Credit Corporation Charter Act (
15 U.S.C. 714
et seq.)) treat such soybean crop as an eligible crop for payments under such Market Facilitation Program.
