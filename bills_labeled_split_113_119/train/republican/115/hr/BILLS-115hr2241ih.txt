The term
transportation network company
means a corporation, partnership, sole proprietorship, or other entity that uses a digital network
to connect riders to drivers affiliated with the entity in order for a
driver to transport the rider using a vehicle owned, leased, or otherwise
authorized for use by the driver to a point chosen by the rider.
(iii)
Commuter vehicle
The term

means a vehicle the seating capacity (not including the driver) of which is at least four and not
more than six.
