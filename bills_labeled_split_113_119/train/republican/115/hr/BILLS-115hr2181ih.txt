(b)
No carryback before January 1, 2018
To the extent that a consolidated net operating loss is allowed or increased by reason of this section or the amendments made by this Act, such loss (or increase in such loss, as the case may be) may not be carried back to a taxable year beginning before January 1, 2018.
(c)
Nontermination of group
No affiliated group shall terminate solely as a result of this section or the amendments made by this Act.
