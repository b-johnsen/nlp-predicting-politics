1.
Janet Lucille Oilar Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 44160 State Highway 299 East Suite 1 in McArthur, California, shall be known and designated as the
Janet Lucille Oilar Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Janet Lucille Oilar Post Office
.
