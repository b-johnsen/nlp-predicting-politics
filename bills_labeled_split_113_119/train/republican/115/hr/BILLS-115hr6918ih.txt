1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Semi-Annual Act of 2018
.
2.
Semi-annual reporting
(a)
In general
Section 13 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 (
15 U.S.C. 78m
) is amended by striking
quarterly
each place such term appears and inserting
semi-annual
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendments made by subsection (a) shall take effect after the end of the 90-day period beginning on the date of enactment of this Act.
