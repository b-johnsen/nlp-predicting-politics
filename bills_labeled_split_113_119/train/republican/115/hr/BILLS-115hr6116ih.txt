1.
Colonel Alfred Asch Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 362 North Ross Street in Beaverton, Michigan, shall be known and designated as the
Colonel Alfred Asch Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Colonel Alfred Asch Post Office
.
