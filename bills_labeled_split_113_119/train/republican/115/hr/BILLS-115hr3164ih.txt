(d)
Study and report on further expansion
(1)
Study
The Comptroller General of the United States shall conduct a study to examine the benefits and drawbacks of expanding the coverage under the Medicare program under title XVIII of the Social Security Act of renal dialysis services as telehealth services, pursuant to the amendments made by this section, to include coverage of renal dialysis services furnished via telehealth and other store-and-forward technologies.
(2)
Report
Not later than two years after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Comptroller General shall submit to Congress a report on the results of the study conducted under paragraph (1).
