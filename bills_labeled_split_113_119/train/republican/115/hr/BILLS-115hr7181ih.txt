1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
400 Act
.
2.
Establishment of number of representatives at 400
(a)
In general
Section 22(a) of the Act entitled
An Act to provide for the fifteenth and subsequent decennial census and to provide for apportionment of Representatives in Congress
, approved June 18, 1929 (
2 U.S.C. 2a(a)
), is amended by striking
the then existing number of Representatives
and inserting
400 Representatives
.
(b)
Effective Date
The amendment made by subsection (a) shall apply with respect to the regular decennial census conducted for 2020 and each subsequent decennial census.
