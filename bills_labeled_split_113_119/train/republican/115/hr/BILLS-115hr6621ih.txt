1.
Major Homer L. Pease Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 530 East Main Street in Johnson City, Tennessee, shall be known and designated as the
Major Homer L. Pease Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Major Homer L. Pease Post Office
.
