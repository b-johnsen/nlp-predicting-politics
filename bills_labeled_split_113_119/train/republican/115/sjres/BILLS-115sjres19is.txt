JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule
submitted by the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection relating to
prepaid accounts under the Electronic Fund Transfer Act and the Truth in
Lending Act.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection relating to prepaid accounts under the Electronic Fund Transfer Act (
15 U.S.C. 1693
et seq.) and the Truth in Lending Act (
15 U.S.C. 1601
et seq.) (81 Fed. Reg. 83934 (November 22, 2016)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
