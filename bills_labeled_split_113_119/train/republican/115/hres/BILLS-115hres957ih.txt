RESOLUTION
Disapproving of the request of the President for the extension, under section 103(c)(1)(B)(i) of
the Bipartisan Congressional Trade Priorities and Accountability Act of
2015, of the trade authorities procedures under that Act to any
implementing bill submitted with respect to any trade agreement entered
into under section 103(b) of that Act after June 30, 2018.
That the House of Representatives disapproves the request of the President for the extension, under section 103(c)(1)(B)(i) of the Bipartisan Congressional Trade Priorities and Accountability Act of 2015, of the trade authorities procedures under that Act to any implementing bill submitted with respect to any trade agreement entered into under section 103(b) of that Act after June 30, 2018.
