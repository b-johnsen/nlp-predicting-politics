JOINT RESOLUTION
Disapproving the rule submitted by the Department of the Interior relating to Bureau of Land
Management regulations that establish the procedures used to prepare,
revise, or amend land use plans pursuant to the Federal Land Policy and
Management Act of 1976.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Bureau of Land Management of the Department of the Interior relating to
Resource Management Planning
(published at 81 Fed. Reg. 89580 (December 12, 2016)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
