JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of a rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to
Oil and Natural Gas Sector: Emission Standards for New, Reconstructed, and Modified Sources
.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to
Oil and Natural Gas Sector: Emission Standards for New, Reconstructed, and Modified Sources
(published at 81 Fed. Reg. 35824 (June 3, 2016)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
