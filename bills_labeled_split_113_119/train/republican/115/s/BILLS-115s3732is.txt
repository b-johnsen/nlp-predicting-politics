1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Protection of Source Code Act
.
2.
Procedure for obtaining certain intellectual property
Section 8 of the Commodity Exchange Act (
7 U.S.C. 12
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(j)
Under this Act, the Commission may not compel a person to produce or furnish source code, including algorithmic trading source code or similar intellectual property that forms the basis for design of the source code, to the Commission unless the Commission first issues a subpoena.
.
