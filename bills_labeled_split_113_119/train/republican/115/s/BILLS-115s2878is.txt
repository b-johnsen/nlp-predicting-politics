4.
GAO report on medical costs
(a)
Definition
In this section, the term
disabled officer
means a public safety officer to whom a benefit is payable under subpart 1 of part L of title I of the Omnibus Crime Control and Safe Streets Act of 1968 (
34 U.S.C. 10281
et seq.) based on the permanent and total disability of the officer, as described in section 1201(b) of that subpart (
34 U.S.C. 10281(b)
).
(b)
Report
Not later than 180 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Comptroller General of the United States shall submit to the Committee on the Judiciary of the Senate and the Committee on the Judiciary of the House of Representatives a report that estimates the average medical costs incurred by a disabled officer over the lifetime of the officer after sustaining the injury that caused the disability.
