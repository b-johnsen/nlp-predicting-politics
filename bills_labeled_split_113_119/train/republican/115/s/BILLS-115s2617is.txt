3.
Recognition of National Aviation Cadet Museum of the United States
(a)
Recognition
The Aviation Cadet Museum in Eureka Springs, Arkansas, is recognized as the National Aviation Cadet Museum of the United States.
(b)
Effect of recognition
(1)
In general
The national museum recognized by subsection (a) is not a unit of the National Park System.
(2)
Use of Federal funds
The recognition of the national museum by subsection (a) shall not require Federal funds to be expended for any purpose related to the national museum.
