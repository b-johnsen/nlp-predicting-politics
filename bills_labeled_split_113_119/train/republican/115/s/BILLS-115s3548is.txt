1.
Encinal Veterans Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 400 N. Main Street in Encinal, Texas, shall be known and designated as the
Encinal Veterans Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Encinal Veterans Post Office
.
