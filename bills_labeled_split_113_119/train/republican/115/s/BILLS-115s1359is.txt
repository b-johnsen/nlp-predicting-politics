(a)
Sense of Congress
It is the sense of Congress that the John F. Kennedy Center for the Performing Arts (referred to in this Act as the

The Center shall place within the Center a plaque containing an inscription to commemorate the 60th anniversary of the signing of the National Cultural Center Act (
20 U.S.C. 76h
et seq.) by President Dwight D. Eisenhower.
