Comprehensive training strategy

(1)
a plan to streamline current training processes, including an assessment of the utility of further consolidating internal training programs, technology, and funding,
(2)
a plan to develop annual training regarding taxpayer rights, including the role of the Office of the Taxpayer Advocate,
(3)
a plan to improve technology-based training, including by developing or adopting state-of-the-art training delivery methods,
(4)
proposals to focus employee training on early, fair, and efficient resolution of taxpayer disputes and to ensure consistency of skill development and employee evaluation throughout the Internal Revenue Service,
(5)

(c)
