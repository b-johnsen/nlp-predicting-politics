3.
Definitions
In this Act:
(1)
Elder justice coordinating council
The term
Elder Justice Coordinating Council
means the Council established under section 2021 of the Social Security Act (
42 U.S.C. 1397k
).
(2)
Guardianship
The term
guardianship
means a legal relationship established by a court in which a person is given the legal authority over another person because the other person is unable to make safe and sound decisions regarding their person or property.
(3)
Individuals subject to guardianship
The term
individuals subject to guardianship
means any individual 18 years or older placed under a guardianship.
