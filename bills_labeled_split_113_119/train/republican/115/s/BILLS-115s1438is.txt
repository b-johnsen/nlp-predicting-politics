1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Gateway Arch National Park Designation Act
.
2.
Designation of Gateway Arch National Park
(a)
Redesignation
The Jefferson National Expansion Memorial established under the Act of May 17, 1954 (
16 U.S.C. 450jj
et seq.), shall be known and designated as the
Gateway Arch National Park
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the Jefferson National Expansion Memorial shall be considered to be a reference to the
Gateway Arch National Park
.
