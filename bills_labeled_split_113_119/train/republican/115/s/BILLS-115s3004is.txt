(b)
Amendments to regulations
Not later than 60 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Securities and Exchange Commission shall make any necessary amendments to regulations of the Commission that are in effect as of the date of enactment of this Act in order to carry out this Act and the amendments made by this Act.
(c)
Effective date
This Act, and the amendments made by this Act, shall take effect on the date that is 60 days after the date of enactment of this Act.
