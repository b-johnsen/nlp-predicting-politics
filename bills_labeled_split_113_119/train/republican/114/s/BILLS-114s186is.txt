1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Taxpayer Accountability Act
.
2.
Deadline for
responses to taxpayer correspondence
Not later than 30 days after receiving any written correspondence from a taxpayer, the Internal Revenue Service shall provide a substantive written response. For purposes of the preceding sentence, an acknowledgment letter shall not be treated as a substantive response.

4.
Deadline for
conclusion of audits of individual taxpayers
Any audit of a tax return of an individual by the Internal Revenue Service shall be concluded not later than 1 year after the date of the initiation of such audit and the Internal Revenue Service shall not assess any tax with respect to which such audit relates after the conclusion of such audit.
