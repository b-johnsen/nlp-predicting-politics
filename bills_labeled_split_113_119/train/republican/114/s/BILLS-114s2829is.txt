(a)

The United States Merchant Marine Academy shall employ or contract with at least 1 full-time sexual assault response coordinator who shall reside on or near the Academy. (B)

(3)
Primary duties
While performing the duties of a victim advocate under this subsection, a designated employee shall芒聙聰
(A)

(B)
