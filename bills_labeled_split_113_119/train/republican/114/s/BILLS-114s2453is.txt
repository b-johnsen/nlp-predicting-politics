(c)
Analysis of reported bills by CBO
Section 202(g) of the Congressional Budget Act of 1974 (
2 U.S.C. 602(g)
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(3)
Duplicative and overlapping Government programs
For each bill or joint resolution of a public character reported by any committee (including the Committee on Appropriations and the Committee on the Budget) the Congressional Budget Office shall submit to Congress an analysis of whether the bill or joint resolution creates any new Federal program, office, or initiative that would duplicate or overlap with any existing Federal program, office, or initiative with similar mission, purpose, goals, or activities along with a listing of all of the overlapping or duplicative Federal program or programs, office or offices, or initiative or initiatives.
