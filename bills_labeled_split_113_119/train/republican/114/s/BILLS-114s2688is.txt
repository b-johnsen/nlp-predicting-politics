1.
Sergeant First Class Terryl L. Pasker Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 615 6th Avenue SE in Cedar Rapids, Iowa, shall be known and designated as the
Sergeant First Class Terryl L. Pasker Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Sergeant First Class Terryl L. Pasker Post Office Building
.
