1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Budgetary Accuracy in Scoring Interest Costs Act of 2016
.
2.
CBO and JCT estimates to include debt servicing costs
(a)
In general
Title IV of the Congressional Budget and Impoundment Control Act of 1974 (
2 U.S.C. 651 et seq.
) is amended by inserting after section 402 the following:
403.
Estimates to include debt servicing costs
Any estimate prepared by the Congressional Budget Office under section 402, and any estimate prepared by the Joint Committee on Taxation, shall include, to the extent practicable, the costs (if any) of servicing the debt subject to limit under section 3101 of title 31, United States Code.
.
(b)
Clerical amendment
The table of contents of such Act is amended by inserting after the item relating to section 402 the following:
403. Estimates to include debt servicing costs.
.
