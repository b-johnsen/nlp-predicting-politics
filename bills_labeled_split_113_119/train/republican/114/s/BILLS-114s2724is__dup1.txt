1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Separation of Powers Restoration Act of 2016
.
2.
Judicial review of statutory and regulatory interpretations
Section 706 of title 5, United States Code, is amended, in the matter preceding paragraph (1), by striking
all relevant questions of law, interpret constitutional and statutory provisions
and inserting
de novo all relevant questions of law, including the interpretation of constitutional and statutory provisions and rules
.
