1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Separation of Powers Act of 2015
.
2.
Prohibition on use of funds
(a)
For deferred action or parole
None of the funds appropriated or otherwise made available to any Federal department or agency may
be used to parole an alien into the United States or grant deferred action
on a final order of removal, for any reason other than on an individual
case-by-case basis for urgent humanitarian reasons.
(b)
For the issuance of work permits or green cards
None of the funds appropriated or otherwise made available to any Federal department or agency may
be used to issue to any alien who, on the date of enactment of this Act,
is unlawfully present in the United States any document attesting to the
lawful permanent resident status of that alien, or to the authorization
for employment in the United States of that alien.
