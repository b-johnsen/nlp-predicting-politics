Loss of Federal Pell Grant eligibility
(A)
Default
An individual who is in default of an obligation to repay a Federal Pell Grant that is being treated as a loan under paragraph (1) shall be ineligible to receive a Federal Pell Grant under this section.

Second time dropout
An individual who has received a Federal Pell Grant for enrollment in a program that is being treated as a loan under paragraph (1), and who leaves such program for a second time (other than for a reason described in clause (i) or (iii) of paragraph (3)(A)), shall be ineligible to receive a Federal Pell Grant under this section.
