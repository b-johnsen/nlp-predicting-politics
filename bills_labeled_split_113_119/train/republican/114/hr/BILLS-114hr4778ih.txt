1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Title X Transparency Act
.
2.
Report on medical items and services being offered by recipients of title X assistance
Not later than 2 years after the date of enactment of this Act, the Comptroller General of the United States shall submit to Congress a report containing an evaluation of all medical items and services being offered in the facilities of recipients of assistance under title X of the Public Health Service Act (
42 U.S.C. 300 et seq.
) or of their affiliates, subsidiaries, successors, or clinics.
