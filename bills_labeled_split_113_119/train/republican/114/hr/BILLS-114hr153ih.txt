(a)
In general
Paragraph (3) of
section 501(c)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 (relating to list of exempt

(b)
Effective date
The amendments made by this section shall apply to taxable years ending after the date of the

The amendments made by this section shall not invalidate or limit any provision of the Federal
