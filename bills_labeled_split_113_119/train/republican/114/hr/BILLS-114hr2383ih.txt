1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Nutritious Options for Meals Act of 2015
.
2.
Amendment
Section 3(k)(1) of the Food and Nutrition Act of 2008 (
7 U.S.C. 2012(k)(1)
) is amended by striking
any food
the first place it appears and all that follows through
consumption
the last place it appears, and inserting the following:
a supplemental food of the kind prescribed by the Secretary under section 17(b)(14) of the Child Nutrition Act of 1966, or lean meat or poultry as prescribed by the Secretary,
.
