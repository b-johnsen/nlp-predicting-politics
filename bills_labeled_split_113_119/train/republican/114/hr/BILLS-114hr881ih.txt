1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Bringing Awareness and Knowledge to Exempt Schools Against Legislative Encroachment Act
.
2.
Prohibition of certain rules
The Secretary of Agriculture shall not implement, administer, or enforce the rule entitled
National School Lunch Program and School Breakfast Program: Nutrition Standards for All Foods Sold in School as Required by the Healthy, Hunger-Free Kids Act of 2010
published by the Department of Agriculture in the Federal Register on June 28, 2013 (78 Fed. Reg. 39068 et seq.), with respect to food sold as a fundraiser that takes place on the grounds of a school, including any requirements with respect to limiting the number of such fundraisers by State agencies or the Secretary of Agriculture.
