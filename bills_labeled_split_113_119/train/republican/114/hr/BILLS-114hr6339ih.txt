1.
Limitation on use of Judgement Fund
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, no amounts may be paid from the fund established under section 1304 of title 31, United State Code (relating to judgments, awards, and compromise settlements), for any compromise settlement in connection with any proceeding brought against the Government under section 1342 of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act (
42 U.S.C. 18062(b)
).
