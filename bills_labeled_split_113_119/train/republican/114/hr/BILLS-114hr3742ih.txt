Payment of premiums by certain qualified third parties on behalf of a qualified individual
A qualified individual enrolled in any qualified health plan may allow certain third parties to pay any applicable premium or cost sharing owed by such individual to the health insurance issuer issuing such qualified health plan, and the health insurance issuer shall accept such payments made on behalf of such individuals, including those from the following:
(1)
