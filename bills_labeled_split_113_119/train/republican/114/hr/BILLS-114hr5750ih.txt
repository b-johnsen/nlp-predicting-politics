3.
Definitions
In this Act:
(1)
Centralized delivery
The term
centralized delivery
means a primary mode of mail delivery whereby mail receptacles of a number of delivery points are grouped or clustered at a single location.
(2)
Curbside delivery
The term
curbside delivery
means a primary mode of mail delivery whereby a mail receptacle is situated at the edge of a sidewalk abutting a road or curb, at a road, or at a curb, and can be served by a letter carrier from a motorized vehicle.
