Use of Emancipation Hall for ceremony to present Congressional Gold Medal to American Fighter Aces
Emancipation Hall in the Capitol Visitor Center is authorized to be used on May 20, 2015, for a ceremony
to present the Congressional Gold Medal to the American Fighter Aces collectively, in recognition of their heroic military service and defense of our country's freedom throughout the history of aviation warfare. Physical preparations for the conduct of the ceremony shall be carried out in accordance with such conditions as the Architect of the Capitol may prescribe.
Passed the House of Representatives April 13, 2015.
Karen L. Haas,
Clerk.
