) introduced the following joint resolution; which was read twice and referred to the
Committee on Agriculture, Nutrition, and Forestry
JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule
submitted by the Secretary of Agriculture relating to inspection of fish
of the order Siluriformes.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Secretary of Agriculture relating to
Mandatory Inspection of Fish of the Order Siluriformes and Products Derived From Such Fish
(80 Fed. Reg. 75590; December 2, 2015), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
