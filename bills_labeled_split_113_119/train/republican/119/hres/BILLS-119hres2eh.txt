In the House of Representatives, U. S.,
January 3, 2025
RESOLUTION
That the Senate be informed that a quorum of the House of Representatives has assembled; that Mike Johnson, a Representative from the State of Louisiana, has been elected Speaker; and that Kevin McCumber, a citizen of the State of Illinois, has been elected Clerk of the House of Representatives of the One Hundred Nineteenth Congress.
Kevin F. McCumber,
Clerk.
