1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Stopping Political Discrimination in Disaster Assistance Act
.
2.
Nondiscrimination in disaster assistance
Section 308(a) of the Robert T. Stafford Disaster Relief and Emergency Assistance Act (
42 U.S.C. 5151(a)
) is amended by striking
or economic status
and inserting
economic status, or political affiliation
.
