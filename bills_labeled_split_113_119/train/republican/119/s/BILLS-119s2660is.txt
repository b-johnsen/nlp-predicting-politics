1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Modern Risk Detection Act of 2025
.
2.
Risk-based approaches
Section 60102 of title 49, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
(u)
Risk-Based approaches
The Secretary shall ensure that the standards prescribed under this chapter allow for the use, to the maximum extent practicable, of risk-based approaches, concepts, or principles in complying with those standards.
.
