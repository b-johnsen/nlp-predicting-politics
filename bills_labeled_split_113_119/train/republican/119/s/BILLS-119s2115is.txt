(b)
Interference with commerce by blocking public roads
(1)
Offense
It shall be unlawful to, in any way or degree, purposely obstruct, delay, or affect commerce or the movement of any article or commodity in commerce by blocking a public road or highway, or to attempt or conspire so to do.
(2)
Penalty
Any person who violates paragraph (1) shall be fined under this title, imprisoned not more than 5 years, or both.
