1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Critical Water Resources Prioritization Act of 2025

Duration and renewal
(A)
Initial period
An exemption granted by the Secretary under paragraph (2) shall be valid for a period of not more than 180 days.

(b)
Regulations
Not later than 180 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Secretary (as defined in section 3 of the Endangered Species Act of 1973 (
