1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Fiscal Accountability for Interest on Reserves Act
or the
FAIR Act
.
2.
Earnings on balances
(a)
In general
Section 19(b) of the Federal Reserve Act (
12 U.S.C. 461(b)
) is amended by striking paragraph (12).
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by subsection (a) shall take effect on the date that is 180 days after the date of enactment of this Act.
