1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Reauthorizing Support and Treatment for Officers in Crisis Act of 2025
.
2.
Reauthorization
Section 1001(a)(21) of title I of the Omnibus Crime Control and Safe Streets Act of 1968 (
34 U.S.C. 10261(a)(21)
) is amended by striking
2020 through 2024
and inserting
2025 through 2029
.
