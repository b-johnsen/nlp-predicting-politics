1.
2nd Lieutenant Mitch Williams Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 50 East 100 North in Moab, Utah, shall be known and designated as the
2nd Lieutenant Mitch Williams Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
2nd Lieutenant Mitch Williams Post Office
.
