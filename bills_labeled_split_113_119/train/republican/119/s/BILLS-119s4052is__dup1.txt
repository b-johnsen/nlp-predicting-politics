1.
Edwin P. Hubble Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 203 North Clay Street in Marshfield, Missouri, shall be known and designated as the
Edwin P. Hubble Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Edwin P. Hubble Post Office
.
