S. J. RES. 15
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
February 5, 2025
Mr. Lee
introduced the following joint resolution; which was read twice and referred to the
Committee on Banking, Housing, and Urban Affairs
JOINT RESOLUTION
Disapproving the rule submitted by the Financial Crimes Enforcement Network relating to
Anti-Money Laundering Regulations for Residential Real Estate Transfers
.
That Congress disapproves the final rule submitted by the Financial Crimes Enforcement Network relating to
Anti-Money Laundering Regulations for Residential Real Estate Transfers
(89 Fed. Reg. 70258 (August 29, 2024)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
