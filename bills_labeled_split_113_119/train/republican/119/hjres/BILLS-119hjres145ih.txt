1.
Each judge of the Supreme Court and each inferior court shall be appointed to serve during good behavior for a term of 20 years.
2.
No person who has served a term of 20 years as a judge of the Supreme Court or an inferior court shall be eligible for reappointment as a judge in the court in which such term was served.
3.
This article shall only apply to an appointment occurring on or after the date of the ratification of this article.
