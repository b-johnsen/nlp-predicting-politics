JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Internal Revenue Service relating to Rules for Supervisory Approval of Penalties.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Internal Revenue Service relating to
Rules for Supervisory Approval of Penalties
(89 Fed. Reg. 104419 (December 23, 2024)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
