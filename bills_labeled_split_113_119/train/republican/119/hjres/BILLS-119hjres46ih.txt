JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to
Decabromodiphenyl Ether and Phenol, Isopropylated Phosphate (3:1); Revision to the Regulation of Persistent, Bioaccumulative, and Toxic Chemicals Under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA)
.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to
Decabromodiphenyl Ether and Phenol, Isopropylated Phosphate (3:1); Revision to the Regulation of Persistent, Bioaccumulative, and Toxic Chemicals Under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA)
(89 Fed. Reg. 91486 (November 19, 2024)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
