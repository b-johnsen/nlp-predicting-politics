JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to
National Primary Drinking Water Regulations for Lead and Copper: Improvements (LCRI)
.
That
Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to
National Primary Drinking Water Regulations for Lead and Copper: Improvements (LCRI)
(89 Fed. Reg. 86418 (October 30, 2024)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
