JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to
Trichloroethylene (TCE); Regulation Under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA)
.
That Congress disapproves the final rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to
Trichloroethylene (TCE); Regulation Under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA)
(89 Fed. Reg. 102568 (December 17, 2024)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
