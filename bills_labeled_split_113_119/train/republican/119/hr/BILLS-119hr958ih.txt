1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Train More Primary Care Doctors Act of 2025
.
2.
Primary care training and enhancement
Section 747(c)(1) of the Public Health Service Act (
42 U.S.C. 293k(c)(1)
) is amended by striking
$48,924,000 for each of fiscal years 2021 through 2025
and inserting
$49,924,000 for each of fiscal years 2025 through 2030
.
