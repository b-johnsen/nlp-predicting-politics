1.
Ursula ellen kennedy post office building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 340 East 1st Street in Tustin, California, shall be known and designated as the
Ursula Ellen Kennedy Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Ursula Ellen Kennedy Post Office Building
.
