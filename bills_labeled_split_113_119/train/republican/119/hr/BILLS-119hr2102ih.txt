(b)
Concurrent receipt generally
Section 1414(b) of title 10, United States Code, is amended to read as follows:
(b)
Special rule for chapter 61 disability retirees
A member or former member of the uniformed services who is entitled to retired pay under chapter 61 of this title for any month and who is also entitled for that month to veterans' disability compensation for a combat-related disability (as that term is defined in section 1413a(e) of this title) is entitled to be paid both for that month without regard to sections 5304 and 5305 of title 38.
.
