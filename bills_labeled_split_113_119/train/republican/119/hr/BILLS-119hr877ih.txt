1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Deliver for Veterans Act
.
2.
Authority of the Secretary of Veterans Affairs to pay costs associated with the delivery of an adaptive vehicle to an eligible person
Section 3902(a) of title 38, United States Code, is amended by striking
by paying the total purchase price of the automobile or other conveyance
and inserting
by paying the total purchase price of the automobile or other conveyance, and the total shipping price to deliver the automobile or other conveyance to the veteran
.
