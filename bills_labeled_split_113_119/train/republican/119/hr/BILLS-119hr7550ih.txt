1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Permanent Tax Relief for Seniors Act
.
2.
Deduction for seniors made permanent
(a)
In general
Section 151(d)(5)(C)(i)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking
In the case of a taxable year beginning before January 1, 2029, there
and inserting
There
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by this section shall apply to taxable years beginning after December 31, 2026.
