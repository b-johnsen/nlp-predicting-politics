1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Upper Raritan River Watershed Wild and Scenic River Study Act of 2025
.
2.
Designation of Upper Raritan River Watershed in New Jersey for study for potential addition to National Wild and Scenic Rivers System
(a)
Designation
Section 5(a) of the Wild and Scenic Rivers Act (
16 U.S.C. 1276(a)
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(147)
Upper raritan river watershed, new jersey
The segments of the North Branch Raritan River, the South Branch Raritan River, the Black or Lamington River, and their tributaries, within the Upper Raritan River watershed, including, but not limited to, the following segments:
(A)
The North Branch Raritan River from its headwaters to its confluence with the South Branch Raritan River.
(B)
The South Branch Raritan River from its headwaters to its confluence with the North Branch Raritan River.
(C)
The Lamington-Black River from its headwaters to its confluence with the North Branch Raritan River.
(D)
All tributaries of the North Branch Raritan River, South Branch Raritan River (including those upstream of the Spruce Run Reservoir), and Lamington-Black River within the Upper Raritan River watershed.
.
