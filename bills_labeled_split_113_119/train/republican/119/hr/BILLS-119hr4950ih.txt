1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Data Broadband Reporting and Integration for Deployment in Geographically Essential Areas Act
or the
Data BRIDGE Act
.
2.
FCC National Broadband Map
(a)
In general
Not later than 180 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Commission shall update (and thereafter maintain) the map described in section 802(c)(1)(A) of the Communications Act of 1934 (
47 U.S.C. 642(c)(1)(A)
) in a manner that incorporates, as a layer of such map, data on the location of agricultural areas.

Definitions
