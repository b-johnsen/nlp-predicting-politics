1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Direct Seller and Real Estate Agent Harmonization Act
.
2.
Definitions
Section 3(e) of the Fair Labor Standards Act of 1938 (
29 U.S.C. 203(e)
) is amended by inserting after paragraph (5) the following:
(6)
The term
employee
does not include any direct seller or qualified real estate agent (as such terms are defined in
section 3508(b)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986).
.
