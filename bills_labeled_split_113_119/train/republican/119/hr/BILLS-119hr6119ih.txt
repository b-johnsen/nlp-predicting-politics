The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 202 South Chestnut Avenue in Marshfield, Wisconsin, shall be known and designated as the

References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the

Gordy
Richard Schmoll Post Office Building
