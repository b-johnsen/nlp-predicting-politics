1.
Deputy Sheriff Brian Harris Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 35 South Main Street in Glendale, Utah, shall be known and designated as the
Deputy Sheriff Brian Harris Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Deputy Sheriff Brian Harris Post Office
.
