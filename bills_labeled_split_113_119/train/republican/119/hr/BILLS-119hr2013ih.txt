1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Medicare Home Health Accessibility Act
.
2.
Eligibility for home health services on the basis of a need for occupational therapy
(a)
Part A
Section 1814(a)(2)(C) of the
Social Security Act
(
42 U.S.C. 1395f(a)(2)(C)
) is amended by striking
or speech therapy
and all that follows through
need occupational therapy
and inserting
, occupational, or speech therapy
.
(b)
Part B
Section 1835(a)(2)(A) of the
Social Security Act
(
42 U.S.C. 1395n(a)(2)(A)
) is amended by striking
or speech therapy
and all that follows through
need occupational therapy
and inserting
, occupational, or speech therapy
.
(c)
Effective date
The amendments made by subsections (a) and (b) shall apply to home health services provided on or after January 1, 2026.
