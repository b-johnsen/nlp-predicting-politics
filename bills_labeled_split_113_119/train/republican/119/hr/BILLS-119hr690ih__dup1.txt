1.
Prohibition on use of Federal funds to implement Salmonella framework for raw poultry products
No Federal funds may be used to finalize, implement, administer, or enforce the proposed rule and proposed determination entitled
Salmonella Framework for Raw Poultry Products
published by the Food Safety and Inspection Service of the Department of Agriculture in the Federal Register on August 7, 2024 (89 Fed. Reg. 64678 et seq.).
