1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Low-Income Housing Tax Credit Elimination Act
.
2.
Sunset of low-income housing tax credit
Section 42
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by adding at the end the following new subsection:
(o)
Sunset
No credit shall be determined under subsection (a) with respect to any building placed in service in a taxable year beginning after the date of the enactment of this subsection.
.
