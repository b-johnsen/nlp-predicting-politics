Short title

Home Health Stabilization Act of 2025
.

(v)
Special rule for 2026 and 2027
For 2026 and 2027, the Secretary shall establish the national, standardized 30-day period payment rate based on the 2025 rate, taking into account the adjustment under clause (iv) and updated based on all other provisions of this section, except that such rate for 2026 or 2027 shall not be further adjusted based on clause (ii) or clause (iii).
(vi)
Exception
In determining actual expenditures under clause (i) for 2026 and 2027 the Secretary shall exclude the additional amounts paid as a result of clauses (iv) and (v), and no adjustments under clause (ii) or clause (iii) shall be made to offset for such amounts.
