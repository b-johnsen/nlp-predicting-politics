1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Welfare Abuse and Laundering Zillions Act
or the
WALZ Act
.
2.
Requiring investigations of certain payment increases under HHS programs
In the case that, with respect to any 6-month period, the total amount paid to providers of services and suppliers under a program administered by the Secretary of Health and Human Services increases by 10 percent or more as compared to the prior 6-month period, the Inspector General of the Department of Health and Human Services shall open an investigation into such program.
