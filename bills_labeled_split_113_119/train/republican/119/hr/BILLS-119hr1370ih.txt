1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Unobligated Spending Adjustment to Focus Investment on Relief and Support for Taxpayers Act
or the
USA FIRST Act
.
2.
Transfer of unobligated funds to the disaster relief fund
(a)
In general
Any unobligated covered funds shall be transferred to the Disaster Relief Fund to carry out the Robert T. Stafford Disaster Relief and Emergency Assistance Act (
42 U.S.C. 5121 et seq.
) with respect to major disasters declared under section 401 of the Robert T. Stafford Disaster Relief and Emergency Assistance Act (
42 U.S.C. 5170
).
