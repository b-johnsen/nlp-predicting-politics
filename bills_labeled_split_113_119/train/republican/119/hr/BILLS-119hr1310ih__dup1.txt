POSTAL Act
.
2.
Limitation on closing or consolidating Postal Service processing and distribution centers in States
(a)
In general
The United States Postal Service may not close, consolidate, downgrade, or take any other similar action with respect to any processing and distribution center in a State if such action would result in no such center being located in that State.
