1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Written Informed Consent Act
.
2.
Expansion of certain directive of the Veterans Health Administration regarding informed consent to apply to certain types of medications
The Secretary of Veterans Affairs shall update VHA Directive 1005 (dated May 13, 2020, and titled
Informed Consent For Long-term Opioid Therapy For Pain
) to apply to the following types of medications:
(1)
Antipsychotics.
(2)
Stimulants.
(3)
Antidepressants.
(4)
Anxiolytics.
(5)
Narcotics.
