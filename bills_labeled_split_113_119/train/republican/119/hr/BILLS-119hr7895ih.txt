Section 408(b)(2)(B) of the Employee Retirement Income Security Act (
29 U.S.C. 1108(b)(2)(B)
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(x)
In the case of a contract or arrangement between a covered plan and a covered service provider for pharmacy benefit management services, no amount of compensation may be paid, directly or indirectly, by such service provider to a brokerage firm, broker, consultant, advisor, or any other individual for the referral of the covered planâs or health insurance issuer's business to the covered service provider.
.
(b)
