1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
No Funds for Repeat Child Care Violations Act
.
2.
Strengthening the authority to withhold funds for fraud
Section 658I(b)(2)(B) of the Child Care and Development Block Grant Act of 1990 (
42 U.S.C. 9858g(b)(2)
) is amended by striking
Secretary may
and inserting
Secretary shall
.
