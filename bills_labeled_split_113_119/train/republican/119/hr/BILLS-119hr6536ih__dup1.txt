(c)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)
Depository institution
The term
depository institution
has the meaning given that term in section 3 of the Federal Deposit Insurance Act (
12 U.S.C. 1813
).
(2)
Federal banking agencies
The term
Federal banking agencies
means the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System, the Comptroller of the Currency, and the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation.
