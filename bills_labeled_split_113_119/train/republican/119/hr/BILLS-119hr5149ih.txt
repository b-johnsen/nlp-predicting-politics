2.
Prohibition on male participation in female sports at DODEA schools
(a)
In general
A male student may not participate in a female sport at a school operated by the Department of Defense Education Activity.
(b)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)
Female
The term
female
refers to an individual who naturally has, had, will have, or would have, but for a developmental or genetic anomaly or historical accident, the reproductive system that at some point produces, transports, and uses eggs for fertilization.
(2)
Female sport
The term
female sport
means an athletic program or activity designated exclusively for participation by students who are female.
(3)
Male
The term
male
refers to an individual who naturally has, had, will have, or would have, but for a developmental or genetic anomaly or historical accident, the reproductive system that at some point produces, transports, and uses sperm for fertilization.
