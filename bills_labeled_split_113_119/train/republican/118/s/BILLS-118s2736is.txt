1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Allowing for Recreational Resources for Outdoor Wellness Act
or the
ARROW Act
.
2.
Exception
Section 8526(7) of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965 (
20 U.S.C. 7906(7)
) is amended by inserting
, except that this paragraph shall not apply to the use of funds under this Act for sports clubs, teams, training, or related activities provided for students
before the period at the end.
