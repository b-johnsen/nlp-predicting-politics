1.
Short titles
This Act may be cited as the
National Oversight of Transported Immigrants to Communities is Essential Act
or the
NOTICE Act
.
2.
Notification requirement
(a)
In general
The Secretary of Homeland Security and the Secretary of Health and Human Services shall notify the governor of a State and the chief executive officer of a unit of local government in such State of any alien that is being transported by the Department of Homeland Security, the Department of Health and Human Services, or another entity receiving Federal funding for such transportation, from a location within 100 miles of the United States border to such State and local jurisdiction before such transportation occurs.
