1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Dependable Classification of Airports Act
.
2.
Treatment of airports reclassified from small to medium hub airports
(a)
In general
Chapter 471
of title 49, United States Code, is amended by inserting after
section 47131
the following:
47132.
Treatment of airports reclassified from small to medium hub airports
With respect to the application of law and regulation, the owner or operator of a small hub airport that, in fiscal year 2021 or any succeeding fiscal year, is designated as a medium hub airport, may elect to be treated as a small hub airport for any period of up to 5 consecutive fiscal years, beginning with the fiscal year in which the small hub airport is to be designated as a medium hub airport.
.
(b)
Clerical amendment
The analysis for
chapter 471
of title 49, United States Code, is amended by inserting after the item relating to
section 47131
the following:
47132. Treatment of airports reclassified from small to medium hub airports.
.
