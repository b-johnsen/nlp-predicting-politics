2.
Motor Vehicle Dealers Trade Regulation Rule
(a)
Prohibition
The Commission may not finalize, implement, or enforce a covered proposed rule unless the Commission satisfies the requirements described in subsection (b), and any other requirements applicable under Federal law, with respect to the covered proposed rule.
