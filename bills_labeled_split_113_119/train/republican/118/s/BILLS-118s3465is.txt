2.
Excise tax on certain large private college and university endowments
(a)
In general
Subchapter H of
chapter 42
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by adding at the end the following new section:
4969.
Excise tax on certain large private college and university endowments
(a)
Tax imposed
There is hereby imposed on each specified applicable educational institution for the institution's first taxable year beginning in 2023 a tax equal to 6 percent of the aggregate fair market value of the assets of the institution at the end of the preceding taxable year.
