1.
Repayment of State funds used to operate units of the National Park System during a Government shutdown
(a)
Definition of Government shutdown
In this section, the term
Government shutdown
means a lapse in appropriations for 1 or more agencies of the Federal Government.
(b)
Repayment required
Not later than 30 days after the date on which a Government shutdown ends, on the request of a State that expended State funds to operate 1 or more units of the National Park System in the State during the Government shutdown, the Secretary of the Interior shall pay to that State an amount equal to the amount expended by the State to operate the 1 or more units of the National Park System in that State during the Government shutdown.
