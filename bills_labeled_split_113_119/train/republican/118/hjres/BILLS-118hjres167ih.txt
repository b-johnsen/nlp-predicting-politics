JOINT RESOLUTION
Disapproving the rule submitted by the Department of Agriculture relating to
Use of Electronic Identification Eartags as Official Identification in Cattle and Bison
.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Department of Agriculture relating to
Use of Electronic Identification Eartags as Official Identification in Cattle and Bison
published in the Federal Register on May 9, 2024 (89 Fed. Reg. 39540 et seq.), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
