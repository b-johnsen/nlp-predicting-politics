JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration entitled
Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards; Automatic Emergency Braking Systems for Light Vehicles
.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration entitled
Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards; Automatic Emergency Braking Systems for Light Vehicles
(89 Fed. Reg. 39686; published May 9, 2024), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
