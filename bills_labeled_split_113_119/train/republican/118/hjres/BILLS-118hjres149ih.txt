JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the United States Fish and Wildlife Service relating to
Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Establishment of a Nonessential Experimental Population of Grizzly Bear in the North Cascades Ecosystem, Washington State
.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the United States Fish and Wildlife Service relating to
Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Establishment of a Nonessential Experimental Population of Grizzly Bear in the North Cascades Ecosystem, Washington State
(89 Fed. Reg. 36982; published May 3, 2024), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
