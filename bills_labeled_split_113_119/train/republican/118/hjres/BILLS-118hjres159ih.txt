JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the United States Fish and Wildlife Service relating to
Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Regulations Pertaining to Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants
.
That
Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the United States Fish and Wildlife Service relating to
Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Regulations Pertaining to Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants
(89 Fed. Reg. 23919; published April 5, 2024), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
