JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Office of the Comptroller of the Currency relating to
Principles for Climate-Related Financial Risk Management for Large Financial Institutions
.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Office of the Comptroller of the Currency relating to
Principles for Climate-Related Financial Risk Management for Large Financial Institutions
(88 Fed. Reg. 74183; published October 30, 2023), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
