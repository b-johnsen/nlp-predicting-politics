JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection relating to
Debt Collection Practices (Regulation F); Deceptive and Unfair Collection of Medical Debt
.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection relating to
Debt Collection Practices (Regulation F); Deceptive and Unfair Collection of Medical Debt
(89 Fed. Reg. 80715; published October 4, 2024), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
