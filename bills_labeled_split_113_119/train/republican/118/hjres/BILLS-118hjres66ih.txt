Disapproving the rule submitted by the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau relating to
Small Business Lending Under the Equal Credit Opportunity Act (Regulation B)

Small Business Lending Under the Equal Credit Opportunity Act (Regulation B)
(88 Fed. Reg. 35150), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
