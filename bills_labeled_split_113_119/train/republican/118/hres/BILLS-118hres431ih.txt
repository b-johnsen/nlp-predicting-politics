1.
Office of Talent and Development
(a)
Finding
The House of Representatives finds that, in order to better serve the American people, the Office of Diversity and Inclusion should be renamed and redirected as the Office of Talent and Development and transferred to the Office of the Chief Administrative Officer as part of a broader centralization of the human resources programs of the House in the Office of the Chief Administrative Officer.
