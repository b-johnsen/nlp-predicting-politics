JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Office of Federal Contract Compliance Programs of the Department of Labor relating to
Rescission of Implementing Legal Requirements Regarding the Equal Opportunity Clause's Religious Exemption Rule
.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Office of Federal Contract Compliance Programs of the Department of Labor relating to
Rescission of Implementing Legal Requirements Regarding the Equal Opportunity Clause's Religious Exemption Rule
(88 Fed. Reg. 12842 (March 1, 2023)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
