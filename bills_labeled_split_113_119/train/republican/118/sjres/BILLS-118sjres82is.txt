JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Food and Drug Administration relating to

Medical Devices; Laboratory Developed Tests
(89 Fed. Reg. 37286 (May 6, 2024)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
