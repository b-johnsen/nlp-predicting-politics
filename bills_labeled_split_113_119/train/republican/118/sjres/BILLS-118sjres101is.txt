JOINT RESOLUTION
Disapproving the rule submitted by the Department of Health and Human Services relating to
Designated Placement Requirements for LGBTQI plus Children
.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Department of Health and Human Services relating to
Designated Placement Requirements for LGBTQI plus Children
published in the Federal Register on April 30, 2024 (89 Fed. Reg. 34818 et seq.), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
