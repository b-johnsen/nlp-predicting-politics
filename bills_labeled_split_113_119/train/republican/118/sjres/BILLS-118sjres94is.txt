JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the proposed rule submitted by the Office of Refugee Resettlement of the Administration for Children and Families of the Department of Health and Human Services relating to the Unaccompanied Children Program Foundational Rule.
That Congress disapproves the final rule submitted by the Office of Refugee Resettlement of the Administration for Children and Families of the Department of Health and Human Services relating to
Unaccompanied Children Program Foundational Rule
(89 Fed. Reg. 34384 (April 30, 2024)), seeking to replace regulations relating to the key aspects of the placement, care, and services provided to unaccompanied alien children, and such proposed rule shall have no force or effect.
