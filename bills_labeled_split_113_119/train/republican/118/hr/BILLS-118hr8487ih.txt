2.
Government pension offset exemption
(a)
In general
Section 202(k)(5) of the Social Security Act (
42 U.S.C. 402(k)(5)
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(D)
Subparagraph (A) shall not apply to a monthly insurance benefit of an individual under subsection (e), (f), or (g) if the individual is entitled to such benefit on the basis of the wages and self-employment income of an individual who died in the line of duty while serving as any of the following:
(i)
A Federal law enforcement officer (as defined in section 2 of the Law Enforcement Congressional Badge of Bravery Act of 2008 (
34 U.S.C. 50301(5)
).
(ii)
A State or local law enforcement officer (as defined in section 2(11) of such Act (
34 U.S.C. 50301(11)
).
(iii)
An employee in fire protection activities (as defined in section 3(y) of the Fair Labor Standards Act of 1938 (
29 U.S.C. 203(y)
).
(iv)
Firefighting personnel (as defined in section 33(a)(5) of the Federal Fire Prevention and Control Act of 1974 (
15 U.S.C. 2229(a)(5)
).
(v)
A correctional officer (as defined in section 4051(a)(1) of title 18, United States Code).
(vi)
A State correctional officer (as defined in section 1121(b)(2) of title 18, United States Code).
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by this Act shall apply in relation to monthly insurance benefits payable for months beginning on or after the date of enactment of this Act.
