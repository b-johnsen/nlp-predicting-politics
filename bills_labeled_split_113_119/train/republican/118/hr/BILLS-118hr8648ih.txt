(2)
Enforcement
An institution of higher education that fails to comply with any provision of subsection (a)(30) for two consecutive award years shall be ineligible to participate in the programs authorized by this title for a period of not less than two years. To regain eligibility to participate in the programs authorized by this title, an institution of higher education shall demonstrate compliance with paragraph (1) prior to the completion of the period during which the institution is ineligible due to failure to comply with such paragraph.
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendments made by subsection (a) shall take effect on the first day of the first award year beginning after the date of enactment of this Act.
