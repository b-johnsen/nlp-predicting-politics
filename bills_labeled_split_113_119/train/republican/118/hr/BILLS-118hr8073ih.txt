(c)
Recertifications
(1)
In general
Not later than 180 days after the date on which the Secretary of State submits to Congress an initial certification under subsection (a), and every 180 days thereafter, the Secretary shall submit to Congress a recertification that the conditions described in subsection (b) are continuing to be met.
(2)
Further limitation
If the Secretary of State is unable to make a recertification described in paragraph (1), or fails to make such a recertification, the limitations on the obligations and expenditures of funds described in of subsection (a) shall apply.
