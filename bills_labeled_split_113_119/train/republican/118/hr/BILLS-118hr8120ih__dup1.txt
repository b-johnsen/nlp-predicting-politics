1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Motorist Tax Abuse Act
.
2.
Prohibition on congestion pricing
Section 1012(b) of the Intermodal Surface Transportation Efficiency Act of 1991 (
23 U.S.C. 149
note) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(9)
Prohibition on congestion pricing
Notwithstanding any other provision of this subsection, the Secretary may not establish or maintain cordon pricing under the value pricing pilot program under this section for the Central Business District Tolling Program for New York City.
.
