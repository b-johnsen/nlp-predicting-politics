1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Safeguarding Veterans 2nd Amendment Rights Act of 2024
.
2.
Prevention of use of unconstitutional gun confiscation laws against veterans
(a)
Prohibition
No officer or employee of the Department of Veterans Affairs shall, in the course of their duties as an officer or employee of such Department, initiate, participate in, or advocate for the removal of a firearm in any proceedings relating to a State gun confiscation law.
(b)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)
The term
gun confiscation law
means a State law that provides the authority for the removal of a firearm through a risk-based, temporary, and preemptive protective order.
(2)
The term
State
has the meaning given such term in section 101 of title 38, United States Code.
