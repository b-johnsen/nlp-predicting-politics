1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Study Abroad Act
.
2.
Revocation of visas
The Secretary of State shall revoke the F, J, or M visa of any alien who has been arrested for rioting or unlawful protest or who has been arrested while establishing, participating in, or promoting an encampment on the campus of an institution of higher education on or since October 7, 2023.
