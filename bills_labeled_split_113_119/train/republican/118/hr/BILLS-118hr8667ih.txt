1.
Designation of Duane E. Dewey VA Clinic
(a)
Designation
The outpatient clinic of the Department of Veterans Affairs located at 1905 North Mitchell Street in Cadillac, Michigan, shall, after the date of the enactment of this Act, be known and designated as the
Duane E. Dewey Department of Veterans Affairs Clinic
or the
Duane E. Dewey VA Clinic
.
(b)
Reference
Any reference in any law, regulation, map, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the community-based outpatient clinic referred to in subsection (a) shall be considered to be a reference to the Duane E. Dewey VA Clinic.
