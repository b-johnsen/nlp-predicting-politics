1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
American Meat Industry Protection Act of 2023
.
2.
No Federal funds supporting lab-grown meat
(a)
In general
No Federal funds may be used to support, directly or indirectly, the production, advancement, or enhancement of lab-grown meat, including throughâ
(1)
conducting or supporting research on lab-grown meat;
(2)
supporting meatpacking plants, organizations, or groups relating to lab-grown meat; and
(3)
excluding from the receipt of any assistance or other benefit under a program administered by the Secretary of Agriculture any farm, ranch, producer, or other entity that produces lab-grown meat.
(b)
Lab-Grown meat defined
In this section, the term
lab-grown meat
means meat sourced from cells of animals and artificially produced in a laboratory.
