1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Preserve Military Recognition Act of 2023
.
2.
Prohibition of gender-neutral pronoun in joint personal military decoration citations
Changes to Figure 3 (regarding opening sentences for joint personal military decoration citations of the Department of Defense) of the Manual of Military Decorations and Awards: DoD Joint Decorations and Awards (Department of Defense Manual 1348.33, Vol. 4 (December 21, 2016)) that became effective on August 7, 2023, and requires the use of the gender-neutral pronoun
themself
in joint personal military decoration citations of the Department of Defense, shall have no force or effect.
