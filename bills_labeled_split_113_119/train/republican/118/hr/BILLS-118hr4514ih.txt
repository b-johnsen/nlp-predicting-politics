1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Disinformation Governance Board Prohibition Act
.
2.
Termination of Disinformation Governance Board
(a)
In general
The Disinformation Governance Board of the Department of Homeland Security is hereby terminated.
(b)
Prohibition on funding the activities of the Disinformation Governance Board
No Federal funds authorized to be appropriated or otherwise made available may be used to establish or carry out the activities of any other entity that is substantially similar to the Disinformation Governance Board terminated by subsection (a).
