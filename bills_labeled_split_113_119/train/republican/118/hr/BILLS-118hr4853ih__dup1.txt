1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Legislative Reports on Estimates of Expenses and Impacts on Revenues of Bills and Laws Under PROMESA Act
.
2.
Purpose
The purpose of this Act is to allow the use of the reports of the Legislature prepared by its Budget Office, so that the information in those reports may be taken into consideration by the Oversight Board in the evaluation of the estimates of expenses and impact on revenues of the bills approved and converted into law carried out by the Oversight Board under the Puerto Rico Oversight, Management, and Economic Stability Act.
