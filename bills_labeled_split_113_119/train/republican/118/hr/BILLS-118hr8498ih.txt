There are authorized to be appropriated to the Department of Transportation for expenses necessary for the phased rehabilitation, modernization, and construction of facilities and infrastructure at the United States Merchant Marine Academy, consistent with the Full Speed Ahead plan developed by the Maritime Security Infrastructure Council as set forth in the Congressional Record dated February 28, 2024, $1,020,000,000, of whichâ

(2)
$107,333,333 shall be made available for each of year fiscal years 2026 through 2034 for construction and contingency purchases necessary to execute the design and construction as described in paragraph (1).
