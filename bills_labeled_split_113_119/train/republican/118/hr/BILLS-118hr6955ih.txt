Chief and Assistant Chief positions within SES
The positions of Chief and Assistant Chief in the United States Secret Service Uniformed Division shall be career reserved positions (as that term is defined in section 3132(a)) within the Senior Executive Service.
.
(b)
Conversion
Any individual occupying the position of Chief or Assistant Chief in the Uniformed Division of the United States Secret Service on date of enactment of this section shall be converted to the Senior Executive Service as soon as practicable after such date.
