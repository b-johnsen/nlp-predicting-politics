1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Preserving Safety Net Integrity Act of 2023
.
2.
Legal effect of final rule
(a)
In general
The final rule of the Department of Homeland Security entitled
Inadmissibility on Public Charge Grounds
(85 Fed. Reg. 41292, August 14, 2019) shall have the full force and effect of law.
(b)
Conforming change
The final rule of the Department of Homeland Security entitled
Public Charge Ground of Inadmissibility
(87 Fed. Reg. 55472, September 9, 2022) shall have no force or effect.
