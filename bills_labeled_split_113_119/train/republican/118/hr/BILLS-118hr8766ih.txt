1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
American Flags to Honor Our Veterans Act of 2024
.
2.
Cemetery flags
Section 6 of title 4, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
(h)
Notwithstanding any other provision of this section, the flag may be displayed in a secured, upright position directly adjacent to the grave site of a deceased member of the Armed Forces (as defined in section 101 of title 10) or veteran (as defined in section 101 of title 38).
.
