1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Stop Spoonfuls of Fake Sugar Act of 2024
.
2.
No food containing high-fructose corn syrup
(a)
Adulteration
Section 402 of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (
21 U.S.C. 342
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(j)
If it is food for human consumption and contains high-fructose corn syrup.
.
(b)
Delayed applicability
Section 402(j) of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act, as added by subsection (a), applies beginning on the date that is 1 year after the date of enactment of this Act.
