1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
HUD Equity Action Plan Elimination Act of 2023
.
2.
Prohibition on funding
Beginning on the date of the enactment of this Act and notwithstanding any other provision of law, no Federal funds may be obligated or expended to implement or carry out the Equity Action Plan of the Department of Housing and Urban Development.
3.
Nullification of proposed rule
