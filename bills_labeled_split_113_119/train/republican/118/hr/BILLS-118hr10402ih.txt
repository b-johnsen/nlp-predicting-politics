1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Republic of Somaliland Independence Act
.
2.
Statement of policy; authorization
(a)
Statement of policy
It is the policy of the United States that all territorial claims by the Federal Republic of Somalia over the area known as Somaliland are invalid and without merit.
(b)
Authorization
The President is authorized to recognize Somaliland of the Federal Republic of Somalia as a separate, independent country, in accordance with subsection (a).
