1.
Eugene E. Siler, Jr. United States Courthouse Annex
(a)
Designation
The United States courthouse annex located at 310 South Main Street in London, Kentucky, shall be known and designated as the
Eugene E. Siler, Jr. United States Courthouse Annex
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the United States courthouse annex referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Eugene E. Siler, Jr. United States Courthouse Annex
.
