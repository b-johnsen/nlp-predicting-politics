1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Defund Extremism Working Group Act
.
2.
Prohibition on use of funds authorized by fiscal year 2024 NDAA for Department of Defense Countering Extremism Work Group
No funds authorized to be appropriated by the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2024 may be used to fund the Department of Defense Countering Extremism Work Group.
