1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Military Families National Parks Access Enhancement Act
.
2.
Lifetime passes
Section 805(b)(2) of the Federal Lands Recreation Enhancement Act (
16 U.S.C. 6804(b)(2)
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(D)
Any individual the Secretary determines for purposes of this subparagraph is next-of-kin to a member of the Armed Forces whose death entitles a survivor to a death gratuity under section 1475 of title 10, United States Code.
.
