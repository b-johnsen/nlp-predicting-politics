1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Commission to Relocate the Federal Bureaucracy Act
.

(a)
Definitions
(1)

(2)
Commission
The term

means the Commission established under subsection (b).
(3)
Covered agency
The term

Factors
In developing the report required under paragraph (1), the Commission shall recommend the transfer of covered agencies with consideration ofâ
