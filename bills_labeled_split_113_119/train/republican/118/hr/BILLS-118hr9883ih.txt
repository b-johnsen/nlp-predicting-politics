1.
Restriction on Proposed Resource Management Plan Amendment and Final Supplemental Environmental Impact Statement for the Miles City Field Office, Montana
The Secretary of the Interior may not finalize, implement, administer, or enforce the Proposed Resource Management Plan Amendment and Final Supplemental Environmental Impact Statement for the Miles City Field Office, Montana, referred to in the notice of availability published by the Bureau of Land Management titled
Proposed Resource Management Plan Amendment and Final Supplemental Environmental Impact Statement for the Miles City Field Office, Montana
(89 Fed. Reg. 43432; published May 17, 2024).
