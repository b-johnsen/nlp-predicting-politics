1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Safety for Our Babies Act of 2024
.
2.
Lap held child restraints
Not later than 90 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Administrator of the Federal Aviation Administration shall update sections 91.107, 121.311, 125.211, and 135.128 of title 14, Code of Federal Regulations, and any such other related regulations as the Administrator determines appropriate, to remove any prohibition, restriction, or condition on an adult accompanying a child under the age of 2 on an aircraft choosing to secure such child in a lap held child restraint approved and provided by such adult during movement on the surface, takeoff, or landing.
