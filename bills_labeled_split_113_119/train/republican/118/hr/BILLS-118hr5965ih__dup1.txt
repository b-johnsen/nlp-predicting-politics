1.
Exemption of reimbursement for certain travel to return to the United States
Section 4 of the State Department Basic Authorities Act of 1956 (
22 U.S.C. 2671
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(e)
In the case of loans made after October 6, 2023, for purposes of evacuations from Israel in response to the Hamas terrorist attacks of October 2023, the reimbursement requirements under subsections (b)(2)(A)(ii) and (d) shall not apply.
.

Comptroller General report
(a)
In general
The Comptroller General of the United States shall submit to Congress a report on any commercial airfare price manipulation and instances of price gouging by airlines relating to the evacuation of United States citizens from Israel after the Hamas attacks in October 2023.
(b)
Matters To be included
