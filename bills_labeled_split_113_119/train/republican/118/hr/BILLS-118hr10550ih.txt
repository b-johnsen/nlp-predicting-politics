(b)
Rule of construction
Nothing in this Act, or the amendments made by this Act, may be construed so as to limit the scope of section 43 of the Trademark Act of 1946 (
15 U.S.C. 1125
) as such section existed prior to the enactment of this Act.
(c)
Application

(d)
Severability
If any provision of this Act, or the application thereof to any person or circumstance, is held invalid, the remainder of the Act, and the application of such provision to other persons or circumstances shall not be affected thereby.
