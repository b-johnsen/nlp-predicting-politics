1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Ko芒聙聶ko芒聙聶 Bird Initiative to Restore Bio-Diversity Semipostal Stamp Act

Ko芒聙聶ko芒聙聶 BIRDS Stamp Act
.
2.
Ko芒聙聶ko芒聙聶 Bird semipostal stamp
(a)
In general
The United States Postal Service shall issue a semipostal stamp (hereinafter in this Act referred to as the
Ko芒聙聶ko芒聙聶 Bird Semipostal Stamp
) in accordance with this section.
(b)
Terms and conditions
The issuance and sale of the Ko芒聙聶ko芒聙聶 Bird Semipostal Stamp shall be governed by the provisions of section 416 of title 39, United States Code, and regulations issued under such section.
(c)
Disposition of proceeds
(1)
In general
All amounts becoming available from the sale of the Ko芒聙聶ko芒聙聶 Bird Semipostal Stamp (as determined under section 416(d) of such title 39) shall be transferred to the Division of Aquatic and Wildlife Resources of the Guam Department of Agriculture for wildlife conservation efforts, programs for native bird rehabilitation and breeding efforts, and brown tree snake eradication, through payments which shall be made at least twice a year.
(2)
Proceeds not to be offset
In accordance with section 416(d)(4) of such title 39, amounts becoming available from the sale of the Ko芒聙聶ko芒聙聶 Bird Semipostal Stamp (as so determined) shall not be taken into account in any decision relating to the level of appropriations or other Federal funding to be furnished in any year to the United States Postal Service.
(d)
Duration
The Ko芒聙聶ko芒聙聶 Bird Semipostal Stamp shall be made available to the public for a period of not less than four years, beginning not later than 12 months after the date of the enactment of this Act.
(e)
Semipostal stamp defined
In this Act, the term
semipostal stamp
refers to a stamp described in section 416(a)(1) of title 39, United States Code.
