1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Pinniped Predator Deterrence Act
.
2.
Set aside for pinniped predator deterrence technology
(a)
In general
The Secretary shallâ
(1)
acquire technology that uses targeted acoustic sound to deter pinniped predators above and below the Bonneville Dam; and
(2)

(b)
Prohibition on certain technology
In carrying out this section, the Secretary may not acquire technology under this section that uses underwater firecrackers or any similar method of deterrence.
(c)
Authorization of appropriations
There is authorized to be appropriated to carry out this section $400,000.
(d)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)
Bonneville Dam
The term

means the Bonneville Dam, Columbia River, Oregon, as authorized by the first section of the Act of August 30, 1935 (chapter 831, 49 Stat. 1038) and the first section and section 2(a) of the Act of August 20, 1937 (
16 U.S.C. 832
, 832a(a)).
(2)
Secretary
The term
Secretary
means the Secretary of the Army, acting through the Chief of Engineers.
