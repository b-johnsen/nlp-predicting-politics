1.
Little Saigon Vietnam War Veterans Memorial Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 9317 Bolsa Avenue in Westminster, California, shall be known and designated as the
Little Saigon Vietnam War Veterans Memorial Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Little Saigon Vietnam War Veterans Memorial Post Office
.
