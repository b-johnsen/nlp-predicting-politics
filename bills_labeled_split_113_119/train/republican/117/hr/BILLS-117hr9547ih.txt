1.
Sgt. Nickolas Mueller Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 120 Doty Street in Kaukauna, Wisconsin, shall be known and designated as the
Sgt. Nickolas Mueller Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Sgt. Nickolas Mueller Post Office Building
.
