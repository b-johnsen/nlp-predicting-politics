1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Respectable Housing Act of 2022
.
2.
Inspections
(a)
Housing quality requirements
The Secretary of Housing and Urban Development shall require each owner of a housing project that contains 100 dwelling units or more for which project-based rental assistance is provided under section 8 of the United States Housing Act of 1937 (
42 U.S.C. 1437f
) to maintain the project, and assisted dwelling units in the project, in a condition that complies with standards which meet or exceed the housing quality standards established under section 6(f) of such Act (
42 U.S.C. 1437d(f)
) with respect to public housing.
(b)
Biennial inspections
The Secretary of Housing and Urban Development shall make inspections of each housing project described in subsection (a) not less frequently than biennially to determine whether the project is, and such dwelling units in the project are, maintained in accordance with the housing quality requirements applicable under subsection (a).
(c)
Inspectors
Inspections of a public housing project required under this paragraph shall be conducted by employees of the Department of Housing and Urban Development or such other inspectors as may be obtained by the Secretary.
(d)
Public availability
The Secretary shall make the results of inspections pursuant to this section publicly available online.
