Prohibition on standards subjecting workers to medical treatments
Section 6 of the Occupational Safety and Health Act of 1970 (
29 U.S.C. 655
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(g)
Nothing in this section may be construed to provide authority to require any drug (as defined in section 201(g)(1) of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act) or vaccine or other biological product (as defined in section 351(i)(1) of the Public Health Service Act) to be administered to any employee.
