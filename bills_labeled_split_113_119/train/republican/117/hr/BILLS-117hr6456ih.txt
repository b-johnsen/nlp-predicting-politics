1.
Prohibition on Federal funds for certain local educational agencies
(a)
In general
No Federal funds shall be provided to a local educational agency that requires a student at a public elementary school or secondary school under the jurisdiction of the local educational agency to wear a face covering or be vaccinated against COVID-19.
(b)
Definitions
In this section, the terms
elementary school
,
local educational agency
, and
secondary school
have the meanings given such terms in section 8101 of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965 (
20 U.S.C. 7801
).
