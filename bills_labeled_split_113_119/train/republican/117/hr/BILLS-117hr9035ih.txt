1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Farmers Deserve Notification Act
.
2.
No new restrictions of registered pesticides without notice
Section 6(a) of the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (
7 U.S.C. 136d(a)
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(3)
Notice required
(A)
In general
The Administrator shall not cancel, suspend or restrict the use of any pesticide registered under this Act without providing, through publication in the Federal Register, advance notice of such cancellation, suspension, or restriction at least 270 days before such cancellation, suspension, or restriction is to take effect.
(B)
Nonapplicability
The 270-day notification period specified in subparagraph (A) shall not be required with respect to a pesticide if a majority of the Scientific Advisory Panel established under section 25(d)(1) votes to not require such notification period with respect to such pesticide.
.
