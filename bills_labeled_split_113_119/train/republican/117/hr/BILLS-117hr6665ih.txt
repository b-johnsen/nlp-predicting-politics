1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Ukraine and Southern Border Act
or the
USB Act
.
2.
Deployment of at least equal numbers of members of the Armed Forces to Southern Border of United States as by reason of situation in Ukraine
The Secretary of Defense shall ensure that the number of members of the Armed Forces who are deployed by reason of the situation in Ukraine does not exceed the number of members of the Armed Forces who are deployed to the Southern Border of the United States.
