1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
DHS Transnational Criminal Investigative Units Supplemental Pay Act
.
2.
Stipends for certain vetted foreign members of Transnational Criminal Investigative Units
(a)
In general
The Director of U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement is authorized to provide stipends to vetted foreign members, as determined by the Director, of Transnational Criminal Investigative Units for participation in such Units by such members.
(b)
Authorization of appropriations
There is authorized to be appropriated $2,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2023 and 2024 to carry out this section.
