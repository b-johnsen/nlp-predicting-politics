1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
1031 Exchange Improvement Act
.
2.
Property identification requirement
(a)
In general
Subparagraph (A) of
section 1031(a)(3)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking
the day which
and all that follows through the comma and inserting
the date such property is received,
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by this section shall apply to exchanges completed after the date of the enactment of this Act.
