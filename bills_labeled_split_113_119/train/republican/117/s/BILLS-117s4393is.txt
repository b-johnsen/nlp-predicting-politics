(a)
In general

116.
Partial exclusion of certain interest received by individuals
(a)
Exclusion from gross income
Gross income does not include the sum of the amounts received during the taxable year by an individual as qualified interest.
(b)
Limitations
The aggregate amount excluded under subsection (a) for any taxable year shall not exceed $300 ($600 in the case of a joint return).

If the aggregate reported amount with respect to the company for any taxable year exceeds the applicable qualified interest of the company for such taxable year, a qualified interest-related dividend is the excess ofâ

(I)
