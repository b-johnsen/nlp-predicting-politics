1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Department of Defense Artificial Intelligence Metrics Act of 2021
.

Performance objectives and accompanying metrics
(1)

In carrying out subsection (a), the Secretary shall require each secretary of a military department and the head of each component of the Department shall芒聙聰

(B)
