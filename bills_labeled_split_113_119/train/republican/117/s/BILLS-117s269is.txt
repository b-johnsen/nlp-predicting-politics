1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Work Opportunity Tax Credit and Jobs Act
.
2.
Work opportunity credit made permanent
(a)
In general
Section 51(c)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking paragraphs (4) and (5).
(b)
Effective date
The amendments made by this section shall apply to individuals who begin work for the employer after December 31, 2020.
