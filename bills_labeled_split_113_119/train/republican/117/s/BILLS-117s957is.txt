1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Dispose Unused Medications and Prescription Opioids Act
or the
DUMP Opioids Act
.
2.
Requirement for certain Department of Veterans Affairs medical facilities to have physical location for the disposal of controlled substances medications
(a)
In general
The Secretary of Veterans Affairs shall ensure that each covered Department medical facility has a physical location where any individual may dispose of controlled substances medications.
(b)
Covered Department medical facility defined
In this section, the term
covered Department medical facility
means a medical facility of the Department of Veterans Affairs with an onsite pharmacy or a physical location dedicated for law enforcement purposes.
(c)
Effective date
This section shall take effect on January 1, 2022.
