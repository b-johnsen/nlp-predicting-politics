1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
State Offices of Rural Health Program Reauthorization Act of 2022
.
2.
State offices of rural health
Section 338J(i)(1) of the Public Health Service Act (
42 U.S.C. 254r(i)(1)
) is amended by striking
$12,500,000 for each of fiscal years 2018 through 2022
and inserting
$15,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2023 through 2027
.
