(c)
Effect on management
(1)
In general
Nothing in this Act or an amendment made by this Act interferes with the existing management of the segment of the Little Manatee River described in paragraph (145) of section 5(a) of the Wild and Scenic Rivers Act (
16 U.S.C. 1276(a)
).
(2)
No justification for restrictive management
The fact that the river segment described in paragraph (145) of section 5(a) of the Wild and Scenic Rivers Act (
16 U.S.C. 1276(a)
) is designated for study under that section shall not be used as justification for more restrictive management of the segment until the date on which Congress acts on the study recommendations.
