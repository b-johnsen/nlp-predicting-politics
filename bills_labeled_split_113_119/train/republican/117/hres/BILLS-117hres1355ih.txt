2d Session
H. RES. 1355
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
September 15, 2022
Mr. Gosar
submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the
Committee on Energy and Commerce
RESOLUTION
Of inquiry requesting the President and directing the Secretary of Health and Human Services to transmit, respectively, certain documents to the House of Representatives relating to ivermectin.
That the President is requested, and the Secretary of Health and Human Services is directed, to transmit, respectively, to the House of Representatives, not later than 14 days after the date of adoption of this resolution, copies of any document, record, memorandum, correspondence, or other recorded communication that refers or relates to ivermectin, to the extent that any such one or more items are within the possession of the President or the Department of Health and Human Services, respectively.
