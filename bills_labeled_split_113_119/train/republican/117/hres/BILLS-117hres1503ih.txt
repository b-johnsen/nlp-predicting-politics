1.
Limiting scope of measures appropriating funds for disaster relief
Rule XXI of the Rules of the House of Representatives is amended by adding at the end the following new clause:
13.
(a)
It shall not be in order to consider a bill, joint resolution, or conference report which appropriates or otherwise makes available funds for relief and emergency assistance in response to a major disaster if the bill, joint resolution, or conference report contains any provision which appropriates or otherwise makes available funds for any other purpose.
(b)
In this clause, the term
major disaster
has the meaning given such term in section 102(2) of the Robert T. Stafford Disaster Relief and Emergency Assistance Act (
42 U.S.C. 5122(2)
).
