3001.
Budgetary Effects
(a)
PAYGO Scorecard
The budgetary effects of this Act and the amendments made by this Act shall not be entered on either PAYGO scorecard maintained pursuant to section 4(d) of the Statutory Pay-As-You-Go Act of 2010 (
2 U.S.C. 933(d)
).
(b)
Senate PAYGO Scorecard
The budgetary effects of this Act and the amendments made by this Act shall not be entered on any PAYGO scorecard maintained for purposes of section 201 of S. Con. Res. 21 (110th Congress).
.
Passed the House of Representatives July 17, 2014.
