1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Legitimate Use of Medicinal Marijuana Act
or the
LUMMA
.
2.
Scheduling of marijuana; prescriptions
(a)
Schedule
Marijuana is moved from schedule I of the Controlled Substances Act to schedule II of such Act.

(2)
Production
No provision of the Controlled Substances Act (
21 U.S.C. 801 et seq.
) or of the Federal Food,
