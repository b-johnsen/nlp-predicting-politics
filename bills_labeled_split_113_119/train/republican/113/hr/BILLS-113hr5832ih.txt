(d)
Retroactive application
In the case of a member of the Army, Navy, Air Force, or Marine Corps who was retired under section
638a, 3911(a), 6323(a), or 8911(a) of title 10, United States Code, during
the period beginning on January 1, 2014, and ending on the date of the
enactment of this Act, the Secretary of the military department concerned
shall modify the rank in which the member was retired if necessary to
comply with the amendments made by this section.
