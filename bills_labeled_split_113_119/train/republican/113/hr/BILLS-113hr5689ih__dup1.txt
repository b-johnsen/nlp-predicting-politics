1.
Approval not required to validate land transactions
(a)
In general
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, without further approval, ratification, or
authorization by the United States, the Miami Tribe of Oklahoma may lease,
sell, convey, warrant, or otherwise transfer all or any part of its
interests in any real property that is not held in trust by the United
States for the benefit of such tribe.
