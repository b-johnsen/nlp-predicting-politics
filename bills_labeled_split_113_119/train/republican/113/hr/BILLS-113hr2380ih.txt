1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Eliminate the Misuse and Abuse of
Public Funds Act of 2013
or the
Eliminate the MAP Act
.
2.
Repeal of market
access program
(a)
Repeal of
program
Section 203 of the
Agricultural Trade Act of 1978 (
7 U.S.C. 5623
) is repealed.
(b)
Repeal of
funding
Section 211 of the
Agricultural Trade Act of 1978 (
7 U.S.C. 5641
) is amended by striking
subsection (c).
