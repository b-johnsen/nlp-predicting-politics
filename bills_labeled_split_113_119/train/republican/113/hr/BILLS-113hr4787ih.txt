1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Regional Financial Relief Act of 2014
.
2.
Distinct and less burdensome requirements for certain banking entities
Paragraph (1) of section 13(e) of the Bank Holding Company Act of 1956 (
12 U.S.C. 1851(e)(1)
) is
amended by adding at the end the following:
Such regulations shall include distinct requirements for banking entities with less than
$50,000,000,000 in trading assets and liabilities that are less burdensome
than the requirements for banking entities with $50,000,000,000 or more in
trading assets and liabilities.
