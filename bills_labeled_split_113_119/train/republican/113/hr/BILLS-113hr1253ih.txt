1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Sequester Pakistan Act
.
2.
Prohibition on foreign assistance to Pakistan
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, no foreign assistance may be provided to Pakistan until expenditures for the military tuition assistance program under sections
1784a
and
2007
of title 10, United States Code, for fiscal year 2013 are at least equal to expenditures for such tuition assistance for fiscal year 2012.
