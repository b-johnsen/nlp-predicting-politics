1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Protecting Americans from Illegal Bailouts Act of 2014
.
2.
Repeal of the risk corridor program established in PPACA
(a)
In general
Section 1342 of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act (
42 U.S.C. 18062
) is repealed.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by subsection (a) shall take effect as of the date of the enactment of this Act.
