1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Safety Through Informed Consumers Act of 2014
.
2.
Passenger motor vehicle information
Section 32302
of title 49, United States Code, is amended by inserting after subsection (b) the
following:
(c)
Crash avoidance
Not later than 1 year after the date of enactment of the Safety Through Informed Consumers Act of
2014, the Secretary shall ensure that crash avoidance information is
provided alongside of and in the same format as crashworthiness
information each place that crashworthiness information is made available
to the public under this section.
.
