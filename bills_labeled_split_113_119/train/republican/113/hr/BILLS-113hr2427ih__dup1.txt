Payment required
Out of any money in the Treasury not otherwise appropriated, the Secretary of the Treasury shall pay to Lori L. Rogers in good faith reliance on erroneous information contained in verbal and written orders to evacuate from Egypt that the United States would pay such expenses incident to her evacuation.
