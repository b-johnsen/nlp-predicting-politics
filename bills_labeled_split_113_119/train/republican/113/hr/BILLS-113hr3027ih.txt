1.
Barry M. Goldwater Post
Office
(a)
Designation
The
facility of the United States Postal Service located at 442 Miller Valley Road
in Prescott, Arizona, shall be known and designated as the
Barry M.
Goldwater Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any
reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the
United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to
be a reference to the
Barry M. Goldwater Post Office
.
