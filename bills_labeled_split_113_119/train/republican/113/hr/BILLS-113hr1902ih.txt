Payment for post-mastectomy external breast prosthesis

beneficiary prior to or after the mastectomy procedure or other breast cancer
surgical procedure. The Secretary shall develop policies to ensure appropriate
beneficiary access and utilization safeguards for such items supplied to a
beneficiary prior to the mastectomy or other breast cancer surgical
procedure.
