1.
Prohibition on payment of bonuses and pay increases for Exchange executives
Section 1311(a) of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act (
42 U.S.C. 18031(a)
) is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph:
(6)
Prohibition on payment of bonuses and pay increases for Exchange executives
The Secretary shall provide that no funds under a grant under this section may be used for a bonus or pay increase (beyond the level of compensation in effect as of October 1, 2013) for a chief executive officer, chief information officer, chief operating officer, or similar executive with respect to the operation of an Exchange on or after October 1, 2013.
