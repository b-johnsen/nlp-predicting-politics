1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Eliminating Pornography from Agencies Act
.
2.
Prohibition on accessing pornographic web sites from Federal computers
(a)
Prohibition
Except as provided in subsection (b), not later than 90 days after the date of the enactment of
this Act, the Director of the Office of Management and Budget shall issue
guidelines that prohibit the access of a pornographic or other explicit
web site from a Federal computer.
(b)
Exception
The prohibition described in subsection (a) shall not apply to any Federal computer that is used
for an investigative purpose that requires accessing a pornographic web
site.
