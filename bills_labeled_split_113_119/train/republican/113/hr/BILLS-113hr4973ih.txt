2.
Findings; Sense of Congress
(a)
Findings
Congress finds the following:
(1)
The National Center for Education Statistics found that high school students from 29 nations
performed better in math than high school students from the United States.
(2)
High school students from 22 nations performed better in science than high school students from the
United States.
