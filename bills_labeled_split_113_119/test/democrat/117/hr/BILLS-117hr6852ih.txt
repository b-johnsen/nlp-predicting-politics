1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Porch Pirates Act of 2022
.
2.
Sense of Congress
It is the sense of Congress that Congress has the authority to extend to private or commercial interstate carriers the same protections enjoyed by letter and mail carriers pursuant to its authority to regulate interstate commerce.
