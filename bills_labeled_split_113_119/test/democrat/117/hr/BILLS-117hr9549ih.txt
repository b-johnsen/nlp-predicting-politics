1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Fostering Secure Family Partnerships Act
.
2.
Increase in Federal financial support for kinship navigator programs
(a)
In general
Section 474(a)(7) of the Social Security Act (
42 U.S.C. 674(a)(7)
) is amended by striking
50
and inserting
75
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by subsection (a) shall take effect on October 1, 2023, and shall apply to payments under part E of title IV of the Social Security Act for calendar quarters beginning on or after such date.
