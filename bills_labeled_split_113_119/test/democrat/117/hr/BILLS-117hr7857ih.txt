1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Minority Fellowship Program Reauthorization Act of 2022
.
2.
Reauthorization of Minority Fellowship Program
Section 597(c) of the Public Health Service Act (
42 U.S.C. 297ll(c)
) is amended by striking
$12,669,000 for each of fiscal years 2018 through 2022
and inserting
$25,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2023 through 2027
.
