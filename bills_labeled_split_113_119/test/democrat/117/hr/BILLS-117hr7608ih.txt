1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
State Registries for Advance Directives Act of 2022
.
2.
Grants for State registries of advance directives
(a)
In general
The Secretary of Health and Human Services may award grants to States to develop, improve, or maintain, directly or by contract, a State registry of advance directives.
(b)
Authorization of appropriations
To carry out this section, there is authorized to be appropriated $5,000,000 for the period of fiscal years 2023 through 2027.
