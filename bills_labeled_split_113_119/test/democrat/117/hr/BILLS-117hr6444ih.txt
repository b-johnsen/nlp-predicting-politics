Conveyance required
Not later than 60 days after the date on which the Three Rivers Levee Improvement Authority submits a written request to the Secretary of the Air Force to proceed with the conveyance described in this section, the Secretary shall convey to the Three Rivers Levee Improvement Authority all right, title, and interest of the United States in and to the parcel of real property consisting of approximately 0.218 acres at Beale Air Force Base in California and identified as

(b)
Form of conveyance and consideration
The Secretary of the Air Force shall convey the real property described in subsection (a) by quitclaim deed and without consideration.
(c)
Map
(1)
Corrections
The Secretary of the Air Force may make clerical and typographical corrections to the map referred to in subsection (a). Such map and any revised map prepared under the authority of this paragraph shall have the same force and effect as if included as part of this Act.
(2)
Public availability
The map referred to in subsection (a) and any revised map prepared under the authority of paragraph (1) shall be available for public inspection on a website of the Department of the Air Force.
(d)
Survey
The exact acreage and legal description of the real property to be conveyed under subsection (a) shall be determined by a survey satisfactory to the Secretary of the Air Force. The cost of the survey shall be borne by the Three Rivers Levee Improvement Authority.
(e)
Additional Terms And Conditions
The conveyance under subsection (a) shall be subject to any other terms and conditions that the Secretary of the Air Force considers appropriate to protect the interests of the United States.
