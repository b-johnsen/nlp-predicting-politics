(d)
Implementation
The Secretaries of Labor, Health and Human Services, and the Treasury may implement the provisions of, including the amendments made by, this section through interim final rule, sub-regulatory guidance, program instruction, or otherwise.
(e)
Effective date
The amendments made by this section shall apply with respect to plan years beginning on or after the date that is 6 months after the date of the enactment of this Act.
