1.
Chairman Richard Milanovich Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 333 North Sunrise Way in Palm Springs, California, shall be known and designated as the
Chairman Richard Milanovich Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Chairman Richard Milanovich Post Office
.
