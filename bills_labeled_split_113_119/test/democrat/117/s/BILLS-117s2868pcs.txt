1.
Temporary extension of public debt limit
(a)
In general
Section 3101(b) of title 31, United States Code, shall not apply for the period beginning on the date of enactment of this Act and ending on December 16, 2022.
(b)
Special rule relating to obligations issued during extension period

(2)

(c)
Extension limited to necessary obligations
An obligation shall not be taken into account under subsection (b)(1) unless the issuance of such obligation was necessary to fund a commitment incurred pursuant to law by the Federal Government that required payment before December 17, 2022.
