1.
Jeanne and Jules Manford Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 7802 37th Avenue in Jackson Heights, New York, shall be known and designated as the
Jeanne and Jules Manford Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Jeanne and Jules Manford Post Office Building
.
