1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Vice Chiefs Parity Act of 2023
.
2.
Grade of Vice Chief of the National Guard Bureau
Section 10505 of title 10, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following new subsection:
(c)
Grade and Exclusion From General and Flag Officer Authorized Strength
(1)
The Vice Chief of the National Guard Bureau shall be appointed to serve in the grade of general.
(2)
The Secretary of Defense shall designate, pursuant to subsection (b) of section 526a of this title, the position of Vice Chief of the National Guard Bureau as one of the general officer and flag officer positions to be excluded from the limitations in subsection (a) of such section.
.
