1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Rural Opportunities and Revitalization Act of 2024
.
2.
Research, technical studies, demonstrations, and pilot programs to further the mission of the Rural Business-Cooperative Service
(a)
Definition of Secretary
In this section, the term
Secretary
means the Secretary of Agriculture.
(b)
Authorization
The Secretary shall carry out the following activities relating to the mission and programs of the Rural Business-Cooperative Service:
(1)
Research.
(2)
Technical studies.
(3)
Demonstrations.
(4)
Pilot programs.
(c)
Special rules
(1)
Resources
In carrying out the activities described in subsection (b), the Secretary shall promote long-term prosperity in rural areas by providing the following resources to help individuals living in rural areas start businesses, grow businesses, or find jobs:
(A)
Capital.
(B)
Training.
(C)
Education.
(D)
Entrepreneurial skills.
(E)
Economic development.
(2)
Demonstrations
In carrying out this section, the Secretary may permit demonstrations involving innovative methods of program delivery that do not meet standards, rules, regulations, or policies of the Department of Agriculture if the Secretary determines thatâ
(A)
permitting a demonstration is in the best interests of the Federal Government;
(B)
the health and safety of individuals in the area in which a demonstration is carried out will not be adversely impacted; and
(C)
deviations from the standards, rules, regulations, or policies do not compromise the core mission of the rural development mission area of promoting rural development, economic growth, and infrastructure development.
(d)
Authorization of appropriations
There is authorized to be appropriated to the Secretary to carry out this section $5,000,000 for each fiscal year.
