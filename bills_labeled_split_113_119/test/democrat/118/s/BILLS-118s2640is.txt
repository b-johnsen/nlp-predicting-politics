1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Fugitive Safe Surrender Reauthorization Act of 2023
.
2.
Fugitive safe surrender program
Section 632(c) of the Adam Walsh Child Protection and Safety Act of 2006 (
34 U.S.C. 20989(c)
) is amended by striking paragraphs (1) through (3) and inserting the following:
(1)
$10,000,000 for fiscal year 2024;
(2)
$10,000,000 for fiscal year 2025;
(3)
$10,000,000 for fiscal year 2026; and
(4)
$10,000,000 for fiscal year 2027.
.
