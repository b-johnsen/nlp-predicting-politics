JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service relating to
Importation of Fresh Beef From Paraguay
.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service relating to
Importation of Fresh Beef From Paraguay
(88 Fed. Reg. 77883 (November 14, 2023)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
