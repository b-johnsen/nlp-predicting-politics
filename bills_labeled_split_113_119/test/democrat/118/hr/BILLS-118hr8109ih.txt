1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Money Follows the Person Permanency Act of 2024
.
2.
Permanent extension of Money Follows the Person rebalancing demonstration
Section 6071(h)(1)(L) of the Deficit Reduction Act of 2005 (
42 U.S.C. 1396a
note) is amended by striking
each of fiscal years 2024 through 2027
and inserting
fiscal year 2024 and each subsequent fiscal year
.
