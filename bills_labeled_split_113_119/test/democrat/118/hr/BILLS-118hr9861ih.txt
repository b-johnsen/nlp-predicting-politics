1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
TREAT Water Act
or the
Transitioning Research to Ensure Appropriate Treatment of Water Act
.
2.
Produced water demonstration program
Section 4008 of the Energy Act of 2020 (
42 U.S.C. 10310
) is amended by redesignating subsection (d) as subsection (f) and inserting after subsection (c) the following:
(d)
Produced water demonstration program
(1)
Establishment
In carrying out this section, the Secretary shall establish a demonstration program for on-site treatment of produced water.
(2)
Prioritization
In carrying out this subsection, the Secretary shall prioritizeâ

first-of-a-kind or new approaches to treating produced water at natural gas or oil development sites; and
(B)

(3)
Consultation
In establishing the demonstration program under this subsection, the Secretary shall consult with the heads of other relevant Federal departments and agencies, including the Department of the Interior and the Environmental Protection Agency.
(e)
Produced water defined
In this section, the term
produced water
means water produced as a byproduct of drilling and fracturing oil or natural gas wells.
