The initial focus of the Federal Data Center Optimization Initiative, which was to consolidate data centers and create new efficiencies, has resulted in, since 2010芒聙聰

The need of the Federal Government for access to data and data processing systems has evolved since the date of enactment in 2014 of subtitle D of title VIII of the Carl Levin and Howard P.
Buck
McKeon National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2015.
(5)
Federal agencies and employees involved in mission critical functions increasingly need reliable access to secure, reliable, sustainable, and protected facilities to house mission critical data and data operations to meet the immediate needs of the people of the United States.
(6)
As of the date of enactment of this Act, there is a growing need for Federal agencies to use data centers and cloud applications that meet high standards for cybersecurity, resiliency, availability, and sustainability.
