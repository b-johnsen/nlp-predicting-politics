1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Passports for Purple Hearts Act
.
2.
Exemption from certain passport fees of an individual who was awarded the Purple Heart
Section 1(a) of the Act of June 1, 1920 (
22 U.S.C. 214
; commonly referred to as the
Passport Act of 1920
) is amended in the third sentence, by inserting
from an individual who was awarded the Purple Heart;
before
or from an individual or individuals abroad
.
