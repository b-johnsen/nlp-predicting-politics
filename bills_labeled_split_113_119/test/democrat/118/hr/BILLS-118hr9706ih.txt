(b)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)
Continuum of care
The term
continuum of care
means a collaborative applicant established and operating for a geographic area for purposes of the Continuum of Care Program under subtitle C of title IV of the McKinney-Vento Homeless Assistance Act (
42 U.S.C. 11381 et seq.
).
(2)
Federal agency
The term
Federal agency
has the meaning given such term
agency
in section 551 of title 5, United States Code.
(3)
Homeless individual
The term
homeless individual
has the meaning given such term in section 103 of the McKinney-Vento Homeless Assistance Act (
42 U.S.C. 11302
).
