(c)
Regulations
Not later than 6 months after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Administrator of the Small Business Administration shall issue such regulations as are necessary to implement the amendments made by subsection (a).
(d)
Effective date
Subsection (a) shall take effect on the date on which the Administrator of the Small Business Administration issues the regulations required by subsection (c).
