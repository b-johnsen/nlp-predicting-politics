Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Government Evaluation, Notification, and Explanation of Reassignments and Accountability with Leadership Act
or the
GENERAL Act
.
2.
Enhanced leadership oversight procedures
Chapter 80
of title 10, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following new section:
1568.
Notice of involuntary reassignment, separation, or retirement of general or flag officers
Not later than 15 days after a general officer or flag officer is involuntarily reassigned, separated, or retired, the Secretary of Defense shall submit to the congressional defense committees a written notification containingâ
(1)
the rationale for the reassignment, separation, or retirement, including whether the reassignment, separation, or retirement was related to conduct, performance, policy disagreements, or other factors; and
(2)
a summary of the process followed in reaching the decision to involuntarily reassign, separate, or retire the officer, including any consultations with the Secretary of the military department concerned.
