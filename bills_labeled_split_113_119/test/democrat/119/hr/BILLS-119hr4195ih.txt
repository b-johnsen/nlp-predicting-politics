(b)
Application
(1)
In general
This Act and the amendments made by this Act shall apply to individuals involuntarily or voluntarily separated from the Foreign Service during the period beginning on January 20, 2025, and ending on the date of enactment of this Act, and to individuals voluntarily separated from the Foreign Service after the date of the enactment of this Act, subject to the terms and conditions of subsection (b) of section 811 of the Foreign Service Act of 1980 (
22 U.S.C. 4051
), as added by subsection (a)(2) of this Act.
(2)
Funding
If amounts in the Foreign Service Retirement and Disability Fund are inadequate to pay benefits payable under such subsection (b), amounts in the general fund of the Treasury not otherwise appropriated may be used to pay for such benefits.
(c)
Sense of Congress
It is the sense of the Congress that any individual who receives an annuity under subsection (b) of section 811 of the Foreign Service Act of 1980 (
22 U.S.C. 4051
), as added by subsection (a)(2) of this Act, shall be eligible to retain health care benefits under the Federal Employees Health Benefits Program if such individual was enrolled in such Program on the date of separation.
