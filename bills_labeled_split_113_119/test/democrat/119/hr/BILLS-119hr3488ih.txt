1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Valve Safety Fairness Act of 2025
.
2.
Application of rule to Type A gas gathering lines
The Administrator of the Pipeline and Hazardous Materials Safety Administration shall take such actions as are necessary to apply the final rule of the Administration, titled
Pipeline Safety: Requirement of Valve Installation and Minimum Rupture Detection Standards
and published on April 8, 2022 (87 Fed. Reg. 20940), to Type A gas gathering lines.
