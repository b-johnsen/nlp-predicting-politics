1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
More Funding for COPS Act
.
2.
Reauthorization of COPS ON THE BEAT grant program
Section 1001(a)(11)(A) of the Omnibus Crime Control and Safe Streets Act of 1968 (
34 U.S.C. 10261(a)(11)(A)
) is amended by striking
$1,047,119,000 for each of fiscal years 2006 through 2009
and inserting
$1,163,032,000 for each of fiscal years 2026 through 2030
.
