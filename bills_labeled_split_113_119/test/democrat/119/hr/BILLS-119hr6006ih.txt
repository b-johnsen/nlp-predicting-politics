1.
Tuskegee Airmen Memorial Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 401 North Elm Street in Tuskegee, Alabama, shall be known and designated as the
Tuskegee Airmen Memorial Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Tuskegee Airmen Memorial Post Office
.
