JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for the reappointment of David M. Rubenstein as a citizen regent of
the Board of Regents of the Smithsonian Institution.
That, in accordance with section 5581 of the Revised Statutes (
20 U.S.C. 43
), the vacancy on the
Board of Regents of the Smithsonian Institution, in the class other than
Members of Congress, occurring by reason of the expiration of the term of
David M. Rubenstein of Maryland on May 7, 2015, is filled by the
reappointment of the incumbent. The reappointment is for a term of 6
years, beginning on May 8, 2015.
