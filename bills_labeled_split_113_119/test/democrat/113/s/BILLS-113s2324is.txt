(b)
Prohibition of certain waivers and exemptions
Subject to subsection (c), the Commission shall not approve the request of a licensee for a waiver
of, or exemption from, a covered regulation applicable to a civilian
nuclear power reactor that has permanently ceased to operate.
(c)
Limitation
Subsection (b) shall not apply to a civilian nuclear power reactor described in that subsection at
which all spent nuclear fuel has been transferred to spent nuclear fuel
dry casks.
