1.
Percentage limitation on
maximum amount of crop insurance premiums paid by Federal Crop Insurance
Corporation
Section 508(e) of
the Federal Crop Insurance Act (
7 U.S.C. 1508(e)
) is amended by adding at the
end the following new paragraph:
(7)
Additional
limitation on portion of premiums paid by Corporation
Notwithstanding any provision of this title
to the contrary, the Corporation shall not pay, under any circumstances, more
than 70 percent of the premium for any coverage or policy or plan of insurance
under this
title.
.

7 U.S.C. 8713
, 8753) are repealed.

3.
Prohibition on
payments to Brazilian Cotton Institute
The Secretary of Agriculture may not
directly, or through the Commodity Credit Corporation, make payments to the
Brazilian Cotton Institute.
