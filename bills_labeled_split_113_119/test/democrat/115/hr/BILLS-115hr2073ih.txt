1.
Limitation on immigration officer uniforms
Section 287 of the Immigration and Nationality Act (
8 U.S.C. 1357
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(i)
Immigration officers or agents of the Department of Homeland Security, including officers and agents of U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement and U.S. Customs and Border Protection, shall not wear any clothing, accessories, or other items on their person bearing the word
police
while performing duties under the immigration laws.
