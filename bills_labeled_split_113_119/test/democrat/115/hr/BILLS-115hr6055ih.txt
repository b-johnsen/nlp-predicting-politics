1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Keep Our Cities Safe Act of 2018
.
2.
Indemnification for government employees
Section 274 of the Immigration and Nationality Act (
8 U.S.C. 1324
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(f)
Exception
This section shall not apply to any officer or employee of the Federal Government, or of a State or local government, or to an officer or employee of an entity with which such a government has a contract, who is acting in accordance with the law of the State or locality in which the employee engaged in conduct which would otherwise violate this section.
.
