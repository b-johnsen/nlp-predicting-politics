1.
Exemption of Social Security, Medicare, and Medicaid from Federal balanced budget requirement
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the following programs and any outlays resulting therefrom shall be exempt from any Federal balanced budget requirement:
(1)
All Social Security benefits payable under title II of the Social Security Act.
(2)
Payments under the Medicare program under title XVIII of the Social Security Act.
(3)
Payments to States under the Medicaid program under title XIX of such Act.
