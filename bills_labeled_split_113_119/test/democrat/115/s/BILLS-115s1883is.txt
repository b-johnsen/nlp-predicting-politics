1.
Rulemaking requirement
Not later than 1 year after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Administrator of the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration and the Administrator of the Federal Railroad Administration shall complete the rulemaking process and publish a final rule regarding the advance notice of proposed rulemaking issued on March 10, 2016, entitled
Evaluation of Safety Sensitive Personnel for Moderate-to-Severe Obstructive Sleep Apnea.
(81 Fed. Reg. 12642).
