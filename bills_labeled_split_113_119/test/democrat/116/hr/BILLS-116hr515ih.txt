1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
State And Local Tax Deduction Fairness Act of 2019
.
2.
Repeal of limitation on deduction for certain State, local, and foreign taxes
(a)
In general
Section 164(b)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking paragraph (6).
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by this section shall apply to taxable years beginning after December 31, 2016.
