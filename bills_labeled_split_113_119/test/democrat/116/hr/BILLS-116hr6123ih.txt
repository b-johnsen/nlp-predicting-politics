1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Northern Mariana Islands Coronavirus Emergency Assistance Act
.
2.
Funds for CNMI government operations
(a)
Appropriations
There is hereby appropriated to the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands $100,000,000 for general government operations.
(b)
Offset
Notwithstanding section 161 of the Energy Policy and Conservation Act (
42 U.S.C. 6241
), the Secretary of Energy shall draw down and sell not to exceed $100,000,000 of crude oil from the Strategic Petroleum Reserve for fiscal year 2020.
