1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Poland Visa Waiver Act of 2019
.
2.
Poland made eligible for the Visa Waiver Program
Notwithstanding any provision of section 217 of the Immigration and Nationality Act (
8 U.S.C. 1187
), the Secretary of Homeland Security may designate Poland as a program country under the visa waiver program established by that section.
