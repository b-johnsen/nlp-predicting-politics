1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Commercial, Low Emissions And Net Energy Reduction Buildings Act
or the
CLEANER Buildings Act
.
2.
Extension of energy efficient commercial buildings deduction
(a)
In general
Section 179D(h) is amended by striking
December 31, 2017
and inserting
December 31, 2019
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by this section shall apply to property placed in service after December 31, 2017.
