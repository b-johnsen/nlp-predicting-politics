1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Wild Bird Conservation Reauthorization Act of 2019
.
2.
Reauthorization of the Wild Bird Conservation Act
Section 116 of the Wild Bird Conservation Act of 1992 (
16 U.S.C. 4915
) is amended by striking
each of the fiscal years 1993, 1994, and 1995
and inserting
fiscal years 2020 through 2025
.
