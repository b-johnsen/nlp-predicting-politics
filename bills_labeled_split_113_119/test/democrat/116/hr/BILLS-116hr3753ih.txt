1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Supporting Graduate Medical Education at Community Hospitals Act of 2019
.
2.
Findings
Congress makes the following findings:
(1)
Graduate Medical Education or
GME
training programs are of critical importance in developing the physician workforce.
(2)
Current caps on GME training programs under Medicare have been in place since 1997.
(3)
Hospitals seeking to launch GME training programs face barriers in establishing physician training programs in community hospitals.
