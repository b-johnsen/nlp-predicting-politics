1.
Holly Veterans Memorial Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 108 West Maple Street in Holly, Michigan, shall be known and designated as the
Holly Veterans Memorial Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Holly Veterans Memorial Post Office
.
