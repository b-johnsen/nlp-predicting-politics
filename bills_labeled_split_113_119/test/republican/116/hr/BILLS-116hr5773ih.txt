Short title

2.
Vehicle weight limitations-Interstate System
Section 127 of title 23, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
(v)
Vehicles hauling perishable commodities or products
(1)
In general
A State may waive the application of any vehicle weight limit established under this section with respect to a covered vehicle hauling a perishable commodity or product.
