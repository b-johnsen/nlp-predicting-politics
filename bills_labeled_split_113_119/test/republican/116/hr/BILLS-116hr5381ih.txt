1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
International Pharmaceutical Transparency Act of 2019
.
2.
Country-of-origin labeling for active ingredients in drugs
(a)
Misbranding
Section 502 of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (
21 U.S.C. 352
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(ee)
If it is a drug and its label fails to list the country of origin of each of its active ingredients.
.
(b)
Applicability
Section 502(ee) of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act, as added by subsection (a), applies beginning on the day that is 6 months after the date of enactment of this Act.
