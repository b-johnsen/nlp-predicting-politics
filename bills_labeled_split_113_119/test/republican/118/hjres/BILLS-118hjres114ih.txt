JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Federal Highway Administration relating to
National Performance Management Measures; Assessing Performance of the National Highway System, Greenhouse Gas Emissions Measure
.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Federal Highway Administration relating to
National Performance Management Measures; Assessing Performance of the National Highway System, Greenhouse Gas Emissions Measure
(88 Fed. Reg. 85364 (December 7, 2023)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
