JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to
Control of Air Pollution From New Motor Vehicles: Heavy-Duty Engine and Vehicle Standards
.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency relating to
Control of Air Pollution From New Motor Vehicles: Heavy-Duty Engine and Vehicle Standards
(88 Fed. Reg. 4296 (January 24, 2023)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
