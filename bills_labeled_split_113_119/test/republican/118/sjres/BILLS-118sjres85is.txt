JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the National Marine Fisheries Service relating to
Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Listing Endangered and Threatened Species and Designating Critical Habitat
.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the National Marine Fisheries Service relating to
Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Listing Endangered and Threatened Species and Designating Critical Habitat
(89 Fed. Reg. 24300 (April 5, 2024)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
