1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Improving Travel for Families Act
.
2.
Extension of Global Entry to certain children
(a)
In general
The Secretary of Homeland Security shall revise section 235.12 of title 8, Code of Federal Regulations, to permit a child who is 10 or under who is not a member of Global Entry in accordance with such section and who is traveling with such childâs parent or legal guardian who is a member of Global Entry in accordance with such section to accompany such parent or legal guardian through a Global Entry lane upon arrival at an international airport in the United States.
(b)
Appointments
The Secretary of Homeland Security shall take such actions as may be necessary to permit a parent or legal guardian of up to four children described in subsection (a) who is applying for membership in Global Entry on behalf of such children to schedule a single appointment for an interview relating to such membership for all such children.
