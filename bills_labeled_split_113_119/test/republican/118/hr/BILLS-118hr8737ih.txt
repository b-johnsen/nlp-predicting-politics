1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Free Market Drives Itself Act
.
2.
Abolishment of electric vehicle working group
(a)
Repeal
Section 25006 of the Infrastructure and Investment and Jobs Act (
23 U.S.C. 151
note) and the item relating to such section in section 1(b) of such Act are repealed.
(b)
Prohibition on continuation or reestablishment of working group
The Secretary of Energy and the Secretary of Transportation may not continue or reestablish the electric vehicle working group described in such section, or establish any substantially similar working group or advisory committee.
