1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Fostering Resource Efficiency in Education by Zero Employment Act
or the
FREEZE Act
.
2.
Hiring freeze and other limitations
(a)
In general
Beginning on the date of the enactment of this Actâ
(1)
no individual may be appointed to any position within the Department of Education; and
(2)
no new position may be established at the Department of Education.
(b)
Rescission of excess sums
There is permanently rescinded, from the unobligated balance of all amounts appropriated or otherwise made available for the salaries and expenses of the Department of Education as of the date of the enactment of this Act, such sums as may be in excess of the amount required to cover the salary or expenses of each person employed by the Department as of such date of enactment.
(c)
Limitation on the use of funds
No Federal funds may be obligated or expended for the salary of any position or employee in violation of subsection (a).
