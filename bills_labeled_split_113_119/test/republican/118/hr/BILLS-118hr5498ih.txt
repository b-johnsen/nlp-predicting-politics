1.
Prohibition on use of Federal funds to provide shelter or housing on certain military installations to certain aliens
(a)
In general
None of the funds authorized to be appropriated or otherwise made available for any fiscal year made be used to provide covered shelter or housing for specified aliens on military installations located in any of the 50 States or the District of Columbia.
(b)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)
The term
covered shelter or housing
means shelter or housing provided to specified aliens pursuant to an applicable Federal, State, or local law, regulation, or order.
(2)
The term
military installation
has the meaning given such term in section 2801 of title 10, United States Code.
(3)
The term
specified alien
means an alien (as defined in section 101 of the Immigration and Nationality Act (
8 U.S.C. 1101 et seq.
) who has not been admitted (as defined in such section).
