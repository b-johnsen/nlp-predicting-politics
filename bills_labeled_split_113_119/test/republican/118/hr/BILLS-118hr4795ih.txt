2.
Attempted murder and trafficking of fentanyl
Section 1111 of title 18, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
(d)
Any individual who has been found to have trafficked fentanyl shall be deemed to have attempted to perpetrate murder under subsection (a) and shall be subject to the penalty pursuant to subsection (b).
