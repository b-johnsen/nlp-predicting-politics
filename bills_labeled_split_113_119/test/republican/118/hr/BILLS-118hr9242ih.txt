1.
Abit Massey Poultry Research Center
(a)
Designation
The U.S. National Poultry Research Center of the Department of Agriculture located in Athens, Georgia, shall be known and designated as the
Abit Massey Poultry Research Center
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Abit Massey Poultry Research Center
.
