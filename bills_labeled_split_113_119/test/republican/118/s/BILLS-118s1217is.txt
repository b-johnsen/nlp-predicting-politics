(A)
In general
The safe harbor described in paragraph (3)(A) shall not apply to a rebate or other remuneration, including a rebate or other remuneration intended to influence formulary tier placement or its equivalent, from a manufacturer of prescription drugs or an entity that provides pharmacy benefits management services.

(II)
any entity that is owned, affiliated, or related under a common ownership structure with a person, business, or entity described in subclause (I).
