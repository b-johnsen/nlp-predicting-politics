1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Cutting Rampant Access to Crack Kits Act of 2023
or the
CRACK Act of 2023
.
2.
Prohibition on use of Federal funds for pipes intended to be used to smoke or inhale illegal scheduled substances
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, Federal funds made available for the treatment of substance use disorders shall not be used to purchase, procure, or distribute pipes or cylindrical objects intended to be used to smoke or inhale illegal scheduled substances.
