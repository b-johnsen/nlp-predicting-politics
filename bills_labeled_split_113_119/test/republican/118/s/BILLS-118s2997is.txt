1.
Appalachian Development Highway System
(a)
In general
Section 14501(a) of title 40, United States Code, is amended, in the second sentence, by striking
three thousand and ninety miles
and inserting
3,105 miles
.
(b)
Purpose
The increase in mileage to the Appalachian Development Highway System made by the amendment made by subsection (a) shall be for improvements to and expansion of Corridor K in North Carolina.
