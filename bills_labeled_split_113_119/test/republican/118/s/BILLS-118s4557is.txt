1.
Use of off-highway vehicles in certain areas of the Capitol Reef National Park, Utah
(a)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)
Covered road
The term
covered road
means the portions of each of Burr Trail Road, Cathedral Road, Hartnet Road, Highway 24, Notom Bullfrog Road, Polk Creek Road, Oil Ranch Road, and Baker Ranch Road that are located within the boundaries of the Capitol Reef National Park in the State.
(2)
Off-highway vehicle
The term
off-highway vehicle
has the meaning given the term in State law.
(3)
State
The term
State
means the State of Utah.
(b)
Applicable law
State law shall apply to the use of motor vehicles (including off-highway vehicles) on a covered road.
