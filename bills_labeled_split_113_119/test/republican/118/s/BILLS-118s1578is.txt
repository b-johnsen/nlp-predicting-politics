1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Accountability for Cryptocurrency in El Salvador Act
or the
ACES Act
.
2.
Reports on adoption of cryptocurrency as legal tender in El Salvador
(a)
In general
Not later than 120 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Secretary of State and the Secretary of the Treasury shall jointly submit to the appropriate committees of Congress a report on the adoption by the Government of El Salvador of a cryptocurrency as legal tender.
(b)
Elements
The report required by subsection (a) shall include the following:

A description of the process followed by the Government of El Salvador to develop and enact the Bitcoin Law (Legislative Decree No. 57, Official Record No. 110, Volume 431, enacted June 9, 2021), which provides the cryptocurrency, Bitcoin, with legal tender status in El Salvador.
