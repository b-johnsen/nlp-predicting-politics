1.
First Lieutenant Thomas Michael Martin Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 410 Dakota Avenue South in Huron, South Dakota, shall be known and designated as the
First Lieutenant Thomas Michael Martin Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
First Lieutenant Thomas Michael Martin Post Office Building
.
