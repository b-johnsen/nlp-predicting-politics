(b)
Updates to certain technology systems
Not later than two years after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Secretary of Veterans Affairs shall complete such updates and modifications to technology systems of the Department of Veterans Affairs as the Secretary considers appropriate to carry out subsection (k) of section 111 of such title, as added by subsection (a) of this section.
