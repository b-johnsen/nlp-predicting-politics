2.
Enrolled

(a)
In

Chapter 77
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is
amended by adding at the end the following new section:
7529.
Enrolled
agents
(a)
In
general
The Secretary may prescribe such regulations as may be
necessary to regulate the conduct of enrolled agents in regards to their
practice before the Internal Revenue Service.
(b)
Use of
credentials
Any enrolled agents properly licensed to practice as
required under rules promulgated under subsection (a) shall be allowed to use
the credentials or designation as
enrolled agent
,
EA
, or
E.A.
.
