1.
Sergeant William Moody Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 232 Southwest Johnson Avenue in Burleson, Texas, shall be known and designated as the
Sergeant William Moody Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Sergeant William Moody Post Office Building
.
