1.
Moratorium
Before the end of the 6-month period beginning on the date of the enactment of this Act, the
Financial Stability Oversight Council may not make a determination under
section 113 of the Financial Stability Act of 2010 (
12 U.S.C. 5323
) that a
nonbank financial company (as defined under section 102 of such Act) shall
be supervised by the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System and
subject to prudential standards.
