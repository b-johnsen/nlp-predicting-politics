(b)
Penalty
Section 409(a) of such Act (
42 U.S.C. 609(a)
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(17)
Failure of state to deny assistance to parent, caretaker relative, or legal guardian of child not attending enough school
The Secretary shall reduce the grant payable to a State under section 403(a)(1) for a fiscal year by the amount of any assistance paid by the State in violation of section 408(a)(13) in the preceding fiscal year.

(c)
Effective date
The amendments made by this section shall take effect on October 1, 2015, and shall apply to attendance in school years beginning on or after August 1, 2014.
