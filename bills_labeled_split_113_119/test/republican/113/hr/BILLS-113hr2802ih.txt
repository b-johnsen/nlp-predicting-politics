1.
Fountain County Veterans
Memorial Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal
Service located at 418 Liberty Street in Covington, Indiana, shall be known and
designated as the
Fountain County Veterans Memorial Post
Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation,
document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred
to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Fountain
County Veterans Memorial Post Office
.
