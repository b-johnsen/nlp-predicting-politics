1.
Commissary and exchange store
privileges for involuntarily separated members with a service-connected
disability and for their dependents
(a)
Extension of
privileges
Section 1146 of
title 10, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following new
subsection:
(d)
Members
involuntarily separated with service-Connected disability;
dependents
(1)
A member of the armed forces who is
involuntarily separated from active duty or the Selected Reserve and who has a
service-connected disability shall be permitted to use commissary and exchange
stores on the same basis as a member of the armed forces entitled to retired or
retainer pay.
(2)
A dependent of a member described in
paragraph (1) shall be permitted to use commissary and exchange stores on the
same basis as a dependent of a member of the armed forces entitled to retired
or retainer
pay.
.
(b)
Implementation
The
Secretary of Defense shall ensure that subsection (d) of section 1146 of title
10, United States Code, as added by subsection (a), is fully operational before
the end of the 90-day period beginning on the date of the enactment of this
Act.
