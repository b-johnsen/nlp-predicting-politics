(a)
Management plan revision
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the Secretary shall revise the management plan for the
conservation pool in Lake Texoma to ensure thatâ

The Secretary shall make the revisions required under subsection (a) not later than 60 days after
the date of enactment of this Act.
(c)

In this section, the term
Secretary
means the Secretary of the Army (acting through the Chief of Engineers).
