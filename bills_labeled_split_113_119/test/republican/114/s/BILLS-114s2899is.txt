Mosquito control waiver
(1)
In general
The Administrator shall waive the permit requirement under this section for 180 days for the purpose of public health pesticide applications of a mosquito control program.
(2)
Existing permits
In the case of a permit issued by the Administrator before the date of enactment of the
Zika Response and Regulatory Relief Act
for the purpose of public health pesticide applications of a mosquito control program, the Administrator shall waive the reporting requirements prescribed under subsection (a)(2) during the 180-day period beginning on the date of enactment of the
Zika Response and Regulatory Relief Act

(3)
Extension of authority
The Administrator may extend the period during which permit and reporting requirements may be waived under paragraphs (1) and (2), respectively, for not more than an additional 180 days beyond the 180-day periods referred to in those paragraphs, as determined necessary by the Administrator to protect the public health.
