1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Block Arms to Iran Act of 2015
.
2.
Limitation on funding for United Nations if arms embargo on Iran is lifted
Of the amounts appropriated or otherwise made available for the Department of State for
Contributions to International Organizations
and available for contributions to the United Nations, 20 percent may not be obligated or expended for such purpose if the arms embargo on Iran pursuant to paragraphs 5 and 6 of United Nations Security Council Resolution 1747 (2007) and paragraph 8 of United Nations Security Council Resolution 1929 (2010) is lifted, reduced, or otherwise eased, notwithstanding paragraph 13 of United Nations Security Council Resolution 1747 (2007) and paragraph 37 of United Nations Security Council 1929 (2010).
