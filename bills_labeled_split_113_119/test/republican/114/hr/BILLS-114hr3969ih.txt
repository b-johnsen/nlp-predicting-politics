1.
Master Chief Petty Officer Jesse Dean Department of Veterans Affairs Community-Based Outpatient
Clinic
(a)
Designation
The Department of Veterans Affairs community-based outpatient clinic in Laughlin, Nevada, shall after the date of the enactment of this Act be known and designated as the
Master Chief Petty Officer Jesse Dean Department of Veterans Affairs Community-Based Outpatient Clinic
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the community-based outpatient clinic referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Master Chief Petty Officer Jesse Dean Department of Veterans Affairs Community-Based Outpatient Clinic
.
