1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Keeping Oil and Natural Gas Flowing for Consumers Act
.
2.
Prohibition against EPA regulation of methane emissions from oil and natural gas source category
(a)
In general
The Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency may not propose, finalize, implement, or enforce any prohibition or restriction under the Clean Air Act (
42 U.S.C. 7401 et seq.
) with respect to the emission of methane from the oil and natural gas source category.
