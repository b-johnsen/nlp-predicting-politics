1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Protect Our Devices Act of 2016
.
2.
Prohibition of requiring the breaking of encryption of wire or oral communications
No officer or entity of the Federal Government shall have any authority to require any person to assist in devising a method for breaking the encryption of a wire or oral communication as such term are defined for the purposes of
chapter 119
of title 18, United States Code.
