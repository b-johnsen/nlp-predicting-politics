1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Congressional Pay Reduction Act
.
2.
Reduction in rates of pay and elimination of future cost-of-living adjustments for members of
congress
Section 601(a) of the Legislative Reorganization Act of 1946 (
2 U.S.C. 4501
) is amended by amending
paragraph (2) to read as follows:
(2)
Effective with respect to pay periods beginning after the date of the regularly scheduled general
election for Federal office held in November 2016, the annual rate of pay
for each of the positions described in paragraph (1) shall be equal to 95
percent of the annual rate of pay for such position for the most recent
pay period preceding such election.
.
