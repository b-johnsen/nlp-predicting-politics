1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Hold Top Officials Accountable Act
.
2.
Official mishandling of classified information
(a)
In general
Chapter 93
of title 18, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
1925.
Official mishandling of classified information
(a)
Offense
Whoever, being a highly placed official of the United States, mishandles classified information shall be fined under this title and imprisoned for not less than 2 nor more than 10 years.
