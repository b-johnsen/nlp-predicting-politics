1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Iran-Russia Cooperation Reporting Act
.
2.
Department of Defense report on cooperation between Iran and the Russian Federation
(a)
Report required
The Secretary of Defense shall submit to Congress a report on cooperation between Iran and the Russian Federation and how and to what extent such cooperation affects United States national security and strategic interests.

(c)
Submission period
The report required by subsection (a) shall be submitted not later than 120 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, and annually thereafter, for such period of time as the Joint Comprehensive Plan of Act remains in effect.
(d)
Form
The report required by subsection (a) shall be submitted in unclassified form, but may contain a classified annex.
