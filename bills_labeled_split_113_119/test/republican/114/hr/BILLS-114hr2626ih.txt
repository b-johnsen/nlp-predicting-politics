1.
Indian tribal governments permitted to be shareholders of S corporations
(a)
In general
Section 1361(b)(1)(B)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking
or an organization described in subsection (c)(6)
and inserting
an organization described in subsection (c)(6), or an Indian tribal government (as defined in section 7701(a)(40) and including any subdivision of such government and any agency or instrumentality of such government or subdivision)
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendments made by this section shall apply to taxable years ending after the date of the enactment of this Act.
