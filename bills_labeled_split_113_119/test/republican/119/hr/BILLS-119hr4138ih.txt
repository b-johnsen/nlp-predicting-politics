This Act may be cited as the

2.
Prohibition on asking about gender identity on any executive agency form or survey
(a)
Prohibition
With respect to any collection of information conducted by or for an executive agency through a form or survey, the head of such agencyâ
(1)

(A)

(b)

Not later than 180 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Director of the Office of Management and Budget shall issue guidance to the heads of covered agencies on the implementation of the provisions of this section.

Female
The term

(4)
Gender identity
The term

(5)
Male
The term

Sex
The term
