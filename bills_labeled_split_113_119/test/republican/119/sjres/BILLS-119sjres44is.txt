JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Department of Energy relating to
Energy Conservation Program: Energy Conservation Standards for Commercial Refrigerators, Freezers, and Refrigerator-Freezers
.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Department of Energy relating to
Energy Conservation Program: Energy Conservation Standards for Commercial Refrigerators, Freezers, and Refrigerator-Freezers
(90 Fed. Reg. 7464 (January 21, 2025)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
