(a)
Designation
In order to recognize and ensure the continued preservation and importance of the history of the United States involvement in World War II, each calendar year the Secretary of the Interior shall designate not less than one city located in one of the several States or a territory of the United States as an
American World War II Heritage City
. Not more than one city in each State or territory may be designated under this section.

The Secretary, in consultation with the Secretary of the Smithsonian Institution or the President of the National Trust for Historic Preservation, shall make each designation under subsection (a) based on the following criteria:
