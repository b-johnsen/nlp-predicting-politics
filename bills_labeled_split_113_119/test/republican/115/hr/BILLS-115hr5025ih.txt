16 U.S.C. 6906

(c)
Limitation on enforcement based on observer reports
No person fishing on a United States flag fishing vessel in the Convention Area shall be subject to any penalty under this Act or any other law or regulation applicable to such vessels based on a report by an observer on such a vessel, unlessâ
(1)
the observer report is provided to the captain and the owner of the vessel within 60 days after the end of the fishing trip; and
(2)
any enforcement action based on such report is brought within one year after the end of the fishing trip.
