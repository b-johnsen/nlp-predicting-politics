1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Social Media Screening for Terrorists Act of 2017
.
2.
Requirement to search records of aliens applying for admission to the United States
The Secretary of Homeland Security shall search all public records, including Internet sites and social media profiles, to determine if an alien applying for admission to the United States is inadmissible under section 212 of the Immigration and Nationality Act (
8 U.S.C. 1182
).
