1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Ensuring Regulations are Successful or Ended Act of 2017
or the
ERASE Act of 2017
.
2.
Sunset for rules
(a)
In general
Except as provided in subsection (b), any rule (as such term is defined in section 551 of title 5, United States Code) that is finalized after the date of the enactment of this Act shall sunset on the date that is one year after the date on which the rule is finalized.
(b)
Congressional approval
The sunset under subsection (a) shall not apply to a rule described in that subsection if a joint resolution is enacted which provides for the continuance of that rule.
