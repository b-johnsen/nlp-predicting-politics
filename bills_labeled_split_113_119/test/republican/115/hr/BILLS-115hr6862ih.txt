1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Congressional Award Program Reauthorization Act of 2018
.
2.
Reauthorization
Section 108 of the Congressional Award Act (
2 U.S.C. 808
) is amended by striking
October 1, 2018
and inserting
October 1, 2023
.
3.
Effective Date
This Act shall take effect as of October 1, 2018.
