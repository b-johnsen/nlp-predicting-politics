Coal-Powered electric generation unit
For purposes of this section, the term
coal-powered electric generation unit
means an electric generation unit (as defined in section 48A(c)(6)) that uses coal to produce not less than 75 percent of the electricity produced by such unit.
(c)
Qualified expenses
For purposes of this section, the term
qualified expenses
means amounts paid or incurred for the operation or maintenance of a coal-powered electric generation unit, other than amounts paid or incurred for coal.

This section shall apply to taxable years beginning after December 31, 2017, and ending before January 1, 2023.
