1.
Spc. Sterling William Wyatt Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 511 East Walnut Street in Columbia, Missouri, shall be known and designated as the
Spc. Sterling William Wyatt Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Spc. Sterling William Wyatt Post Office Building
.
