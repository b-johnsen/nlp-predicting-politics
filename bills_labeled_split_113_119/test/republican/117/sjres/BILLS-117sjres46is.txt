JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Department of Justice and the Department of Homeland Security relating to
Procedures for Credible Fear Screening and Consideration of Asylum, Withholding of Removal, and CAT Protection Claims by Asylum Officers
.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Department of Justice and the Department of Homeland Security relating to
Procedures for Credible Fear Screening and Consideration of Asylum, Withholding of Removal, and CAT Protection Claims by Asylum Officers
(87 Fed. Reg. 18078 (March 29, 2022)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
