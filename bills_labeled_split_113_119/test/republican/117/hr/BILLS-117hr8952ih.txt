(1)
In general
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, in the case that the Secretary of Homeland Security finds that the entry of any alien or any class of aliens would be detrimental to the interests of the United States, the Secretary may, after submitting a notice in accordance with paragraph (2), suspend the entry of all aliens or any class of aliens as immigrants or nonimmigrants for not more than 60 days.
