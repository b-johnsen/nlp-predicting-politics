1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Relief for our Truckers Act
.
2.
Repeal of tax on diesel fuel
(a)
In general
Section 4041(a)(1)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is hereby repealed.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by this section shall take effect on the date of the enactment of this Act.
