1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Isolate Russian Government Officials Act of 2022
.
2.
Exclusion of government officials of the Russian Federation
(a)
Policy of the United States
It is the policy of the United States to exclude government officials of the Russian Federation, to the maximum extent practicable, from attendance at the following:
(1)
The Spring Meetings and Annual Meetings of the World Bank Group and the International Monetary Fund.
(2)
Proceedings of the Group of 20, including Ministerial Meetings and Leader Summits.
(3)
Plenary meetings of the Financial Action Task Force.
(4)
Annual General Meetings of the Bank for International Settlements.
(b)
Implementation
The Secretary of the Treasury and the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System, as the case may be, shall take all necessary steps to advance the policy described under subsection (a).

Termination
