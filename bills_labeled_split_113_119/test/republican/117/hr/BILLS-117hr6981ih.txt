Short title

.
2.
Waiver of export criteria and transportation and related costs for transportation of certain ammunition and ordnance to Ukraine
(a)
In general
In the case of ammunition or ordnance with Export Classification Number 0A501 or Category 3 on the U.S. munitions list that is to be delivered to a confirmed military logistical supply entity providing ammunition to Ukraine, as designated by the Secretary of Defense, the President mayâ
(1)
provide for the transportation of the ammunition or ordnance without charge to Ukraine for the costs of such transportation; and
(2)
waive any applicable export criteria of the Department of State or the Department of Commerce to expedite the delivery of the ammunition or ordnance.
(b)
Termination
The authority under subsection (a) shall terminate when the armed conflict in Ukraine ends.
