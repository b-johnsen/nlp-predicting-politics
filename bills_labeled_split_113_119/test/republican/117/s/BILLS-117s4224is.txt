1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Make Border Security a Priority Act of 2022
.
2.
Requirement to expend equal amount on border security as on Ukraine Security Assistance Initiative
During fiscal years 2022 and 2023, the Secretary of Homeland Security shall expend on efforts to enhance security along the southern land border of the United States an amount that is equal to the amount appropriated to the Secretary of Defense for the Ukraine Security Assistance Initiative for that fiscal year.
