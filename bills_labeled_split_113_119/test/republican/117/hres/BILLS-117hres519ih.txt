1.
Legislative branch select commission budget outlays
(a)
In general
Clause 2 of rule II of the Rules of the House of Representatives is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph:
(l)
The Clerk shall make information received under clause 6(f) of rule X with respect to the budget outlays of a select commission established in the legislative branch available to the public on a Government website in a searchable, sortable, and downloadable format as soon as possible after it is received.
.
