In the House of Representatives, U. S.,
December 7, 2021
RESOLUTION
Expressing the profound sorrow of the House of Representatives on the death of the Honorable Robert Joseph Dole.
That the House has heard with profound sorrow of the death of the Honorable Robert Joseph Dole, a Senator from the State of Kansas.
That the Clerk communicate these resolutions to the Senate and transmit a copy thereof to the family of the deceased.
That when the House adjourns today, it adjourn as a further mark of respect to the memory of the deceased Senator.
Cheryl L. Johnson,
Clerk.
