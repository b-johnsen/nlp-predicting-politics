Alaska

Under authority of the act of March 12, 1914 (38 Stat. 305), it is hereby ordered that lots 1, 2, and 3, block 12, of the Federal Addition to the town-site of Seward, Alaska, containing 5,000 square feet each, according to the plat thereof approved May 22, 1916, be, and the same are hereby, reserved and set aside for the use of the Department of Agriculture.

This order shall continue in full force and effect unless and until revoked by the President or by act of Congress.
