By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the provisions of paragraph Eighth of subdivision SECOND of section 2 of the Civil Service Act of January 16, 1883 (22 Stat. 403, 404), it is hereby ordered that Mrs. Hilda McTeer may be appointed to a position in the classified service of the Government without compliance with the requirements of the civil-service rules.

Mrs. McTeer is the widow of John H. McTeer, who had long and meritorious service in the Treasury Department, and who died on July 18, 1935, leaving his widow and a minor child without adequate means of support. Mrs. McTeer has also served a number of years under temporary appointments in the Treasury Department and o their branches of the Government service.

This order is recommended by the Secretary of the Treasury.
