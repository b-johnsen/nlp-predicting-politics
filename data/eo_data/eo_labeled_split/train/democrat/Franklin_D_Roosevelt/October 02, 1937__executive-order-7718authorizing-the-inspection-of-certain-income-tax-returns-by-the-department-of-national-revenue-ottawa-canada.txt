By virtue of the authority vested in me by section 55 (a) of the Revenue Act of 1936 (ch. 690, 49 Stat. 1648, 1671), it is hereby ordered that income tax withholding returns, Form 1042B, filed for the calendar year 1937 and each subsequent calendar year, shall be open to inspection by the Department of National Revenue, Ottawa, Canada, for the purpose of enabling the United States Treasury Department to administer effectively the provisions of the Tax Convention, dated December 30, 1936, between the United States and Canada which was ratified August 13, 1937. Such inspection shall be in accordance with the rules and regulations prescribed by the Secretary of the Treasury in the Treasury Decision relating to the inspection of returns by the Department of National Revenue, Ottawa, Canada, approved by me this date.
