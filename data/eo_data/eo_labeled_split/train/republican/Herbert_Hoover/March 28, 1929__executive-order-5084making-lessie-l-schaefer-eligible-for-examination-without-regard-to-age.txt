The maximum age limit of fifty years for admission to the general clerical examination in the Eleventh United States Civil Service District which closes April 5, 1929, is hereby waived to permit the examination of Mrs. Lessie L. Schaefer of Pendleton, Oregon.

It is stated that Mrs. Schaefer is in good health, brilliant, thoroughly fitted and experienced in clerical work, and that it is necessary that she be self-supporting.
