Policy

. Faith-based and other community-serving organizations are vital to our  's ability to address the needs of, and lift up, low-income and other underserved persons and communities, notably including persons of color. The   people are key drivers of fundamental change in our country, and few institutions are closer to the people than our faith-based and other community organizations. It is important that   strengthen the ability of such organizations and other nonprofit providers in our communities to deliver services effectively in partnership with  , and local governments and with other private organizations, while preserving our fundamental constitutional commitments guaranteeing the equal protection of the laws and the free exercise of religion and forbidding the establishment of religion.   can preserve these fundamental commitments while empowering faith-based and secular organizations to assist in the delivery of vital services in our neighborhoods. These partnerships are also vital for the success and effectiveness of   diplomatic, international development, and humanitarian work around the world.
