Executive Order 3714—Civil Service Preference to War Veterans
August 01, 1922
Warren G. Harding

The executive order of October 14, 1921, granting certain preference in connection with presidential postmaster examinations to persons who served in the World War, is hereby amended to extend the same preference to honorably discharged veterans of the Spanish-American War.

WARREN G. HARDING

The White House, August 1, 1922.
