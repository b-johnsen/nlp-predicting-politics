Executive Order 7772—Revocation of Executive Order No. 5711 of September 14, 1931, Withdrawing Land for Classification and in Aid of Legislation
September 14, 1931
Franklin D. Roosevelt

MONTANA

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of June 25, 1910, ch. 421, 36 Stat. 847, as amended by the act of August 24, 1912, ch. 369, 37 Stat. 497, Executive Order No. 5711 of September 14, 1931, temporarily withdrawing and reserving certain land for classification and in aid of legislation, is hereby revoked.

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

The White House,

December 14, 1937.
