Executive Order 8765—Authorizing the Appointment of Alexander M. Knauss to the Position of Junior Historical Aide at the Vanderbilt Mansion National Historic Site, National Park Service, Department of the Interior, Without Regard to the Civil Service Rules
June 03, 1941
Franklin D. Roosevelt

By virtue of the authority vested in me by the provisions of paragraph Eighth of subdivision SECOND of section 2 of the Civil Service Act (22 Stat. 403, 404), it is hereby ordered that Alexander M. Knauss may be appointed to the position of Junior Historical Aide at the Vanderbilt Mansion National Historic Site, National Park Service, Department of the Interior, without compliance with the requirements of the Civil Service Rules.

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

The White House,

June 3, 1941.
