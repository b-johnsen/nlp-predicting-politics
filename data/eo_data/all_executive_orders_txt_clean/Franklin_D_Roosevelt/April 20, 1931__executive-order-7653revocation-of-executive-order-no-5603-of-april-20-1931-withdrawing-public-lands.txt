Executive Order 7653—Revocation of Executive Order No. 5603 of April 20, 1931, Withdrawing Public Lands
April 20, 1931
Franklin D. Roosevelt

WYOMING

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of June 25, 1910, ch. 421, 36 Stat. 847, as amended by the act of August 24, 1912, ch. 369, 37 Stat. 497, Executive Order No. 5603 of April 20, 1931, withdrawing public lands in Wyoming, pending resurvey, is hereby revoked.

This order shall become effective upon the date of the official filing of the plats of the resurvey of the lands now subject to said Executive Order No. 5603.

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

The White House,

July 9, 1937.
