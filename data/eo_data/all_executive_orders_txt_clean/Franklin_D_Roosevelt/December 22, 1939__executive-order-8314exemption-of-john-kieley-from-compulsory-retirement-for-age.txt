Executive Order 8314—Exemption of John Kieley From Compulsory Retirement for Age
December 22, 1939
Franklin D. Roosevelt

Whereas

, in my judgment, the public interest requires that John Kieley, Assistant to the Secretary of the Treasury, who, during the current month, will reach the retirement age prescribed for automatic separation from the service, applicable to him, be exempted from compulsory retirement for age for a period of one year:

Now, Therefore

, by virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by section 204 of the act of June 30, 1932, 47 Stat. 382, 404 (U.S.C., title 5, sec. 715a), I hereby exempt the said John Kieley from compulsory retirement for age for a period of one year ending December 31, 1940.

FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT

The White House,

December 22, 1939.
