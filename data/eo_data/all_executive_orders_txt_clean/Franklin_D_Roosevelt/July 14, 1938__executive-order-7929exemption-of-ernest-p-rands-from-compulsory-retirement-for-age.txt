Executive Order 7929—Exemption of Ernest P. Rands From Compulsory Retirement for Age
July 14, 1938
Franklin D. Roosevelt

Whereas

, in my judgment, the public interest requires that Ernest P. Rands, technical adviser, General Land Office, Department of the Interior, who during the current month, will reach the retirement age prescribed for automatic separation from the service, applicable to him, be exempted from compulsory retirement for age for a period of one year:

Now, Therefore

, by virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by section 204 of the act of June 30, 1932, 47 Stat. 382, 404 (U.S.C., title 5, sec. 715a), I hereby exempt the said Ernest P. Rands from compulsory retirement for age for a period of one year ending July 31, 1939.

FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT

The White House,

July 14, 1938.
