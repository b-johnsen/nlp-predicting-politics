Executive Order 8241—Revocation of Executive Order No. 6774 of June 30, 1934, Withdrawing Public Lands
June 30, 1934
Franklin D. Roosevelt

WASHINGTON

By virtue of the authority vested in me by the act of June 25, 1910, ch. 421, 36 Stat. 847, Executive Order No. 6774 of June 30, 1934, withdrawing public lands in Washington pending a resurvey, is hereby revoked.

This order shall become effective upon the date of the official filing of the plat of the resurvey of the lands involved.

FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT

The White House,

September 6, 1939.
