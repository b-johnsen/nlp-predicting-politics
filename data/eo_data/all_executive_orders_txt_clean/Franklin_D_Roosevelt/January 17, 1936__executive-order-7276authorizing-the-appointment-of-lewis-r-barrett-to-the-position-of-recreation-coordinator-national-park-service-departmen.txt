Executive Order 7276—Authorizing the Appointment of Lewis R. Barrett to the Position of Recreation Coordinator, National Park Service, Department of the Interior, Without Regard to Civil-Service Rules
January 17, 1936
Franklin D. Roosevelt

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the provisions of paragraph Eighth of subdivision SECOND of section 2 of the Civil Service Act of January 16, 1883 (22 Stat. 403, 404), it is hereby ordered that Lewis R. Barrett may be appointed to the position of Recreation Coordinator in the National Capital Parks, National Park Service, Department of the Interior, without compliance with the requirements of the civil-service rules and without being accorded a classified status.

FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT

The White House,

January 17, 1936.
