Executive Order 7426—Designating the Vice Chairman of the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System
August 06, 1936
Franklin D. Roosevelt

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by section 10 of the Federal Reserve Act (38 Stat. 260) as amended by section 203(a) of the Act of August 23, 1935 (49 Stat. 704), I hereby designate Ronald Ransom as Vice Chairman of the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System, to serve as such for a term of four years.

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

THE WHITE HOUSE,

August 6, 1936.
