Executive Order 7236—Charlotte, North Carolina, Little Rock, Arkansas, and Dubuque, Iowa, Abolished as Customs Ports of Entry
November 27, 1935
Franklin D. Roosevelt

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of August 1, 1914, 38 Stat. 609, 623 (19 U.S.C., sec. 2), the following customs ports of entry in the customs collection districts named are hereby abolished effective 30 days from the date of this order:

Charlotte, North Carolina, customs collection district no. 15 (North Carolina)

Little Rock, Arkansas, customs collection district no. 43 (Tennessee)

Dubuque, Iowa, customs collection district no. 44 (Iowa)

FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT

The White House,

November 27, 1935.
