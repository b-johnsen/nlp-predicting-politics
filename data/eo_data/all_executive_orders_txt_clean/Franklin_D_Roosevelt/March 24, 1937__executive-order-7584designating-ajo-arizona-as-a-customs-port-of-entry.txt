Executive Order 7584—Designating Ajo, Arizona, as a Customs Port of Entry
March 24, 1937
Franklin D. Roosevelt

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of August 1, 1914, 38 Stat. 609, 623 (U.S.C., title 19, sec. 2), I hereby designate the customs station of Ajo, Arizona, a customs port of entry in Customs Collection District No. 26 (Arizona), with headquarters at Nogales, Arizona, effective thirty days from the date of this order.

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

The White House,

March 24, 1937.
