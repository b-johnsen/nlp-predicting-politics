Executive Order 8777—Authorizing the Appointment of Dr. Harold C. Bryant as Superintendent, Grand Canyon National Park, Arizona, National Park Service, Department of the Interior, Without Regard to Civil Service Rules
June 11, 1941
Franklin D. Roosevelt

By virtue of the authority vested in me by the provisions of paragraph Eighth of subdivision SECOND of section 2 of the Civil Service Act (22 Stat. 403, 404), it is hereby ordered that Dr. Harold C. Bryant may be appointed to the classified position of Superintendent of Grand Canyon National Park, Arizona, in the National Park Service, Department of the Interior, without compliance with the requirements of the Civil Service Rules.

This order is recommended by the Secretary of the Interior.

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

The White House,

June 11, 1941.
