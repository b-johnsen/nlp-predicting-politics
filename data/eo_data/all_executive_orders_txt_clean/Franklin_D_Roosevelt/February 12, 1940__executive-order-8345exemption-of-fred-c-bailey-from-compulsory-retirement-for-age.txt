Executive Order 8345—Exemption of Fred C. Bailey From Compulsory Retirement for Age
February 12, 1940
Franklin D. Roosevelt

Whereas

, in my judgment, the public interest requires that Fred C. Bailey, Fleet Manager, Maintenance and Repair Division, United States Maritime Commission, who was exempted from compulsory retirement for age for a period of one year ending March 31, 1940, by Executive Order No. 8060 of March 7, 1939, be further exempted from such compulsory retirement for a period of one year:

Now, Therefore

, by virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by section 204 of the act of June 30, 1932, 47 Stat. 382, 404 (U.S.C., title 5, sec. 715a), I hereby further exempt the said Fred C. Bailey from compulsory retirement for age for a period of one year ending March 31, 1941.

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

The White House,

February 12, 1940.
