Executive Order 8823—Providing for an Additional Member of the Committee on Fair Employment Practice in the Office of Production Management, Established by Section 3 of Executive Order No. 8802 of June 25, 1941
June 25, 1941
Franklin D. Roosevelt

By virtue of the authority vested in me by the Constitution and the statutes, section 3 of Executive Order No. 8802 of June 25, 1941 establishing in the Office of Production Management a Committee on Fair Employment Practice consisting of a chairman and four other members, is hereby amended to provide that the Committee shall consist of a chairman and five other members.

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

The White House,

July 18, 1941.
