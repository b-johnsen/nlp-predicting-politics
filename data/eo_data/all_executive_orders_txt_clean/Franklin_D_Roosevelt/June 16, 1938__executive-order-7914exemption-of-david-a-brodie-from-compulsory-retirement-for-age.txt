Executive Order 7914—Exemption of David A. Brodie From Compulsory Retirement for Age
June 16, 1938
Franklin D. Roosevelt

Whereas

, in my judgment, the public interest requires that David A. Brodie, farm superintendent, St. Elizabeths Hospital, Department of the Interior, who during the month of July, 1938, will reach the retirement age prescribed for automatic separation from the service, applicable to him, be exempted from compulsory retirement for age for a period of one year:

Now, Therefore

, by virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by section 204 of the act of June 30, 1932, 47 Stat. 382, 404 (U.S.C., title 5, sec. 715a), I hereby exempt the said David A. Brodie from compulsory retirement for age for a period of one year ending July 31, 1939.

FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT

The White House,

June 16, 1938.
