Executive Order 7284—Revocation in Past of Executive Order No. 6076, of March 15, 1933, Withdrawing Public Lands, New Mexico
March 15, 1933
Franklin D. Roosevelt

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of June 25, 1910, ch. 421, 36 Stat. 847, as amended by the act of August 24, 1912, ch. 369, 37 Stat. 497, Executive Order No. 6076, of March 15, 1933, withdrawing, together with other lands, public lands in T. 4 N., R. 18 W. of the New Mexico principal meridian, New Mexico, pending resurvey, is hereby revoked as to said township.

This order shall become effective upon the date of the official filing of the plat of resurvey of said township.

FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT

The White House,

February 3, 1936.
