Executive Order 5050—Authorizing the Appointment of Samuel H. Thompson Without Regard to Civil Service Rules
February 21, 1929
Calvin Coolidge

Mr. Samuel H. Thompson may be appointed to an appropriate position in or under the Indian Bureau without reference to the civil service rules.

Mr. Thompson has had long service in high grade supervisory positions outside the Government service.

CALVIN COOLIDGE

The White House,

February 21, 1929.
