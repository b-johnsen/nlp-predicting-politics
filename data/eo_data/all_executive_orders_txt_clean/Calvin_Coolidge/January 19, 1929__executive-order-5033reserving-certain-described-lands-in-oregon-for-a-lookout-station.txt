Executive Order 5033—Reserving Certain Described Lands in Oregon for a Lookout Station
January 19, 1929
Calvin Coolidge

Under authority of the act of Congress approved June 25, 1910 (36 Stat. 847), as amended by the act of August 24, 1912 (37 Stat. 497), and subject to the conditions therein expressed, it is hereby ordered that the SW¼ SE¼, Sec. 15, T. 40 S., R. 7 E., W.M., Oregon, containing 40 acres, be and it is hereby temporarily withdrawn from settlement, location, sale or entry, and reserved for use by the State of Oregon as a lookout station, the timber thereon to remain subject to sale by the United States in accordance with the provisions of the act of June 9, 1916 (39 Stat. 218).

This order shall remain in full force and effect unless and until revoked by the President or by act of Congress.

CALVIN COOLIDGE

The White House,

January 19, 1929.
