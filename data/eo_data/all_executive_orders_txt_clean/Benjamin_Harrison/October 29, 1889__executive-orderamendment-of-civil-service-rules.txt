Executive Order—Amendment of Civil Service Rules
October 29, 1889
Benjamin Harrison

Section 2 of Special Departmental Rule No. I is hereby amended by adding to the places excepted from examination in the Bureau of Engraving and Printing the following: "plate cleaners, transferrers, hardeners, provers, pressmen, machinists, plumbers, carpenters, and blacksmiths."

Approved:

BENJ. HARRISON
