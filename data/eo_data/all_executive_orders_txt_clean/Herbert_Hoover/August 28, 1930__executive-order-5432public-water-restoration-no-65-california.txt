Executive Order 5432—Public Water Restoration No. 65, California
August 28, 1930
Herbert Hoover

So much of the order of withdrawal dated January 24, 1914, creating Public Water Reserve No. 14, as affects the land hereinafter listed is hereby revoked, and all such land not otherwise reserved or withdrawn is hereby restored to the public domain and shall become subject to disposition under the laws applicable thereto upon such date and after such notice as may be hereafter determined and announced.

SAN BERNARDINO MERIDIAN

T. 3 S., R. 5 E., sec. 18, SE. ¼ SW. ¼.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

August 28, 1930.
