Executive Order 5280—Deep Creek Administrative Site Addition
February 17, 1930
Herbert Hoover

(Near Helena National Forest)

Montana

Under authority of the act of Congress approved June 25, 1910 (36 Stat. 847), as amended by the act of August 24, 1912 (37 Stat. 497), and on the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture, it is hereby ordered that the SW. ¼ SE. ¼ sec. 30, T. 7 N., R. 4 E., Montana principal meridian, Great Falls land district, Montana, 40 acres, be temporarily withdrawn from settlement, location, sale, or entry, except as provided in said acts, and be reserved for use by the Forest Service as an addition to the Deep Creek Ranger Station in connection with the administration of the Helena National Forest.

This order shall continue in full force and effect unless and until revoked by the President or by act of Congress.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

February 17, 1930.
