Executive Order 5781—Authorizing the Appointment of Mrs. Florence C. Brock
January 28, 1932
Herbert Hoover

Mrs. Florence C. Brock, widow of Thomas S. Brock who served for over 30 years in and under the Department of the Interior and the Department of Agriculture, may be appointed to the position of junior clerk, Bureau of Public Roads, Department of Agriculture, without reference to the requirements of the civil-service rules.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

January 28, 1932.
