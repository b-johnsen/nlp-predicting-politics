Executive Order 5806—Partial Restoration of Public Land for Airport, Alaska
February 25, 1932
Herbert Hoover

Executive Order No. 3672, dated May 8, 1922, reserving certain therein-described land in Alaska for town-site purposes, is hereby revoked, in so far as it applies to tract 32, containing 140.24 acres, shown on a supplemental plat of survey, Fourth Addition, Anchorage Town Site, township 13 north, range 3 west, Seward meridian, Alaska, accepted May 29, 1931.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

February 25, 1932.
