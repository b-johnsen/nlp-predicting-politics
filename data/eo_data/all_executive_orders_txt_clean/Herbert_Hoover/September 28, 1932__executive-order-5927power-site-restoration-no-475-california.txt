Executive Order 5927—Power-Site Restoration No. 475, California
September 28, 1932
Herbert Hoover

So much of the order of February 25, 1919, creating Power-Site Reserve No. 706, as affects the lands hereinafter described is hereby revoked:

Mount Diablo Meridian

T. 2 N., R. 16 E., sec. 16, SW. ¼ NE. ¼, W. ½ NE. ¼ SW. ¼, SE. ¼ NE. ¼ SW. ¼.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

September 28, 1932.
