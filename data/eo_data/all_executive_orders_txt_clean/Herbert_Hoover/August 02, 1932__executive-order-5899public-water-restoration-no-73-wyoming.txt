Executive Order 5899—Public Water Restoration No. 73, Wyoming
August 02, 1932
Herbert Hoover

So much of Executive order of April 19, 1912, creating Public Water Reserve No. 2, Wyoming No. 1, as affects the land hereinafter listed is hereby revoked, and all such land not otherwise reserved or withdrawn is hereby restored to the public domain and shall become subject to disposition under the laws applicable thereto upon such date and after such notice as may be hereafter determined and announced.

Sixth Principal Meridian

T. 14 N., R. 89 W., sec. 11, E. ½ E. ½;

sec. 12, NW. ½ SW. ¼.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

August 2, 1932.
