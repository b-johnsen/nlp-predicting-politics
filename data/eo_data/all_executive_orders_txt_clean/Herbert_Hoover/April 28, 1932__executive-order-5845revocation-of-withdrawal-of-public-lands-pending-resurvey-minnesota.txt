Executive Order 5845—Revocation of Withdrawal of Public Lands Pending Resurvey, Minnesota
April 28, 1932
Herbert Hoover

It is hereby ordered that Executive Order No. 5309, dated March 24, 1930, which withdrew the public lands in T. 62 N., R. 2 E., of the fourth principal meridian, Minnesota, pending resurvey, be, and the same is hereby, revoked.

All the lands in this township are within the limits of the Superior National Forest.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

April 28, 1932.
