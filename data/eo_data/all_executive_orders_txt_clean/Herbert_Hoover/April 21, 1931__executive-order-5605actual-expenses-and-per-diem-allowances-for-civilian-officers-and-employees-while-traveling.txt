Executive Order 5605—Actual Expenses and Per Diem Allowances for Civilian Officers and Employees While Traveling
April 21, 1931
Herbert Hoover

Executive Order No. 5014, issued December 19, 1928, prescribing regulations for the allowance and payment of actual expenses and the fixing and payment of per diem allowances for civilian officers and employees while traveling on official business and away from their designated posts of duty, is hereby revoked, effective July 1, 1931. These regulations, with amendments, are prescribed in the amended edition of the Standardized Government Travel Regulations, approved by the President April 21, 1931, to become effective July 1, 1931.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

April 21, 1931.
