Executive Order 5861—Authorizing the Appointment of Eugene R. Capozio
June 23, 1932
Herbert Hoover

Eugene R. Capozio may be appointed to the position of clerk at $1,260 per annum in the Veterans' Administration without reference to the requirements of the civil-service rules.

This order is issued upon the recommendation of the Administrator of Veterans' Affairs.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

June 23, 1932.
