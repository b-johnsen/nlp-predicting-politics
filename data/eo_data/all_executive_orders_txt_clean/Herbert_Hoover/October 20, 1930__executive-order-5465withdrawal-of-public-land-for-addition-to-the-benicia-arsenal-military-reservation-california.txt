Executive Order 5465—Withdrawal of Public Land for Addition to the Benicia Arsenal Military Reservation, California
October 20, 1930
Herbert Hoover

It is hereby ordered that lot 1, sec. 6, T. 2 N., R. 2 W., Mount Diablo meridian, in Solano County, State of California, containing 6.22 acres, be, and the same is hereby, withdrawn from settlement, location, entry, sale, and all forms of appropriation and reserved for military purposes as an addition to the Benicia Arsenal Military Reservation, Calif.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

October 20, 1930.
