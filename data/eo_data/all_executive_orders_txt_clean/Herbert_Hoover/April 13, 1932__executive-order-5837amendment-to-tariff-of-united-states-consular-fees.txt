Executive Order 5837—Amendment to Tariff of United States Consular Fees
April 13, 1932
Herbert Hoover

The following is established as item 47 of the Tariff of United States Consular Fees:

47. For certifying to the correctness of a copy of any certificate of American origin, manufacture, or inspection issued by any department or office of the Government of the United States, where such certified copy is required for the purpose of advancing the export trade of the United States----------No fee.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

April 13, 1932.
