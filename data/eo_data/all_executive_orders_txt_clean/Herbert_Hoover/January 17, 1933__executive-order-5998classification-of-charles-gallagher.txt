Executive Order 5998—Classification of Charles Gallagher
January 17, 1933
Herbert Hoover

Charles Gallagher, who, since July 1, 1930, has been serving in a position in the Post Office Department excepted from civil-service requirements, may be appointed in the classified service without compliance with civil-service rules.

This order is issued upon the recommendation of the Postmaster General.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

January 17, 1933.
