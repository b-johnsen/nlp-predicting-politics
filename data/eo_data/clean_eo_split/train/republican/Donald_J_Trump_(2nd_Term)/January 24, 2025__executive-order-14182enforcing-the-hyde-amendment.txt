Purpose and Policy

. For  , the   has   enacted the Hyde Amendment and similar laws that prevent   funding of elective abortion, reflecting a longstanding consensus that   taxpayers should not be forced to pay for that practice. However, the previous administration disregarded this established, commonsense policy by embedding forced taxpayer funding of elective abortions in a wide variety of Federal programs.

It is the policy of  , consistent with the Hyde Amendment, to end the forced use of Federal taxpayer dollars to fund or promote elective abortion.
