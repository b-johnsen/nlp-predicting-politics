By virtue of, and pursuant to, the authority vested in me by the "Emergency Appropriation Act,  ", appropriating $525,000,000 to meet the emergency and necessity for relief in stricken agricultural areas, there is hereby allocated to the Director of Emergency Conservation Work for the establishment and maintenance of   camps, the sum of $12,500,000; to   for making loans to farmers for seed, feed, freight,   fallowing, and similar purposes, under such terms and conditions as the Governor thereof may prescribe, the sum of $25,000,000; to   for making grants to   the sum of $56,250,000, and for the purpose of increasing employment through the purchase of lands in the stricken areas, the sum of $12,500,000; and to the Secretary of Agriculture or such agency as he may designate the sum of $43,750,000 for the purchase, sale, gift, or other disposition of seed, feed, and livestock, and for transportation thereof.
