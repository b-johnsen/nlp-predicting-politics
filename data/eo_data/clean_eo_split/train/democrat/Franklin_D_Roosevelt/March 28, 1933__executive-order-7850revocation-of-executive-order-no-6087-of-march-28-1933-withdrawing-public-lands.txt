COLORADO

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of  , ch. 421, 36 Stat. 847, as amended by the act of  , ch. 369, 37 Stat. 497, Executive Order No.  , withdrawing public lands in   pending a resurvey, is hereby revoked.

This order shall become effective upon the date of the official filing of the plats of the resurvey of the said lands.
