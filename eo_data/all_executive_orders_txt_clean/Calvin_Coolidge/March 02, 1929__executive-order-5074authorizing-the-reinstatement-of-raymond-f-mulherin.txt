Executive Order 5074—Authorizing the Reinstatement of Raymond F. Mulherin
March 02, 1929
Calvin Coolidge

Mr. Raymond F. Mulherin, for many years a post office employee and post office inspector, may be reinstated to an appropriate position in the classified service without regard to the time limit upon his eligibility for reinstatement.

CALVIN COOLIDGE

The White House,

March 2, 1929.
