Executive Order 5049—Authorizing the Appointment of Anna M. Stack Without Regard to Civil Service Rules
February 19, 1929
Calvin Coolidge

Miss Anna M. Stack, of Northampton, Massachusetts, may be appointed to an appropriate position in the classified service, without regard to the Civil Service rules. This order is issued because of my personal knowledge of the qualifications of Miss Stack.

CALVIN COOLIDGE

The White House,

February 19, 1929.
