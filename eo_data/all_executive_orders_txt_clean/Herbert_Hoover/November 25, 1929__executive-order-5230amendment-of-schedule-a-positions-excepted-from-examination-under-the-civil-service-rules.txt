Executive Order 5230—Amendment of Schedule A, Positions Excepted from Examination Under the Civil Service Rules
November 25, 1929
Herbert Hoover

Schedule A, positions excepted from the requirement of examination under the civil service rules, is hereby amended by the addition of a new subdivision, to be numbered XXI, as follows:

XXI. FEDERAL FARM BOARD

1. One private secretary or confidential clerk to each Member of the Federal Farm Board.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

November 25, 1929.
