Executive Order 5666—Permitting Persons Holding Certain Positions to Receive Appointments Under the Department of Commerce
July 03, 1931
Herbert Hoover

Executive Order No. 9 of January 17, 1873, prohibiting the holding of Federal and State offices at the same time, is hereby amended so as to permit persons holding State, Territorial, or municipal positions to receive, unless prohibited by law, appointments under the Department of Commerce when the Secretary of that department deems such employment necessary to secure a more efficient administration of the duties of his department.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

July 3, 1931.
