Executive Order 5459—Placing the Position of Shipping Commissioner in the Classified Service
October 04, 1930
Herbert Hoover

The position of Shipping Commissioner at the several ports of the United States is hereby placed in the competitive classified service.

This order supersedes Executive Order No. 3807 of March 15, 1923.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

October 4, 1930.
