Executive Order 5739—Power-Site Restoration No. 464, Idaho
October 30, 1931
Herbert Hoover

So much of the orders of February 25, 1910, creating Temporary Power-Site Withdrawal No. 117, and July 2, 1910, creating Power-Site Reserve No. 117, as affects the lands hereinafter described, is hereby revoked:

Boise Meridian

T. 3 S., R. 1 E., sec. 21, W. ½ NE. ¼, N ½ SE. ¼, SE. ¼ SE. ¼;

sec. 22, SW. ¼ SW. ¼.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

October 30, 1931.
