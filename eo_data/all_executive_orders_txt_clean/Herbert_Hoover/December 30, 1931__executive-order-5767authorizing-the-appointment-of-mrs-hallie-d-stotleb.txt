Executive Order 5767—Authorizing the Appointment of Mrs. Hallie D. Stotleb
December 30, 1931
Herbert Hoover

Mrs. Hallie D. Stotler may be appointed to the position of clerk in the Bureau of Prohibition, Department of Justice, without compliance with the requirements of the civil-service rules.

This order is based on a recommendation of the Attorney General, who states that Mrs. Stotler is the widow of a prohibition officer who met his death in line of duty. The Civil Service Commission concurs in the recommendation of the Attorney General.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

December 30, 1931.
