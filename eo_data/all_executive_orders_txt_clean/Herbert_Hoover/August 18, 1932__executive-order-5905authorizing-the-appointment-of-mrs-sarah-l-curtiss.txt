Executive Order 5905—Authorizing the Appointment of Mrs. Sarah L. Curtiss
August 18, 1932
Herbert Hoover

Mrs. Sarah L. Curtiss may be appointed to a clerkship at $1,260 per annum in the Veterans' Administration without regard to the civil-service requirements.

Mrs. Curtiss is the widow of Dr. William W. Curtiss, formerly a commissioned officer in the Public Health Service and an employee of the Veterans' Administration. This order is issued upon the recommendation of the Administrator of Veterans' Affairs.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

August 18, 1932.
