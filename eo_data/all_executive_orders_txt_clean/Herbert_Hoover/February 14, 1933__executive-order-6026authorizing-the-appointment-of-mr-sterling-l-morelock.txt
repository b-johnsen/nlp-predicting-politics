Executive Order 6026—Authorizing the Appointment of Mr. Sterling L. Morelock
February 14, 1933
Herbert Hoover

Mr. Sterling L. Morelock may be given a probational appointment as guard in the Department of Agriculture without reference to his relative standing on the civil-service register. He is a disabled veteran of the World War, having been awarded the Congressional Medal of Honor as well as the Order of the Purple Heart.

This order is recommended by the Secretary of Agriculture.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

February 14, 1933.
