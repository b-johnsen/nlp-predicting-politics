Executive Order 5159—Establishing Reidsville, North Carolina as a Customs Port of Entry
July 18, 1929
Herbert Hoover

By virtue of the authority vested in me by the Act of Congress approved August 1, 1914, making appropriations for the sundry, civil expenses of the Government for the fiscal year ended June 30, 1915, Reidsville, North Carolina, is hereby created a port of entry in Customs Collection District No. 15 (North Carolina) with headquarters at Wilmington, North Carolina, effective thirty days from the date of this order.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

July 18, 1929.
