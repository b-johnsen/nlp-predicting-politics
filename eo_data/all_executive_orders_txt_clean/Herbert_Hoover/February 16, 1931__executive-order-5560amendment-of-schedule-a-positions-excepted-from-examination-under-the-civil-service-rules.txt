Executive Order 5560—Amendment of Schedule A, Positions Excepted from Examination Under the Civil Service Rules
February 16, 1931
Herbert Hoover

Schedule A, positions excepted from the requirement of examination under the civil service rules, is hereby amended by the addition of a new subdivision, to be numbered XXII, as follows:

XXII. Federal Power Commission

1. One private secretary or confidential clerk to each member of the Federal Power Commission.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

February 16, 1931.
