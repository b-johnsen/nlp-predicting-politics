Executive Order 6048—Authorizing the Appointment of Harry E. Timmis
February 25, 1933
Herbert Hoover

Harry E. Timmis, who has been serving in a position under the Department of Commerce excepted from civil-service requirements, may be appointed in the classified service without compliance with civil-service rules.

This order is issued upon recommendation of the Secretary of Commerce.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

February 25, 1933.
