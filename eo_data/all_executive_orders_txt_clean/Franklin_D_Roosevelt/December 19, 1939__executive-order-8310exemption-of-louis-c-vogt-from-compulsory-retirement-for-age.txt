Executive Order 8310—Exemption of Louis C. Vogt From Compulsory Retirement for Age
December 19, 1939
Franklin D. Roosevelt

Whereas

, in my judgment, the public interest requires that Louis C. Vogt, Assistant Superintendent of Planning, Government Printing Office, who, during the month of January, 1940, will reach the retirement age prescribed for automatic sep-aration from the service, applicable to him, be exempted from compulsory retirement for age for a period of one year:

Now, Therefore

, by virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by section 204 of the act of June 30, 1932, 47 Stat. 382, 404 (U.S.C., title 5, sec. 715a), I hereby exempt the said Louis C. Vogt from compulsory retirement for age for a period of one year ending January 31, 1941.

FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT

The White House,

December 19, 1939.
