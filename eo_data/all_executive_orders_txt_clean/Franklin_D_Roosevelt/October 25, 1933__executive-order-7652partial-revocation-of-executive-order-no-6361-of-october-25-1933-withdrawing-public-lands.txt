Executive Order 7652—Partial Revocation of Executive Order No. 6361 of October 25, 1933, Withdrawing Public Lands
October 25, 1933
Franklin D. Roosevelt

CALIFORNIA

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of June 25, 1910, ch. 421, 36 Stat. 847, as amended by the act of August 24, 1912, ch. 369, 37 Stat. 497, Executive Order No. 6361 of October 25, 1933, temporarily withdrawing certain lands in California for classification and pending determination as to the advisability of including such lands in a national monument, is hereby revoked as to the following-described public land:

SAN BERNARDINO MERIDIAN

T. 1 S., R. 4 E., sec. 31, E½SW½, W½SE¼, 160 acres.

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

The White House,

July 2, 1937.
