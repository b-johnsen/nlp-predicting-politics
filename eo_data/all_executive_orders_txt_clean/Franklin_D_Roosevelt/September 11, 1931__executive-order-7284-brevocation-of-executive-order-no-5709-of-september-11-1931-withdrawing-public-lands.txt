Executive Order 7284-B—Revocation of Executive Order No. 5709, of September 11, 1931, Withdrawing Public Lands
September 11, 1931
Franklin D. Roosevelt

CALIFORNIA

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of June 25, 1910, ch. 421, 36 Stat. 847, as amended by the act of August 24, 1912, ch. 369, 37 Stat. 497, Executive Order No. 5709, of September 11, 1931, withdrawing public lands in T. 15 S., R. 20 E. of the San Bernardino meridian, California, pending a resurvey, is hereby revoked.

This order shall become effective upon the date of the official filing of the plat of resurvey of said township.

FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT

The White House,

February 6, 1936.
