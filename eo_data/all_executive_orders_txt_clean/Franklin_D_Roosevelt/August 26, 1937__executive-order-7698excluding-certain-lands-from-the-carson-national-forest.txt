Executive Order 7698—Excluding Certain Lands from the Carson National Forest
August 26, 1937
Franklin D. Roosevelt

NEW MEXICO

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of June 4, 1897, 30 Stat. 11, 34, 36 (U.S.C., title 16, sec. 473), and upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture, it is ordered that the following-described lands in New Mexico, embraced in color of title application Santa Fe 058183, be, and they are hereby, excluded from the Carson National Forest:

NEW MEXICO PRINCIPAL MERIDIAN

T. 25 N., R. 8 E., sec. 2, tracts 37, 38, and 39, 8.89 acres.

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

The White House,

August 26, 1937.
