Executive Order 7608—Revocation of Executive Order No. 5596 of April 9, 1931, Withdrawing Public Lands
April 9, 1931
Franklin D. Roosevelt

NEVADA

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of June 25, 1910, ch. 421, 36 Stat. 847, as amended by the act of August 24, 1912, ch. 369, 37 Stat. 497, Executive Order No. 5596 of April 9, 1931, withdrawing public lands in Nevada, pending resurvey, is hereby revoked.

This order shall become effective upon the date of the official filing of the plat of the resurvey of said township.

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

The White House,

April 22, 1937.
