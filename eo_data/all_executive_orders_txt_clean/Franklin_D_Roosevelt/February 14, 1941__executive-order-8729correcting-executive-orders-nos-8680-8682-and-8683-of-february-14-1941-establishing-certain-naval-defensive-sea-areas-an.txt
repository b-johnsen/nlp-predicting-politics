Executive Order 8729—Correcting Executive Orders Nos. 8680, 8682, and 8683 of February 14, 1941, Establishing Certain Naval Defensive Sea Areas and Naval Airspace Reservations
February 14, 1941
Franklin D. Roosevelt

The phrase "the territorial waters between the extreme high-water marks in the three-mile marine boundaries" occurring in the first paragraph of Executive Orders Nos. 8680, 8682, and 8683 of February 14, 1941, establishing certain naval defensive sea areas and naval airspace reservations, is hereby corrected to read "the territorial waters between the extreme high-water marks and the three-mile marine boundaries".

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

The White House,

April 2, 1941.
