Executive Order 7460—Designating the Chairman of the United States Maritime Commission
September 26, 1936
Franklin D. Roosevelt

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by section 201(a) of the Merchant Marine Act, 1936 (49 Stat. 1985), I hereby designate Henry A. Wiley as Chairman of the United States Maritime Commission.

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

THE WHITE HOUSE,

September 26, 1936.
