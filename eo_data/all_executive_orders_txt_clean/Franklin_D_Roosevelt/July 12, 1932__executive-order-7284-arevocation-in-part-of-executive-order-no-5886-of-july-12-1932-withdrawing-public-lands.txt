Executive Order 7284-A—Revocation in Part of Executive Order No. 5886, of July 12, 1932, Withdrawing Public Lands
July 12, 1932
Franklin D. Roosevelt

WYOMING

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of June 25, 1910, ch. 421, 36 Stat. 847, as amended by the act of August 24, 1912, ch. 369, 37 Stat. 497, Executive Order No. 5886, of July 12, 1932, withdrawing, together with other lands, public lands in T. 26 N., R. 118 W. of the sixth principal meridian, Wyoming, pending a resurvey, is hereby revoked as to said township.

This order shall become effective upon the date of the official filing of the plat of resurvey of said township.

FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT

The White House,

February 6, 1936.
