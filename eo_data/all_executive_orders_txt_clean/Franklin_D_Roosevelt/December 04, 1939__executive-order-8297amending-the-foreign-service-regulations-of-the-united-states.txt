Executive Order 8297—Amending the Foreign Service Regulations of the United States
December 04, 1939
Franklin D. Roosevelt

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by section 1752 of the Revised Statutes of the United States (22 U.S.C. S 132), it is ordered that the Tariff of United States Foreign Service Fees, section V-15 of the Foreign Service Regulations of the United States be, and it is hereby amended, to be effective on January 1, 1940, as follows:

Tariff of United States Foreign Service Fees

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

The White House,

December 4, 1939.
