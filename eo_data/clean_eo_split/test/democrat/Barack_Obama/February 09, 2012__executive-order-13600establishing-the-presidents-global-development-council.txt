Mission and Functions.

The   shall advise and support the President, through   and   staff, in furtherance of the policy set forth in section 1 of this order. The   shall meet regularly and shall:

(a) inform the policy and practice of   global development policy and programs by providing advice to the President and other senior officials on issues including:

(i) innovative, scalable approaches to development with proven demonstrable impact, particularly on sustainable economic growth and good governance;

(ii) areas for enhanced collaboration between   and public and private sectors to advance development policy;

(iii) best practices for and effectiveness of research and development in low and middle income economies; and

(b) support new and existing public-private partnerships by:

(i) identifying key areas for enhanced collaboration and any barriers to collaboration; and
