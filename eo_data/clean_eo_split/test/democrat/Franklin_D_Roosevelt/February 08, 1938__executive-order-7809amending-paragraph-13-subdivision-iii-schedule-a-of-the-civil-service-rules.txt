By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by   (22 Stat. 403), it is ordered that paragraph 13,  , Schedule A of  , excepting from competitive civil service requirements all persons actually employed in leprosy,   spotted fever, yellow fever, and psittacosis investigation stations under  ,  , be, and it is hereby, amended to read as follows:

"13.  : All persons actually employed in leprosy, yellow fever, and psittacosis investigation stations."

This amendment removes from the excepted class all positions at   investigation stations the incumbents of which are engaged in Rocky Mountain spotted fever work. The incumbents of such positions may acquire an appropriate civil service status in accordance with the provisions of section 6 of Civil Service Rule II, as amended.
