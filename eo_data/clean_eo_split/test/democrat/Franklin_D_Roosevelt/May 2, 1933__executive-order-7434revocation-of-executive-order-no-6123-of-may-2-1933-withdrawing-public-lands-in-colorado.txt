By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of  , ch. 421, 36 Stat. 847, as amended by the act of  , ch. 369, 37 Stat. 497, Executive Order No. 6123 of  , withdrawing public lands in T. 4 N.,   78 W. of the sixth principal meridian,  , pending a resurvey, is hereby revoked.
