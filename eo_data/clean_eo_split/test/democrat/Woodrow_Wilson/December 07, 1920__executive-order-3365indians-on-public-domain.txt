It is hereby ordered, under authority found in the act of  ,   (Thirty-fourth Statutes at Large, pages three hundred and twenty-five and three hundred and twenty-six), that the trust or other period of restriction against alienation contained in any patent heretofore issued to any   for any lands on the public domain be, and the same is hereby, extended for a further period of   from the date on which any such trust would otherwise expire.
