By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by section 4 of the act of  , Public Law 230, 77th  , it is hereby ordered that whenever the duties assigned to warrant officers of the   include (1) the command of stations, units, or detachments, (2) the disbursement and administration of funds, including the certification of vouchers and pay rolls, (3) the issue of travel orders, bills of lading, and transportation requests, (4) the receipt for, accountability for, and administration of property, (5) the certification and verification of official papers, or (6) the performance of similar routine administrative duties, such warrant officers shall be vested with all the powers usually exercised by commissioned officers in the performance of such duties.
