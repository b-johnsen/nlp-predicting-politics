By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of  , ch. 421, 36 Stat. 847, as amended by the act of  , ch. 369, 37 Stat. 497, Executive Order No.  , of  , withdrawing, together with other lands, public lands in T. 43 N.,   86 W. of the sixth principal meridian,  , pending a resurvey, is hereby revoked as to said township.
