GENERAL ORDERS, NO. 102

WAR DEPARTMENT,

 -GENERAL'S OFFICE

The President of   commands it to be made known that all soldiers who have deserted their colors, and who shall, on or before  , surrender themselves at any military station, shall receive a full pardon, only forfeiting the pay and allowances due them at the time of desertion, and shall be restored to duty without trial or punishment on condition that they faithfully serve through the term of their enlistment.

By order of the Secretary of War:
