By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me under the Emergency Relief Appropriation Act of 1935, approved April 8, 1935 (Public Resolution No, 11, 74th Congress), I hereby appoint the following persons to serve as members of the National Advisory Committee of the National Youth Administration, established by Executive Order No. 7086 of June 26, 1935:

The said members shall serve without compensation, except that each shall receive, in addition to his necessary expenses of transportation, an allowance of twenty-five dollars for each calendar day while necessarily away from his home incident to the performance of the authorized work of the said Committee, payable under the appropriation made by the said Emergency Relief Appropriation Act of 1935.
