By virtue of the authority vested in me by the Constitution and the statutes of the United States, it is hereby ordered that Executive Order No. 9139 of April 18, 1942, establishing the War Manpower Commission in the Executive Office of the President, be, and it is hereby, amended to include in the membership of the said Commission a representative of the National Housing Agency and a joint representative of the War Shipping Administration and the Office of Defense Transportation.
