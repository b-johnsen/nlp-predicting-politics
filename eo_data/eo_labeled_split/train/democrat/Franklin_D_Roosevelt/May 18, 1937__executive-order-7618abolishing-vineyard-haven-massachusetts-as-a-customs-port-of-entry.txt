By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of August 1, 1914, 38 Stat. 609, 623 (US.C., title 19, sec. 2), it is ordered that Vineyard Haven, Massachusetts, be, and it is hereby, abolished as a customs port of entry in Customs Collection District No. 4 (Massachusetts), as a customs port of entry, effective immediately.
