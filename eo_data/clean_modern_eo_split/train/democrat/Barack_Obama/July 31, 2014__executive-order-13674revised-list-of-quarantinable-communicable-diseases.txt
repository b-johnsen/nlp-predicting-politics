Section 1.

Amendment to Executive Order

Based upon the recommendation of the Secretary of  , in consultation with  , and for the purposes set forth in section 1 of Executive Order 13295 of  , as amended by Executive Order   of  , section 1 of Executive Order 13295 shall be further amended by replacing subsection (b) with the following:

"(b) Severe acute respiratory syndromes, which are diseases that are associated with fever and signs and symptoms of pneumonia or other respiratory illness, are capable of being transmitted from person to person, and that either are causing, or have the potential to cause, a pandemic, or, upon infection, are highly likely to cause mortality or serious morbidity if not properly controlled. This subsection does not apply to influenza."
