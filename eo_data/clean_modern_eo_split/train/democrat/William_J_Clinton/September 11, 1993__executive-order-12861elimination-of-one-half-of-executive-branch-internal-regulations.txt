Section 1.  . Each executive department and agency shall undertake to eliminate not less than 50 percent of its civilian internal management regulations that are not required by law within   of the effective date of this order. An agency internal management regulation, for the purposes of this order, means an agency directive or regulation that pertains to its organization, management, or personnel matters. Reductions in agency internal management regulations shall be concentrated in areas that will result in the greatest improvement in productivity, streamlining of operations, and improvement in customer service.

 . 2. Coverage. This order applies to all executive branch departments and agencies.

 . 3. Implementation. The Director of   shall issue instructions regarding the implementation of this order, including exemptions necessary for the delivery of essential services and compliance with applicable law.

 . 4.  . All independent regulatory commissions and agencies are requested to comply with the provisions of this order.
