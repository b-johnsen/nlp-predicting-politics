Section 1. Determinations. The subdivisions of the Department of Justice set forth in section 2 of this order are hereby determined to have as a primary function intelligence, counterintelligence, investigative, or national security work. It is further determined that chapter 71 of title 5, United States Code, cannot be applied to these subdivisions in a manner consistent with national security requirements and considerations.

Sec. 2. Amendment of Executive Order 12171. Executive Order 12171 of November 19, 1979, as amended, is further amended by adding to the end of section 1–209 the following new subsections:

"(c) United States Attorneys' Offices.

(d) Criminal Division.

(e)

INTERPOL - U.S. National Central Bureau.

(f) National Drug Intelligence Center.

(g)

Office of Intelligence Policy and Review."
