Executive Order 7703—Designation of Jeanne Kavanagh To Sign Land Patents
September 10, 1937
Franklin D. Roosevelt

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by section 450 of the Revised Statutes, as amended (U.S.C., title 43, sec. 8), Jeanne Kavanagh of Iowa, appointed by the Secretary of the Interior August 24, 1937, to the position of clerk to sign land patents, is hereby designated to sign my name to said patents.

FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT

The White House,

September 10, 1937.
