Executive Order 8788—Withdrawing Public Land for Use of the War Department
June 14, 1941
Franklin D. Roosevelt

ALASKA

By virtue of the authority vested in me as President of the United States, it is ordered that the following-described public land be, and it is hereby, withdrawn from all forms of appropriation under the public land laws, including the mining laws, and reserved for the use of the War Department for military purposes:

SEWARD MERIDIAN

T. 13 N., R. 3 W.,

Sec. 15, NE¼ containing 160 acres.

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

The White House,

June 14, 1941.
