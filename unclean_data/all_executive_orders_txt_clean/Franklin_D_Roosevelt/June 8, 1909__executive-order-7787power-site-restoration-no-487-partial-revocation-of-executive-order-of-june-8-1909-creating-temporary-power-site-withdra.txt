Executive Order 7787—Power-Site Restoration No. 487. Partial Revocation of Executive Order of June 8, 1909, Creating Temporary Power-Site Withdrawal No. 20, and Executive Order of July 2, 1910, Creating Power-Site Reserve No. 20
June 8, 1909
Franklin D. Roosevelt

MONTANA

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of June 25, 1910, ch. 421, 36 Stat. 847, as amended by the act of August 24, 1912, ch. 369,37 Stat. 497, the Executive Order of June 8, 1909, withdrawing lands for Temporary Power-Site Withdrawal No. 20, and the Executive Order of July 2, 1910, withdrawing lands for Power-Site Reserve No. 20, are hereby revoked as to the following-described lands:

PRINCIPAL MERIDIAN

T. 3 N., R. 6 W.,

Sec. 35, NE¼, NE¼NW¼, and NE¼SE¼;

Sec. 36, W½NW¼. NW¼SW¼, and S½SW¼.

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

The White House,

January 10, 1938.
