Executive Order 8009—Withdrawal of Public Lands
November 18, 1938
Franklin D. Roosevelt

[Public Water Reserve No. 160]

CALIFORNIA, IDAHO, OREGON, AND WYOMING

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of June 25, 1910, c. 421, 36 Stat. 847, as amended by the act of August 24, 1912, c. 369, 37 Stat. 497, and subject to the conditions therein expressed and to valid existing rights, it is ordered that the following-described public lands be, and they are hereby, withdrawn from settlement, location, sale, or entry and reserved for public use in accordance with the provisions of section 10 of the act of December 29, 1916, c. 9, 39 Stat. 862, 865:

CALIFORNIA

MOUNT DIABLO MERIDIAN

T. 7 S., R. 20 E., sec. 2, NE¼SE¼.

IDAHO

BOISE MERIDIAN

T. 31 N., R. 3 W.,

sec. 26, lot 3;

sec. 27, lot 5.

OREGON

WILLAMETTE MERIDIAN

T. 9 S., R. 23 E., sec. 18, NE¼SW¼.

WYOMING

SIXTH PRINCIPAL MERIDIAN

T. 40 N., R. 84 W., sec. 25, SE¼SE¼.

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

The White House,

November 18, 1938.
