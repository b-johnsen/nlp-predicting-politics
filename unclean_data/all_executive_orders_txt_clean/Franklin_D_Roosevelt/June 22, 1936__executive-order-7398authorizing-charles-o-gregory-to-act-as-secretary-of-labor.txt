Executive Order 7398—Authorizing Charles O. Gregory to Act as Secretary of Labor
June 22, 1936
Franklin D. Roosevelt

By virtue of the authority vested in me under the provisions of section 179 of the Revised Statutes of the United States (5 U.S.C., sec. 6), I hereby authorize and direct Charles O. Gregory, Solicitor of Labor, to perform the duties of Secretary of Labor during the absence of the Secretary of Labor and the Assistant Secretary of Labor.

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

THE WHITE HOUSE,

June 22, 1936.
