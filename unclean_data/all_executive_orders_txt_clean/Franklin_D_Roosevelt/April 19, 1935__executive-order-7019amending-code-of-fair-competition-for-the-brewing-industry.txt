Executive Order 7019—Amending Code of Fair Competition for the Brewing Industry
April 19, 1935
Franklin D. Roosevelt

Whereas

the Federal Alcohol Control Administration has submitted for my approval amendments to the Code of Fair Competition for the Brewing Industry and has rendered its report and recommendations and findings thereon; and

Whereas

due notice of public hearing on said proposed amendments has been given and a public hearing thereon has been held pursuant to such notice;

Now, Therefore

, by virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by Title I of the National Industrial Recovery Act of June 16, 1933 (ch. 90, 48 Stat. 195), and in order to effectuate the purposes of said Title, I hereby approve said amendments and modify my previous approval of the Code of Fair Competition for the Brewing Industry to include an approval of said Code in its entirety as hereby amended.

FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT

The White House,

April 19, 1935.
