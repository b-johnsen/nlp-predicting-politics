Executive Order 8188—Revocation of Executive Order No. 4539 of November 6, 1926, Withdrawing Public Lands
November 6, 1926
Franklin D. Roosevelt

UTAH

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of June 25, 1910, c. 421, 36 Stat. 847, Executive Order No. 4539 of November 6, 1926, withdrawing public lands in Utah pending a resurvey, and heretofore partially revoked, is hereby revoked as to the remainder of the lands affected thereby.

This order shall become effective upon the date of the official filing of the plat of the resurvey of the lands involved.

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

The White House,

June 29, 1939.
