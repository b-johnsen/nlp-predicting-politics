Executive Order 7602—Revocation of Executive Order No. 7261-A of December 31, 1935, Amending the Instructions to Diplomatic Officers of the United States
December 31, 1935
Franklin D. Roosevelt

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by section 1752 of the Revised Statutes (U.S.C. title 22, sec. 132), Executive Order No. 7261-A of December 31, 1935, amending the Instructions to Diplomatic Officers of the United States, is hereby revoked.

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

The White House,

April 9, 1937.
