Executive Order 7254—Excluding a Certain Tract of Land From Tongass National Forest and Restoring it to Entry
December 21, 1935
Franklin D. Roosevelt

ALASKA

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of June 4, 1897, 30 Stat. 11, 36 (16 U.S.C., sec. 473), and upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture, it is ordered that the following-described tract of land in Alaska, occupied as a homesite, be, and it is hereby, excluded from the Tongass National Forest and restored to entry under the applicable public-land laws:

COPPER RIVER MERIDIAN

T. 51 S., R. 68 E., sec. 6, NE¼SW¼SE¼SW¼, N½SE¼SW¼SE¼SW¼, fractional

NW¼SE¼SE¼SW¼, fractional N½SW¼SE¼SE¼SW¼, aggregating 5.30 acres.

FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT

The White House,

December 21, 1935.
