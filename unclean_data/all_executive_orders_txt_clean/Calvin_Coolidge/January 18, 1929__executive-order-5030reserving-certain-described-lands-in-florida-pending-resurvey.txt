Executive Order 5030—Reserving Certain Described Lands in Florida Pending Resurvey
January 18, 1929
Calvin Coolidge

Under authority contained in the act of June 25, 1910 (36 Stat. 847), as amended by the act of August 24, 1912 (37 Stat. 497), and subject to the conditions, provisions and limitations of said acts, it is hereby ordered that the public land in Ts. 14 and 15 S., R. 25 E., Tal.M., Florida, be and the same is hereby withdrawn from settlement, location, sale, entry and all forms of appropriation, pending a resurvey of said townships under the act of March 3, 1909 (35 Stat. 845), as amended by the Joint Resolution of June 25, 1910 (36 Stat. 884).

This order shall continue in full force and effect unless and until revoked by the President or by an act of Congress.

CALVIN COOLIDGE

The White House,

January 18, 1929.
