Executive Order 5056—Authorizing the Appointment of Mary Scott Mills Without Regard to Civil Service Rules
February 23, 1929
Calvin Coolidge

Mrs. Mary Scott Mills, widow of Mr. John S. Mills who served the Government very efficiently for fifty-one years and from 1897 in the Treasury Department, may be appointed to an appropriate classified position without reference to the civil service rules.

This Order is recommended by the Secretary of the Treasury. The Civil Service Commission is unable to concur.

CALVIN COOLIDGE

The White House,

February 23, 1929.
