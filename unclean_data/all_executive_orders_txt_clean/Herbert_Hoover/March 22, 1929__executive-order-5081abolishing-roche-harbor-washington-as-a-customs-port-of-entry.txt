Executive Order 5081—Abolishing Roche Harbor, Washington as a Customs Port of Entry
March 22, 1929
Herbert Hoover

By virtue of the authority vested in me by the Act of Congress approved August 1, 1914, making appropriations for the sundry civil expenses of the Government for the fiscal year ended June 30, 1915, Roche Harbor is hereby abolished as a port of entry in Customs Collection District No. 30 (Washington) with headquarters at Seattle, Washington, effective thirty days from the date of this Order.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

March 22, 1929.
