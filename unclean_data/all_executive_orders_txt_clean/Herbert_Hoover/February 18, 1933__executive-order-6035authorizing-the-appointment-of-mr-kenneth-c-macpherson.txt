Executive Order 6035—Authorizing the Appointment of Mr. Kenneth C. Macpherson
February 18, 1933
Herbert Hoover

Kenneth C. Macpherson, who, since July 11, 1922, has been employed in the Department of Commerce and the Post Office Department in positions excepted from civil-service requirements, may be appointed in the classified service without compliance with civil-service rules.

This order is issued upon the recommendation of the Postmaster General.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

February 18, 1933.
