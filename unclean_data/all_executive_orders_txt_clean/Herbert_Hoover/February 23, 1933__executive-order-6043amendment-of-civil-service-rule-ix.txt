Executive Order 6043—Amendment of Civil Service Rule IX
February 23, 1933
Herbert Hoover

Civil Service Rule IX, section 1 (b), is hereby amended to read as follows:

(b) A former classified employee entitled to military preference in appointment may be reinstated without time limit.

The object and result of this amendment is to make the military-preference benefits uniform.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

February 23, 1933.
