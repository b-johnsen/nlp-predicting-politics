Executive Order 6020—Reinstatement of Richard H. Knight
February 09, 1933
Herbert Hoover

Mr. Richard H. Knight may be reinstated as a substitute clerk, foot of the roll, in the New York, N.Y., Post Office, without regard to the requirements of the civil- service rules.

This order is recommended by the Postmaster General.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

February 9, 1933.
