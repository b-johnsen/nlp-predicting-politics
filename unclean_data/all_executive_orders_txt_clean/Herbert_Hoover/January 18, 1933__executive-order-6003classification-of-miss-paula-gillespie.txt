Executive Order 6003—Classification of Miss Paula Gillespie
January 18, 1933
Herbert Hoover

Miss Paula Gillespie, who has been employed in the office of the Assistant Secretary of the Navy, as senior clerk-stenographer, without a competitive classified status since April 22, 1930, is hereby classified.

This order is issued upon the recommendation of the Assistant Secretary of the Navy.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

January 18, 1933.
