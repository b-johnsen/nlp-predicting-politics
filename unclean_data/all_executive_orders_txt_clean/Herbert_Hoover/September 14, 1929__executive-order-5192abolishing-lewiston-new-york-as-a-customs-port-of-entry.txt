Executive Order 5192—Abolishing Lewiston, New York, as a Customs Port of Entry
September 14, 1929
Herbert Hoover

By virtue of the authority vested in me by the Act of Congress approved August 1, 1914, Lewiston, New York, is hereby abolished as a port of entry in Customs Collection District No. 9 (Buffalo) with headquarters at Buffalo, New York, effective thirty days from the date of this order.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

September 14, 1929.
