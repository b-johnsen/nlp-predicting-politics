Executive Order 5810—Revocation of Withdrawal of Public Lands Pending Resurvey, Utah
February 27, 1932
Herbert Hoover

It is hereby ordered that Executive Order No. 4426, dated April 19, 1926, which withdrew the public lands in T. 1 S., R. 2 E., Salt Lake meridian, Utah, pending a resurvey, be, and the same is hereby, revoked.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

February 27, 1932.
