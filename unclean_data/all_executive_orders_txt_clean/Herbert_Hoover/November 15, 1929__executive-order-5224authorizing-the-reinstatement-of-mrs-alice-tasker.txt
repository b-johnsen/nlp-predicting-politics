Executive Order 5224—Authorizing the Reinstatement of Mrs. Alice Tasker
November 15, 1929
Herbert Hoover

Mrs. Alice Tasker, formerly a matron in the New York Office of the Veterans' Bureau from December 15, 1921, to February 8, 1924, when she was discharged without prejudice for continued absence due to injury incurred in an accident in the subway at 42nd Street, New York, may be reinstated in an appropriate position in any part of the service. The Director of the Veterans' Bureau states that her services were satisfactory during her employment under his office.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

November 15, 1929.
