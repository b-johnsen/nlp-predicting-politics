Executive Order 5954—Exemption of Persons from Compulsory Retirement for Age
November 26, 1932
Herbert Hoover

By virtue of the authority vested in me by section 204 of Part II of the act entitled "AN ACT Making appropriations for the Legislative Branch of the Government for the fiscal year ending June 30, 1933, and for other purposes," approved June 30, 1932, the following-named persons who, during the current month, reach the retirement age prescribed for automatic separation from the service are hereby exempted from the provisions of that section.

Department or Establishment

Office of the Architect of the Capitol

Mrs. Otilla A. Reagan
