Executive Order—Restoring Portion of White Mountain Indian Reservation to Public Domain
June 21, 1874
Ulysses S. Grant

EXECUTIVE MANSION, July 21, 1871.

It is hereby ordered that all that portion of the White Mountain Indian Reservation in Arizona Territory lying east of 109 degrees 30 minutes west longitude be restored to the public domain.

U. S. GRANT.

SOURCE: Kappler, Indian Affairs, Laws and Treaties, US GPO, 1904, p 814
