Eustaquio Vallecer may be given a classified status as master, Harbor Boat Service, War Department, Philippine Islands.

This order is issued in recognition of Mr. Vallecer's loyal and efficient service for the United States Government in the Philippines since August 13, 1898. He is recommended as being one of the most capable pilots of harbor craft, and during the Spanish-American War demonstrated his loyalty by transporting troops under fire.

This order is recommended by the Secretary of War and by the Civil Service Commission.
