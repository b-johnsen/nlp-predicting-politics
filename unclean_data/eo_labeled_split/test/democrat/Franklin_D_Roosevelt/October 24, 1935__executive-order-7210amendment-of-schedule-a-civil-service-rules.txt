By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the provisions of paragraph Eighth of subdivision SECOND of section 2 of the Civil Service Act of January 16, 1883 (22 Stat. 403, 404), schedule A of the civil-service rules is hereby amended by adding thereto a new subdivision reading as follows:

"

XXV. United States High. Commissioner to the Philippine Islands

"1. Junior messengers and under clerks with a salary range of $180 to $250 per annum in the office of the United States High Commissioner to the Philippine Islands."
