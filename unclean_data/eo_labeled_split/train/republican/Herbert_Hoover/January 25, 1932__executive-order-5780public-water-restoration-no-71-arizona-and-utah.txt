So much of Executive orders of April 17, 1916, creating Public Water Reserve No. 34, Arizona No. 5, as modified by Interpretation No. 29 of November 20, 1926; and of March 29, 1912, creating Public Water Reserve No. 1, Utah No. 1, as affects the land hereinafter listed is hereby revoked, and all such land not otherwise reserved or withdrawn is hereby restored to the public domain and shall become subject to disposition under the laws applicable thereto upon such date and after such notice as may be hereafter determined and announced.

Arizona

GILA AND SALT RIVER MERIDIAN

Lands affected in Public Water Reserve No. 34:

T. 39 N., R. 13 W., sec. 5, S. ½ NW. ¼, N. ½ SW. ¼, NW. ¼ SE. ¼.

Utah

SALT LAKE MERIDIAN

Lands affected in Public Water Reserve No. 1:

T. 20 S., R. 19 W., sec. 10, NE. ¼ SE. ¼.
