By virtue of the authority vested in me by the act of Congress approved August 1, 1914 (38 Stat. 623), making appropriations for the sundry civil expenses of the Government for the fiscal year ended June 30, 1915, the customs station of Sasabe, Ariz., is hereby designated a customs port of entry in Customs Collection District No. 26 (Arizona) with headquarters at Nogales, Ariz., effective 30 days from the date of this order.
