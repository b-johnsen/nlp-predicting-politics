Under authority of the act of June 25, 1910 (36 Stat. 847-848), as amended by the act of August 24, 1912 (37 Stat. 497), and subject to the conditions of said acts, it is hereby ordered that the W. ½ E. ½ SW. ¼ NE. ¼ sec. 17, T. 4 S., R. 14 E., Willamette meridian, Oregon, be, and is hereby, withdrawn and reserved for use of the Bureau of Fisheries of the Department of Commerce, subject to an existing withdrawal for power purposes and to any valid existing rights.

This withdrawal shall remain in effect until revoked by the President or by act of Congress.
