Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Department of the Treasury and the Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services relating to

Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act; Updating Payment Parameters, Section 1332 Waiver Implementing Regulations, and Improving Health Insurance Markets for 2022 and Beyond
(86 Fed. Reg. 53412 (September 27, 2021)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
