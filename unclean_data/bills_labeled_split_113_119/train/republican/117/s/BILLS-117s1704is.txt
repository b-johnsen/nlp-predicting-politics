1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Telehealth Expansion Act of 2021
.
2.
Exemption for telehealth services
(a)
In general
Subparagraph (E) of
section 223(c)(2)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking
In the case of plan years beginning on or before December 31, 2021, a plan
and inserting
A plan
.
(b)
Certain coverage disregarded
Clause (ii) of
section 223(c)(1)(B)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking
(in the case of plan years beginning on or before December 31, 2021)
.
(c)
Effective date
The amendments made by this section shall take effect on the date of the enactment of this Act.
