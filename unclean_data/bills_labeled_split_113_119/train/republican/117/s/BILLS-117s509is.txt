1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Leveraging Integrated Networks in Communities to Address Social Needs Act of 2021

2.
Program to support establishment or enhancement of State community integration network infrastructure
(a)
Grant program
The Secretary shall award grants on a competitive basis to States to support such States, through public-private partnerships, to establish new or enhance existing community integration network infrastructure through authorized activities under subsection (b).
(b)
Authorized activities
A State shall use a grant under this section to carry out activities and services to establish new or enhance existing community integration network infrastructure, on a statewide basis through direct network operations or collaborations among multiple associated entities, which may include such entities that operate regionally.
