Short title

Empowering States to Deport Illegal Immigrants Act
.
2.
Authorization of State and local law enforcement officers to enforce certain Federal immigration laws
(a)
In general
States may authorize State and local law enforcement officers to enforce provisions of Federal immigration law that prohibit the unlawful entry into the United States, including sections 271 through 280 of the Immigration and Nationality Act (8 U.S.C. 1321 through 1330).
(b)
Effect of authorization on Supreme Court ruling
Subsection (a) effectively abrogates the 2012 decision of the Supreme Court in Arizona v. United States (567 U.S. 387).
