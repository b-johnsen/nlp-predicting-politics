(b)
Updates to E-Rate program
(1)
In general
Not later than 180 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Commission shall amend the E-rate program to provide that芒聙聰
(A)
activities that are integral, immediate, and proximate to the education of a student, even if those activities are performed in a covered household, qualify as activities that are for educational purposes for the purposes of the E-rate program;
(B)
a covered household in which a student is engaged or participating in distance learning shall be deemed to be a classroom for the purposes of the E-rate program; and
(C)
the eligibility of a school under the E-rate program, or any support received by a school through the E-rate program, shall not be diminished if the school extends services obtained through the E-rate program to a covered household.
(2)
Coordination
In making the amendments required under paragraph (1), the Commission shall coordinate with the Secretary of Education and appropriate instrumentalities of State and local governments.
