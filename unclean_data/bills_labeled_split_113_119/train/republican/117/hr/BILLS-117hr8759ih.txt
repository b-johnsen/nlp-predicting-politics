1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Bureaucrats to the Border Act
.
2.
DHS Volunteer Force renamed DHS Mission Support Force
The DHS Volunteer Force of the Office of the Chief Human Capital Officer of the Department of Homeland Security shall be known, on and after the date of the enactment of this Act, as the
DHS Mission Support Force
, and any reference to the DHS Volunteer Force in any law, regulation, rule, guidance, paper, map, or other document of the United States shall be deemed to be a reference to the DHS Mission Support Force.
3.
New employees of the IRS to provide support at southern United States border
(a)
In general
The Secretary of the Treasury shall require any specified new employee of the Internal Revenue Service to complete a 30-day support detail with the DHS Mission Support Force within 365 days of the date of hire of such specified new employee.
