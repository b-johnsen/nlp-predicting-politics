Short title

.
2.
Additional transparency in Federal procurement during supply chain disruptions
(a)
Requirement
Chapter 17 of division B of subtitle I of title 41, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
1714.
Additional transparency in Federal procurement during supply chain disruptions
During a period in which there is a disruption in the supply chain of an item of food, as determined by the Secretary of Commerce, the head of each executive agency shall transmit in a timely manner information concerning the procurement of any such item by the agency during such period to the Administrator of General Services for entry into the Federal Procurement Data System referred to in section 1122(a)(4) (or any successor system).
.
(b)
Clerical amendment
The table of contents at the beginning of chapter 17 of such title is amended by inserting after the item relating to section 1713 the following new item:
1714. Additional transparency in Federal procurement during supply chain disruptions.
.
