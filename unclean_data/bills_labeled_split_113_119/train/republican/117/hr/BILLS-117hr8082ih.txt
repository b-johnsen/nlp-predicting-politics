1.
Corporal Frank Buckles Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 101 West Washington Street in Charles Town, West Virginia, shall be known and designated as the
Corporal Frank Buckles Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Corporal Frank Buckles Post Office
.
