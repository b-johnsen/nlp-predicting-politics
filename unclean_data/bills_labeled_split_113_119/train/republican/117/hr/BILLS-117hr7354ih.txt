1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Preventing Terrorist Sympathizers from Appeasing Terrorists Act
.
2.
Foreign Terrorist Organization designation
Notwithstanding section 219 of the Immigration and Nationality Act (
8 U.S.C. 1189
), the designation of the Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps as a foreign terrorist organization under such section, on April 15, 2019, may not be revoked or rescinded, except by a joint resolution of Congress.
