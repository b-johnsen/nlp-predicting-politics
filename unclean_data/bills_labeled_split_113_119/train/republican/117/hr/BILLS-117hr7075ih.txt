1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Ukrainian Independence Park Act of 2022
.
2.
Designation of Ukrainian Independence Park
(a)
Designation
(1)
In general
The area described in paragraph (2) shall be designated as
Ukrainian Independence Park
.
(2)
Description of area
The area designated as
Ukrainian Independence Park
under paragraph (1) is comprised of the following properties within the approximate triangular area formed by 22nd Street Northwest to the east, P Street Northwest to the south, and Florida Avenue Northwest to the west, in Washington, District of Columbia:
(A)
The property designated by the National Park Service as reservation 57D.
(B)
The property designated by the National Park Service as reservation 360, including the portion of such property that contains the Taras Shevchenko Memorial.
(b)
Reference
Any reference in any law, regulation, document, record, map, paper, or other record of the United States to the area or properties described in subsection (a) is deemed to be a reference to
Ukrainian Independence Park
.

Signage
The Secretary of the Interior may post signs on or near Ukrainian Independence Park that include information on the importance of Ukraineâs independence, freedom, and sovereignty and the solidarity between the people of Ukraine and the United States.
