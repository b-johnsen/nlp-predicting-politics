1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Rail Employee Training Modernization Act
.
2.
Amendment of definitions
(a)
In general
Not later than 90 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Secretary of Transportation shall issue such regulations as are necessary to define the term
classroom
for the purposes of parts 240 and 242 of title 49, Code of Federal Regulations, to mean a physical or virtual space where training is provided by a qualified instructor.
(b)
Rule of construction
Nothing in this Act may be construed as intending to amend any certification requirements, including the length of classroom training, materials provided or covered in classroom training, or additional training requirements outside classroom training, including simulator, computer-based, correspondence, on-the-job training, or other formal training.
