(b)
Accrued benefits
Section 5121 of title 38, United States Code is amended by adding at the end the following new subsection:
(d)
The amount of the accrued benefit payable under subsection (a) on behalf on an individual is the total amount of the periodic monetary benefits (other than insurance and servicemen's indemnity) under laws administered by the Secretary to which the individual was entitled at death for the entire period of time for which the individual was entitled to, but did not receive, such benefits.
.
(c)
Effective date
The amendments made by this section shall take effect on the date of the enactment of this Act and shall apply with respect to deaths that occur on or after that date.
