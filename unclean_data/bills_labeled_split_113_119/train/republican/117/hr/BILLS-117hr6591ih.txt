(g)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)
Administrator
The term
Administrator
means the Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency.
(2)
Covered entity
The term
covered entity
means a manufacturer, wholesaler, supplier, or retailer that is responsible for the labeling or retail packaging of a disposable nonwoven product that is sold or offered for sale in the United States.
(3)
Disposable nonwoven product
The term
disposable nonwoven product
means a premoistened, nonwoven disposable wipe that has significant potential to be flushed, as determined by the Administrator.
