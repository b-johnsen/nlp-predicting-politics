2d Session
H. RES. 1276
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
July 26, 2022
Mr. Luetkemeyer
submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the
Committee on Small Business
RESOLUTION
Of inquiry directing the Administrator of the Small Business Administration to transmit certain documents to the House of Representatives relating to the Community Navigator Pilot Program.
That the Administrator of the Small Business Administration is directed to transmit to the House of Representatives, not later than 14 days after the date of the adoption of this resolution, copies of any document, memorandum, correspondence, and other communication or any portion of any such communication, that refers or relates to the Community Navigator pilot program established under section 5004 of the American Rescue Plan Act of 2021 (
15 U.S.C. 9013
), including the following:
(1)
All documents and communications relating to the mission of the Community Navigator pilot program.
(2)
All documents and communications relating to the use of funds of the Community Navigator pilot program to direct small business concerns (as defined under section 3 of the Small Business Act (
15 U.S.C. 632
)) to private sector resources.
(3)
All documents and communications relating to the evaluation of applicants for the Community Navigator pilot program, including the 20 point scoring metric used to assess such applicants.
(4)
All documents and communications relating to the specific assistance available to small business concerns by and through the Community Navigator pilot program.
(5)
Copies of each agreement that the Administrator entered into with respect to a grant made under section 5004(b)(1) of the American Rescue Plan Act of 2021 (
15 U.S.C. 9013(b)(1)
).
(6)
Copies of each contract and cooperative agreement that the Administrator entered into under such section 5004(b)(1).
