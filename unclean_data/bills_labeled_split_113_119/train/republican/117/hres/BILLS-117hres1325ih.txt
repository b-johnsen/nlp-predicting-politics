RESOLUTION
Of inquiry requesting the President and directing Attorney General Merrick B. Garland to transmit, respectively, a copy of the affidavit to the House of Representatives related to the raid on the former President.
That the President is requested, and the Attorney General Merrick B. Garland is directed, to transmit, respectively, to the House of Representatives, not later than 14 days after the date of adoption of this resolution, a copy of the affidavit submitted for the warrant on the former President, to the extent that any such affidavit is within the possession of the President or the Department of Justice, respectively.
