JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of a rule submitted by the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration relating to civil penalties.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration relating to civil penalties published on April 1, 2022 (87 Fed. Reg. 18994), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
