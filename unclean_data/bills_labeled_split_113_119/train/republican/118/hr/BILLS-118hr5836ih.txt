1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Deterring Attempts at Dirty Deals by Youngsters Act
or the
DADDY Act
.
2.
Limit on foreign employment of immediately family of certain officials
(a)
In general
An immediate family member of a covered person may not be employed by a foreign company.
(b)
Penalty
Any individual who willfully violates subsection (a) shall be fined not more than $250,000 or imprisoned for not more than 5 years, or both.
(c)
Effective date
This Act shall take effect on the date that is two weeks after the date of the enactment of this Act.
