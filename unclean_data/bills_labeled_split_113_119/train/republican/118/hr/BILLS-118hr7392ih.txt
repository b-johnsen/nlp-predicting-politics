1.
Reprogramming of amounts appropriated for UNRWA
(a)
In general
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, all Federal funds that, as of the date of the enactment of this Act, have been appropriated for, but not provided to, the United Nations Relief and Works Agency for Palestine Refugees in the Near East shall be made available for the construction of a permanent physical barrier along the southwest border of the United States.
(b)
Prohibition of future funding
Beginning on the date of the enactment of this Act, no Federal funds may be provided to the United Nations Relief and Work Agency for Palestine Refugees in the Near East.
