1.
Colonel Hans Christian Heg Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located S74w16860 Janesville Road in Muskego, Wisconsin, shall be known and designated as the
Colonel Hans Christian Heg Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Colonel Hans Christian Heg Post Office
.
