1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Air Reserve Component Fighter Recapitalization Act of 2023
.
2.
Limitation on termination of fighter squadrons
(a)
Limitation
The Secretary of the Air Force may not terminate the fighter flying mission of any fighter squadron of the Air National Guard or the Air Force Reserve until a period of 180 days has elapsed following the date on which the Secretary submits the plan required under subsection (b).
(b)
Plan required
(1)
In general
The Secretary of the Air Force, in coordination with the Director of the Air National Guard and the Commander of the Air Force Reserve, shall develop a notional plan for the recapitalization of all fighter squadrons of the Air National Guard and the Air Force Reserve.
