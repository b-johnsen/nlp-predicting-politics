1.
Nell Patten Roquemore Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 15 South Valdosta Road in Lakeland, Georgia, shall be known and designated as the
Nell Patten Roquemore Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Nell Patten Roquemore Post Office
.
