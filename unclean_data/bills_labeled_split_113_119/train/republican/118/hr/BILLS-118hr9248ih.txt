1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Railroad Responsibility Act of 2024
.
2.
State authority to limit blocking highway-Rail grade crossings
(a)
General jurisdiction of Surface Transportation Board
Section 10501(c)(3) of title 49, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
(C)
State authority To limit blocking highway-Rail grade crossings
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, this title does not preempt a State from the adoption or enactment of any law, regulation, order, or other requirement related to limiting the duration that a railroad carrier may block a highway-rail grade crossing.
.
(b)
Preemption
Section 20106(a) of title 49, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
(3)
State authority To limit blocking highway-Rail grade crossings
(A)
In general
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, this title does not preempt a State from the adoption or enactment of any law, regulation, order, or other requirement related to limiting the duration that a railroad carrier may block a highway-rail grade crossing.
(B)
State defined
In this paragraph, the term
State
means a State of the United States and the District of Columbia.
.
