1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
No Tolls on Oregon Roads Act of 2023
.
2.
Prohibition on use of funds for tolling on Interstate Route 5 or Interstate Route 205
(a)
Congestion relief program
Section 129(d)(6) of title 23, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
(D)
Exception
The Secretary shall not allow the use of tolls on Interstate Route 5 or Interstate Route 205 in the State of Oregon as part of a project carried out with a grant under the program.
.
(b)
Toll roads, bridges, tunnels, and ferries
Section 129 of title 23, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
(e)
Prohibition on tolling on certain Interstates
Notwithstanding this section or any other provision of law, the Secretary may not approve Federal participation in the tolling of Interstate Route 5 or Interstate Route 205 in the State of Oregon.
.
(c)
Value pricing pilot program
Section 1012(b)(4) of the Intermodal Surface Transportation Efficiency Act of 1991 (
23 U.S.C. 129
note(b)) is amended by inserting
, except that the Secretary shall not allow tolling of Interstate Route 5 or Interstate Route 205 in the State of Oregon
before the period at the end.
