1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Stop Executive Capture of Banking Regulators Act
.
2.
Report on the implementation of recommendations from the FSOC Chairperson and Executive Orders
(a)
Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System
Section 10 of the Federal Reserve Act (
12 U.S.C. 247b
) is amended by inserting after paragraph (10) the following:
(11)
Report on the implementation of recommendations from the FSOC Chairperson and Executive Orders
The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System may not implement a non-binding recommendation made by the Chairperson of the Financial Stability Oversight Council or contained in an Executive Order unless the Board of Governors first provides the Committee on Financial Services of the House of Representatives and the Committee on Banking, Housing, and Urban Affairs of the Senate withâ
(A)
notice that the Board of Governors intends to implement such recommendation;
(B)
a report containing the proposed implementation by the Board of Governors and a justification for such implementation; and
(C)
upon request, not later than the end of the 120-day period beginning on the date of the notice under subparagraph (A), testimony on such proposed implementation.
.
