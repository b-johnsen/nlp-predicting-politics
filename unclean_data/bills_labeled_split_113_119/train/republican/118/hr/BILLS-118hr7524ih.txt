1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
GSA Technology Accountability Act
.
2.
Transparency of GSA funded information technology services
(a)
Transparency on use of the Federal Citizen Services Fund
Section 323 of title 40, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
(g)
Requirement for annual report to Congress
The Administrator shall submit a report to the Committee on Oversight and Accountability of the House of Representatives and the Committee on Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs of the Senate not later than September 30 of each year, which shall include, at a minimum, the following:
(1)
A list of each project funded by expenditures during the previous year, or that have been funded by expenditures in the previous five-year period, including the following:
(A)
An explanation of the project.
(B)
An explanation of the projectâs progress, including a description of any major changes to project scope, project cost, or project funding; a description of any project delays; and an explanation for any abandoned or failed projects.
(C)
The date of project initiation.
(D)
The date of project completion, if applicable, or the anticipated date of project completion if the project is ongoing.
(E)
The total cost for the project, including the total cost to be covered through expenditures, or the estimated total cost for the project and anticipated total cost to be covered through expenditures if the project is ongoing.
(F)
The amount of reimbursements associated with the project from another source of funds or another agency, if applicable, or the amount of reimbursements anticipated to be associated with the project, if applicable.
(G)
Any additional information, data, or analysis used to determine the information estimated within the report, if applicable.
(2)
For a project that does not have any associated reimbursement or estimated associated reimbursement, an explanation for the absence of associated reimbursements, an analysis of the need for the project, and an estimate of the number of General Services Administration personnel responsible for supporting the project.
(h)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)
Administrator
The term
Administrator
means the Administrator of General Services.
(2)
Expenditure
The term
expenditure
means any obligation of funds from the Fund.
(3)
Project
The term
project
means services provided by the General Services Administration that are funded in whole or in part by an expenditure.
.
