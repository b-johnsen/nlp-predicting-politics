1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Chinese Electronics Defense Act of 2023
.
2.
Prohibition on computers or printers acquisitions involving entities owned or controlled by china
(a)
In general
The Secretary of Defense may not acquire any computer or printer if the manufacturer, bidder, or offeror is a covered Chinese entity.
(b)
Applicability
This section shall apply only with respect to contracts or other agreements entered into, renewed, or extended after the date of the enactment of this Act.
(c)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)
Covered Chinese entity
