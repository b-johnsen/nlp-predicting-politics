1.
Samuel J. Mitchell Jr. Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 265 Main Street in Philo, Ohio, shall be known and designated as the
Samuel J. Mitchell Jr. Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Samuel J. Mitchell Jr. Post Office
.
