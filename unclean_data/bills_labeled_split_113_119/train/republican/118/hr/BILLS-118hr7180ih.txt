1.
Kingsland
Johnny Cash
Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 80 1st Street in Kingsland, Arkansas, shall be known and designated as the
Kingsland
Johnny Cash
Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Kingsland
Johnny Cash
Post Office
.
