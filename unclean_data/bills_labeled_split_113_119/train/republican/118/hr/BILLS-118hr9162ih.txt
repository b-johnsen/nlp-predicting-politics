(1)
In general
The Commission shall, by rule, require each covered investment adviser, for each private fund advised by the covered investment advisor, to disclose to the private fund and the Commission, every quarter, a plain English report that outlines the anticipated effects, to the extent it would be material to investors, of a scenario of the type described in section 13(t)(2) of the Securities and Exchange Act of 1934 on the returns of such fund.
