319.
Public Participation Division
(a)
In general
There shall be a division within the Office, to be known as the Public Participation Division, administered by a Director appointed by the Director of the Office.
(b)
Rate of pay
(1)
Director
The Director shall be compensated at an equivalent rate of basic pay prescribed for a director in the Office.
(2)
Employees
The Director may appoint, and assign the duties of, employees of such Division, and, with the concurrence of the Office, fix the compensation of such employees and procure temporary and intermittent services to the same extent as is authorized under section 3109 of title 5, United States Code.
