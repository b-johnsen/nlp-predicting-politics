(b)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)
Covered banking institution
The term
covered banking institution
means an insured depository institution or depository institution holding company.
(2)
Interest rate swap
With respect to a covered banking institution, the term
interest rate swap
means a swap entered into by the covered banking institution for the purpose of hedging against the interest rate risk of debt securities or loans held on the balance sheet of the covered banking institution.
