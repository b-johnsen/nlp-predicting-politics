1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
No Gun Rights Infringement Sham Act
or the
No GRISHAM Act
.
2.
Availability of Federal financial assistance for States that suspend open or concealed firearm carry licenses
Any State or local government that suspends open or concealed firearm carry licenses may not receive any Federal financial assistance (as such term is defined in section 7501(a)(5) of title 31, United States Code).
