1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Energy Choice Act
.
2.
Limitations on regulation of energy choice
(a)
In general
No State or local government, or instrumentality or regulatory agency thereof, may adopt a law, ordinance, building code, or efficiency standard that prohibits, or has the effect of prohibiting, the connection, reconnection, modification, installation, or expansion of an energy service based on the type or source of energy to be delivered to an end-user of such energy service.
