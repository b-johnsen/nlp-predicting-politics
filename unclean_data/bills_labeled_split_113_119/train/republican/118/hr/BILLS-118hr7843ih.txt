1.
Short title
This Act may be referred to as the
Guam EPA Cooperation Act
.
2.
Authority to enter into Memorandum Of Understanding with the Environmental Protection Agency
The Secretary of Defense may enter into a Memorandum of Understanding with the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency to provide technical assistance and carry out both federally and locally required environmental inspections for military construction projects in the territory of Guam. Such memorandum of understanding may include or be carried out in consultation with the Guam Environmental Protection Agency. The Department of Defense may compensate the U.S. EPA for activities carried out pursuant to such memorandum of understanding.
