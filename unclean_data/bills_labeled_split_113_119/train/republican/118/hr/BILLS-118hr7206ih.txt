1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
UNRWA FTO Designation Act
.
2.
Fto designation
Not later than 10 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Secretary of State shall designate the United Nations Relief and Works Agency as a foreign terrorist organization pursuant to section 219(a) of the Immigration and Nationality Act (
8 U.S.C. 1189(a)
).
