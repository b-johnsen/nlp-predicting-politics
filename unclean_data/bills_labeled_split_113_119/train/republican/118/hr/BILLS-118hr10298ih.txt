1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Keep our Correctional Officers Paid Act
.
2.
Payment of salaries of certain Bureau of Prisons employees working during a Federal Government shutdown
Out of any money in the Treasury not otherwise appropriated, there is appropriated such sums as are necessary to pay, during any period of a lapse in discretionary appropriations during any fiscal year beginning on or after the date of the enactment of this section, the salaries and expenses of any correctional staff of the Bureau of Prisons excepted from furlough during such lapse.
