2.
Siting of interstate electric transmission facilities under the Federal Power Act
(a)
Elimination of right of eminent domain; prohibition of use of Federal funds
(1)
In general
Section 216 of the Federal Power Act (
16 U.S.C. 824p
) is amended by striking subsection (e) and inserting the following:
(e)
No right of eminent domain
Effective beginning on the date of enactment of the
Protecting Our Land from Federal Overreach Act of 2024
, no Federal funds may be used for any effort to exercise the right of eminent domain for electric transmission facilities under any provision of law.
