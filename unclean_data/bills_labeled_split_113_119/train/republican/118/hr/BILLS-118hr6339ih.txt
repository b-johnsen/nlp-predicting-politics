1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
States Choose Common Sense Act of 2023
.
2.
Prohibition on withholding or revoking Federal funds based on the definition of
sex
used by a State
(a)
Prohibition
No Federal funds authorized to be made available to a State may be withheld or revoked on the basis of the State having in place any law, regulation, or policy that defines
sex
based on the biological sex of an individual, as determined by the anatomy or genetics of the individual at the time of birth.
(b)
Definition
In this Act, the term
State
means the following:
(1)
The 50 States of the United States.
(2)
The District of Columbia.
(3)
A territory or possession of the United States.
(4)
Any political subdivision, instrumentality, or authority of a State.
