1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Continuing Opportunities for People with Disabilities to Excel Act
.
2.
Withdrawal of proposed rule relating to section 14(c) certificates
The Secretary of Labor shall withdraw the proposed rule titled
Employment of Workers With Disabilities Under Section 14(c) of the Fair Labor Standards Act
(89 Fed. Reg. 96466; published December 4, 2024).
