1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Enforcing the Rule of Law on Campus Act
.
2.
Ineligibility of colleges and universities that employ illegal aliens to receive Federal funding
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, any institution of higher education (as defined in section 102 of the Higher Education Act of 1965 (
20 U.S.C. 1002
)) with a policy or practice of employing aliens who are unlawfully present in the United States at any campus of such institution in violation of section 274A of the Immigration and Nationality Act (
8 U.S.C. 1324a
) shall be ineligible to receive any Federal funding.
