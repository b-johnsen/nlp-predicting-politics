1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Medicare Multi-Cancer Early Detection Screening Coverage Act
.
2.
Purpose
The purpose of this Act is to create a covered benefit for multi-cancer early detection screening tests to ensure Medicare beneficiary access to these tests without unnecessary delay once approved under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act.
