1.
Sergeant Major Billy D. Waugh Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 1106 Main Street in Bastrop, Texas, shall be known and designated as the
Sergeant Major Billy D. Waugh Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Sergeant Major Billy D. Waugh Post Office
.
