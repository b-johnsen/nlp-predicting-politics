1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Farmers Freedom Act of 2023
.
2.
Prior converted cropland
For purposes of the definition of the term
waters of the United States
under the Federal Water Pollution Control Act (
33 U.S.C. 1251 et seq.
) and exclusions from that term, including under section 328.3 of title 33 and section 120.2 of title 40, Code of Federal Regulations (and any successor regulations), the Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency and the Secretary of the Army, acting through the Chief of Engineers, shall ensure that the term
prior converted cropland
has the meaning given the term in the final rule of the Corps of Engineers and the Environmental Protection Agency entitled
The Navigable Waters Protection Rule: Definition of
Waters of the United States
(85 Fed. Reg. 22250 (April 21, 2020)).
