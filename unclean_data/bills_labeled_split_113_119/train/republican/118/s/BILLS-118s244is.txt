1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Stop Trying to Obsessively Vilify Energy Act
or the
STOVE Act
.
2.
Prohibition on promulgating any rule or guidance that bans gas stoves in the United States
No Federal agency may propose, implement, or finalize a rule or guidance that categorically restricts or bans the use and purchase of natural gas-powered appliances in the United States.
