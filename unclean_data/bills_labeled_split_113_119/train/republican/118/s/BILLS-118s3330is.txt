(c)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)
Bona fide service fees
The term
bona fide service fees
means fees paid by a manufacturer, customer, or client (other than a group health plan or health insurance issuer) of an entity providing pharmacy benefit management services, to an entity providing pharmacy benefit management services, that represent fair-market value for bona fide, itemized services actually performed on behalf of the manufacturer, customer, or client and that the manufacturer, customer, or client would otherwise perform or contract for in the absence of the service arrangement, and that are not passed on in whole or in part to a client or customer of an entity, whether or not the entity takes possession of the drug.
