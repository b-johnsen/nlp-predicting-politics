(C)
Data
(i)
Unemployment compensation data
A State that receives an allotment under this subtitle shall facilitate access for local boards in the State to unemployment compensation data, at the individual level, for participants in training services, to enable the local boards to meet the requirements of this section and section 122.
(ii)
Wage data
A State that receives an allotment under this subtitle shall facilitate access for providers of training services in the State to wage data for participants in training services, to enable the providers to meet the requirements of this section and section 122.
(iii)
Privacy
In facilitating access under this subparagraph, a State shall not reduce any protections required of or afforded by the State concerning the privacy of information on the individuals covered by the data involved.
