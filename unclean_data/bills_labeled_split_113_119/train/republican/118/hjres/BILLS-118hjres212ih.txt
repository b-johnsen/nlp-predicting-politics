JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Financial Crimes Enforcement Network relating to
Anti-Money Laundering Regulations for Residential Real Estate Transfers
.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Financial Crimes Enforcement Network relating to
Anti-Money Laundering Regulations for Residential Real Estate Transfers
(89 Fed. Reg. 70258 (August 29, 2024)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
