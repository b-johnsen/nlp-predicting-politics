JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Department of Defense, the General Services Administration, and the National Aeronautics and Space Administration relating to the
Federal Acquisition Regulation: Use of Project Labor Agreements for Federal Construction Projects
.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Department of Defense, the General Services Administration, and the National Aeronautics and Space Administration relating to the
Federal Acquisition Regulation: Use of Project Labor Agreements for Federal Construction Projects
(88 Fed. Reg. 88708 (December 22, 2023)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
