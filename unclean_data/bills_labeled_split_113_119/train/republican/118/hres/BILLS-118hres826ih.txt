1.
Limitation on privileged status of motion to vacate chair
Clause 2(a) of rule IX of the Rules of the House of Representatives is amended by adding at the end the following new subparagraph:
(3)
A resolution causing a vacancy in the Office of Speaker shall not be privileged except if offered by direction of not fewer than 112 Members from the majority party or 112 Members from the minority party.
