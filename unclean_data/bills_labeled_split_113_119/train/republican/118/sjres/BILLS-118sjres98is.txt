JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Department of Agriculture relating to
Use of Electronic Identification Eartags as Official Identification in Cattle and Bison
.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Department of Agriculture relating to
Use of Electronic Identification Eartags as Official Identification in Cattle and Bison
(89 Fed. Reg. 39540 (May 9, 2024)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
