JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the multiple agencies relating to
Partnerships With Faith-Based and Neighborhood Organizations
.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Department of Education, the Department of Homeland Security, the Department of Agriculture, the Agency for International Development, the Department of Housing and Urban Development, the Department of Justice, the Department of Labor, the Department of Veterans Affairs, and the Department of Health and Human Services relating to
Partnerships With Faith-Based and Neighborhood Organizations
(89 Fed. Reg. 15671 (March 4, 2024)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
