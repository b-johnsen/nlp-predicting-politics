TSA misconduct inspections
(1)
In general
Not later than 60 days after the date of the enactment of this subsection, the Administrator of the Transportation Security Administration (TSA) shallâ

(2)
Department of Homeland Security review
(A)
In general
On a quarterly basis, the official specified in paragraph (1)(A) shall provide to the Chief Human Capital Officer of the Department of Homeland Security the results of unannounced inspections conducted pursuant to such paragraph.
(B)
Identification
The Chief Human Capital Officer of the Department of Homeland Security shall review the results of the unannounced inspections conducted pursuant to paragraph (1)(A) to identify trends and, as appropriate, make recommendations, including timeframes for implementation, to the Administrator of the TSA to address employee misconduct.
(C)
Implementation
The Administrator of the TSA shall implement recommendations made by the Chief Human Capital Officer of the Department of Homeland Security within the timeframes established by the Chief Human Capital Officer.
