1.
Howard B. Pate, Jr. Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 225 North Main Street in Spring Lake, North Carolina, shall be known and designated as the
Howard B. Pate, Jr. Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Howard B. Pate, Jr. Post Office
.
