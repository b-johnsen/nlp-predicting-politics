1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Stop EPA Overregulation of Rural Americans Act
.
2.
Repeal of rule for new residential wood heaters
The final rule entitled
Standards of Performance for New Residential Wood Heaters, New Residential Hydronic Heaters and Forced-Air Furnaces
published at 80 Fed. Reg. 13672 (March 16, 2015) shall have no force or effect and shall be treated as if such rule had never been issued.
