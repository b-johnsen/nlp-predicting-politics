1.
Grazing advisory boards reauthorized
(a)
Short title
This section may be cited as the
Grazing Advisory Board Reauthorization Act
.
(b)
Reauthorization of grazing advisory boards
Section 403(f) of the Federal Land Policy and Management Act of 1976 (
43 U.S.C. 1753(f)
) is amended by striking
December 31, 1985
and inserting
on the date that is 10 years after the date of the enactment of the Grazing Advisory Board Reauthorization Act
.
