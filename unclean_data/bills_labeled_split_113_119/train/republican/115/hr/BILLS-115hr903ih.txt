1.
Restriction on funding for the Preparatory Commission for the Comprehensive Nuclear-Test-Ban Treaty Organization
(a)
Statement of policy
Congress declares that United Nations Security Council Resolution 2310 (September 23, 2016) does not obligate the United States nor does it impose an obligation on the United States to refrain from actions that would run counter to the object and purpose of the Comprehensive Nuclear-Test-Ban Treaty.
(b)
Restriction on funding
(1)
In general
No United States funds may be made available to the Preparatory Commission for the Comprehensive Nuclear-Test-Ban Treaty Organization.
(2)
Exception
The restriction under paragraph (1) shall not apply with respect to the availability of United States funds for the Comprehensive Nuclear-Test-Ban Treaty Organizationâs International Monitoring System.
