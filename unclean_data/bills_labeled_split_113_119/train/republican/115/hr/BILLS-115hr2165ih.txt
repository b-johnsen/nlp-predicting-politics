(e)
Chief Human Capital Officer
(1)
In general
Not later than 60 days after receipt of the TSA employee code of conduct policy and the table of offenses and penalties under subsection (a), the Chief Human Capital Officer of the Department of Homeland Security shall review such policy and such table to ensure that such policy and such table align, as appropriate, with the policies and tables developed by the Chief Human Capital Officer for the Department.
(2)
Monitoring
The Chief Human Capital Officer of the Department of Homeland Security shall, as part of existing working groups established by such Officer, monitor through fiscal year 2020 the implementation under subsection (b) of the TSA employee code of conduct.
