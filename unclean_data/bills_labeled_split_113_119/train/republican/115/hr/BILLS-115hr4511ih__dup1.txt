1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
No Golden Parachutes for Public Service Act
.
2.
Prohibition against lobbying of Congress by former Members
(a)
Senators
Section 207(e)(1)(A) of title 18, United States Code, is amended by striking
within 2 years
.
(b)
Members and Officers of the House of Representatives
Section 207(e)(1)(B)(i) of such title is amended by striking
within 1 year
.
3.
Effective date
The amendments made by this Act shall apply with respect to individuals who leave office on or after the date of the enactment of this Act.
