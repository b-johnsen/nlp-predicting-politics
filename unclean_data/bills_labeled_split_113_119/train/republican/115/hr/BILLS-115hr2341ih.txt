1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Seeking an End to Restrictions on Votive Employees Act
or as the
SERVE Act
.
2.
Clarification of retirement income account rules relating to church-controlled organizations
(a)
In general
Subparagraph (B) of
section 403(b)(9)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by inserting
(including an employee described in section 414(e)(3)(B))
after
employee described in paragraph (1)
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by this section shall apply to years beginning before, on, or after the date of the enactment of this Act.
