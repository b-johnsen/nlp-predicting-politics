(b)
Qualifications To work on the staff or advice
(1)
Contributions while serving
It disqualifies a person for further advising or working on the staff of an officer or employee to whom this section applies that such person makes a contribution to a candidate for elective office or to a political party during such service.
(2)
Statement under penalty of perjury required
Before taking up the duties of working in the staff of an officer to whom this section applies, a person must sign, under penalty of perjury, a statement that such person has not made, within the 8-year period ending on the date such person begins such duties, a contribution to a candidate for elective office or to a political party.
