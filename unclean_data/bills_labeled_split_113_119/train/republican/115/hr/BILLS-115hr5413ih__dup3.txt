1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Improving Veterans Access to Congressional Services Act of 2018
.
2.
Use of facilities of the Department of Veterans Affairs by Members of Congress
(a)
In general
Upon request of a Member of Congress and subject to regulations prescribed under subsection (b), the Secretary of Veterans Affairs shall permit the Member to use a facility of the Department of Veterans Affairs for the purposes of meeting with constituents of the Member.
