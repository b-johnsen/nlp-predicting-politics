1.
Sgt. Josh Rodgers Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 1211 Towanda Avenue in Bloomington, Illinois, shall be known and designated as the
Sgt. Josh Rodgers Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Sgt. Josh Rodgers Post Office
.
