JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5, United States Code, of the final rule of the Bureau of Land Management relating to
Onshore Oil and Gas Operations; Federal and Indian Oil and Gas Leases; Measurement of Oil
.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Bureau of Land Management relating to
Onshore Oil and Gas Operations; Federal and Indian Oil and Gas Leases; Measurement of Oil
(81 Fed. Reg. 81462 (November 17, 2016)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
