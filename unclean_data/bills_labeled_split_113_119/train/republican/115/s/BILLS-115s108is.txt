1.
Short
title
This Act may be cited as the
Medical Device Access and Innovation Protection Act
.
2.
Repeal of
medical device excise tax
(a)
In
general
Chapter 32
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking subchapter E.
(b)
Conforming
amendments
(1)
Subsection (a) of section 4221 of such Code is amended by striking the last sentence.
(2)
Paragraph (2) of section 6416(b) of such Code is amended by striking the last sentence.
(c)
Clerical
amendment
The table of subchapters for chapter 32 of such Code is amended by striking the item related to subchapter E.
(d)
Effective
date
The amendments made by this section shall apply to sales after December 31, 2017.
