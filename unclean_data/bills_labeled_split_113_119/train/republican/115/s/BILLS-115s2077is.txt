1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Vehicular Terrorism Prevention Act of 2017
.
2.
Report on vehicular terrorism
(a)
Definition
In this section, the term
vehicular terrorism
means an action that uses automotive transportation to inflict violence and intimidation on individuals for a political purpose.
(b)
Report

(5)
an examination of actions Congress can take to help the Department of Homeland Security mitigate the threat of vehicular terrorism.
