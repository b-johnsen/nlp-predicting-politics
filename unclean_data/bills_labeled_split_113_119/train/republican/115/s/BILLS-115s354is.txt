Invalidity of certain petitions and applications
Any petition under section 204 of the Immigration and Nationality Act (
8 U.S.C. 1154
) seeking classification of an alien under a family-sponsored immigrant category that was eliminated by the amendments made by this section and filed after the date on which this Act was introduced and any application for an immigrant visa based on such a petition shall be considered invalid.
