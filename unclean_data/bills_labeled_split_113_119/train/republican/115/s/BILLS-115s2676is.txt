1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Saving Lives Through Proper Notification of Overdoses Act
.
2.
Communication with families during emergencies
(a)
Promoting awareness of authorized disclosures during emergencies
The Secretary of Health and Human Services shall annually notify health care providers regarding permitted disclosures during emergencies, including overdoses, of certain health information to families and caregivers under Federal health care privacy laws and regulations.

Use of material
