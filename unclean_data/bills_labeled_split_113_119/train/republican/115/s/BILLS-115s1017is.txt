1.
Edward T. Schafer Agricultural Research Center
(a)
Designation
The Red River Valley Agricultural Research Center in Fargo, North Dakota, shall be known and designated as the
Edward T. Schafer Agricultural Research Center
.
(b)
References
Any reference in any law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Edward T. Schafer Agricultural Research Center.
