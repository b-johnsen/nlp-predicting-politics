1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Waterway LNG Parity Act of 2017
.
2.
Liquefied natural gas equivalent for purposes of Inland Waterways Trust Fund financing rate
(a)
In general
Section 4042(b)(2)(A)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended to read as follows:
(A)
The Inland Waterways Trust Fund financing rate is 29 cents per gallon (per energy equivalent of a gallon of diesel, in the case of liquefied natural gas).
.
(b)
Energy equivalent of a gallon of diesel
Section 4042(b)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by adding at the end the following:
(5)
Energy equivalent of a gallon of diesel with respect to liquefied natural gas
For purposes of paragraph (2)(A), the term
energy equivalent of a gallon of diesel
means 6.06 pounds of liquefied natural gas.
.
(c)
Effective date
The amendments made by this section shall apply to any sale or use of fuel during any calendar quarter beginning after the date of the enactment of this Act.
