1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Improving Investment Research for Small and Emerging Issuers Act
.
2.
Research study
(a)
Study required
The Securities and Exchange Commission shall conduct a study to evaluate the issues affecting the provision of, and reliance upon, investment research into small issuers (referred to in this section as
covered research
