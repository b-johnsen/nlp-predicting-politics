JOINT RESOLUTION
Proposing an amendment to the Constitution of the United States to provide that Representatives
shall be apportioned among the several States according to their
respective numbers, counting the number of persons in each State who are
citizens of the United States.
That the following article is proposed as an amendment to the Constitution of the United States, which shall be valid to all intents and purposes as part of the Constitution when ratified by the legislatures of three-fourths of the several States within seven years after the date of its submission for ratification:

Representatives shall be apportioned among the several States according to their respective numbers, which shall be determined by counting the number of persons in each State who are citizens of the United States.
