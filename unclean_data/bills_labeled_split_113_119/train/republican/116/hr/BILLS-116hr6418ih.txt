(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 509 Fairhope Avenue in Fairhope, Alabama, shall be known and designated as the
William
Jack
Jackson Edwards III Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
William
Jack
Jackson Edwards III Post Office Building
.
