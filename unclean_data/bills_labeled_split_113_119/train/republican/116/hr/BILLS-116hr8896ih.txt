1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Abandoning Online Censorship Act
or the
AOC Act
.
2.
Repeal of section 230 of the Communications Act of 1934 (commonly referred to as the Communications Decency Act)
(a)
Section 230 of the Communications Act of 1934
Effective as of enactment of this language, section 230 of the Communications Act of 1934 (
47 U.S.C. 230(c)
) is repealed.
