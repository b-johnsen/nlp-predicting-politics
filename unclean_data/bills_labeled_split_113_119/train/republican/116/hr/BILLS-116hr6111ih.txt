1.
Election to recalculate production history
Section 1405(b) of the Agricultural Act of 2014 (
7 U.S.C. 9055(b)
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(4)
Election to recalculate production history
In the case of a participating dairy operation not described in paragraph (1) or (2), the participating dairy operation may make a 1-time election to recalculate the production history of the participating dairy operation to be equal to the annual milk marketings of the dairy operation during the full calendar year before the date of the enactment of this paragraph.
