1.
Reimbursement of interest payments related to public assistance
Title IV of the Robert T. Stafford Disaster Relief and Emergency Assistance Act is amended by adding at the end the following:
431.
Reimbursement of interest payments related to public assistance
(a)
In general
The President may provide financial assistance to a local government as reimbursement for qualifying interest.
