6.
Regulations
(a)
Rules implementing new requirements
Not later than 90 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Secretary of Health and Human Services shall promulgate rules on the record to carry out the amendments made by this Act, in accordance with
chapter 5
of title 5, United States Code.
(b)
Other rules
The Secretary of Health and Human Services shall promulgate rules on the record to carry out any of the provisions of section 503A of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (
21 U.S.C. 353a
) other than those amended by this Act, in accordance with
