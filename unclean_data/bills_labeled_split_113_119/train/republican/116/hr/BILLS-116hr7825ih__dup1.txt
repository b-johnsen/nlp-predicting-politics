3.

(a)
HSA
Paragraph (2) of
section 223(d)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986, as amended by the preceding provisions of this Act, is amended by adding at the end the following new subparagraph:
(I)
Disallowance of costs for abortion
Such term shall not include any amounts paid for any abortion or premiums of any health insurance that includes coverage of abortion, other thanâ
(i)
in the case of a physical disorder, physical injury, or physical illness which would, as certified by a physician, place the pregnant individual in danger of death unless an abortion is performed, including a life-endangering physical condition caused by or arising from the pregnancy itself, and
(ii)
in the case of a pregnancy resulting from an act of rape or incest.
.
