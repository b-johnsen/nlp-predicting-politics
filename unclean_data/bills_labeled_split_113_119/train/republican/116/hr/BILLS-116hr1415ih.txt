1.
Designation
The Federal Building and United States Courthouse located at 401 Southeast 1st Avenue, Gainesville, Florida, shall be known and designated as the
Maurice M. Paul Federal Building and United States Courthouse
.
2.
Reference
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the Federal Building and United States Courthouse referred to in section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Maurice M. Paul Federal Building and United States Courthouse
.
