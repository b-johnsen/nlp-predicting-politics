RESOLUTION
Affirming that States maintain primacy for the regulation of hydraulic fracturing for oil and
natural gas production on State and private lands, that the President has
no authority to declare a moratorium on the use of hydraulic fracturing on
State and private lands, and that the President should not attempt to
declare a moratorium on the use of hydraulic fracturing on Federal lands
(including the Outer Continental Shelf) or lands held in trust for an
Indian Tribe, unless the moratorium is authorized by an Act of Congress.
