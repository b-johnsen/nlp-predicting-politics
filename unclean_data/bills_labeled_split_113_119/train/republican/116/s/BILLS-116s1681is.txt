(b)
Continuing medical education
The Secretary shall advance education and awareness among health care providers regarding biosimilar biological products, including by developing or improving continuing medical education programs that advance the education of such providers on the prescribing of, and relevant clinical considerations with respect to, biosimilar biological products.
