Reduction relating to after-Tax income

(9)
Reduction relating to increase in net assets of nonprofit organizations
For an eligible recipient that is a nonprofit organization, the amount of loan forgiveness under this section shall be reduced by the amount of the increase, if any, in the net assets of the eligible recipient during the 1-year period beginning on the date of issuance of the covered loan, up to the amount of the covered loan amount.
