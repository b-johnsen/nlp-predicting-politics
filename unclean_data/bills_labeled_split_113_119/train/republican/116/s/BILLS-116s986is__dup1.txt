1.
Release of reversionary interest
The United States releases to the State of Arkansas the reversionary interest described in sections 2 and 3 of the Act entitled
An Act authorizing the transfer of part of Camp Joseph T. Robinson to the State of Arkansas
, approved June 30, 1950 (64 Stat. 311, chapter 429), in and to the surface estate of the land constituting Camp Joseph T. Robinson, Arkansas, which lies in a part of section 35, township 3 north, range 12 west, Pulaski County, Arkansas, consisting of approximately 141.52 acres.
