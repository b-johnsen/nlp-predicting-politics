1.
Joseph G. Demler Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 104 East Main Street in Port Washington, Wisconsin, shall be known and designated as the
Joseph G. Demler Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Joseph G. Demler Post Office
.
