2.
Expensing of property used to comply with Federal laws and regulations
(a)
In general
Part VI of subchapter B of
chapter 1
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by inserting after section 179E the following new section:
179F.
Election to expense property used to comply with Federal laws and regulations
(a)
In general
A taxpayer may elect to treat the cost of any Federal compliance property as an expense which is not chargeable to capital account. Any cost so treated shall be allowed as a deduction for the taxable year in which the Federal compliance property is placed in service.

Election
(1)
In general
An election under this section for any taxable year shall be made on the taxpayerâs return of the tax imposed by this chapter for the taxable year. (2)
Election irrevocable
Any election made under this section may not be revoked except with the consent of the Secretary.
