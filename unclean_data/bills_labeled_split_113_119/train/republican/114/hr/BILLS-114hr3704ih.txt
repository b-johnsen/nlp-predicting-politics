Exemption from Truth in Lending Act
Section 129E(i) of the Truth in Lending Act (
15 U.S.C. 1639e(i)
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(4)
Rule of construction related to appraisal donations
For purposes of paragraph (1), if a fee appraiser voluntarily donates appraisal services to an organization eligible to receive tax-deductible charitable contributions, such voluntary donation shall be deemed customary and reasonable.
