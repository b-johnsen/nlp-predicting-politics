1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Developing Roadway Infrastructure for a Vibrant Economy Act of 2015
or the
DRIVE Act of 2015
.
2.
Findings
Congress finds the following:
(1)
A robust national highway system is critical to the Nationâs economy.
(2)
Diverting user fees, such as the fuel tax, away from funding projects for the construction of roads, bridges, and other critical national infrastructure has jeopardized the solvency of the Highway Trust Fund.
(3)
Projects for the construction of mass transit facilities, bike paths, and other non-highway infrastructure should be funded from revenue sources other than the Highway Trust Fund.

Transportation alternatives
The term
transportation alternatives
