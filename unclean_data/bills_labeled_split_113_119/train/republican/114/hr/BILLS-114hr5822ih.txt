Department of Veterans Affairs uniform policy guidelines for lessors of property for medical facilities
(a)
Uniform policy guidelines
By not later than 180 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Secretary of Veterans Affairs shall develop uniform policy guidelines for officials, including contracting officers, of the Real Property Service of the Office of Construction and Facilities Management of the Department of Veterans Affairs in evaluating potential lessors for purposes of leasing property on which to build medical facilities. (3)

(4)
such other matters as the Secretary determines appropriate.
(b)
Consultation
In developing the guidelines under subsection (a), the Secretary shall consult with the Federal Real Property Council of the General Services Administration.
(c)
Report to Congress
Not later than 30 days before the guidelines developed under subsection (a) take effect, the Secretary shall submit to Congress a report on the guidelines.
