1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Obamacare Regressive Tax Relief Act
.
2.
Flatlining the individual mandate penalty
(a)
Percentage of income
Section 5000A(c)(2)(B)(iii)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking
2.5 percent
and inserting
1.0 percent
.
(b)
Applicable dollar amount
Section 5000A(c)(3)(A) of such Code is amended by striking
$695
and inserting
$95
.
(c)
Repeal of inflation adjustment
Section 5000A(c)(3) of such Code is amended by striking subparagraph (D).
(d)
Effective date
The amendments made by this section shall apply to taxable years beginning after December 31, 2015.
