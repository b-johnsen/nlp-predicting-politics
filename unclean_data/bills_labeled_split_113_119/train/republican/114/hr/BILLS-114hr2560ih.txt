2.
Waiver from asbestos NESHAP requirements for demolition or renovation of condemned buildings
(a)
Waiver
At the request of a State or local government, the Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency (in this section referred to as the
Administrator
) may waive any emission standard or other requirement under section 112 of the Clean Air Act (
42 U.S.C. 7412
) applicable to the control of asbestos emissions in the demolition or renovation of a building.

(c)
Time period for approval or disapproval of request for waiver
The Administrator shall approve or disapprove a request for a waiver under subsection (a) within 90 days after the date on which the request is received by the Administrator. If the Administrator fails to approve or disapprove such a request by the end of such 90-day period, the request is deemed to be approved.
