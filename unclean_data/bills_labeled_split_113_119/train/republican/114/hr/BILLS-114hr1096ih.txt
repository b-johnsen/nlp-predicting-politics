1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Real Choice for Veterans Act
.
2.
Clarification of distance requirement
Section 101(b)(2)(B) of the Veterans Access, Choice, and Accountability Act of 2014 (
38 U.S.C. 1701
note) is amended by striking
medical facility
and all that follows through the semicolon and inserting the following:
closest medical facility of the Department, including a community-based outpatient clinic, that is able to provide to the veteran the care and services that the veteran seeks;
.
