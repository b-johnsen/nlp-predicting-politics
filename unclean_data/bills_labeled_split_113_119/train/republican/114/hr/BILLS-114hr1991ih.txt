1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Federal Lands Recreation Enhancement Act Extension Act of 2015
.
2.
Extension of authority under Federal Lands Recreation Enhancement Act
Section 810 of the Federal Lands Recreation Enhancement Act (
16 U.S.C. 6809
) is amended to read as follows:
810.
Sunset provision
The authority of the Secretary to carry out this title shall terminate on September 30, 2017.
.
