1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Stop Punishing Innocent Taxpayers Act
.
2.
Restriction on recovery of certain overpayments
Section 204(a)(1)(A) of the Social Security Act (
42 U.S.C. 404(a)(1)(A)
) is amended by inserting after
section 3720A of title 31, United States Code
the following:
(except that recovery by such means of reduction in tax refunds or by any other means may not be used with respect to any payment to a person who had not attained the age of 18 at the time such payment was made)
.
