1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Families Flying Together Act of 2015
.
2.
Findings
Congress finds the following:
(1)
Airlines need to prioritize seating families traveling with small children together.
(2)
The Department of Transportation continues to receive
unsatisfactory seat assignment
complaints from parents whose seat assignments were separate from their small children.
3.

In general

(2)
make the policy described in paragraph (1) available to the public on an appropriate Internet Web site of the air carrier.
