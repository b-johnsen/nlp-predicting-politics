1.
LCpl Garrett W. Gamble, USMC Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 3130 Grants Lake Boulevard in Sugar Land, Texas, shall be known and designated as the
LCpl Garrett W. Gamble, USMC Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
LCpl Garrett W. Gamble, USMC Post Office Building
.
