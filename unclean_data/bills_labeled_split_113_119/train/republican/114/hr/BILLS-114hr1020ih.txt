2.
Definition of STEM education
For purposes of carrying out STEM education activities at the National Science Foundation, the Department of Energy, the National Aeronautics and Space Administration, the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration, the National Institute of Standards and Technology, and the Environmental Protection Agency, the term
STEM education
means education in the subjects of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics, including computer science.
