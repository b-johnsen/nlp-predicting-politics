1.
Elimination of daily newspaper cross-ownership rule
(a)
No force or effect
Paragraph (d) of section 73.3555 of title 47, Code of Federal Regulations, shall have no force or effect after the date of the enactment of this Act.
(b)
Removal from rules
Not later than 1 year after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Federal Communications Commission shall complete all actions necessary to remove such paragraph from its rules.
