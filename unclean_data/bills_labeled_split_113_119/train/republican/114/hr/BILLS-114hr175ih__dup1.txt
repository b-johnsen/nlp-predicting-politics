1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
ENERGY STAR Regulatory Improvement Act
.
2.
Third-party certification under Energy Star program
Section 324A of the Energy Policy and Conservation Act (
42 U.S.C. 6294a
) is amended by adding at
the end the following:
(e)
Third-Party certification
(1)
In general
Subject to paragraph (2), not later than 180 days after the date of enactment of this subsection,
the Administrator shall revise the certification requirements for the
labeling of consumer, home, and office electronic products for program
partners that have complied with all requirements of the Energy Star
program for a period of at least 18 months.
(2)
Administration
In the case of a program partner described in paragraph (1), the new requirements under paragraph

and performance verification for a sample of products.
(3)
Third parties
Nothing in this subsection prevents the Administrator from using third parties in the course of the
administration of the Energy Star program.
(4)
Termination
(A)
In general
Subject to subparagraph (B), an exemption from third-party certification provided to a program
partner under paragraph (1) shall terminate if the program partner is
found to have violated program requirements with respect to at least 2
separate models during a 2-year period.
(B)
Resumption
A termination for a program partner under subparagraph (A) shall cease if the program partner
complies with all Energy Star program requirements for a period of at
least 3 years.
