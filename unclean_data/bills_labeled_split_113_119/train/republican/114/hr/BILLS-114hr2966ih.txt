1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Reducing Poverty through Employment Act
.
2.
Purposes of the TANF Program to Include Reducing Poverty
Section 401(a) of the Social Security Act (
42 U.S.C. 601(a)
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(5)
reduce poverty by increasing employment entry, retention, and advancement.
.
3.
Effective date
The amendment made by this Act shall take effect on October 1, 2015.
