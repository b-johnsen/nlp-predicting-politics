2.
Facilitating State implementation of existing ozone standards
(a)
Designations
(1)
Designation Submission
Not later than October 26, 2024, notwithstanding the deadline specified in paragraph (1)(A) of section 107(d) of the Clean Air Act (
42 U.S.C. 7407(d)
), the Governor of each State shall designate in accordance with such section 107(d) all areas (or portions thereof) of the Governor芒聙聶s State as attainment, nonattainment, or unclassifiable with respect to the 2015 ozone standards.
(2)
Designation Promulgation
Not later than October 26, 2025, notwithstanding the deadline specified in paragraph (1)(B) of section 107(d) of the Clean Air Act (
42 U.S.C. 7407(d)
), the Administrator shall promulgate final designations under such section 107(d) for all areas in all States with respect to the 2015 ozone standards, including any modifications to the designations submitted under paragraph (1).
(3)
State Implementation Plans
Not later than October 26, 2026, notwithstanding the deadline specified in section 110(a)(1) of the Clean Air Act (
42 U.S.C. 7410(a)(1)
), each State shall submit the plan required by such section 110(a)(1) for the 2015 ozone standards.

Definitions
In this Act:
(1)
Administrator
The term

(4)
National ambient air quality standard
The term
national ambient air quality standard
means a national ambient air quality standard promulgated under section 109 of the Clean Air Act (

Preconstruction permit
The term
preconstruction permit
芒聙聰

2015 ozone standards
The term
2015 ozone standards
means the national ambient air quality standards for ozone published in the Federal Register on October 26, 2015 (80 Fed. Reg. 65292).
