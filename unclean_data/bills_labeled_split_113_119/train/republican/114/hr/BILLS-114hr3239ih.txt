1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Armed Forces Career Center Protection Act
.
2.
Installation of reinforced bullet-proof glass and entry doors at Armed Forces recruitment centers
The Secretary of each of the military departments, and the Secretary of the Department in which the Coast Guard is operating, shall provide for the installation of reinforced bullet-proof glass and entry doors at Armed Forces recruitment centers, including facilities leased for use as recruitment centers, under the jurisdiction of the Secretary as an additional security measure to protect members of the Armed Forces serving as recruiters and potential recruits and other individuals visiting recruitment centers.
