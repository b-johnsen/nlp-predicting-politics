1.
Limitation on resettlement of refugees
Section 412 of the Immigration and Nationality Act (
8 U.S.C. 1522
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(g)
Limitation on resettlement
(1)
In general
Notwithstanding any other provision of this section, the Director or the Federal agency administering subsection (b)(1) may not provide for the resettlement of any refugee in a fiscal year in any State where the Governor of that State has taken any action formally disapproving of the resettlement of refugees, or of certain refugees, in that State during that fiscal year.
