1.
Use of Emancipation Hall for the unveiling of the marble bust of Vice President Richard Cheney
(a)
In general
Emancipation Hall in the Capitol Visitor Center is authorized to be used for a ceremony to unveil the marble bust of Vice President Richard Cheney on December 3, 2015.
(b)
Preparations
The Architect of the Capitol and the Capitol Police Board shall take such action as may be necessary with respect to physical preparations and security for the ceremony described in subsection (a).
