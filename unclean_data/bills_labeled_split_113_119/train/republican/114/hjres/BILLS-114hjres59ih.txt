JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule
submitted by the Army Corps of Engineers and the Environmental Protection
Agency relating to the definition of
waters of the United States
under the Clean Water Act.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Army Corps of Engineers and the Environmental Protection Agency relating to the definition of
waters of the United States
under the Clean Water Act (80 Fed. Reg. 37053), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
