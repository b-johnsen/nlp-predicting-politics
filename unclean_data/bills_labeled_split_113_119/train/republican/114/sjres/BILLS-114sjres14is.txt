S. J. RES. 14
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES

Mr. Paul

Committee on Commerce, Science, and Transportation
JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule
submitted by the Federal Communications Commission regulating broadband
Internet access.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Federal Communications Commission relating to regulating broadband Internet access (published at 80 Fed. Reg. 19737 (April 13, 2015)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
