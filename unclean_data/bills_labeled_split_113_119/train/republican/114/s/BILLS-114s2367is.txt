(b)
Hardship pay
In addition to any other compensation to which a border patrol agent or customs and border protection officer is otherwise entitled, a border patrol agent or customs and border protection officer who is assigned to highly-trafficked rural areas shall be entitled to receive hardship duty pay.
(c)
Establishment of rate
The Commissioner of U.S. Customs and Border Protection shall establish the rate of hardship duty pay for purposes of subsection (b), which may not exceed the rate of special pay to which members of a uniformed service are entitled under section 310 of title 37.
