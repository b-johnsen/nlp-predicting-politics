This Act may be cited as the

2013
.
2.
Findings
The Congress finds the following:
(1)
Liquefied natural gas has an energy content
of 74,700 Btu per gallon (lower heating value).
(2)
Diesel has an
energy content of 128,700 Btu per gallon (lower heating value).
(3)
A
gallon of liquefied natural gas only produces 58.04 percent of the energy
produced from a gallon of diesel; however, the Federal excise taxes on
liquefied natural gas and diesel are both 24.3 cents per gallon.
(4)
The Federal excise
tax on compressed natural gas is 18.3 cents per energy equivalent of a gallon
of gasoline.
