In general

of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by adding at the

(E)
Special rule for contributions relating to withdrawal from school
In the case of a beneficiary who receives a refund of any qualified higher education expenses from

enrollment at such institution, subparagraph (A) shall not apply to that
portion of any distribution for the taxable year which is recontributed to

2013.
