1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Teacher Tax Deduction Enhancement Act of 2013
.
2.
Deduction for certain expenses of preschool, elementary and secondary school teachers
(a)
In general
Section 62(a)(2)(D)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended to read as follows:
(D)
Certain expenses of preschool, elementary and secondary school teachers
In the case of taxable years beginning before 2020, the deductions allowed by section 162 which consist of expenses, not in excess of the applicable amount, paid or incurred by an eligible educator in connection with books, supplies (other than nonathletic supplies for courses of instruction in health or physical education), computer equipment (including related software and services) and other equipment, and supplementary materials used by the eligible educator in the classroom.
.
