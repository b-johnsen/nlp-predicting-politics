1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Bureau Advisory Commission Transparency Act
.
2.
Application of FACA
Section 1013 of the Consumer Financial Protection Act of 2010 (
12 U.S.C. 5493
) is amended by adding
at the end the following:
(h)
Application of FACA
Notwithstanding any provision of the Federal Advisory Committee Act (5 U.S.C. App.), such Act shall
apply to each advisory committee of the Bureau.
.
