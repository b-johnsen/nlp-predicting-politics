(b)
Definitions
In this section, the following definitions apply:
(1)
Flight
The term
flight
means, with respect to an aircraft, the period beginning when the aircraft takes off and ending when the aircraft lands.
(2)
Mobile communications device
(A)
In general
The term
mobile communications device
means any portable wireless telecommunications equipment utilized for the transmission or reception of voice data.
(B)
Limitation
The term
mobile communications device
does not include a phone installed on an aircraft.
.
