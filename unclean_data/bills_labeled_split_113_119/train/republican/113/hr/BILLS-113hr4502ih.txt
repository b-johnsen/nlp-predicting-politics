1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Stop Meth Labs and Enhance Patient Access Act of 2014
.
2.
Exemption if it is not practical to use product in illicit manufacture of methamphetamine
Section 310(e)(3) of the Controlled Substances Act (
21 U.S.C. 830(e)(3)
) is amended by striking
if the Attorney General determines that the product cannot be used in the illicit manufacture of
methamphetamine
and inserting
if the Attorney General determines that it is not practical by processes known to be employed by
clandestine laboratory operators to use the product in the illicit
manufacture of methamphetamine
.
