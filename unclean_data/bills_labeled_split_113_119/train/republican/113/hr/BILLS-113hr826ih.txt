1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Freedom to Fish Act of 2013
.
2.
Restricted areas at Corps of Engineers dams
(a)
In general
The Secretary of the Army, acting through the Chief of Engineers, shall not take any action to establish a restricted area prohibiting public access to waters downstream of a dam owned by the Corps of Engineers.
(b)
Effective date
(1)
In general
Subject to paragraph (2), this section shall apply to an action described in subsection (a) on or after August 1, 2012.
(2)
Existing restrictions
