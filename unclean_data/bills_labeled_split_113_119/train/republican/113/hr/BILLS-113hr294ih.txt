1.
Revocation of
requirement
Section 5.2(e) of
Executive Order 13618,
Assignment of National Security and Emergency
Preparedness Communications Functions
(July 6, 2012; 77 Fed. Reg.
40779) is revoked to the extent that it refers to communications resources that
are privately owned.
2.
Limitation on
Federal use and control of privately owned communications
resources
No agency or
instrumentality of the Federal Government may satisfy priority communications
requirements (as such term is used in section 5.2(e) of Executive Order 13618)
through the use of privately owned communications resources, and no funds
appropriated pursuant to any provision of law may be used to take any action
that expands the authority of the Federal Government to control privately owned
communications resources.
