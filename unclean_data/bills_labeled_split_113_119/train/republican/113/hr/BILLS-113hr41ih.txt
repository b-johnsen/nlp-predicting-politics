1.
Temporary increase in
borrowing authority for National Flood Insurance Program
(a)
Section 1309(a) of the National Flood
Insurance Act of 1968 (
42 U.S.C. 4016(a)
) is amended by striking
$20,725,000,000
and inserting
$30,425,000,000
.
(b)
The amount provided by this section is
designated by the Congress as an emergency requirement pursuant to section
403(a) of S. Con. Res. 13 (111th Congress), the concurrent resolution on the
budget for fiscal year 2010, and as an emergency pursuant to section 4(g) of
the Statutory Pay-As-You-Go Act of 2010 (
2 U.S.C. 933(g)
).
