1.
Lance Corporal Phillip D.
Vinnedge Post Office
(a)
Designation
The
facility of the United States Postal Service located at 5323 Highway N in
Cottleville, Missouri, shall be known and designated as the
Lance
Corporal Phillip Vinnedge Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any
references in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the
United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to
be a reference to the
Lance Corporal Phillip Vinnedge Post
Office
.
