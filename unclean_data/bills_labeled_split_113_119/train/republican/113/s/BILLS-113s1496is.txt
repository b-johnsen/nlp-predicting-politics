(b)
Prohibition
Notwithstanding any other provision of law,
as a condition on the receipt of Federal funds by any public transportation
agency, the public transportation agency shall be prohibited from providing
annual compensation to the chief executive, or any other officer or employee,
of the public transportation agency in an amount that is in excess of the
annual compensation of the President of the United States as described in
section 102
of title 3, United States Code.
