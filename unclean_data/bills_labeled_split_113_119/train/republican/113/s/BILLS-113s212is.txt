1.
Transfer of Yellow Creek Port
properties
In accordance with
section 4(k) of the Tennessee Valley Authority Act of 1933 (
16 U.S.C. 831c(k)
),
Congress approves the conveyance by the Tennessee Valley Authority, on behalf
of the United States, to the State of Mississippi of the Yellow Creek Port
properties owned by the United States and in the custody of the Authority at
Iuka, Mississippi, as of the date of enactment of this Act.
