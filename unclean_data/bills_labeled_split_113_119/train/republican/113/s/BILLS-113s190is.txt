1.
Short
title
This Act may be cited as
the
Restoring the Constitutional
Balance of Power Act of 2013
.
2.
Prohibition on
funding of certain NLRB activities
No Federal funds may be used by the National
Labor Relations Board to undertake or enforce activities commencing on or after
January 4, 2012 that require authorization by not less than a quorum of the
members of the Board.
3.
Prohibition on
funding of certain CFPB activities
No funds may be transferred from the Federal
Reserve to be used by the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection to carry out
activities that are authorized only upon the confirmation of a Director of the
Bureau.
