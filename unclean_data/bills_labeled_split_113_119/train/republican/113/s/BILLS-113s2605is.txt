3.
Sense of the Senate
It is the sense of the Senate that the Food and Drug Administration should study whether
contraceptives that are available with a prescription, on the date of
enactment of this Act, would be safe and
effective for adults if available without a prescription.
4.
Employee protection

not prohibit an employee from

dispensing laws, a drug or medical device, including a contraceptive, that
is regulated by
the Food and Drug Administration.
