1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Holding the IRS Leadership Accountable Act
.
2.
Prohibition on bonuses until IRS complies with Congressional document requests
The Commissioner of Internal Revenue shall not provide any performance award (including bonuses,
step increases, and leave time) to senior-level executives of the Internal
Revenue Service until such time as the Treasury Inspector General for Tax
Administration certifies that the Internal Revenue Service has provided
all documents, including all electronic communications, requested by the
relevant congressional committees in due course of their investigation
into the targeting of certain taxpayers who applied for designation as an
organization described in section 501(c)(4) of the Internal Revenue Code
of 1986.
