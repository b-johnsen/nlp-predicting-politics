1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
United States-Abraham Accords Cooperation and Security Act of 2025
.
2.
Establishment of Abraham Accords Office within Food and Drug Administration
(a)
In general
Chapter X of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (
21 U.S.C. 391 et seq.
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
1015.
Abraham Accords Office
(a)
In general
The Secretary, acting through the Commissioner of Food and Drugs, shall establish within the Food and Drug Administration an office, to be known as the Abraham Accords Office, to be headed by a director.

(d)
Abraham Accords country defined
In this section, the term
Abraham Accords country
means a country identified by the Department of State as having signed the Abraham Accords Declaration.
(e)
National security
Nothing in this section shall be construed to require any action inconsistent with a national security recommendation provided by the Federal Government.
