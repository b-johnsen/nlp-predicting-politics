1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Curtailing Agency Reach and Bureaucracy Overreach on Net-Zero Act
or the
CARBON Act
.
2.
Exclusion of carbon dioxide, methane, and nitrous oxide
Section 302(g) of the Clean Air Act (
42 U.S.C. 7602(g)
) is amended by adding at the end
Such term does not include carbon dioxide, methane, or nitrous oxide.
.
