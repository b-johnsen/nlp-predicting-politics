1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
State-Level Departments of Government Efficiency Establishment Act
or the
State-Level DOGE Establishment Act
.
2.
Condition on Federal funding to States
(a)
Condition
(1)
In general
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, in fiscal year 2026 and each fiscal year thereafter, no discretionary appropriations may be disbursed to any State unless such State has established an entity described in subsection (b).
(2)
Exception
For purposes of paragraph (1), the term
discretionary appropriations
does not include any discretionary appropriations within the security category (as that term is defined in section 250(c) of the Balanced Budget and Emergency Deficit Control Act of 1985 (
2 U.S.C. 900(c)
).
