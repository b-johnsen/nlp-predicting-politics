Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Capping Excessive Awarding of SBLC Entrants Act
or the
CEASE Act
.
2.
Limitation on number of small business lending companies
Section 23 of the Small Business Act (
15 U.S.C. 650
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(k)
Limitation on number of small business lending companies
The Administrator shall ensure that not more than 16 small business lending companies that are not nonprofit entities are authorized to make loans under section 7 at any time.
