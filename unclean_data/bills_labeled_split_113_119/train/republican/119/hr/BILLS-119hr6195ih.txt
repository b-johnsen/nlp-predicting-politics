In general
The National Security Act of 1947 (
50 U.S.C. 3001 et seq.

(1)

(2)

(b)
Penalties
Any person who violates subsection (a) shallâ
(1)
in the case of the first offense, be fined under title 18, United States Code, imprisoned for not more than 180 days, or both;
