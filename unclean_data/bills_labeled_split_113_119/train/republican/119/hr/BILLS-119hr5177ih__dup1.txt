1.
Weigh station enforcement of certain safety requirements
(a)
In general
Subchapter III of
chapter 311
of title 49, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
31152.
Weigh station enforcement of certain safety requirements
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the Secretary shall ensure that each State enforces sections 3 and 4 of Executive Order 14286 (relating to Enforcing Commonsense Rules of the Road for America's Truck Drivers (signed on April 28, 2025)) on each commercial motor vehicle that enters a weigh station.
.
(b)
Clerical amendment
The analysis for
chapter 311
of title 49, United States Code, is amended by inserting after the item relating to
section 31151
the following:
31152. Weigh station enforcement of certain safety requirements.
.
