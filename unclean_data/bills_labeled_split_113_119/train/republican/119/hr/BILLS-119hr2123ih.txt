1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
NFIP Extension Act of 2025
.
2.
Extension of the National Flood Insurance Program
(a)
Financing
Section 1309(a) of the National Flood Insurance Act of 1968 (
42 U.S.C. 4016(a)
) is amended by striking
September 30, 2023
and inserting
September 30, 2025
.
(b)
Program expiration
Section 1319 of the National Flood Insurance Act of 1968 (
42 U.S.C. 4026
) is amended by striking
September 30, 2023
and inserting
September 30, 2025
.
(c)
Retroactive effective date
If this Act is enacted after March 14, 2025, the amendments made by subsections (a) and (b) shall take effect as if enacted on March 14, 2025.
