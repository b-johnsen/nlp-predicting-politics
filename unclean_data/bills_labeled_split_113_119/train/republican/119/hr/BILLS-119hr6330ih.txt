2.
Lump-sum relocation payments for relocated Federal employees
(a)
In general
Subchapter II of
chapter 57
of title 5, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
5739a.
Authority for lump sum payment for relocation
(a)
In general
Notwithstanding any other provision of this subchapter, when the head of the agency concerned (or a designee) authorizes or approves, an agency, through the proper disbursing official, may pay to an employee who relocates in the interest of the Government, a one-time lump sum payment in lieu of any payment otherwise authorized or required under this subchapter.
