1.
Mary Granados Memorial Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 4551 East 52nd Street in Odessa, Texas, shall be known and designated as the
Mary Granados Memorial Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Mary Granados Memorial Post Office Building
.
