1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Rural Health Clinic Location Modernization Act of 2025
.
2.
Maintaining standards for qualification as a rural health clinic under the Medicare program
(a)
In general
Section 1861(aa)(2) of the Social Security Act (
42 U.S.C. 1395x(aa)(2)
) is amended, in the first sentence of the matter following subparagraph (K), by striking
an urbanized area (as defined by the Bureau of the Census)
and inserting
an urban area (as defined by the Bureau of the Census) with a population of 50,000 or more
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendments made by subsection (a) shall apply beginning January 1, 2027.
