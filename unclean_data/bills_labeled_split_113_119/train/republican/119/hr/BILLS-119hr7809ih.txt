1.
Ulysses S. Grant Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 201 East Grant Avenue in Georgetown, Ohio, shall be known and designated as the
Ulysses S. Grant Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Ulysses S. Grant Post Office Building
.
