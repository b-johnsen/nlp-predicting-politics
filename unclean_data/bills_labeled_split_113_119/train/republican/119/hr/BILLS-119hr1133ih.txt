1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Repeal Community Development Block Grants Act of 2025
.
2.
Abolition of grants
(a)
Abolition of grants
Section 101 and sections 103 through 122 of title I of the Housing and Community Development Act of 1974 (
42 U.S.C. 5301 et seq.
) are repealed.
(b)
Effective date
The amendments made by this section shall take effect on October 1, 2025.
