1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Protect American Widows Act of 2025
or the
PAW Act of 2025
.
2.
Surviving spouse definition expanded
(a)
In general
Section 2(a)(1)(A)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking
two taxable years
and inserting
five taxable years
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendments made by this section shall apply to returns filed for taxable years beginning after December 31, 2024.
