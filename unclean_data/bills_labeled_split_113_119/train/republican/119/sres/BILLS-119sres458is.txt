1.
Extension of enforcement of budgetary points of order in the Senate
Notwithstanding any provision of the Congressional Budget Act of 1974 (
2 U.S.C. 621 et seq.
), subsections (c)(2) and (d)(3) of section 904 of the Congressional Budget Act of 1974 (
2 U.S.C. 621
note) shall remain in effect for purposes of Senate enforcement through September 30, 2027.
