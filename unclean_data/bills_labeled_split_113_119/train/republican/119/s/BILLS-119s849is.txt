2.
Prohibition on displaying or purchasing flags of countries other than the United States
(a)
Prohibition on display
The flag of a country other than the United States may not be displayed at any location on the United States Capitol Grounds, as described in section 5102 of title 40, United States Code.
(b)
Prohibition on purchase
