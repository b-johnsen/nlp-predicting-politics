1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Poarch Band of Creek Indians Parity Act
.
2.
Applicability of Indian Reorganization Act to the Poarch Band of Creek Indians
(a)
In general
The Poarch Band of Creek Indians shall be considered
now under Federal jurisdiction
as of June 18, 1934, for purposes of the Act of June 18, 1934 (commonly known as the
Indian Reorganization Act
) (48 Stat. 984, chapter 576;
25 U.S.C. 5101 et seq.
).
(b)
Lands taken into trust
All lands taken into trust by the United States for the benefit of the Poarch Band of Creek Indians before the date of enactment of this Act are reaffirmed as trust land, and the actions of the Secretary of the Interior in taking those lands into trust under the Act of June 18, 1934 (commonly known as the
Indian Reorganization Act
) (48 Stat. 984, chapter 576;
25 U.S.C. 5101 et seq.
), are ratified and confirmed.
