(b)
Audits
Section 658K of the Child Care and Development Block Grant Act of 1990 (
42 U.S.C. 9858i
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(c)
Federal audits
Each child care provider that receives a payment under this subchapter shall prepare a record of attendance in the provider's program and of the provider's provision of child care services, and maintain the record for a period of 7 years after the date of preparation of such record. The provider shall make such records available for audits by the Secretary, the Attorney General, and the Comptroller General of the United States.

For purposes of the preceding sentence, in the case of a State that has a 2-year legislative session, each year of the session is deemed to be a separate regular session of the State legislature.
