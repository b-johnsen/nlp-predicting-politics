Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Improving Access to Small Business Information Act
.
2.
Paperwork Reduction Act requirements exemptions
Section 4(i) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 (
15 U.S.C. 78d(i)
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(10)
Preservation of information collection burden review

In general
Actions taken by the Advocate for Small Business Capital Formation under this subsection shall not be a
collection of information
for purposes of subchapter I of
chapter 35
of title 44, United States Code (commonly known as the
Paperwork Reduction Act

(ii)
