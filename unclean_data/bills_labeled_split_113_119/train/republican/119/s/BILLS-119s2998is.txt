1.
Designation of Charlie Kirk Patriot Way
(a)
In general
The area of H Street Northwest between Connecticut Avenue Northwest and Vermont Avenue Northwest in Washington, District of Columbia, shall be known and designated as
Charlie Kirk Patriot Way
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the area referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to
Charlie Kirk Patriot Way
.
