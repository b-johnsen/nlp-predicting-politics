Additional priority for States that have not received a grant

In general
The Secretary shall prioritizing consideration of any eligible entity located in a State in which an entity has applied but not received a grant under this section.
(B)
Scoring preference
If no entity in a particular State has received a grant under this section, the Secretary shall give all eligible entities in that State that apply for such a grant a scoring preference until at least one grant is awarded to an eligible entity in that State.
