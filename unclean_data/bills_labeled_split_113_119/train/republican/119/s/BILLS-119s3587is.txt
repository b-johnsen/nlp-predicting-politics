.

Part III of subchapter B of

.
(b)
Conforming amendment
The table of sections for part III of subchapter B of
chapter 1
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by inserting before the item relating to section 140 the following new item:
Sec. 139M. Interest paid to taxpayers following an audit or litigation.
.
(c)
Effective date
The amendments made by this section shall apply to taxable years beginning after December 31, 2025.
