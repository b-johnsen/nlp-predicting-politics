3.
Purpose
The purpose of this Act is to require retirement and welfare benefit plans that are covered by the Employee Retirement Income Security Act of 1974 (
29 U.S.C. 1001
et seq.), to adopt and implement policies in consideration of ESG factors when making investment decisions, because considering ESG factors is relevant to the fiduciary duty of prudence, as such factors help ensure investmentsâ long-term sustainability.
