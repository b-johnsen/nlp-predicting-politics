(a)

The Secretary of Health and Human Services (in this section referred to as the
Secretary

To be eligible to receive a grant under this section, an organization shallâ

42 U.S.C. 1936
et seq.) have access to free drop-in child care services during prenatal and postpartum appointments, including identification of the funding necessary to carry out such actions.

(g)
