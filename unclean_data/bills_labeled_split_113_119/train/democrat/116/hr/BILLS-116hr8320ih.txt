(b)
Funding
Of the amount appropriated under subsection (d), the Secretary of Labor shall divide the amount for each fiscal year equally among the outlying areas.
(c)
Outlying area defined
The term
outlying area
means American Samoa, Guam, the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands, and the United States Virgin Islands.
