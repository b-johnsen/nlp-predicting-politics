1.
Project labor agreement credit in offshore leasing
(a)
In general
Section 8(a) of the Outer Continental Shelf Lands Act (
43 U.S.C. 1337(a)(1)
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(9)
Project labor agreement credit
(1)
In general
In evaluating a bid under this section, the Secretary shall award a 10 percent credit to value of a bid if the bidder commits to enter into a project labor agreement with respect to any employees who, upon their employment, will be engaged in building or construction with respect to the offshore wind energy development within the leased tract. The Secretary shall include such commitment as a term in any lease the bid for which included such commitment.
