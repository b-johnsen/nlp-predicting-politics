1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Virtual Reality Technologies Enabling Coaching and Honing Skills in Government Act of 2019
or the
VR TECHS in Government Act of 2019
.
2.
Establishment of the Federal Advisory Committee on the Usability of Reality Technologies Within the Federal Government
(a)
In General
Not later than 120 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Administrator of General Services (referred to in this Act as the
Administrator
), in coordination with the Director of the National Institute of Standards and Technology, shall establish an advisory committee to be known as the Federal Advisory Committee on the Usability of Reality Technologies Within the Federal Government (referred to in this Act as the
Advisory Committee
).
