1.
William T. Coleman, Jr., Federal Building
(a)
In general
The headquarters building of the Department of Transportation located at 1200 New Jersey Avenue, SE, in Washington, DC, shall be known and designated as the
William T. Coleman, Jr., Federal Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the building referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
William T. Coleman, Jr., Federal Building
.
