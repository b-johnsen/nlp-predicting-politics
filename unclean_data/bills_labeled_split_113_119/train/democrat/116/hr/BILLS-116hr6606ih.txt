1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Clean Skies Act
.
2.
Aircraft greenhouse gas emission standards
(a)
In general
Not later than 18 months after the date of enactment of this Act, the Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency shall promulgate final regulations establishing emission standards for emissions of greenhouse gases from both new and in-service aircraft pursuant to section 231 of the Clean Air Act (
42 U.S.C. 7571
).
