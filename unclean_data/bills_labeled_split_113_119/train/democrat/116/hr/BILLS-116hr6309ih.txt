1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Natural Disaster Safe Inmate Relocation Act
.
2.
Safe Inmate Relocation
The Administrator of the Federal Emergency Management Agency shall encourage States and localities that receive assistance under the Robert T. Stafford Disaster Relief and Emergency Assistance Act (
42 U.S.C. 5121
et seq.) to adopt policies regarding the safe relocation of prisoners before, during, and after major disasters and emergencies, as appropriate.
