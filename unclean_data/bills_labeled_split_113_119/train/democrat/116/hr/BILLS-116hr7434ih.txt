1.
Interest rates on economic injury disaster loans
(a)
In general
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, during the period beginning on January 31, 2020, and ending on December 31, 2020, the Administrator of the Small Business Administration shall ensure that the interest rate on a loan made under section 7(b)(2) of the Small Business Act (
15 U.S.C. 636(b)(2)
) is 1 percent.
