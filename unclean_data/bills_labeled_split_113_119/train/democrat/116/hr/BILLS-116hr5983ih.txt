1.
Woodie Rucker-Hughes Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 4150 Chicago Avenue in Riverside, California, shall be known and designated as the
Woodie Rucker-Hughes Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Woodie Rucker-Hughes Post Office Building
.
