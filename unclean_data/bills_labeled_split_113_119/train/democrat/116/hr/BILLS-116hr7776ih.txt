2.
Authority to purchase mental health supports to protect children who receive certain child care services
A child care provider who receives payment for services provided under the Child Care and Development Block Grant Act of 1990 (
42 U.S.C. 9857
et seq.) may use a portion of such payment to purchase mental health supports necessary to protect the health of children who receive such services and child care workers who provide such services.
