1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Wireless Infrastructure Resiliency during Emergencies and Disasters Act
or the
WIRED Act
.
2.
Regulatory treatment of mobile services
Section 332(c)(3)(A) of the Communications Act of 1934 (
47 U.S.C. 332(c)(3)(A)
) is amended by inserting after
other terms and conditions of commercial mobile services
the following:
including reasonable requirements to promote resilient wireless communications infrastructure for situational awareness during a natural disaster (as such term is defined in section 602 of the Robert T. Stafford Disaster Relief and Emergency Assistance Act (
42 U.S.C. 5195a(a)(2)
))
.
