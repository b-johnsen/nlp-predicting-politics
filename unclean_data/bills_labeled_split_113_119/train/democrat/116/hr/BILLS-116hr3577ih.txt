3701.
Purpose
The purpose of this chapter is to enable the Postal Service to increase its net revenues through providing specific nonpostal property, products, and services that are expressly authorized by this chapter. Postal Service revenues and expenses under this chapter shall be funded through the Postal Service Fund.

In general
Before beginning to provide property, products, or services under an agreement described in subsection (a), the Postal Service shall provide for a public comment period of not less than 30 days, during which the public may post comments relating to the provision of the property, products, or services on the website of the Postal Service.
