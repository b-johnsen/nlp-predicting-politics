(b)
Authority To make grants to certain entities To collect broadband infrastructure data
Notwithstanding any other provision of a covered authority, the Secretary may make a grant under a covered authority to an eligible entity for the purpose of enabling the collection of data relating to where broadband infrastructure is located and which homes are provided with nonsatellite broadband service, without regard to any applicable household service percentage requirement.

Effective date
