2.
Cooperative associations that support rural infrastructure development
Section 11 of the Federal Home Loan Bank Act (
12 U.S.C. 1431
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(m)
Cooperative associations that support rural infrastructure development
(1)
In general
Each Federal Home Loan Bank is authorized to purchase investment-grade securities from nonmember lenders thatâ
(A)
are organized as cooperatives;
(B)
have received financing from the Federal Financing Bank; and
(C)
have demonstrated experience in making loans to cooperatives that are eligible to receive loans or commitments for loans from the Rural Utilities Service (or any successor agency).
(2)
Secured investments
The securities described in paragraph (1) shall be secured investments collateralized by loans of the cooperative lender.
(3)
Discretion
The purchase of the securities described in paragraph (1) shall be at the sole discretion of each Federal Home Loan Bank, consistent with such regulations, restrictions, and limitations as may be prescribed by the Agency.
