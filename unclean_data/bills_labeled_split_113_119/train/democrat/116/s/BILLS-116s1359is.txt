610.
Federal clean energy standard
(a)
Purpose
The purpose of this section is to establish a technology-neutral, market-oriented standard for electric energy generation thatâ
(1)
stimulates clean energy innovation and allows the United States to achieve a net-zero emission electric sector at the lowest cost;
(2)
will guide power sector investment and provide regulatory certainty, while helping to ensure that the United States is the leader and dominant competitor in the global clean energy transition; and
(3)
will result inâ
(A)
hundreds of billions of dollars in domestic health and environmental benefits by the mid-21st century; and
(B)
save tens of thousands of lives in the United States.
(b)

The term

In general

For purposes of subparagraph (A), not later than 180 days after the date of enactment of this section, the Secretary shall develop appropriate processes for determining the quantity of behind-the-meter generation consumed by electric consumers served by a retail electricity supplier, including by requiring from the retail electricity supplier relevant documentation of behind-the-meter electric energy consumption, such as records associated with net-metering.
