1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Spam Calls Task Force Act of 2018
.
2.
Interagency working group
(a)
In general
The Attorney General, in consultation with the Federal Communications Commission, shall convene an interagency working group to study the enforcement of section 227(b) of the Communications Act of 1934 (
47 U.S.C. 227(b)
).

(3)
