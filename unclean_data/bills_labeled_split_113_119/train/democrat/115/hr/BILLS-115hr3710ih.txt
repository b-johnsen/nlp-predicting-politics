Javits Act of 2017
.

Authorization of Appropriations

20 U.S.C. 1134d
) is amended by striking
2009
and inserting
2018
.
3.
Fellowship awards for music education
The Secretary of Education shall ensure that music education is included among the fields of study for which a fellowship may be awarded under subpart 1 of part A of title VII of the Higher Education Act of 1965 (
20 U.S.C. 1134
et seq.).
