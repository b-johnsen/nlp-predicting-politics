1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Space Debris Removal Act
.
2.
Orbital debris removal technologies
Section 31501 of title 51, United States Code, is amended to read as follows:
31501.
Orbital debris
The Administrator, in conjunction with the heads of other Federal agencies, shall take steps to develop or acquire technologies and policies that will enable the Administration to decrease the risks associated with and remove orbital debris.
.
