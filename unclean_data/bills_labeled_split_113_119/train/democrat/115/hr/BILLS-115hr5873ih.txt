1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Disclosing Official Spending at Presidential Businesses Act
.
2.
Disclosure of expenditure of Federal funds at businesses owned by the President
(a)
In general
The head of each Executive agency shall submit a quarterly report to the Director of the Office of Government Ethics listing each instance, during the most recently ended quarter, that the Executive agency obligated or expended Federal funds with any privately held company that is owned, either in whole or in part, directly or indirectly, including through trusts, by the President. (c)
Definition of Executive agency
In this section, the term
Executive agency
has the meaning given that term in section 105 of title 5, United States Code, and includes the United States Postal Service, the Postal Regulatory Commission, the White House, and the Executive Office of the President.
