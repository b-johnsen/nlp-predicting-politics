1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Fry Scholarship Enhancement Act
.
2.
Expansion of Yellow Ribbon G.I. Education Enhancement Program
(a)
In general
Section 3317(a) of title 38, United States Code, is amended by striking
paragraphs (1) and (2) of section 3311(b)
and inserting
paragraphs (1), (2), (8), and (9) of section 3311(b) of this title
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by subsection (a) shall take effect on August 1, 2018.
