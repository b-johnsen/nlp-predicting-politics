(c)
Exception
(1)
In general
Subsection (b) shall not apply to expenditures by the United States Secret Service or any other agency for protective mission purposes relating to the protection of an employee of an Executive department.
(2)
Reporting requirement
Not later than 90 days after the date on which an agency makes an expenditure that would be prohibited under subsection (b) but for the exception under paragraph (1) of this subsection, the agency shall submit a report describing the expenditure to the Office of Government Ethics.
