1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Redistribution of Fines to Our Communities Act
.
2.
Use of funds
The Secretary of Energy shall retain amounts collected for safety-related fines pursuant to section 234A of the Atomic Energy Act of 1954 (
42 U.S.C. 2282a
), and shall distribute the portion of those amounts that the Secretary determines appropriate to local governments to mitigate any economic impacts in connection with the closure of the nuclear facility with respect to which the fines were collected.
