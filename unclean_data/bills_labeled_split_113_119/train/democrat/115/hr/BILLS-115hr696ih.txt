1.
Prohibition on hiring freeze from affecting Department of Veterans Affairs
The memorandum titled
Presidential Memorandum Regarding the Hiring Freeze
published on January 23, 2017, or any other substantially similar memorandum, Executive order, or other action by the President to prevent a department or agency of the Federal Government from filling vacant Federal civilian employee positions or creating new such positions, shall have no force or effect with respect to the Department of Veterans Affairs.
