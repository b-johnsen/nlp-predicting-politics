1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Veterans Fair Debt Notice Act of 2017
.
2.
Use of certified mail and plain language required in certain debt collection activities
(a)
In general
Not later than 120 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Secretary of Veterans Affairs shall amend section 1.911 of title 38, Code of Federal Regulations, to require that certified mail be used to send any of the following:
(1)
A written demand from the Secretary to a debtor under subsection (b) of such section.
(2)
A request for a waiver from a debtor to the Secretary under subsection (c)(2) of such section.
(3)
A notification from the Secretary to a debtor under subsection (d) or (e) of such section.
(b)
Plain language required
The Secretary shall write the following in plain language:
(1)
Any demand described in subsection (a)(1), which shall include an explanation of why the debtor owes money to the Department of Veterans Affairs.
(2)
Any notification described in subsection (a)(3), which shall include options available to the debtor.
