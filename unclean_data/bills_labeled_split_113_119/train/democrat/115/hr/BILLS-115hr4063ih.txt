The Secretary of Energy shall promulgate a rule to require each electric utility to report to the Secretary of Energy, acting through the Administrator of the Energy Information Administration, information on electricity outages of the electric utility for outages that interrupt electric service resulting in a loss of more than 300 megawatts or affecting at least 50,000 electric consumers.
