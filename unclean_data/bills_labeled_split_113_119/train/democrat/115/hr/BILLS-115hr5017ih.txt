243.

(a)
Purpose
The purpose of this section is to establish an initiative to provide financial and technical assistance to healthy food producers and retailers, food hubs, and mid-tier value chains that serve healthy food retailers and other healthy food business enterprises芒聙聰
(1)
to improve access to, and expand the supply of, healthy food in low-income, underserved communities;
(2)

(4)

(5)
