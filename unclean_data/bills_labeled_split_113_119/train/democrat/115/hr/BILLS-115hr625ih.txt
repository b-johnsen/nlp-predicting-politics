1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Reporting Efficiently to Proper Officials in Response to Terrorism Act of 2017
or the
REPORT Act
.
2.
Duty to report
(a)
Duty imposed
Whenever an incident of terrorism occurs in the United States, it shall be the duty of the Secretary of Homeland Security, in coordination with the Attorney General, the Director of the Federal Bureau of Investigation, and, as appropriate, the head of the National Counterterrorism Center, to submit, not later than upon completion of the investigation by the primary Government agency conducting the investigation, an unclassified report (which may be accompanied by a classified annex) to Congress concerning that incident.

Content of reports

(c)
Definition
In this section, the term
incident of terrorism
means an event declared by the Federal Bureau of Investigation to be an act of terrorism.
