Clarification for paragraph (2)
(A)
Definition of port facilities
For purposes of paragraph (2)(A), the term
port facilities
means all port facilities for coastwise, intercoastal, inland waterways, and Great Lakes shipping and overseas shipping, including, wharves, piers, sheds, warehouses, terminals, yards, docks, control towers, container equipment, maintenance buildings, container freight stations and port equipment, including harbor craft, cranes, and straddle carriers.
(B)
Applicability of clean air act
Nothing in paragraph (1) shall limit the rights reserved to any State or a political subdivision thereof under the Clean Air Act (
42 U.S.C. 7401
et seq.).
