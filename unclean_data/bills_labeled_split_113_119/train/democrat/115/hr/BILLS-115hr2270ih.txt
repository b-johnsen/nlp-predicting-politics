The safeguards to prevent fraud and abuse developed under subsection (a)(1) shall apply under this paragraph.

Reinstatement of loans
The Secretary may promulgate regulations to reinstate the obligation of, and resume collection on, loans discharged under this paragraph in cases in which the Secretary determines that the reinstatement and resumption is necessary and appropriate based upon the regulations developed under subsection (a)(1).
