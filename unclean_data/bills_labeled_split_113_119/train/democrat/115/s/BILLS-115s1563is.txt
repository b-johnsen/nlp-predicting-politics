Short title

3.
Program for extraction and recovery of rare earth elements and minerals from coal and coal
byproducts
(a)
In general
The Secretary of Energy, acting through the Assistant Secretary for Fossil Energy (referred to in this section as the
Secretary
), shall carry out a program under which the Secretary shall develop advanced separation technologies for the extraction and recovery of rare earth elements and minerals from coal and coal byproducts.
(b)
Authorization of appropriations
There is authorized to be appropriated to the Secretary to carry out the program described in subsection (a) $20,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2018 through 2025.
