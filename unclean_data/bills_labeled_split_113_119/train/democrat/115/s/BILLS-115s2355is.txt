1.
John Fitzgerald Kennedy Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 25 New Chardon Street Lobby in Boston, Massachusetts, shall be known and designated as the
John Fitzgerald Kennedy Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
John Fitzgerald Kennedy Post Office
.
