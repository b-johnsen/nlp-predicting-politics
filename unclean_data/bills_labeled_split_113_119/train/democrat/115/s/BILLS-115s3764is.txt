3.
Pre-application notice of pesticide use
Section 11 of the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (
7 U.S.C. 136i
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(f)
Pre-Application notice of pesticide use
Not later than 7 days before the application of a pesticide classified for restricted use, a certified applicator of restricted use pesticides shall post signage in the area in which the application is to occur that is reasonably calculated to provide notice of the application.
.
