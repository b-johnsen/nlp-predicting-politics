1.
Short
title
This Act may be cited as the
Driver Fatigue Prevention Act
.
2.
Amendment to the
Fair Labor Standards Act of 1938
Section 13(b)(1) of the Fair Labor Standards Act of 1938 (
29 U.S.C. 213(b)(1)
) is amended by inserting before the semicolon the following:
, except a driver of an
over-the-road bus
characterized by an elevated passenger deck located over a baggage compartment.
.
