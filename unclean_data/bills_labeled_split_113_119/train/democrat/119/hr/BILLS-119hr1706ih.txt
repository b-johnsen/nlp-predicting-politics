1.
William J. Donovan Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 1200 William Street, Room 200, in Buffalo, New York, shall be known and designated as the
William J. Donovan Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
William J. Donovan Post Office Building
.
