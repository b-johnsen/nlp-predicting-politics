1.
Firefighter Trevor Brown Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 46164 Westlake Drive in Sterling, Virginia, shall be known and designated as the
Firefighter Trevor Brown Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Firefighter Trevor Brown Post Office Building
.
