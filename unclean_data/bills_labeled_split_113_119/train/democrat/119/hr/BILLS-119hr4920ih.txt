1.

This Act may be cited as the
Bureau of Industry and Security Information Technology Modernization Act

2.
Modernization of information technology systems and applications of the Bureau of Industry and Security
(a)
Sense of Congress
It is the sense of Congress that the effective use of Bureau authorities requires that芒聙聰
(1)

(2)

(3)

(b)
In general
Subject to the availability of appropriations, the Under Secretary of Commerce for Industry and Security shall, on an ongoing basis through fiscal year 2030, modernize the information technology systems of the Bureau.
