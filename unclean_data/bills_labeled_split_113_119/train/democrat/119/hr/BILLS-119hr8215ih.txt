Short title

Volume II Transparency Act of 2026
.
2.
Disclosure of volume II of report prepared by Special Counsel Jack Smith
(a)
Requirement
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, not later than 7 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Attorney General shall publish on the internet website of the Department of Justice volume II of the report prepared by Special Counsel Jack Smith.

(c)
Public interest
A redaction made under subsection (b)(3) may be made public if the Attorney General determines that such publication is in the public interest.
