3.
Additional funding for the Medicare part A trust fund
In addition to amounts otherwise made available, there is appropriated to the Federal Hospital Insurance Trust Fund under section 1817 of the Social Security Act (
42 U.S.C. 1395i
) for fiscal year 2026, out of any money in the Treasury not otherwise appropriated, $1,700,000,000, to remain available until expended.
