1.
Jay P. Larson Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 5225 Harrison Avenue in Rockford, Illinois, shall be known and designated as the
Jay P. Larson Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Jay P. Larson Post Office Building
.
