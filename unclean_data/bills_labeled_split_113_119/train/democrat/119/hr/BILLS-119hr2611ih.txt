1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Homeland Operations and Unilateral Tactics Halting Incursions: Preventing Coordinated Subversion, Military Aggression and Lawless Levies Granting Rogue Operatives Unchecked Power Act of 2025
or the
HOUTHI PC SMALL GROUP Act of 2025
.
2.
Communication of classified information on mobile or desktop messaging application
(a)
In general
Chapter 37
of title 18, United States Code, is amended by inserting after
section 798A
the following:
798B.
Communication of classified information on mobile or desktop messaging application
Whoever knowingly communicates, furnishes, transmits, or otherwise makes available any classified information on a mobile or desktop messaging application shall be fined under this title, imprisoned not more than 10 years, or both.
.
(b)
Clerical amendment
The table of sections for
chapter 37
of title 18, United States Code, is amended by inserting after the item related to section 798A the following:
798B. Communication of classified information on mobile or desktop messaging application.
.
