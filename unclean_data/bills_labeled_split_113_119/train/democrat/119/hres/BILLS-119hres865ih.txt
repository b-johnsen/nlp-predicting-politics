Requirement with respect to district work periods
Clause 13 of rule I of the Rules of the House of Representatives is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph:
(d)
(1)
On any day during a district work period described in paragraph (a), any Member may submit to the Clerk a letter calling for an end to such district work period. The Clerk shall make any such letter submitted a matter of public record, causing the names of the Members who have submitted such a letter during a district work period to be published in a portion of the Congressional Record designated for that purpose and making cumulative lists of such names available each day for public inspection in an appropriate office of the House. The Clerk shall devise a means for making such lists available to offices of the House and to the public in electronic form.
