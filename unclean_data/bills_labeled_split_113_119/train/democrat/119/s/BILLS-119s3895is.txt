1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Fairness in Federal Disaster Declarations Act of 2026
.
2.
Regulatory action required
(a)
In general
Not later than 120 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Administrator of the Federal Emergency Management Agency (in this Act referred to as the
Administrator
) shall amend the rules of the Administrator under section 206.48 of title 44, Code of Federal Regulations, as in effect on the date of enactment of this Act, in accordance with the provisions of this Act.

(c)
Effective date
The amended rules issued under subsection (a) shall apply to any disaster for which a Governor requested a major disaster declaration under the Robert T. Stafford Disaster Relief and Emergency Assistance Act (
42 U.S.C. 5121 et seq.
) that was denied on or after January 1, 2012.
