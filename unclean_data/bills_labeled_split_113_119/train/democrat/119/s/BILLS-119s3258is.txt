1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Aviation Medication Transparency Act of 2025
.
2.
Aviation medication transparency; list of approved medications
(a)
Purpose
The purpose of this Act is to ensure that aviation stakeholders, including airmen, air traffic control specialists, and individuals training to become airmen or air traffic control specialists, who are applicants for medical certification are informed, in a user-friendly and accessible manner, of the medications they may be safely prescribed.
(b)

(d)
Annual update
Not later than 1 year after the date of publication of the list required under subsection (b), and annually thereafter, the Administrator shall update such list, as appropriate.
