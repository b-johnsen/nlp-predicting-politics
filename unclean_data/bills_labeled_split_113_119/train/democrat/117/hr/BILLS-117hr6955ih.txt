1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Learn and Serve American Reinvestment Act
.
2.
Direct appropriation of funds for Learn and Serve America
In addition to amounts otherwise available, there is appropriated to the Corporation for National and Community Service for fiscal year 2022 and each succeeding fiscal year, out of any money in the Treasury not otherwise appropriated, $40,000,000, for subtitle B of title I of the National and Community Service Act of 1990 (
42 U.S.C. 12521 et seq.
).
