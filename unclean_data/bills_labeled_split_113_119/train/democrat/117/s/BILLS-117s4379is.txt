Special rule for State active duty
(1)
In general
Subject to regulations prescribed by the Secretary of Defense, in the case of a member of the National Guard who serves on State active duty during a one-year period during which the member would, but for this subsection, be credited with fewer than 50 points under subsection (a), such service may be counted under subsection (a).
(2)
Computation
Subject to paragraph (3), a member of the National Guard described in paragraph (1) shall be credited with 1 point for each day of State active duty during a one-year period described in that paragraph.

(4)
State active duty defined
In this subsection, the term
State active duty
