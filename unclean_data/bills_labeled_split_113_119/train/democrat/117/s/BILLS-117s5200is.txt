1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Expanding Home Loans for Guard and Reservists Act
.
2.
Expansion of eligibility of members of the National Guard for housing loans guaranteed by the Secretary of Veterans Affairs
Section 3701(b)(7) of title 38, United States Code, is amended by striking
full-time National Guard duty
and inserting
active service
.
3.
Determination of budgetary effects
The budgetary effects of this Act, for the purpose of complying with the Statutory Pay-As-You-Go-Act of 2010, shall be determined by reference to the latest statement titled
Budgetary Effects of PAYGO Legislation
for this Act, submitted for printing in the Congressional Record by the Chairman of the Senate Budget Committee, provided that such statement has been submitted prior to the vote on passage.
