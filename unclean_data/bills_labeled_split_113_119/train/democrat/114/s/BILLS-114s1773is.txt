Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Consumer Reporting Fairness Act of 2015
.
2.
Discharge of debt in bankruptcy
Section 525 of title 11, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
(d)
If a creditor has provided or furnished to a consumer reporting agency, as defined in section 603 of the Fair Credit Reporting Act (
15 U.S.C. 1681a
), any item of information pertaining to an account based on a debt discharged in a case under this title, the creditor shall inform the consumer reporting agency that the debt has been discharged in bankruptcy and has a zero balance.
