1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Getting Results through Enhanced Accountability and Transparency Act of 2016
.
2.
Purpose
The purpose of this Act is to increase the efficiency and effectiveness of the Federal Government in measuring and managing unnecessary duplication, fragmentation, and overlap in Government programs and in addressing recommendations from the Government Accountability Office.
