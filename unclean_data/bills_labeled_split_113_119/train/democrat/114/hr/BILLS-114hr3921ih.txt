Hedge fund ownership disclosures
(1)
In general
A hedge fund that has $100,000,000 or more in assets under management and that is, directly or indirectly, the beneficial owner of more than 1 percent of a class of any security shall file a quarterly report with the Commission disclosing such ownership interest.
(2)
Inclusion of derivatives
In determining beneficial ownership under paragraph (1), a hedge fund shall include any equity security with respect to which the hedge fund has an ownership interest in any derivative instrument which includes the opportunity, directly or indirectly, to profit or share in any profit derived from any increase in the value of such equity security.
