1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
MadeInAmerica.gov Act
.
2.
Definitions
(a)
Secretary
The term
Secretary
means the Secretary of Commerce.
(b)
Made in America
The term
Made in America
shall have the meaning prescribed by the Secretary pursuant to section 3 of this Act.
3.
MadeInAmerica.gov
(a)
Website creation
The Secretary shall create and maintain a website named
MadeInAmerica.gov
.

(6)
be searchable by product, company, type of product, and type of company.
