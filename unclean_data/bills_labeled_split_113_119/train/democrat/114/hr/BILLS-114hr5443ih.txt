1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
PHMSA Accountability Act
.
2.
Actions by private persons
Section 60121 of title 49, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
(e)
Mandamus
A person may bring a civil action in an appropriate district court of the United States to compel the Secretary to perform a nondiscretionary duty under this chapter that the Secretary has failed to perform.
.
