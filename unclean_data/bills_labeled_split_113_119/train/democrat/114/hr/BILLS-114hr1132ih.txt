1.
W. Ronald Coale Memorial Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 1048 West Robinhood Drive in Stockton, California, shall be known and designated as the
W. Ronald Coale Memorial Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
W. Ronald Coale Memorial Post Office Building
.
