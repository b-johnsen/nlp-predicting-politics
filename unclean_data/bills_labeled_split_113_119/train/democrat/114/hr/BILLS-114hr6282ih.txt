1.
Dr. Roscoe C. Brown, Jr. Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 2024 Jerome Avenue, in Bronx, New York, shall be known and designated as the
Dr. Roscoe C. Brown, Jr. Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Dr. Roscoe C. Brown, Jr. Post Office Building
.
