1.
Use of rotunda for ceremony to award congressional gold medal to the next of kin or personal
representative of Raoul Wallenberg
(a)
In general
The rotunda of the Capitol is authorized to be used on July 9, 2014, for a ceremony to award the
Congressional Gold Medal to the next of kin or personal representative of
Raoul Wallenberg in recognition of his achievements and heroic actions
during the Holocaust.
(b)
Preparations
Physical preparations
for the ceremony described in subsection (a) shall be carried out in
accordance with such conditions
as the Architect of the Capitol may prescribe.
