Prohibiting waiver of requirement to include travel information in financial disclosure reports of Members, officers, and employees of the House
Clause 5 of rule XXV of the Rules of the House of Representatives is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph:
(j)
The Committee on Ethics may not use its authority under this clause or
section 111(2)
of the Ethics in Government Act of 1978 (
5 U.S.C. App. 112(2)
) to waive any requirement under title I of such Act that Members, Delegates, Resident Commissioners, officers, or employees include information on reimbursements for travel (as described in section 102(a)(2)(B) of such Act) in the reports such individuals are required to file under title I of such Act.
