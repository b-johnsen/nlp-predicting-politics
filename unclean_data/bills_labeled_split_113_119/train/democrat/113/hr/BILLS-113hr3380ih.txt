1.
Congressional findings
The Congress finds that the Administrator of the Federal Emergency Management Agency has the duty, pursuant to section 100216 of the Biggert-Waters Flood Insurance Reform Act of 2012 (
42 U.S.C. 4101b
) and section 1360(f) of the National Flood Insurance Act of 1968 (
42 U.S.C. 4101(f)
), to review and update the flood insurance rate maps under the National Flood Insurance Program.
