1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
POW/MIA Accounting and Recovery
Support Act of 2013
.
2.
Exception to civilian
furlough requirements for Department of Defense employees deployed in support
of Joint POW/MIA Accounting Command accounting and recovery
efforts
The Secretary of
Defense shall require that, with respect to a civilian employee of the
Department of Defense who is required to take a furlough that would otherwise
occur during the deployment of that employee in support of accounting and
recovery efforts by the Joint POW/MIA Accounting Command, the employee may take
the furlough after returning from the deployment.
