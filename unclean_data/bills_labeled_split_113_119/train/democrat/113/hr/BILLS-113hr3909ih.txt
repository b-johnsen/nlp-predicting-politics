1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Postmark Payment Act of 2014
.
2.
Date of postmark to be treated as date of payment of a bill, invoice, or statement of account due
(a)
In general
Chapter 26
of title 39, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
2606.
Date of postmark to be treated as date of payment of a bill, invoice, or statement of account due
(a)
If any payment required to be made on or before a prescribed date is, after such date, delivered by the Postal Service to the payee, such payment shall be deemed received by the payee on the date of the United States postmark stamped on the envelope or other cover in which such payment is mailed.
(b)

(3)
which is subject to any other provision of Federal law specifying how a postmark date shall be used in determining the date on which such payment shall be deemed to have been delivered or made.
(c)
Subsection (a) shall apply only ifâ

(2)

(d)
Subsection (a) shall not apply in the case of a postmark not made by the Postal Service.

3.
Effective date
The amendments made by this Act shall apply with respect to any mailing postmarked after the end of the 3-month period beginning on the date of enactment of this Act.
