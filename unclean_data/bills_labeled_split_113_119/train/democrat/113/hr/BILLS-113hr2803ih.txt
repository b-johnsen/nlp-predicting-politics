1.
High efficiency gas
turbines
(a)
In
general
The Secretary of
Energy, through the Office of Fossil Energy, shall carry out a multiyear,
multiphase program of research, development, and technology demonstration to
improve the efficiency of gas turbines used in power generation systems and to
identify the technologies that ultimately will lead to gas turbine combined
cycle efficiency of 65 percent or simple cycle efficiency of 50 percent.
