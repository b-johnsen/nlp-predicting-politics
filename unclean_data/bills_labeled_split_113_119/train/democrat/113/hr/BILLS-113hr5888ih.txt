1.
Nutterville Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 200 Westport Road in Kansas City,
Missouri, shall be known and designated as the
Nutterville Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to
the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a
reference to the
Nutterville Post Office Building
.
