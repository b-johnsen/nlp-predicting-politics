1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Ensuring Continuous Coverage under
SCHIP Act of 2013
.
2.
Requirement of
12-month continuous coverage under SCHIP
(a)
In
general
Section 2102(b) of the Social Security Act (42 U.S.C.
1397bb(b)) is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph:
(6)
Requirement for
12-month continuous eligibility
In the case of a State child
health plan that provides child health assistance under this title through a
means other than described in section 2101(a)(2), the plan shall provide for
implementation under this title of the 12-month continuous eligibility option
described in section 1902(e)(12) for targeted low-income children whose family
income is below 200 percent of the poverty
line.
.
(b)
Conforming
amendment
In section
2105(a)(4) of such Act, strike subparagraph (A) and redesignate the following
subparagraphs accordingly.
(c)
Effective
date
The amendments made by
this section shall apply to determinations (and redeterminations) of
eligibility made on or after the date of the enactment of this Act.
