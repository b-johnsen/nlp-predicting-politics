2.
Disallowance of
deduction for direct-to-consumer advertisement of prescription drugs
(a)
General
rule
Part IX of subchapter B of chapter 1 of the Internal Revenue
Code of 1986 (relating to items not deductible) is amended by adding at the end
the following new section:
280I.
Direct-to-consumer
advertisement of prescription drugs
No deduction shall be allowed under this
chapter for any amount paid or incurred for a direct-to-consumer advertisement
of a prescription
drug.
.
