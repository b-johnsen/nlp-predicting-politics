1.
Redesignation
The railroad station located at 2955 Market Street in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, commonly known as
30th Street Station
, shall be known and designated as the
William H. Gray III 30th Street Station
.
2.
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to
the railroad station referred to in section 1 shall be deemed to be a
reference to the
William H. Gray III 30th Street Station
.
