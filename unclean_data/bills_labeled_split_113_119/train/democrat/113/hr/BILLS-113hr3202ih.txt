(c)
Corrective action plan; program reforms
Not later than 60 days after the Secretary submits the assessment under subsection (a), the Secretary shall issue a corrective action plan based on such assessment that responds to the findings of the cost-benefit analysis under subsection (b)(2) and enhances security or reduces security risk for facilities and vessels referred to in subsection (a). The plan may include programmatic reforms, revisions to regulations, or proposals for legislation.
