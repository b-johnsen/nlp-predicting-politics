1.
Florence Louise Thomas Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 4500 Sunny Isle Shopping Center in
Christiansted, St. Croix, United States Virgin Islands, shall be known and
designated as the
Florence Louise Thomas Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to
the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a
reference to the
Florence Louise Thomas Post Office
.
