Drugs and devices for use by terminally ill patients
The Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act is amended by inserting after section 561 (21 U.S.C.
360bbb) the following:
561A.
Drugs and devices for use by terminally ill patients
(a)
In general
Nothing in this Act or section 351 of the Public Health Service Act prevents or restricts, and the
Food and Drug Administration shall not implement or enforce any provision
of law preventing or restricting, the importation, distribution, or sale
of an investigational drug or device intended for use by a terminally ill
patient in accordance with subsection (b).
