1.
Designation
The headquarters building of the Coast Guard
on the campus located at 2701 Martin Luther King, Jr., Avenue Southeast in the
District of Columbia shall be known and designated as the
Douglas A.
Munro Coast Guard Headquarters Building
.
2.
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation,
document, paper, or other record of the United States to the building referred
to in section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Douglas A.
Munro Coast Guard Headquarters Building
.
