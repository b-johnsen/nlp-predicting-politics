Work opportunity tax credit for veterans hired in the field of renewable energy
(a)
In general
Section 51(d)(14)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended to read as follows:
(14)
Certain veterans hired in the field of renewable energy
(A)
In general
For purposes of this subpart, an individual shall be treated a member of a targeted group if such
individual is a specified veteran, but qualified wages with respect to
such individual shall include only wages attributable to services rendered
in a field of renewable energy.
(B)
Specified veteran
For purposes of this paragraph, the term
specified veteran

(i)

(ii)

on the hiring date, or
(iii)
having completed a LEED certification with the United States Green Building Council.
