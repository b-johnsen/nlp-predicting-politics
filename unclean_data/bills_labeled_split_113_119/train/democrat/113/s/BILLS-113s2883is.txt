1.
Report on entrepreneurial impact of technology transfer
(a)
In general
Not later than 180 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Comptroller General of the
United States shall submit
to Congress a report on the entrepreneurial impact of technology transfer
at the National Laboratories (as the term is defined in section 2 of the
Energy Policy Act of 2005 (42 U.S.C.
15801)).
(b)
Basis of report
The report under subsection (a) shall be based on an evaluation of quantitative performance
metrics, includingâ
(1)
the number of licenses granted to small businesses;
(2)
the number of start-up businesses created;
(3)
the number of cooperative research and development agreements and collaborations involving small
businesses and the total number of businesses involved in those agreements
and collaborations;
(4)
the period of time required for execution of a license; and
(5)
the number of jobs created.
