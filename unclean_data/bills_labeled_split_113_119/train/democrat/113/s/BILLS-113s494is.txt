1.
Expansion of livestock
available for bonus depreciation
(a)
In
general
Subparagraph (E) of section 168(k)(2) of the Internal
Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by adding at the end the following new
clause:
(v)
Dairy producing
calves and cows
In the case of any dairy producing calf or cow,
subparagraph (A) shall be applied without regard to clause (ii)
thereof.
.
(b)
Effective
date
The amendment made by subsection (a) shall apply to property
placed in service after December 31, 2012, in taxable years ending after such
date.
