(A)
Definition of
port facilities
In paragraph (2)(A), the term
port
facilities
means all port facilities for coastwise, intercoastal, inland
waterways, and Great Lakes shipping and overseas shipping, including wharves,
piers, sheds, warehouses, terminals, yards, docks, control towers, container
equipment, maintenance buildings, container freight stations, and port
equipment, including harbor craft, cranes, and straddle carriers.
(B)
Applicability
of Clean Air Act
Nothing in paragraph (1) limits the rights
reserved to any State or political subdivision of a State under the Clean Air
Act (42 U.S.C. 7401 et
seq.).
