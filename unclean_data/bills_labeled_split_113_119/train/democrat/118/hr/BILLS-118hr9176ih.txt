1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Loans In Our Neighborhoods Act of 2024
or the
LIONs Act of 2024
.
2.
Maximum loan amount for a 7
(a)
loan
Section 7(a)(3)(A) of the Small Business Act (
15 U.S.C. 636(a)(3)(A)
) is amended by striking
$3,750,000 (or if the gross loan amount would exceed $5,000,000)
and inserting
$7,500,000 (or if the gross loan amount would exceed $10,000,000)
.
