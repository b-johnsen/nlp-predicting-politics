(C)
Minimum period
A borrower described in subparagraph (A) who performed covered duty for a total period of less than 10 days in a year may not receive credit for a qualifying monthly payment under this paragraph.
(D)
No annual cap on number of qualifying payments credited to a borrower
(i)
In general
A borrower described in subparagraph (A) may receive credit for more than 12 qualifying monthly payments in a year if each such payment qualifies to be counted under this paragraph or paragraph (1).
(ii)
Exception to requirement for corresponding monthly payment
The requirement for a borrower to make a corresponding payment for each month credited as qualifying payment under this paragraph, as described in clauses (i) and (ii) of subparagraph (A), shall not apply to any qualifying payment in excess of the twelfth payment credited to a borrower for a year.
(E)
Definitions
In this paragraph:
(i)
The term
covered duty
means any Federal or State duty (including inactive duty training, active duty training, and active duty) performed by a member of a reserve component of an Armed Force, except such term does not include active duty for a period of more than 30 days.
(ii)
The terms
Armed Force
and
active duty for a period of more than 30 days
have the meanings given those terms in section 101 of title 10, United States Code.
