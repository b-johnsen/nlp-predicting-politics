(2)
Eligible activity
The term
eligible activity
means one of the following activities with respect to a public water system:
(A)
Installing or upgrading water treatment facilities.
(B)
Constructing or rehabilitating storage tanks.
(C)
Installing or replacing water transmission and distribution pipes.
(D)
Installing or upgrading pump stations.
(E)
Installing emergency generators.
(F)
Upgrading supervisory control and data acquisition systems.
(G)
Installing advanced metering infrastructure.
(H)
Installing or upgrading wells.
(I)
Facilitating compliance with any primary or secondary drinking water regulation promulgated under this Act.
(J)
Any other activity determined appropriate by the Administrator.
(3)
Rural area
The term
rural area
means an area with a population of 20,000 or fewer inhabitants.
(b)
Water infrastructure enhancement grant program
(1)
In general
The Administrator shall establish a program to provide grants to suppliers of water to carry out eligible activities for the purpose of making infrastructure improvements to public water systems in accordance with this section.
(2)
Other considerations

(B)

(3)
Cost-sharing
The Federal share of the cost of an eligible activity carried out using a grant under this section shall be not more than 90 percent.
(4)
Authorization of appropriations
There is authorized to be appropriated to carry out this section $800,000,000 for each of the fiscal years 2024 through 2029.
