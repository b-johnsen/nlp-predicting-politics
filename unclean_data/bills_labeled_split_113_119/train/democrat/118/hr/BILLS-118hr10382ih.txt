1.
Eligibility of grant funds to satisfy non-Federal share requirements
(a)
National infrastructure project assistance grants
Section 6701(i)(2) of title 49, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following new subparagraph:
(D)
Allowable use of Amtrak grant funds
(i)
In general
If Amtrak is recipient of a grant under this section, Amtrak may use any Federal grant funds provided to Amtrak (other than grant funds awarded under the program and subject to any terms or conditions placed on such Federal grant funds) to carry out any activity described in subsection (h)(1), so long as the involvement of Amtrak in such activity is not limited to monetary contributions.
(ii)
Non-applicability of limitation
Any Federal grant funds used by Amtrak pursuant to clause (i) may not be treated as Federal assistance for purposes of subparagraph (B).
.
(b)
Consolidated rail infrastructure and safety improvements grants
Section 22907(h) of title 49, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph:
(5)
Treatment of Amtrak grant funds
If Amtrak is an applicant under this section, any Federal funds provided to Amtrak may be used to satisfy the non-Federal share requirements.
