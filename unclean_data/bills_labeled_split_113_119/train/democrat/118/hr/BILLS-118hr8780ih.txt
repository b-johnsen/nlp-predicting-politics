1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Prioritizing Resources for Outreach, Safety, Violence Prevention, Youth Empowerment and Resilience Act of 2024
or the
PROSPER Act of 2024
.
2.
Authorization of Youth Gun Violence Prevention Program
(a)
In general
The Attorney General is authorized to carry out a program for youth gun violence prevention.
