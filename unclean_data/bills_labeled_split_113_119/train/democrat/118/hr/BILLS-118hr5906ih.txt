.
2.
Exclusion of expenditures attributable to State medicaid fraud control unit from payment limits
Section 1108(g)(4)(A) of the Social Security Act (
42 U.S.C. 1308(g)(4)(A)
) is amended by inserting
and with respect to fiscal years beginning on or after the date of the enactment of the Medicaid Accountability Act of 2023, if Guam, the Northern Mariana Islands, or American Samoa qualify for a payment under section 1903(a)(6) for a calendar quarter of such fiscal year,
before
the payment shall not be taken into account
.
