(a)
Meat and poultry
Notwithstanding any other provision of law (including regulations), effective beginning on the date of enactment of the
Local Procurement Act

Voluntary Labeling of FSIS-Regulated Products With U.S.-Origin Claims
(89 Fed. Reg. 19470 (March 18, 2024)).

(1)
