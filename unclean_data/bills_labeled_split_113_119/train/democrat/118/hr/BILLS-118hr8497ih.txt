In this subsection, the term
critical hiring need
means personnel necessary for the implementation of this Act and associated work.
(3)
Reporting
Not later than 1 year after the date of enactment of this Act, and annually thereafter for the following 4 years, the Secretary of Homeland Security, in consultation with the Director of the Office of Personnel Management, shall submit to Congress a report that includesâ

Sunset
The authority to make an appointment under this subsection shall terminate on the date that is 5 years after the date of the enactment of this Act.
