(a)
Feasibility study and report
(1)
Study
The Secretaries of the military departments concerned shall conduct a feasability study onâ

(2)
Report
Not later than 120 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Secretaries of the military departments concerned shall submit to the Committees on Armed Services of the House of Representatives and the Senate a report that includes a summary of the findings of such study.
(b)
Military housing areas specified
The military housing areas specified in this subsection are military housing areas for which the applicable rates of basic allowance for housing under section 403 of title 37, United States Code, are, on the date of the enactment of this Act, among the highest twenty-five percent of such applicable rates for all military housing areas located in the continental United States.
