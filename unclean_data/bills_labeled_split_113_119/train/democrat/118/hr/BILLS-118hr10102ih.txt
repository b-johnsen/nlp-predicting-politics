1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Sunsetting the Inspector General for Afghanistan Reconstruction Act
or the
SIGAR Act
.
2.
Sunset and transition assistance for Office of the Special Inspector General for Afghanistan Reconstruction
(a)
Sunset
Section 1229(o)(1) of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2008 (
5 U.S.C. 415
note) is amended by striking
terminate 180 days
and all that follows through the period at the end and inserting
terminate on January 31, 2026.
.
(b)
Eligibility for ICTAP
Any individual who is an employee of the Office of the Special Inspector General for Afghanistan Reconstruction on the date of the enactment of this section shall beâ
(1)
eligible for appointment under the Interagency Career Transition Assistance Program under subpart G of part 330 of title 5, Code of Federal Regulations (or any successor regulation), subject to the terms and conditions of such Program;
(2)
considered to be displaced and ICTAP eligible as those terms are defined in section 330.702 of such subpart (or any successor regulation) for purposes of the Program; and
(3)
considered to have established proof of eligibility under section 330.710 of such subpart (or any successor regulation) for the purposes of the Program.
(c)
Transfer of funds
Any unobligated funds available for such Office on February 1, 2026, may be transferred to and merged with any available account under any Act making appropriations for the Department of State, foreign operations, and related programs. In carrying out any such transfer, the Secretary of State shall consult with the Committees on Appropriations and Foreign Relations of the Senate and the Committees on Appropriations and Foreign Affairs of the House of Representatives.
