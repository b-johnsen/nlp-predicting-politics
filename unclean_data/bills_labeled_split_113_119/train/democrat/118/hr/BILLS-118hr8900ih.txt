(B)
Credit period after election
In the case of a building with respect to which an election is made under subparagraph (A), the term
credit period
shall include the 15-year period beginning on the first day of the first taxable year with respect to which such election is made.
(C)
Limitation
A taxpayer may not make an election under subparagraph (A) with respect to a building with respect to which an election has been made under clause (ii) of such subparagraph in any preceding taxable year.
(D)
Treated as placed in service
For purposes of this section, a building with respect to which a taxpayer makes an election under subparagraph (A) shall be treated as being placed in service on the date on which such election is made.
