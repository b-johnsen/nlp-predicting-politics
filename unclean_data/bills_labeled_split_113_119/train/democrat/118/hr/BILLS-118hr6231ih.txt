Congressional oversight

All policy issuance documents canceled in the prior year.
(C)
An explanation of the methodology used to prioritize the review of policy issuance documents.
(D)
The procedures used by the Department to track and coordinate with Department components the issuance, review, and cancellations of policy issuance documents.
(E)
The number of staff and vacancies within the Management Directorate responsible for supporting the Review Process.
