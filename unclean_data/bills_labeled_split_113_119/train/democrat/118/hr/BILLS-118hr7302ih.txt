1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Contracting Accountability and Transparency Act
or the
CAT Act
.
2.
Disclosure required
The Secretary of Housing and Urban Development shall, not later than 1 year after the date of the enactment of this Act, require each public housing agencies to publicly disclose, on the website of the public housing agency, with respect to each contract entered into by such public housing agency the following informationâ
(1)
all material information about the contract, including the goods and service provided;
(2)
the vendor;
(3)
the date of the solicitation of the contract;
(4)
the bids and quotes solicited; and
(5)
and the official who solicited the contract.
