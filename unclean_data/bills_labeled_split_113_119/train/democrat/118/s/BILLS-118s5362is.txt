1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Fair Ball Act
.
2.
Application of the Fair Labor Standards Act of 1938 to minor league baseball players
Section 13(a)(19) of the Fair Labor Standards Act of 1938 (
29 U.S.C. 213(a)(19)
) is amended by striking
a contract
and all that follows through the period at the end and inserting
an unexpired collective bargaining agreement.
.
