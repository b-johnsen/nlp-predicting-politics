(b)
Grants
The Assistant Secretary may make grants to eligible entities to provide financial planning services related to the needs of family caregivers.
(c)
Eligible entity
To be eligible to receive a grant under subsection (b), an entity shall be a State or local government agency, a nonprofit organization, an area agency on aging, the provider of a multipurpose senior center, an institution of higher education, or a tribal organization.
(d)
Application
To be eligible to receive a grant under subsection (b), an entity shall submit an application to the Assistant Secretary at such time, in such manner, and containing such information as the Assistant Secretary may require.
