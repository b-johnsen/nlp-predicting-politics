Comprehensive guidance
Not later than 1 year after the date of enactment of this Act, the President, acting through the Administrator, shall issue comprehensive guidance to State and Tribal governments relating to the measures and investments described in section 406(b)(3)(A) of the Robert T. Effective date
This Act, and the amendments made by this Act, shall take effect on the date that is 180 days after the date of enactment of this Act.
