(d)
Publication of Consumer Price Index for Elderly Consumers
The Bureau of Labor Statistics of the Department of Labor shall prepare and publish an index for each calendar month to be known as the
Consumer Price Index for Elderly Consumers
that indicates changes over time in expenditures for consumption which are typical for individuals in the United States who have attained age 62.
