1.
First Lieutenant Richard Arne Koski Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 31143 State Highway 65 in Pengilly, Minnesota, shall be known and designated as the
First Lieutenant Richard Arne Koski Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
First Lieutenant Richard Arne Koski Post Office
.
