1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Stabenow-Blunt Behavioral Health Legacy Act
.
2.
Stabenow-Blunt Community Behavioral Health Clinics
(a)
Designation
A clinic that has been certified as a certified community behavioral health clinic in accordance with the requirements of section 223(b) of the Protecting Access to Medicare Act of 2014 (
42 U.S.C. 1396a
note) (or a successor section) shall be known as a
Stabenow-Blunt Community Behavioral Health Clinic
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to a clinic referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to a
Stabenow-Blunt Community Behavioral Health Clinic
.
