RESOLUTION
Amending the Rules of the House of Representatives to require a specific oath be administered to any witness appearing before a committee.
That clause 2(m)(2) of rule XI of the Rules of the House of Representatives is amended by striking
may administer oaths to witnesses
and inserting
shall administer the following oath to witnesses:
Do you solemnly swear or affirm that the testimony that you are about to give is the truth, the whole truth, and nothing but the truth, so help you God?
