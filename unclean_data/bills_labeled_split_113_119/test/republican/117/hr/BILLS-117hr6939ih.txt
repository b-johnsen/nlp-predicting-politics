1.
Prohibition on importation of oil and natural gas from the Russian Federation
(a)
In general
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the President shall take such actions as may be necessary to prohibit the importation of oil and natural gas from the Russian Federation.
(b)
Regulations
The President shall promulgate such regulations that may be necessary and appropriate to carry out this section.
