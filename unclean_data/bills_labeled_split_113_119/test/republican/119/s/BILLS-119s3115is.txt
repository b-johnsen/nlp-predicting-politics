Short title

2.
Creating stability at the National Labor Relations Board
(a)
In general
Section 10 of the National Labor Relations Act (
29 U.S.C. 160
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(n)
An order of the Board issued under this section shall not conflict with a decision of the court of appeals of the United States in the circuit in which the unfair labor practice in question is alleged to have occurred.
.
