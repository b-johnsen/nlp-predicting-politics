1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Stop Antisemitism on College Campuses Act
.
2.
Prohibition of antisemitic events on campus
Section 487(a) of the Higher Education Act of 1965 (
20 U.S.C. 1094(a)
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(30)
(A)
The institution will not authorize, facilitate, provide funding for, or otherwise support any event promoting antisemitism on campus.
(B)
In this paragraph, the term
antisemitism
has the meaning given the working definition of antisemitism adopted by the International Holocaust Remembrance Alliance on May 26, 2016, including the contemporary examples of antisemitism cited by the Alliance.
.
