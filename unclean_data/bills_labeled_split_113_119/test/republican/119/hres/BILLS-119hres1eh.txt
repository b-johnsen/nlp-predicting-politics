In the House of Representatives, U. S.,
January 3, 2025
RESOLUTION
That Kevin McCumber of the State of Illinois be, and is hereby, chosen Clerk of the House of Representatives.
That William McFarland of the State of Maryland be, and is hereby, chosen Sergeant-at-Arms of the House of Representatives.
That Catherine Szpindor of the Commonwealth of Virginia be, and is hereby, chosen Chief Administrative Officer of the House of Representatives.
Kevin F. McCumber,
Clerk.
