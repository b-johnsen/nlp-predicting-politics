1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
No Tax on Length of Service Award Programs Act
or the
No Tax on LOSAP Act
.
2.
Qualified payment exclusion amount increased
(a)
In general
Section 139B(c)(2)(B)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking
$50
and inserting
$1,000
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by this section shall apply to amounts awarded after the date of the enactment of this Act.
