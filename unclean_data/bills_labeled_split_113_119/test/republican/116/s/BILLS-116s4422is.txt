(b)

(1)

There is established in the Treasury of the United States a fund to be known as the Connecting Minority Communities Fund.
(2)
Use of fund
Amounts in the Connecting Minority Communities Fund established under paragraph (1) shall be available to the Assistant Secretary to provide support under the rules promulgated under subsection (a).
(c)
Interagency coordination
When making grants under the Pilot Program, the Assistant Secretary shall coordinate with other Federal agencies, including the Commission and the Department of Education.
(d)

(1)

For each of fiscal years 2021 and 2022, the Inspector General of the Department of Commerce shall conduct an audit of the Pilot Program according to the requirements established under subsection (a)(2)(D).

There is authorized to be appropriated to the Connecting Minority Communities Fund established under subsection (b) $100,000,000 for fiscal year 2020, which shall remain available until expended.
