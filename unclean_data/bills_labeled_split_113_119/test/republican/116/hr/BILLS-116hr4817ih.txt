2.
Requirement for certain Department of Veterans Affairs medical facilities to have physical location for the disposal of controlled substances medications
(a)
In general
The Secretary of Veterans Affairs shall ensure that each covered Department medical facility has a physical location where patients may dispose of controlled substances medications.
