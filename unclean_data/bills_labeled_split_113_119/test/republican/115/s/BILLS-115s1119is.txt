7.
International ocean shipping supply chain information portal demonstration project
(a)
In general
The Federal Maritime Commission may enter into a cooperative agreement (as that term is used in section 6305 of title 31, United States Code) with one or more private, academic, or other non-Federal persons to develop and demonstrate the feasibility of an Internet-based national seaport information portal for the collection and dissemination of information to increase overall supply chain visibility, reliability, and resilience with respect to international ocean shipping.
(b)
Information
Information referred to in subsection (a) includes the information needs of key elements of the import-shipping and export-shipping supply chains, including those regarding container availability, chassis availability, trucking operations, and other ocean carrier, seaport, and marine terminal operations.
