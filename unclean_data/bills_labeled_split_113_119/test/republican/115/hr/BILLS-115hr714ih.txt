1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Legitimate Use of Medicinal Marihuana Act
or the
LUMMA
.
2.
Scheduling of marihuana; prescriptions
(a)
Schedule
Marihuana is moved from schedule I of the Controlled Substances Act to schedule II of such Act.
(b)

(D)

(2)
Production
No provision of the Controlled Substances Act (
21 U.S.C. 801
et seq.) or of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (
21 U.S.C. 301
et seq.) shall prohibit or otherwise restrict an entity authorized by a State, in which marihuana may be prescribed by a physician for medical use, for the purpose of producing marihuana for prescription by a physician for medical use, from producing, processing, or distributing marihuana for such purpose.

4.
Relation of Act to certain prohibitions relating to smoking
This Act does not affect any Federal, State, or local law regulating or prohibiting smoking in public.
