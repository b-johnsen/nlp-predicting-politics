(b)
Authority covered
The authority described in this subsection is the authority of the head of an agency, pursuant to section 3554 of title 44, United States Code, to take any action to limit, restrict, or prohibit access to a website or to test, deploy, or update a cybersecurity measure if the head of the agency determines such action is necessary to carry out the responsibilities of the head of the agency under such section 3554.
(c)
Definition of agency
In this section, the term
agency
has the meaning given the term in section 3502 of title 44, United States Code.
