1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Disaster Assistance Rental Fairness Act
.
2.
Federal assistance to individuals and households
Section 408 of the Robert T. Stafford Disaster Relief and Emergency Assistance Act (
42 U.S.C. 5174
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(k)
Prohibition
Notwithstanding any other provision of this section, the President shall not consider the income of an individual or household in determining whether to provide, or continue to provide, to that individual or household rental assistance under this section.
.
