1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Knowledgeable Innovators and Worthy Investors Act
or the
KIWI Act
.
2.
Nonimmigrant traders and investors
For purposes of clauses (i) and (ii) of section 101(a)(15)(E) of the Immigration and Nationality Act (
8 U.S.C. 1101(a)(15)(E)
), New Zealand shall be considered to be a foreign state described in such section if the Government of New Zealand provides similar nonimmigrant status to nationals of the United States.
