1.
Prohibition on smoking in facilities of the Veterans Health Administration
(a)
Prohibition
Section 1715 of title 38, United States Code, is amended to read as follows:
1715.
Prohibition on smoking in facilities of the Veterans Health Administration
(a)
Prohibition
(1)
No person may smoke indoors in any facility of the Veterans Health Administration.
(2)
No person may smoke outdoors in any facility of the Veterans Health Administration on or after October 1, 2022.
(b)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)
The term
smoke
includes the smoking of cigarettes (including e-cigarettes or electronic cigarettes), cigars, pipes, and any other combustion of tobacco.
(2)
The term
facility of the Veterans Health Administration
