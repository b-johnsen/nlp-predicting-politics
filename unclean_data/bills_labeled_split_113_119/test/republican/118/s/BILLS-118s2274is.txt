1.
Dessie A. Bebout Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 112 Wyoming Street in Shoshoni, Wyoming, shall be known and designated as the
Dessie A. Bebout Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Dessie A. Bebout Post Office
.
