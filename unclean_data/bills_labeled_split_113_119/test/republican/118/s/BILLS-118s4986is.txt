1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Unlock Pipeline Direct Access To Entry Act
or the
UPDATE Act
.
2.
Improving permitting of energy transport facilities by eliminating duplicative State certification over Federal permits or licenses
Section 401(a) of the Federal Water Pollution Control Act (
33 U.S.C. 1341(a)
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(7)
No certification shall be required under this subsection for a license or permit issued to a facility for the transportation of natural gas in interstate or foreign commerce under section 3 or 7 of the Natural Gas Act (
15 U.S.C. 717b
, 717f).
.
