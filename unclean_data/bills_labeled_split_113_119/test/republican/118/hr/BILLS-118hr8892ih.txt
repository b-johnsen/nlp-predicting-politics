(b)
Statement of policy
(1)
In general
It shall no longer be the policy of the United States to apply a
strong presumption of denial
to exports of materials considered Category 1 or 2 of the Missile Technology Control Regime to North Atlantic Treaty Organization (NATO) allies or countries of the
Five Eyes
intelligence alliance.
(2)
Five eyes intelligence alliance defined
In this subsection, the term
Five Eyes intelligence alliance
means Australia, Canada, New Zealand, the United Kingdom, and the United States.
