1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Families of Armed Forces Increased Relief Pensions Act
or the
FAIR Pensions Act
.
2.
Government pension offset exemption
(a)
In general
Section 202(k)(5) of the Social Security Act (
42 U.S.C. 402(k)(5)
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(D)
Subparagraph (A) shall not apply to a monthly insurance benefit of an individual under subsection (e), (f), or (g) if the individual is entitled to such benefit on the basis of the wages and self-employment income of a member of a uniformed service (as defined in section 210(m)), including such a member who died while a member of a uniformed service or who was discharged or released therefrom under conditions other than dishonorable.
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by this Act shall apply to a monthly insurance benefit of an individual for any month beginning on or after the date of enactment of this Act.
