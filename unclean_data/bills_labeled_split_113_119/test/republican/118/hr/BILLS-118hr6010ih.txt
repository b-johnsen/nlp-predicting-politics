1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Stop the Iranian Hostage Business Act
.
2.
Invalidation of passports for travel to Iran
(a)
In general
The Secretary of State shall take such steps as may be necessary, pursuant to the authority provided by section 1 of the Act entitled
An Act to regulate the issue and validity of passports, and for other purposes
, approved July 3, 1926 (
22 U.S.C. 211a
; commonly referred to as the
Passport Act
), to declare United States passports invalid for travel to, in, or through Iran.
