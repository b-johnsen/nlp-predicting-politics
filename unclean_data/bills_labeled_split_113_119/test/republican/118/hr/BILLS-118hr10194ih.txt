1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Flexibility in Housing Act of 2024
.
2.
Application of certain rules by the Secretary of Housing and Urban Development
(a)
Prohibition
The Secretary of Housing and Urban Development may not finalize, implement, or enforce the proposed rule entitled
Affirmatively Furthering Fair Housing
, which was published on February 9, 2023, nor may such rule be reissued in substantially the same form, and a new rule that is substantially the same as such a rule may not be issued, unless the reissued or new rule is specifically authorized by a law enacted thereafter.
(b)
Reinstatement
The final rule of the Department of Housing and Urban Development entitled,
Preserving Community and Neighborhood Choice
(85 Fed Reg 47899, August 7, 2020) shall have the full force and effect of law.
