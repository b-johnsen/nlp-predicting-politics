1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
American Community Survey Choice Act
or the
ACS Choice Act
.
2.
Voluntary response to American Community Survey
Section 221 of title 13, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
(d)
The penalty under subsection (a) shall not apply to any individual who refuses or willfully neglects to answer or otherwise respond to any question in the survey conducted by the Secretary of Commerce commonly referred to as the
American Community Survey
(or any successor survey).
.
