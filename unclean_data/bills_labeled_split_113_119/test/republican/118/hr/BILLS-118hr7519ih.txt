1.
Permitted use of certain homeland security grants
(a)
Temporary increase in limitation
(1)
In general
Notwithstanding the limitation under subsection (b)(2)(A) of section 2008 of the Homeland Security Act of 2002 (
6 U.S.C. 609
), not more than 75 percent of an amount awarded to a recipient of grant under section 2003 or 2004 of such Act (6 U.S.C. 604 and 605) in any fiscal year may be used to pay for covered expenses described in paragraph (2) in support of the permitted uses under subsection (a) of such section 2008 until September 30, 2025.
(2)
Covered expenses described
Covered expenses described in this paragraph are the following:
(A)
The hiring of personnel, including public safety personnel.
(B)
Overtime and backfill costs.
(b)
Other grants
Notwithstanding any limitations under section 2009 of the Homeland Security Act of 2002 (
6 U.S.C. 609a
), including any implementing rules, regulations, or guidance, relating to facility security personnel costs under subparagraph (C) of subsection (c)(1) of such section, the Secretary of Homeland Security, acting through the Administrator of the Federal Emergency Management Agency, shall permit a recipient of a grant under such section to pay not more than 75 percent of an amount awarded to such recipient for such costs until September 30, 2025.
