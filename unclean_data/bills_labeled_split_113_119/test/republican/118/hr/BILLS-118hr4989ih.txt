1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Healthy Babies Act of 2023
.
2.
Infant food combinations and dinners under WIC
Section 17 of the Child Nutrition Act of 1966 (
42 U.S.C. 1786
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(t)
Infant food combinations and dinners
Not later than 1 year after the date of the enactment of this subsection, the Secretary shall update the regulations prescribed pursuant to subsection (f)(11)(A) to amend the supplemental foods available to include infant food combinations and dinners.
.
