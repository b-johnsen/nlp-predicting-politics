1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Maximizing Office Value and Efficiency Act of 2023
or the
MOVE Act of 2023
.

(b)
Commencement of consolidation
Not later than 1 year after submission of the plan pursuant to subsection (a)(2), the Administrator shall commence implementation of such plan.
(c)
Alternative plan
If the Administrator, in coordination with the Director, fails to identify Federal agencies pursuant to subsection (a)(1) and complete a consolidation under subsection (a)(2) not later than 3 years after the submission date of the plan described in subsection (a)(2), the Administrator shall consolidate the General Services Administration personnel based in the GSA Headquarters Building into another federally-owned public building occupied by another Federal agency and prepare the GSA Headquarters Building for sale.
(d)
Sales
(1)
In general
Not later than 1 year after implementation of the plan pursuant to subsection (a)(2), the Administrator shall sell any public buildings vacated as a result of such plan for fair market value at highest and best use.
(2)
Alternative sale
In the event, the alternative plan is carried out pursuant to subsection (c), the Administrator shall sell the GSA Headquarters Building for fair market value at highest and best use.
(3)
Net proceeds
Any net proceeds from a sale under this subsection shall be paid into an account in the Federal Buildings Fund established under section 592 of title 40, United States Code, and upon deposit, such proceeds may be expended only subject to a specific future appropriation.
(e)
Definitions
In this Act:
(1)
Actual utilization rate
The term
actual utilization rate
means the total useable square footage of a public building or federally-leased space divided by the occupancy.
(2)
Administrator
The term
Administrator
means the Administrator of General Services.
(3)
Building utilization
The term
building utilization
means the percentage of utilization generated by comparing the actual utilization rate with the capacity based on a utilization benchmark of 150 useable square feet per person.
(4)
Capacity
The term
capacity
means the total usable square footage of a public building or federally-leased space divided by a utilization benchmark.
(5)
Director
The term
Director
means the Director of the Office of Management and Budget.
(6)
Federal agency
The term
Federal agency
means an executive department covered by the CFO Act of 1990 (
Public Law 101â576
).
(7)
GSA Headquarters Building
The term
GSA Headquarters Building
means the property located at 1800 F Street NW in Washington, District of Columbia.
(8)
Occupancy
The term
occupancy
means the total number of employees performing duties in-person in a public building or federally-leased space at least 5 days per week on a regular basis.
