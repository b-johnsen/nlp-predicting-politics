(d)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)
Decentralized finance
The term
decentralized finance
means blockchain-based protocols that allow users to engage in financial transactions in a self-directed manner so that a third-party intermediary does not effectuate the transactions or take custody of digital assets of a user during any part of the transactions.
