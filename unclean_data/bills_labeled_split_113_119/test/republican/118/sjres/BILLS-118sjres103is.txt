JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Federal Communications Commission relating to
Safeguarding and Securing the Open Internet; Restoring Internet Freedom
.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Federal Communications Commission relating to
Safeguarding and Securing the Open Internet; Restoring Internet Freedom
(89 Fed. Reg. 45404 (May 22, 2024)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
