In the House of Representatives, U. S.,
June 20, 2023
RESOLUTION
Authorizing video recording in the House Chamber during a joint meeting of Congress for certain educational purposes.
That the Speaker, in concurrence with the Minority Leader, is authorized to direct the Chief Administrative Officer to record proceedings during a joint meeting of Congress on June 22, 2023, to provide a virtual reality experience for educational use by the public.
Cheryl L. Johnson,
Clerk.
