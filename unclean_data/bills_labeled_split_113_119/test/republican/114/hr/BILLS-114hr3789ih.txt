1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Honor Our Fallen Heroes Act of 2015
.
2.
Department of Veterans Affairs headstones and markers for individuals whose remains are identified and available but the location of the gravesite is unknown
(a)
In general
Section 2306(b)(1) of title 38, United States Code, is amended by inserting before the period at the end of the first sentence the following:
or an eligible individual whose remains are identified and available but the location of the gravesite is unknown
.
(b)
Applicability
The amendment made by subsection (a) shall apply with respect to deaths occurring before, on, or after the date of the enactment of this Act.
