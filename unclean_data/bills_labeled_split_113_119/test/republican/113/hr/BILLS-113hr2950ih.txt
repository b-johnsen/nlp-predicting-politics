1.
Delay of application of
individual health insurance mandate for individuals who have not attained age
27
(a)
In
general
Section 5000A of the
Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by adding at the end the following new
subsection:
(h)
Delayed
application to individuals who have not attained age 27
In the case of any month beginning before
January 1, 2015, no penalty shall be imposed under subsection (a) with respect
to any individual who has not attained age 27 as of December 31,
2013.
.
(b)
Effective
date
The amendment made by this section shall take effect as if
included in section 1501 of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care
Act.
