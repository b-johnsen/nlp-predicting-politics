1.
Prohibition on acquisition of
land
(a)
Short
title
This Act may be cited
as the
Land Disposal Transparency and Efficiency Act
.
(b)
Prohibition on
acquisition of land
No land
or interests in land may be added by acquisition, donation, transfer of
administrative jurisdiction, or otherwise to the inventory of land and
interests in land administered by the Bureau of Land Management until a
centralized database of all lands identified as suitable for disposal by
Resource Management Plans for lands under the administrative jurisdiction of
the Bureau is easily accessible to the public on a website of the
Bureau.
