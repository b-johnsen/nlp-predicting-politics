3.
Designation of National Nordic Museum
(a)
Designation
The Nordic Museum located at 2655 N.W. Market Street, Seattle, Washington, is designated as the
National Nordic Museum
.
(b)
Effect of designation
(1)
In general
The museum designated by subsection (a) is not a unit of the National Park System.
(2)
Use of Federal funds
The designation of the museum by subsection (a) shall not require Federal funds to be expended for any purpose related to the museum.
