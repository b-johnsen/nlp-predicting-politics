(c)
Effective date; application of amendments
(1)
Effective date
This section shall take effect on July 1, 2017, or on the date of enactment of this Act, whichever is later.
(2)
Application of amendments
The amendments made by this section shall apply to quarterly fees payable under section 1930(a)(6) of title 28, United States Code, as amended by this section, for disbursements made in any calendar quarter that begins on or after the effective date of this section.
