(c)
Energy opportunity zone defined
For the purposes of this section, the term
energy opportunity zone
means a municipality or county that has within its boundaries a nuclear power plant that ceased operation during the 10-year period beginning on January 1, 2018.
.
(b)
Clerical amendment
The table of subchapters for chapter 1 of such Code is amended by adding at the end the following new item:
Subchapter Z. Energy opportunity zones
.
(c)
Effective date
The amendments made by this section shall apply with respect to taxable years beginning after the date of the enactment of this Act.
