Prenatal opioid exposure and neonatal abstinence syndrome
(a)
Research
The Director of NIH shall expand, intensify, and coordinate research and other activities of the National Institutes of Health with respect to the prevention, identification, and treatment of prenatal opioid exposure and neonatal abstinence syndrome.
