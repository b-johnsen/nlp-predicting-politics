1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
National Ocean Policy Act of 2018
.
2.
Legal effect of Executive Order 13547
(a)
Legal effect
Executive Order 13547 (75 Fed. Reg. 43021), entitled
Stewardship of the Ocean, Our Coasts, and the Great Lakes
, signed on July 19, 2010, shall have the full force and effect of law.
(b)
Conforming change
The Executive Order entitled
Executive Order Regarding the Ocean Policy to Advance the Economic, Security, and Environmental Interests of the United States
, signed on June 19, 2018, shall have no force or effect.
