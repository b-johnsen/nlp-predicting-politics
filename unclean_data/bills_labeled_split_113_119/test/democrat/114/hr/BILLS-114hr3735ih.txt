1.
Maya Angelou Memorial Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 200 Town Run Lane in Winston Salem, North Carolina, shall be known and designated as the
Maya Angelou Memorial Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Maya Angelou Memorial Post Office
.
