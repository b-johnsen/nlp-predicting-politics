(b)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)
Broadband internet access service
The term
broadband internet access service
has the meaning given such term in section 8.1(b) of title 47, Code of Federal Regulations (or any successor regulation).
(2)
Commission
The term
Commission
means the Federal Communications Commission.
(3)
Gigabit broadband internet access service
The term
gigabit broadband internet access service
means broadband internet access service with a downstream speed of at least 1 gigabit per second.
