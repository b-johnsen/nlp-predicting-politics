1.
Limitation on use of nondisclosure agreements for employees of the White House or the Executive
Office of the President
(a)
In general
An officer or employee of the White House or the Executive Office of the President may not be required to sign any nondisclosure agreement.
(b)
Limitation on enforcement
No Federal funds may be used to enforce any nondisclosure agreement entered into by an officer or employee of the White House or the Executive Office of the President.
(c)
Application
This Act shall not be construed to allow the disclosure of any information otherwise required by law, rule, or regulation to be kept secret in the interest of national security.
