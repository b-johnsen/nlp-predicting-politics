1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Improving Legal Services for Female Veterans Act
.
2.
Department of Veterans Affairs public-private partnership on legal services for women veterans
(a)
Partnership required
The Secretary of Veterans Affairs shall establish a partnership with at least one nongovernmental organization to provide legal services to women veterans.
(b)
Focus
The focus of the partnership established under subsection (a) shall be on the 10 highest unmet needs of women veterans as set forth in the most recently completed Community Homelessness Assessment, Local Education and Networking Groups for Veterans (CHALENG for Veterans) survey.
