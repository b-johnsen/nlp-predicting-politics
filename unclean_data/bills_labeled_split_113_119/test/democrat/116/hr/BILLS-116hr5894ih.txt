1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Transparency in Prescription Drug Advertising Act
.
2.
Pricing transparency in drug advertising
(a)
In general
Not later than one year after the date of enactment of this Act, the Secretary of Health and Human Services, in consultation with the Commissioner of Food and Drugs and the Administrator of the Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services, shall issue department-wide guidance requiring the list prices of drugs to be included, as appropriate, in all advertising of drugs, including by means of television, newspapers, the internet, or other media.
