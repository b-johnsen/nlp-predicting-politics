1.
Jessie Field Shambaugh Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 101 South 16th Street in Clarinda, Iowa, shall be known and designated as the
Jessie Field Shambaugh Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Jessie Field Shambaugh Post Office Building
.
