In this Act:
(1)
Federal pool
The term
Federal pool
means each of pools 13 through 21 of the San Luis Canal/California Aqueduct, which are owned by the United States and operated by the California Department of Water Resources under the agreement entitled
Agreement Between the United States of America and the Department of Water Resources of the State of California for the Construction and Operation of the Joint-Use Facilities of the San Luis Unit
and dated December 30, 1961.
(2)
Non-federal pool
The term
