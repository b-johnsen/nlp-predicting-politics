Grants or contracts for activities of national significance
The Assistant Secretary shall make grants to or enter into contracts with States, public or private nonprofit agencies or organizations, institutions of higher education, or other organizations, including tribal organizations, for conducting activities of national significance to promote quality and continuous improvement in the support provided to family caregivers and older relative caregivers through the program carried out under section 373 through activities that include, with respect to such program, program evaluation, training, technical assistance, and research.

Administrative expenses
Of the funds appropriated under section 216 to carry out this subsection for a fiscal year, not more than 5 percent may be used by the Administration for administrative expenses.
