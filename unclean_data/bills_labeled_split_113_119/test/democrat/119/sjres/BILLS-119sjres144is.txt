JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8

Fair Credit Reporting Act; Preemption of State Laws

Fair Credit Reporting Act; Preemption of State Laws
(90 Fed. Reg. 48710 (May 12, 2025)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
