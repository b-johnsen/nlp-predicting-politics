1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Extreme Heat Emergency Act of 2025
.
2.
Definition of major disaster
Section 102(2) of the Robert T. Stafford Disaster Relief and Emergency Assistance Act (
42 U.S.C. 5122(2)
) is amended by inserting
extreme temperature,
before
or drought
.
3.
No additional funds
No additional funds are authorized to be appropriated for the purpose of carrying out this Act.
