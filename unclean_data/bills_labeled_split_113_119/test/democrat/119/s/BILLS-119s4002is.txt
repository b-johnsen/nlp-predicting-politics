1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Foreign Service Age Integration and Reform (FAIR) Act of 2026
.
2.
Modification of mandatory retirement age
Section 812(a)(1) of the Foreign Service Act of 1980 (
22 U.S.C. 4052(a)(1)
) is amended by striking
age 65
and inserting
age 67 or the applicable retirement age (as defined in section 216(l) of the Social Security Act (
42 U.S.C. 416(l)
)), whichever is greater,
.
