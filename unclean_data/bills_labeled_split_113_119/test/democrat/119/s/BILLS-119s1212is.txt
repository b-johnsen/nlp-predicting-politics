1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Livestock Owned by Communities to Advance Local Foods Act of 2025
or the
LOCAL Foods Act of 2025
.
2.
Exemptions from inspection
Section 23(a) of the Federal Meat Inspection Act (
21 U.S.C. 623(a)
) is amended by striking
the slaughtering by any person of animals of his own raising, and the preparation by him and transportation in commerce of the carcasses, parts thereof, meat and meat food products of such animals exclusively for use by him and members of his household and his nonpaying guests and employees
and inserting
the slaughtering of animals by any person that is an owner of the animals in whole or in part, or the preparation or transportation in commerce of the carcasses or parts thereof or meat and meat food products from those animals by an owner, if such slaughter, preparation, or transportation is exclusively for the use of an owner or the household, nonpaying guests, or employees of an owner, subject to the condition that if an owner designates an agent to assist in such slaughter, preparation, or transportation, the owner shall maintain custody and specific identification of the carcasses or parts thereof or meat and meat food products, as determined by the Secretary
.
