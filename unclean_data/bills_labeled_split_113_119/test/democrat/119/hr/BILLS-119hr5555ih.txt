1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Monterey Bay National Heritage Area Study Act
.
2.
Study
(a)
In general
The Secretary of the Interior, in consultation with the State of California, local organizations and governmental agencies, Tribal Governments, nonprofit organizations, and other appropriate entities, including the Monterey Bay Economic Partnership, shall conduct a study to assess the suitability and feasibility of designating the areas described in subsection (b) as a National Heritage Area, to be known as the
Monterey Bay National Heritage Area
.
