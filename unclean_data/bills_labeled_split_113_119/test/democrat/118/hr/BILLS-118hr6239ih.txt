1.
Betty W. Henderson Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 250 Edward Street in Branchville, South Carolina, shall be known and designated as the
Betty W. Henderson Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Betty W. Henderson Post Office Building
.
