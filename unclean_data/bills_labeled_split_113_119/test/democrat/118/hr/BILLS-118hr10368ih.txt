Exceptions
(a)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)

The term

7 U.S.C. 5602

(2)
Good
The term

Exception relating to importation of goods
A requirement to block and prohibit all transactions in all property and interests in property referred to in section 1 shall not include the authority or a requirement to impose sanctions on the importation of goods.
