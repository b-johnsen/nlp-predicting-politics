(h)
Definitions
Section 919(b) of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (
21 U.S.C. 387s(b)
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(8)
Definitions
For purposes of this subsection:
(A)
The term
gross domestic sales
means the total amount in dollars, not to include taxes, duties, and fees, of the sale by manufacturers and importers of finished tobacco products in the United States.
(B)
The term
other tobacco product
means a tobacco product that is made or derived from tobacco, or contains nicotine from any source, that does not fit within a product class listed in paragraph (2)(B)(i).
.
