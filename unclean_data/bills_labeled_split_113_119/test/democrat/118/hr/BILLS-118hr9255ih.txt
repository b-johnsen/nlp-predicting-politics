1.
Jack Greenberg Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 114 John Street in New York, New York, shall be known and designated as the
Jack Greenberg Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Jack Greenberg Post Office
.
