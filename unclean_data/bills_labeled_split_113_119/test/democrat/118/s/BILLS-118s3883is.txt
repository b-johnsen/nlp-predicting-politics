1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Showing Up for Students Act
.
2.
Funding for the Office for Civil Rights of the Department of Education
In addition to amounts otherwise made available, there is appropriated, out of any money in the Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for expenses necessary for the Office for Civil Rights of the Department of Education, as established under section 203 of the Department of Education Organization Act (
20 U.S.C. 3413
), $280,000,000 for the fiscal year ending September 30, 2024, to remain available until September 30, 2025.
