1.
Designation of the visitor center for the First State National Historical Park in New Castle, Delaware, as the
Thomas R. Carper Visitor Center
(a)
Designation
The visitor center for the First State National Historical Park to be located at the Sheriff's House in New Castle, Delaware, or any successor to that visitor center that is constructed after the date of enactment of this Act and serves as the primary visitor center for the First State National Historical Park, shall be known and designated as the
Thomas R. Carper Visitor Center
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the visitor center referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Thomas R. Carper Visitor Center
.
