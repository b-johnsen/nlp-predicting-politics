1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Filling Public Safety Vacancies Act
.
2.
Appropriation
(a)
In general
There is appropriated, out of amounts in the Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the fiscal year ending September 30, 2023, to remain available until expended, $162,000,000 for additional amounts for grants for the hiring and rehiring of additional career law enforcement officers under section 1701 of title I of the Omnibus Crime Control and Safe Streets Act of 1968 (
34 U.S.C. 10381
).

3.
Emergency designation
(a)
In general
The amounts provided by this Act are designated as an emergency requirement pursuant to section 4(g) of the Statutory Pay-As-You-Go Act of 2010 (
2 U.S.C. 933(g)
).
(b)
Designation in house and senate
The amounts provided by this Act are designated as being for an emergency requirement pursuant to section 4001(a)(1) of S. Con. Res. 14 (117th Congress), the concurrent resolution on the budget for fiscal year 2022, and section 1(e) of H.
