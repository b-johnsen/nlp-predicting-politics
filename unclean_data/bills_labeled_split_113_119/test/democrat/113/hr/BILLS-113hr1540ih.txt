The Secretary, in cooperation with the
Sacramento Regional County Sanitation District, California, is authorized to
participate in the design, planning, and construction of, and land acquisition

(b), within Sacramento County, California.

The project referred to in subsection (a) shall consist
of three phases, the first of which shall include the planning, design, and
construction of water conveyance and related infrastructure to provide recycled
water to irrigate approximately 4,500 acres of land in southern Sacramento
County.
