1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Reward and Encourage New Business Act of 2013
.
2.
Increased deduction for business start-up expenditures
(a)
In general
Clause (ii) of
section 195(b)(1)(A)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking
$5,000
and inserting
$10,000
.
(b)
Conforming amendment
Section 195(b) of such Code is amended by striking paragraph (3).
(c)
Effective date
The amendment made by this section shall apply to amounts paid or incurred in taxable years beginning after the date of the enactment of this Act.
