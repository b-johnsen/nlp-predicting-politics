IV
116th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. CON. RES. 9
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
Providing for a joint session of Congress to receive a message from the President.
That the two Houses of Congress assemble in the Hall of the House of Representatives on Tuesday, February 5, 2019, at 9:00 p.m., for the purpose of receiving such communication as the President of the United States shall be pleased to make to them.
Passed the House of Representatives January 29, 2019.
Karen L. Haas,
Clerk.
