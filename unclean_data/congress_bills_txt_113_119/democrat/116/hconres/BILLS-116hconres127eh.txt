116th CONGRESS
2d Session
H. CON. RES. 127
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
Directing the Clerk of the House of Representatives to make a correction in the enrollment of H.R. 133.
That, in the enrollment of the bill H.R. 133 the Clerk of the House of Representatives shall make the following correction:
Amend the title so as to read:
Making consolidated appropriations for the fiscal year ending September 30, 2021, providing coronavirus emergency response and relief, and for other purposes.
.
Passed the House of Representatives December 21, 2020.
Cheryl L. Johnson,
Clerk.
