IV
116th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. CON. RES. 81
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
Directing the Clerk of the House of Representatives to make a correction in the enrollment of H.R. 1158.
That, in the enrollment of the bill H.R. 1158, the Clerk of the House of Representatives shall make the following correction: Amend the long title so as to read:
Making consolidated appropriations for the fiscal year ending September 30, 2020, and for other purposes.
.
Passed the House of Representatives December 17, 2019.
Cheryl L. Johnson,
Clerk.
