Recognizing and honoring Dawn Wooten and the integral role that whistleblowers play in holding accountable those that endanger members of the public and violate this Nationâs laws, often at great personal expense and risk.
Whereas more than 20 whistleblower statutes protect employees from retaliation for reporting violations of various laws related to public health and safety;

Whereas Dawn Wooten has been a practicing nurse for more than 10 years, 3 of which she was employed at the Irwin County Detention Center;
Whereas Dawn Wooten contends that she was demoted and reprimanded when she spoke out about abusive practices;
