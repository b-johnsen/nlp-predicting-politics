H. RES. 761
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
December 11, 2019
Ms. Lofgren
(for herself and

) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the
Committee on Rules
RESOLUTION
Permitting individuals to be admitted to the Hall of the House in order to obtain footage of the
House in session for inclusion in the orientation film to be shown to
visitors at the Capitol Visitor Center.
That the Speaker, in consultation with the minority leader, may designate individuals to be admitted to the Hall of the House and the rooms leading thereto in order to obtain film footage of the House in session for inclusion in the orientation film to be shown to visitors at the Capitol Visitor Center.
