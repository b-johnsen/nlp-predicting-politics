1.
Election of Members to Joint Committee of Congress on the Library and Joint Committee on Printing
(a)
Joint Committee of Congress on the Library
The following Members are hereby elected to the Joint Committee of Congress on the Library, to serve with the chair of the Committee on House Administration and the chair of the Subcommittee on the Legislative Branch of the Committee on Appropriations:
(1)
Mr. Butterfield.
(2)
Mr. Rodney Davis of Illinois.
(3)
Mr. Loudermilk.
(b)
Joint Committee on Printing
The following Members are hereby elected to the Joint Committee on Printing, to serve with the chair of the Committee on House Administration:
(1)
Mr. Raskin.
(2)
Mrs. Davis of California.
(3)
Mr. Rodney Davis of Illinois.
(4)
Mr. Loudermilk.
