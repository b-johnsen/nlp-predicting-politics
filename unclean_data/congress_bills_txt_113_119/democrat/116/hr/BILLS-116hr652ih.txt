1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Comprehensive Care for Seniors Act of 2019
.
2.
Directing the Secretary of Health and Human Services to issue a final regulation based on the proposed regulation relating to the Programs of All-Inclusive Care for the Elderly (PACE) under the Medicare and Medicaid programs
Not later than April 1, 2019, the Secretary of Health and Human Services shall issue a final regulation based on the provisions of the proposed regulation titled
Medicare and Medicaid Programs; Programs of All-Inclusive Care for the Elderly (PACE)
(81 Fed. Reg. 54666).
