1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Keeping Our Children Safe Act
.
2.
Appropriations for title I of CAPTA
There is authorized to be appropriated to the Secretary of Health and Human Services, and there is appropriated, out of any monies in the Treasury not otherwise appropriated, to carry out title I of the Child Abuse Prevention and Treatment Act (
42 U.S.C. 5101
et seq.), $20,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2020 through 2024, to be available until expended.
