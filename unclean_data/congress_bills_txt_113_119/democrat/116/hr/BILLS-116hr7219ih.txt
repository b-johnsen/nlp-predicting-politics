1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Digital Overseas Voting Act
.
2.
Permitting submission of Federal write-in absentee ballot by mail, electronic mail, or fax
(a)
Submission of ballot
Section 103(b) of the Uniformed and Overseas Citizens Absentee Voting Act (
52 U.S.C. 20303(b)
) is amended in the matter preceding paragraph (1) by striking the period at the end of the first sentence and inserting the following:
, except that the appropriate State election official shall accept the submission of the ballot by the absent uniformed services voter or overseas voter by mail, electronic mail, or fax.
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by subsection (a) shall apply with respect to any ballot submitted on or after the date of the enactment of this Act.
