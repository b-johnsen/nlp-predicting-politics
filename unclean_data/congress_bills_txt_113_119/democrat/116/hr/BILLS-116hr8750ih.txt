1.
National sea level rise risk analysis
(a)
Purposes
The Secretary of Commerce, acting through the Administrator of the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration, shall establish a National Coastal Data Information System toâ
(1)
combine existing observations, modeling, predictions, products and services into an integrated framework for producing and maintaining authoritative and timely data, maps, and information services which quantify and communicate coastal flood risk to the States;
(2)

(3)

(2)
