1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
No Funds for Apportionment Exclusion Act
.
2.
Prohibition on use of Federal funds
No Federal funds, resources, or fees made available to any Federal agency may be used to implement, administer, enforce, or carry out (including through the issuance of any regulations) any of the policy changes set forth in the Presidential Memorandum on Excluding Illegal Aliens From the Apportionment Base Following the 2020 Census, issued on July 21, 2020.
