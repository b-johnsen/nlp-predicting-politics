1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Allowing More Transparency in Reviewing Advertising Construction Act
or the
AMTRAK Act
.
2.
Limitation on exemption for Amtrak from certain legal requirements
Section 24902(j) of title 49, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
(k)
Limitation on exemptions
Subsection (j) shall not apply to an improvement activity carried out for the purpose of construction or display of an advertisement.
.
