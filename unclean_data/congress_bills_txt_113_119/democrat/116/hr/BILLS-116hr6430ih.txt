Cancellation of meal debts owed as of date of enactment
The Secretary shall reimburse each school food authority and local educational agency participating in the school lunch program under the Richard B. Russell National School Lunch Act (

(b)
Debt collection prohibition during emergency period
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the Secretary shall require that during the emergency period each school food authority and local educational agency described in subsection (a) may not collect unpaid meal fees or use a debt collector (as such term is defined in section 803 of the Consumer Credit Protection Act (
15 U.S.C. 1692a
)) to collect such unpaid meal fees.
