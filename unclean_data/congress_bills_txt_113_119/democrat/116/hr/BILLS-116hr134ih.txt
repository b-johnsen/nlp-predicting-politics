1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Unpaid Intern Protection Act of 2019
.
2.

3.
Procedures
The procedures applicable to a claim for a violation of this Act areâ

(3)

4.
Remedies
The district courts of the United States shall have jurisdiction to grant such legal and equitable relief as may be appropriate for a violation of this Act.
5.
State Immunity
A State shall not be immune under the eleventh article of amendment to the Constitution of the United States from an action in a court of the United States for a violation of this Act. In an action against a State for a violation of this Act, remedies (including remedies at law and in equity) are available for the violation to the same extent as such remedies are available in an action against any public or private entity other than a State.
