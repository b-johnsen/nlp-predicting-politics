Supplemental appropriation
There is appropriated to the Community Development Financial Institutions Fund, out of amounts in the general fund not otherwise appropriated, $1,000,000,000 for fiscal year 2020, for providing financial assistance and technical assistance under subparagraphs (A) and (B) of section 108(a)(1) of the Community Development Banking and Financial Institutions Act of 1994 (
12 U.S.C. 4707(a)(1)
), except that subsections (d) and (e) of such section 108 shall not apply to the provision of such assistance.
