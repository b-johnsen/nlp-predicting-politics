(c)
Effective date
(1)
In general
The amendments made by this Act shall take effect upon the expiration of the 30-day period beginning on the date of enactment of this Act, and shall apply to any civil action commenced on or after such date.
(2)
Commencement of State actions
With respect to a civil action commenced in State court and removed to Federal court, the civil action shall be deemed to have commenced on the date that the civil action was commenced, within the meaning of State law, in State court.
