1.
Placement of statues from territories in national statuary hall
(a)
Placement of statues
Section 1814 of the Revised Statutes of the United States (
2 U.S.C. 2131
) is amended by adding at the end the following new sentence:
For purposes of this section, the term
State
includes American Samoa, Guam, the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands, the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico, and the United States Virgin Islands.
.
(b)
Conforming amendment relating to replacement of statues
Section 311 of the Legislative Branch Appropriations Act, 2001 (
2 U.S.C. 2132
) is amended by adding at the end the following new subsection:
(f)
For purposes of this section, the term
State
includes American Samoa, Guam, the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands, the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico, and the United States Virgin Islands.
.
