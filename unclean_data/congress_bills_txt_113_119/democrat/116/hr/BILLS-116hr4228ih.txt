(k)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)
National voluntary health association
The term
national voluntary health association
means a national nonprofit organization with chapters, other affiliated organizations, or networks in States throughout the United States with experience serving the population of individuals with a rare disease or condition and have demonstrated experience in rare disease or condition research, care, and patient services.
(2)
Rare
The term
rare
, with respect to a disease or condition, means having a prevalence of fewer than 200,000 individuals in the United States.
