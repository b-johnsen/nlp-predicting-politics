1.
Marylu Giddings Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 183 San Geronimo Valley Drive in Woodacre, California, shall be known and designated as the
Marylu Giddings Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Marylu Giddings Post Office
.
