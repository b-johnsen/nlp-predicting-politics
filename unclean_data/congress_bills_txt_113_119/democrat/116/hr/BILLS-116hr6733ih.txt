Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Federal Frontline Worker Leave Protection Act
.
2.
Restore annual leave
(a)
In general
Section 6304(d) of title 5, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph:
(5)
For the purposes of this subsection, the service of an employee responding to the COVIDâ19 pandemic shall be deemed to be an exigency of the public business, and any leave that, by reason of such service, is lost by the employee by operation of this section (regardless of whether such leave was scheduled) shall be restored to the employee and shall be credited and available in accordance with paragraph (2).
.
(b)
Applicability
The amendment made by subsection (a) shall apply to any leave lost before, on, or after the date of enactment of this Act.
