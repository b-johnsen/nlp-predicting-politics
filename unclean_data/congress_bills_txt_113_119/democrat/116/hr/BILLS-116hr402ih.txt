1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Flagstaff Federal Reversion Relief Act
.
2.
Release of reversionary interest, Flagstaff, Arizona
(a)
General removal of interest
Any reversionary interest of the United States pursuant to the Act of July 27, 1866 (14 Stat. 292, chapter 278), shall no longer be subject to the reversionary interest described in that Act.
(b)
Removal of interest in particular parcel
