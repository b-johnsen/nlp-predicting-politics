4.
Eminent domain
(a)
Statement of policy
With respect to the construction and operation of natural gas pipelines, it is the policy of the United States to protect the rights of citizens of the United States to their private property, including by limiting the taking of private property by the Federal Government to situations in which the taking is for public use, with just compensation, and required by the public convenience and necessity, and not merely to advance the economic interests of private parties that would be given ownership or use of the property taken.
