(2)
Rule of construction
Paragraph (1) may only be construed as requiring the Securities and Exchange Commission to amend Regulation D with respect to presentations and communications and not with respect to purchases or sales.
(3)
No pre-existing substantive relationship by reason of event
Attendance at an event described in paragraph (1) shall not qualify, by itself, as establishing a pre-existing substantive relationship between an issuer and a purchaser for the purposes of section 230.506(b) of title 17, Code of Federal Regulations.
