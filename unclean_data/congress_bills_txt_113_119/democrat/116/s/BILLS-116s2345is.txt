2.
Treatment of certain preparatory courses as programs of education for purposes of Post-9/11 Educational Assistance
(a)
In general
Chapter 33
of title 38, United States Code, is amended by adding the following new section:
3315B.
Preparatory courses for licensure, certification, or national tests
(a)
In general
An individual entitled to educational assistance under this chapter shall also be entitled to payment for a preparatory course for a licensing or certification test that is required or used to enter into, maintain, or advance in employment in a predetermined and identified vocation or profession.
