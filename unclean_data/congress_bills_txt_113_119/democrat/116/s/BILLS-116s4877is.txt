(a)
Allowance of credit
In the case of an eligible individual, there shall be allowed as a credit against the tax imposed by this subtitle for the taxable year an amount equal to the lesser ofâ

(b)
Limitation based on modified adjusted gross income
The amount of the credit allowed by subsection (a) (determined without regard to this subsection) for any taxable year shall be reduced (but not below zero) by 2.5 percent of so much of the taxpayer's modified adjusted gross income (as defined in section 24(b)(1)) as exceeds $50,000 ($100,000 in the case of a joint return).
