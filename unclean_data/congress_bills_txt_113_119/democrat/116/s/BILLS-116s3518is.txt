(1)
In general

Increased grant amount
The Administrator may make a grant under this section of not more than $100,000 if the Administrator demonstrates that doing so is necessary to assist eligible small business concerns, private nonprofit organizations, and small agricultural cooperatives that the Administrator determines are vital to their local economies.
(3)
Single award
No eligible small business concern, private nonprofit organization, or small agricultural cooperative may receive or directly benefit from more than 1 award made under this section.
