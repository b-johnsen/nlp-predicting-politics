3.
Limitation on fees charged to civilians for emergency medical treatment provided at military medical treatment facilities
Section 1079b of title 10, United States Code, is amendedâ
(1)
by redesignating subsection (b) as subsection (c); and
(2)
by inserting after subsection (a) the following new subsection (b):
(b)
Limitation on fees for certain treatment
(1)
Under the procedures developed under subsection (a), a military medical treatment facility may not charge a civilian who is not a covered beneficiary any fee for covered treatment provided to the civilian at the facility.
(2)
In this subsection, the term
covered treatment
, with respect to treatment provided to a civilian at a military medical treatment facility, means emergency medical treatment that can be provided only at the military medical treatment facility due to the severity of the condition of the civilian and the location of the civilian.
