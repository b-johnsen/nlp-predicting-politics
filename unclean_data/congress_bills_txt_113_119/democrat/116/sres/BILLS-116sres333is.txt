RESOLUTION
Instructing the managers on the part of the Senate on the bill S. 1790 (116th Congress) to insist
upon the provisions contained in subtitle B of title
XI of the
House amendment (relating to paid family leave for Federal personnel).
That the managers on the part of the Senate at the conference on the disagreeing votes of the two Houses on the House amendment to the bill S. 1790 (116th Congress) are instructed to insist upon the provisions contained in subtitle B of title XI of the House amendment (relating to paid family leave for Federal personnel).
