RESOLUTION
To constitute the minority party's membership on certain committees for the One Hundred Sixteenth
Congress, or until their successors are chosen.
That the following shall constitute the minority party's membership on the following committees for the One Hundred Sixteenth Congress, or until their successors are chosen:
Committee on Energy and Natural Resources:
Mr. Manchin, Mr. Wyden, Ms. Cantwell, Mr. Sanders, Ms. Stabenow, Mr. Heinrich, Ms. Hirono, Mr. King, Ms. Cortez Masto.
