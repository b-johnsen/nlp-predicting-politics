1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Delivering Essentials to Mothers Amid Natural Disasters Act of 2022
or the
DEMAND Act of 2022
.
2.
Breastfeeding supplies and support services
(a)
In general
Not later than 90 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Administrator of the Federal Emergency Management Agency, in consultation with the Co-chairs of the Federal Interagency Breastfeeding Workgroup, shall include breast pumps, other lactation supplies appropriate for use in emergency settings, other equipment necessary to support the safe use of such breast pumps, and services by lactation support providers as eligible for financial assistance under section 408(e) of the Robert T. Stafford Disaster Relief and Emergency Assistance Act (
42 U.S.C. 5174(e)
).
(b)
Lactation support providers defined
In this section, the term
lactation support providers
includes breastfeeding and lactation educators or counselors, including a lactation consultant, breastfeeding counselor, breastfeeding peer counselor, or lactation educator, without regard to the type of license or certification such individual holds.
