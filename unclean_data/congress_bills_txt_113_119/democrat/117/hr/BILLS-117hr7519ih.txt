1.
Dr. Ezra S. Parke Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 2050 South Boulevard in Bloomfield Township, Michigan, shall be known and designated as the
Dr. Ezra S. Parke Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Dr. Ezra S. Parke Post Office Building
.
