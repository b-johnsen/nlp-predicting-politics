(c)
Rules of construction
(1)
State laws
Nothing in this section shall preempt the authority of a State to enact laws relating to assisted suicide within such State.
(2)
Suicide
Nothing in this section shall be interpreted to make unlawful or otherwise criminalize the act of suicide or deliberate self-injury, or punish the person committing any such act.
