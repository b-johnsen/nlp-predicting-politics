John Lewis Civil Rights Fellowship Program
(a)
Establishment
There is established the John Lewis Civil Rights Fellowship Program (referred to in this section as the

The Bureau of Educational and Cultural Affairs (referred to in this section as the

The Board shall annually select qualified individuals to participate in the Fellowship Program. The Board may determine the number of fellows selected each year, which shall be not fewer than 25 whenever feasible.

The term
