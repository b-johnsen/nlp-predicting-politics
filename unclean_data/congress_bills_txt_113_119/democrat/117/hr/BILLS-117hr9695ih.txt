1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Platform Integrity Act
.
2.
Exclusion of promoted content from Good Samaritan liability exemption
Section 230(c)(1) of the Communications Act of 1934 (
47 U.S.C. 230(c)(1)
) is amended by striking the period at the end and inserting the following:
, unless such provider or user has promoted, suggested, amplified, or otherwise recommended such information on such interactive computer service.
.
