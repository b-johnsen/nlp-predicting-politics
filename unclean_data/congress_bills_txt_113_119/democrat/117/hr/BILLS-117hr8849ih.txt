1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Stop Delta Tunnels Act
.
2.
Prohibition on Delta Conveyance Project permit
The Secretary of the Army, acting through the Chief of Engineers, may not issue a permit under section 404 of the Federal Water Pollution Control Act (
33 U.S.C. 1344
) relating to the Delta Conveyance Project referred to in the document published by the California Department of Water Resources on January 15, 2020, entitled
Notice of Preparation of Environmental Impact Report for the Delta Conveyance Project
.
