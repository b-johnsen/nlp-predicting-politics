1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Valley Forge Park Realignment Permit and Promise Act
.
2.
Right-of-way permit for pipeline segment at Valley Forge NHP
(a)
In general
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the Secretary may issue a right-of-way permit pursuant to part 14 of title 36, Code of Federal Regulations (as in effect on the date of the enactment of this Act), for the covered pipeline segment if the covered pipeline segment is relocated to a proposed realignment of Valley Forge Park Road and North Gulph Road within the Park.
(b)
Limitations
(1)
No expansion, upgrade, or improvement
A right-of-way permit issued under subsection (a) shall not allow expansion, upgrade, or improvement of the covered pipeline segment except as may be required by law for safe operation and maintenance of the pipeline.
(2)
Scope of authority
The authority to grant a right-of-way permit under subsection (a) shall apply only to the covered pipeline segment and shall not apply to any other part of the natural gas distribution pipeline system or any other pipeline system within the Park.
(c)
Additional terms and condition
The Secretary may include any additional terms and conditions in the right-of-way permit issued under subsection (a) as the Secretary determines necessary.
