(a)
In general
A group health plan and a health insurance issuer offering group or individual health insurance coverage may not, with respect to a health care provider located in an emergency area during an emergency period that, as of the day before the date of the beginning of such period, had in effect a contractual relationship for furnishing items and services under such plan or coverageâ

unless such lesser reimbursement or such termination is based on fraud, abuse, or other quality concerns with respect to such provider or such provider being placed on the exclusion list maintained by the Inspector General of the Department of Health and Human Services.
