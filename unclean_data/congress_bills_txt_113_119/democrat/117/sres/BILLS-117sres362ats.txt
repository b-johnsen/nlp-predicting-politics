Expressing the support of the Senate for the contributions and achievements of student parents and recognizing September 2021 as National Student Parent Month.

Whereas student parents make up roughly a quarter of the student population, totaling nearly 4,000,000 individuals;
Whereas 70 percent of student parents are women and 43 percent of student parents are single mothers, with nearly half of such student parents being first-generation college students;
Whereas 51 percent of student parents are students of color;
Whereas 47 percent of student parents are military-connected students;
