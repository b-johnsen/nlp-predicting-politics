(2)
Transition from elementary or secondary school
Section 202(d)(7)(B) of such Act (
42 U.S.C. 402(d)(7)(B)
) is amended by adding at the end the following sentence:
An individual who has been in full-time attendance at an elementary or secondary school shall, during a succeeding period of nonattendance at such school, be deemed to be a qualifying post-secondary school student if (i) such period is 4 calendar months or less, and (ii) the individual shows to the satisfaction of the Commissioner that he intends to be in at least half-time attendance at a post-secondary school or a comprehensive transition and postsecondary program immediately following such period.

Effective date
The amendments made by this section shall take effect on the date of enactment of this Act, and shall apply to applications for child's insurance benefits under title II of the Social Security Act (
42 U.S.C. 401 et seq.
) filed on or after such date.
