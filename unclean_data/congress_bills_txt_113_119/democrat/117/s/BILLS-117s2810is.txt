1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Black Lung Benefits Disability Trust Fund Solvency Act of 2021
.
2.
Extension of black lung disability trust fund excise tax
(a)
In general
Section 4121(e)(2)(A)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking
December 31, 2021
and inserting
December 31, 2031
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendments made by this section shall apply on and after the first day of the first calendar month beginning after the date of the enactment of this Act.
