1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Metropolitan Areas Protection and Standardization Act of 2021
or the
MAPS Act of 2021
.
2.
Requirements for modification of CBSA standards
(a)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)
Core-based statistical area
The term
core-based statistical area
has the meaning given the term by the Office of Management and Budget in the Notice of Decision entitled
2010 Standards for Delineating Metropolitan and Micropolitan Statistical Areas
, published in the Federal Register on June 28, 2010 (75 Fed. Reg. 37246), or any successor to that Notice.
(2)
Director
The term
Director
means the Director of the Office of Management and Budget.
