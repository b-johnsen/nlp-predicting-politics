1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Eliminating Limits to Justice for Child Sex Abuse Victims Act of 2021
.
2.
Elimination of the statute of limitations
Section 2255 of title 18, United States Code, is amended by striking subsection (b) and inserting the following:
(b)
Statute of limitations
There shall be no time limit for the filing of any action commenced under this section.
.
