(b)
Program
The Secretary shall establish a program, to be known as the
Lab-Embedded Entrepreneurship Program
, under which the Secretary shall award grants to eligible entities for the purpose of establishing or maintaining a covered program.

In carrying out a covered program, an eligible entity shall give priority to supporting entrepreneurial fellows with respect to professional development and development of a relevant technology.

In participating in a covered program, an entrepreneurial fellow shall retain all rights relating to intellectual property developed by the entrepreneurial fellow through activities carried out under the covered program.
