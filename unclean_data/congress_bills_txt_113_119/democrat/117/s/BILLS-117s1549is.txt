Not later than 180 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Administrator of NASA shall undertake a comprehensive review of the domestic supplier preferences of NASA and the obligations of NASA under the Federal acquisition regulations to ensure compliance, particularly with respect to Federal acquisition regulations provisions that apply to foreign-based subcontractors.

Elements
