1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Combating Deceptive Immigration Enforcement Practices Act of 2022
.
2.
Limitation on immigration officer uniforms
Section 287 of the Immigration and Nationality Act (
8 U.S.C. 1357
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(i)
Immigration officers and agents of the Department of Homeland Security, including officers and agents of U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement or U.S. Customs and Border Protection, may not wear any clothing, accessories, or other items on their person that bear the word
police
while performing duties under the immigration laws.
.
