21 U.S.C. 321(ff)(3)(B)
) is amended in each of clauses (i) and (ii) by inserting
(other than hemp, hemp-derived cannabidiol, or a substance containing any other ingredient derived from hemp)
after
an article

21 U.S.C. 321

hemp

after
made public
