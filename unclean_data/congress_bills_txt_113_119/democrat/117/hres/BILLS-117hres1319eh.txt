117th CONGRESS
2d Session
H. RES. 1319
In the House of Representatives, U. S.,
August 12, 2022
RESOLUTION
Expressing the profound sorrow of the House of Representatives on the death of the Honorable Jackie Walorski.
That the House has heard with profound sorrow of the death of the Honorable Jackie Walorski, a Representative from the State of Indiana.
That the House has heard with profound sorrow of the death of Zachery Potts and Emma Thomson, staff members of the Honorable Jackie Walorski.
That the Clerk communicate these resolutions to the Senate and transmit a copy thereof to the families of the deceased.
That when the House adjourns today, it adjourn as a further mark of respect to the memory of the deceased.
Cheryl L. Johnson,
Clerk.
