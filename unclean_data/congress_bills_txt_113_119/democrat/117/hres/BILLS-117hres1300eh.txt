RESOLUTION
Waiving a requirement of clause 6(a) of rule XIII with respect to consideration of certain resolutions reported from the Committee on Rules, and for other purposes.
That the requirement of clause 6(a) of rule XIII for a two-thirds vote to consider a report from the Committee on Rules on the same day it is presented to the House is waived with respect to any resolution reported through the legislative day of July 29, 2022.
2.
(a)
Section 7 of House Resolution 1289 shall have no force or effect.
(b)
Clause 7(a)(1) of rule XV shall not apply through the legislative day of Friday, September 16, 2022.
Cheryl L. Johnson,
Clerk.
