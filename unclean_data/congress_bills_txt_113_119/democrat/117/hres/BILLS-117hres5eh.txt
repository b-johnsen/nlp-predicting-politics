IV
117th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 5
In the House of Representatives, U. S.,
January 3, 2021
RESOLUTION
That the Clerk be instructed to inform the President of the United States that the House of Representatives has elected Nancy Pelosi, a Representative from the State of California as Speaker, and Cheryl L. Johnson, a citizen of the State of Louisiana as Clerk, of the House of Representatives of the One Hundred Seventeenth Congress.
Cheryl L. Johnson,
Clerk.
