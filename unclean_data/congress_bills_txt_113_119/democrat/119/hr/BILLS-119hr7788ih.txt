1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Maryland Whole Watershed Program Federal Partnership Act
.
2.
Chesapeake Bay Program Office authority
(a)
In general
The Chesapeake Bay Program Office shall serve as an advisory member of the State Management Team for the Maryland Whole Watershed Program and any other substantially similar program in a State.

Authorized activities

(c)
Chesapeake Bay Program Office defined
