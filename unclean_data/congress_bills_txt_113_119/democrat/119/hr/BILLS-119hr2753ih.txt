.

Section 310 of the Congressional Budget Act of 1974 (
2 U.S.C. 641

(h)
Limitation on cuts to certain programs

(1)
reduces enrollment or benefits for individuals enrolled in the Medicaid program under title XIX of the Social Security Act; or
(2)
reduces eligibility or benefits for households that participate in the supplemental nutrition assistance program under the Food and Nutrition Act of 2008.
(i)
Expiration of limitation on cuts to certain programs
Subsection (h) shall expire on January 20, 2029.
