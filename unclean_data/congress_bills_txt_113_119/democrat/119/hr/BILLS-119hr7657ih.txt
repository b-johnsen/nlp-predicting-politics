1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Military Family Diaper Partnership Act
.
2.
Provision of funds for establishment of Military Family Diaper Fund
(a)
Provision of Funds
The Secretary of Defense shall seek to enter an agreement with the National Diaper Bank Network to provide to the Network $1,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2027 through 2030 for the purpose of establishing a military family diaper fund to distribute diapers and diapering supplies to military families.
