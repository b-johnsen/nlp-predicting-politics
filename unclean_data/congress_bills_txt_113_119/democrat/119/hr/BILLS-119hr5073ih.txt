1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Immigration Notification for Facility Oversight and Relocation Management Act of 2025
or as the
INFORM Act of 2025
.
2.
In general
In the case of any individual detained by the Secretary of Homeland Security pursuant to the immigration laws (as such term is defined in section 101(a) of the Immigration and Nationality Act) at a facility, notice must be provided to the immediate family of that individual not later than 24 hours after that individual is transferred to a different facility.
