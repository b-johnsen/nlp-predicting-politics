2.
Restoration of access to curricula, books, and other learning materials
(a)
Restoration and temporary limitation on further restrictions

As soon as practicable after the date of the enactment of this Act, but not later than 30 days after such date, the Secretary of Defense shall restore access to all curricula, books, and other learning materials that were available at covered educational institutions before January 20, 2025.
