1.
Bernie Bluestein Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 1300 East Northwest Highway in Palatine, Illinois, shall be known and designated as the
Bernie Bluestein Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Bernie Bluestein Post Office Building
.
