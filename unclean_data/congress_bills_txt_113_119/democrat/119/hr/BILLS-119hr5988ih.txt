1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Ceasing Outrageous Restitution and Reimbursement Under President Trump Act
or the
CORRUPT Act
.
2.
Exception to Federal tort claims act
Section 2680 of title 18, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
(o)
Any claim brought by the President of the United States or a covered relative of such President during the term for which such President shall have been elected. For the purposes of this subsection, a
covered relative
means a spouse, child, sibling, or in-law of the President.
.
