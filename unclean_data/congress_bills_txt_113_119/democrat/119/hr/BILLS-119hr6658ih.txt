(b)
Revisions required
Not later than 60 days after the date of the enactment of this section, part 3 of the Federal Acquisition Regulation and part 200, Code of Federal Regulations, shall be revised to prohibit any officer or employee of an executive branch agency or department from awarding a covered Federal award if to do so would violate section 2(a) of this Act in accordance with this section.
(c)
Guidance
The Office of Government Ethics shall issue regulatory guidance on this section and the amendment made by this section.
