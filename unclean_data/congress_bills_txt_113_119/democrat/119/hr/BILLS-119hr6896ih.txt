2.
Oversight and approval for certain building demolition or substantial alteration
(a)
Approval of Congress for demolition
(1)
In general
A public building located in the District of Columbia shall not be demolished, in whole or in part, or substantially altered without the express authority of Congress.
(2)
Requirement
As a requirement for a demolition subject to paragraph (1), the site to be demolished shall have an approved and finalized plan for construction.
(3)
Definitions
In this subsection:
(A)
Public building
The term
public building
means a building, whether for single or multitenant occupancy, and its grounds, approaches, and appurtenances, which is generally suitable for use as office or storage space or both by 1 or more Federal agencies or mixed-ownership Government corporations.
