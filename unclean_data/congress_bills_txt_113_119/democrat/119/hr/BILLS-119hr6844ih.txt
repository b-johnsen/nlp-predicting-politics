1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Tobacco Tracking, Reporting, and Accountability for Compliance in Enforcement Act
or the
Tobacco TRACE Act
.
2.
Tobacco product codes for tracking or tracing
Section 920(b)(3) of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (
21 U.S.C. 387t(b)(3)
) is amended by striking
The Secretary may
and inserting
Beginning not later than June 1, 2026, the Secretary shall
.
