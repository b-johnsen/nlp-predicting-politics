1.
Coast Guard policy on prohibiting divisive or hate symbols and flags
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the Commandant of the Coast Guard may not issue any guidance that is less restrictive on prohibiting divisive or hate symbols and flags than the memorandum titled
Coast Guard Policy and Lawful Order Prohibiting Divisive or Hate Symbols and Flags
issued on November 20, 2025 (COMDTINST 12750.4).
