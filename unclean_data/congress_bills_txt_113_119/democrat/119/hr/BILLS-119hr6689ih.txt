1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Ensuring Transparency, Honesty, and Integrity for Contracting, Acquisition, and Lifecycle Procurement Act
or the
ETHICAL Procurement Act
.

(b)
Regulations
Not later than 30 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Secretary of Defense shall issue regulations to implement this section, including definitions, thresholds, and procedures for identifying individuals and entities with respect to which the prohibition under subsection (a) applies.
(c)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)
Immediate family member
The term
immediate family member
means a parent, child, sibling, spouse, or domestic partner.
(2)
Special Government employee
The term
special Government employee
has the meaning given such term in section 202 of title 18, United States Code.
