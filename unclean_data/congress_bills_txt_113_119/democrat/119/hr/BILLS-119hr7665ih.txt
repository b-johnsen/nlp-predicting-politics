1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Friends in the Field Act
.
2.
Addition of biological pest control to high-priority research and extension areas
Section 1672(d) of the Food, Agriculture, Conservation, and Trade Act of 1990 (
7 U.S.C. 5925(d)
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(21)
Biological pest control
Research and extension grants may be made under this section for the purposes of supporting research, development, or education materials, information, and outreach programs regarding biological pest control to limit crop damage and food-borne illnesses.
.
