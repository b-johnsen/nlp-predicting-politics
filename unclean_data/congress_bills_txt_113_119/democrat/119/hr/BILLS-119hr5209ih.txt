Section 213A of the Caribbean Basin Economic Recovery Act (
19 U.S.C. 2703a
), as amended by this Act, is further amended by adding at the end the following:

Technical assistance To increase and diversify exports from Haiti to the United States
(1)
In general
The United States Trade Representative shall provide technical assistance to the agencies, entities, and individuals specified in clauses (i) through (v) of paragraph (2)(B), to increase and diversify exports from Haiti to the United States with the objectives ofâ
(A)

(C)
developing utilization strategies for Haiti to further explore export opportunities under United States trade preference programs.

(C)

Priority
Technical assistance provided pursuant to this subsection shall focus onâ
(A)

A description of the actions taken regarding the participatory process under paragraph (3)(B) during the preceding calendar year, including an identification of the agencies, entities, and individuals specified in clauses (i) through (v) of paragraph (2)(B) that participated.
(B)
Coordination
The President shall seek to coordinate with the International Trade Center in carrying out this paragraph.
