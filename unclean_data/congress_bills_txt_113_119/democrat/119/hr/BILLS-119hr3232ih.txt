1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Federal-State Partnership for Intercity Passenger Rail Reauthorization Act
.
2.
Authorization of appropriations for Federal-State partnership for intercity passenger rail grants
Section 24911(h) of title 49, United States Code, is amended to read as follows:
(h)
Authorization of appropriations
(1)
In general
There is authorized to be appropriated to the Secretary for use carrying out this section $7,500,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2027 through 2031, to remain available until expended.
(2)
Project management oversight expenses
The Secretary may use up to 2 percent of amounts appropriated under paragraph (1) in a fiscal year to carry out project management oversight with respect to any grant program under this title authorized to be carried out in such fiscal year.
.
