1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Persian Gulf Act
.
2.
Prohibition on use of Federal funds to rename Persian Gulf
(a)
In general
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, no Federal funds are authorized to be appropriated or otherwise made available to rename the Persian Gulf.
(b)
References
No law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States may refer to the Persian Gulf by a different name without express authorization of Congress.
