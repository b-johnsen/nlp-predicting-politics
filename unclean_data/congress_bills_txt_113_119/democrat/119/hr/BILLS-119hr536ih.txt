1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Agricultural Environmental Stewardship Act of 2025
.
2.
Extension of energy credit for qualified biogas property
(a)
In general
Section 48(c)(7)(C)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking
December 31, 2024
and inserting
December 31, 2025
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by this section shall apply to property the construction of which begins after December 31, 2024.
