JOINT RESOLUTION
Terminating the national emergency declared to impose duties on articles imported from Canada.
That, pursuant to section 202 of the National Emergencies Act (
50 U.S.C. 1622
), the national emergency declared on February 1, 2025, by the President in Executive Order 14193 (90 Fed. Reg. 9113) is terminated.
