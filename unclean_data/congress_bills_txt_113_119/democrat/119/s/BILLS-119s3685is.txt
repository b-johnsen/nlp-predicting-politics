1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
No Taxpayer Funds for Corporate Investment in Venezuelan Oil Act
.
2.
No payment of reimbursements for qualified capital expenditures
(a)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)
Capital expenditure
The term
capital expenditure
means any amount paid for new buildings or for permanent improvements or betterments made to increase the value of any property or estate.
(2)
Person
The term
person
meansâ
(A)
any United States citizen or alien admitted for permanent residence in the United States; and
(B)
any foreign or domestic corporation, partnership, or other organization.
(3)
Qualified capital expenditure
The term
qualified capital expenditure
means a capital expenditure made in the oil and gas sector located in the Bolivarian Republic of Venezuela, or a legal successor.
(b)
Prohibiting certain payments
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, no funds may be obligated, expended, or otherwise made available from the Treasury of the United States or any account owned, controlled, or accessible by the United States or a person acting on behalf of the United States for the payment of reimbursements for qualified capital expenditures by a person.
