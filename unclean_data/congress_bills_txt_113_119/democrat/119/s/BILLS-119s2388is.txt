(f)
Uses of grants
(1)
In general
Amounts from grants received under this section may be used for engineering, design, construction, and final testing of alternative water source projects designed to meet critical water supply needs.
(2)
Prohibition
Amounts from grants received under this section may not be used for planning, feasibility studies, operation, or maintenance.
(3)
Intelligent water infrastructure technologies
(A)
In general
Amounts from grants received under this section may be used for engineering, design, construction, implementation, training, and operations relating to the adoption and use of intelligent water infrastructure technology.
(B)
Applicability
For purposes of paragraph (2), any costs with respect to intelligent water infrastructure technology shall not be considered operation or maintenance costs.
