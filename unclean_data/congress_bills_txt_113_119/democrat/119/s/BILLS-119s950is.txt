1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Save Our Forests Act of 2025
.
2.
Definitions
In this Act:
(1)
National forest system
The term
National Forest System
has the meaning given the term in section 11(a) of the Forest and Rangeland Renewable Resources Planning Act of 1974 (
16 U.S.C. 1609(a)
).
(2)
Secretary
The term
Secretary
means the Secretary of Agriculture.
(3)
Service
The term
Service
means the Forest Service.
