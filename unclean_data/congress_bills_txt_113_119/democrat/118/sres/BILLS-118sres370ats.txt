To constitute the majority party's membership on certain committees for the One Hundred Eighteenth Congress, or until their successors are chosen.

Committee on Foreign Relations:
Mr. Cardin (Chair), Mr. Menendez, Mrs. Shaheen, Mr. Coons, Mr. Murphy, Mr. Kaine, Mr. Merkley, Mr. Booker, Mr. Schatz, Mr. Van Hollen, Ms. Duckworth.
Committee on Small Business and Entrepreneurship:
Mrs. Shaheen (Chair), Ms. Cantwell, Mr. Cardin, Mr. Markey, Mr. Booker, Mr. Coons, Ms. Hirono, Ms. Duckworth, Ms. Rosen, Mr. Hickenlooper.
