CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
Authorizing the use of Emancipation Hall in the Capitol Visitor Center for a historic ceremony to commemorate Slavery Remembrance Day.
1.
Short title
This resolution may be cited as the
Original Resolution to Commemorate Slavery Remembrance Day in Emancipation Hall
.
2.
Use of Emancipation Hall for ceremony to commemorate Slavery Remembrance Day
(a)
Authorization
Emancipation Hall in the Capitol Visitor Center is authorized to be used on August 10, 2024, for a ceremony to commemorate Slavery Remembrance Day.
(b)
Preparations
Physical preparations for the conduct of the ceremony described in subsection (a) shall be carried out in accordance with such conditions as the Architect of the Capitol may prescribe.
