RESOLUTION
Honoring the BAPS Hindu-American community and the 50th Anniversary of its Mandir in Flushing, New York.
Whereas the Bochansanwasi Akshar Purushottom Swaminarayan Sanstha (BAPS) is a pillar of the Hindu-American community, providing spiritual guidance, community service, and cultural enrichment;
Whereas BAPS has grown to an international movement with millions of followers, dedicated to the pillars of practical spirituality and Hindu values, traditions, and charitable activities;
Whereas the BAPS Shri Swaminarayan Mandir in Flushing, New York, was established 50 years ago and has since become a beacon of faith, culture, and community service in the region;
Whereas the Mandir has served as a place of worship, education, and cultural preservation, offering various programs and services to individuals of all ages, backgrounds, and beliefs;
Whereas the BAPS community has consistently contributed to society by participating in civil society organizations, interfaith dialogues, health fairs, educational programs, and disaster relief efforts;
Whereas the dedication and commitment of the BAPS volunteers and devotees have positively impacted the lives of countless individuals, fostering a sense of unity, compassion, and service; and
Whereas the celebration of the 50th anniversary of the BAPS Shri Swaminarayan Mandir in Flushing, New York, is a significant milestone that reflects the enduring legacy and contributions of the BAPS Hindu-American community: Now, therefore, be it
