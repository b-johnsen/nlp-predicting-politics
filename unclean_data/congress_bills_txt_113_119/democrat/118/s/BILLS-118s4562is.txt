2.
Prohibition on intentional addition of excrement to animal food
Section 301 of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (
21 U.S.C. 331
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(jjj)
(1)
The manufacture or the introduction or delivery for introduction into interstate commerce of an animal food that contains intentionally-added excrement.
(2)
For purposes of subparagraph (1), the use of livestock or poultry manure as crop fertilizer shall not be considered to be the intentional addition of excrement.
