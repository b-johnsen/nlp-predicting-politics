Sunset
The authority and requirements under paragraphs (2), (3), (4), and (5) shall terminate on December 31, 2027.
(7)
Family member defined
In this subsection, the term

means a spouse, father, mother, child, brother, sister, grandparent, grandchild, aunt, uncle, nephew, niece, cousin, father-in-law, mother-in-law, son-in-law, daughter-in-law, brother-in-law, sister-in-law, stepfather, stepmother, stepson, stepdaughter, stepbrother, stepsister, half brother, or half sister.
