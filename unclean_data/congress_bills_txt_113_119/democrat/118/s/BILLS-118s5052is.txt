1.
Prohibition on importation of critical minerals from the Russian Federation
(a)
In general
On and after the date of the enactment of this Act, the importation of critical minerals extracted or otherwise produced in the Russian Federation is prohibited.
(b)
Critical mineral defined
In this section, the term
critical mineral
has the meaning given that term in section 7002(a) of the Energy Act of 2020 (
30 U.S.C. 1606(a)
).
