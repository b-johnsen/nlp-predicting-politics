(b)
Recovery of costs
The Commission shall allow a transmission developer of an interregional transmission project that meets the criteria described in subsection (a)(2)(A) to submit to the Commission a request to recover the cost of the project if the transmission developer demonstrates to the satisfaction of the Commission thatâ
(1)

(2)

(3)
the benefits of the project outweigh the costs of the project.
(c)
Rulemaking
Not later than 18 months after the date of enactment of this Act, the Commission shall promulgate a final rule to implement this section.
