1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Heat pump Energy Assistance and Training Act
or the
HEAT Act
.
2.
Definitions
In this Act:
(1)
Industrial heat pump
The term
industrial heat pump
means a vapor compression device that transforms low grade production waste heat by increasing the temperature of air or process fluid, such as water, to meet the heating needs of agricultural, industrial, or commercial processes.
(2)
Secretary
The term
Secretary
means the Secretary of Agriculture.

(b)
Coordination
The Secretary is encouraged to coordinate with the Department of Energy with respect to the implementation of industrial heat pump programs.
(c)
Authorization of appropriations
There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this section such sums as are necessary for each of fiscal years 2024 through 2028.
