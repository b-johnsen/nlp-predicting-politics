Operation Stonegarden
(a)
In general
Subtitle A of title XX of the Homeland Security Act of 2002 (
6 U.S.C. 601 et seq.

Establishment
There is established in the Department a program, which shall be known as
Operation Stonegarden
, under which the Secretary, acting through the Administrator, shall award grants to eligible law enforcement agencies, through the State administrative agency, to enhance border security in accordance with this section.

(d)
Period of performance
The Secretary shall award grants under this section to grant recipients for a period of not less than 3 years.
