1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Tribal Nutrition Program Administration Act of 2023
.
2.
Elimination of the administrative and distribution cost sharing requirement
Section 4(b) of the Food and Nutrition Act of 2008 (
7 U.S.C. 2013(b)
) is amended by striking paragraph (4) and inserting the following:
(4)
Administrative and distribution costs
The Secretary shall pay 100 percent of administrative costs and distribution costs on Indian reservations.
.
