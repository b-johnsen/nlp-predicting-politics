Establishment
The Secretary shall establish a competitive grant program to provide financial assistance to eligible entities to establish and carry out innovative programs so that business concerns owned and controlled by socially and economically disadvantaged individuals may achieve proficiency to compete, on an equal basis, for contracts and subcontracts in projects carried out with financial assistance from the Secretary.
