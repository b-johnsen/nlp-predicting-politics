1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
American Tax Dollars for American Solar Manufacturing Act
.
2.
Prohibition on allowance of advanced manufacturing production credit for components produced by foreign entities of concern
(a)
In general
Section 45X(d)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph:
(5)
Prohibition on allowance of credit for components produced by foreign entities of concern
No credit shall be allowed under subsection (a) with respect to any eligible component which is produced by a foreign entity of concern (as defined in section 9901(8) of the William M. (Mac) Thornberry National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2021 (
15 U.S.C. 4651(8)
)).
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by this section shall apply to components produced and sold after the date of enactment of this Act.
