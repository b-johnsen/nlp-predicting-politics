Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Dobbins Civil Engineering Support Act
.
2.
Authorization of amounts for planning and design of a civil engineering group facility at Dobbins Air Reserve Base, Georgia
There is authorized to be appropriated to the Secretary of Defense for fiscal year 2025 $1,200,000 for the planning and design of a civil engineering group facility at Dobbins Air Reserve Base, Georgia.
