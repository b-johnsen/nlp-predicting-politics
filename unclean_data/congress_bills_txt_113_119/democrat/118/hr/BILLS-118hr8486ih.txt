1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Thriving Communities Act of 2024
.
2.
Thriving communities program
(a)
Establishment
The Secretary of Transportation, in coordination with the Secretary of Housing and Urban Development, shall establish a thriving communities grant program to provide technical assistance and capacity building to help underserved communities advance transformative, equitable and environmentally friendly infrastructure projects.
