Automatic extension of residential lease during deployment by lessee under Servicemembers Civil Relief Act
(a)
In general
The Servicemembers Civil Relief Act (
50 U.S.C. 3901 et seq.
) is amended by inserting after section 305A (
50 U.S.C. 3956
) the following new section:
305B.
Extension of residential lease
(a)
Automatic extension
Subject to subsection (b), the term of a residential lease of premises occupied or intended to be occupied by a servicemember that shall end during a deployment of such servicemember shall be extended on a monthly basis through the month in which the day that is 30 days after the end of such deployment occurs.
(b)

Deployment defined
In this section, the term
deployment
means the movement or mobilization of a servicemember to a location for a period of longer than 30 days and not longer than 540 days pursuant to temporary or permanent official ordersâ

(2)
