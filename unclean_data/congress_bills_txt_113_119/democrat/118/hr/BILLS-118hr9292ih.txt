Briefing on the future role and structure of Multi-Domain Task Forces
(a)
In general
Not later than December 31, 2024, the Secretary of the Army shall provide to the Committees on Armed Services of the Senate and the House of Representatives a briefing on the plans of the Army with respect to the future role and structure of Multi-Domain Task Forces.
