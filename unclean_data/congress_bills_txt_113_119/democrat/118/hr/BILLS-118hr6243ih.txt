(c)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)
Employee; employer
The terms
employee
and
employer
have the meaning given such terms in section 3 of the Occupational Safety and Health Act of 1970 (
29 U.S.C. 652
).
(2)
Opioid overdose reversal drug or device
The term
opioid overdose reversal drug or device
means an opioid overdose reversal drug or device approved, licensed, cleared, or otherwise authorized for marketing by the Food and Drug Administration.
(3)
Secretary
The term
Secretary
means the Secretary of Labor.
