1.
Millie Dunn Veasey Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 2777 Brentwood Road in Raleigh, North Carolina, shall be known and designated as the
Millie Dunn Veasey Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Millie Dunn Veasey Post Office
.
