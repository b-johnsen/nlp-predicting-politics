1.
Repayment plan for public service employees
Section 455(m) of the Higher Education Act of 1965 (
20 U.S.C. 1087e(m)
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(5)
Fulbright eligibility
For purposes of loan cancellation under this subsection, participation in a Fulbright Teacher Exchange Program or the Fulbright English Teaching Assistant Program shall be treated as employment in a public service job.
