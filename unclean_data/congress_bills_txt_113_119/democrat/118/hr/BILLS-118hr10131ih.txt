1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Health Coverage for Inclusive and Valued Families Act of 2024
or the
Health Coverage for IVF Act of 2024
.

Fertility treatment and care defined
For purposes of paragraph (1)(K), the term
fertility treatment and care
means the following medically appropriate items and services furnished to an individual regardless of whether such individual has a diagnosis of infertility:
(A)
Preservation of human oocytes, sperm, or embryos for later reproductive use.
(B)
Artificial insemination, including intravaginal insemination, intracervical insemination, and intrauterine insemination.
(C)
Assisted reproductive technology, including in vitro fertilization and other treatments or procedures in which reproductive genetic material, such as oocytes, sperm, fertilized eggs, and embryos, are handled, when clinically appropriate.
(D)
Genetic testing of embryos.
(E)
Medications prescribed or obtained over-the-counter, as indicated for fertility.
(F)
Gamete donation.
(G)
Such other information, referrals, treatments, procedures, medications, laboratory testing, technologies, and services relating to fertility as the Secretary determines appropriate.
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendments made by subsection (a) shall apply to plan years beginning on or after the date that is 1 year after the date of the enactment of this Act.
