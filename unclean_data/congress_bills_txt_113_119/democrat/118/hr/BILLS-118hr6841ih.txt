2.
Amendments to National Estuarine Research Reserve System program
(a)
Designation of additional reserves
(1)
In general
Not later than 5 years after the date of the enactment of this section, the Secretary shall designate not less than 5 new national estuarine reserves in the System.
(2)
Prioritization
In making each designation under paragraph (1), the Secretary shall prioritize nominated sites that will promote the following with respect to the System:
(A)
The presence of a National Estuarine Research Reserve in each coastal State.
(B)
Full representation of biogeographic regions to ensure research in areas with distinct biodiversity or estuarine geography.
