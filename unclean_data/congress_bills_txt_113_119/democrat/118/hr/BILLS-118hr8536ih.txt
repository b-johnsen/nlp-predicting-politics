There is established, within the Department of Health and Human Services, an Office of the Ombudsperson for Immigrant Children in Immigration Custodyâ

7.
Rule of construction
Nothing in the Act shall be construed to preclude or limit Flores settlement agreement class counsel from conducting independent investigations or seeking enforcement actions relating to violations of the Flores settlement agreement in any appropriate district court of the United States.
