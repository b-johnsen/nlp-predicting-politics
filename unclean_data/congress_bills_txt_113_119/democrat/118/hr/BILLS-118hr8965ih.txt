3.
Plan and policy reviews
(a)
Sense of Congress
It is the sense of Congress that the Administrator of the National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) should take every action to ensure that robust cybersecurity measures are in place to protect sensitive technology data relating to space systems developed within NASA, at NASA contractors, or under commercial services arrangements.

(d)
