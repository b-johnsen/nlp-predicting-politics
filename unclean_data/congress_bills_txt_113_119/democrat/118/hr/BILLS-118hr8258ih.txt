1.
Redesignation of the Cottonwood Visitor Center at Joshua Tree National Park as the
Senator Dianne Feinstein Visitor Center
(a)
Redesignation
The Cottonwood Visitor Center at Joshua Tree National Park shall be known and designated as the
Senator Dianne Feinstein Visitor Center
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the visitor center referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Senator Dianne Feinstein Visitor Center
.
