1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the

.

Monitoring wildfires by satellite
(a)
Grant program To monitor wildfires by satellite

Establishment
Not later than one year after the date of the enactment of this section, the Secretary shall establish a competitive grant program under which the Secretary shall make at least three grants to eligible entities to monitor wildfires by satellite (in this section referred to as the
Program
).

Each grant awarded under the Program shall be in an amount the Secretary determines appropriate.
