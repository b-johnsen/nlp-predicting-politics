1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Denying Infinite Security and Government Resources Allocated toward Convicted and Extremely Dishonorable Former Protectees Act
or the
DISGRACED Former Protectees Act
.
2.
Denying certain felons secret service protection
Section 3056(a) of title 18, United States Code, is amended by striking
declined.
and inserting
declined. The protection authorized in paragraphs (1) through (8) shall terminate for any person upon sentencing following conviction for a Federal or State offense that is punishable for a term of imprisonment of at least one year.
