CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
Providing for a correction in the enrollment of H.R. 815.
That, in the enrollment of H.R. 815, the Clerk of the House of Representatives shall amend the title so as to read:
Making emergency supplemental appropriations for the fiscal year ending September 30, 2024, and for other purposes.
