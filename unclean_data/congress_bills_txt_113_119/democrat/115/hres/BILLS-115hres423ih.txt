RESOLUTION
Condemning in the strongest terms the unprovoked and racially motivated murder of Lt. Richard W.
Collins III in College Park, Maryland, on May 20, 2017, expressing concern
for the rising tide of racist and hate-based activities being committed on
our college campuses, and reaffirming our support for inclusion,
diversity, and safety in our higher education institutions in the wake of
these attacks.
