RESOLUTION
Amending the Rules of the House of Representatives to require the text of any legislation that will be marked up at a meeting for the markup of legislation by a committee to be publicly available in electronic form at least 72 hours prior to the commencement of the meeting.
1.
Requiring text of legislation to be available at least 72 hours prior to markup
Clause 2(g)(4) of rule XI of the Rules of the House of Representatives is amended by striking
24 hours
each place it appears and inserting
72 hours
.
