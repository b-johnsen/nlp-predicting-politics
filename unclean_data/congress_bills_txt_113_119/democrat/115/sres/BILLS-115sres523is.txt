Encouraging companies to apply privacy protections included in the General Data Protection
Regulation of the European Union to citizens of the United States.

GDPR

Whereas the GDPR takes effect on May 25, 2018;
Whereas the rules of the GDPR will apply to many entities in the United States that serve users and customers in both Europe and the United States;
