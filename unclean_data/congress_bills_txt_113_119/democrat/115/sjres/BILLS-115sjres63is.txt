JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule

Secretary of Health and Human Services relating to
Short-Term, Limited Duration Insurance
.

Short-Term, Limited Duration Insurance
(83 Fed. Reg. 38212 (August 3, 2018)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
