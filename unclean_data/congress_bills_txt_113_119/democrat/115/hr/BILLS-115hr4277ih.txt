1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Waiver of Emergency Payments Act
.
2.
Federal share of assistance
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, including any agreement, the Federal share of assistance, including direct Federal assistance, provided for Puerto Rico in connection with Hurricanes Irma and Maria under sections 403, 404, 406, 407, and 408 of the Robert T. Stafford Disaster Relief and Emergency Assistance Act (
42 U.S.C. 5170b
, 5170c, 5172, 5173, and 5174) shall be 100 percent of the eligible costs under such sections.
