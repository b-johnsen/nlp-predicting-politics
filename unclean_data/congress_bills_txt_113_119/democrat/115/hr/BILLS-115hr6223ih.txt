1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Child Advocate Program Reauthorization Act
.
2.
Child Advocate Program reauthorized
Section 235(c)(6)(F) of the William Wilberforce Trafficking Victims Protection Reauthorization Act of 2008 (
8 U.S.C. 1232(c)(6)(F)
) is amended in clause (ii) by striking
the fiscal years 2016 and 2017
and inserting
fiscal years 2018 through 2022
.
