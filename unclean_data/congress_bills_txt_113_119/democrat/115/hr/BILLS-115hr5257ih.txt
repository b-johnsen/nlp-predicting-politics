1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Secure Communities and Safe Schools Act
.
2.
Prohibition on expenditure of certain Homeland Security grant funds to purchase firearms
Subsection (b) of section 2008 of the Homeland Security Act of 2002 (
6 U.S.C. 609
) is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph:
(6)
Firearms
A grant awarded under section 2003 or 2004 may not be used to purchase firearms and firearms accessories, such as ammunition, including for use by teachers.
.
