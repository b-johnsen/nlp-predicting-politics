1.
Short title
This Act may cited as the
Natural Sugar Labeling Act
.
2.
Labeling of certain single ingredient foods
The food labeling requirements under section 403(q) of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (
21 U.S.C. 343(q)
) shall not require that the nutrition facts label of any single ingredient sugar, honey, agave, and syrup, including maple syrup, that is packaged and offered for sale as a single ingredient food bear the declaration
Includes Xg Added Sugars
.
