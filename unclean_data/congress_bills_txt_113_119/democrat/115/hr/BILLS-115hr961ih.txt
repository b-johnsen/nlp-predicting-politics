1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Ban Aquaculture in the Great Lakes Act
.
2.
Prohibition on aquaculture in the Great Lakes
(a)
Prohibition
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, a person shall not engage in aquaculture in the Great Lakes.
(b)
Administration
The Secretary of the Interior shall administer this section.
(c)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)
Aquaculture
The term
aquaculture
means the propagation or rearing (or both) of aquatic species in a controlled or selected environment. The term does not include any fish hatchery operated by a Federal or State agency.
(2)
Great Lakes
The term
Great Lakes
means Lake Ontario, Lake Erie, Lake Huron, Lake St. Clair, Lake Michigan, and Lake Superior, including the connecting waters thereof, to the extent that those lakes or waters, respectively, are subject to the jurisdiction of the United States.
(d)
Effective date
This section shall take effect at the end of the 90-day period beginning on the date of the enactment of this Act.
