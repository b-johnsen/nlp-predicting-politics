Community partner
For purposes of this title, the term
community partner
means a nonprofit organization that has expertise in the planning and delivery of education, career training, and related programs, in forging coordination and cooperation between educators and other members of the community, in training educators and other deliverers of educational services, or in development and implementation of data systems that measure the progress of students, schools and institutions of higher education, and programs.
