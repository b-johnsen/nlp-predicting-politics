1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Veterans Apprenticeship and Labor Opportunity Reform Act
or the
VALOR Act
.
2.
Designation of State approving agencies for multi-State apprenticeship programs
Paragraph (1) of subsection (c) of section 3672 of title 38, United States Code, is amended to read as follows:
(2)

(B)
In this paragraph, the term
