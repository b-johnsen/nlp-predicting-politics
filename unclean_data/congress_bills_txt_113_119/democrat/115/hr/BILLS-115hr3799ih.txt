1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Demanding Oversight from Justice Act of 2017
.
2.
Civil action by Attorney General
Section 210401(b) of the Violent Crime Control and Law Enforcement Act of 1994 (
34 U.S.C. 12601(b)
) is amended by striking
may in a civil action
and inserting
shall in a civil action
.
