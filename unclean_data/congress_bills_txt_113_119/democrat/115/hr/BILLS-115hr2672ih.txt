1.
Sgt. Douglas J. Riney Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 520 Carter Street in Fairview, Illinois, shall be known and designated as the
Sgt. Douglas J. Riney Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Sgt. Douglas J. Riney Post Office
.
