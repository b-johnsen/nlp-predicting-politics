(a)

(b)
Prohibitions
Until the date on which the Secretary issues the adjustments to the Rule required under subsection (a)â

(c)
Study
The Secretary, jointly with the Commissioner of the Internal Revenue Service, shall carry out a study to develop a method of adjusting earnings data under the Rule to account for the underreporting of income by graduates of covered programs.
