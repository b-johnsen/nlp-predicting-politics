2.
Prohibition on access to and use of certain Russian-owned diplomatic facilities and properties by the Government of the Russian Federation
(a)
Prohibition
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the President may not permit access to or use by the Government of the Russian Federation to any Russian-owned diplomatic facilities and properties described in subsection (b).
