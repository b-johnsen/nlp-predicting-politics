(i)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)
The term
self-harm
means any behavior that is self-directed and deliberately results in injury or the potential for injury to oneself, including non-lethal forms of injuries to oneself, including cutting, asphyxiation, overdose of drugs (including through the use of opioids), and gun-related injuries, either with or without the intent to kill oneself.
(2)
The term
suicidal ideation
means thinking about, considering, or planning suicide.
