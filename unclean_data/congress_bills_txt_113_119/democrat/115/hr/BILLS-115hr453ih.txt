1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Relief from New Source Performance Standards Act of 2017
.
2.
Step 2 compliance date for standards of performance for new residential wood heaters, new residential hydronic heaters, and forced-air furnaces
(a)
In general
The Step 2 compliance date (as such term is used in the final rule entitled
Standards of Performance for New Residential Wood Heaters, New Residential Hydronic Heaters and Forced-Air Furnaces
published at 80 Fed. Reg. 13672 (March 16, 2015)) is deemed to be May 15, 2023
(b)
Conforming changes
Not later than 60 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency shall make such technical and conforming changes to rules and guidance documents as may be necessary to implement subsection (a).
