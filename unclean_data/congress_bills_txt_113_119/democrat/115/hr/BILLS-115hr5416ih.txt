Program authorized
The Secretary shall repay or cancel a qualified loan amount in accordance with subsection (b) for a loan made, insured, or guaranteed under part B or D for any borrower who meets one or more of the following:
(1)
The borrower is a woman who has been an owner of a small business concern owned and controlled by women for not less than 3 consecutive years.

(c)
Exclusion from income
The amount of a loan, and interest on a loan, which is canceled under this section shall not be considered income for purposes of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986.

No borrower may receive a reduction of loan obligations under both this section and section 428J, 428K, 428L, 455(m), or 460.

In this section:

The term
