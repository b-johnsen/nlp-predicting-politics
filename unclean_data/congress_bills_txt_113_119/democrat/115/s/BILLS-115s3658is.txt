1.
Report on death of Jamal Khashoggi
(a)
In general
Not later than 30 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Director of National Intelligence shall submit to Congress a report on the death of Jamal Khashoggi. Such report shall include identification of those who carried out, participated in, ordered, or were otherwise complicit in or responsible for the death of Jamal Khashoggi.
(b)
Form
The report submitted under subsection (a) shall be submitted in unclassified form.
