IV
113th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 225
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
May 20, 2013
Mr. Grayson
submitted
the following resolution; which was referred to the
Committee on
Rules
RESOLUTION
Raising a question of the privileges of the
House.
That the House of Representatives
shall not consider H.R. 3, the
Northern Route Approval Act
because: (1) it violates rule XXI of the House, and (2) it affects the dignity
and integrity of the proceedings of the House since it is
unconstitutional.
