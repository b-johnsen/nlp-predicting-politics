1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
SAFE ID Act of 2013
.
2.
Truncated social
security numbers permitted on wage reporting to employees
(a)
In
general
Paragraph (2) of
section 6051(a) of the Internal Revenue Code for 1986 is amended by striking
social security number
and inserting
taxpayer
identification number
.
(b)
Effective
date
The amendment made by this section shall apply to statements
furnished after the date of the enactment of this Act.
