1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Guaranteed 3% COLA for Seniors Act of
2013
.
2.
Consumer Price
Index for Elderly Consumers
(a)
In
general
The Bureau of Labor Statistics of the Department of Labor
shall prepare and publish an index for each calendar month to be known as the
Consumer Price Index for Elderly Consumers
that indicates changes
over time in expenditures for consumption which are typical for individuals in
the United States who are 62 years of age or older.
(b)
Effective
date
Subsection (a) shall apply with respect to calendar months
ending on or after July 31 of the calendar year following the calendar year in
which this Act is enacted.
(c)
Authorization of
appropriations
There are authorized to be appropriated such sums
as are necessary to carry out the provisions of this section.

(d)

This section and
the amendments made thereby shall not be construed as a general benefit
