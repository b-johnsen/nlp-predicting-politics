1.
Philmore Graham Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 100 Admiral Callaghan Lane in Vallejo,
California, shall be known and designated as the
Philmore Graham Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to
the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a
reference to the
Philmore Graham Post Office Building
.
