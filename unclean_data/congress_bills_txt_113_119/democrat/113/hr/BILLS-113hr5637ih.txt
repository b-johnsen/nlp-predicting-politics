Special rules relating to qualified consumer indebtedness
(1)
Qualified consumer indebtedness defined
For purposes of this section, the term
qualified consumer indebtedness
means any indebtedness of a natural person arising out of a transaction in which the money,
property, or services which are the subject of the transaction are
primarily for personal, family, or household purposes.
