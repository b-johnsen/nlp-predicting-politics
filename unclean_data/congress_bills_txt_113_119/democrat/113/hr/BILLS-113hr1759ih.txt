7735.
Quarterly

(a)
Reports
Not later than 30 days after the end of
each fiscal quarter, the Secretary shall submit to Congress a report on covered
requests.

Matters
included

determines that the request is completed.
(c)
Covered request
defined
In this section, the
term
covered request
means a request by the Secretary to the head
of another department or agency of the United States for information required
by the Secretary in adjudicating a claim for disability compensation under
chapter 11 of this
title.
