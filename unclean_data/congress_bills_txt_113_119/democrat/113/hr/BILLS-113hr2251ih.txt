1.
Designation
The United States courthouse located at 118
South Mill Street, in Fergus Falls, Minnesota, shall be known and designated as
the
Edward J. Devitt United States Courthouse
.
2.
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation,
document, paper, or other record of the United States to the United States
courthouse referred to in section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Edward J. Devitt United States Courthouse
.
