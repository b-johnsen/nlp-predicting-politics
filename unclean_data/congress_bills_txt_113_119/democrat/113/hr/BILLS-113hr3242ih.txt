1.
Two-year extension of special expensing rules for certain film and television productions
(a)
In general
Subsection (f) of
section 181
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking
December 31, 2013
and inserting
December 31, 2015
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by this section shall apply to productions commencing after December 31, 2013.
