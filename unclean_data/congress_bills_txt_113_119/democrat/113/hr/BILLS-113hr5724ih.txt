1.
Short title

Permanent Investment in Health Research Act of 2014
.
2.
Funding for National Institutes of Health
(a)
Funding
Section 402A(a) of the Public Health Service Act (
42 U.S.C. 282a(a)
) is amended to read as follows:
(a)
In general
Out of any money in the Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the purpose of carrying out this
title, there are hereby appropriated to the National Institutes of Health,
to remain available until expended, the following amounts:
(1)
For fiscal year 2015, $32,000,000,000.
(2)
For each of fiscal years 2016 through 2024, the amount appropriated under this subsection for the
preceding fiscal year, adjusted by any percentage increase in nominal
gross domestic product during the preceding calendar year.
.
