3.
Authorization of
assistance to Israel for Iron Dome anti-missile defense system
The President, acting through the Secretary
of Defense and the Secretary of State, is authorized to provide assistance,
upon request by the Government of Israel, for the procurement, maintenance,
enhancement, and sustainment of the Iron Dome defense system for purposes of
intercepting short-range and medium-range rockets, missiles, and projectiles
launched against Israel.
