1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Member of Congress Pay Sequestration
and Fairness Act
.
2.
Inclusion of member
pay among accounts subject to budget enforcement through sequestration
(a)
Inclusion
Paragraph (11) of section 250(c) of the
Balanced Budget and Emergency Deficit Control Act of 1985 (
2 U.S.C. 900(c)
) is
amended by inserting before the period the following:
, and such term
includes any item for the pay of Senators or Representatives in, or Delegates
or the Resident Commissioner to, the Congress
.
(b)
Effective
date
The amendment made by
subsection (a) shall apply with respect to any sequestration occurring under
part C of the Balanced Budget and Emergency Deficit Control Act of 1985 during
the One Hundred Fourteenth Congress or any succeeding Congress.
