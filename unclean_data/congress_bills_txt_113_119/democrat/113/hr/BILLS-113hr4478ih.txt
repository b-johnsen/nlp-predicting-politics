1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Section 8 Relief Act of 2014
.
2.
40-year terms for section 8 project-based contracts
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, any housing assistance payments contract to provide
rental assistance under section 8 of the United States Housing Act of 1937
(
42 U.S.C. 1437f
) that is attached to a structure and is entered into
after the date of the enactment of this Act shall have a term of 40 years.
