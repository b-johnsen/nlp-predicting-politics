1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Winter Roads Safety Act of 2014
.
2.
Temporary waiver
(a)
In general
During the period beginning on the date of enactment of this Act and ending on April 15, 2014, the
Secretary of Transportation shall waive, for a covered logging vehicle,
the application of any vehicle weight limit established under section 127
of title 23, United States Code.
