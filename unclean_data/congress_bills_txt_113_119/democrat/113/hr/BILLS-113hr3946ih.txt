Extension of employer wage credit for employees who are active duty members of the uniformed services
(a)
In general
Subsection (f) of
section 45P
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking
December 31, 2013
and inserting
December 31, 2014
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by this section shall apply to payments made after December 31, 2013.
