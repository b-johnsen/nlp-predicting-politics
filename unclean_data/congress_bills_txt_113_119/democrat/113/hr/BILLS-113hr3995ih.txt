1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Mortgage Tax Cut Extension Act
.
2.
Extension of deduction for mortgage insurance premiums
(a)
In general
Section 163(h)(3)(E)(iv)(I)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking
December 31, 2013
and inserting
December 31, 2015
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by this section shall apply to amounts paid or incurred after December 31, 2013.
