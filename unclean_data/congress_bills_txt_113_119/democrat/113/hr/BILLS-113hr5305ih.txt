1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Puerto Rico Chapter 9 Uniformity Act of 2014
.
2.
Amendment
Section 101(52)
of title 11, United States Code, is amended to read as follows:
(52)
The term
State
includes Puerto Rico and, except for the purpose of defining who may be a debtor under chapter 9
of this title, includes the District of Columbia.
.
