(2)

A provider

A provider of
commercial mobile service or commercial mobile data service on a mobile device

all information that was placed on such device after its manufacture.

A person may not manufacture in the United States or

(1)
Prohibition
It shall be unlawful toâ
(A)

subparagraph (A).
(2)
Penalty
Any

(e)

In

(1)

The term

The term

(3)
Mobile

The term

(4)

The

.
(b)

Not later than 1 year after the date of the enactment of this
Act, each provider of commercial mobile service or commercial mobile data
service that provides such service on a mobile device shall submit to the
Federal Communications Commission a report onâ
(1)

(2)

(c)
Effective

(1)
In

Such section 343
shall take effect on the date that is 2 years after the date of the enactment
of this Act.
(2)
Devices
previously manufactured or imported
In the case of a mobile
device that was manufactured in the United States (or imported into the United
States, if such device was manufactured outside the United States) before the
date that is 2 years after the date of the enactment of this Act, a provider of
commercial mobile service or commercial mobile data service shall only be
required to comply with subsections (a) and (b) of such section to the extent
technologically feasible.
(d)
Definitions
In this section, a term that is defined in
such section 343 shall have the meaning given such term in such section.
