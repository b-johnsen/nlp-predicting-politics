1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Stop Penalizing Taxpayers for Sports Owner Fouls Act of 2014
.
2.
Denial of deduction for fines paid by owner of professional sports franchise
(a)
In general
Section 162
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by redesignating subsection (q) as
subsection (r) and by inserting after subsection (p) the following new
subsection:
(q)
Fines and penalties paid by owners of professional sports franchises
In the case of an individual who owns (directly or indirectly) a professional sports franchise, no
deduction shall be allowed under subsection (a) for any fine or similar
penalty paid to the professional sports league or association of which the
franchise is a member.
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by this section shall apply to amounts paid or incurred after December 31, 2013.
