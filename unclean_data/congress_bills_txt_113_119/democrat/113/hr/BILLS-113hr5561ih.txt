1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Military Corridor Transportation Improvement Act of 2014
.
2.
High priority corridors on National Highway System
(a)
In general
Section 1105(c) of the Intermodal Surface Transportation Efficiency Act of 1991 (105 Stat. 2032) is
amended by adding at the end the following:
(81)
United States Route 117/Interstate Route 795 from United States Route 70 in Goldsboro, Wayne
County, North Carolina, to Interstate Route 40 west of Faison, Sampson
County, North Carolina.
(82)
United States Route 70 from its intersection with Interstate Route 40 in Garner, Wake County, North
Carolina, to the Port at Morehead City, Carteret County, North Carolina.
.
(b)
Inclusion of certain route segments on Interstate System
Section 1105(e)(5)(A) of the Intermodal Surface Transportation Efficiency Act of 1991 (105 Stat.
2031; 109 Stat. 597; 115 Stat. 872; 118 Stat. 293; 126 Stat. 426) is
amended in the first sentence by striking
and subsection (c)(57)
and inserting
subsection (c)(57), subsection (c)(81), and subsection (c)(82)
.
