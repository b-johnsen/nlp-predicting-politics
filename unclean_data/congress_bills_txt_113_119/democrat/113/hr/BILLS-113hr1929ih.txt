The Secretary shall carry out, under such terms and

identify best practices for employment and training programs under this Act to
raise the number of work registrants who obtain unsubsidized employment,
increase their earned income, and reduce their reliance on public assistance,
including but not limited to the supplemental nutrition assistance
program.
