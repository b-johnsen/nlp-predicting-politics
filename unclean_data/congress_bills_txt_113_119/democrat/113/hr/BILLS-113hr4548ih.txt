1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Re-Integration of Ex-Offenders Through Technology Act
.
2.
Inclusion of technology literacy in ex-offender job training programs
The Secretary of Labor shall include programs that teach technology literacy in any job training
program for ex-offenders offered under the Workforce Investment Act of
1998 (
29 U.S.C. 2801 et seq.
).
