Mr. Schumer

) submitted the following

RESOLUTION
Providing for members on the part of the Senate of the
Joint Committee on Printing and the Joint Committee of Congress on the
Library.
That the following named Members be,
and they are hereby, elected members of the following joint committees of
Congress:
Joint Committee on
Printing:
Mr. Schumer, Mr. Udall of New Mexico, Mr. Warner, Mr.
Roberts, and Mr. Chambliss.
Joint Committee of Congress on
the Library:
Mr. Schumer, Mr. Durbin, Mr. Leahy, Mr. Roberts, and
Mr. Blunt.
