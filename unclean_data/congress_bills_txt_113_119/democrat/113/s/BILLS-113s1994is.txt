1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
TRICARE Moms Improvement Act of 2014
.
2.
Availability of breastfeeding support, supplies, and counseling under the TRICARE program
Section 1079(a)
of title 10, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following new
paragraph:
(18)
Breastfeeding support, supplies (including breast pumps and associated equipment), and counseling
shall be provided as appropriate during pregnancy and the postpartum
period.
.
