1.
Termination of employees of the Department of Veterans Affairs who retaliate against whistleblowers
(a)
In general
Subject to
chapter 12
of title 5, United States Code, including the right of an employee to
obtain judicial review under section 1215(a)(4) of that title, and
notwithstanding any other provision of law, any employee of the Department
of Veterans Affairs
who is determined under section 1215 of that title to have violated
paragraph (8), (9), or (13) of section 2302(b) of that title shall be
terminated.
(b)
Applicability
Subsection (a) shall apply to an act committed before, on, or after the date of enactment of this
Act.
