1.
Sergeant Brett E. Gornewicz Memorial Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 13127 Broadway Street in Alden, New
York, shall be known and designated as the
Sergeant Brett E. Gornewicz Memorial Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to
the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a
reference to the
Sergeant Brett E. Gornewicz Memorial Post Office
.
