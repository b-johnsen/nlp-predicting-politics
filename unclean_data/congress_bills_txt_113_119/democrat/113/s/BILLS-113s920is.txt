1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Fond du Lac Band of Lake Superior
Chippewa Non-Intercourse Act of 2013
.
2.
Congressional approval
of certain land transactions
(a)
In
general
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, and without
further approval, ratification, or authorization by the United States, the Fond
du Lac Band of Lake Superior Chippewa in the State of Minnesota (referred to in
this Act as the
Band
) may lease, sell, convey, warrant, or
otherwise transfer all or any portion of the interest of the Band in any real
property that is not held in trust by the United States for the benefit of the
Band.
