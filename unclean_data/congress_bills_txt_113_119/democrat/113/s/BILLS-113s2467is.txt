1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Continuing Care for Veterans Act of 2014
.
2.
Prohibition on altering available health care and wait times for appointments for health care for
certain veterans
The Secretary of Veterans Affairs may not alter the hospital care, medical services, or other
health care available to a veteran who is enrolled in the patient
enrollment system of the Department of Veterans Affairs under section 1705
of title 38, United States Code, or the amount of time such veteran is
required to wait for an appointment for such care or services based solely
on the length of time since such veteran has last received such care or
services from the Department.
