1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Grand Jury Transparency Act
.
2.
Preferential treatment for COPS grants for States with sunshine laws
Section 1701(c) of title I the Omnibus Crime Control and Safe Streets Act of 1968 (
42 U.S.C. 3796dd(c)
) is amended by inserting before the period at the end the following:
, or to applications from a jurisdiction, or an entity located in a jurisdiction, which has in place a law or ordinance to make available to the public grand jury transcripts and other records considered by grand juries
.
