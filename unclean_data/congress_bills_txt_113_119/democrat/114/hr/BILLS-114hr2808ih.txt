2.
Prohibition on certain provisions in U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement contracts requiring a minimum number of immigration detention beds
(a)
In general
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the Assistant Secretary of U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement may not negotiate with a private detention company a contract that contains any provision relating to a guaranteed minimum number of immigration detention beds at any specific facility.
