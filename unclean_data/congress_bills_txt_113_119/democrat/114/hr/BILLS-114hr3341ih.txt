1.
Designation of portion of Moffett Federal Airfield, California, as Moffett Air National Guard Base
(a)
Designation
The 111-acre cantonment area at Moffett Federal Airfield, California, utilized by the 129th Rescue Wing of the California Air National Guard shall be known and designated as
Moffett Air National Guard Base
.
(b)
References
Any reference in any law, regulation, map, document, paper, other record of the United States to the cantonment area at Moffett Federal Airfield described in subsection (a) shall be considered to be a reference to Moffett Air National Guard Base.
