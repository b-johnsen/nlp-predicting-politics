1.
Extension of credit for biodiesel and renewable diesel used as fuel
(a)
In general
Subsection (g) of
section 40A
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking
December 31, 2016
and inserting
December 31, 2017
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by this section shall apply to fuel sold or used after December 31, 2016.
