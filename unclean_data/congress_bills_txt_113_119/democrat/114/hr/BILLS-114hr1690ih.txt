1.
Designation
The United States courthouse located at 700 Grant Street in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, shall be known and designated as the
Joseph F. Weis Jr. United States Courthouse
.
2.
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the United States courthouse referred to in section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Joseph F. Weis Jr. United States Courthouse
.
