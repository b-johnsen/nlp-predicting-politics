(a)
In general
Whoever, while employed by any department or agency of the United States other than the Department of Defense and stationed or deployed in Canada pursuant to a treaty or executive agreement in furtherance of a border security initiative, engages in conduct (or conspires or attempts to engage in conduct) in Canada that would constitute an offense for which a person may be prosecuted in a court of the United States had the conduct been engaged in within the United States or within the special maritime and territorial jurisdiction of the United States shall be punished as provided for that offense.
(b)

(B)
being present or residing in Canada in connection with such employment; and
(C)
not being a national of or ordinarily resident in Canada.
(2)
Grant agreement

grant agreement
means a legal instrument described in section 6304 or 6305 of title 31, other than an agreement between the United States and a State, local, or foreign government or an international organization.
(3)
Grantee

grantee
means a party, other than the United States, to a grant agreement.
