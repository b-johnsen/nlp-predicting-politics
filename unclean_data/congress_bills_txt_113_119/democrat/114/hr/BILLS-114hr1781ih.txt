(2)
Limitation on wages per year taken into account
The amount of wages which may be taken into account under paragraph (1) with respect to any apprenticeship employee for each qualified training year shall not exceed $2,000.

The Secretary shall, by regulations, provide for recapturing the amount of any post-apprenticeship credit allowed under subsection (a) with respect to any individual who is employed by the employer for less than 1 year after the individual completed such program.
