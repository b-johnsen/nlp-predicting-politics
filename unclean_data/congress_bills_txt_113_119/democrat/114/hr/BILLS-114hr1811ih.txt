I
Cost-of-Living Increases
101.
Consumer price index for elderly consumers
(a)
In General
The Bureau of Labor Statistics of the Department of Labor shall prepare and publish an index for each calendar month to be known as the
Consumer Price Index for Elderly Consumers
that indicates changes over time in expenditures for consumption which are typical for individuals in the United States who are 62 years of age or older.
(b)
Effective Date
Subsection (a) shall apply with respect to calendar months ending on or after July 31 of the calendar year following the calendar year in which this Act is enacted.
(c)
Authorization of Appropriations
There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as are necessary to carry out the provisions of this section.
