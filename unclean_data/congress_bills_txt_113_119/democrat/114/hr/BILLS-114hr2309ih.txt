2.
Prohibition against discrimination on account of sexual orientation or gender identity
Subsection (a)(1) of section 701 of the Equal Credit Opportunity Act (
15 U.S.C. 1691
) is amended, by striking
on the basis of race, color, religion, national origin, sex or marital status,
and inserting
on the basis of actual or perceived race, color, religion, national origin, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, or marital status,
.
