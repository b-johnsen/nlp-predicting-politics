2.
Prohibiting enforcement of certain photo identification requirements
(a)
Requirements applicable to voter registration
Section 8 of the National Voter Registration Act of 1993 (
52 U.S.C. 20507

(j)
Prohibiting enforcement of photo identification requirements
(1)
Prohibition
An election official may not enforce any requirement that an individual produce a photo identification as a condition of registering to vote in an election for Federal office, except to the extent that the requirement was in effect as of June 25, 2013.
(2)
Rule of construction
Nothing in this subsection may be construed to prohibit a State from repealing a requirement under State law that an individual produce a photo identification as a condition of registering to vote in an election for Federal office.
.

Nothing in this subsection may be construed to prohibit a State from repealing a requirement under State law that an individual produce a photo identification as a condition of voting in an election for Federal office.
