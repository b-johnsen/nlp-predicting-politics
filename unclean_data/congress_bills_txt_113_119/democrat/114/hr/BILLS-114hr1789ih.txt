Security Enhancements
Not later than 1 year after the date of enactment of this Act, the Administrator of the Transportation Security Administration shall issue such regulations as are necessary to enhance the security of tank cars by requiring that all rail safety coordinators ensure that no tank car containing crude oil is left unattended during any period of time that such tank car is being transferred between railroad carriers or between a railroad carrier and a shipper.
