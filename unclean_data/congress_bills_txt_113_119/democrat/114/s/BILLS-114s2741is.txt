1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Keep Estimated Extra Payments Act
.
2.
Authority to elect not to recoup benefits overpayments
Section 4022B of the Employee Retirement Income Security Act of 1974 (
29 U.S.C. 1322b
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(c)
Authority To elect not To recoup benefits overpayments
The Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation and the Secretary of Labor may elect not to recoup benefits overpayments made to a participant under this title.
.
