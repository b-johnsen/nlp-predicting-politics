1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Fort Ontario Study Act
.
2.
Definitions
In this Act:
(1)
Secretary
The term
Secretary
means the Secretary of the Interior.
(2)
Site
The term
site
means Fort Ontario in Oswego, New York.
