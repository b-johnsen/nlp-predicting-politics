3.
Program study
(a)
In general
Not later than 180 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Secretary of Energy shall carry out, and submit to Congress a report describing the results of, a study on the effectiveness of the advanced fossil loan guarantee incentive program and other incentive programs for advanced fossil energy of the Department of Energy.
