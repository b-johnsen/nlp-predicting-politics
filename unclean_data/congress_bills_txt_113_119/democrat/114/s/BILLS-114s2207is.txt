1.
Rewards authorized
In accordance with the Rewards for Justice program authorized under section 36(b) of the State Department Basic Authorities Act of 1956 (
22 U.S.C. 2708(b)
), the Secretary of State shall offer a reward of not less than $10,000,000 to any individual who furnishes information leading to the arrest or conviction in any country of any individual responsible for the 1988 bombing of Pan Am Flight 103.
