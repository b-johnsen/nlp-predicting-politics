1.
Harry S. Truman Union
Station
(a)
Designation
Union Station, located at 40 Massachusetts Avenue, NE., Washington, DC, shall be known and designated as
Harry S. Truman Union Station
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Harry S. Truman Union Station
.
