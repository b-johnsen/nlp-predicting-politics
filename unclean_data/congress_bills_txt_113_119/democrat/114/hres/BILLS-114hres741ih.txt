Nonprofit Organization (NPO) Recognition Month

Whereas the nonprofit sector continues to expand in size and scope;
Whereas nonprofits contribute nearly $1,000,000,000,000 to the United States economy annually;
Whereas nonprofits serve a vital role in facilitating charitable giving and social action;
Whereas the combined donations and volunteer hours of individuals to nonprofit organizations are valued at hundreds of billions of dollars; and
Whereas September would be an appropriate month to designate as
Nonprofit Organization (NPO) Recognition Month
