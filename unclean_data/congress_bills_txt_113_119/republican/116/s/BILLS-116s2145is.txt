2.
Linking of award and incentive fees to contract outcomes
(a)
Guidance
Not later than 180 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Director of the Office of Management and Budget, in coordination with the heads of Federal departments and agencies, shall issue guidance, with detailed implementation instructions (including definitions), on the appropriate use of award and incentive fees in department or agency programs.

(c)
Return of unearned bonuses
Any funds intended to be awarded as incentive fees that are not paid due to the inability of a contractor to meet the criteria established by this section shall be returned to the Treasury of the United States.
