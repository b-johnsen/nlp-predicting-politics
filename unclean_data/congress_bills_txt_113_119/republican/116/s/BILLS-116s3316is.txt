1.
License required for certain reexports to entities on entity list
(a)
In general
Not later than 90 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Secretary of Commerce shall revise the Export Administration Regulations to apply the 10 percent de minimis rule under section 734.4(c) of the Export Administration Regulations to reexports described in paragraph (1), (2), or (3) of that section to an entity on the entity list.
(b)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)
Entity list
The term
entity list
means the list maintained by the Bureau of Industry and Security and set forth in Supplement No. 4 to part 744 of the Export Administration Regulations.
(2)
Export administration regulations
The term
Export Administration Regulations
means subchapter C of chapter VII of title 15, Code of Federal Regulations.
