Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Reviving America's Scenic Byways Act
.
2.
National scenic byways program
(a)
Request for nominations
Not later than 90 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Secretary of Transportation shall publish in the Federal Register and on the appropriate website of the Department of Transportation a request for nominations for roads to be designated under the national scenic byways program under section 162 of title 23, United States Code.
