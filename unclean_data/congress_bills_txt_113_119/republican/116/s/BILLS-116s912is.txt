Section 8(o) of the United States Housing Act of 1937 (

) is amended by adding at the end the following:

(B)
Requirement
A covered public housing agency that has jurisdiction over the area in which a portable family is seeking to use the voucher received from an initial public housing agencyâ
(i)

(iii)
may not bill the initial public housing agency for those assistance payments.
