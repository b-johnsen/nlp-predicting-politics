2.
Establishment and use of Trail Stewardship for Economic Recovery Fund
(a)
Establishment
There is established in the Treasury of the United States a fund, to be known as the

(b)
Deposit into the Fund
On the date of enactment of this Act, out of amounts in the Treasury not otherwise obligated, the Secretary of the Treasury shall deposit into the Fund $200,000,000, to remain available until expended and without further appropriation or fiscal year limitation, to carry out the purposes described in subsection (c).
