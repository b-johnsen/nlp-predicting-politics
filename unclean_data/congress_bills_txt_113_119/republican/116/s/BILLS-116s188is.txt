2.
Additional funding for border security
(a)
Transfer of certain revenue from duties to Secretary of Homeland Security
(1)
In general
The Secretary of the Treasury shall transfer to the Secretary of Homeland Security from the general fund of the Treasury amounts determined by the Secretary of the Treasury to be equivalent to 50 percent of the amounts received into the general fund that are attributable to duties imposed under section 301 of the Trade Act of 1974 (
19 U.S.C. 2411
) with respect to goods imported from the Peopleâs Republic of China on or after July 6, 2018.
(2)
Administration of transfers
(A)
In general
The amounts required to be transferred under paragraph (1) shall be transferred not less frequently than quarterly from the general fund of the Treasury to the Secretary of Homeland Security on the basis of estimates made by the Secretary of the Treasury of the amounts described in paragraph (1) that are received into the Treasury.
(B)
Adjustments
Proper adjustments shall be made in the amounts described in paragraph (1) subsequently transferred under that paragraph to the extent prior estimates were in excess of, or less than, the amounts required to be transferred.
