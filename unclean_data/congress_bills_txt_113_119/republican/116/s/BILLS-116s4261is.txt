1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Safely Creating Healthy Opening Options Locally Act
or the
SCHOOL Act
.
2.
Findings
Congress finds the following:
(1)
The absence of in-person learning has reminded the Nation of how critical schools are for children, parents, families, and their communities.
(2)
Our Nation's goal is safely getting students back to school in the Fall. To do so successfully, our teachers and schools will need the support necessary to maintain safety.
(3)
School districts must apply recommendations from State and local health officials to ensure that students, teachers, and faculty can safely return to the classroom.
