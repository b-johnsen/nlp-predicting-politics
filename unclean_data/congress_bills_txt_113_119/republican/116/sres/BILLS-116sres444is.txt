RESOLUTION
Condemning the VH1 television show Cartel Crew.
Whereas the VH1 television show Cartel Crew glorifies drug cartels and individuals who live in luxury as a result of the wealth that drug cartels corruptly amass;
Whereas there is concern that youth across the United States may watch Cartel Crew and come to believe that crime is profitable;
Whereas the words and deeds of the cast members of Cartel Crew suggest that those cast members have no interest in separating themselves from their illicit pasts;
Whereas the relatives of the cast members of Cartel Crew are some of the worst criminals in history;

Cocaine Godmother
