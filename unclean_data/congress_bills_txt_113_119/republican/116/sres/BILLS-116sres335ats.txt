III
116th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 335
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
September 25, 2019
Ms. McSally
submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to
RESOLUTION
Instructing the managers on the part of the Senate on the bill S. 1790 (116th Congress) to insist
upon the members of the conference to include the provisions contained in
section 2906 of the Senate bill (relating to replenishment of certain
military construction funds).
That the managers on the part of the Senate at the conference on the disagreeing votes of the two Houses on the House amendment to the bill S. 1790 be instructed to insist upon the provisions contained in section 2906 of the Senate bill (relating to replenishment of certain military construction funds).
