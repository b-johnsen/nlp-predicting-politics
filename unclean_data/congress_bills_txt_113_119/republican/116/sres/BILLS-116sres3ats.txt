S. RES. 3
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES

Mr. McConnell

RESOLUTION
To elect Chuck Grassley, a Senator from the State of Iowa, to be President pro tempore of the
Senate of the United States.
That Chuck Grassley, a Senator from the State of Iowa, be, and he is hereby, elected President of the Senate pro tempore.
