(d)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)
Great Lakes coastline
The term
Great Lakes coastline
means the shoreline along the five Great Lakes and their connecting channels in the states of Illinois, Indiana, Michigan, Minnesota, New York, Ohio, Pennsylvania, and Wisconsin.
(2)

Secretary
