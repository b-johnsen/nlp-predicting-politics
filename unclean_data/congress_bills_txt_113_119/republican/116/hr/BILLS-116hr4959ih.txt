Short title

2.
Estimates of major legislation
(a)
In general
Part A of the Congressional Budget and Impoundment Control Act 1974 is amended by adding at the end the following new section:
407.
Estimates of major legislation
(a)
CBO cost estimates
An estimate provided by the Congressional Budget Office under section 402 for any major legislation shall, to the extent practicable, incorporate the budgetary effects of changes in economic output, employment, capital stock, and other macroeconomic variables resulting from such legislation.
(b)
Joint committee on taxation cost estimates
An estimate provided by the Joint Committee on Taxation to the Director of the Congressional Budget Office under section 201(f) for any major legislation shall, to the extent practicable, incorporate the budgetary effects of changes in economic output, employment, capital stock, and other macroeconomic variables resulting from such legislation.
