The requirements described in this subparagraph are as follows:
(i)

Seventy-five percent of the project selection must be composed of local government hazard mitigation projects and eligible entities must give at least 3 weeks notice to their local governments before requiring all hazard mitigation projects to be submitted.

Eligible entities may not provide more than $5,000,000 in loan funds toward the completion of any single hazard mitigation project.
(iv)
A participating entity must agree to the revolving loan repayment terms issued by the Administrator upon being selected to participate.
