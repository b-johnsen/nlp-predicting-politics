1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Defending Our National Marine Sanctuaries from Damaging Chemicals Act of 2019
.
2.
Prohibition on the use of oxybenzone and octinoxate
The Secretary of Commerce shall issue regulations prohibiting use, including application to another individual, of sunscreen containing oxybenzone or octinoxate in a National Marine Sanctuary in which coral is present.
