1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Miracle Mountain Designation Act
.
2.
Findings
Congress finds as follows:
(1)
On September 13, 2018, the Bald Mountain Fire burned nearly 20,000 acres of land in Utah.
(2)
Elk Ridge City, located in Utah County, was nearly the victim of this fire.
(3)
Suddenly, the fire halted its progression and, instead of burning into Elk Ridge City, stayed behind the mountain and spared the city.
(4)
Congress, in acknowledgment of this event, believes this mountain holds special significance to the residents of Elk Ridge City and surrounding communities.
(5)
The presently unnamed peak has been referred to as
Miracle Mountain
by many residents since the fire that nearly went into Elk Ridge City.
