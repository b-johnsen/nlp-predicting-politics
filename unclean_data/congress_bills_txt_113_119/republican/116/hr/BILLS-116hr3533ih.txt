The amendments made by this Act shall apply to any offense committed on or after the date of the enactment of this Act.

Rule of construction
The amendments made by this Act shall not be construed to create any right to challenge a sentence imposed before the date of the enactment of this Act under section 924 of title 18, United States Code.
