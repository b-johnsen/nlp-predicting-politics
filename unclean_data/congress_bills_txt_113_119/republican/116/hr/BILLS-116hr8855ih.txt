2.
Limit Federal employees adverse action appeal avenues
(a)
In general
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, an employee who alleges or appeals, as the case may be, a covered matter to a reviewing entity that has authority to consider such allegation or appeal may not subsequently or simultaneously allege or appeal such covered matter to any other reviewing entity.
