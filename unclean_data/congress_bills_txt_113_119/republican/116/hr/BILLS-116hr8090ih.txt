1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Leave Local Government Alone Act of 2020
.
2.
Limitation on recoupment of Coronavirus Relief Fund amounts
(a)
In general
Section 601(f)(2) of the Social Security Act (
42 U.S.C. 801(f)(2)
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
In no case is a State or unit of local government authorized to recover any amount provided under a payment made under this section from any unit of local government.
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by subsection (a) shall apply with respect to payments under section 601 of the Social Security Act made before or after the date of enactment of this Act.
