1.
Paycheck protection program eligibility for certain nonprofit organizations serving developmentally disabled individuals
Section 7(a)(36)(D) of the Small Business Act (
15 U.S.C. 636(a)(36)(D)
) is amended by adding at the end the following new clause:
(vii)
Nonprofits serving certain disabled individuals
(I)
Size standard waiver
For the purposes of determining whether a covered nonprofit organization is eligible to receive a covered loan, the Administrator may deem such organization as employing not more than the size standard in number of employees established by the Administration for the industry in which such organization operates or, if no such size standard is applicable, not more than 500 employees.
(II)
Revenue not basis for ineligibility
A covered nonprofit organization may not be determined to be ineligible for a covered loan due to the annual gross receipts of such organization.
