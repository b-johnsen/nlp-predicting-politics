Three-dimensional printing study
(a)
In general
(1)
Study required
Not later than 2 years after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Consumer Product Safety Commission, in coordination with the head of any other appropriate Federal agency, shall conduct a study on the impact of three-dimensional printing on United States businesses conducting interstate commerce.
