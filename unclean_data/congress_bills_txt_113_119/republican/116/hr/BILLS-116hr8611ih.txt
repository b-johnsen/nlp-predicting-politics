1.
Joseph Bullock Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 4755 Southeast Dixie Highway in Port Salerno, Florida, shall be known and designated as the
Joseph Bullock Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Joseph Bullock Post Office Building
.
