CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
Authorizing the use of the rotunda of the Capitol for the Congressional National Prayer Breakfast.
1.
Use of rotunda for Congressional National Prayer Breakfast
(a)
Authorization
(1)
In general
The rotunda of the United States Capitol is authorized to be used on February 5, 2026, for the Congressional National Prayer Breakfast.
(2)
Food and beverage service
The authorization provided under paragraph (1) shall include the service of food and beverages at the event described in such paragraph.
(b)
Preparations
Physical preparations for the conduct of the event described in subsection (a) shall be carried out in accordance with such conditions as may be prescribed by the Architect of the Capitol.
