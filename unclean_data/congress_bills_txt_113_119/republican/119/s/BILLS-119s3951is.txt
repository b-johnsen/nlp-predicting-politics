(b)
Budgetary discretion
Notwithstanding the Impoundment Control Act of 1974 (
2 U.S.C. 681 et seq.
), if the President determines, in consultation with the Secretary of the Treasury and the Office of Management and Budget, that there will be a deficit for a fiscal year, the President may exercise budgetary discretion and decline to obligate covered budgetary resources available for that fiscal year, as the President determines appropriate, in an amount that is not more than the amount required to eliminate the deficit.
