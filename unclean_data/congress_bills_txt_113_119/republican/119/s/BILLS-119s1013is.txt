1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Consolidating Aerospace Programs Efficiently at Canaveral Act
or
CAPE Canaveral Act
.
2.
Transfer of National Aeronautics and Space Administration headquarters to Brevard County, Florida
Not later than 1 year after the date of the enactment of this Act, the headquarters of the National Aeronautics and Space Administration shall be transferred to Brevard County, Florida.
