3.
Improving border security
(a)
Checkpoint Program Management Office
(1)
Establishment
The Commissioner for U.S. Customs and Border Protection shall establish and operate, within the U.S. Border Patrol, the Checkpoint Program Management Office, which shall provide oversight over checkpoint operations nationwide.
(2)
Assistant chief
The Chief shall select an Assistant Chief to manage the CPMO for a term of at least 2 years.
