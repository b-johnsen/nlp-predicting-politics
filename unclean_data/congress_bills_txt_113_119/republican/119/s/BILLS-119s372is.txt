(b)
Comprehensive guidance
Not later than 1 year after the date of enactment of this Act, the President, acting through the Administrator of the Federal Emergency Management Agency, shall issue comprehensive guidance to State and Tribal governments relating to the measures and investments described in section 406(b)(3)(A) of the Robert T. Stafford Disaster Relief and Emergency Assistance Act (
42 U.S.C. 5172(b)(3)(A)
), as amended in subsection (a).
3.
Effective date; appropriations
(a)
Effective date
This Act, and the amendments made by this Act, shall take effect on the date that is 1 year after the date of enactment of this Act.
(b)
Appropriations
The amendments made by this Act shall be carried out only with funds appropriated to the Federal Emergency Management Agency on or after the date of enactment of this Act.
(c)
No additional funds
No additional funds are authorized to be appropriated for the purpose of carrying out this Act.
