1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Semiquincentennial Tourism and Access to Recreation Sites Act
or the
STARS Act
.
2.
Waived fees at public recreational sites in honor of the 250th anniversary of
the United States
(a)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)
Entrance fee
The term
entrance fee
has the meaning given the term in section 802 of the Federal Lands Recreation Enhancement Act (
16 U.S.C. 6801
).
(2)
Standard amenity recreation fee
The term
standard amenity recreation fee
has the meaning given the term in section 802 of the Federal Lands Recreation Enhancement Act (
16 U.S.C. 6801
).
