1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
In God We Trust Act
.
2.
Display of national motto on Federal buildings
(a)
Definition of public building
In this section, the term
public building
has the meaning given the term in section 3301(a) of title 40, United States Code.
(b)
Requirement
Not later than 1 year after the date of enactment of this Act, the Administrator of General Services shall inscribe or display in a place of prominence on every public building the national motto of the United States, as described in section 302 of title 36, United States Code.
