1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
National Education Association Charter Repeal Act
.
2.
Repeal of Federal charter to the National Education Association
(a)
In general
Chapter 1511
of title 36, United States Code (which granted a Federal charter to the National Education Association), is repealed.
(b)
Clerical amendment
The table of chapters for title 36, United States Code, is amended by striking the items relating to chapter 1511.
