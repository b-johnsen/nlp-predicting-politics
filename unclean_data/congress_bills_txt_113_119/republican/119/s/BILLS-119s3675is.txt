Section 111 of title 18, United States Code, is amended by striking subsection (b) and inserting the following:
(b)
Enhanced penalties
(1)
In general
Subject to paragraph (2), whoever, in the commission of an act described in subsection (a), uses a deadly or dangerous weapon (including a weapon that is intended to cause death or danger but fails to do so by reason of a defective component) or inflicts bodily injury, shall be fined under this title or imprisoned not more than 40 years, or both.
