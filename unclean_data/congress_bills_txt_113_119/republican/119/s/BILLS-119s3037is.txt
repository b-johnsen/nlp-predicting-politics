1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
No AliPay Act of 2025
.
2.
Prohibition on use of AliPay
(a)
In general
A United States person may not conduct a financial transaction with AliPay (China) Internet Technology Company Limited (referred to in this Act as
AliPay
), including by using any application or payment processing service operated by AliPay.
