1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
S-Corporation Additional Participation Act of 2025
or the
S-CAP Act of 2025
.
2.
Increase in number of shareholders to qualify as an S corporation
(a)
In general
Section 1361(b)(1)(A)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking
100
and inserting
250
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by this section shall apply to taxable years beginning after December 31, 2025.
