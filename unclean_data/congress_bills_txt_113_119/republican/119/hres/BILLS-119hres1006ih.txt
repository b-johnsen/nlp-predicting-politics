RESOLUTION
Removing the Director of the Congressional Budget Office.
That effective immediately on the date of the adoption of this resolution, the Director of the Congressional Budget Office is hereby removed pursuant to section 201(a)(4) of the Congressional Budget and Impoundment Control Act of 1974 (
2 U.S.C. 601(a)(4)
).
