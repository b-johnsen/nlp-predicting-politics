1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
No Funding for Illegal Migrant Billboards Act
.
2.
Prohibition on certain advertising with respect to the Immigration Detention Ombudsman
Section 405 of the Homeland Security Act of 2002 (
6 U.S.C. 205
) is amended by adding at the end the following new subsection:
(f)
Advertisement
The Secretary may not obligate or expend any funds to advertise to the general public, by billboard or otherwise, the office or functions of the Ombudsman.
.
