1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Streamlining the Solid Start Communications Act
.
2.
Improvement to certain outreach under Solid Start program of Department of Veterans Affairs
Paragraph (2) of section 6320(b) of title 38, United States Code, is amended by striking
tailored mailings
and inserting
tailored lines of communication, including mailings, text messaging, virtual chatting, and other electronic forms of messaging,
.
