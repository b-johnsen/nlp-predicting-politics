1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Federal Air Improvements for Rural Access and Industry Resilience Act
or the
FAIR AIR Act
.
2.
Deadline for conversion to quiet technology aircraft
Section 35001(b)(1) of the MAP-21 (
49 U.S.C. 40128
note(b)(1)) is amended by striking
Not later than 15 years after the date of enactment of this Act,
and inserting
Not later than December 31, 2032,
.
