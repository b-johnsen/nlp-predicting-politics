1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Endowment Accountability Act
.
2.
Excise tax based on investment income of private colleges and universities
(a)
Increase in rate of tax
Section 4968(a)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking
1.4 percent
and inserting
10 percent
.
(b)
Lowering asset per student threshold for definition of applicable educational institution
Section 4968(b)(1)(D) of such Code is amended by striking
$500,000
and inserting
$200,000
.
(c)
Effective date
The amendments made by this section shall apply to taxable years beginning after the date of the enactment of this Act.
