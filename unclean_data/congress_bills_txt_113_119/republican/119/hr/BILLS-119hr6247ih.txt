1.
Mayor Ed Honea Memorial Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 13355 North Lon Adams Road in Marana, Arizona, shall be known and designated as the
Mayor Ed Honea Memorial Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Mayor Ed Honea Memorial Post Office
.
