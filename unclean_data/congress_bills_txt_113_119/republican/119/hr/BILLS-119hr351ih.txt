Prohibition on congestion or cordon pricing
Section 1012(b) of the Intermodal Surface Transportation Efficiency Act of 1991 (
23 U.S.C. 149
note) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(9)
Prohibition on congestion pricing
Notwithstanding any other provision of this subsection, the Secretary may not establish or maintain a value pricing program under this section that includes value pricing, congestion pricing, or cordon pricing.
