1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Consolidating Aerospace Programs Efficiently at Canaveral Act
or the
CAPE Canaveral Act
.
2.
Transfer of National Aeronautics and Space Administration headquarters to Brevard County, Florida
Not later than one year after the date of the enactment of this Act, the headquarters of the National Aeronautics and Space Administration shall be transferred to Brevard County, Florida.
