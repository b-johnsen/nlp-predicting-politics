1.
Guaranteeing in-State tuition for certain residents of Guam, the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands, American Samoa, and the United States Virgin Islands
(a)
In-State tuition rates
Part C of title I of the Higher Education Act of 1965 (
20 U.S.C. 1015 et seq.
) is amended by inserting after section 135 the following:
135A.
In-State tuition rates for certain residents of Guam, the Commonwealth of the
Northern Mariana Islands, American Samoa, and the United States Virgin
Islands
(a)
In general
A public institution of higher education that receives assistance under this Act shall not charge a covered individual tuition for attendance at such institution at a rate that is greater than the rate charged for residents of the State in which such institution is located.
(b)

)).
