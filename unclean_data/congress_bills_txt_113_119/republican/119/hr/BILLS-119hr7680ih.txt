1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Protect our Ballots Act of 2026
.
2.
Certain nonprofits required to comply with E-Verify
Section 402(e) of the Illegal Immigration Reform and Immigrant Responsibility Act of 1996 is amended by adding at the end the following:
(4)
Certain nonprofits
Any entity described under
section 501(c)(3)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 and exempt from taxation under section 501(a) of such Code, if that entity pays any individual for the provision of personal services for any political purpose or to take an active part in political management or a political campaign, shall elect to participate in the E-Verify program under section 403(a), and shall comply with the terms and conditions of such an election.
.
