1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Protecting Resources Of Taxpayers to Eliminate Childhood Transgender Surgeries Act of 2025
or the
PROTECTS Act of 2025
.
2.
Prohibiting Federal funds from being used to provide certain gender transition procedures to minors
(a)
In general
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, no Federal funds may be used or otherwise made available to provide or refer for a specified gender transition procedure to an individual under the age of 18 or to reimburse any entity for providing such a procedure to such an individual.

(3)
Certain terms
For purposes of this section:
(A)

The term

(B)
Female
The term
female

(C)
Male
The term
male
, when referring to a natural person, means an individual who naturally has, had, will have, or would have, but for a developmental or genetic anomaly or historical accident, the reproductive system that at some point produces, transports, and utilizes sperm for fertilization.
