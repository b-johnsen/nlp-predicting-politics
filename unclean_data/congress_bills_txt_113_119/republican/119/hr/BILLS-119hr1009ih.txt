1.
Paul Piperato Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 86 Main Street in Haverstraw, New York, shall be known and designated as the
Paul Piperato Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Paul Piperato Post Office Building
.
