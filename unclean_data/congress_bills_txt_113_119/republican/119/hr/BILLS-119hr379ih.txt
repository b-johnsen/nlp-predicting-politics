1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Healthcare Freedom and Choice Act
.
2.
Short-term, limited duration insurance rule nullification
The final interagency rule published by the Internal Revenue Service, the Employee Benefits Security Administration, and the Health and Human Services Department relating to
Short-Term, Limited-Duration Insurance and Independent, Noncoordinated Excepted Benefits Coverage
(89 Fed. Reg. 23338 (April 3, 2024)) shall have no force or effect.
