JOINT RESOLUTION
Relating to a national emergency declared by the President on March 13, 2020.
That, pursuant to section 202 of the National Emergencies Act (
50 U.S.C. 1622
), the national emergency declared by the finding of the President on March 13, 2020, in Proclamation 9994 (85 Fed. Reg. 15337) is hereby terminated.
