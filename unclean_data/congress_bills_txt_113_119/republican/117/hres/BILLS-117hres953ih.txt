RESOLUTION
Honoring the first responders who acted swiftly on Sunday, January 16, 2022, to ensure the safety of the residents affected by tornadoes that struck southwest Florida.
Whereas, on January 16, 2022, multiple tornadoes touched down in Lee and Collier Counties, resulting in $8,300,000 in property damage, leaving 15,000 people without power;
Whereas winds reached upward of 118 miles per hour, 4 people were injured, 28 homes were destroyed, and 68 homes were deemed unlivable;
Whereas the Lee County Sheriffâs Office, Fire Department, and Emergency Medical Services acted swiftly to assess damage and account for the lives of all civilians affected by these tornadoes; and
Whereas the American Red Cross, United Way, local community groups, and faith-based organizations assisted in providing food, water, and shelter to those displaced as a result of these tornadoes: Therefore, be it
That the House of Representatives thanked the first responders of southwest Florida for their critical actions that ensured the safety of thousands of Floridians during the tornadoes of January 16, 2022.
