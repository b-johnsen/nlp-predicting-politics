117th CONGRESS
2d Session
H. RES. 1004
In the House of Representatives, U. S.,
March 28, 2022
RESOLUTION
Expressing the profound sorrow of the House of Representatives on the death of the Honorable Donald E. Young.
That the House has heard with profound sorrow of the death of the Honorable Donald E. Young, a Representative from the State of Alaska and beloved Dean of the House.
That the Clerk communicate these resolutions to the Senate and transmit a copy thereof to the family of the deceased.
That when the House adjourns today, it adjourn as a further mark of respect to the memory of the deceased.
Cheryl L. Johnson,
Clerk.
