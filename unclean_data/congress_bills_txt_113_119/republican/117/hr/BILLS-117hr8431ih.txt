1.
Prohibition on the use of LOGINK
(a)
Agency prohibition
A Federal agency may not use LOGINK.
(b)
Contracting prohibition
(1)
In general
The head of a Federal agency may not enter into any contract with an entity that uses LOGINK and shall prohibit the use of LOGINK in any contract entered into by the agency.
(2)
Applicability
This subsection applies with respect to any contract entered into on or after the date of the enactment of this section.
(c)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)
Federal agency
The term
Federal agency
has the meaning given the term
executive agency
in section 133 of title 41, United States Code.
(2)
LOGINK
The term
LOGINK
means the public, open, shared logistics information network known as the National Public Information Platform for Transportation & Logistics by the Ministry of Transport of China.
