1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Stop NEPA Expansion Act
.
2.
Codification of regulations
The revisions to the Code of Federal Regulations made pursuant to the final rule of the Council on Environmental Quality titled
Update to the Regulations Implementing the Procedural Provisions of the National Environmental Policy Act
and published on July 16, 2020 (85 Fed. Reg. 43304), shall have the same force and effect of law as if enacted by an Act of Congress.
