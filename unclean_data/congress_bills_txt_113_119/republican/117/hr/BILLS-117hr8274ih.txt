(r)
Prohibition with respect to agricultural companies
(1)
In general
Notwithstanding any other provision of this section, persons who are acting on behalf of or otherwise directed by the government of a prohibited country may not carry out any merger, acquisition, or takeover that could result in foreign control of a United States agricultural company.
(2)
Prohibited country
In this subsection, the term
prohibited country
means each of the following:
(A)
China.
(B)
Russia.
(C)
Iran.
(D)
North Korea.
.
(b)
Report required
Not later than 180 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, and every 180 days thereafter, the Secretary of Agriculture shall submit to the Committee on Agriculture of the House of Representatives and the Committee on Agriculture, Nutrition, and Forestry of the Senate a report on the risks that foreign purchases of agricultural companies pose to the agricultural sector of the United States.
