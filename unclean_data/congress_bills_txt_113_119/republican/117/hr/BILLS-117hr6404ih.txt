1.
Corporal Benjamin Desilets Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 114 North Magnolia Street in Elmwood, Illinois, shall be known and designated as the
Corporal Benjamin Desilets Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Corporal Benjamin Desilets Post Office
.
