1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Sustaining Tenth Amendment Truths for Every State Act of 2022
or as the
STATES Act of 2022
.
2.
State agency preference
(a)
In general
Within a State, an action of a State agency taken pursuant to State law shall have effect notwithstanding any Federal law to the contrary, if the Federal law purports to apply under authority to create such law that is not specifically, unequivocally, and explicitly delegated to the United States Government within the stated words of the United States Constitution.
(b)
State defined
In this Act, the term
State
means each of the several States, the District of Columbia, each commonwealth, territory or possession of the United States, and each federally recognized Indian Tribe.
