1.
Dorothy Hansine Andersen Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 33 Coxe Avenue, in Asheville, North Carolina, shall be known and designated as the
Dorothy Hansine Andersen Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Dorothy Hansine Andersen Post Office
.
