3.
Preemption with respect to financial institution safeguards
Section 507 of the Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act (
15 U.S.C. 6807
) is amended to read as follows:
507.
Relation to State laws
This subtitle preempts any law, rule, regulation, requirement, standard, or other provision having the force and effect of law of any State, or political subdivision of a State, with respect to a financial institution (other than a financial institution engaged in providing insurance) or affiliate thereof securing personal information from unauthorized access or acquisition, including notification of unauthorized access or acquisition of data.
