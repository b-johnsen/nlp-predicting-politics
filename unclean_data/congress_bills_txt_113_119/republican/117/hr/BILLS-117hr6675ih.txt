Short title

2.
Audit of health care system of the Department of Veterans Affairs
(a)
Audit
The Secretary of Veterans Affairs shall seek to enter into an agreement with a nongovernmental entity to conduct an audit of the health care system of the Department of Veterans Affairs, including with respect to the timeliness of available appointments for veterans to receive hospital care and medical services.
(b)
Submission
