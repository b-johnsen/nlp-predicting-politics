1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
No Alternatives to Detention for Illegals Act of 2022
.
2.
Prohibition on certain alternative to detention programs
Beginning on the date of the enactment of this Act, no Federal funds may be used by the Secretary of Homeland Security to enact any program with respect to the use of a home curfew or other alternatives to detention for an alien who is in the custody of the Secretary and housed at an immigration detention center.
