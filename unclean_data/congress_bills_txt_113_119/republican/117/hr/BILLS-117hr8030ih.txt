1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Fentanyl is a WMD Act
.
2.
Treatment of illicit fentanyl as a weapon of mass destruction
The Assistant Secretary for the Countering Weapons of Mass Destruction Office of the Department of Homeland Security shall treat illicit fentanyl as a weapon of mass destruction for purposes of title XIX of the Homeland Security Act of 2002 (
6 U.S.C. 590 et seq.
).
