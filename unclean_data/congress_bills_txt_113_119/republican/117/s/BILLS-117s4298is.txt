Not later than 180 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Administrator of the TSA shall brief Congress on progress made toward the implementation of this section.
(d)
Deadline
Not later than 2 years after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Administrator of the TSA shall complete the standardization of the enrollment and renewal system for individuals applying for or renewing enrollment in multiple TSA security threat assessment programs described in subsection (a).
