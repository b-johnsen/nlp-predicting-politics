1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Restoring Trust in Public Health Act
.
2.
Appointment of the Director of the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention
Part A of title III of the Public Health Service Act (
42 U.S.C. 241 et seq.
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
310B.
Appointment of the Director of the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention
The President shall appoint, by and with the advice and consent of the Senate, the Director of the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention.
.
