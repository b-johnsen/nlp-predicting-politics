2.
Designation of community-based outpatient clinic of Department of Veterans Affairs in San Angelo, Texas
(a)
Designation
The community-based outpatient clinic of the Department of Veterans Affairs in San Angelo, Texas, shall after the date of the enactment of this Act be known and designated as the
Colonel Charles and JoAnne Powell Department of Veterans Affairs Clinic
or the
Colonel Charles and JoAnne Powell VA Clinic
.
(b)
Reference
Any reference in any law, regulation, map, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the community-based outpatient clinic referred to in subsection (a) shall be considered to be a reference to the Colonel Charles and JoAnne Powell Department of Veterans Affairs Clinic.
