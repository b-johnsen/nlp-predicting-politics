(b)

The strategy required by subsection (a) shall include efforts to develop a more reliable and secure supply chain of critical minerals, including the following:
(1)
Plans to partner, consult, and coordinate with the governments of the other countries in the Quadrilateral Security Dialogue in a concerted effort to make significant strategic investments in development projects, production technologies, and refining facilities.
