1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Biodiesel Tax Credit Extension Act of 2021
.
2.
Extension of tax incentives for biodiesel and renewable diesel
(a)
Income tax credit
(1)
In general
Section 40A(g) is amended by striking
December 31, 2022
and inserting
December 31, 2025
.
(2)
Effective date
The amendment made by this subsection shall apply to fuel sold or used after December 31, 2022.
(b)
Excise tax incentives
(1)
Termination
(A)
In general
Section 6426(c)(6) is amended by striking
December 31, 2022
and inserting
December 31, 2025
.
(B)
Payments
Section 6427(e)(6)(B) is amended by striking
December 31, 2022
and inserting
December 31, 2025
.
(2)
Effective date
The amendments made by this subsection shall apply to fuel sold or used after December 31, 2022.
