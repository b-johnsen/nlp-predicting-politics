1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
No Land Grab Act of 2021
.
2.
Protection of private property
(a)
In general
Section 216 of Executive Order 14008 (86 Fed. Reg. 7627; relating to tackling the climate crisis at home and abroad (February 1, 2021)) shall have no force or effect.
(b)
Recommendations and reports
No Federal agency may take an action if the action is solely authorized by a report or recommendation issued pursuant to section 216 of the Executive Order described in subsection (a).
