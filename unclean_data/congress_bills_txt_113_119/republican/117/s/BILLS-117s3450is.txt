1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Sun River Hydropower Authorization Act
.
2.
Authorization of Sun River Project, Montana
(a)
Authorization
The Secretary of the Interior, acting through the Commissioner of Reclamation and pursuant to the reclamation laws, may construct, operate, and maintain facilities in the Sun River project, Montana, for the purpose of hydroelectric power generation.
