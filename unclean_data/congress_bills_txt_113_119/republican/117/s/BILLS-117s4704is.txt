1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Patent Examination and Quality Improvement Act of 2022
.
2.
Definitions
In this Act:
(1)
Advanced data science analytics
The term
advanced data science analytics
means techniques, such as artificial intelligence, machine learning, and other methods of analyzing large data sets, that may be used to make or implement policy recommendations.
(2)
Director
The term
Director
means the Under Secretary of Commerce for Intellectual Property and Director of the Office.
(3)
Office
The term
Office
means the United States Patent and Trademark Office.
