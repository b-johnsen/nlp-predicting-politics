JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule

Eastern Interior Proposed Resource Management Plan and Final Environmental Impact Statement

Eastern Interior Proposed Resource Management Plan and Final Environmental Impact Statement
, for which notice of availability was published in the Federal Register (81 Fed. Reg. 50009 (July 29, 2016)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
