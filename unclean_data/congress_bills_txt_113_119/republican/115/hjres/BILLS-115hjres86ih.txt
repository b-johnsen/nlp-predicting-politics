JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule
submitted by the Federal Communications Commission relating to
Protecting the Privacy of Customers of Broadband and Other Telecommunications Services
.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Federal Communications Commission relating to
Protecting the Privacy of Customers of Broadband and Other Telecommunications Services
(81 Fed. Reg. 87274 (December 2, 2016)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
