1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
DOE Loan Guarantee Program Repeal Act
.
2.
Repeal of loan guarantee programs
(a)
Repeal
Title XVII of the Energy Policy Act of 2005 (
42 U.S.C. 16511
et seq.) is repealed.
(b)
Rescission
(1)
Initial rescission
On the date of enactment of this Act, any amounts made available to carry out title XVII of the Energy Policy Act of 2005 (as in effect on the date before the date of enactment of this Act), that are not required to pay possible claims on loan guarantees made under such title before the date of enactment of this Act, are rescinded.
(2)
Rescission of remaining amounts
On the date on which all outstanding loans guaranteed under such title have been repaid in full, any remaining amounts made available to carry out such title are rescinded.
(c)
Management
(1)
In general
Notwithstanding subsection (a), the terms and conditions of any loan guarantee made before the date of enactment of this Act under title XVII of the Energy Policy Act of 2005 (as in effect on the date before the date of enactment of this Act) shall continue to apply, and the Secretary of Energy shall ensure rigorous continued management and oversight of each such outstanding loan, until each such loan is repaid in full.
(2)
Report
