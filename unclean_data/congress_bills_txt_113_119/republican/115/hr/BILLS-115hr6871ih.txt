2.
Rural Broadband Tax Credit
(a)
In general
The Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by inserting after subsection 48D the following new section:
48E
Rural Broadband Credit
(a)
In general
For the purposes of section 38, the rural broadband credit for any taxable year is an amount equal to 30 percent of the basis of qualified broadband property placed in service by the taxpayer during such taxable year.
