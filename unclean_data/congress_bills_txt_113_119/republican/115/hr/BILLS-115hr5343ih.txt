Regulations and guidance
The Secretary may issue such regulations and guidance as may be necessary for implementation of this section.
(e)
Definitions
In this section:
(1)
The term
drug
has the meaning given to such term in section 201 of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act.
(2)
The term
health insurer
means a health insurance issuer (as defined in section 2791) offering health insurance coverage (as defined in such section) in the individual or group market or a sponsor of a group health plan (as defined in such section).
(3)
The term
prescription drug
means a drug subject to section 503(b)(1) of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act.
