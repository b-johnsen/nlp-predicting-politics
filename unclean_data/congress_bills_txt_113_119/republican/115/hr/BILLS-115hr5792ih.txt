1.
Deputy Sheriff Heath McDonald Gumm Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 90 North 4th Avenue in Brighton, Colorado, shall be known and designated as the
Deputy Sheriff Heath McDonald Gumm Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Deputy Sheriff Heath McDonald Gumm Post Office
.
