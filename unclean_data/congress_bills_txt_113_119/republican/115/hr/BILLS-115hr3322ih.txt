1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Smoke Free Affordable Housing Act
.
2.
Low-income housing credit allocation preference for non-smoking buildings
(a)
In general
Section 42(m)(1)(B)(ii)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by adding at the end the following new subclause:
(IV)
projects consisting only of buildings in which smoking is not permitted, and
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by this section shall apply to allocations made after the date of the enactment of this Act.
