1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Restoring Financial Market Freedom Act of 2017
.
2.
Repeal of title VIII
(a)
Repeal
Title VIII of the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act (
12 U.S.C. 5461
et seq.) is repealed, and provisions of law amended by such title are restored and revived as if such title had never been enacted.
(b)
Clerical amendment
The table of contents in section 1(b) of the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act is amended by striking the items relating to title VIII.
