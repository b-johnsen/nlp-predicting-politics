2.
Requirement for certain Department of Veterans Affairs medical centers to have physical location for the disposal of controlled substances medications
(a)
In general
The Secretary of Veterans Affairs shall ensure that each covered Department medical center has a physical location where patients may dispose of controlled substances medications.
