1.
Corporal Jeffery Allen Williams Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 3s101 Rockwell Street in Warrenville, Illinois, shall be known and designated as the
Corporal Jeffery Allen Williams Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Corporal Jeffery Allen Williams Post Office Building
.
