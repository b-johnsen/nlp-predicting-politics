1.
Consideration of Margaret R. Pardee Memorial Hospital as covered entity for purposes of 340B drug
discount program
Notwithstanding the definition of a
covered entity
under paragraph (4) of section 340B(a) of the Public Health Service Act (
42 U.S.C. 256b(a)
), the Secretary of Health and Human Services shall consider the Margaret R. Pardee Memorial Hospital located in Hendersonville, North Carolina, as a covered entity under such paragraph for purposes of allowing the Margaret R. Pardee Memorial Hospital to participate in the drug discount program under such section 340B.
