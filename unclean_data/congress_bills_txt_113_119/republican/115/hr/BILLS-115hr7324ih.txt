(B)
Expiration
Subparagraph (A) shall not apply with respect to any drug or biological product after the date on which such drug or biological product is approved or licensed by the Food and Drug Administration for marketing for any indication other than the treatment of a rare disease or condition.
(C)

rare disease or condition

of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986.
