1.
Direct Medicare payment for services furnished by physician assistants
(a)
In general
Section 1832(a)(2)(B) of the Social Security Act (
42 U.S.C. 1395k(a)(2)(B)
) is amended by adding at the end the following new clause:
(v)
services of a physician assistant but only if no facility or other provider charges or is paid any amounts with respect to the furnishing of such services;
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by subsection (a) shall apply with respect to services furnished on or after July 1, 2019.
