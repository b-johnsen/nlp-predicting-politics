1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Disaster Certainty Act
.
2.
Automatic 60-day delay for taxpayers in area with respect to which federally declared disaster is requested
(a)
In general
Section 7508A(a)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by adding at the end the following:
In the case of a bona fide resident of an area with respect to which the Governor for such area
requests a declaration with respect to a disaster under the Robert T.
Stafford Disaster Relief and Emergency Assistance Act, the 60-day period
beginning on the date of such request shall be disregarded in the same
manner as a period specified under the preceding sentence.
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendments made by this section shall apply with respect to disasters declared in taxable years beginning after December 31, 2017.
