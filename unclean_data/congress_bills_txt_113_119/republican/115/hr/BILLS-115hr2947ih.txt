1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Strengthening Coastal Communities Act of 2017
.
2.
Replacement of certain Coastal Barrier Resources System maps
(a)
St. Andrew Complex P31 (1 of 3)
The map entitled
St. Andrew Complex P31 (1 of 3)
and dated January 11, 2016, as included in the set of maps entitled
Coastal Barrier Resources System
referred to in section 4(a) of the Coastal Barrier Resources Act (
16 U.S.C. 3503(a)
), is hereby replaced by another map relating to that unit entitled
St. Andrew Complex P31 (1 of 3)
and dated October 7, 2016.
