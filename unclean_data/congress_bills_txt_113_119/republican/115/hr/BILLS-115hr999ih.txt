2.
Findings
The Congress finds the following:
(1)
College costs have increased tremendously over the past decade. When adjusted for inflation, the average published price for in-State students at public 4-year universities is 34 percent higher than it was a decade ago and more than twice as high as it was 20 years ago. The price of private nonprofit 4-year institutions has increased 24 percent during the last 10 years and 51 percent during the last 20 years.
(2)
Federal financial aid for students has also increased rapidly but failed to mitigate the issues of rising college costs.
