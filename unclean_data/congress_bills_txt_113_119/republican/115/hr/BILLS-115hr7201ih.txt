1.
Patent infringement in exclusive economic zone of the United States
(a)
Amendment
Chapter 10
of title 35, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following new section:
106.
Inventions in the exclusive economic zone
Any act occurring on or below the waters of the exclusive economic zone of the United States established by Presidential Proclamation 5030 of March 10, 1983 (
16 U.S.C. 1453
note), including activity on the seabed of such zone, that is related to the economic exploitation of, or exploration for, natural resources or marine scientific research shall be considered an act occurring within the United States for the purposes of section 271.
.
(b)
Technical and conforming amendment
The table of sections for
chapter 10
of title 35, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following new item:
106. Inventions in the exclusive economic zone.
.
