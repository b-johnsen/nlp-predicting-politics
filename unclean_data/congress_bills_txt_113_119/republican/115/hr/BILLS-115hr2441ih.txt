1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Sex Trafficking Demand Reduction Act of 2017
.
2.
Additional authorized use of Byrne JAG funds
Section 501(a)(1) of the Omnibus Crime Control and Safe Streets Act of 1968 (
42 U.S.C. 3751(a)(1)
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(I)
Programs to combat human trafficking (including programs to reduce the demand for trafficked persons).
.
