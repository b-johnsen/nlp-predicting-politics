Name of Department of Veterans Affairs community-based outpatient clinic, Pago Pago, American Samoa
The Department of Veterans Affairs community-based outpatient clinic in Pago Pago, American Samoa, shall after the date of the enactment of this Act be known and designated as the
Faleomavaega Eni Fa'aua'a Hunkin VA Clinic
. Any reference to such community-based outpatient clinic in any law, regulation, map, document, record, or other paper of the United States shall be considered to be a reference to the Faleomavaega Eni Fa'aua'a Hunkin VA Clinic.
