RESOLUTION

Whereas the National Wildlife Refuge System under the United States Fish and Wildlife Service manages 566 national wildlife refuges that constitute a national network of land and water managed for the conservation of fish, wildlife, and plants in the United States;
Whereas national wildlife refuges provide opportunities for people to discover and appreciate nature;
Whereas there is a refuge located within a 1-hour drive of every metropolitan area in the United States;
