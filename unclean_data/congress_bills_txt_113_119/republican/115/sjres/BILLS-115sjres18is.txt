JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the
final rule submitted by the Department of the Interior relating to
Non-Subsistence Take of Wildlife, and Public Participation and Closure
Procedures, on National Wildlife Refuges in Alaska.
That Congress disapproves the final rule submitted by the Department of the Interior relating to Non-Subsistence Take of Wildlife, and Public Participation and Closure Procedures, on National Wildlife Refuges in Alaska (81 Fed. Reg. 52248 (August 5, 2016)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
