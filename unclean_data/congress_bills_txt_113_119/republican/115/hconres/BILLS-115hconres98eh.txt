IV
115th CONGRESS
2d Session
H. CON. RES. 98
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
Directing the Secretary of the Senate to make a correction in the enrollment of the bill S. 139.
That in the enrollment of the bill S. 139, the
Secretary of the Senate shall make the following correction: Amend the long title so as to read:
An Act to amend the Foreign Intelligence Surveillance Act of 1978 to improve foreign intelligence collection and the safeguards, accountability, and oversight of acquisitions of foreign intelligence, to extend title VII of such Act, and for other purposes.
.
Passed the House of Representatives January 11, 2018.
Karen L. Haas,
Clerk.
