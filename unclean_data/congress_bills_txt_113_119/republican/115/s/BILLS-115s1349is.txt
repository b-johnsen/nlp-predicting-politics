2.
Rate of military basic pay for the Senior Enlisted Advisors to the commanders of the combatant commands
(a)
In general
Effective for months beginning on or after January 1, 2017, the rate of monthly basic pay for the Senior Enlisted Advisors to the commanders of the combatant commands shall be equivalent to the rate of monthly basic pay for the Senior Enlisted Advisor to the Chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff.
