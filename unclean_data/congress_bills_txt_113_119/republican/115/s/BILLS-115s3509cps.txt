1.
Short
title
This Act may be cited as the
Congressional Award Program Reauthorization Act of 2018
.
2.
Termination
(a)
In general
Section 108 of the Congressional Award Act (
2 U.S.C. 808
) is amended by striking
October 1, 2018
and inserting
October 1, 2023
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by subsection (a) shall take effect on October 1, 2018.
