(b)
Sense of Congress
It is the sense of the Congress that the Government of North Korea should be designated as a state sponsor of terrorism.
(c)
Determination
Not later than 90 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Secretary of State shall submit to the appropriate congressional committees a determination as to whether North Korea meets the criteria for designation as a state sponsor of terrorism.
(d)
Form
The report required by subsection (c) shall be submitted in unclassified form, but may include a classified annex, if appropriate.
