1.
Review of Medicare payment determinations for disproportionate share hospitals
(a)
In General
Section 1886(r) of the Social Security Act (
42 U.S.C. 1395ww(r)
) is amended by striking paragraph (3).
(b)
Effective Date
The amendment made by subsection (a) shall apply with respect to any claim relating to a determination made under paragraph (2) of such section 1886(r) that is pending on a date that is on or after the date of the enactment of this Act.
