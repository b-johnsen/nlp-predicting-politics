(2)
Authority
(A)
In general
Except as provided in subparagraph (B), and notwithstanding subsection (c), if a qualified employee acting in his or her official capacity at an international service center that processes inbound international mail has probable cause to believe that a covered package contains illicit materials, including matter that is nonmailable under section 3001, the employee shall, without obtaining a search warrant, inspect the contents of the covered package.
(B)
Safety exception
The requirement under subparagraph (A) shall not apply with respect to a covered package if a qualified employee believes that inspecting the contents of the covered package would pose a risk of personal injury to the employee or other individuals.
