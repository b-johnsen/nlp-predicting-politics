CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
To correct the enrollment of S. 140.
That in the enrollment of S. 140, an Act to amend the White Mountain Apache Tribe Water Rights Quantification Act of 2010 to clarify the use of amounts in the WMAT Settlement Fund, the Secretary of the Senate shall amend the title so as to read:
A bill to authorize appropriations for the Coast Guard, and for other purposes.
