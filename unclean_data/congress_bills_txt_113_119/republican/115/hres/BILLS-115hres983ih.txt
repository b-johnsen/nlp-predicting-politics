RESOLUTION
Expressing the sense of the House of Representatives that the Nation now faces a more complex and
grave set of threats than at any time since the end of World War II and

security, and aids the adversaries of the United States.

Whereas the National Defense Strategy released on January 19, 2018, highlights these threats and acknowledges a return to great power competition;

;
Whereas fiscal year 2009 was the last fiscal year the Department of Defense was not forced to operate under any continuing resolution;
