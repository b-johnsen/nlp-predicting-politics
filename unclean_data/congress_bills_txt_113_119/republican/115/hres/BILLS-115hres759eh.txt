115th CONGRESS
2d Session
H. RES. 759
In the House of Representatives, U. S.,
March 1, 2018
RESOLUTION
That the House of Representatives offers its deep sympathies to the members of the family of the late Reverend Billy Graham.
That the House of Representatives honors the legacy of Reverend Billy Graham for his life-long commitment to preaching the Gospels, his fight for civil rights, his opposition to communism, and the spiritual guidance that he provided to millions of people in the United States and around the world.
That when the House adjourns today, it do so as a mark of respect to the memory of the late Billy Graham.
Karen L. Haas,
Clerk.
