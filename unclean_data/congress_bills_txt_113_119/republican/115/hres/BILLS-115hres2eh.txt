IV
115th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 2
In the House of Representatives, U. S.,
January 3, 2017
RESOLUTION
To inform the Senate that a quorum of the House has assembled and of the election of the Speaker and the Clerk
That the Senate be informed that a quorum of the House of Representatives has assembled; that Paul D. Ryan, a Representative from the State of Wisconsin, has been elected Speaker; and that Karen L. Haas, a citizen of the State of Maryland, has been elected Clerk of the House of Representatives of the One Hundred Fifteenth Congress.
Karen L. Haas,
Clerk.
