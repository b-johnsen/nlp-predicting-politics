1.
Short Title
This Act may be cited as the
Preventing the EPA from Garnishing Wages Act of 2014
.
2.
Prohibition on Wage Garnishment by the Environmental Protection Agency
Section 3720D
of title 31, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
(i)
This section shall not apply to a debt owed pursuant to a program administered by the Environmental
Protection Agency.
.
