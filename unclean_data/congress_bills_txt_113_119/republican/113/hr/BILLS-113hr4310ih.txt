1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Ready to Work Act
.
2.
Deadline to issue regulations
(a)
In general
Not later than 1 year after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Secretary of Labor shall
issue a final rule to carry out section 303(l)(1)(A)(ii) of the Social
Security Act.
(b)
Special rule if interim rule not issued after 180 days
If the Secretary of Labor has not issued an interim final rule to carry out such section as of the
date that is 180 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the
final rule required to be issued under subsection (a) shall provide that,
for purposes of such section, the State agency charged with the
administration of the State unemployment compensation law shall determine
whether an occupation regularly conducts drug testing.
