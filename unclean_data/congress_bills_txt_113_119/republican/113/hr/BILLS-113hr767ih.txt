1.
Pilot Project offices of Federal Permit Streamlining Pilot Project
Section 365 of the Energy Policy Act of 2005 (
42 U.S.C. 15924
) is amended by striking subsection (d) and inserting the following:
(d)
Pilot project offices
The following Bureau of Land Management Offices shall serve as the Pilot Project offices:
(1)
Rawlins Field Office, Wyoming.
(2)
Buffalo Field Office, Wyoming.
(3)
Montana/Dakotas State Office, Montana.
(4)
Farmington Field Office, New Mexico.
(5)
Carlsbad Field Office, New Mexico.
(6)
Grand Junction/Glenwood Springs Field Office, Colorado.
(7)
Vernal Field Office, Utah.
