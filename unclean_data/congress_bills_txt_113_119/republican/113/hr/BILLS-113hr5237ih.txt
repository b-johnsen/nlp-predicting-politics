(b)
Aliens described
An alien described in this subsection is an alien having lawful status under section
101(a)(15)(E)(ii) of the Immigration and Nationality Act (8 U.S.C.
1101(a)(15)(E)(ii)) by reason of a change of nonimmigrant classification
made in the United States under section 248 of such Act (
8 U.S.C. 1258
).
(c)
Travel documents
The Secretary of Homeland Security shall commence issuance of travel documents determined
appropriate by the Secretary to carry out this section, such as re-entry
permits, advance parole travel documents, or any other travel documents
deemed appropriate by the Secretary for this purpose.
