1.
Richard K. Salick Post
Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal
Service located at 500 North Brevard Avenue in Cocoa Beach, Florida, shall be
known and designated as the
Richard K. Salick Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation,
document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred
to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Richard K.
Salick Post Office
.
