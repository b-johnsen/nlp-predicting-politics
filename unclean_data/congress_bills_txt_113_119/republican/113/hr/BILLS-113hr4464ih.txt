1.
Look-through treatment of payments between related controlled foreign corporations made permanent
(a)
In general
Paragraph (6) of
section 954(c)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking
subparagraph (C).
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by this section shall apply to taxable years of foreign corporations beginning
after December 31, 2013, and to taxable years of United States
shareholders with or within which such taxable years of foreign
corporations end.
