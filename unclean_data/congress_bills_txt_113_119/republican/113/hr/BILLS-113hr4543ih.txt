(b)
Sense of the House of Representatives
It is the sense of the House of Representatives that the Secretary of Health and Human Services
should use the waiver authority provided under the amendments made by this
section to provide, in a budget neutral manner, programs of all-inclusive
care for the elderly (PACE programs) with increased operational
flexibility to support the ability of such programs to improve and
innovate and to reduce technical and administrative barriers that have
hindered enrollment in such programs.
