1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Focusing the Fed on the Currency of the United States Act of 2013
or the
FFOCUS Act of 2013
.
2.
Removal of dual mandate
Section 2A of the Federal Reserve Act (
12 U.S.C. 225a
) is amended by striking
maximum employment, stable prices,
and inserting
stable prices
.
