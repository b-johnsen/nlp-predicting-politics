1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Suspending the Individual Mandate Penalty Law Equals Fairness Act
or as the
SIMPLE Fairness Act
.
2.
Delay in implementation of penalty for failure to comply with individual health insurance mandate
(a)
In general
Section 5000A(c)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by adding at the end the following
new paragraph:
(5)
Delay in implementation of penalty
Notwithstanding any other provision of this subsection, the monthly penalty amount with respect to
any taxpayer for any month beginning before January 1, 2015, shall be
zero.
