1.
Leasing of non-excess property of military departments and Defense Agencies; treatment of value
provided by local education agencies and elementary and secondary schools
Section 2667
of title 10, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following new
subsection:
(k)
Leases for education
In the case of a lease under this section to a local education
agency or an elementary or secondary
school (as those terms are
defined in section 9101 of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of
1965 (
20 U.S.C. 7801
)), consideration may be at or below fair market value
or for no consideration.
