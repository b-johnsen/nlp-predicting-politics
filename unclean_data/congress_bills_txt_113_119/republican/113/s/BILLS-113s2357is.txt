Dependent defined
In this section, the term
dependent
, with respect to a member of the Armed Forces, means a person described in section 1072(2) of
title 10, United States Code, except that, in the case of a parent or
parent-in-law of the member, the income requirements of subparagraph (E)
of such section do not apply.
