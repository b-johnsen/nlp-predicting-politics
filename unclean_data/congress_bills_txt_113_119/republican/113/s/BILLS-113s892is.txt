A person described in this subsection is a person the

(d)
Rule of

Nothing in this section shall be construed to
prohibit any person from, or authorize or require the imposition of sanctions
with respect to any person for, conducting or facilitating any transaction in
the currency of the country in which the person is operating at the time of the
transaction for the sale of agricultural commodities, food, medicine, or
medical devices.

(3)
Foreign

The term

(4)
Iranian

The term

U.S.C. 8513b(d)).
(5)
Medical
device
The term

the term

in section 201 of the Federal Food, Drug, and
Cosmetic Act (
21 U.S.C. 321
).
(6)
Medicine
The

has the meaning given the term
drug
in
section 201 of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (
21 U.S.C. 321
).
(7)
Transaction
The

includes a foreign exchange swap, a foreign
exchange forward, and any other type of currency exchange or conversion or
derivative
instrument.
