Rule of construction
The amendments made by subsection (a) shall not exempt, nor shall they be construed to exempt, any
law firm or licensed attorney engaged in any activity other than those
activities specifically described in the amendments from being subject to
any other applicable provision of the Fair Debt Collection Practices Act.
