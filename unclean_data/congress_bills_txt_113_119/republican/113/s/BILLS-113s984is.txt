1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Conditioning United States-Egypt
Military Exercises Act of 2013
.
2.
Contingent limitation
on availability of funds for United States participation in joint military
exercises with Egypt
(a)
Limitation
No amounts appropriated or otherwise made
available for the United States Government may be used for United States
participation in joint military exercises with Egypt if the Government of Egypt
abrogates, terminates, or withdraws from the 1979 Egypt-Israel peace treaty
signed at Washington, DC, on March 26, 1979.
(b)
Waiver
The
President may waive the limitation in subsection (a) if the President certifies
to Congress in writing that the waiver is in the national security interests of
the United States.
