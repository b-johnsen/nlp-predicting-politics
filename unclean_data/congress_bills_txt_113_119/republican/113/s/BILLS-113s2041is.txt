1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
May 31, 1918 Act Repeal Act
.
2.
Definitions
In this Act:
(1)
1918 Act
The term
1918 Act
means the Act of May 31, 1918 (40 Stat. 592, chapter 88).
(2)
Tribes
The term
Tribes
means the Shoshone-Bannock Tribes of the Fort Hall Indian Reservation.
3.
Repeal
The 1918 Act is repealed.
