JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval
under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the
Internal Revenue Service of the Department of the Treasury relating to taxable
medical devices.
That Congress disapproves the rule
submitted by the Internal Revenue Service of the Department of the Treasury
relating to taxable medical devices (published at 77 Fed. Reg. 72924 (December
7, 2012)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
