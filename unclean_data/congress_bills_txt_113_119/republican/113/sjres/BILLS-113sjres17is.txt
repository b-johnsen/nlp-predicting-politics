Proposing an amendment to the Constitution of the United
States authorizing Congress to prohibit the physical desecration of the flag of
the United States.

proposed as an amendment to the Constitution of the United States, which shall
be valid to all intents and purposes as part of the Constitution when ratified
by the legislatures of three-fourths of the several States within 7 years after
the date of its submission for ratification:
