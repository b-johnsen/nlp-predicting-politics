1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Transparency on Prioritizing Spending Act
.
2.
Priority of spending if the debt ceiling is reached
Not later than 30 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the President shall submit to Congress a report listing, in descending order of prioritization, which obligations of the United States Government will be paid in the event that the debt of the United States Government, as defined in
section 3101
of title 31, United States Code, reaches the statutory limit.
