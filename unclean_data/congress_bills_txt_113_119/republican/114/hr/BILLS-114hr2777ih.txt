The OIG identified approximately 6.5 million numberholders age 112 or older who did not have death information on the Numident.

(8)
The OIG determined that resolving these discrepancies will improve the accuracy and completeness of the Death Master File and help prevent future misuse of these SSNs.

(10)
In line with the OIGГўВҖВҷs recommendations, the SSA should take proactive action to protect the American taxpayer by ensuring that there are controls in place to annotate death information on the Numident records of numberholders who exceeded maximum reasonable life expectancies.
