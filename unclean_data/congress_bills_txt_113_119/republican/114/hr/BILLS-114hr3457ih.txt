1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Justice for Victims of Iranian Terrorism Act
.
2.
Prohibition on lifting of sanctions on Iran pending payment of certain judgments
(a)
In general
The President may not waive, suspend, reduce, provide relief from, or otherwise limit the application of statutory sanctions with respect to Iran under any provision of law, or refrain from applying any such sanctions pursuant to an agreement described in section 135(a) of the Atomic Energy Act of 1954 (
42 U.S.C. 2160e(a)
), until the President has certified to the Congress that the Government of Iran has paid each judgment against Iran (whether or not the judgment was also entered against another defendant) that is described in subsection (b).
