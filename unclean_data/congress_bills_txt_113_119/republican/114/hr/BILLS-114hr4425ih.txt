1.
Eugene J. McCarthy Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 110 East Powerhouse Road in Collegeville, Minnesota, shall be known and designated as the
Eugene J. McCarthy Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Eugene J. McCarthy Post Office
.
