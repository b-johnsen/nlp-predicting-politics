1.
Enhancing combination products review
Section 503(g)(4)(C) of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (
21 U.S.C. 353(g)(4)(C)
) is amended by adding at the end the following new clause:
(iii)
Not later than 18 months after the date of the enactment of the
21st Century Cures Act
, the Secretary shall issue final guidance that describes the responsibilities of each agency center regarding its review of combination products. The Secretary shall, after soliciting public comment, review and update the guidance periodically.
