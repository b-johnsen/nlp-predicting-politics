1.
Sharing of data generated through NIH-funded research
Section 402 of the Public Health Service Act (
42 U.S.C. 282
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(m)
Sharing of data generated through NIH-Funded research
(1)
Authority
Subject to paragraph (2), the Director of NIH may require recipients of the award of an NIH grant or other financial support, provided that the research is fully funded through such grant or other support, to share scientific data generated from research conducted through such support for research purposes.

Limitation
