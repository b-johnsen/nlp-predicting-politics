1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Social Security Lump-Sum Death Benefit Improvement and Modernization Act of 2015
.
2.
Increase in maximum amount of lump-sum death payments
(a)
In general
Section 202(i) of the Social Security Act (
42 U.S.C. 402(i)
) is amended by striking
$255
and inserting
$1,000
.
(b)
Effective date
The amendment made by this section shall apply with respect to deaths that occur after the date of the enactment of this Act.
