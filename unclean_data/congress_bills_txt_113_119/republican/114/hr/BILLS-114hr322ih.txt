1.
Sgt. Zachary M. Fisher Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 16105 Swingley Ridge Road in Chesterfield, Missouri, shall be known and designated as the
Sgt. Zachary M. Fisher Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Sgt. Zachary M. Fisher Post Office
.
