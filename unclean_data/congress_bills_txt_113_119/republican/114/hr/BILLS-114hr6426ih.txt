1.
Temporary prohibition on employment of political appointees in career civil service positions
(a)
In general
Subchapter I of
chapter 31
of title 5, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
3115.
Employment of former political appointees; restrictions
(a)
An individual occupying a political position in the civil service may not be appointed to a career position in the civil service during the period of 2 years immediately following the date the individual separates from such political position.

Application
