4.
Definition
In this Act, the term
Member of the House of Representatives
includes a Delegate or Resident Commissioner to the Congress.
5.
Effective Date
This Act shall apply with respect to trips taken on or after the date of the enactment of this Act, except that this Act does not apply with respect to any trip which began prior to such date.
