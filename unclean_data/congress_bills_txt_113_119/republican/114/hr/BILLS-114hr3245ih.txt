1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Government Refusal of Abortion in Contracting and Enterprise
or the
GRACE Act
.
2.
Prohibition on contracting with entities that donate to Planned Parenthood Federation of America, Inc
(a)
Prohibition
A Federal entity may not enter into a contract with a person or entity that donates or matches employee donations to Planned Parenthood Federation of America, Inc., or any affiliate or clinic of Planned Parenthood Federation of America, Inc.
