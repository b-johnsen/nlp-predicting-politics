(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 501 North Main Street in Florence, Arizona, shall be known and designated as the
Adolfo
Harpo
Celaya Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Adolfo
Harpo
Celaya Post Office
.
