1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
IDENT Fingerprint Digitization Completion Act of 2016
.
2.
Fingerprint digitization
Not later than 90 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Director of U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement shall complete the digitization of all remaining paper-based fingerprint records for inclusion in the Automated Biometric Identification System (IDENT) of the Department of Homeland Security.
