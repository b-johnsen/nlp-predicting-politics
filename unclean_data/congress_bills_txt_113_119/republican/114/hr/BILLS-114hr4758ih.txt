1.
Expansion of Presidential Memorial Certificate program
(a)
In general
Section 112(a) of title 38, United States Code, is amended by inserting after
conditions,
the following:
persons eligible for burial in a national cemetery by reason of paragraph (2) or (3) of section 2402(a) of this title,
.
(b)
Application
The amendment made by subsection (a) shall apply with respect to the death of a person eligible for burial in a national cemetery by reason of paragraph (2) or (3) of section 2402(a) of title 38, United States Code, occurring on or after the date of the enactment of this Act.
