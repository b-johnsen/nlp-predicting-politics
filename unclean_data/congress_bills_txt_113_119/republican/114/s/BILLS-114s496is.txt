1.
Prohibition against finalizing, implementing, or enforcing the proposed rule entitled
Standards for the Growing, Harvesting, Packing, and Holding of Produce for Human Consumption
No Federal funds may be used to finalize, implement, or enforce the proposed rule entitled
Standards for the Growing, Harvesting, Packing, and Holding of Produce for Human Consumption
published by the Department of Health and Human Services on January 16, 2013 (78 Fed. Reg. 3503), or any successor or substantially similar rule.
