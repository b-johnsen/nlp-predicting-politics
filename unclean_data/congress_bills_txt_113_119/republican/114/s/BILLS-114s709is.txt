1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Restoring Access to Medication Act of 2015
.
2.
Repeal of disqualification of expenses for over-the-counter drugs under certain accounts and arrangements
(a)
HSAs
Section 223(d)(2)(A)
of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking the last sentence.
(b)
Archer MSAs
Section 220(d)(2)(A) of such Code is amended by striking the last sentence.
(c)
Health flexible spending arrangements and health reimbursement arrangements
Section 106 of such Code is amended by striking subsection (f).
(d)
Effective date
The amendments made by this section shall apply to expenses incurred after December 31, 2014.
