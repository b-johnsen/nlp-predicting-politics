aboard the
El Faro

Whereas the
El Faro
departed Jacksonville, Florida for Puerto Rico on September 29, 2015, with 33 crew members aboard;
Whereas the crew of the
El Faro

Whereas the
El Faro
sent distress alerts on October 1, 2015;
Whereas members of the Coast Guard, Navy, and Air Force valiantly searched for the crew members of the
El Faro
; and
Whereas the people of the United States mourn the loss of the 33 seamen aboard the
El Faro
: Now, therefore, be it

El Faro

(3)
offers heartfelt condolences to the family, friends, and loved ones of the crew members of the
El Faro
