III
114th CONGRESS
1st Session
S. RES. 1
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
January 6, 2015
Mr. McConnell
submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to
RESOLUTION
Informing the President of the United States that a quorum of each House is assembled.
That a committee consisting of two Senators be appointed to join such committee as may be
appointed by the House of Representatives to wait upon the President of
the United States and inform him that a quorum of each House is assembled
and that the Congress is ready to receive any communication he may be
pleased to make.
