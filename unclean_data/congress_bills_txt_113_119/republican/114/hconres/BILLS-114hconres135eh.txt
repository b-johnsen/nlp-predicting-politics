IV
114th CONGRESS
2d Session
H. CON. RES. 135
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
Directing the Secretary of the Senate to make technical corrections in the enrollment of S. 2328.
That in the enrollment of the bill S. 2328, the Secretary of the Senate shall make the following correction:
Amend the long title so as to read
An Act to establish an Oversight Board to assist the Government of Puerto Rico, including instrumentalities, in managing its public finances, and for other purposes.
.
Passed the House of Representatives June 9, 2016.
Karen L. Haas,
Clerk.
