CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
Expressing the sense of the Congress regarding the treatment of State Governors who have made a
determination with respect to Syrian refugees.
Whereas a Governor is the chief executive officer of their State; and
Whereas the Federal Government should not be able to override a Governorâs decision with respect to refugees: Now, therefore, be it
That in the case of a Governor requesting that Syrian refugees not be resettled in their State (whether temporarily or permanently), no Federal official should take any adverse action against, or withhold funds from, the State, and no Federal official should take any other action designed to persuade or entice the Governor to reverse such a request.
