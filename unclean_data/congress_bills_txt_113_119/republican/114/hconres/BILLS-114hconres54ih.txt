CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
Authorizing the reprinting of the 25th edition of the pocket version of the United States
Constitution.
1.
Pocket version of the United States Constitution
(a)
In General
The 25th edition of the pocket version of the United States Constitution shall be reprinted as a House document under the direction of the Joint Committee on Printing.
(b)
Additional Copies

(1)
285,400 copies of the document, of which 235,400 copies shall be for the use of the House of Representatives and 50,000 copies shall be for the use of the Senate; or
(2)

(c)
Distribution
The copies of the document reprinted for the use of the House and the Senate under subsection (a) shall be distributed in accordance withâ
(1)
a distribution plan approved by the chair and ranking minority member of the Committee on House Administration of the House of Representatives, in the case of the copies printed for the use of the House; and
(2)
a distribution plan approved by the chair and ranking minority member of the Committee on Rules and Administration of the Senate, in the case of the copies printed for the use of the Senate.
