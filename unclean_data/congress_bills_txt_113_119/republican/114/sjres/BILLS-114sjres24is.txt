JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of a rule
submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to
Carbon Pollution Emission Guidelines for Existing Stationary Sources: Electric Utility Generating
Units
.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to
Carbon Pollution Emission Guidelines for Existing Stationary Sources: Electric Utility Generating Units
(published at 80 Fed. Reg. 64662 (October 23, 2015)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
