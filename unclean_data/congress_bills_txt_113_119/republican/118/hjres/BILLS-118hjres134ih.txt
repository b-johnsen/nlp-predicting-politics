JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Department of Labor relating to
Improving Protections for Workers in Temporary Agricultural Employment in the United States
.

Improving Protections for Workers in Temporary Agricultural Employment in the United States
(89 Fed. Reg. 33898 (April 29, 2024)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
