JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8
of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Department of Education relating to
Nondiscrimination on the Basis of Sex in Education Programs or Activities Receiving Federal Financial Assistance
.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Department of Education relating to
Nondiscrimination on the Basis of Sex in Education Programs or Activities Receiving Federal Financial Assistance
(89 Fed. Reg. 33474; published April 29, 2024), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
