JOINT RESOLUTION
Disapproving the rule submitted by the Office of Personnel Management relating to Postal Service Reform Act; Establishment of the Postal Service Health Benefits Program.
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Office of Personnel Management relating to Postal Service Reform Act; Establishment of the Postal Service Health Benefits Program (89 Fed. Reg. 37061), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
