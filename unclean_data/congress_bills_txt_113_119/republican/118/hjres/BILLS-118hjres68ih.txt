JOINT RESOLUTION
Relating to a national emergency declared by the President on October 27, 2006.
That, pursuant to section 202 of the National Emergencies Act (
50 U.S.C. 1622
), the national emergency declared by the finding of the President on October 27, 2006, in Executive Order 13413 is hereby terminated.
