Providing for congressional disapproval under
chapter 8

Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Regulations Pertaining to Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants

(89 Fed. Reg. 23919 (April 5, 2024)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
