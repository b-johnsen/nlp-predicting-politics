1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Thrifty Food Plan Accountability Act of 2023
.
2.
Adjustments to the thrifty food plan in the supplemental nutrition assistance program
Section 3(u) of the Food and Nutrition Act of 2008 (
7 U.S.C. 2012(u)
) is amended in the matter preceding paragraph (1) by inserting after the second sentence the following:
Each re-evaluation shall be budget-neutral and adjust the cost of the diet only in accordance with paragraphs (1) through (4), as applicable.
.
