1.
Limiting flags and pennants acquired or displayed by legislative branch offices
(a)
Limitation
Except as provided in subsection (b), an office of the legislative branch may not use any funds appropriated or otherwise made available under Federal law during a fiscal year to acquire or display any flag or pennant other than the flag of the United States.
(b)
Exceptions
Subsection (a) does not apply to any of the following flags or pennants:
(1)
The flag or pennant of any State or territory or possession of the United States.
(2)
The flag or pennant of any department or agency of the Government.
(3)
The POW/MIA flag (as defined in section 1082 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 1998).
(4)
The flag or pennant of any of the armed forces (as defined in section 101 of title 10, United States Code).
(c)
Effective date
This Act shall apply with respect to fiscal year 2024 and each succeeding fiscal year.
