1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Hamas Supporters Have No Home Here Act
.
2.
Aliens charged with any crime related to their participation in pro-terrorism or antisemitism rallies or demonstrations
(a)
Participants in pro-terrorism or antisemitism rallies or demonstrations who are charged with a crime
Section 212(a)(3) of the Immigration and Nationality Act (
8 U.S.C. 1182(a)(3)
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(H)
Participants in antisemitism rallies
Any alien who is charged with any crime related to their participation in pro-terrorism or antisemitism rallies or demonstrations is inadmissible.
.
(b)
Ineligibility for relief
Section 241(b)(3) of the Immigration and Nationality Act (
8 U.S.C. 1231(b)(3)
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(D)
Ineligibility for relief
Any alien who is charged with any crime related to their participation in pro-terrorism or antisemitism rallies or demonstrations.
.
(c)
Conforming amendment
Section 237(a)(4)(B) of the Immigration and Nationality Act (
8 U.S.C. 1227(a)(4)(B)
) is amended by striking
subparagraph (B) or (F)
and inserting
subparagraph (B), (F), or (H)
.
