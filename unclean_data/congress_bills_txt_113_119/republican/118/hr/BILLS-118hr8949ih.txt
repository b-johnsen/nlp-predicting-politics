101.

(a)
Ratification
(1)
In general
Except as modified by this Act, and to the extent the Yavapai-Apache Nation Water Rights Settlement Agreement does not conflict with this Act, the Agreement is authorized, ratified, and confirmed.
(2)
Amendments
If an amendment to the Agreement, including an amendment to any exhibit attached to the Agreement requiring the signature or approval of the Secretary, is executed in accordance with this Act to make the Agreement consistent with this Act, the amendment is authorized, ratified, and confirmed, to the extent the amendment is consistent with this Act.
(b)
Execution
(1)
In general
To the extent the Agreement does not conflict with this Act, the Secretary shall execute the Agreement, including all exhibits to, or parts of, the Agreement requiring the signature of the Secretary.
