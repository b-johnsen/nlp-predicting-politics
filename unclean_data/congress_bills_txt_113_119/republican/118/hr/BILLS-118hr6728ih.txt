2.
Required designation of school athletic teams
(a)
Types of designations
An intercollegiate, intramural, or club athletic team or sport that is sponsored by a public school, or a public institution of higher education, or that is a competing team or sport sponsored by a non-public school or institution, and that receives Federal financial assistance, shall be expressly designated as, and composed of students of, only 1 of the following categories of team or club members based on biological sex at birth:
(1)
Males.
(2)
Females.
(3)
Coed members described in paragraphs (1) and (2).
(b)
Requirement
The membership of athletic teams or sports designated only for females shall only be open to biological females at birth.
3.
Penalty
All Federal financial assistance shall be terminated for a failure to comply with section 2.
