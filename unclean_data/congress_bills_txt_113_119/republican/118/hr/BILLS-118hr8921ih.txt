(c)
Limitation on applicability
The amendments made by this section shall not apply with respect to a fiscal year unless the amount made available under section 425 of the Social Security Act for the fiscal year meets or exceeds 103 percent of the amount made available in fiscal year 2024 to enable the Secretary of Health and Human Services to reserve 3 percent of the amount for payments to Indian tribes and tribal organizations under subpart 1 of part B of title IV of such Act without reducing the allotment of any State for the fiscal year under section 423 of such Act. Should the threshold for reserving 3 percent for payments to Indian tribes and tribal organizations not be met in a fiscal year, then amounts paid under section 428(a) of such Act shall be deemed to be a part of the allotment (as determined under section 423 of such Act) for the State in which such Indian tribal organization is located.
