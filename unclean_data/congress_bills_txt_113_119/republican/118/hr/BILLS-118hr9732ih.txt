1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Protection of Child Victims from Online Predators Act
.
2.
Amendment to title 18, United States Code
Section 2251(d) of title 18, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
(3)
For purposes of this subsection, a notice or advertisement can occur in a private, one-on-one communication.
.
