1.
SFC Shawn McCloskey Post Office
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 151 Highway 74 South in Peachtree City, Georgia, shall be known and designated as the
SFC Shawn McCloskey Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
SFC Shawn McCloskey Post Office
.
