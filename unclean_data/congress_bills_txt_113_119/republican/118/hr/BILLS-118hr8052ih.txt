1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Year-Round E15 Act of 2024
.
2.
Temporary applicability of E10 Reid vapor pressure limitations to E15
During the period that begins on May 1, 2024, and ends on September 15, 2024, in the States of Illinois, Iowa, Minnesota, Missouri, Nebraska, Ohio, South Dakota, and Wisconsin, the Reid vapor pressure limitations applicable to fuel blends containing gasoline and 15 percent denatured anhydrous ethanol under subsections (f) and (h) of section 211 of the Clean Air Act (
42 U.S.C. 7545
) (including any waiver thereunder) shall be the same as any such limitations applicable to fuel blends containing gasoline and 10 percent denatured anhydrous ethanol under such subsections.
