1.
Army SSG Ryan Christian Knauss Memorial Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 9355 113th Street in Seminole, Florida, shall be known and designated as the
Army SSG Ryan Christian Knauss Memorial Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Army SSG Ryan Christian Knauss Memorial Post Office Building
.
