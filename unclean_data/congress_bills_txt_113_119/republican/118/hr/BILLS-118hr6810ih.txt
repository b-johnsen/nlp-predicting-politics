1.
U.S. Army Air Corps Major Thomas B. McGuire Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 518 North Ridgewood Drive in Sebring, Florida, shall be known and designated as the
U.S. Army Air Corps Major Thomas B. McGuire Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
U.S. Army Air Corps Major Thomas B. McGuire Post Office Building
.
