Definitions
In this Act:
(1)
Administrator
The term
Administrator
means the Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency.

The term

has the meaning given to such term in section 211(o)(1) of the Clean Air Act (
42 U.S.C. 7545(o)(1)
).
(3)
Batch of renewable fuel
The term

has the meaning given to such term in Section 80.1426(d) of title 40, Code of Federal Regulations.

(5)
Eligible acre
The term

means an acre of agricultural land on which farming practices, meeting the criteria established under Section 4(b), are implemented that result in reduced greenhouse gas emissions associated with that acre of agricultural land.
