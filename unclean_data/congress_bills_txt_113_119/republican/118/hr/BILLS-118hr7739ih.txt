1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Remain in Canada Act of 2024
.
2.
Implementation of MPP at the northern border
The Secretary of Homeland Security shall, as of the date of enactment of this Act, begin implementation of the Migrant Protection Protocols in accordance with the memorandum of Secretary of Homeland Security Nielsen entitled
Policy Guidance for Implementation of the Migrant Protection Protocols
, dated January 25, 2019, except that such protocols shall be implemented with regard to the border between the United States and Canada.
