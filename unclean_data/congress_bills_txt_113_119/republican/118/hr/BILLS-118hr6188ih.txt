1.
Nancy Yount Childs Post Office Building
(a)
Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 420 Highway 17 North in Surfside Beach, South Carolina, shall be known and designated as the
Nancy Yount Childs Post Office Building
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the
Nancy Yount Childs Post Office Building
.
