Further adjustment for significant under projection of local area growth
Beginning with rates calculated for 2025 when computing rates for purposes of calculating benchmark amounts for an applicable plan, the Secretary shall adjust the growth percentage under subparagraph (A) to reflect the rate of growth in the MA local area, including making adjustments to growth estimates for previous years back no further than 2024. For purposes of this subparagraph, an applicable plan is an MA local plan in a service area where the area wage index under section 1886(d)(3)(E) increased by more than 20 percent in a prior year on average across the service area of the plan. Upon request from an applicable plan, the Secretary shall issue the adjustment for that year for the MA local plan as soon as practicable.
