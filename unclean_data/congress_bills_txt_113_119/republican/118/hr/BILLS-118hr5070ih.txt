Coordination of data
The Office of Pest Management Policy shall coordinate with the Environmental Protection Agency during the pesticide registration and reevaluation processes under the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (
7 U.S.C. 136 et seq.

(i)

(ii)
Commercially available agronomic use data.
(iii)
Industry agronomic use data.
(B)
Economic viability of alternatives
The Office of Pest Management Policy shall coordinate with the Environmental Protection Agency to provide information relating to the economic viability of alternatives for consideration by the Environmental Protection Agency in making registration and reevaluation decisions described in subparagraph (A) that may affect agricultural producers.
