(3)
Covered mine site
The term
covered mine site
means the land, water, and surrounding watersheds where extraction, beneficiation, or processing of hardrock ores or minerals occurred, but has been discontinued, including discontinued temporarily.
(4)
Indian country
The term
Indian country
has the meaning given the term in section 1151 of title 18, United States Code.
(5)
Navajo Nation abandoned uranium mine site
The term
Navajo Nation abandoned uranium mine site
means an abandoned uranium covered mine site on land of the Navajo Nation.
(6)
Office
The term
Office
means the Office of Mountains, Deserts, and Plains established by subsection (b)(1).
(7)
Regional Office
The term
Regional Office
means a Regional Office of the Environmental Protection Agency.
(b)
Establishment
(1)
In general
There is established within the Office of Land and Emergency Management of the Environmental Protection Agency the Office of Mountains, Deserts, and Plains.
(2)
Director
The Office shall be headed by a Director, to be appointed by the Administrator (or a designee).
