1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
UNRWA Elimination Act
.
2.
Statement of policy
It is the policy of the United States thatâ
(1)
the United Nations Relief and Works Agency for Palestine Refugees in the Near East (UNRWA) should be disbanded completely; and
(2)
all refugee services and responsibilities in the West Bank and Gaza strip should be transferred to the United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees.
3.
Authorizations to advance policy to disband UNRWA
(a)
In general
The President shall take such steps as may be necessary to advocate for and advance the policy described in section 2, including byâ
(1)
coordinating international efforts to achieve the outcomes described in such section; and
(2)
ensuring that any facility of the United Nations Relief and Works Agency for Palestine Refugees in the Near East in the United States is removed.
(b)
Funding
Any Federal funds made available to provide assistance to any foreign country shall include as a condition for receiving such funding that the country publicly supports the policy described in section 2.
(c)
Prohibition
No Federal funds are authorized to be appropriated or otherwise made available to the United Nations Relief and Works Agency for Palestine Refugees in the Near East, including for the salaries or expenses of any employee of such Agency, and no form of United States support is authorized to be made available for the programs, projects, or activities of such Agency.
