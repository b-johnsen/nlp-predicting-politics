1.
Saratoga National Battlefield Park
(a)
Redesignation
Saratoga National Historical Park shall hereafter be known and designated as
Saratoga National Battlefield Park
.
(b)
References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to Saratoga National Historical Park shall be deemed to be a reference to Saratoga National Battlefield Park.
