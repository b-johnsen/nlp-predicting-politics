1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Designating the Guam and Western Pacific USDA Rural Development Office Act
.
2.
Designation of the Guam and Western Pacific USDA Rural Development Office
Within 90 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Secretary of Agriculture and the Undersecretary for Rural Development shall designate the Guam and Western Pacific USDA Rural Development office to be the representative of USDA Rural Development in Guam, the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands, American Samoa, the Republic of the Marshall Islands, Palau, and the Federated States of Micronesia, and shall delegate to that office the same rights and responsibilities as are delegated to the USDA Rural Development office for any State.
