1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Streamline Emergency Care Act
.
2.
Grants for emergency room operations
(a)
Awards
The Secretary of Health and Human Services (in this section referred to as the
Secretary
), acting through the Administrator of the Health Resources and Services Administration, shall carry out a program of awarding grants to eligible entities for expanding, modernizing, or streamlining emergency room operations.
(b)
Eligible entities
In this section, the term
eligible entity
means a health care provider thatâ
(1)
is a nonprofit organization; and
(2)
at the time of applying for a grant under this section, is already operating an emergency room.
(c)
Amount
The amount of a grant under subsection (a) shall not exceed $500,000.
(d)
Use of funds
A grant received under this section may be used for any of the following:
(1)
The hiring of individuals to work in the emergency room.
(2)
The purchase of equipment to optimize and modernize emergency room capacity.
(3)
Triage and other training to increase the capacity of existing personnel to assist in the emergency room.
(e)
Report
Not later than the end of the third fiscal year beginning after the date of enactment of this Act, the Secretary shall submit to the Committee on Energy and Commerce of the House of Representatives and the Committee on Health, Education, Labor, and Pensions of the Senate a report on the effectiveness and impacts of the grant program under this section.
(f)
Authorization of appropriations
To carry out this section, there is authorized to be appropriated $20,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2024 through 2028.
