1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Veterans Education and Technical Skills Opportunity Act of 2024
or the
VETS Opportunity Act of 2024
.
2.
Treatment of certain independent study programs under educational assistance programs of Department of Veterans Affairs
(a)
In general
Section 3680A(4)(B)(iii)(II) of title 38, United States Code, is amended to read as follows:
(II)
an institution of higher education, as such term is defined in section 102 of the Higher Education Act of 1965 (
20 U.S.C. 1002
), that is qualified to participate in the student financial assistance programs authorized by title IV of that Act; and
.
(b)
Applicability
The amendment made by subsection (a) shall apply with respect to a quarter, semester, or term, as applicable, that begins on or after August 1, 2024.
