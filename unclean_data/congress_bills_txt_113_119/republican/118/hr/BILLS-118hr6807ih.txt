1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Knock Out Cancer Act
or the
KO Cancer Act
.
2.
Increasing NCI budget for cancer research
To conduct or support cancer research, there is hereby appropriated, for each of fiscal years 2024 through 2028, to the National Cancer Institute, out of amounts in the Treasury not otherwise appropriated, an amount that is equal to 25 percent of the total amount appropriated to the National Cancer Institute for fiscal year 2022, to remain available until expended. Amounts appropriated pursuant to the preceding sentence shall be in addition to amounts otherwise made available to the National Cancer Institute.
