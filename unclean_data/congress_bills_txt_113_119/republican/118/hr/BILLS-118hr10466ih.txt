1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Land And Social Security Optimization Act
or
LASSO Act
.
2.
Public land revenue for Social Security Trust Fund
(a)
In general
Notwithstanding any other law, each fiscal year, 10 percent of amounts collected by the Department of the Interior and the Department of Agriculture from the revenue generated by covered public lands during the preceding fiscal year shall be deposited into the Federal Old-Age and Survivors Trust Fund established under section 201(a) of the Social Security Act (
42 U.S.C. 401(a)
).
