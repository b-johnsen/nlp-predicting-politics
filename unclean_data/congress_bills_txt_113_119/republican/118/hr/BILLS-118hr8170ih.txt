(1)
Exemption from mileage limitation
The Appalachian Regional Commission may approve a waiver of the limitation in subsection (b) for a construction project under this section if the final alignment as determined through the National Environmental Policy Act documentation process of a route approved for assistance under this section exceeds such limitation.
(2)
Treatment of project mileage
Any mileage in excess of the limitation in subsection (b) that is approved under paragraph (1) shall be added to the mileage limitation in such subsection and shall be considered eligible development highway system miles under this section.
(3)
Application
To be eligible for a waiver under paragraph (1), a participating State shall submit to the Commission an application in such manner, and containing such information, as the Commission may require.
