RESOLUTION
Electing Representative Patrick T. McHenry Speaker pro tempore.
That the Honorable Patrick T. McHenry, a Representative from the State of North Carolina, be, and he is hereby, elected Speaker pro tempore for not longer than November 17, 2023, or until a new Speaker is elected, whichever is earlier.
2.
The Clerk of the House shall notify the President and the Senate of the election of the Honorable Patrick T. McHenry as Speaker pro tempore during the absence of the Speaker.
