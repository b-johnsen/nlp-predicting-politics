Whereas more than 540,000 people attended the 2022 Florida Strawberry Festival;
Whereas the Strawberry Ball, hosted by the Florida Strawberry Festival, raised $50,000 for Plant City芒聙聶s local food bank in 2022;

Whereas more than 10,000 acres of strawberries are planted annually in the local area, and its 2,800 farms make Hillsborough County a national leader in producing strawberries, citrus, tomatoes, cucumbers, eggplant, squash, okra, peppers, beans, dairy products, eggs, ornamental horticulture, tropical fish, beef cattle, swine, and other related products; and
