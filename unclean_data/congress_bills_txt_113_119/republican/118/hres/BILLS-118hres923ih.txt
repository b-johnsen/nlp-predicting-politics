1.
Designating hearing room in honor of Chairman Don Young
(a)
Designation
The main hearing room of the Committee on Transportation and Infrastructure (room 2167 of the Rayburn House Office Building) is designated as the
Chairman Don Young Hearing Room
.
(b)
Commemorative plaque
(1)
Placement of plaque over doorways of hearing room
The Architect of the Capitol shall place a plaque over each of the doorways leading from the public hallway outside the hearing room referred to in subsection (a) to the hearing room.
(2)
Contents of plaque
The Architect of the Capitol shall engrave on the plaque described in paragraph (1) the following (in such form and presentation as the Architect considers appropriate):
