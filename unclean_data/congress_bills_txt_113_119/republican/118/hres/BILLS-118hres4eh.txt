IV
118th CONGRESS
1st Session
H. RES. 4
In the House of Representatives, U. S.,
January 7 (legislative day, January 6), 2023
RESOLUTION
That the Clerk be instructed to inform the President of the United States that the House of Representatives has elected Kevin McCarthy, a Representative from the State of California as Speaker, and Cheryl L. Johnson, a citizen of the State of Louisiana as Clerk, of the House of Representatives of the One Hundred Eighteenth Congress.
Cheryl L. Johnson,
Clerk.
