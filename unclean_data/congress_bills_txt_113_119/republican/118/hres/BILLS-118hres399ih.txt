RESOLUTION
Expressing support for the designation of the week of May 7, 2023, through May 13, 2023, as
National Correctional Officers Week
.
Whereas the operation of correctional facilities represents a critical component of the criminal justice system of the United States;
Whereas correctional officers play an important role in protecting the right of the public to be safeguarded from criminal activity, as with law enforcement officers;
Whereas correctional officers are responsible for the care, custody, and dignity of the inmates charged to their care;
Whereas correctional officers work under demanding circumstances and face danger and violence in their daily work lives; and
Whereas the first week of May is recognized as
National Correctional Officers Week
: Now, therefore, be it
That the House of Representativesâ
(1)
supports the goals and ideals of
National Correctional Officers Week
; and
(2)
honors all correctional officers for their service to their communities and States, and to the Nation.
