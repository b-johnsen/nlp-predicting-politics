RESOLUTION
Condemning antisemitism on college campuses.
That the House of Representativesâ
(1)
calls on campus administrators to condemn all forms of antisemitism on college campuses;
(2)
calls on campus administrators to ensure Jewish faculty, students, and guests can exercise the same free speech rights as guaranteed to all other faculty, students, and guests can exercise the same free speech rights as are guaranteed to all other faculty, students, and guests without intimidation;
(3)
calls on campus administrators to ensure that no speaker is prevented from speaking on campus because of their Jewish heritage;
(4)
calls on campus administrators to ensure Jewish students have the same opportunities as all other students on campus;
(5)
calls for campus administrators to ensure the security and safety of their Jewish students; and
(6)
supports the right of Americans to freely exercise their religious and cultural beliefs while pursuing higher education.
