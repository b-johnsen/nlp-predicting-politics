Congratulating the University of Missouri Tigers for winning the 2023 Goodyear Cotton Bowl Classic.
Whereas, on December 29, 2023, the University of Missouri Tigers football team (referred to in this preamble as the
Tigers
) defeated the nationally ranked Ohio State University Buckeyes, 14 to 3, to win the 2023 Goodyear Cotton Bowl Classic (referred to in this preamble as the
