RESOLUTION
To express the sense of the Senate that the slogan
From the river to the sea, Palestine will be free
and its derivations are antisemitic and a call for genocide and the destruction of the Jewish state.
1.
Sense of the Senate regarding the meaning of the slogan
From the river to the sea, Palestine will be free
It is the sense of the Senate that the slogan
From the river to the sea, Palestine will be free
and its derivations are antisemitic and a call for genocide and the destruction of the Jewish state.
