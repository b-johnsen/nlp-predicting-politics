1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Waiving Arbitrary and Inconsistent Veteran Home Eligibility and Requirements Act
or the
WAIVER Act
.
2.

(a)
In general
The Secretary of Veterans Affairs shall waive under subsection (b) of section 70914 of the Build America, Buy America Act (subtitle A of title IX of division G of
Public Law 117â58

(b)
