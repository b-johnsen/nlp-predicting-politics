2.
Prohibition on loan forgiveness for certain individuals
(a)
Prohibition
(1)
In General
Notwithstanding any other provision of law, an individual described in paragraph (2) shall not be eligible to have any covered loan, or a portion of such loan, forgiven, cancelled, waived, or modified under the Higher Education Act of 1965 (
20 U.S.C. 1001 et seq.
) or under any other Executive Order or action of the Department of Education.
(2)
Affected individual
