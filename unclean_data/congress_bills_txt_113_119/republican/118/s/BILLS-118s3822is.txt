1.
Designation of Thomas H. Corey VA Medical Center in West Palm Beach, Florida
(a)
In general
The medical center of the Department of Veterans Affairs in West Palm Beach, Florida, shall after the date of the enactment of this Act be known and designated as the
Thomas H. Corey VA Medical Center
.
(b)
Reference
Any reference in any law, regulation, map, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the medical center referred to in subsection (a) shall be considered to be a reference to the Thomas H. Corey VA Medical Center.
