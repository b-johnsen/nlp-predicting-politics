1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
The Rural Vital Emergency Transportation Services (VETS) Act
.
2.
Reimbursement of ambulance cost for care for highly rural veterans
(a)
In general
Under regulations prescribed by the Secretary of Veterans Affairs, the Secretary shall pay, or reimburse a highly rural veteran for, the cost of transporting the veteran by ambulance, including air ambulance, to a provider of the Department of Veterans Affairs or a non-Department provider for care authorized under the laws administered by the Secretary, or for which subsequent payment or reimbursement is authorized under the laws administered by the Secretary, regardless of whether the veteran qualifies for payments or allowances for beneficiary travel under section 111 of title 38, United States Code.
(b)
Highly rural veteran defined
In this section, the term
highly rural veteran
means a veteran who is located in an area rated as a 10 or higher under the rural-urban commuting areas coding system of the Department of Agriculture.
