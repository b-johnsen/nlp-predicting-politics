1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Grand Village of the Natchez Indians and Jefferson College Affiliated Areas Establishment Act
.
2.
Definitions
In this Act:
(1)
Affiliated area
The term
affiliated area
means an affiliated area of the Park established by section 3(a)(1).
(2)
Management entity
The term
management entity
means the management entity for the affiliated areas designated by section 3(d).
(3)
Park
The term
Park
means the Natchez National Historical Park.
(4)
Secretary
The term
Secretary
means the Secretary of the Interior.
3.
Establishment of affiliated areas
(a)
Establishment
(1)
In general
On a determination by the Secretary that the sites described in paragraph (2) meet the criteria for designation as an affiliated area, the sites are established as affiliated areas of the Park to preserve, protect, and interpret for the benefit of present and future generations resources associated with the sites.
(2)
Description of sites

(b)
Map
(1)
In general
As soon as practicable after the date of establishment of the affiliated areas, the Secretary shall prepare a map depicting the boundaries of the affiliated areas.
(2)
Availability
The map prepared under paragraph (1) shall be on file and available for public inspection in the appropriate offices of the National Park Service.

The Mississippi Department of Archives and History shall be the management entity for the affiliated areas.
(e)
Cooperative agreements
The Secretary may provide technical assistance to, and enter into cooperative agreements with, the management entity for the purpose of providing financial assistance for the marketing, marking, interpretation, and preservation of the affiliated areas.
