1.
Enterprise units for continuous and fallow cropping systems
Section 508(e)(5) of the Federal Crop Insurance Act (
7 U.S.C. 1508(e)(5)
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(F)
Fallow cropping systems
Beginning with the 2024 crop year, the Corporation shall make available separate enterprise units for fallow and continuous acreage of crops in counties.
