1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Pathways to Paychecks Act
.
2.
Employment service offices
Section 9 of the Wagner-Peyser Act (
29 U.S.C. 49h
) is amended by adding at the end the following:
(d)
States may use State merit staff or other staff allowable under part 200 of title 2, Code of Federal Regulations (or a successor regulation), to carry out the duties of employment service offices under this Act.
.
