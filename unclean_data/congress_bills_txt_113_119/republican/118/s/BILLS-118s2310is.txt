1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Respect for Hispanic Americans Act
.
2.
Prohibition on use of
Latinx
(a)
Agency defined
In this section, the term
agency
has the meaning given the term in section 551 of title 5, United States Code.
(b)
Prohibition
The head of an agency and any employee of the agency may not use the term
Latinx
in any official communication or form of the agency.
