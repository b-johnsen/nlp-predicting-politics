1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
Mountain View Corridor Completion Act
.
2.
Definitions
In this Act:
(1)
Covered land
The term
covered land
means the approximately 200.18 acres of land depicted as
Potential Conveyance Modification
on the map entitled
Mountain View Corridor Completion Act
and dated August 17, 2023.
(2)
Secretary
The term
Secretary
means the Secretary of the Interior, acting through the Director of the Bureau of Land Management.
(3)
State
The term
State
means the State of Utah.
3.
Conveyance of Bureau of Land Management land to State of Utah
(a)
Conveyance required
Not later than 90 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Secretary shall convey to the State all rights, title, and interest of the United States in and to the covered land.
(b)
Requirements

valid existing rights; and
(2)

(c)
Application of Executive Order

Public Law 101â628

(d)
Map and legal description
(1)
In general
As soon as practicable after the date of enactment of this Act, the Secretary shall finalize a map and a legal description of the covered land to be conveyed under this section.
(2)
Controlling document
In the case of a discrepancy between the map and legal description finalized under paragraph (1), the map shall control.
(3)
Corrections
The Secretary and the State, by mutual agreement, may correct minor errors in the map or the legal description finalized under paragraph (1).
(4)
Map on file
The map and legal description finalized under paragraph (1) shall be kept on file and available for public inspection in each appropriate office of the Bureau of Land Management.
