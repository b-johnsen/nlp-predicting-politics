1.
Short title
This Act may be cited as the
No Money Bail Act
.
2.
Prohibition of money bail in Federal criminal cases
Notwithstanding any provision of Federal law, no justice, judge, or other judicial official in any court created by or under article III of the Constitution of the United States may use payment of money as a condition of pretrial release in any criminal case.

(1)
Alternatives to money bail
The Assistant Attorney General may make grants to State and tribal court systems and eligible entities for developing alternatives to the use of payment of money bail as a condition of pretrial release with respect to criminal cases.

National Pretrial Reporting Program
The Assistant Attorney General may make grants to eligible entities to implement a National Pretrial Reporting Program to collect data on the processing of defendants by courts of States and units of local government.
