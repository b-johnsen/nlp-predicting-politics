9.
Conveyance of certain land in Acadia National Park to the Town of Bar Harbor, Maine
(a)
In general
The Secretary shall convey to the Town of Bar Harbor (referred to in this section as the
Town
) all right, title, and interest of the United States in and to the .29-acre parcel of land in Acadia National Park identified as lot 110-055-000 on the tax map of the Town for section 110, dated April 1, 2015, to be used for a solid waste transfer facility.

Reversion
