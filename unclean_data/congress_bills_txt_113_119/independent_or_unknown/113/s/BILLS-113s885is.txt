1.
Thaddeus Stevens Post
Office
(a)
Designation
The
facility of the United States Postal Service located at 35 Park Street in
Danville, Vermont, shall be known and designated as the
Thaddeus Stevens
Post Office
.
(b)
References
Any
reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the
United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to
be a reference to the
Thaddeus Stevens Post Office
.
