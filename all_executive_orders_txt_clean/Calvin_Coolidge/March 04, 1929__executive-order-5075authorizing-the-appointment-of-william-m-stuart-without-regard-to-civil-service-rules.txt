Executive Order 5075—Authorizing the Appointment of William M. Stuart Without Regard to Civil Service Rules
March 04, 1929
Calvin Coolidge

Mrs. William M. Stuart (nee Sarah V. Kenner) may be appointed to an appropriate clerical position in the Federal Service without regard to the length of her separation therefrom.

Mrs. Stuart was in the competitive classified service from July 1, 1902, to December 31, 1918.

For charity.

CALVIN COOLIDGE

The White House,

March 4, 1929.
