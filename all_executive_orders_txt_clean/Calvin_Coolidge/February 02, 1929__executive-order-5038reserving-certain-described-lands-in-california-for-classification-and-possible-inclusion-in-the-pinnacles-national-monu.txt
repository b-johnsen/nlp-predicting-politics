Executive Order 5038—Reserving Certain Described Lands in California for Classification and Possible Inclusion in the Pinnacles National Monument
February 02, 1929
Calvin Coolidge

It is hereby ordered under authority of the act of Congress approved June 25, 1910 (36 Stat. 847) as amended by the act of August 24, 1912 (37 Stat. 497), that the following described area in the State of California be, and the same is hereby, temporarily withdrawn from settlement, location, sale or entry, subject to prior claims legally initiated and maintained and to the conditions and limitations of said acts, for classification and pending determination as to the advisability of adding same to the Pinnacles National Monument:

MT. DIABLO BASE AND MERIDIAN.

Sections 20 to 29, inclusive, W½, and W½ E½, Section 33, and Sections 35 and 36, T. 16 S. R. 7 E.

Section 1, E½, and E½ NW¼ of Section 2, E½ of Section 9, E½ of Section 11, Sections 12 to 15, inclusive, and Sections 22 to 24, inclusive, T. 17 S., R. 7 E.

This order shall continue in full force and effect unless and until revoked by the President or by act of Congress.

CALVIN COOLIDGE

The White House,

February 2, 1929.
