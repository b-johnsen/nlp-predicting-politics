Executive Order—Amendment of Civil Service Rules
March 06, 1894
Grover Cleveland

SPECIAL INDIAN RULE NO. I.

Exceptions from examination are hereby made as follows: One superintendent and the necessary teachers, not exceeding four in number, lot the organization and equipment of a normal school to be established at Albuquerque, N. Mex., this rule to expire by limitation six months after the date of its approval.

Approved:

GROVER CLEVELAND.
