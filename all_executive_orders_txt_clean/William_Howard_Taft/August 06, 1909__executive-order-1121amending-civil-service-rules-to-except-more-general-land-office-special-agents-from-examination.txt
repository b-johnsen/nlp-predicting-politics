Executive Order 1121—Amending Civil Service Rules to Except More General Land Office Special Agents from Examination
August 06, 1909
William Howard Taft

Schedule A of the Civil Service Rules is hereby amended by striking out the word three in paragraph 11 of Section VI relating to the Interior Department and inserting in lieu thereof the word six, so that the paragraph shall read:

11. Six special agents of the General Land Office to investigate fraudulent entries and other matters of a criminal nature.

WM. H. TAFT.

THE WHITE HOUSE,

August 6, 1909.
