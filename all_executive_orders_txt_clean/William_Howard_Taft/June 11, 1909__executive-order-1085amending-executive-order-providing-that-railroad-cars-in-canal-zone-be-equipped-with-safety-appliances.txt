Executive Order 1085—Amending Executive Order Providing that Railroad Cars in Canal Zone Be Equipped With Safety Appliances
June 11, 1909
William Howard Taft

Under authority vested in me by law, it is ordered:

Paragraph 6 of Executive Order, dated January 6, 1909, is hereby amended as follows:

6. This order shall take effect not later than six months from the date of the promulgation thereof, except for the use of old French dump cars in use on the Panama Canal, including also the couplers of locomotives necessary to operate them, until July 1, 1910, after which date it shall likewise apply to this equipment.

WM. H. TAFT.

THE WHITE HOUSE,

June 11, 1909.
