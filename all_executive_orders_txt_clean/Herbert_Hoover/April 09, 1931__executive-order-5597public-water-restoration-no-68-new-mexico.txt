Executive Order 5597—Public Water Restoration No. 68, New Mexico
April 09, 1931
Herbert Hoover

So much of Executive order of February 8, 1923, creating Public Water Reserve No. 86, as affects the land hereinafter listed is hereby revoked, and all such land not otherwise reserved or withdrawn is hereby restored to the public domain and shall become subject to disposition under the laws applicable thereto upon such date and after such notice as may be hereafter determined and announced.

New Mexico Meridian

T. 17 S., R. 19 W., sec. 10, NE. ¼, N. ½ SE. ¼;

sec. 11, SW. ¼ NW. ¼, NW. ¼ SW. ¼.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

April 9, 1931.
