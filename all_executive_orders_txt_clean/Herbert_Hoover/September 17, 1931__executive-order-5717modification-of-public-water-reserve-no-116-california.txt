Executive Order 5717—Modification of Public Water Reserve No. 116, California
September 17, 1931
Herbert Hoover

The Executive order of May 16, 1928, creating Public Water Reserve No. 116, is hereby modified to the extent of authorizing the Federal Power Commission to issue a license for Project No. 1123 on the following-described lands:

San Bernardino Meridian

T. 1 N., R. 1 W., sec. 14, SW. ¼ SW. ¼.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

September 17, 1931.
