Executive Order 5520—Authorizing the Appointment of Mrs. Lulie Hunt Turnipseed
December 23, 1930
Herbert Hoover

Mrs. Lulie Hunt Tumipseed may be appointed to a suitable position in the Treasury Department without regard to the requirements of the civil service rules.

Mrs. Tumipseed is the widow of a commissioned medical officer of the Public Health Service who died in the service on April 25, 1930, at Spokane, Wash.

This order is recommended by the Secretary of the Treasury.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

December 23, 1930.
