Executive Order 5381—Transfer of Operation of the Hotel Tivoli, Canal Zone
June 24, 1930
Herbert Hoover

The action of the Governor of the Panama Canal, in transferring the operation of the Hotel Tivoli from the Panama Canal to the Panama Railroad Co. under the terms and conditions as expressed in his memorandum dated July 1, 1929, is hereby approved.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

June 24, 1930.
