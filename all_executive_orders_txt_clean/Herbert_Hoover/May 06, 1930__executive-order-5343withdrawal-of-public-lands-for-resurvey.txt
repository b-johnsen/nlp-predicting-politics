Executive Order 5343—Withdrawal of Public Lands for Resurvey
May 06, 1930
Herbert Hoover

Nevada

Under authority contained in the act of June 25, 1910 (36 Stat. 817), as amended by the act of August 24, 1912 (37 Stat. 497), and subject to the conditions, provisions, and limitations of said acts, it is hereby ordered that the public lands in Tps. 20 and 21 N., R. 53 E., Mount Diablo meridian, Nevada, be, and the same are hereby, withdrawn from settlement, location, sale, entry, and all forms of appropriation, pending a resurvey of said townships under the act of March 3, 1909 (35 Stat. 845).

This order shall continue in full force and effect unless and until revoked by the President or by an act of Congress.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

May 6, 1930.
