Executive Order 5676—Modification of Reservoir-Site Reserve No. 1, Wenatchee Lake Reservoir, Washington
August 07, 1931
Herbert Hoover

The Executive order of January 17, 1911, creating Reservoir-Site Reserve No. 1, is hereby modified to the extent of authorizing the Federal Power Commission to issue a license for Project No. 1150 on the following-described lands:

Willamette Meridian

T. 26 N., R. 17 E., sec. 8, E. ½ SW. ¼ SW. ¼ SE. ¼;

sec. 17, NW. ¼ NE. ¼.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

August 7, 1931.
