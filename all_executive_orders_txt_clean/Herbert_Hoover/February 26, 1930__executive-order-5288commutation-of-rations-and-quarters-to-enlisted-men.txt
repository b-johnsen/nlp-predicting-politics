Executive Order 5288—Commutation of Rations and Quarters to Enlisted Men
February 26, 1930
Herbert Hoover

Executive Order No. 5256, approved January 3, 1930, is revoked.

Executive Order No. 4728, approved September 29, 1927, and Executive Order No. 5022, approved January 5, 1929, are restored to full force and effect.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

February 26, 1930.
