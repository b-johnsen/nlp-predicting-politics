Executive Order 6004—Exemption of Oliver M. Maxam from Compulsory Retirement for Age
January 19, 1933
Herbert Hoover

By virtue of the authority vested in me by section 204 of Part II of the act entitled "AN ACT Making appropriations for the Legislative Branch of the Government for the fiscal year ending June 30, 1933, and for other purposes," approved June 30, 1932, Oliver M. Maxain, Chief of the Division of Operations, United States Coast Guard, Treasury Department, who, during the current month, reaches the retirement age prescribed for automatic separation from the service, is hereby exempted from the provisions of that section.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

January 19, 1933.
