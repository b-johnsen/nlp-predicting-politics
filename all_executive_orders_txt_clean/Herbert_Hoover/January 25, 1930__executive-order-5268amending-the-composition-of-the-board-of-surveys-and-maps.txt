Executive Order 5268—Amending the Composition of the Board of Surveys and Maps
January 25, 1930
Herbert Hoover

The composition of the Board of Surveys and Maps created by Executive Order No. 3206, of December 30, 1919, is hereby amended by the addition of the Bureau of Lighthouses, Department of Commerce.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

January 25, 1930.
