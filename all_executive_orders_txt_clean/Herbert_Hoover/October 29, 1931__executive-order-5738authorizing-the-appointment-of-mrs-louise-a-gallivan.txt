Executive Order 5738—Authorizing the Appointment of Mrs. Louise A. Gallivan
October 29, 1931
Herbert Hoover

Mrs. Louise A. Gallivan may be appointed, as Grade CAF-1 Clerk in the Boston Regional Office of the Veterans' Administration without regard to the requirements of the civil-service rules.

This order is issued upon the recommendation of the Administrator of Veterans' Affairs.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

October 29, 1931.
