Executive Order 5235—Authorizing the Reinstatement of Mrs. Marguerite Barrett Without Regard to Civil Service Rules
December 06, 1929
Herbert Hoover

Mrs. Marguerite Barrett may be reinstated in the Treasury Department without regard to the length of time she had been separated from the service.

Mrs. Barrett had a classified status in the War Trade Board and the Public Health Service of the Treasury Department but rendered less than five years' service and has been separated for more than five years. She was left by her husband with three children to support.

This order is recommended by the Secretary of the Treasury.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

December 6, 1929.
