Executive Order 5076—Designating Helen V. McLeod to Sign the Pressident's Name to Land Patents During the Absence of the Clerk
March 07, 1929
Herbert Hoover

Mrs. Helen V. McLeod, of the General Land Office, Department of the Interior, is hereby designated to sign my name to patents during the absence of the clerk regularly designated for that purpose.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

March 7, 1929.
