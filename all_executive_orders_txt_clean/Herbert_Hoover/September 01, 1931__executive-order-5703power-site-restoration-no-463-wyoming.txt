Executive Order 5703—Power-Site Restoration No. 463, Wyoming
September 01, 1931
Herbert Hoover

So much of the orders of April 12, 1910, creating Temporary Power-Site Withdrawal No. 137, and July 2, 1910, creating Power-Site Reserve No. 137, as affects the lands hereinafter described is hereby revoked:

Sixth Principal Meridian

T. 39 N., R. 94 W., sec. 7, lot 3.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

September 1, 1931.
