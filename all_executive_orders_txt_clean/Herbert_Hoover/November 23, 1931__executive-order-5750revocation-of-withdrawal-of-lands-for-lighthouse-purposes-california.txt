Executive Order 5750—Revocation of Withdrawal of Lands for Lighthouse Purposes, California
November 23, 1931
Herbert Hoover

It is hereby ordered that so much of Executive order of January 26, 1867, as withdrew a tract of land at Point Buchon, Calif., for lighthouse purposes, and Executive order of November 27, 1905, which withdrew lots 8 and 9, fractional sec. 36, township 29 south, range 10 east of the Mount Diablo meridian, California, for lighthouse purposes be, and are hereby, revoked.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

November 23, 1931.
