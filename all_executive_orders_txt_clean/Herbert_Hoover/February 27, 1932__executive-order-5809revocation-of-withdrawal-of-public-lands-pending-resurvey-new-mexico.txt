Executive Order 5809—Revocation of Withdrawal of Public Lands Pending Resurvey, New Mexico
February 27, 1932
Herbert Hoover

It is hereby ordered that Executive Order No. 4843, dated March 23, 1928, which withdrew, with other lands, the public lands in T. 20 N., R. 1 E., New Mexico principal meridian, pending resurvey, be, and the same is hereby, revoked as to stud township.

All the lands in this township are included in the Santa Fe National Forest.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

February 27, 1932.
