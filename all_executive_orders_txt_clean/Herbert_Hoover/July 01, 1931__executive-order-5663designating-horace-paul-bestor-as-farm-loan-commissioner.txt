Executive Order 5663—Designating Horace Paul Bestor as Farm Loan Commissioner
July 01, 1931
Herbert Hoover

Under the provisions of section 3 of the Federal Farm Loan Act, approved July 17, 1916 (39 Stat. 360), Horace Paul Bestor, who has been duly appointed a member of the Federal Farm Loan Board, is hereby designated Farm Loan Commissioner of said board until otherwise directed.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

July 1, 1931.
