Executive Order 5795—Power-Site Restoration No. 468, Wyoming
February 09, 1932
Herbert Hoover

So much of the order of July 13, 1911, creating Power-Site Reserve No. 190, as affects the lands hereinafter described, is hereby revoked:

Sixth Principal Meridian

T. 38 N., R. 110 W., sec. 36, SW. ¼.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

February 9, 1932.
