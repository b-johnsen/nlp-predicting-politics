Executive Order 5267—Fixing the Salary of the War Claims Arbiter
January 25, 1930
Herbert Hoover

Pursuant to and under the authority of the provisions of section 3(a) of the Settlement of War Claims Act of 1928, the salary of Judge James Waldron Remick as War Claims Arbiter is hereby fixed at $10,000 per annum, effective as of the date on which he takes office.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

January 25, 1930.
