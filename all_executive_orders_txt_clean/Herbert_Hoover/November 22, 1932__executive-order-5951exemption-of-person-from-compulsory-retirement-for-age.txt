Executive Order 5951—Exemption of Person from Compulsory Retirement for Age
November 22, 1932
Herbert Hoover

By virtue of the authority vested in me by section 204 of Part II of the act entitled "AN ACT making appropriations for the Legislative Branch of the Government for the fiscal year ending June 30, 1933, and for other purposes," approved June 30, 1932, George R. Wales, who today reaches the retirement age prescribed for automatic separation from the service, is hereby exempted from the provisions of that section.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

November 22, 1932.
