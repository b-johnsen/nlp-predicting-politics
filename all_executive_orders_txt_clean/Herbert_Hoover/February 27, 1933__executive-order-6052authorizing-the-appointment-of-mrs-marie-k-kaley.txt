Executive Order 6052—Authorizing the Appointment of Mrs. Marie K. Kaley
February 27, 1933
Herbert Hoover

Mrs. Marie K. Kaley may be appointed an inspectress of customs in the Office of the Collector of Customs at New York, N. Y., without compliance with the requirements of the civil-service rules.

This order is recommended by the Secretary of the Treasury, who states that Mrs. Kaley is the widow of Deputy Collector of Customs Francis Kaley, who gave 44 years of exceptionally faithful and intelligent service to the Government and died on November 30, 1932, leaving his widow without adequate means of support.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

February 27, 1933.
