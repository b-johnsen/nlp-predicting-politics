Executive Order 5745—Power-Site Restoration No. 466, Wyoming
November 07, 1931
Herbert Hoover

So much of the orders of May 26, 1909, creating Temporary Power-Site Withdrawal No. 5, July 21, 1909, creating Temporary Power-Site Withdrawal No. 30, and July 2, 1910, creating Power-Site Reserves Nos. 5 and 30, as affects the lands hereinafter described, is hereby revoked:

Sixth Principal Meridian

T. 35 N., R. Ill W., sec. 8, S. ½ NE. ¼, NE. ¼ SE. ¼.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

November 7, 1931.
