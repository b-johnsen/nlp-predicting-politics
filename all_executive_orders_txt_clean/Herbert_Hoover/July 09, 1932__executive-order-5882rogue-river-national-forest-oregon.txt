Executive Order 5882—Rogue River National Forest, Oregon
July 09, 1932
Herbert Hoover

In order to avoid the confusion growing out of a similarity of names of the Crater Lake National Park and the Crater National Forest, in the State of Oregon, it is hereby ordered that the name of the "Crater National Forest" be changed to "Rogue River National Forest."

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

July 9, 1932.
