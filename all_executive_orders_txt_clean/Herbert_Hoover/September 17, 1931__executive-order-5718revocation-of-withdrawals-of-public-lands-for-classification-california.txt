Executive Order 5718—Revocation of Withdrawals of Public Lands for Classification, California
September 17, 1931
Herbert Hoover

The withdrawal made by Executive Order No. 4331 of October 28, 1925, of lot 6 sec. 6, lots 1 and 2 sec. 7, T. 7 N., R. 1 E., Humboldt meridian, California, is hereby revoked, and the withdrawal made by Executive Order No. 5237 of December 10, 1929, is also hereby revoked in so far as it affects the above-described lands.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

September 17, 1931.
