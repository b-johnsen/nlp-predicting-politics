Executive Order 5294—Withdrawal of Public Lands for Resurvey Partially Revoked
March 05, 1930
Herbert Hoover

Nevada

Executive Order No. 5208, dated October 12, 1929, withdrawing the public lands in T. 19 S., R. 56 E., Mt. Diablo meridian, Nevada, pending a resurvey in the township, is hereby revoked as to sections 25, 26, 35, and 36, said township.

All of said lands are within the Dixie National Forest, having been reserved for forest purposes by departmental order of January 4, 1906, proclamation of November 5, 1906, and Executive orders of April 6, 1916, and May 10, 1916, which remain in full force and effect as to said lands.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

March 5, 1930.
