Executive Order 5252—Restoring Certain Reserved Lands Withdrawn for Petroleum Reserve No. 61, Colorado No. 2, to Public Domain
December 31, 1929
Herbert Hoover

So much of the order of withdrawal of October 25, 1918, creating Petroleum Reserve No. 61, Colorado No. 2, as affects the lands hereinafter described is hereby revoked, and all such lands not otherwise reserved or withdrawn are hereby restored to the public domain and to disposition under the laws applicable thereto.
