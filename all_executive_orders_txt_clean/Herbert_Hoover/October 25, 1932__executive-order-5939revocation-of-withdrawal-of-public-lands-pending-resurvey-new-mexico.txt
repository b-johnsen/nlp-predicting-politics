Executive Order 5939—Revocation of Withdrawal of Public Lands Pending Resurvey, New Mexico
October 25, 1932
Herbert Hoover

It is hereby ordered that Executive Order No. 5452, dated September 25, 1930, which withdrew the public lands in T. 26 N., R. 11 W., New Mexico principal meridian, New Mexico, pending a resurvey, be, and the same is hereby, revoked.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

October 25, 1932.
