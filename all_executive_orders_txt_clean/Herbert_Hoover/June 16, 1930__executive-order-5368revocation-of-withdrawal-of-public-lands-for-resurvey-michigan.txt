Executive Order 5368—Revocation of Withdrawal of Public Lands for Resurvey, Michigan
June 16, 1930
Herbert Hoover

It is hereby ordered that Executive Order No. 4921 dated June 29, 1928, withdrawing among other lands the public lands in T. 25 N., R. 7 E., Michigan meridian, Michigan, pending a resurvey, be, and the same is hereby, revoked as to said township.

All of said lands are within the limits of Huron National Forest, having been reserved for forest purposes by proclamations of February 11, 1909, and July 30, 1928, which remain in full force and effect as to said lands.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

June 16, 1930.
