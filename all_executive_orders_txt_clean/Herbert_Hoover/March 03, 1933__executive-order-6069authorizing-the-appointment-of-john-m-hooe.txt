Executive Order 6069—Authorizing the Appointment of John M. Hooe
March 03, 1933
Herbert Hoover

Mr. John M. Hooe may be permanently appointed as marine gasoline engineer (qualified as airplane pilot) on the force of the Collector of Customs at Buffalo, N. Y., without compliance with the requirements of the civil-service rules.

This order is recommended by the Secretary of the Treasury, who states that Mr. Hooe has been serving under temporary appointment in an essential position since March 19, 1932, in the absence of qualified civil-service eligibles, and that his permanent appointment will be in the interest of the service.

The Civil Service Commission concurs in recommending the Order.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

March 3, 1933.
