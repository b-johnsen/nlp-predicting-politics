Executive Order 5844—Revocation of Withdrawal of Public Lands Pending Resurvey, Michigan
April 28, 1932
Herbert Hoover

It is hereby ordered that Executive Order No. 4921, dated June 29, 1928, which withdrew, with other lands, the public lands in T. 25 N., R. 4 E., Michigan meridian, Michigan, pending resurvey, be, and the same is hereby, revoked as to said township.

All the lands in this township are within the limits of the Huron National Forest.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

April 28, 1932.
