Executive Order 5628—Authorizing the Appointment of Mrs. Amy Cowing
May 20, 1931
Herbert Hoover

Mrs. Amy Cowing may be appointed as a clerk-typist in the Department of Agriculture without reference to the provisions of the civil-service rules.

This order is issued upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

May 20, 1931.
