Executive Order 5641—Restoration of Certain Lands from Salmon Hatchery Reservation, Alaska
June 08, 1931
Herbert Hoover

It is hereby ordered that Executive order of February 1, 1906, reserving certain lands on Yes Lake in the Cleveland Peninsula in southeastern Alaska as a site for a salmon hatchery be, and the same is hereby, revoked as to the tract of land now identified as United States Survey No. 1981, comprising 6.33 acres.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

June 8, 1931.
