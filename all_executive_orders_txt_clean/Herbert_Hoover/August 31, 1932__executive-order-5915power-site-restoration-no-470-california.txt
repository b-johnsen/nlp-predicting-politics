Executive Order 5915—Power-Site Restoration No. 470, California
August 31, 1932
Herbert Hoover

So much of Executive order of January 14, 1915, creating Power-Site Reserve No. 471, as affects the lands hereinafter described is hereby revoked:

Mount Diablo Meridian

T. 15 S., R. 28 E., sec. 21, N. ½ SE. ¼, SE. ¼ SE. ¼;

sec. 28, NE. ¼ E. ½ NW. ¼.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

August 31, 1932.
