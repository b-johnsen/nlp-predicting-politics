Executive Order 5376—Salaries of American Members, International Joint Commission
June 23, 1930
Herbert Hoover

It is hereby directed that the salary of each of the members of the American Section of the International Joint Commission for the adjustment of questions regarding the use of boundary waters between the United States and Canada and other purposes, under article 8 of the treaty signed at Washington, January 11, 1909, is fixed at the rate of $10,000 per annum beginning July 1, 1930.

Executive Order No. 1440 dated December 4, 1911, is hereby revoked.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

June 23, 1930.
