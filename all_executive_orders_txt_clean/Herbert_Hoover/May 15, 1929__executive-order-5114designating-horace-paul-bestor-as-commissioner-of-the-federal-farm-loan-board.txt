Executive Order 5114—Designating Horace Paul Bestor as Commissioner of the Federal Farm Loan Board
May 15, 1929
Herbert Hoover

Under the provisions of Section 3 of the Federal Farm Loan Act, approved July 17, 1916, Horace Paul Bestor, who has been duly appointed a member of the Federal Farm Loan Board, is hereby designated Farm Loan Commissioner of said Board until otherwise directed.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

May 15, 1929.
