Executive Order 5078—Establishing Lincoln, Nebraska as a Customs Port of Entry
March 12, 1929
Herbert Hoover

By virtue of the authority vested in me by the Act of Congress approved August 1, 1914, making appropriations for the sundry civil expenses of the Government for the fiscal year ended June 30, 1915, Lincoln, Nebraska, is hereby created a port of entry in Customs Collection District No. 46 (Omaha) with headquarters at Omaha, Nebraska, effective thirty days from the date of this order.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

March 12, 1929.
