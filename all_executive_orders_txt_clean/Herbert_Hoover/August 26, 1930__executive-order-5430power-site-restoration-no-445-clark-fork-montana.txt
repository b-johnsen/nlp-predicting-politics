Executive Order 5430—Power-Site Restoration No. 445, Clark Fork, Montana
August 26, 1930
Herbert Hoover

MONTANA MERIDIAN

T. 11 N., R. 15 W., sec. 14, S ½ SW ¼;

sec. 22, N ½ NE ¼.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

August 26, 1930.
