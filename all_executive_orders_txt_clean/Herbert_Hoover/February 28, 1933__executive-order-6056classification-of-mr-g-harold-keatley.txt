Executive Order 6056—Classification of Mr. G. Harold Keatley
February 28, 1933
Herbert Hoover

G. Harold Keatley, of Pennsylvania, who, since April 4, 1929, has been serving in a position in the Department of State excepted from civil-service requirements, may be appointed in the classified service without compliance with civil-service rules.

This order is issued upon the recommendation of the Secretary of State.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

February 28, 1933.
