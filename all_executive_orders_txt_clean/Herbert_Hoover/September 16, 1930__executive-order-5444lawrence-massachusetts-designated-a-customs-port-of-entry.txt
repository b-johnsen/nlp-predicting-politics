Executive Order 5444—Lawrence, Massachusetts, Designated a Customs Port of Entry
September 16, 1930
Herbert Hoover

By virtue of the authority vested in me by the act of Congress approved August 1, 1914 (38 Stat. 623), making appropriations for the sundry civil expenses of the Government for the fiscal year ended June 30, 1915, Lawrence, Mass., is hereby designated a customs port of entry in Customs Collection District No. 4 (Massachusetts) with headquarters at Boston, Mass., and the townships of Lawrence, Methuen, and Andover are included within the limits of said port, effective 30 days from the date of this order.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

September 16, 1930.
