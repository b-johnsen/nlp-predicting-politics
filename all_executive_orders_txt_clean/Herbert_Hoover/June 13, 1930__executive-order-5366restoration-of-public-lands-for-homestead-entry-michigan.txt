Executive Order 5366—Restoration of Public Lands for Homestead Entry, Michigan
June 13, 1930
Herbert Hoover

Under authority of the act of Congress approved June 25, 1910 (36 Stat. 847), as amended by the act of Congress approved August 24, 1912 (37 Stat. 497), it is hereby ordered that Executive Order No. 4430, dated April 23, 1926, withdrawing, with other lands, all lands within three miles of the shore of Lakes Michigan, Huron, and Superior, is hereby revoked as to SW. ¼ SW. ¼ sec. 20, W. ½ NW. ¼ sec. 29, and NE. ¼ NE. ¼ sec. 30, T. 27 N., R. 15 W., Michigan meridian, Michigan (160 acres), to make possible the allowance of the application to make homestead entry filed by Joseph S. Cooper.

HERBERT HOOVER

The White House,

June 13, 1930.
