Executive Order 7829—Revocation of Executive Order No. 5428 of August 20, 1930, Withdrawing Land for Classification and in Aid of Legislation
August 20, 1930
Franklin D. Roosevelt

MONTANA

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of June 25, 1910, ch. 421, 36 Stat. 847, Executive Order No. 5428 of August 20, 1930, temporarily withdrawing and reserving certain land for classification and in aid of legislation, is hereby revoked.

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

The White House,

March 7, 1938.
