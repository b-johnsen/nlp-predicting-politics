Executive Order 8064—Designating Fairbanks, Alaska, as a Customs Port of Entry, and Discontinuing Seward, Alaska, as a Customs Port of Entry
March 09, 1939
Franklin D. Roosevelt

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of August 1, 1914, 38 Stat. 609, 623 (U.S.C., title 19, sec. 2), it is ordered as follows:

1. Fairbanks, Alaska, is hereby designated as a customs port of entry in Customs Collection District No. 31 (Alaska), with headquarters at Juneau, Alaska.

2. Seward, Alaska, is hereby discontinued as a customs port of entry in Customs Collection District No. 31 (Alaska).

3. This order shall become effective thirty days from the date hereof.

FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT

The White House,

March 9, 1939
