Executive Order 7958—Exemption of Samuel J. Gompers From Compulsory Retirement for Age
August 19, 1938
Franklin D. Roosevelt

Whereas

, in my judgment, the public interest requires that Samuel J. Gompers, Chief Clerk, Department of Labor, who, during the month of September, 1938, will reach the retirement age prescribed for automatic separation from the service, applicable to him, be exempted from compulsory retirement for age for a period of one year:

Now, Therefore

, by virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by section 204 of the Act of June 30, 1932, 47 Stat. 382, 404 (U.S.C., title 5, sec. 715a), I hereby exempt the said Samuel J. Gompers from compulsory retirement for age for a period of one year ending September 30, 1939.

FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT

The White House,

August 19, 1938.
