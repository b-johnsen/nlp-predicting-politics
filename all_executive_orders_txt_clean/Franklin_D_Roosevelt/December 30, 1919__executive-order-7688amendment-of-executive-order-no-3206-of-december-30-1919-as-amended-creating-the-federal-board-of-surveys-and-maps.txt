Executive Order 7688—Amendment of Executive Order No. 3206 of December 30, 1919, as Amended, Creating the Federal Board of Surveys and Maps
December 30, 1919
Franklin D. Roosevelt

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me as President of the United States, Executive Order No. 3206 of December 30, 1919, as amended, creating the Federal Board of Surveys and Maps, is hereby further amended by adding the Division of Maps and Charts, The National Archives, to the list of organizations entitled to membership on the said Board.

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

The White House,

August 10, 1937.
