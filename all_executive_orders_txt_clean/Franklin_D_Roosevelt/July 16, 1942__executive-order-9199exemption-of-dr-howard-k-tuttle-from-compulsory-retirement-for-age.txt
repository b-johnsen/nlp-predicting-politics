Executive Order 9199—Exemption of Dr. Howard K. Tuttle From Compulsory Retirement for Age
July 16, 1942
Franklin D. Roosevelt

Whereas

, in my judgment, the public interest requires that Dr. Howard K. Tuttle, assistant chief of Surgical Clinic, Health Department, The Panama Canal, who, during the month of August, 1942, will reach the retirement age prescribed for automatic separation from the service, applicable to him, be exempted from compulsory retirement for age for a period of one year:

Now, Therefore

, by virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by section 204 of the act of June 30, 1932, 47 Stat. 382, 404 (U.S.C., title 5, sec. 715a), I hereby exempt the said Dr. Howard K. Tuttle from compulsory retirement for age for a period of one year ending August 31, 1943.

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

The White House,

July 16, 1942.
