Executive Order 7992—Revocation of Executive Order No. 3674 of May 17, 1922, Withdrawing Lands For the Use and Occupancy of the DuBois Rifle Club For Rifle Practice
May 17, 1922
Franklin D. Roosevelt

WYOMING

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of June 25, 1910, ch. 421, 36 Stat. 847, as amended by the act of August 24, 1912, ch. 369, 37 Stat. 497, Executive Order No. 3674 of May 17, 1922, withdrawing lands for the use and occupancy of the DuBois Rifle Club, DuBois, Wyoming, for rifle practice, is hereby revoked.

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

The White House,

October 25, 1938.
