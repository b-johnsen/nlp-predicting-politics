Executive Order 6239—Organization of Executive Agencies
August 02, 1933
Franklin D. Roosevelt

Under the provisions of section 22 of Executive Order No. 6166, dated June 10, 1933, issued pursuant to section 16 of the act of March 3, 1933 (Public, No. 428, 47 Stat. 1517), the effective date for the abolition of the Federal Coordinating Service, as directed in section 17 of said Executive order, is deferred to October 10, 1933:

Provided

, That such abolition may be made effective, in whole or in part, between August 10, 1933, and October 10, 1933, by order of the President.

FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT

The White House,

August 2, 1933.
