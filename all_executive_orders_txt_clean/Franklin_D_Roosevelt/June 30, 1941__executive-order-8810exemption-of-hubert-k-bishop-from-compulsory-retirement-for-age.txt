Executive Order 8810—Exemption of Hubert K. Bishop From Compulsory Retirement for Age
June 30, 1941
Franklin D. Roosevelt

Whereas

, in my judgment, the public interest requires that Hubert K. Bishop, Chief, Division of Construction, Public Roads Administration, Federal Works Agency, who was exempted from compulsory retirement for age for a period of one year by Executive Order No. 8413 of May 18, 1940, be further exempted from such compulsory retirement for a period of one year:

Now, Therefore

, by virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by section 204 of the act of June 30, 1932, 47 Stat. 382, 404 (U.S.C., title 5, sec. 715a), I hereby further exempt the said Hubert K. Bishop from compulsory retirement for age fot a period of one year ending June 30, 1942.

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

The White House,

June 30, 1941.
