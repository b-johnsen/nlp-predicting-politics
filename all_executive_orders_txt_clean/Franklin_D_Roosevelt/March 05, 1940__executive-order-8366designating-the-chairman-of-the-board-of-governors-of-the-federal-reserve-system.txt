Executive Order 8366—Designating the Chairman of the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System
March 05, 1940
Franklin D. Roosevelt

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by section 10 of the Federal Reserve Act (38 Stat. 260), as amended by section 203(a) of the act of August 23, 1935 (49 Stat. 704), I hereby designate Marriner S. Eccles as Chairman of the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System, to serve as such for a term of four years, effective February 1, 1940.

FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT

The White House,

March 5, 1940.
