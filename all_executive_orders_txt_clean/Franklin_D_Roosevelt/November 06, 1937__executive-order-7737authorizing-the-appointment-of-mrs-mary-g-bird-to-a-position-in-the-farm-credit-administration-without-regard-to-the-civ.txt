Executive Order 7737—Authorizing the Appointment of Mrs. Mary G. Bird to a Position in the Farm Credit Administration Without Regard to the Civil Service Rules
November 06, 1937
Franklin D. Roosevelt

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the provisions of paragraph Eighth of subdivision SECOND of section 2 of the Civil Service Act (22 Stat. 403), it is hereby ordered that Mrs. Mary G. Bird may be appointed to a classified position in the Farm Credit Administration without compliance with the requirements of the Civil Service Rules.

Mrs. Bird would have become eligible to acquire a civil service status under the provisions of Executive Order No. 6758 of June 29, 1984, had she not been appointed to an excepted position in the Farm Credit Administration on June 1, 1934.

This order is recommended by the Governor of the Farm Credit Administration.

FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT

The White House,

November 6, 1937.
