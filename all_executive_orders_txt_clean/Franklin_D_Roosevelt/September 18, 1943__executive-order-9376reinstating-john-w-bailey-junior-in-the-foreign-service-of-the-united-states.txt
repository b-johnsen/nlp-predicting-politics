Executive Order 9376—Reinstating John W. Bailey, Junior, in the Foreign Service of the United States
September 18, 1943
Franklin D. Roosevelt

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by section 12 of the act of February 23, 1931, 46 Stat. 1207, 1208, it is ordered that John W. Bailey, Junior, who resigned his position as an officer in the Foreign Service of the United States on March 31, 1935, to accept appointment to a position in the Department of State, be, and he is hereby, reinstated in the Foreign Service of the United States in Class III, his reinstatement to be effective upon the date his nomination to a position in such class is confirmed by the Senate.

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

The White House,

September 18, 1943.
