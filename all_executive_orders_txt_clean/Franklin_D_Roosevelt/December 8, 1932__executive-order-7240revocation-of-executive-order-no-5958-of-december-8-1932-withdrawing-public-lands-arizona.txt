Executive Order 7240—Revocation of Executive Order No. 5958, of December 8, 1932, Withdrawing Public Lands, Arizona
December 8, 1932
Franklin D. Roosevelt

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of June 25, 1910, ch. 421, 36 Stat. 847, as amended by the act of August 24, 1912, ch. 369, 37 Stat. 497, Executive Order No. 5958, of December 8, 1932, withdrawing public lands in T. 17 S., R. 31 E. of the Gila and Salt River meridian, Arizona, pending a resurvey, is hereby revoked.

This order shall become effective upon the date of the official filing of the plat of resurvey of said township.

FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT

The White House,

December 4, 1935.
