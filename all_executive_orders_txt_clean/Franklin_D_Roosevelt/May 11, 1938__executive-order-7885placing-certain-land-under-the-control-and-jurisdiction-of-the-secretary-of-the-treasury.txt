Executive Order 7885—Placing Certain Land Under the Control and Jurisdiction of the Secretary of the Treasury
May 11, 1938
Franklin D. Roosevelt

ALASKA

By virtue of the authority vested in me as President of the United States it is ordered that Block 27, in the Townsite of Anchorage, Alaska, be, and it is hereby, placed under the control and jurisdiction of the Secretary of the Treasury for use as a site for Federal buildings.

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

The White House,

May 11, 1938.
