Executive Order 9207—Extending the Limits of the Customs Port of Entry of Salem, Massachusetts, in Customs Collection District Number 4 (Massachusetts)
July 29, 1942
Franklin D. Roosevelt

By virtue of the authority vested in me by section 1 of the act of August 1. 1914, 38 Stat 609, 623 (U.S.C., title 19. sec. 2), it is ordered that the limits of the customs port of entry of Salem, Massachusetts, in Customs Collection District Number 4 (Massachusetts), be, and they are hereby, extended to include the territory embracing the municipality of Peabody, Massachusetts.

This order shall become effective on the thirtieth day following the date hereof.

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

The White House,

July 29, 1942.
