Executive Order 9394—Amending Subdivision VII of Schedule A of the Civil Service Rules
November 04, 1943
Franklin D. Roosevelt

By virtue of the authority vested in me by section 2 of the Civil Service Act (22 Stat. 403), Subdivision VII of Schedule A of the Civil Service Rules is hereby amended by adding thereto the following paragraph:

"10. Fourth-class postmasters in the Hawaiian Islands."

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

The White House,

November 4, 1943.
