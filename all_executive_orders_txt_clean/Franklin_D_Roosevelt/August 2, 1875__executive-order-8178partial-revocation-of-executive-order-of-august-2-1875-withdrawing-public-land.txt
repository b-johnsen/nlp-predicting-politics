Executive Order 8178—Partial Revocation of Executive Order of August 2, 1875, Withdrawing Public Land
August 2, 1875
Franklin D. Roosevelt

FLORIDA

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me as President of the United States, the Executive order of August 2, 1875, reserving lands for lifesaving purposes, is hereby revoked as to the following-described land:

FLORIDA

TALLAHASSEE MERIDIAN

T. 32 S., R. 40 E., sec. 20, E½ of lot 1, containing ten acres.

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

The White House,

June 21, 1939.
