Executive Order 7551—Amendment of Paragraph 1, Subdivision VI, Schedule A, Civil Service Rules
February 11, 1937
Franklin D. Roosevelt

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by paragraph Eight, subdivision SECOND, section 2 of the Civil Service Act (22 Stat. 403, 404), it is ordered that paragraph 1, Subdivision VI, Schedule A of the Civil Service Rules be, and it is hereby, amended by revoking subdivisions (c), (d), and (e) thereof, so that the paragraph, as amended, shall read as follows:

"1. (a) The Director of the Bureau of Prisons and not more than three Assistant Directors.

"(b) Members of the Board of Parole."

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

The White House,

February 11, 1937.
