Executive Order 7950—Exemption of William H. Egberts From Compulsory Retirement for Age
August 09, 1938
Franklin D. Roosevelt

Whereas

, in my judgment, the public interest requires that William H. Egberts, chief preparator in anthropology in the United States National Museum, Smithsonian Institution, who, during the month of September, 1938, will reach the retirement age prescribed for automatic separation from the service, applicable to him, be exempted from compulsory retirement for age for a period of one year:

Now, Therefore

, by virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by section 204 of the act of June 30, 1932, 47 Stat. 382, 404 (U.S.C., title 5, sec. 715a), I hereby exempt the said William H. Egberts from compulsory retirement for age for a period of one year ending September 30, 1939.

FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT

The White House,

August 9, 1938.
