Executive Order 8093—Revocation of Executive Order No. 6119 of May 2, 1933, Withdrawing Public Lands
May 2, 1933
Franklin D. Roosevelt

CALIFORNIA

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of June 25, 1910, c. 421, 36 Stat. 847, as amended by the act of August 24, 1912, c. 369, 37 Stat. 497, Executive Order No. 6119 of May 2, 1933, withdrawing public lands in California pending a resurvey, and heretofore partially revoked by Executive Orders Nos. 7535 of January 12, 1937, and 7590 of March 29, 1937, is hereby revoked as to the remainder of the lands affected thereby.

This order shall become effective upon the date of the official filing of the plat of the resurvey of the lands involved.

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

The White House,

April 17, 1939.
