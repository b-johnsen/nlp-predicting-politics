Executive Order 7190—Amendment of Executive Order No. 7129, of August 6, 1935, Authorizing the Acquisition of Land on Isle Royale for Emergency Conservation Work
August 6, 1935
Franklin D. Roosevelt

Executive Order No. 7129 of August 6, 1935 is hereby amended by striking out the words "from the appropriation made by the said Deficiency Act for carrying into effect the provisions of the said act of March 31, 1933," at the end of the fourth paragraph of the said order and inserting in lieu thereof the words "from funds appropriated or made available by the said Emergency Relief Appropriation Act of 1935."

FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT

The White House,

September 25, 1935.
