Executive Order 9213—Extending the Existence of the Quetico-Superior Committee
August 04, 1942
Franklin D. Roosevelt

By virtue of the authority vested in me as President of the United States, I hereby extend the existence of the Quetico-Superior Committee, created by Executive Order No. 6783 of June 30, 1934, for a period of four years, from June 30, 1942, to June 30, 1946.

This order shall be effective as of June 30, 1942.

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

The White House,

August 4, 1942.
