Executive Order 8274—Exemption of Edward A. Neill From Compulsory Retirement for Age
October 26, 1939
Franklin D. Roosevelt

Whereas

, in my judgment, the public interest requires that Edward A. Neill, Chief Mechanic, Naval Hospital, Washington, D.C., who, during the month of December, 1939, will reach the retirement age prescribed for automatic separation from the service, applicable to him, be exempted from compulsory retirement for age for a period of one year:

Now, Therefore

, by virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by section 204 of the act of June 30, 1932, 47 Stat. 382, 404 (U.S.C., title 5, sec. 715a), I hereby exempt the said Edward A. Neill from compulsory retirement for age for a period of one year ending December 31, 1940.

FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT

The White House,

October 26, 1939.
