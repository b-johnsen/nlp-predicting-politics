Executive Order 8058—Exemption of Ales Hrdlicka From Compulsory Retirement For Age
February 23, 1939
Franklin D. Roosevelt

Whereas

, in my judgment, the public interest requires that Ales Hrdlicka, Curator of Physical Anthropology in the United States National Museum, Smithsonian Institution, who, during the month of March, 1939, will reach the retirement age prescribed for automatic separation from the service, applicable to him, be exempted from compulsory retirement for age for a period of one year:

Now, Therefore

, by virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by section 204 of the act of June 30, 1932, 47 Stat. 382, 404 (U.S.C., title 5, sec. 715a), I hereby exempt the said Ales Hrdlicka from compulsory retirement for age for a period of one year ending March 31, 1940.

FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT

The White House,

February 23, 1939.
