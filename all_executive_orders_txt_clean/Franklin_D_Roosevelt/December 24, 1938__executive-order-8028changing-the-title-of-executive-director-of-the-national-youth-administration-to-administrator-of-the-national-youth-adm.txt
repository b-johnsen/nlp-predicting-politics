Executive Order 8028—Changing the Title of Executive Director of the National Youth Administration to Administrator of the National Youth Administration
December 24, 1938
Franklin D. Roosevelt

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me as President of the United States, it is hereby ordered that the title of the officer appointed and designated to have the immediate supervision of the National Youth Administration be changed from Executive Director to Administrator of the National Youth Administration.

FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT

The White House,

December 24, 1938
