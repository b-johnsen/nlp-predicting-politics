Executive Order 8394—Partial Revocation of Executive Order of June 8, 1866, Withdrawing Public Land
June 8, 1866
Franklin D. Roosevelt

OREGON

By virtue of the authority vested in me as President of the United States, the Executive order of June 8, 1866, reserving lands for lighthouse purposes, is hereby revoked as to the following-described land:

WILLAMETTE MERIDIAN

T. 5 N., R. 11 W., sec. 1, lot 2, containing 45.64 acres.

FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT

The White House,

April 13, 1940.
