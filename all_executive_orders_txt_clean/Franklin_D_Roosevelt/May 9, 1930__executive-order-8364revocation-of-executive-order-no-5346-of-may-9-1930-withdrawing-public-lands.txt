Executive Order 8364—Revocation of Executive Order No. 5346 of May 9, 1930, Withdrawing Public Lands
May 9, 1930
Franklin D. Roosevelt

MINNESOTA

By virtue of the authority vested in me by the act of June 25, 1910, c. 421, 36 Stat. 847, Executive Order No. 5346 of May 9, 1930, withdrawing public lands in Minnesota pending a resurvey, is hereby revoked.

This order shall become effective upon the date of the official filing of the plat of the resurvey of the lands involved.

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

The White House,

March 4, 1940.
