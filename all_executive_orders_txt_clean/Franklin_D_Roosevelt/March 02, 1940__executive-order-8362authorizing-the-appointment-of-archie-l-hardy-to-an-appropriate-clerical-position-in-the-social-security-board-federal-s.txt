Executive Order 8362—Authorizing the Appointment of Archie L. Hardy to an Appropriate Clerical Position in the Social Security Board, Federal Security Agency, Without Compliance With the Requirements of the Civil Service Rules
March 02, 1940
Franklin D. Roosevelt

By virtue of the authority vested in me by the provisions of paragraph Eighth of subdivision SECOND of section 2 of the Civil Service Act (22 Stat. 403, 404), it is hereby ordered that Archie L. Hardy may be appointed to an appropriate clerical position in the Social Security Board of the Federal Security Agency without compliance with the requirements of the Civil Service Rules.

This action is taken upon the recommendation of the Federal Security Administrator in order to permit Mr. Hardy's reemployment in the Social Security Board under a civil-service status which he would have acquired as of August 19, 1939, had he not resigned on July 4, 1939, to accept temporary employment in the Department of Labor.

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

The White House,

March 2, 1940.
