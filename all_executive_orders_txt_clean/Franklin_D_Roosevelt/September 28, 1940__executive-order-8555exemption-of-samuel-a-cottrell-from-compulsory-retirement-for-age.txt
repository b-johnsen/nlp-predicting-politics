Executive Order 8555—Exemption of Samuel A. Cottrell From Compulsory Retirement for Age
September 28, 1940
Franklin D. Roosevelt

Whereas

, in my judgment, the public interest requires that Samuel A. Cottrell, auditor, Income Tax Unit, Bureau of Internal Revenue, Treasury Department, who was exempted from compulsory retirement for age for a period of one year by Executive Order No. 8262 of September 26, 1939, be further exempted from such compulsory retirement for a period of nine months:

Now, Therefore

, by virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by section 204 of the act of June 30, 1932, 47 Stat. 382, 404 (U.S.C., title 5, sec. 715a), I hereby further exempt the said Samuel A. Cottrell from compulsory retirement for age for a period of nine months ending June 30, 1941.

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

The White House,

September 28, 1940.
