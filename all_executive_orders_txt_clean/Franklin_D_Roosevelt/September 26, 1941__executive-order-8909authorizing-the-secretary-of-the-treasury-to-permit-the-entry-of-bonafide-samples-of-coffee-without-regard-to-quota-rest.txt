Executive Order 8909—Authorizing the Secretary of the Treasury To Permit the Entry of Bonafide Samples of Coffee Without Regard to Quota Restrictions
September 26, 1941
Franklin D. Roosevelt

By virtue of the authority vested In me by section 2 of the joint resolution of Congress approved April 11, 1941 (Public Law 33, 77th Cong.), I hereby authorize the Secretary of the Treasury, under such conditions as he may prescribe, to permit the entry into the United States of

bona-fide

samples of coffee for testing purposes without regard to the quota restrictions provided for in the Inter-American Coffee Agreement.

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

The White House,

September 26, 1941.
