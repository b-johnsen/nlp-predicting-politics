Executive Order 7131—Revocation in Part of Executive Order No. 4914, of June 23, 1928, Withdrawing Public Lands
June 23, 1928
Franklin D. Roosevelt

WYOMING

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of June 25, 1910 (ch. 421, 36 Stat. 847), as amended by the act of August 24, 1912 (ch. 369, 37 Stat. 497), Executive Order No. 4914, of June 23, 1928, withdrawing public lands in Wyoming pending a resurvey, is hereby revoked as to the following-described townships, this revocation to become effective upon the date of the official filing of the plats of resurvey of said townships:

Tps. 23 and 24 N., R. 97 W., sixth principal meridian, Wyoming.

FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT

The White House,

August 7, 1935.
