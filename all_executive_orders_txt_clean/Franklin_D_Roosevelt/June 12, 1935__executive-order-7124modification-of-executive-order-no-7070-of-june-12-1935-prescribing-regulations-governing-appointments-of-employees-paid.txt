Executive Order 7124—Modification of Executive Order No. 7070, of June 12, 1935, Prescribing Regulations Governing Appointments of Employees Paid From Emergency Funds
June 12, 1935
Franklin D. Roosevelt

Executive Order No. 7070, of June 12, 1935, prescribing regulations governing appointments of employees paid from emergency funds, is hereby modified to the extent that transfers of employees of the Federal Emergency Relief Administration performing duties in the States of Massachusetts, Ohio, Louisiana, Georgia, and North Dakota shall not be subject to the provisions of paragraphs 3 and 4 of the order.

FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT

The White House,

August 5, 1935.
