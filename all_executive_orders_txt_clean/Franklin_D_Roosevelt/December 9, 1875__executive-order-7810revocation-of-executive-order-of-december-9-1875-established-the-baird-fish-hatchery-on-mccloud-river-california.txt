Executive Order 7810—Revocation of Executive Order of December 9, 1875, Established the Baird Fish Hatchery on McCloud River, California
December 9, 1875
Franklin D. Roosevelt

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of February 9, 1871, 16 Stat. 594, and the act of June 25, 1910, ch. 421, 36 Stat. 847, as amended by the act of August 24, 1912, ch. 369, 37 stat. 497, the Executive Order of December 9, 1875, establishing the Baird Fish Hatchery on the McCloud River, California, is hereby revoked.

FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT

The White House,

February 8, 1938.
