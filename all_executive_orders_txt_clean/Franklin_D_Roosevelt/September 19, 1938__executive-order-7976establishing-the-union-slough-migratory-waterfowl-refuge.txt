By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me as President of the United States, and in order to effectuate further the purposes of the Migratory Bird Conservation Act (45 Stat. 1222), it is ordered that the following-described lands, containing 1,334.61 acres, more or less, acquired or to be acquired by the United States, in Kossuth County, Iowa, be, and they are hereby, reserved and set apart for the use of the Department of Agriculture, subject to valid existing rights, as a refuge and breeding ground for migratory birds and other wildlife:

Provided

, that any private lands within the area shall become part of the refuge hereby established upon acquisition of title thereto or lease thereof by the United States:
