January 27, 1938
Franklin D. Roosevelt

NORTH DAKOTA

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me as President of the United States, and in order to effectuate further the purposes of the Migratory Bird Conservation Act (45 Stat. 1222), it is ordered that the lands acquired or to be acquired by the United States in the following-described areas, comprising 10,544.74 acres, more or less, in Bottineau and McHenry Counties, North Dakota, be, and they are hereby, reserved and set apart, subject to valid existing rights, for the use of the Department of Agriculture, as an addition to the Lower Souris Migratory Waterfowl Refuge established by Executive Order No. 7170 of September 4, 1935:

Provided

, that any private lands within the areas described shall become a part of the refuge upon the acquisition of title thereto or lease thereof by the United States:
