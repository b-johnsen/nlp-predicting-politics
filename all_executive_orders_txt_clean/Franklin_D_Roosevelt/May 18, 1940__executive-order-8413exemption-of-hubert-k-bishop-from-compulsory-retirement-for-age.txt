Executive Order 8413—Exemption of Hubert K. Bishop From Compulsory Retirement for Age
May 18, 1940
Franklin D. Roosevelt

Whereas

, in my judgment, the public interest requires that Hubert K. Bishop, Chief, Division of Construction, Public Roads Administration, Federal Works Agency, who, during the month of June, 1940, will reach the retirement age prescribed for automatic separation from the service, applicable to him, be exempted from compulsory retirement for age for a period of one year:

Now, Therefore

, by virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by section 204 of the act of June 30, 1932, 47 Stat. 382, 404 (U.S.C., title 5, see. 715a), I hereby exempt the said Hubert K. Bishop from compulsory retirement for age for a period of one year ending June 30, 1041.

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

The White House,

May 18, 1940.
