Executive Order 7947—Designating Del Bonita, Montana, as a Customs Port of Entry
August 09, 1938
Franklin D. Roosevelt

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of August 1, 1914, 38 Stat. 609, 623 (U.S.C., title 19, sec. 2), it is ordered that Del Bonita, Montana, be, and it is hereby, designated as a customs port of entry in Customs Collection District No. 33 (Montana and Idaho), with headquarters at Great Falls, Montana.

This order shall become effective thirty days from the date hereof.

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

The White House,

August 9, 1938.
