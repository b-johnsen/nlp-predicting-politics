Executive Order 7355—Revocation of Executive Order No. 6054 of February 28, 1933, Withdrawing Public Lands in Colorado
February 28, 1933
Franklin D. Roosevelt

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of June 25, 1910, ch. 421, 36 Stat. 847, as amended by the act of August 24, 1912, ch. 369, 37 Stat. 497, Executive Order No. 6054 of February 28, 1933, withdrawing public lands in T. 1 S., R. 75 W. of the sixth principal meridian, Colorado, pending a resurvey, is hereby revoked.

This order shall become effective upon the date of the official filing of the plat of resurvey of said township.

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

THE WHITE HOUSE,

April 30, 1936.
