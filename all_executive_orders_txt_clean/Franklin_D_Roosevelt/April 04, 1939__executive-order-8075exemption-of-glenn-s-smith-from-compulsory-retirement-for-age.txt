Executive Order 8075—Exemption of Glenn S. Smith from Compulsory Retirement for Age
April 04, 1939
Franklin D. Roosevelt

Whereas

in my judgment, the public interest requires that Glenn S. Smith, chief engineer (topographic), Geological Survey, Department of the Interior, who, during the month of April, 1939, will reach the retirement age prescribed for automatic separation from the service, applicable to him, be exempted from compulsory retirement for age for a period of one year:

Now, Therefore

, by virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by section 204 of the act of June 30, 1932, 47 Stat. 382, 404 (U.S.C., title 5, sec. 715a), I hereby exempt the said Glenn S. Smith from compulsory retirement for age for a period of one year ending April 30, 1940.

FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT

The White House,

April 4, 1939.
