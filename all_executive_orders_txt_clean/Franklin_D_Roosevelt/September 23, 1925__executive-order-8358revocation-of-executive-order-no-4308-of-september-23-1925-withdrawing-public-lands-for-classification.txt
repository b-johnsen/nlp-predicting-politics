Executive Order 8358—Revocation of Executive Order No. 4308 of September 23, 1925, Withdrawing Public Lands for Classification
September 23, 1925
Franklin D. Roosevelt

ARIZONA

By virtue of the authority vested in me by the act of June 25, 1910, c. 421, 36 Stat. 847, Executive Order No. 4308 of September 23, 1925, temporarily withdrawing public lands in Arizona for classification, pending examination by the Smithsonian Institution of certain Indian ruins located thereon, is hereby revoked.

FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT

The White House,

March 2, 1940.
