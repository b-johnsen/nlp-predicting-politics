Executive Order 7285—Prescribing the Official Flag of the Vice President of the United States
February 07, 1936
Franklin D. Roosevelt

By virtue of the authority vested in me as President of the United States, I hereby prescribe an official flag of the Vice President of the United States, to be in accordance with the plan and design accompanying and forming a part of this order.

FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT

The White House,

February 7, 1936.
