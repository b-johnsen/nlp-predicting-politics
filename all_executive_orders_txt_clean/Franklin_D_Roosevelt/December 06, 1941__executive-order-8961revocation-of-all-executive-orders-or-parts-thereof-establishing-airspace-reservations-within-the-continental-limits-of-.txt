Executive Order 8961—Revocation of All Executive Orders or Parts Thereof Establishing Airspace Reservations Within the Continental Limits of the United States, Except the District of Columbia
December 06, 1941
Franklin D. Roosevelt

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by section 4 of the Air Commerce Act of 1926 (44 Stat. 570), as amended, it is ordered that all Executive orders or parts thereof establishing airspace reservations within the continental limits of the United States, except the District of Columbia, be, and they are hereby, revoked.

This Order shall not apply to Alaska or to the Panama Canal Zone.

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

The White House,

December 6, 1941.
