Executive Order 9239—Amending Subdivision XI of Schedule A and Revoking Paragraph 2 of Subdivision III of Schedule B of the Civil Service Rules
September 08, 1942
Franklin D. Roosevelt

By virtue of the authority vested in me by paragraph Eighth, subdivision SECOND, section 2 of the Civil Service Act (22 Stat. 404), it is ordered as follows:

1. Subdivision XI, Schedule A of the Civil Service Rules is hereby amended by adding thereto the following paragraph:

"15. Bureau of the Census: Special agents employed in collecting cotton statistics."

2. Paragraph 2, Subdivision III of Schedule B of the Civil Service Rules is hereby revoked.

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

The White House,

September 8, 1942.
