Executive Order 8845—Revoking the Designation of Grand Rapids, Michigan, as a Customs Port of Entry
August 08, 1941
Franklin D. Roosevelt

By virtue of the authority vested in me by section 1 of the act of August 1, 1914, 38 Stat. 609, 623 (U.S.C., title 19, sec. 2), it is ordered that the designation of Grand Rapids, Michigan, as a customs port of entry in Customs Collection District No. 38 (Michigan), be, and it is hereby, revoked.

This order shall become effective at the close of business on the thirtieth day from the date hereof.

FRANKLIN D ROOSEVELT

The White House,

August 8, 1941.
