National database for human trafficking investigations
(a)
Establishment
The Secretary of   (referred to in this Act as the
Secretary

(b)
Contribution of information
(1)
In general
The head of a Federal, State, or local law enforcement agency conducting an investigation related
to human trafficking shall ensure that such information relating to that
investigation as the Secretary determines appropriate is entered into the
database established pursuant to subsection (a).
