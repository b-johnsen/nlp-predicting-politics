1.
Job skills training for older individuals
(a)
Targeted pilot program
The Secretary of   shall establish a pilot program pursuant to section 171 of the Workforce Investment Act of   (
29 U.S.C.  
) to provide grants to entities eligible under such section to provide job skills training to and specific for older individuals, particularly in the areas of computer literacy, advanced computer operations, and resume writing.
(b)
Definition
For purposes of the program established under subsection (a), the term
older individual
has the meaning given that term in section 101 of the Workforce Investment Act of   (
29 U.S.C.  
).
