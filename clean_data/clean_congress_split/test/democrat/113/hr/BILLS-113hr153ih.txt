564.
Definition of
outreach
For purposes of this
subchapter, the term
outreach
means the act or process of taking
steps in a systematic manner to provide information, services, and benefits
counseling to veterans, and the survivors of veterans, who may be eligible to
receive benefits under the laws administered by the Secretary to ensure that
those individuals are fully informed about, and assisted in applying for, any
benefits and programs under such laws for which they may be eligible.
