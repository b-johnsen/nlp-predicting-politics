Executive Order—Returning Tulerosa Indian Land to Public Domain
 
   ,  .

All orders establishing and setting apart the Tulerosa Valley, in  —described as follows: Beginning at the headwaters of the Tulerosa River and its tributaries in the mountains, and extending down the same 10 miles on each side for a distance of 30 miles—as an   reservation, are hereby revoked and annulled; and the said described tract of country is hereby restored to the public domain.
