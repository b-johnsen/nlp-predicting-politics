By the authority vested in me as President by the Constitution and the laws of  , and in order to promote the prompt appointment of judges to the   courts, it is hereby ordered as follows:

Section 1. Policy. The   courts play a central role in the   justice system. For the   courts to function effectively, judicial vacancies in those courts must

be filled in a timely manner with well-qualified candidates.

 . 2. Plan. The presidential plan announced on  , calls for timely consideration of judicial nominees, with the President submitting a nomination to fill a vacancy in   courts of appeals and district courts within   after the President receives notice of a vacancy or intended retirement, absent extraordinary circumstances.

 . 3. Responsibilities. The Counsel to the President shall take all appropriate steps to ensure that the President is in a position to make timely nominations for judicial vacancies consistent with this plan. All Federal departments and agencies shall assist, as requested and permitted by law, in the implementation of this order.

 . 4. Reservation of Authority. Nothing in this order shall be construed to affect the authority of the President to fill vacancies under clause 3 of section 2 of article II of the Constitution.
