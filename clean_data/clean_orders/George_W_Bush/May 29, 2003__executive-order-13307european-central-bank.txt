Executive Order  — 
 
 

By the authority vested in me as President by the Constitution and the laws of  , including sections 1 and 15 of   (22 U.S.C. 288 and 288f–5), I hereby extend to   the privileges, exemptions, and immunities provided to public international organizations designated by the President under

the International Organizations Immunities Act.

This extension of such privileges, exemptions, and immunities is not intended to abridge in any respect privileges, exemptions, or immunities that   otherwise may have acquired or may acquire by international agreements or by law.
