Executive Order—Withdrawing Sioux Indian Land
 
 

Executive  ,  .

It is hereby ordered that the lands embraced within the three existing Executive additions to the Great Sioux Reservation, in  , east of the Missouri River, viz, the one opposite  , the one opposite the mouth of Grand River and the site of the old  , and the one opposite the mouth of Big Cheyenne River and  , be, and the same are hereby, restored to the mass of the public domain, the same being no longer needed for the purpose for which they were withdrawn from sale and settlement.
