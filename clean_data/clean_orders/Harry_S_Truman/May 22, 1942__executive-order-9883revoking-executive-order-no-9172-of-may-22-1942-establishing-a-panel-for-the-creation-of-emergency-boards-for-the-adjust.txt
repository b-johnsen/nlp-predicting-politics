Executive Order  — . 9172 of  ,   for the Adjustment of Railway Labor Disputes
 
 

WHEREAS Executive Order No. 9172 of  , established for the duration of the war and   thereafter a National Railway Labor Panel of nine members for the creation of emergency boards for the adjustment of railway labor disputes; and

WHEREAS it appears that the procedures available under the Railway Labor Act are now adequate for the handling and adjustment of such disputes:

NOW, THEREFORE, by virtue of the authority vested in me by the Constitution and the statutes of  , it is ordered that the said Executive Order No. 9172 be, and it is hereby, revoked.

HARRY S. TRUMAN
