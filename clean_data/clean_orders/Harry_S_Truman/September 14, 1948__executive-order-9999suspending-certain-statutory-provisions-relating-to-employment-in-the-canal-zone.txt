Executive Order  —Suspending Certain Statutory Provisions Relating to Employment in the Canal Zone
 
 

By virtue of the authority vested in me by section 2 of  ,   (Public Law 782, 80th  ), section 103 of  ,   (Public Law 753, 80th  ), and section 4 of the Military Functions Appropriation Act,   (Public Law 766, 80th  ), relating to certain kinds of employment in the Canal Zone, and deeming such course to be in the public interest, I hereby suspend, from and including the effective dates of the said Acts, compliance with the provisions of the said sections during the continuance of the present national emergency.

Finding such action necessary because of a shortage of housing, I hereby suspend for   the application of those portions of the cited sections of the respective Acts which require employment of citizens of   or of   in skilled, technical clerical, administrative, executive, or supervisory positions.

HARRY S. TRUMAN
