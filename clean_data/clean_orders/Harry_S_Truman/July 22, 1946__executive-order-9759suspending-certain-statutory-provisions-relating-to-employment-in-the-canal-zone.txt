Executive Order  —Suspending Certain Statutory Provisions Relating to Employment in the Canal Zone
 
 

By virtue of the authority vested in me by section 2 of the War Department Civil Appropriation Act,   (public Law 374, 79th  ), section 107 of the Naval Appropriation Act,   (Public Law 492, 79th  ), and section 6 of the Military Appropriation Act,   (Public Law 515, 79th  ), relating to certain kinds of employment in the Canal Zone, and deeming such course to be in the public interest, I hereby suspend, from and including the effective dates of the said Acts, compliance with the provisions of the said sections during the continuance of any of the wars in which   is now engaged or of the present national emergency.

HARRY S. TRUMAN
