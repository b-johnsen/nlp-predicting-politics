Executive Order—Creation of Hoh River Reserve
 
   ,  .

It is hereby ordered that the following-described lands situated and lying in  , viz: Commencing at a point in the middle of the mouth of the Hoh River,  ,  , and running thence up said river in the middle of the channel thereof one mile; thence due south to   of said river; thence due south from said south bank one mile; thence due west to the Pacific Ocean, and thence with the Pacific coast line to the place of beginning, lie, and the same are hereby, withdrawn from sale and settlement and set apart as a reservation for the   not now residing upon any   reservation: Provided, however, That any tract or tracts, if any, the title to which has passed out of  , or to which valid legal rights have attached under existing laws of   providing for the disposition of the public domain, are hereby excepted and excluded from the reservation hereby created.
