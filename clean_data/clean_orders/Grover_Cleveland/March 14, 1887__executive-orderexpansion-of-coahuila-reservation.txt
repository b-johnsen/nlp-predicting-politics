Executive Order—Expansion of Coahuila Reservation
 
 

EXECUTIVE  ,  ,  .

It is hereby ordered that the lands embraced in section 23, township 7 south, range 2 east,   meridian,  , be, and the same hereby are, withdrawn from sale and settlement, and set apart for the use and occupation of the Mission Indians as an addition to the   reservation.

GROVER CLEVELAND.

SOURCE:  ,  ,  ,   GPO,  , p 824
