Executive Order  —Amending Section 149 of the Laws of the Canal Zone, Concerning Penalties for Murder in the First Degree
 
 

Under authority vested in me by law, it is ordered: That Section 149 of Act No. 14 of the Laws of the Canal Zone is amended to read as follows:

 . 149. Every person guilty of murder in the first degree shall suffer death, or if there be extenuating circumstances, confinement in the penitentiary for life; and every person guilty of murder in the second degree is punishable by imprisonment in the penitentiary not  .
