Executive Order  —Special Leaves of Absence to be Given Disabled   in Need of Medical Treatment
 
 

With respect to medical treatment of disabled veterans who are employed in the executive civil service of  , it is hereby ordered that, upon the presentation of an official statement from duly constituted medical authority that medical treatment is required, such   or sick leave as may be permitted by law and such leave without pay as may be necessary shall be granted by the proper supervisory officer to a disabled veteran in order that the veteran may receive such treatment, all without penalty in his efficiency rating.

The granting of such leave is contingent upon the veteran's giving prior notice of definite   and hours of absence required for medical treatment in order that arrangements may be made for carrying on the work during his absence.
