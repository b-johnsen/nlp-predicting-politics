Executive Order  —Rescinding the Executive Order Restricting   from Certain Ports in the Orient to a United States Port
 
 

Whereas

it appears from reports received that the unusual prevalence of epidemic cerebrospinal meningitis infection in the Orient has subsided and the danger of introducing this disease into   now can be controlled satisfactorily through the exercise of ordinary quarantine procedures at   ports of arrival;

Therefore

, Executive Order No.  , dated  , "Restricting for the Time Being the Transportation of Passengers From Certain Ports in the Orient to a United States Port", hereby is rescinded.
