Executive Order 5924—Authorizing Priority to Employees Dropped or Furloughed in Reduction of Force
 
 

  may set up procedure and promulgate regulations under which employees with appropriate status who have been recently or who are about to be furloughed or dropped for reduction of force without delinquency or misconduct may be afforded priority in the filling of vacancies when in the opinion of the commission the conditions of good administration warrant such priority.
