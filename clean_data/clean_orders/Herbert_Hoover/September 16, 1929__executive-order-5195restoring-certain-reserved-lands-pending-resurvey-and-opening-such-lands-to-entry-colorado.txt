And it is hereby ordered, pursuant to public resolution No. 29 of   (41 Stat. 434), as amended   and   (42 Stat.  ), that the above described lands, if otherwise subject to disposition, subject to valid rights, shall be opened to entry under the homestead or desert land laws only, by qualified ex-service men of the War with  , under the terms and conditions of such resolutions, and the regulations issued thereunder, for a period of  , beginning with the official filing of the plat of resurvey of T. 28 S.,   56 W., 6th P.M., and thereafter to appropriation under any public land law applicable thereto, by the general public.
