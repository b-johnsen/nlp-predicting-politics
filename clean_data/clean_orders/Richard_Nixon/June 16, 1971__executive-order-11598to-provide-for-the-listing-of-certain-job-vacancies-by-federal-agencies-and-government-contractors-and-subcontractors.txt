Executive Order  —To Provide for the Listing of Certain Job Vacancies by   and Subcontractors
 
 

Large numbers of veterans are now leaving the  's armed forces, and many of them have been encountering severe difficulties in making the transition to civilian life—in particular, many have found it difficult to locate and secure a job.

The   owes these veterans not only its deepest thanks for their sacrifice and their service, but also its assistance in their efforts to resume normal civilian activities.

In order to provide such assistance,   has established a policy of helping veterans obtain employment, including the provision of special programs of job counseling and placement. It also is the policy of   to require that veterans be given a preference in job referrals through the employment service system.

It would facilitate the employment of returning veterans—and thereby further the   policy of aiding their transition to civilian life—to require that   agencies and Federal contractors and their subcontractors list certain employment openings with the employment service system.
