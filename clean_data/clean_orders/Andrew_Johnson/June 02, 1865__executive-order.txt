Executive Order
 
 DEPARTMENT OF STATE

Whereas, pursuant to the order of the President and as a means required by the public safety, directions were issued from this  , under date of  , requiring passports from all travelers entering  , except immigrant passengers directly entering an   port from a foreign country; and

Whereas the necessities which required the adoption of that measure are believed no longer to exist:

Now, therefore, the President directs that from and after this date the order above referred to shall be, and the same is hereby, rescinded.

Nothing in this regulation, however, will be construed to relieve from due accountability any enemies of   or offenders against their peace and dignity who may hereafter seek to enter the country or at any time be found within its lawful jurisdiction.
