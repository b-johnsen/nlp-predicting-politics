Executive Order  —Giving Chief of Staff Control over   in the Absence of the Secretary of War and Assistant Secretary of War
 
 

In accordance with the provisions of Section 179 of the Revised Statutes, as amended by an Act making appropriations for the legislative, executive, and judicial expenses of the Government, approved   (22 Stats., 238), the Chief of Staff,  , is authorized and directed to perform the duties of Secretary of War during the illness or temporary absence from the seat of Government of the Secretary of War, whenever during such illness or absence the Assistant Secretary of War is also absent.
