Executive  —Amendment to the Instructions to Diplomatic Officers of  
 
 

The following section is added to Chapter XVI of the Instructions to Diplomatic Officers of  :

XVI-21. Hostesses in missions

In the absence of the wife of the head of a diplomatic mission, only the daughter, mother, or sister of the head of the mission or the wife of the counselor or highest ranking secretary (  officer), having a wife present at the post may act as hostess for the head of the mission.
