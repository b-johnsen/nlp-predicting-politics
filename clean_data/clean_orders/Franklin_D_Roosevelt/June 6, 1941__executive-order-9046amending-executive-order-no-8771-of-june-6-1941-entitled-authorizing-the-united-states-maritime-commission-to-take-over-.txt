By virtue of the authority vested in me by the act of   entitled, "An Act to authorize the acquisition by   of title to or the use of domestic or foreign merchant vessels for urgent needs of commerce and national defense, and for other purposes", approved   (Public Law 101, 77th  ), it is ordered that paragraph (c) of section 2 of Executive Order No.   of  , entitled, "Authorizing   to Take Over Certain Foreign Merchant Vessels", be, and it is hereby, amended to read as follows:

"(c) To document any or all of such vessels under the laws of   or of any other country of the Western Hemisphere."
