Executive Order 7333—Modification of Executive Order No.   of  , Prescribing Regulations Governing Appointments of Employees Paid From Emergency Funds
 
 

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me as President of  , paragraphs 3 and 4 of Executive Order No.   of  , prescribing regulations governing appointments of employees paid from emergency funds, are hereby modified so as to make said paragraphs inapplicable to transfers of persons who at the time of transfer are employed in part time positions, or at wages fixed pursuant to the provisions of Executive Order No.   of  , or any amendment or supplement thereto.
