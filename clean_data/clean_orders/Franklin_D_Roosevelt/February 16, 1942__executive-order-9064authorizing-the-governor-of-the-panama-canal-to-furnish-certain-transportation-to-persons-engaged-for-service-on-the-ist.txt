Executive Order 9064—Authorizing the Governor of the Panama Canal To Furnish Certain Transportation to Persons Engaged for Service on the Isthmus of  
 
 

By virtue of the authority vested in me by section 81 of title 2 of the Canal Zone Code, as amended by section 3 of the act of  , c. 470, 50 Stat. 487, it is hereby ordered as follows:

1. Notwithstanding the provisions of paragraph 3 of Executive Order No.   of  , relating to conditions of employment in the   service, the Governor of The Panama Canal is authorized (1) to furnish free transportation, or to make reimbursement of cost thereof, from any point within the continental   to the port of departure for the Isthmus of  , to any person engaged for service with   on the Isthmus; (2) to furnish free transportation from the port of departure to the Isthmus; and (3) to pay to such person a subsistence allowance not in excess of six dollars a day while en route to the port of departure and awaiting transportation therefrom.

2. The Governor of The Panama Canal may prescribe such regulations as may be necessary to carry out the provisions of this order.

3. This order shall be effective as of  , and shall remain in force during the continuance of the present war and for   after the termination thereof.
