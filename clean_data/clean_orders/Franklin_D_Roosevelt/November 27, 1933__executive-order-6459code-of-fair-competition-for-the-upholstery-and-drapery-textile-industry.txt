An application having been duly made, pursuant to and in full compliance with the provisions of title I of  , approved  , for my approval of a Code of Fair Competition for the Upholstery and Drapeiy Textile Industry and hearings having been held thereon and the Administrator having rendered his report containing an analysis of the said code of fair competition together with his recommendations and findings with respect thereto, and the Administrator having found that the said code of fair competition complies in all respects with the pertinent provisions of title I of said Act and that the requirements of clauses (1) and (2) of subsection (a) of section 3 of the said act have been met:
