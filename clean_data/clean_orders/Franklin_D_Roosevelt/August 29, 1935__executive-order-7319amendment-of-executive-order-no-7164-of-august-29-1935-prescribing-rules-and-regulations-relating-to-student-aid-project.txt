Definition of student-aid projects.

Student-aid projects are hereby defined to be projects financed, in whole or in part, from funds appropriated by   of  , which provide financial assistance to needy young people in amounts which will permit them to continue their education in primary schools, high schools, colleges, or graduate schools, in exchange for part-time work upon useful projects."

"3.

Amount of aid.

(a) No primary-school or high-school student shall be paid more than $6 per month for  .
