Executive  —Delegating Certain Powers to the Attorney General Under the Trading With the Enemy Act
 
 

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by section 5 (a) of the Trading with the Enemy Act, approved   (40 Stat. 411, 415), I hereby authorize and direct the Attorney General of   to exercise all power and authority conferred upon the President by section 12 of the said act, as amended.
