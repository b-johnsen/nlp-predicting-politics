Executive Order 7010-H—Dismissal of Complaint Under Section 3 (e) of Title I of the National Industrial Recovery Act With Respect to Imports of Pig Iron
 
 

A complaint under Section 3 (e) of  , addressed to me by a tariff committee representing the eastern group of merchant pig iron producers under authority conferred on said group by  , has been filed with   alleging that pig iron is being imported into   on such terms or under such conditions as to render ineffective or seriously to endanger the maintenance of the Code of Fair Competition for the Iron and Steel Industry.

After an examination of said complaint and the report and recommendation of   with respect thereto, I find that the facts do not appear to be such as to warrant further investigation in accordance with Section 3 (e) of said Act.

I, therefore, direct that no further action be taken with regard to said complaint and that the complainant be so notified.

 

Approval Recommended:

National Industrial Recovery Board

L C Marshall

Executive Secretary.
