Executive Order  — 
 
 

By the authority vested in me as President by the Constitution of the United States of America, and in order to ensure that the Export Administration Act of   is implemented with the minimum regulatory burden, Executive Order No.  , entitled "On Federal Policy Regarding the Export of Banned or Significantly Restricted Substances," is hereby revoked.
