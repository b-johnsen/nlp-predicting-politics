The Executive Order of  , in regard to the admission of deaf mutes to civil service examinations is amended to read as follows:

Deaf mutes may be admitted to examinations for all places in the classified civil service of   whose duties in the opinion of the heads of the several Executive Departments they may be considered capable of performing, and each   will furnish to   a list of such positions, which list shall not be changed without previous notice to the   and in accordance with which the   shall certify or withhold from certification deaf mutes as they are reached in their order.
