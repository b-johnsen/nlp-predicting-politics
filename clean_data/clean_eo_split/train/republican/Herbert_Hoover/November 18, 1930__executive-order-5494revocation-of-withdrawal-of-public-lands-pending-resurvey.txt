 

It is hereby ordered that Executive Order No.  , dated  , which withdrew, among other lands, the public lands in   6,  , and 18, T. 29 N.,   38 E., and all of T: 30  ,   38 E.,  ,  , be, and the same is hereby, revoked as to the above-mentioned lands.

And it is hereby ordered, pursuant to Public Resolution 85, Seventy-first  , approved  , that, subject to valid rights, the unreserved public lands in the above-mentioned areas, if otherwise subject to disposition, shall be opened under the terms and conditions of such resolution and the regulations issued thereunder, to entry under the homestead or desert land laws only, by qualified ex-service men for whose service recognition is granted by the resolution, for a period of   beginning with the date of the official filing of the plats of resurvey of said townships, and thereafter to appropriation by the general public under any public land law applicable thereto.

Subsequent to the date hereof and prior to the date of restoration to general disposition as herein provided, no right may be acquired to the released lands by settlement in advance of entry or otherwise, except strictly in accordance herewith.
