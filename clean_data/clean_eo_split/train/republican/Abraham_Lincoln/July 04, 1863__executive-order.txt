WAR DEPARTMENT,

The President announces to the country that news from   10 o'clock p.m. of the 3d is such as to cover that army with the highest honor, to promise a great success to the cause of the Union, and to claim the condolence of all for the many gallant fallen; and that for this he especially desires that on   He whose will, not ours, should ever be done be everywhere remembered and ever reverenced with profoundest gratitude.
