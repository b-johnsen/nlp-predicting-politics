By virtue of the authority vested in me by the act of   (ch. 421, 36 Stat. 847), as amended by the act of   (ch. 369, 37 Stat. 497), Executive Order No.  , dated  , withdrawing, with other lands, public lands in T. 42 N.,   87 W. of the sixth principal meridian,  , pending resurvey, is hereby revoked as to said township.

And it is hereby ordered, pursuant to Public Resolution 85, approved   (ch. 471, 46 Stat. 580), that, subject to valid rights and the provisions of existing withdrawals, the public lands in said township shall be opened only to entry under the homestead or desert-land laws by qualified ex-service men for whose service recognition is granted by said resolution, under the terms and conditions of said resolution and the regulations issued thereunder, for a period of   beginning with the date of the filing of the plat of resurvey of said township, and thereafter to appropriation by the general public under any public-land law applicable thereto.

Subsequent to the date hereof and prior to the date of restoration to general disposition as herein provided, no right may be acquired to the released land by settlement in advance of entry or otherwise except strictly in accordance with the provisions of this order.
