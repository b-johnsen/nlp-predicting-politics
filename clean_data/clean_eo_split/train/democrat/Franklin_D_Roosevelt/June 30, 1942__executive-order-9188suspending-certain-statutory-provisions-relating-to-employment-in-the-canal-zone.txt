By virtue of the authority vested in me by section 109 of the Naval Appropriation Act,   (Public No. 441, 77th  ), and section 2 of the War Department Civil Appropriation Act,   (Public No. 527, 77th  ), relating to certain kinds of employment in the Canal Zone, and deeming such course to be in the public interest, I hereby suspend, effective  , compliance with the provisions of the said sections during the continuance of any of the wars in which   is now engaged.
