 . 2.

Civil jurisdiction of Canal Zone.

—The areas of land composing this reservation shall continue to be subject to the civil jurisdiction of   in conformity with the provisions of the Canal Zone Code.

 . 3.

Restrictions respecting use of lands.

—The military authorities shall erect on this reservation no structure within two hundred feet of the banks of the Panama Canal, and The Panama Canal shall have the right to use areas not contiguous to barracks or quarters for the pasturage of cattle, provided that the pasturage areas shall be available for use by troops when required for military maneuvers or target practice.

 . 4.

Certain prior orders superseded.

—This order supersedes Executive  . 3203 of  , and   of September 15,  , relating to the Fort William D. Davis Reservation, Canal Zone; and any lands affected by such orders and not contained within the areas described in this order are hereby released from the said reservation.
