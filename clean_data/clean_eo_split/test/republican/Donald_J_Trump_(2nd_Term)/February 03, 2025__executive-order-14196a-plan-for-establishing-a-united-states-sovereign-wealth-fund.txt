Policy and Purpose

. It is the policy of   to maximize the stewardship of our national wealth for the sole benefit of   citizens. To this end, it is in the interest of the   people that   establish a sovereign wealth fund to promote fiscal sustainability, lessen the burden of taxes on   families and small businesses, establish economic security for future generations, and promote   economic and strategic leadership internationally.
