Policy

. It is the policy of the executive branch to create opportunities for   workers to compete for jobs, including jobs created through   contracts. These opportunities, particularly in regions where   remains the largest employer, are especially critical during the economic dislocation caused by the   novel coronavirus (COVID-19) pandemic. When employers trade   jobs for temporary foreign labor, for example, it reduces opportunities for   workers in a manner inconsistent with the role guest-worker programs are meant to play in the  's economy.
