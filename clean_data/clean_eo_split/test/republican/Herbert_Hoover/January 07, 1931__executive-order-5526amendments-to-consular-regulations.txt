562. Final account and draft.

No draft drawn by a   officer who has resigned, retired, or for any other cause become separated from the service will be paid. Payment from official funds may be made for all obligations, including those for transportation, incurred prior to the date of retirement or separation from the service of the officer, provided payment therefor is also made prior to such date. Accounts covering the period immediately preceding the date of retirement or separation from the service must be submitted to cover all drafts drawn and expenditures made from official funds and must not show a balance due to the officer or to  . Any obligations incurred prior to the date of retirement or separation from the service for which expenditures from official funds have not been made, and all obligations incurred subsequent to such date, must be submitted to the department on regular official accounting forms as a claim. Such claims will be forwarded to   for audit prior to payment, which will be effected by check on the Treasurer of   for the amount found due after the settlement of the officer's final accounts.
